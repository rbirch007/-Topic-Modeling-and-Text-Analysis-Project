She assisted in the planning and preparation of Our Legacy Relief Society Anthology of Verse and also A Centenary of Relief Society published by the General Board in commemoration of the one hundredth anniversary of the Society. In the latter, the section titled "Relief Society Work" which involved considerable research, was written by Mrs. Pohlman. She also aided in the preparation of other centennial souvenirs.
Relief Society will long benefit from official bulletins prepared by Mrs. Pohlman for the local Societies. They have contained instructions and rulings, and have clarified policies and procedures.
Her minutes of General Board meetings are a clear, detailed, accurate, well-written account of proceedings and General Board action. She views minutes as important historical records and she has taught secretaries of the local Societies to so regard them. One of Mrs. Pohlman's outstanding accomplishments has been to give these secretaries a vision of the importance of their work, and a training in the keeping of complete and precise records.
Vera W. Pohlman is sincerely appreciative of commendation but in no way solicits it. She is genuinely spiritual. She believes in Christian brotherhood and practices it. Her beneficences are many and always quietly performed. She is fair and just, a delightful traveling companion, loyal to her friends and devoted to her family. She is true to every trust and faithful to her Church. Her enduring values and abiding have enduring value.

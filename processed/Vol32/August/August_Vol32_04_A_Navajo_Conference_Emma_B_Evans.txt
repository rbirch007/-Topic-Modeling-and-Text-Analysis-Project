ANNUAL CONFERENCE, NAVAJO-ZUNI MISSION held on the mountain top, Toadlena Mountain, New Mexico Golden daisies and purple lupines filled every spot left vacant by lacy ferns. What a perfect setting for the spiritual feast of which we had come to partake! Soon Brother and Sister Polacca arrived in a covered wagon. Then, after awhile, a bright spot appeared on the pine-clad horizon and from every direction others came walking, on horseback, by wagon, and by automobile. Two carloads came from Blanding; one, from Arizona. fairly spoke the words of  High on the Mountain Top," and everyone sang to its inspiring rhythm.

To My Friend Lucy May Green
======================================================================

FRIEND. By Lucy May Green. I hope in richest measure Life's gifts will come to you. May all your days be pleasure, Your friends be ever true, Love ever guard and cherish, Peace hover o'er your way, Health be your constant portion, Faith be your guide and stay.

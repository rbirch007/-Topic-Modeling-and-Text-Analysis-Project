CARROT MARMALADE Submitted by Frances Kolarik 4 large carrots 4 lernons Grate the carrots and slice the lemons thin. Of the carrots and lemons and let stand overnight. 4 pints of water 5 pounds of sugar Pour the water over the mixture Next morning, boil the mixture slowly for one hour, then add the sugar and boil until the mixture jells.

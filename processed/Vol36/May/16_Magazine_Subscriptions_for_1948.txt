GPEAKING of Christmas bells, the large ones are ideal to use for wedding decorations. One lovely garden wedding where a big bell had been hung (on a wide white satin ribbon) from the branch of a beautiful old tree. Under the bell was a round table holding the exquisite wedding cake and the charming picture was spotted with a flood light. Try pinning small white flowers to these Christmas bells as table decorations, OT, for a very striking effect, make a bell of chicken wire and completely cover it by wiring flowers to it. But, whatever you do, don't forget to sprinkle the flower arrangements with ice water and place them in the refrigerator until needed.

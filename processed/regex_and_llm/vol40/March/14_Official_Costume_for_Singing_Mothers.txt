Official Costume for Singing Mothers HE official costume of the Singing Mothers as recommended in the. Relief Society Handbook is a white blouse and dark skirt. 'This costume has given a distinctive uniformity to these choruses over the years and throughout many lands, and has received much favorable comment. In view of this recommendation and the fact that Singing Mothers' choruses are receiving ever-increasing recognition, only those pictures which portray the Singing Mothers in the official costume will be accepted hereafter in the Notes From the Field department of the Magazine.

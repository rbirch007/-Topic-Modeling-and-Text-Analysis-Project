Many of the prayers have included supplication for the Constitution of the United States and for the preservation of the civil liberties which protect the exercise of free agency. Many have expressed gratitude for the auxiliary organizations of the Church, and blessings upon the sisters of Relief Society have been asked of the Heavenly Father: "that they may be abundantly blessed in relieving the suffering of the destitute and sorrowful, arresting the wayward, and inspiring them with love for pure intelligence, and educating them in the way of life and faith, that our youth may find Thee early, and the aged be established in Thy righteousness forever." Now that two European temple sites have been dedicated, the saints in Britain and on the Continent may look forward to the blessings of temple ordinances for themselves and for their kindred dead. They will have the privilege of going up into houses of the Lord, and join with other saints throughout the Church in rejoicing that "God remembers still his promise made of old." —V. P. C.

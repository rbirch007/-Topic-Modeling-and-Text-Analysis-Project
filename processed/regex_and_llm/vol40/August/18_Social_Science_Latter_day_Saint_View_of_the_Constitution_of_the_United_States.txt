Groscost TOOELE STAKE (UTAH), TOOELE NINTH WARD YOUNG MOTHERS AND CHILDREN IN ATTENDANCE AT WORK MEETING Shown in the photograph are twenty-one mothers and thirty-three children. The Tooele Ninth Ward was organized in January 1951, and since that time the membership in Relief Society has more than doubled. Many hobbies and homemaking skills have been introduced and put into practice in the homes of the sisters. Hazel Syddall is president of the Ninth Ward Relief Society, with Ardella Smith and Lunnette Lowry as her counselors, and Elaine Johnson as secretary. Leona P. Boyce is president of Tooele Stake Relief Society.

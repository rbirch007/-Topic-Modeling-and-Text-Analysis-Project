I serve Eternity For I have talked with timeless space And depth— So have glimpsed the endlessness Of heaven's unfolding. I fear not 'Time's threatenings, For I have communed with everlasting hills And skies At morning, Unafraid of 'Time's affairs With the chant of changes. I have countered its pendulumed minutes By the recognition Of the unmoving now. I humor Time As I may suffer a persuasive dream, But the covers of Eternity Are over me.

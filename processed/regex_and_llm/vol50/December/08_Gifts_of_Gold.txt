Lela Foster Morris On a sacred night they came to Bethlehem, Quiet little town so blessed, For there in a manger on the hay The infant Jesus lay at rest. Shepherds came to worship him, Groups of heaven's angels sang, "Glory to God in the highest, Peace on earth,"' their voices rang. Holy light shone on the scene, The patient oxen did not stir; The wise men knelt with gifts of gold, Rarest frankincense and myrrh. At last they found the Prince of Peace Their treasured offerings to bring, To the Babe in the lowly manger, To Christ the Lord our King.

God chose this young man. Where did he get his intelligence from? Imagined wrong, the feeling of hatred assumes power and dominion over one's thoughts, sleep, health, happiness and even over one's appearance. The most. Expensive. Clothes and_ best beauty treatment cannot blot out the hard looks and appearance that are the by-products of hatred, resentment, and the unforgiving soul.

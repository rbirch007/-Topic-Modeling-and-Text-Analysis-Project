SYMBOL William A. Forsyth Last night the earth was dark and dull With a rumbling, rolling cloud; From mountaintop to mountaintop It formed the valley's shroud. Today no clouds are in the sky; The air is fresh and new. This morning marked the season's turn? The grass was wet with dew. Today a lone, brave panzy bloomed ? When it heard a robin sing; A purple splotch on neutral gray? Sure symbol of the spring.

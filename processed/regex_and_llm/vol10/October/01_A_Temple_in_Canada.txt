A Temple in Canada Helen Orgill, Alberta Canada
Sing aloud, ye souls immortal! Waiting for the Temple ordinance To be freed from prison bars.
From your dwelling 'mid the stars, Heaven's veil seems nigh to bursting, You have prayed and waited, too, And your God in mercy hearkens, All the praise to Him is due.
Amongst northern rolling prairies, But in sight of Rockies high, Lo, behold another Temple Looms majestic to the sky!
Here the remnant sons of Laman, Heirs of Heaven's choicest blessings Now are gathering to the fold, Which the Gospel will unfold.
Then, O sing, ye souls immortal! For the work will roll along. Let hosannahs rend the heavens, Praising God in joyful song!

Nurse Aids' Class In January there will be another opportunity for young women of the Church to enter the L. D. S. Hospital for a one-year course in training in the Relief Society Nurse Aids' Class. Young women be: ween the ages of 18 and 35, who have had an eighth grade education (or the equivalent thereof), who can present a doctor's certificate showing their health to be good, and a recommendation of character from the ward Relief Society president, are eligible to this class. Ambitious girls who desire to take advantage of this fine opportunity to qualify themselves to give nursing service in the home and community, should communicate at once with Mrs. Amy Brown Lyman, General Secretary, 28 Bishop's Building, Salt Lake City, Utah.

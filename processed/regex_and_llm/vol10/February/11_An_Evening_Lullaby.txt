A Friend
Julia Farr
When happy laughter turns to tears, And darkness fills the soul with fears, When all seems lost mid shadowy gloom, And phantom danger seems to loom,— God sends a friend.
When "testing faith" takes all our will, We realize our weakness,—still, When we are struggling to do right, To help us on to win the fight, God sends a friend.
And from such friendship, given free, We seem to hear Divinity, "Whatever lot is yours on earth, Remember, from the day of birth, God is your friend."

Notes From the Field
Amy Brown Lyman
Northern States Mission.
A reorganization of the Peru branch Relief Society, of the Northern Indiana conference, was effected recently. The former officers were given a hearty vote of thanks for their faithful work in the Relief Society. The new officers are: Frieda Schmidt, president; Louise B. Rentzel and Martha E. Rentzel, counselors; Lucy Schmidt, secretary-treasurer; Virginia Crim,
organist; Mattie Crim, class leader. A picture of this Society is printed herewith.
At one of the meetings a special program was given at which a teachers' demonstration was given, which portrayed the effect of earnest and prayerful teaching. The motto of this organization is: "Pure religion and undefiled before God and the Father is this, To visit the fatherless and widows in their affliction, and to keep himself unspotted from the world." James 1:27.

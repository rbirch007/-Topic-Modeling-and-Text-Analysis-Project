The program held March 17 in the Second Ward was splendid. It included the playing of the recorded greetings from President Grant and President Lyman, a pageant, "Long-Remembered Words," a talk, "One Hundred Years of Relief Society," by Sarah Kirkham, musical selections, a humorous reminiscence, and a retold story. The observance also included a luncheon at which a beautiful birthday cake was cut by Sarah Young and Mary E. Thomas, oldest members in this ward. To conclude the entertainment, members of both the Shelley First and Second Wards attended a dance held in the tabernacle. Lula Bowler is president of the Second Ward Relief Society.

My Magazine Mabel M. Tanner Each month I get a magazine, My Relief Society Magazine; It excels most publications twice its price, With its poems—soul inspiring, "Special Features" —never tiring, And its pages full of sound and sage advice, There are helps to make home beautiful, And aids to keep wife dutiful, And stories, sweet and clean, that warm the heart; Recipes for healthful living, At the same time ever giving Beauty to that culinary art. Let's be loyal to our Magazine, Our Relief Society Magazine; It gives us wealth from out its bounteous store, With its plans for every meeting. How I thrill with joyous greeting When I see it in the mailbox at my door!

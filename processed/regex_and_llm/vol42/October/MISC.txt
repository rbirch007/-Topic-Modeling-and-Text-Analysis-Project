High, true, sky blue Wed to the water's will; Woods' bold leaf gold Caught where the pond is still; Trees white, clouds light Traced on the halted stream; One fine, thin line Severing substance and dream. Tall, slim, pale stem Framed in an autumn nook; Gold grove, pool's trove, Twinned on bank and brook; Boughs' grace, twigs' lace, Beauty on water, on water and land, Save for a shore's horizon, Undivided, stand.

Srom Near and Sar Because of a new baby in the house, we were having a particularly trying time with our two-year-old, when the July issue of the Magazine came, with Christie Lund Coles' article "Kneel to Your Child." I do sincerely thank you and her for this marvelous article. It certainly aided me in developing more patience and a positive attitude toward the situation.

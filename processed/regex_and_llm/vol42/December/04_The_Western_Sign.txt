She Western Sign Elsie F. Parton ".. Know ye not that the testimony of two nations is a witness unto you that I am God, that I remember one nation like unto another?..."' (2 Nephi 29:8). It was sunset in the western land, Yet light shone on the earth. It was the sign by Samuel told, To herald Jesus' birth. Six hundred years had passed and gone Since Lehi and his band Took journey from Jerusalem To seek the promised land. They traveled to the far unknown, By land and over "the wall," The light of faith to spur them on Wherever their God should call. A sign beheld, by prophets told, A night with glory briight— A witness to the western world Of Christ, the Life and Light. And far away in Bethlehem His star shone in the sky; The heavens were rent and angels sang Glory to God on high.

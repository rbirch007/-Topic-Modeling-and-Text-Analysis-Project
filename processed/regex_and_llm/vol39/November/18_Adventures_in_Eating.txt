This was given me by a woman from Pennsylvania, and I have no exact measurements. Take about equal parts cooked macaroni and raw potatoes, sliced thin. Put these in layers in a deep baking dish, with a little sliced onion near the center layer. Over the top, pour tomato juice or puree, being sure it gets through all the layers to the bottom of the pan. Have it come about to the top of the potato-macaroni mixture. It may be necessary to add more during the cooking, so it won't be dry. Salt and pepper to taste, and over the top, place slices of bacon. Cook for about 1% hours in a rather slow oven, 300° to 350°.

She Path to Home Mabel Law Atkinson When hammers of the rain beat on my head, And temper tantrums of the hurnicane, Shouting its uncontrolled and furious wrath, Strike fear that numbs my heart, I take the path That leads to home, and soon I feel again Secure and calm. Love's mantle gently spread About my trembling form gives me release— The path to home will lead to God and peace. From_ The Field General Secretary-Treasurer, Margaret C.

Sor Christmas Eve Leone E. McCune Let there be moonlight and one star More brilliant than the rest, and light To guide a troubled world, where strife And turmoil live, this holy night, Is peace on earth forever lost, Its golden echoes ceased to Ting? Oh, world, give pause and heed this night The words of Jesus Christ, our King! Let those who guide the destinies Of men, beware! Let hatred die, Proud heads be bent and knees be bowed The anguished heart, the tortured an Dear God, in thy compassion, Let peace, as a gentle dove, Descend to join all hearts tonight In one white crucible of love.

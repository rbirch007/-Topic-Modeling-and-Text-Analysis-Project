Snowfall Grace Sayre The falling snow is wnting poems All along the countryside; You can feel a metered rhythm Pulsing, drifting—far and wide. The falling snow is writing poems White upon a hemlock spray; Closely on the needled branches Snow inscribes an icy lay. The falling snow is writing poems Out upon the wild, gray sea. No words are lost; though winds are stormy, Tides will bring them back to me.

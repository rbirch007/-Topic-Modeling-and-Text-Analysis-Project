Shey Looked for Him Lael W. Hill - They looked for him to come in a great splendor, Robed in the purple of a potentate, Charioted and crowned, after the manner Of David or of Solomon. Through their long wait They looked for him to come with a loud sounding Of trumpets, with a bright sword in his hand, To vanquish all the Caesars and the Herods And bring new wealth to glorify the land. Their priests and prophets, telling of Messiah, Said he would be a king come saving them— 'And so they looked for him to be with armies, Invincible Some few, at Bethlehem— Poor shepherds from the hill, three weary Magi, The humble-minded and the truly wise— Noted

RUTH MAY FOX, former president of the Y. W. M. LA., became ninety-eight years old on November sixteenth. Richards, of Salt Lake City, who turned ninety-five on the twelfth of December, died January 25, 1952. 'Though her body was confined to bed for some time preceding her death, her mind moved clearly and commandingly. She astonished her-friends with her accurate memory.

A Certain Pledge Harriett L. George A tree stands like a certain pledge Of God's unfailing love; So sturdy and dependable, It reaches far above The other growing things of earth; And he who plants a tree Has placed his hand in that of God For all the world to see. A storm may lay the garden low And strip the shrubs of bloom, May scatter fear in human hearts, Create an air of gloom. But when the sky is clear again And men resume their calm, The tree stands firmly in its place, Its strength a healing balm. Page 306

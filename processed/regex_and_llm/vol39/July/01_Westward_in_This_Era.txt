Power stood Fire-ringed above their barricades, Greater than. The might of man, or mile That raised against The Word a bitter threat In sullen stretch of sage or voice of guile. Drouth could not quench their spirits' living springs, Nor rob their hunger of its vital bread. Where parching winds had gnawed the dim trail through, They only stayed to splice the broken thread.

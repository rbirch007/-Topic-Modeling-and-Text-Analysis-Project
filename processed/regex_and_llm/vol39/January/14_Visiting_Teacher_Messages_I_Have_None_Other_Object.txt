Visiting Seacher ITlessages Book of Mormon Gems of Truth Lesson 7—"I Have None Other Object Save It Be the Everlasting Welfare of Your Souls" (2 Nephi 2:30). Leone O. Jacobs For Tuesday, April 1, 1952 Objective: To help us appreciate the fact that there are many people who are interested in our everlasting welfare. N this text Father Lehi, that great Book of Mormon prophet, gives tender and solicitous advice to his sons. 'Truly this instruction is from the. Heart of a real father, for an honest parent strives to the best of his ability to instill righteous principles into the lives of his children. Almost all fathers and mothers have the welfare of their children at heart, so far as they understand it. But there are others, in addition to parents, who have our everlasting welfare at heart. How gratifying it is to know this.

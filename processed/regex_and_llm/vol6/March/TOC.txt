Why canst thou not speak? That I may find thy name, That which I most seek. That you may live and dwell with Christ, Who reigns in courts above; He made the plan that man might be, And live forever in His love. That you, as I, be born again, That we may go therein; Go down into the watery grave, Which cleanseth from all sin. MASONIC LODGE, NAUVOO Where the Relief Society was Organized, March 17, 1842. This building was originally three stories high. The Masonic Lodge room or hall was in the third story. The original windows in the lower story were square, as if arranged for a store. The steps, now on the right side, were originally in the center, and a large double door faced them. The half circle window tops now on the lower story, were taken from the third story windows.

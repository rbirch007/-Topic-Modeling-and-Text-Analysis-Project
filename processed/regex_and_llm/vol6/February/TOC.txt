Complete Equipment MY WORK Maud Baggarley My goal is nearly won If death be the goal of life My work but begun Yet impotent and vain Is the thought That I may achieve Success and cheat the pain That would rob me of breath Steal my spring blossoms And give me to Death To thrust in his dungeon But what is my work That now I should rue it Help me, oh Father You sent me to do it Turn my eyes from the hues of the sky (Tho' not to forget them) If I must needs die Let me come home As a bird to its nest Thro' dim glory fly swiftly To safety and rest

THE RELIEF SOCIETY MAGAZINE SEGO LILY Eva Willes Wangsgaard Have you seen the sego lily Lift her cup upon the hills? Multiplying willy-nilly, Catching light the desert spills? _ Have you plucked a dainty cluster? Touched the petals, white and thin? Thrilled to note translucent luster Fine as Dresden's porcelain? Have you heard the simple story How the segos played a part In our people's pain and glory?. See! Each wears a purple heart.

G reat or Small Hazel Loomis Man, great man, Subduer of the earth! The blade you wrought—a mountain now is gone, A river's course is changed. You give us comfort, ease our pain, Enhance our vision to outer space. Space rockets out! Great man explore! And yet You cannot make a blade of grass, A grain of wheat. You cannot change the circuit of the wind Or cause the desert's blossoming. You cannot give the heart A greater ecstasy — a soul reborn. Man, great man, alone, Is small.

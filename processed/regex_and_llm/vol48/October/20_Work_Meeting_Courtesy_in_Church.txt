Work Meeting) Discussion 4 — Courtesy in Church Elaine Anderson Cannon For Tuesday, January 9, 1962 Objective: To emphasize that we honor our Father in heaven when we practice and encourage respect for others in Church and for the edifices themselves. ECAUSE of the constant and varied needs which our Church edifices serve, they receive hard wear under normal circumstances. Only when every member considerately co-operates to protect these buildings can their sacred influence be fully enjoyed. 'This respect for churches and people is a_ visible component of reverence. Children, as well as people of all ages, are welcomed to our Church services.

Cannon AY THOMAS, wife of Lowell Thomas, Jr., explorer and commentator, has written an interesting account of the adventures of the Thomas family, Lowell, Sr., Lowell, Jr., herself, and two-year-old daughter, Anne, while exploring America's last frontier and forty-ninth State, Alaska. Her book Follow the North Star is published by Doubleday and Company, Inc., Garden City, New York.

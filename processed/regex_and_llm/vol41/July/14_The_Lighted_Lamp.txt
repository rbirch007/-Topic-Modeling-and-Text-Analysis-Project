She Lighted Lamp Josephine J. Harvey There are storms That crash and thunder Leaving me wind-tossed And lost. In the stillness Beyond the darkness When quiet moments come, I hear the echoes Of immortal melodies And the flame of faith Burns ever constant. I can gather strength of life From eternal whisperings, And though I cannot see the mountans Through the storm, I know that they are there. And it is love that lights the lamp, Dispelling blackness from my way. 495 Desert Mlorning Enola Chamberlin Slowly the dark surrenders, Reluctant stars take flight.

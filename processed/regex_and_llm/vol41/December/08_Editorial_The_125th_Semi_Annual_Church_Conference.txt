The presence and words of two of the General Authorities were missed—Elder Adam S. Bennion and Bishop Joseph Wirthlin both of whom were recovering from illnesses. Great coverage was afforded by radio and television in Western States, originating with KSL and KSL-T'V, and through a closed circuit the proceedings of the Priesthood meeting went to stake chapels in Utah, Idaho, Wyoming, Arizona, and up and down the Pacific Coast, where members of the Priesthood were assembled for this unusual opportunity. The spread of the gospel, geographically, was indicated by President McKay's extending love and greetings to Church members in Canada, Mexico, Europe, South Africa, South America, New Zealand, Australia, the Polynesian Islands, Japan, China, Greenland, Iceland, to groups in Korea, the Philippines, on Guam, and other islands of the Pacific.

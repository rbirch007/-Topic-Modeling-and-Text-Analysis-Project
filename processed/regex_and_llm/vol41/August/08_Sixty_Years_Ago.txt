Greene Richards Page 518 ELDORA MAUGHAN, Preston, Idaho, a Latter-dav Saint mother of seven JOHNSON MP. Children, was selected as "Idaho Mother of 1954.' A devoted Relief Society worker and active in other Church work, Mrs. Maughan accompanied her husband on a mission to New Zealand and taught school at the Maorn Agricultural College. She has served in many civic positions of responsibility, including vice-president of the National Congress of Parents and Teachers, and delegate to the Umited Nations Educational, Scientific, and Cultural departments.

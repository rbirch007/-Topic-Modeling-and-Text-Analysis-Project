Family Night Mabel Jones Gabbott When ice and snow have covered up the sandpile and the swing, And roller skates and jump-the-ropes are put away till spring; We gather in the kitchen where the smell of bread, new done, And popped corn, hot and salted, spell a lot of family fun. Then Mother tells us stories, Daddy leads us in a song, And it seems that nothing in the world could possibly go wrong.

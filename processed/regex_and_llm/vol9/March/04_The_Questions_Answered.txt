THE QUESTIONS ANSWERED. Vera Lee Kearl. " Twinkle, twinkle, little star! How I wonder what you are, Shining, laughing, dancing there; Making night so bright and fair; Smiling from the heaven's dome; Bidding us a welcome home. Do you God's bright message know That you smile the long night so? Standing here beneath the sky Wondering, asking, seeking why— Wishing I could pierce the blue, Tell me, star-bright, who are you? Laughing, dancing, darling child; Sparkling blue-eyes undefiled, Know you I am born of God? His hand shaped me from the sod Burning! Glowing! Fallen here From a higher, better sphere. World children can be bright "Tis but God's reflected light. Therefore, child, in all you do Ne'er forget his hand made you.

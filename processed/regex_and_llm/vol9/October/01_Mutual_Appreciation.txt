Mutual Appreciation By L. Lula Greene Richards If I have done you good, dear friend, In this I take delight, The kindly thanks which you extend, Are more than jewels bright. Yes, more to me than wealth or fame, Or worldly honors great; That in your prayers you breathe my name, I more appreciate.

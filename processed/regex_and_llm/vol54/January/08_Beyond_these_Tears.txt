Remembering thus, it will not matter whether One day or two or more keep us apart; For we will know love lasts beyond these years, Beyond these lonely nights, beyond these tears. More at Christmas than at other times, Miss Gold reflected, life had seemed to pass her by. A boy she once loved had been killed in an automobile accident.

TIME Dixie Randall Oveson Whoever said time flows or glides? It swoops and jerks, drags, or flies. For time is teasing, mean and dear It's here and gone; then gone and here. Each hour and minute drags or fleets: When time is needed, on it speeds. When anxious, waiting for some word, It hovers, stays, is never heard. It's so elusive, no man yet Has caught time in a human net. With all our scientific skill Time comes and goes just as it will! ~

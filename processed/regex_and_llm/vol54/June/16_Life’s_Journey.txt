LIFE'S JOURNEY The path, narrow and steep, winds its way to the top of the lofty, majestic mountain. Sharp rocks, thorns, and tangled underbrush reach out, threaten to trip, to turn me aside. Sometimes | feel myself slipping backwards. I'm tired. | stop to get a firm foothold, to renew my strength, then | continue to climb. Wouldn't it be easier to follow the wide paved road that travels around the base of the mountain? No. | must reach the top. That is my goal.

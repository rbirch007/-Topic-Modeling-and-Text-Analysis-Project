DIRECTIONS Dixie Randall Oveson A little child has asked the way, And do you point and tell? Or do you show him day by day, And bid him follow well? A little heart is broken, lost— And do you watch and weep, Or do you firmly lift it up And save it from the deep? A little soul has strayed away, And do you stand and scold, Or do you gently lead him back Into his Father's fold? To us is given the power to guide— From God, a sacred trust, That each child's steps directed be, And lead the way we must!

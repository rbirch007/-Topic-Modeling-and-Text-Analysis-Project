Spafford, General President of Relief Society, received a citation from the University of Utah as a "Distinguished Alumnus" at the Founders Day banquet February 28, 1967, sponsored by the University of Utah Alumni Association. President Spafford was signally honored for her Church and civic service and for her world-wide leadership among women. She was presented a bronze plaque in honor of the distinction awarded to her, the only woman so honored on this occasion. Members of the Cambridge, Massachusetts, Ward Relief Society, under the direction of Laurel Ulrich, have put together a ''thoroughly, wonderfully readable, imaginative, and practical guide to just about everything in and around Boston,'' announced the Boston Globe.

GOLGOTHA Margery S. Stewart What thorn bush stripped To circle ultimate anguish? What nails forged To pierce blessings? (All roads empty now Of hallow and healing). Those who drink of the Grail and fall Must be pierced By the spear. God, forgive us— Forgive us Who eat the bread And are destroyed Thereby. Water, tears, Forgiveness. Empty us of the black Tides of forgetting. Is not the hollowness In us the forgetting? But in all Our caves Will, dwell.

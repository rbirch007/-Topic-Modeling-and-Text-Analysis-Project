Swallows resting — gay notes patterned On the rippled rose of sky — Fabricate this healing vision On some child's enchanted eye. Fold the wings of time forever, Seal the peace of youth and bird In the memory of this silence, In this music never heard. Bound by years and winter-burdened, Let him stand, a child, and stare, In the innocence of morning In the coral hush of air.

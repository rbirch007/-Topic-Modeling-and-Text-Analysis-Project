Contented, we would go to bed and fall dreamlessly asleep. Of course I now realize that Mother and Dad were the magic of our family nights. 'They were wise enough to realize what it means to children to have their parents play with them. In spite of long, arduous days of farm work, they truly enjoyed themselves when joining us in play.

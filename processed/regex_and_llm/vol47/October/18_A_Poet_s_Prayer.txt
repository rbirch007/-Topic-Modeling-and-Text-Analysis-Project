A Poet s Prayer Matia McClelland Burk O Lord, Give me clean, new thoughts, Brought to time from eternity. Give me strong thoughts On which the world may lean. Then let me weave a cloth of words, Spotless and beautiful, Shining and varied, Sturdy and durable. And let me fit this fabric to these thoughts,, That I may becomingly clothe Their nakedness, When I bring them before thee And thy children.

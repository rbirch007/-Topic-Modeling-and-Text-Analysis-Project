Anchor Celia Luce \A\JHEN a ship is ready to put out to sea, the first thing that is done is to pull up the anchor. 'The anchor holds the ship in the harbor, or, if it should teach the open sea with the anchor dragging, it forms a weight that keeps the ship from making any real progress. My bad habits are like an anchor dragging at the wrong times. They hold me back and keep me from making any real progress. Instead of blaming the stormy weather for my slow speed ahead, I had better go to work on my bad habits, pull up anchor, and be free to surge forward.

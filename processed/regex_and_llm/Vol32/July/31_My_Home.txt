MY HOME Minnie I. Hodapp The trailer-man says, "Roll your home And through the world enchanted roam; Indite each night a travelogue By hill or lake or river bog; Upon the highways of the world Your destiny shall be unfurled." But I, who own a meadow-nook, With buttercups and sunny brook, Would in one selfsame spot abide Along the open country-side, Oh, not for me to rove and roam! I cannot roll my home-sweet-home!

Christ's Example Objective: To show that Jesus in his own life exemplified true charity, demonstrating that a life of charity is filled with love and is free from self- pity, intolerance, envy, and hate. 2. "Charity Suffereth Long and Is Kind" Objective: To inspire patience and kindness in our human relations. 3. "Charity Envieth Not" Objective: To show that the cultivation of the spirit of charity will help rid our lives of envy. 4."Charity Vaunteth Not Itself, Is Not Pulled Up? Objective: To show that true charity is self-forgetting. 5. "Charit}* Doth Not Behave It-self Unseemly" Objective: To show that charity is tactful and courteous because it is inspired by a reverence for life. 6. "Charity Rejoiceth In the Truth" Objective: To show that one who is truly charitable rejoices in the truth. 7. "Charity Hopeth All Things, Charity Endureth All Things" Objective: To show that charity meets offense with patience, understanding, and hope. 8. "Charity Never Faileth" Objective: To encourage people to put their trust in charity as the unfailing means of improving themselves and influencing others for good.

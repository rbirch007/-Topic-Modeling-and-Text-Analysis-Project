MRS, ANNIE D. PALMER O WHITE-WINGED GULL Mrs. Annie D. Palmer, Provo, Utah, Awarded First Prize the Eliza R. Snow Memorial Contest Thou white-winged gull! When e'er I hear The tale that made thy memory dear, How, when my native state was young, Her future in the balance hung, When Famine, gaunt and grim and keen, With tortuous hand outstretched, was seen, And thy swift mercy saved the day— I bow my head and humbly say: "I thank Thee, Lord. I thank Thee." Thou heaven-sent bird!

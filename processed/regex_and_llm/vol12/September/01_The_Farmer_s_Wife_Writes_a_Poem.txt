The Farmer's Wife Writes a Poem
Annie Pike Greenwood

Lake Phoebe, Big Cottonwood Canyon

Vol. XII SEPTEMBER, 1925 No. 
The Bible
Members of the present generation know more about the Bible as a literary product than other generations have known. The series of books on the Bible as literature, written by Prof. Richard Green Moulton, has done much to quicken the conscience of the English speaking people in this respect. Yet there are periods of time behind us when people had a more intimate knowledge of the Bible than we have. So thorough was their acquaintance with the book that all the writings of the day seemed to take on a beauty of form and majesty of movement characteristic of Bible literature.

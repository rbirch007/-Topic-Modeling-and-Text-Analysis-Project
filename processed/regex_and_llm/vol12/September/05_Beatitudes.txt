Beatitudes
Lula Greene Richards
Peace, Heart?dear Heart! The darkest hour can never be So dense but Truth can reach us. And Faith, the strongest power of all Will penetrate the blackest pall, And where earth's deepest shadows fall By light from heaven our eyes can see The lessons Hope would teach us.
Peace, peace dear Heart!
Trust, Soul beloved! Behold a heavier cross than thine And hasten aid to render. The one by whom that cross is borne, Wronged thee, perhaps, but yestermorn, Yet Charity's fair mantle worn By thee will waken Love divine, Forgiveness sweet and tender.
Trust, Soul beloved!

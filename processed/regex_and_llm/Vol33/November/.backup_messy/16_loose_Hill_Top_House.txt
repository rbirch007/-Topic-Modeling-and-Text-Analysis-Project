HILL-TOP HOUSE Maude Blixt Trone We climbed a hill, my love and I, And built our house against the sky, So near the clouds that we could share Their wanderings down lanes of air. At night we leave our shutters wide, Believing that the world outside Will count our light another link In Luna's jewels. We like to think The valley man looks up and sees A new star in Hyades.

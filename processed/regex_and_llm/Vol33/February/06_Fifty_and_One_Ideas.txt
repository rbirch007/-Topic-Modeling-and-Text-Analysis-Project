FIFTY AND ONE IDEAS FOR YOU AND FOR YOUR HOME IT is true that the people in a home make the home, but there are other things needed, too. We must work to make and keep our homes more attractive, gayer, and happier. How can this be accomplished? By keeping them simple. Simplicity is beauty, simplicity is charm. If your home looks cluttered up, do you know why? It may be that there are too many things in it for which there is no need. New furniture, new materials, and many other things people have been waiting for will soon be in the stores. But before one starts to buy, one should take an inventory of what one has and then plan what is needed. In buying furniture, it is better to look for quality; a little good furniture is far better than much that is indifferent. A living room should be arranged so that the family and friends can talk easily together. ?FEBRUARY

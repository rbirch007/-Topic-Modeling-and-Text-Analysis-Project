Woman's Sphere Ramona W. Cannon HUNDREDS of thousands of girls, the world over, are returning to their homes after giving magnificent service to their countries. With eyes more widely opened to the needs of daily living for the multitudes, and hearts more receptive to the hungers of the soul, they should prove as great an asset in peace as in war. BRITAIN'S only woman cabinet minister, "wee Ellen" Wilkinson, Minister of Education, grew up in a home where the father never earned more than $16.00 per week. She acquired her education through scholarships, which, by the way, are becoming more and more available to Britain's humbly born.

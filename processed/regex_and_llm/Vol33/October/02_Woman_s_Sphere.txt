Woman's Sphere Romana W. Cannon THREE Utah poets all contributors to The  have recently won national or local prizes. Mrs. Blanche Kendall McKey, now a resident of Washington, D. C., received the prize offered by the National League of American Pen Women for her short drama, The Unuttered Heart. Mrs. McKey attended the authors' breakfast at the biennial convention of the League, held in Washington, D. C. At this function, Myrtle McKay of Dallas, Texas, read Mrs. McKey's prize work entirely from memory. The many brilliant women writers present regarded Mrs. McKey's poetical drama as a triumph.

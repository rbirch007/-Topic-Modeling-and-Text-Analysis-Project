ACRES OF WHEAT Gene Romolo Acres of wheat, yellow-ripe for the reaping, Acres of wheat to be garnered and sheaved And powdered by mills into flour for keeping A leash upon hunger and peace among nations; Acres of wheat to be leavened and kneaded Into thousands of loaves. Every farmer whose hands Have wheat-seeded acres and watered and weeded Those-acres with vigor and patience and skill, Has shared with the Lord in the work of creation And leaned from fruition the power of will.

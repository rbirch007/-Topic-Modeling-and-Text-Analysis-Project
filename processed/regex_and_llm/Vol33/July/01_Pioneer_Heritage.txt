Called to the General Board in 19009, she has served as a Board member, assistant general secretary, general secretary, general secretary-treasurer, first counselor in the general presidency, and as general president. She has seen the organization grow in scope and influence, and has been instrumental in directing that growth. Through her desire to render broader service to the Society, Sister Lyman has taken special training in social welfare work and she has given years of service in this field, receiving national and international recognition for her outstanding achievements. Active in civic welfare, Amy Lyman has been a member of the House of Representatives in the Utah State Legislature, and has served on numerous committees devoted to the social betterment of humanity.

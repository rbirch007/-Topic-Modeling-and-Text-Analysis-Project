TO THE FEMALE RELIEF SOCIETY: W. H. Apperley, D. M. O angels of mercy, O angels of light, The servants of others from morning till night;  The thinkers and planners for all that is best, So tired but thankful you go to your rest. While sleeping and dreaming, God shows you the way, You rise with the lark prepared for the day;  While working and smiling you kindle the flame Of service and love in the true Master's name. The workers above and the workers below Are all by your side wherever you go;  You feel their blest presence, they lighten your task. They give you the courage and wisdom you ask. Dear sisters, God bless you, press on with your load. So easy to carry when you tread the right road;  Our Father is with you, he gives you his love. While feeding the hungry— bread comes from above. Good sisters, true love I give unto you, Our Lord's faithful workers, so wise and so true;  A halo of light encircles each form, I feel it, I see it — it keeps my heart warm. . Logan, Utah.

Relief Society Workers Isabella E. King. We're a band of noble sisters, United heart and hand, To battle with all evil And keep the Lord's command. Then, love ye one another And words of peace impart, And by our love and kindness Bind up the broken heart. Visit the afflicted, And give them help and cheer, And by our acts convince them They are our sisters dear. Clothe and feed the needy, Was our Savior's word. Letting not a murmur In our ranks be heard. Then let us work like bees, In the hive of desert. Dispersing all around us The welcome gifts we get. All hail! Ye noble mothers. Be faithful to your charge With charity unceasing; — Ye are working for the Lord.

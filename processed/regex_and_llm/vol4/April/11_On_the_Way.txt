ON THE WAY. Spring is comin'. Think I hear the bees a hummin'; Caught a glimpse of bluebird's wing, Heard a speckled med'lark sing, Felt a touch of balmy breeze, Heard it whisperin' to the trees, "Spring is comin'." Spring is comin'. Hear the wood-pecker drummin'; See the green blades peepin' thru, And a blue-eyed violet too! Hark, I hear a robin's song! Makes me happy all day long, MANTI, UTAH. Spring is comin'. Mrs. Parley Nelson. EDITORIAL Entered as second class matter at the Post Office, Salt Lake City, Utah.

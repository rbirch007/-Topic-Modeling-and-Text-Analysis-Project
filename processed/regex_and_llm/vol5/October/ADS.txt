A Word to Utah Mothers and Their Sons! HERE IS A MESSAGE FROM THE UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT TO YOUR SON. WILL YOU KINDLY SEE THAT HE GETS IT?

"Enter College if you are fitted to do so or return to college if you are already enrolled, and enlist with the Students Army Training Corps. The Students Army Training Corps at the University of Utah is an organized body of men in college who are receiving military training under the direct supervision of military officers detailed by the War Department. The Student serves in the military activities of the nation, but he does so while continuing his school work. Complete equipment is provided by the government and each student is paid the regulation allowance of $30 per month. Mothers, urge your boys who registered with the United States government on September 12, also to register with the University of Utah on September 26. Send for our descriptive literature and catalogue."

UNIVERSITY OF UTAH, Salt Lake City John A. Widtsoe, President Elbert D. Thomas, Secretary-Registrar

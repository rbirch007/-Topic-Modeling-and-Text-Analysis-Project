PEAR BREAD OR FRUIT BREAD  
Pears (dry and sliced) 16 oz., raisins 5 oz., currants 5 oz., walnuts (chopped) 5 oz., sugar 4 oz., cinnamon 2 teaspoons, cloves 1/2 teaspoon, anise 1 teaspoon, brandy or cider 4 oz. Mix well and leave stand over night in a stone or glass jar. In the morning make sponge: 1 qt. water, 1 1/2 lbs. flour, 3 cakes Fleischmann's Yeast, 1 tablespoon sugar. When light (about 1 1/2 hours) mix the fruit in the sponge, add about 1 1/2 lbs. flour make a medium stiff dough, let rise until about double size, make into loaves, let rise to double size and bake. If served with cheese is delicious. This bread will keep for weeks, and improves with age. The fruit may be prepared days or even weeks ahead of baking. It will improve in flavor.

THANKSGIVING. By Mary Foster Gibbs.  
For all Thy providences, God of Peace and War, I thank Thee! Thank Thee for storms on my uncovered, cringing head? For tears and pain? For all the awful clang and whirr of cruel war? Say that again!  
For all Thy providences, God of Peace and War, I thank Thee! Thank Thee for life, for motherhood, for work, for death. For my free will, The power to climb from deeps to heights, while earth and I Both learn to trust Thee still.  
For all Thy providences, God of Peace and War, I thank Thee!  

LO, THE POOR, RICH INDIAN. Cartoon by Jack Sears.

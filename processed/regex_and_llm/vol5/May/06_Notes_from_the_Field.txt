Notes from the Field. By Amy Brown Lyman, General Secretary. RELIEF SOCIETY STAKE CONFERENCES FOR 1918.

The Relief Society stake conferences appointed for May, June and July will be held in connection with the stake quarterly conferences; those appointed for November will be held independently. 

Conference Dates. 
May 4th and 5th Curlew, Alberta, St. Johns, Raft River, South Sanpete, Wayne. 
May 11th and 12th Emery, Millard, Juab, Taylor, Snowflake. 
May 18th and 19th San Juan, Moapa, Shelley, Bannock, Maricopa, Malad, Blackfoot. 
May 19th and 20th Teton. 
May 25th and 26th Bingham, Portneuf, St. Joseph, Pocatello, Young. 
June 1st and 2nd Rigby, Panguitch, Big Horn, Idaho, San Luis. 
June 8th and 9th Uintah, Kanab, Morgan, Oneida. 
June 15th and 16th St. George, North Sanpete, Star Valley, Union. 
June 22nd and 23rd Sevier, Fremont, Bear Lake, Deseret, Carbon, Parowan. 
June 29th and 30th Duchesne, Montpelier. 
July 20th and 21st Benson, Hyrum, Tooele, Tintic. 
July 27th and 28th Wasatch, Woodruff, Cassia, Yellowstone, Beaver. 
August 3rd and 4th Boise. 
November (dates to be arranged later) Alpine, Bear River, Box Elder, Cache, Carbon, Cottonwood, South Davis, Ensign, Granite, Jordan, Liberty, Nebo, North Davis, North Weber, Ogden, Pioneer, Salt Lake, Summit, Utah, Weber.

Jones: Dear Lord DEDICATORY ANTHEM Words by Alberta H. Christensen Music by Florence J. Madsen Members, General Board of Relief Society Thy blessing on this house, dear Lord, And bless the earth on which it stands; For here is a witness to our faith in thee, A testament of willing hands; a dream— A cherished dream come true. Let thy protecting spirit arm its sturdy walls, Defending them from harm. Beneath its sheltering roof O let there be peace as proof That harmony and love abide within— That love and harmony abide within. For Jesus said, "Ye shall do the work Which ye see me do." Help us, Lord, to ever keep, Thy great command to "feed my sheep," That angels shall companion us Who serve thee in thy holy cause, And truth and knowledge ever flow In promised blessings on our heads. O let thy spirit ever be With those who build this house to thee, That all may grow in faith and love And glorify thy holy name, Thy holy name, O Lord!

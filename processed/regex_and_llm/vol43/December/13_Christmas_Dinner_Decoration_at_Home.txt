CHRISTMAS DINNER DECORATION AT HOME Inez R. Allen This holiday table displays the richness and glitter of the season, but not with the usual colors of red and green. To harmonize with the china, crystal, and silver used on the table, pink and silver Christmas ornaments were used. 'These were complemented by candles and carnations of a very delicate pink. The nchness of the damask cloth and the tinted stemware completed the color scheme.

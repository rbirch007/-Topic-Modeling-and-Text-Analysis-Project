Appreciation for the Singing Mlothers &xpressed by President David C. MeKay (At the closing session of the 126th General Conference of the Church) E have said "thank you" to the members of the Choirs, but let me mention them again.... We appreciate what they did our Singing Mothers. I cannot mention the name "mother" without being overcome with emotion. 'Those mothers who furnished that singing were the mothers of 1600 children! Even their title, "Singing Mothers," tells a story of sacrifice, a story of love, a story of home. No wonder they could sing the songs of Zion so inspirationally under the masterful leadership of Sister Florence Jepperson Madsen.

INy Prayer Irene E. Jones Dear Lord, to thee I raise my prayer, That I may always kindness share, And fill with love another's need: Dear Lord, this is my humble creed. Oh, give me daily of thy grace, To lighten every darkened place Where shadows dwell and fears abide; Be ever present at my side. Lord, whomsoever I chance to meet, Who doubting knows and fears defeat, May I have strength to strengthen him, To dry the tears his eyes bedim. Oh, give me of thy wisdom, Lord, That I may teach in sweet accord, And with thy everlasting plan, Lift nearer thee, each stricken man. In all I do, may I fulfill Thy holy mission, and instill In all who mourn, a part of me That comes, O Lord, direct from thee.

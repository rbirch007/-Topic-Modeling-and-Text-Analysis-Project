She Kingbird Ethel Jacobson His court is the orchard on the hill, His throne is a peach-tree bough; A ruby winks from the sable crown That graces his royal brow. In robes of velvet, ermine-trimmed, He watches with lively eye Over his feathered colony, Watches the wide blue sky That no marauding hawk or crow May swoop with threatening wing "While a small bird sits on a peach-tree bough, A guardian and a king! Willard Luce MONTEZUMA WELL, ARIZONA _

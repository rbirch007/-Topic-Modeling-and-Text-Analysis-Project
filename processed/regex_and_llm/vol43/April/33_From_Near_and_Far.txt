The inspiring address by President J. Reuben Clark Jr. In the December issue brought joy and peace to my soul and added desire in my heart to live the teachings of our glorious gospel. That discussion of "Children in the Scriptures" is of such value and interest and gives us the Lord's instructions regarding children.

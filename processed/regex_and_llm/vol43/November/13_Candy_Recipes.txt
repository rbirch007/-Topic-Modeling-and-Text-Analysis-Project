Butter (melt with cocoa butter) Melt chocolate in a double boiler, not too hot. Beat until smooth. Add melted butters and mix until thoroughly blended. Pour into 7" bowl. Add mint. Place in refrigerator or other cold place until it feels cool but not thick. Stir occasionally and scrape from sides of bowl while cooling (about 20 minutes). Watch closely so that it does not harden (in which case place over warm water a few minutes and beat smooth, but do not allow to become warm). When about the consistency of very soft custard, beat 10 to 15 minutes, or until fluffy, but not too heavy. Use electric beater. The mixture should be soft enough to spread like heavy whipped cream.

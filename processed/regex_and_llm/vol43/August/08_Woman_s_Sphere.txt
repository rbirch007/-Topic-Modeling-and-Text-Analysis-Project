Woman's Sphere Ramona W. Cannon Swedish doctor was puzzled because the wound of a housewife did not close. The woman told him that was so because a cooking flavoring or essence had run into the wound. The doctor began to investigate. American physicians took up the problem. Result, the discovery of Dicumarol and Dicumarin, which thin blood clots and are used in treating phlebitis and some coronary troubles. ARGERY S.

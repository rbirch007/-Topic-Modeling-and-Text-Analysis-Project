She writes: "The Relief Society Magazine means a great deal to me, especially since the Traverse City Branch is forty miles away from my home, and Relief Society meetings there are held at a time when I cannot attend. However, I have found the Magazine a wonderful way to keep in touch with the organization, and I look forward eagerly to each issue."

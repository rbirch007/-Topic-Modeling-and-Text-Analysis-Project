Woman's Sphere Ramona W. Cannon RS. LUCY NORDEN, some years ago, fled from Nazi Germany to the United States. A remarkably skilled pianist, she had accompanied opera singers and great instrumentalists in her native land. She soon became a member of the teaching staff of the New York Conservatory of Music, and now, at the age of eighty-four, still teaches sixteen pupils and plays the piano herself with exquisite artistry.

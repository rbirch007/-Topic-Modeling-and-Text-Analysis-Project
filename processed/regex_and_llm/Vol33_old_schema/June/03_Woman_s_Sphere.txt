Woman's Sphere Ramona W. Cannon RISCILLA L. EVANS (Mrs. Frank Evans) of the Relief Society General Board has been re-elected to the post of corresponding secretary of the National Council of Women of the United States. Mrs. Evans was a delegate from the Relief Society to the national organization six years ago when she was serving as a missionary in the Eastern States Mission over which her husband presided. At that time she was made corresponding secretary and has held that office ever since. She will serve two more years. She accompanied President Belle S. Spafford and Edith S. Elliot of the General Board of Relief Society to the inter-continental congress of the National Councils of Women of the United States, Canada, and some of the Latin-American countries held in New York City, May 6, 7, and 8,

At the choked sound from within, she opened the door. "Why, you poor child," she murmured. There was Rosalee, trying ineffectually to force a wire down the sink drain. Her face was smudged with tears. — "You just put that wire down, honey," said Alta, setting the pie on one corner of the cluttered table. "Wash your face, and call your little boys in from play. Her husband had left her enough to live on, if she were careful, until her little boys, aged three and five, were old enough to go to school.

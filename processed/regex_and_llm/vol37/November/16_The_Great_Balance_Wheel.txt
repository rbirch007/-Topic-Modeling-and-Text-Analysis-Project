Winds, Blow Lightly Grace Barker Wilson Oh, winds, blow lightly. My child is asleep, And will not awaken Though over him sweep Storm gales and thunder, His sleep is so deep. But, winds, blow lightly. Oh, rains, fall softly, Though he will not hear Should you beat in torrents. And never a fear Would trouble his sleeping As darkness comes near.

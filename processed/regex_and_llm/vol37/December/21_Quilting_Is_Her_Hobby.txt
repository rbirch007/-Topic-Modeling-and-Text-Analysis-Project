QUILTING IS HER HOBBY At the age of eighty-four, Mary Ann Bringhurst is still making quilts. Last year she purchased an electric sewing machine in order to do her work more quickly and with less effort. A former resident of Toquerville, Utah, Mrs. Bringhurst is the mother of six sons and six daughters. She has fifty-five grandchildren. It is her desire to make enough star quilts for all her children, grandchildren, and also for many of her friends. She has already completed more than_ one hundred star quilts and countless "nine-patch" quilts.

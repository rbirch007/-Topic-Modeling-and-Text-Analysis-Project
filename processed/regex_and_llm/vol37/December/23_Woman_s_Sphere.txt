MAMIE PETERS CALL'S brief novel, Lady Laura, is a lively story of a Welsh immigrant girl with a beautiful singing voice, who finds herself, not by design, in a Mormon pioneer train on its way to "The Valley." Touches of humor and authentic detail of pioneer life add to the interest. LARA L. JARVIS of Utah has been elected first vice-president of the National Order of Women Legislators. Thirty-one year old California girl, Florence Chadwick, swam the English Channel from Cap Griz Nez, France, to Dover, England, on August 8th, in thirteen hours and twenty-three minutes.

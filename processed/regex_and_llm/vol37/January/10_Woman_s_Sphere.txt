The family complied with the wishes of the community. FeXECUTIVE Director of the "" Children's Book Council in New York City is a former Utahn, Sybil V. Jacobson. This council sponsors National Book Week and Mrs. Jacobson originated the theme of the Week, "Make Friends with Books," as well as seventeen other publicity ideas. She corresponds with teachers, librarians, parents, and children, and hopes to increase last year's attendance of 25,000 children at the great New York Book Fair to 125,000 this year.

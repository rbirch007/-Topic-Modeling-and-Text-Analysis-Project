Memo Jo An Old Love LeRoy Burke Meagher I thought that we had settled all The apple orchard where we kissed, My debts for loving you, The songs we used to sing. And that my last receipt had read So will you please return at once There was no balance due. And carry out your part; But you forgot to gather up I hate unfinished business... And— These hills we climbed last spring, P. S.... Bring back my heart.

Visiting Seacher ITlessages—Our Savior Speaks Lesson 8—'Be of Good Cheer" (Matt. 14:27) Mary Grant Judd For Tuesday, May 2, 1950 Objective: To reaffirm that in bringing help and comfort to others, we gain joy for ourselves. S we reach the last message for this year, we earnestly hope that the visiting teachers have gained for themselves, and have been able to help others to gain, a nearness to our Savior which exceeds any that they may have felt before. We wish to conclude with a salutation which Christ frequently used to give encouragement to those with whom he mingled. "Be of good cheer" he told the man who was afflicted with palsy (Matt. 9:2).

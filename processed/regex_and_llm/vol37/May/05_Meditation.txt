Meditation Bessie G. Hale To him who knows the sweetness Of prayerful quietude, The things which are revealed In lofty solitude, There comes a benediction As of moonlight over land; One feels the very presence Of his gracious, guiding hand. Art Florence Berrett Dunford Fashioning a poem is like Capturing a moment out of time; The moment lengthens, Becomes a piece of that far horizon, A lost smile— A bit of love's perfection; These are yours to keep, When you write a poem.

THE CHIMES Pliny A. Wiley I heard the chimes on New Year's morn, In silvery cadence far and near, Proclaim another monarch born, Lord of the cycle of the year. They rang to honor and to praise The King of Time whom all revere. He grants the months, the weeks, the days, The seasons of the circling year. They rang so long, so clear, so sweet That heaven and earth repeat the strain. Men prayed for Time?Time to complete Their tasks, God grants a year again,

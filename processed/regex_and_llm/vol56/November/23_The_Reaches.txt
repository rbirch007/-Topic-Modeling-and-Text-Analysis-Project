THE REACHES The sea rolls over, rubbing a briny back on the shore.
Small bubbles burst with a sigh. Wind whirls through the treetops, and full of presence leaves chatter.
Thunder rips through a gather of clouds unleashing the rain.
Wherever silence opens, sound rushes in, welcome, and all held-back riches tumble forth.

—Bernice Ames

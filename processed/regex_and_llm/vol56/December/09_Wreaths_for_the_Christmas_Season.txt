Wreaths for the Christmas Season
A Wreath for Welcoming
The large wreath, eight feet wide and eight feet high, was made from a circle of plywood, covered with linoleum. Pine cones and gold and brown pods were attached to the circle with airplane glue. A large bow of plastic ribbon, bright green in color, decorated the top of the wreath. Small gold lights illuminated the wreath which was hung on a peg and attached to the house so that the lower part of the wreath formed the threshold to the house.

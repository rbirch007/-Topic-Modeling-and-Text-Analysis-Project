Someone /, Announcement of Winners

Mary R. Decker Safsten

The General Board of Relief Society is happy to announce the names of the winners in the first annual Relief Society Song Writing Contest. The first prize of sixty dollars for the music and forty dollars for the lyrics is awarded to the song-writing team of Mary D. Safsten and Ranghild L. Safsten for a lovely hymn, "Lo, Thou Art Near." Mary D. Safsten wrote the music, and the lyrics were penned by her mother-in-law. Both women are members of the Bellingham Ward, Cascade Stake, in Washington.

The second prize of seventy-five dollars is awarded to Ruth B. Lehenbauer of Logan, Utah, for her hymn "My Cup of Joy."

The prize-winning songs are the property of the General Board of Relief Society, and will be programmed for performance by Singing Mother choruses. The General Board is very pleased with the response to this first contest. There were 222 songs submitted to a committee of eminently qualified judges. The winners are to be congratulated for their very fine abilities.

The caliber of the songs submitted was very high, showing the great talent of Latter-day Saint women in the musical field. The General Board once again congratulates these sisters and all others who entered the contest on their accomplishments and encourages even more widespread participation in future competition.
 
Mary R.

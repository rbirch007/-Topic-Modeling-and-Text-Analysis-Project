A BRACE OF HORSES LEARN FROM FICTION. BIOGRAPHY IS THE STORY OF ONE MAN, FICITION OFFERS AN ACCOUNT OF MANKIND. RESOLVE TO READ A GOOD BOOK EACH MONTH OR EVEN EACH WEEK. THEN MAKE THE EFFORT TO LIVE UP TO YOUR RESOLVE.

"PICKING THE BRAINS" OF OTHERS SOUNDS ALMOST UNPARDONABLE, BUT THAT IS WHAT THE MENTALLY ALERT DO EVERY DAY OF THEIR LIVES. IF YOU MEET A CERAMIC ARTIST, LEARN ABOUT CERAMICS. IF YOU MEET AN OLD-TIMER, LEARN MORE ABOUT YOUR COMMUNITY. IF YOU MEET A SCOUT-EXPLORER, LEARN MORE ABOUT MOUNTAINEERING.

A GROUP OF SEVENTY-FIVE ADULTS LIVING NEAR THE HUNTINGTON LIBRARY WHERE RARE BOOK EXHIBITS ARE ALWAYS OPEN FREE TO THE PUBLIC WAS ASKED HOW OFTEN EACH OF THE GROUP VISITED THE LIBRARY. SOME HAD NEVER BEEN THERE. SOME HAD BEEN ONLY WHEN TAKING OUT-OF-TOWN GUESTS. ONLY THREE VISITED WITH EACH CHANGE OF EXHIBIT.

IF THERE ARE LIBRARIES, MUSEUMS, ART GALLERIES IN YOUR AREA, YOU MAY DECIDE TO GROW MENTALLY THROUGH VISITING THESE PLACES. PERHAPS YOU WANT TO PULL OUT OF YOUR RUT IN A REALLY BIG WAY. PLAN TO ATTEND SOME ADULT EDUCATION OR COLLEGE EXTENSION CLASSES. EVEN GO BACK TO SCHOOL IF THERE IS AN OPPORTUNITY IN YOUR AREA.

"FOR WHAT IS A MAN PROFITED, IF HE SHALL GAIN THE WHOLE WORLD AND LOSE HIS OWN SOUL?" (Matt. 16:26.)

HAVE YOU GROWN CARELESS AND SUBSTITUTED ROUTINE CHURCH ACTIVITY FOR REAL SPIRITUALITY? CHURCH ATTENDANCE IS EXCELLENT, BUT UNLESS IT IS AN EVIDENCE OF A "HUNGER AND THIRST AFTER RIGHTEOUSNESS," YOU WILL STILL FIND YOURSELF IN A RUT. DON'T GO TO CHURCH ONLY TO HAVE YOUR BODY COUNTED.

TO REALLY COMMUNE WITH GOD AND HEAR HIS ANSWERS TO OUR PRAYERS WE MUST CLOSE EVERYTHING ELSE OUT OF OUR MINDS. OUR MINDS MUST BE OPEN TO RECEIVE THE HELP AND THE SOLACE THAT PRAYER CAN BRING.

WHY DON'T YOU TRY READING ANY OF THE SCRIPTURES STRAIGHT THROUGH WITH A GOOD COMMENTARY AT YOUR ELBOW?

Ruts are dangerous not only for a low-centered car on a high-centered road, but for any man or woman, boy or girl, who has ever read the commandment, "Be ye therefore perfect, even as your Father which is in heaven is perfect." (Matt. 5:48.) Remember that it takes just a brace of horses whose names are Resolution and Effort to free you and set you high on the shoulder again.

(John 3:5.) At the end of his earthly ministry, he placed the greatest possible stamp of authoritativeness on what he had taught and exemplified by giving up his life, not only to provide the basic requisite—victory over death—but to open the way to eternal membership in his Father's heavenly kingdom to all who have the desire and the will to attain to it. This is his Church—and through his prophets he has continued to emphasize the great joy and rewards that come through following the path he has charted. His greatest work was to offer and make possible salvation for others.

Wind, needle the patchwork hill,
If you will,
And scissor the willow to a winter fringe,
But oh, wind, fold the lake gently;
pleat the water—
Gather it in wide white ruffles;
Fashion a fine gown
For sky,
That shy Blue matron of autumn
To wear tomorrow.

Maxine R. Jennings

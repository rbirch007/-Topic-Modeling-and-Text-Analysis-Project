Autumn reminds me
Of rains washing the earth of dust to make ready her winter sleep.
And mothers washing the summer's play from the necks of squirming children.

Of lasting autumn flowers bursting with the giving of themselves into full blooms of perfection, bronzed copper and scented gold. Of cold cement sidewalks warmed by bright falling leaves, capped with a cloudless violet sky too deep for summer's frivolity. Of the fresh crispness of young love innocent of the hardships ahead, but filled with the courage of youth and trust in the love of God.

Nola Marilyn Hansen
Relief Society

General Announcement
The Relief Society General Board is pleased to announce the second contest for Latter-day Saint women in song writing (lyrics and music) to be conducted annually. The purpose of the contest is to encourage creativity in this form of art and excellence in the general field of music among the women of the Church. The General Board will be happy to receive entries following this announcement until the closing date of the contest (see below).

Name of Contest
This contest is to be known as THE RELIEF SOCIETY SONG CONTEST.

Dates
This year's contest (1969-70) opens with this announcement and closes March 1, 1970. All entries must be postmarked before midnight, March 1, 1970.

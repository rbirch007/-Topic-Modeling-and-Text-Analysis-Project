CHILD ASLEEP Seeing you asleep, | weep: Weep for the soft Gentleness the night Has laid upon your face, Like love, like light: My fingers trace The way your hair, Silken and fair, Falls in soft grace. Oh, child, so innocent, So content; | hear the beat Of tomorrow's firm, Relentless feet. But, tonight, sleep. Sleep sweet. Oh, child of innocence And of wonder, sleep.

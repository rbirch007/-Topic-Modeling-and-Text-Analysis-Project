SWEETEN UP YOUR DAY! CHERRIES JUBILEE SAUCE A sauce so glamorous, elegant, and tasty, served over ham. Your guests and family will rave!

1. No. 2 can red sour cherries
   1 cup cider vinegar
   1/4 cup flour
   12 whole cloves
   1 tablespoon lemon juice
   1 stick cinnamon
   Red food coloring

2. Drain cherries, reserve liquid.
3. In pan, combine sugar, 2 cups cherry juice, vinegar, cloves, and cinnamon stick.
4. Bring mixture to a boil, reduce heat, cook about 10 minutes.
5. Meanwhile, mix flour with 4 tablespoons cherry liquid until smooth.
6. Gradually strain spice mixture into flour and cook until thick.
7. Add cherries, lemon juice, and a few drops of red food coloring and continue cooking until cherries are heated.

To serve: pour over sliced ham which has been baked according to directions on wrapper.

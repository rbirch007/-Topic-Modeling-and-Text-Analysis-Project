REMEMBRANCE IN THE RAIN It rained in San Pedro— First day in a long time. From way up on the hill | saw Low gray clouds Stretching all across the valley; Bright lights on cars Hurrying home in the evening; Big white ocean liners Gliding into the channel. And my boy Slightly bouncing as he walked, Holding his black and yellow umbrella Straight up! And broadly grinning. And right behind, two cats Softly bouncing as they walked With fluffy black and gray tails Straight up!. And grinning too, | think —Helen Clegg Broadbent 369 J. Udyth S.

Elder David B. Haight Called to Be an Assistant to the Twelve @

Elder David B. Haight was sustained as an Assistant to the Twelve at the Solemn Assembly held at General Conference April 6, 1970. At the time of his call to be a General Authority, Elder Haight was serving as assistant to President Ernest L. Wilkinson at Brigham Young University, and had served as a member of the Church Priesthood Missionary Committee, and as a Regional Representative of the Twelve. From 1963-1966 he served as president of the Scottish Mission. Prior to that he served for twelve years as president of the Palo Alto Stake in California.

Elder Haight has been active in civic affairs throughout his lifetime. He served as mayor of Palo Alto, Chamber Downtown tion, director of the Red Cross Peninsula Blood Bank, on hospital and area councils.

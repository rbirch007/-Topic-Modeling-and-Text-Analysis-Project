They say that happiness is an elusive thing
That merely touches one, then flits away;
But happiness came in my door quite recently
And did my bidding when I told it "stay."

A morning spent in my old brown rocking chair
With ears toward the television screen
To hear the words of truth from men of God
Uplifted me and made my home serene.

Then it carried over as I went about my work.
This quiet, happy feeling in my heart,
And gave me extra patience which was felt
By those around me. Now I've made a start

Toward becoming better than before.
One happy day arrived and beckoned more.

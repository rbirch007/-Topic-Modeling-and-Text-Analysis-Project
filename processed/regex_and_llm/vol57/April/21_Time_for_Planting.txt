TIME FOR PLANTING Spring is time for planting. Green shoots start, Blossoms line the bough, then Summer gives each time for growing. When autumn comes the corn is high And wears a silken tassel. Apples, grapes and pears Blend brilliant colors With carrots, cabbages and pumpkin. Oh, resplendent autumn saffron, Flame, scarlet and leaf green, From your maturity of fruit And seed we gain�the promise of Another yield, Another spring, Another planting. �Margaret B.

God gave his Son
To speed the day
When | in light,
Could find the way.
Can | do less?
My sons must go
To other sheep
To let them know!

He gave the gift to all
Eternal life can we
Do less for him who said
"Here am I, send me!"

We have sent three sons
Our blessings manifold
And count it not as sacrifice
This great rejoicing in the fold.

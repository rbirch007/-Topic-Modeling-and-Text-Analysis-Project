I made a special kind of quilt 
To use up scraps, and fill a need; 
They go together, hit or miss, 
And color schemes I do not heed. 

Some blocks are pretty, I am pleased, 
And some are madly wry, 
So shall I laugh, or throw them out, 
And who is crazy, the quilt or \? 

Ethel H. Harp 
Virginia D. Nielsen, Nuku'alofa, Tonga

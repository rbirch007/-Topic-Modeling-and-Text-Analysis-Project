Her years were very few upon this earth,
The curtain fell too soon upon her drama,
So unexpectedly, during the second act;
And we who stayed behind to carry on,
Wondered if we had played our roles
As well, as wisely, as the parts were written.
 
Ruth G. Rothe Relief Society Activities

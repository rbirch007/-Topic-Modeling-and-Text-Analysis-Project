Shanksgiving Grace Ingles Frost I thank thee, Heavenly Father, For the harvest autumn yields— The fruitage of the orchards And the bounty of the fields. I thank thee, too, my Father, For the autumn's regal grace, For habiliments of beauty, And the glory of her face. I thank thee, Heavenly Father, For all thy kindness sends; But most of all I thank thee For the gift of gracious friends,

Andrew Jenson, Assistant Church Historian, computed in 1930, that from the time missionary work began in Sweden until 1930, 19,147 persons had been baptized, of whom 8,545 had emigrated to Utah. In August 1957 there were 2,404 members of the Church in Sweden, located in thirty-six branches. Harry T. Oscarson is the mission president. Forty Relief Socicty organizations, with 535 members, were reported in December 1956. Ruth 'T. Oscarson presides over the Swedish Mission Relief Society. Note: The cover for this Magazine, "Summer Sunset, Magelungen Lake, Near Stockholm, Sweden," was submitted by the former President of the Swedish Mission Relief Society, Ethel E. Blomquist.

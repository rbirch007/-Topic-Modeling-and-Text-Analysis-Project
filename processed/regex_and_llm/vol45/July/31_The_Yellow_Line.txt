My heart stopped racing, and I was no longer afraid. Then the comforting thought came to me—how very much like life was the highway I was traveling. There was danger at every curve, and care was needed to drive every mile. How like life, too, were the yellow signs. God had given us signs to warn us of dangers in life; to guide us in the tight path. His constant, comforting inspiration and love were like the constant yellow line. Oh, dear Father, thank you for your love and comfort. Thank you for your guidance and your understanding of our needs. Thank you for the yellow line.

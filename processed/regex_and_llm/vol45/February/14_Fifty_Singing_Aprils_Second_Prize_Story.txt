"Another singing April!" she said softly to no one in_ particular. "Another singing April, and I have a letter to write this morning!" Her step was buoyant as_ she walked the winding path of stepping stones from her back garden to her kitchen door, noticing the bordering tulips still in their green nightcaps, and the snow-on-thePage 80 mountain just beginning to send out its green tendrils. A half hour later, she was sitting at her desk when the doorbell rang. She looked up to greet four smiling little boys. "Happy anniversary, Grandma!" they called in unison.

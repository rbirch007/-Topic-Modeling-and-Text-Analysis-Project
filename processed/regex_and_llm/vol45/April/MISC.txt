Beyond the meadow and the seeded plain The cycle of their destiny is known— Above the river and the reedy shore, Over the measured land where grain is sown And we who seek for further latitudes Now mark the symmetry, the circling wing, To find again in that repeated skein The compact and the testament of spring!

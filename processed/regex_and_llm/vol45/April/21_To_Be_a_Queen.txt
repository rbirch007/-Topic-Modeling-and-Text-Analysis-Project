She was a remote little figure sitting here with all of these pretty, vivacious, and confident would-be queens. The garden we picture in our minds has borders full of blooms throughout the season—and, of course, there are never any weeds to mar that picture, nor are there any bugs or plant diseases or soil problems. The desire to have a garden full of color throughout the growing season presents quite a challenge.

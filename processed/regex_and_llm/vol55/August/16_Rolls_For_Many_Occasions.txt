Rolls For Many Occasions BUTTER-HORN ROLLS
Roll dough (made from basic recipe) into a 10-inch circle, ½-inch thick. Brush with melted butter; cut, like pie, into twelve pieces. Roll each piece from wide end. Lay on a well-greased baking sheet, with tip of dough on underneath side. Let rise about 30 minutes in warm place. Bake approximately 20 minutes at 300°-350°.

Shape dough (made from basic recipe) in 6x9 inch rectangular shape, 2-inch thick. Brush with melted butter. Mix 2 c. Sugar and 2 tsp. Cinnamon, and sprinkle over dough. Roll like a jelly roll, flatten ends and turn under, pinching in place. Place in well-greased 5x9-inch pan. Let rise until light, and bake at 350° until brown, as desired.

Sewing Carol Hood Blackhurst w Sewing can be many things. It can be mending, patching, creating; it can be making over, or redesigning. Yes, sewing can be many things. It can be something plain, something fancy, something new, or something remade. But whatever it is, it is something of real accomplishment. What a thrill to see it finished, what joy and happiness! It could be a fancy new dress or just a patch on Tommy's little jeans. It is something of us, a part of body, mind, soul, and spirit. Thank goodness for sewing!

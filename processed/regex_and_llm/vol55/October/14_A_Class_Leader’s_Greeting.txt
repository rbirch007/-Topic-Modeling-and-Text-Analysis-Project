A CLASS LEADER'S GREETING The days have come when, in response To crisp October's weather, Intent on intellectual gain Again we meet together. Back to our classes and our books, Upon whose fragile pages Is found the learning of the past, The wisdom of the ages. Give thanks for books, this boon to man; Give thanks for those who wrote them, And thus preserved their choicest thoughts That we might use and quote them. Give thanks for this rich heritage That we can read, and so, Adding such wisdom to our own, In happier ways may go. 

Vilate R. McAllister 777 Lesson Department

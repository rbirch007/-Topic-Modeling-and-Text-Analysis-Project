Catherine B. Bowles 
The Lord's Counselor Marianne C. Sharp
Address Delivered at the General Session of the Relief Society Annual General Conference, October 2, 1968

We read in Section 68 of the Doctrine and Covenants commands given by the Lord to parents in Zion. Never, I feel, was it more necessary to obey these commandments for the eternal welfare of ourselves and our children than it is at the present time. These are fateful last days in the history of the world.

Section 1, the Preface to the Doctrine and Covenants, says in different verses, the voice of warning shall be unto all people, by the mouths of my disciples, whom I have chosen in these last days.... The hour is not yet, but is nigh at hand, when peace shall be taken from the earth, and the devil shall have power over his own dominion. (D&C 1:4,35.)

Among the commandments given to parents, we read "And they shall also teach their children to pray and to walk uprightly before the Lord." (D&C 68:28.) It is not an easy thing in these last days for mothers to teach children to walk uprightly before the Lord. It is true that there have always been temptations in the world, but temptations today seem to be more available, with evil sounds and evil examples allowed, through modern media, to invade the sacred precincts of the home.

This know, dear heart (be comforted)
In every love that is, this comes:
A time to give it back to God,
Who gave it to you.
Who strains to keep is discomfited,
(His love takes on so many forms)
Love can be staff or can be rod.
Know this, for it is true,
That when one love seems ended,
And your joy turned into pain,
Remember God attended
You formerly, and stands again
Ready, though the world seems empty...

Love lives, eternally!
 
Katherine F. Larsen

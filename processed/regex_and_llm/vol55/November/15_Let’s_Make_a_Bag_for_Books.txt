Let's Make a Bag for Books Margaret Woods, Walsall, England

A We prepare the lesson, accumulate the visual aids, place them all ready—and then cannot think in what to carry them to meeting. But if we make a smart, simple bag that will be weatherproof, too, we shall always be ready. Then, if we make contrasting bags to hold each set of books, we shall be completely organized. The bag is large enough to hold several large magazines, plus three or four average size books. Adaptations can be made in the pattern for special sized books.

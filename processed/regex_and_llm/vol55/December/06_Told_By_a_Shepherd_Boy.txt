Much of the fun of the song has always been trying to remember its long list of "gifts" that "my true love sent to me," ranging all the way from two turtle doves to twelve drummers drumming. Yet, Christmas Seals also mean another gift. The breath of life. Take a deep breath. Easy, isn't it? Most of the time we are totally unaware of the breathing process. But for many, each breath is a painful struggle for life. These are the victims of emphysema, chronic bronchitis, asthma, tuberculosis and other respiratory diseases. They are not few in number.

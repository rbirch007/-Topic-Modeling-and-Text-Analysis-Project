Each of us had our Bethlehem,
A mother's arms, a star-filled night,
An angels' choir singing there...
Beyond remembrance, beyond sight.

When we left our heavenly home,
We knew, as he, the path would be
Beset by the treachery and tears
Which followed him to Calvary.

He could mend a broken soul
As we would kiss away a tear.
We cannot do the miracles
He could do when he was here.

Sell

When Christmas is upon the land,
Faith's message is ringing clear.
The Heavenly Father, who sent his Son,
Is the same who sent us here.

Dorothy O. Rea

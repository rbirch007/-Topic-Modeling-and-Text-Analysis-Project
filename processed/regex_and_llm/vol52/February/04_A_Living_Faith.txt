A Living Faith Mabel Jones Gabbott There is no peace except within the soul Divinely tuned to God and earth and sky, Whose pulses leap to meet each day, to try With nobler effort to achieve each goal. There is no joy except the happiness That comes from deep within. To lose oneself In serving others will ensure a wealth Of pure content, of lasting loveliness. There is no promise of security Without a living faith in God's supremacy, Oh, help us to believe and kneel and pray, And learn acceptance of thy gracious way; Unless we love thee, and our fellow men, There is no peace, and all our search is vain.

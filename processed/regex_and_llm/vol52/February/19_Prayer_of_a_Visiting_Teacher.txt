Prayer of a Visiting Teacher Lucile R. Smith | pray, Lord, as a teacher To be worthy of my call; To strive for understanding And have charity for all. Teach me to have forbearance That | shall not try to judge The weaknesses of others But keep free from fault and grudge. Help me to grow in knowledge Yet be humble and devout; Guide me to teach with wisdom And leave faith where there is doubt. Give me a willing spirit; Make me mindful of the scope Of ways to help where there is need Of comfort, care, and hope. Wilt thou grant my supplication And bless me to the end, That every sister | may visit Will find in me a friend.

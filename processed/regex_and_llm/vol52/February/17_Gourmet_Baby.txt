Gourmet Baby Vesta N. Fairbairn We have no eating problem With our child; By each new taste he's tempted And beguiled. He'll drink his milk from bottle Or from cup. He takes his cereal by spoon And slurps it up. Whatever the food that we add To his diet He's happy to put in his mouth And to try it. Bananas, pears, and peaches, Shredded meats, And carrots, squash, and peas Are things he eats. And to these basic foods He adds his own— Papers, pebbles, perfume, The puppy's bone.

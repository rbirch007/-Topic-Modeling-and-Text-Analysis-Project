CHERRY-RHUBARB PIE Judith Leigh-Kendall Temperature: 425 degrees Time: 35 to 45 minutes Makes one 9-inch pie Make pastry for a two-crust pie. Line pan. Mix together: 1 pt. Frozen sugar-packed rhubarb, thawed 1 no. 2 can tart red pie cherries Y%, to 1 cup sugar 3 tbsp. Quick-cooking tapioca Pour into pastry-lined pie pan. Dot with 1 tbsp. Butter. Cover with top crust and cut slits in it. Bake until nicely browned and juice begins to bubble through slits in crust. Serve warm. A

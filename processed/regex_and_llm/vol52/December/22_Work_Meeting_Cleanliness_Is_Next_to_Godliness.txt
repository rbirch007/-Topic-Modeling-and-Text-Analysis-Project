PLAN YOUR WORK WORK YOUR PLAN A busy mother who maintained a clean, sparkling home and yet had time for her husband and family and who enriched her life with Church activities and some civic responsibilities confided, "Housekeeping by the inch is a cinch, but housekeeping by the ward is hard.''* A regular work plan or schedule will avoid many hours of laborious cleaning. The kitchen range is a case in point.

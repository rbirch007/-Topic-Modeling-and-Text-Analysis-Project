WISH ON THE MOON Bernice Ames Spill on my shoulder Nuggets of light, As you sail by, older And high tonight. Enchant the meadow To move as a sea, Shifting each shadow Of fence and tree, Till the field runs over With flowing gold, And ships cruise the clover, Jewels in their hold.

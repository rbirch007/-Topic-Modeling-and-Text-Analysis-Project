WISH ON THE MOON Bernice Ames Spill on my shoulder Nuggets of light, As you sail by, older And high tonight. Enchant the meadow To move as a sea, Shifting each shadow Of fence and tree, Till the field runs over With flowing gold, And ships cruise the clover, Jewels in their hold. Tomorrow the willow Will be just a tree; But tonight make it hollow, A galleon for me. ROSE PARADE TOURS via San Diego via San Francisco Dec. 26 to Jan.

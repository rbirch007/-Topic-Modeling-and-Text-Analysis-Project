Truth Is Many-Faceted Vilate R. McAllister Truth is many-faceted, A gem of wondrous worth. Though men have prayed and searched for it Unto the ends of earth, Truth is not hid, but on a hill Where anyone can see: Continual flashes scintillate, Enlightening you. And me. No human eye may see the whole, But each must be content With one small portion, with the gleam In his direction sent. One ray of color reaches me, Another reaches you, And so we must dispute awhile Which color is the true. Although new facets may be seen In changing light and shade, And viewpoints may be altered When journeyings are made, Our vision still is limited To facets of this gem. Only God can fully see The perfect diadem.

Rosalie Evelyn Fjeldsted Her name is Rosalie, A little girl with dreams to share, With sky blue mirrored in her eyes, And sunlight tangled in her hair. Her sandal footsteps challenge silence, A flash of color like the flower Named — and now her given name, She lives in time's unfolding hour. Little girl, with all your dreams, Dance along where life may fead, But let us look into your world, The world for you alone decreed. Yet for a moment may we see, The little girl we once could be.

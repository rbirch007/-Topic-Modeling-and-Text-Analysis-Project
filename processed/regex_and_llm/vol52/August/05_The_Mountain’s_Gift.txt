But my clear mind says now that | must go Back to the duties of life, the debts | owe. And then they beg again that | should take A cedar sprig, pine cone for memory's sake. These are heart things. | let the mountains plead. For strength, not tears is what | greatly need, And this the mountain calm, the peace distills— A present from the everlasting hills.

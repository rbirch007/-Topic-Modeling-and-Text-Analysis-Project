Woman's Sphere Ramona W. Cannon RS. GENEVIEVE RAINE CURTIS, Salt Lake City, a great-granddaughter of Brngham Young, and wife of A. R. Curtis, retired businessman, was named Utah Mother of the Year. Mrs. Curtis is the first and only woman to be elected to the Salt Lake City Board of Education. <A director of several business firms, she has also been very active in civic assignments and Church work. Adept in the domestic virtues, she has reared eight sons, two daughters, (one died at seventeen), and a foster daughter for twelve years. Mrs. Curtis has given years of devoted service to Relief Society. All her children hold positions of responsibility. RS. DAN SHERWOOD, sixtyseven-year-old Latter-day Saint widow of Mesa, Arizona, was selected as Arizona Mother of 1957. Mrs. Sherwood is the mother of ten sons and four daughters, all of unusual accomplishments. She also has sixtyseven grandchildren. ERRI BUCHER, Latter-day Saint girl from Salt Lake City, is doing some beautiful ballet work.

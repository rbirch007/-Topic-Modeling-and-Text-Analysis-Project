Woman's Sphere Ramona W. Cannon AUDE ADAMS, one of America's greatest actresses, is presented with charm and authenticity in a new biography Maude Adams: An Intimate Portrait, by Phyllis Robbins. Miss Adams, a granddaughter of Barnabas Lothrop Adams, a member of Brigham Young's first pioneer company, received her start as an actress in Salt Lake City. At a high point in her career (1902), she wrote from Switzerland: "The Alps are inspiring, but not friendly like the mountains that protect the lovely valley of Salt Lake. My childhood was guarded by the kindly Wasatch Range, and the Rockies were friends from my beginning."

Andrew Jenson, Assistant Church Historian, estimated in 1930, that, from 1851 to the end of 1930, converts baptized in Norway numbered 8,555. Of this number he computed, approximately 3,500 had emigrated to Utah. At the close of the year 1956, there were 1,623 members of the Church in Norway, located in fourteen branches. Ray Engebretsen of Seattle, Washington, is serving as the mission president. In December 1956, twelve Relief Society organizations were reported with 340 members. Zina R.

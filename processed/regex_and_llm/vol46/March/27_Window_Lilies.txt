Window Lilies Evelyn Fyeldsted Summoned by the valley lark And compassed by spring shafts of light, A brown bulb, waking in the dark, Sent new leaves to reach the window site. And window lily gifts appeared, Secret parcels numbering four, Half-blown waxen blooms that cleared A mystery of flower lore. Morning came like candle giow And lilies wearing claret dress, Threw back their calyx wraps to show Silent, regal queenliness. SALT 'Hal Rumel ARRANGEMENT BY FLORENCE WILLIAMS

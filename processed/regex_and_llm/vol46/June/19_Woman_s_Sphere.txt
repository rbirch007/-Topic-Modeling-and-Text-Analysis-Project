BOYD, of the University of Wyoming, through a grant in aid from the University of Wyoming Graduate School Council, have made an interesting survey of all available material on the subject of the poor spelling of American high school graduates and college students. They have narrowed down the "spelling demons" that overthrow students most frequently to ninety-eight words. This would be a list for everybody to possess and study.

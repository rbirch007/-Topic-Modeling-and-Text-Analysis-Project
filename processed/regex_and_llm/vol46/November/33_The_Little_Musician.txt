She Little Musician Evelyn Fyjeldsted Music in the wind accompanied her On inspiration's silent way; And flowers in the shadows waking, With rhythm seemed to bend and sway. Her thoughts were like the drifting leaves, Light and winged and free from earthly gain; Melodies from far would call to her To improvise in nature's reign. Her fingertips were swift and sure To prove a mood or talent rare, And we who listened followed her, Above the commonplace to visions there.

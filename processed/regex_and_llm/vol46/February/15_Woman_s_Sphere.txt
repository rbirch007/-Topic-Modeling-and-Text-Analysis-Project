HELEN DUPREY BULLOCK, Washington, D. C., makes a specialty of planning "Dinners with the Presidents" at the famous Mayflower Hotel. The menus and the recipes are secured from the families of the Presidents, or from descendants of those deceased. This year the dinner honors go to James Monroe, and the menu consists of roast duckling, deviled eggs with anchovy on water cress, green pea soup with mint, grilled tomatoes, sweet potatoes, patty pan squash, purple plum jelly, and other delicacies.

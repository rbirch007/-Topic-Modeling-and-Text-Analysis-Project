SOCIAL September 30, 1958 Second row, right to left: President Juanita Cooley; First Counselor Phyllis Anderson; Second Counselor Marilyn Zollinger. Ada J. Taylor, President, Farr West Stake Relief Society, reports that this branch was organized in February 1956 and is participating in Relief Society work in an outstanding manner. A complete organization is maintained despite the branch being composed entirely of construction workers and their families, which creates a problem in keeping the branch functioning when there are so many transient members. "We are so thrilled with this Relief Society, and are pleased with the participation of so many young mothers."

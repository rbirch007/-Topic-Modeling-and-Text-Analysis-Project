NOTES FROM THE FIELD 119 messages and a letter from Elder Delbert L. Stapley of the Council of the Twelve, to the visiting teachers. On the back cover of the booklet were the words: "Add years to your life! Add life to your years through Relief Society activities.' "Over 100 women, grandmothers, mothers, and great-grandmothers enjoyed the day renewing old acquaintances and meeting new friends. 'The inspirational talk entitled 'Pills for Precious People' and the beautiful music rendered by our sisters from Ajo, who came 125 miles to sing for us, made the day complete. 'Delicious punch was served from a large cake of blue ice carved to resemble a tonic bottle and decorated with yellow chrysanthemums. The visiting teachers were honored through their presidents, as each president was presented a very lovely. Clear vase with blue stones inside and net on the outside."

RECIPE FOR COLD WATER SOAP 5 pounds strained cool grease 1 cup ammonia 1 heaping tablespoon borax 3 pits cold water 1 can lye Use a galvanized or enamel pan or kettle (not aluminum). Dissolve the lye in one pint of warm water. When cool, add the grease and stir well. Then add the ammonia, borax, and the remainder of the water. Stir for fifteen minutes and allow the mixture to set. The soap may be cut into bars as soon as it is firm.

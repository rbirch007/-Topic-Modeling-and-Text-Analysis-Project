No, she thought, she would probably never be called upon to leave a beloved bedstead along a muddy trail, but, perhaps, she might need to abandon a few too high-priced ideas for her husband's broad shoulders to carry. Slowly, gently, she replaced the notebook in the little chest. She would read it often, as her mother had promised her that she would.

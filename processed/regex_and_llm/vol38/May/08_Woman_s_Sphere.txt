James Monroe Redd), wellknown and gifted Latter-day Saint author of two Relief Society Magazine serials (""I'omorrow's Cup" and "Where Trails Run Out,'") and many poems, passed away March 17, 1951. She has particularly enriched the literary heritage of her people with her stories and poems of the San Juan country of Southeastern Utah.

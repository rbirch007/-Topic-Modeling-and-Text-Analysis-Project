The printed program and the presentation of the numbers in this festival were outstanding. 'The program featured the Relief Society colors, a gold background, with blue printing. 'Theda Farnsworth, who acted as the reader, also prepared the script. Each of the seven wards of Palo Alto Stake (Burlingame, San Mateo, Redwood City, Palo Alto, San Jose, Willow Glen, and Naglee Park) presented a number and the combined chorus of the stake Singing Mothers rendered ''Song of the Soul" and "Let All My Life Be Music," with Fredona Neilson as the organist, Irma Hannibal at the piano, and Ruby Slade as the chonister.

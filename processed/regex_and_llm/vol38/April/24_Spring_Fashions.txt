Spring Fashions Ruth H. Chadwick Gray veiling streams across the sky From Mother Earth's new hat; Beneath its folds gay colors lie, An impish flower mat. A turquoise band holds all in place, ' And anchors down the swirls Of wispy gauze that frames her face, Scalloped round with curls.

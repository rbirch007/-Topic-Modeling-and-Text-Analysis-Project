TRIBUTE Tribute to Adele Cannon Howells (Continued from page 382) Though comparatively young in years, Adele Cannon Howells was old in hours. She had lost no time. The distance on life's journey ls marked not by leaves torn from the calendar but by the number of good deeds done. Sister Howells lived by faith. She had faith in her co-workers and faith in her leaders. She had an unwavering faith in the gospel of Jesus Christ. She trusted in her Heavenly Father with all her heart.

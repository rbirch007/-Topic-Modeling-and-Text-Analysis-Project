Mary Loveless of Cornell University Medical College, New York, has been working for seventeen years on immunizing patients allergic to the stings of various kinds of bees. She captures bees, then, with a delicate operation, removes the venom sac for the innoculating agent. Having done this more than thirty thousand times, she can now do a bee a minute.

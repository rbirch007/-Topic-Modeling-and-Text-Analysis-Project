Dew on a Peace Rose Judy Paget Tumbaco, Ecuador Rosio, this rose began to burn And would have withered in the sun, Already its outer petals Are wide and low, But you came with enchanted fingers And cast a diamond net Of dreams upon its flesh, Sealing the perfume and the pain Within a silken heart.

Time Evelyn Fjeldsted Time is the one great friend of man Drawing back the portals of each day. Previewing man's eternity, Time directs him on his way. Time heals all the wounds of man, Gently veiling every scar; Teaching by experience, The way of peace — the way of war. Time, the truest friend of man, Though seeming harsh, is always kind. Even memories too keen to bear, Are dimmed by time in every mind.

My soul delighteth in the song of the heart; yea, the song of the righteous is a prayer unto me, and it shall be answered with a blessing upon their heads." Yes, these Singing Mothers, this beautiful Singing Mothers chorus that we have with us today, and the Singing Mothers throughout the Church have received many blessings upon their heads because of the songs of righteousness which they have sung to our Heavenly Father. There was a sister who was the wife of a serviceman who was away with her little family and her husband for the first time. She was invited to join the small Relief Society that was organized on the military base for wives of servicemen. She said that the sweet spirit of sisterhood that existed there, and the concern that those sisters had for each other, and the sweet association, were a great source of comfort and joy to her. She was taken seriously ill and was unable to take care of her little family while her husband was on duty. The Relief Society sisters came to her aid. They prepared meals and kept her home in order until she was able to return and was strong enough to take over for herself. It was here that this sister realized the true meaning of sisterhood and of service. She bore a_ beautiful testimony of 71 MARCH 1964 thanksgiving to her Heavenly Father for her membership in the Relief Society. She said that she was grateful to belong to an organization that offers opportunities for every member to be of service. And she appreciated the compact course of study and the great amount of help she received in rearing her family. But, above all, she was grateful for the spirituality which it brought into her home. Yes, the Relief Society does bless the sisters everywhere, but with these blessings comes a great responsibility. What about all of our neighbors, all of our inactive sisters, all of those who have not yet caught the spirit of Relief Society work? What are we doing about them? I pray that each of us will go away from this conference with a renewed determination to serve the Lord with all our heart, might, mind, and strength. With a prayer in our hearts, that with tolerance, and with humility and love and charity, that we will go out and reach those sisters, that we may touch their hearts, that they will have a strong desire to become members with us in this wonderful organization, that they, too, may share in the glorious blessings which are ours by being affiliated with the Relief Society organization. As we do this, always with a prayer in our hearts, let us keep our motto in mind, that "charity never faileth. Wherefore, cleave unto charity." I am sure the Lord will bless us that our numbers will increase, and that we will receive much joy and happiness because we are able to share the blessings of Relief Society with all of our sisters. I ask for all of this humbly, in Jesus' name. Amen.

Breeze A mother's baking day is always evidence of love toward her hungry family, but this treat is more than just evidence. It even looks like a token of love. The special equipment needed for this heart-shaped dessert is one eightinch square cake tin and one eight-inch round cake tin. Use them in which to bake your favorite cake. When the cake is cool, cut the round layer in half. These halves make the top sides of the heart. The Square layer placed at diamond angle forms the bottom of the heart.

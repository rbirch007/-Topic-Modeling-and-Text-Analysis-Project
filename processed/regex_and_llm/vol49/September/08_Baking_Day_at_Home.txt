Grated orange rind 3 or 4 tbsp. Soft butter Peel and cut oranges and combine with grated rind and sugar. Cook until a heavy syrup forms. Roll out dough and spread with melted butter and with the orange mixture which has been cooled. Roll up like a cinnamon roll and slice. Bake at 400° about 20 minutes. Agnes Just Reid I called the roll in my garden This bright September day. Most everyone was present, Flaunting his colors gay. Golden glow, dahlia, pansy, "Mum," Delphinium, "glad," Morning glory, petunia, The best we had ever had. Alyssum, low and thrifty, Poppies so bright, so gold, African daisies, roses Blooming in spite of the cold. I gave them a hearty greeting, But my throat was strangely dry, I hope they didn't know it — But I also said goodbye.

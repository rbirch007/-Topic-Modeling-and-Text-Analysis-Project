Return Henrietta B. McNeely Wild geese crying in the night Through the darkness and the rain, Winging on their northward flight — On true course — to nest again. What sure beacon is their guide? Who leads, on the way they go, As unerringly they glide To the home they used to know? Would that I could be as sure Of my way through doubt and pain, In thy love to feel secure, Never to be lost again. Lord, if from true course I stray — In some dark hour fail to see — Let thy beacon show the way; Bring me safely home to thee.

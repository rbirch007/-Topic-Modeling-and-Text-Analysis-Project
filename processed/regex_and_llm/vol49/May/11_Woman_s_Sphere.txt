ENORITA IRMA ARANGO, twenty-four, pretty and charming, and also a dynamo of energy, is the director of Panama's Tourist Bureau. Hoping to make tourism an outstanding industry, she advertises her country's scenic and _ historical attractions all over the hemisphere, working for conventions and visits from vacationers.

Wait for Me, Sun Mabel Jones Gabbott The bluest of skies hangs overhead, An inverted bowl with not even a chip: The day awaits: unwritten, unread. This length of hours too soon will slip Into tomorrow. Wait for me, sun, While I put in my pocket a bit of song, And love enough for everyone, With a sparkle of humor to go along Into each hour, for I shall live This day but once. I have much to give. |

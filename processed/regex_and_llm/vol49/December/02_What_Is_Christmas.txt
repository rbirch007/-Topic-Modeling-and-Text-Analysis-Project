What Is Christmas? Mabel Jones Gabbott Is it the star atop the tree; Is it the gift box — mbbon-bright? Is it the song the angels sang To humble shepherds that star-filled night? Is it the Wise Men from the east; Is it their frankincense and myrrh? Is it the gladness in Mary's face; Or in Joseph's protecting her? Is it a child's expectancy; Is it the gay wreath at the door? Is it the love that Jesus taught? Oh, yes. All this and more.

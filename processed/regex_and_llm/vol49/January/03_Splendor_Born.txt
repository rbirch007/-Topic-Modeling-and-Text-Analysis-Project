he New Year Viola Ashton Candland What can the new year mean to me? A golden opportunity, A gift of time from God above To grow in wisdom and in love. What can the new year bring to me? A sense of true humility, A love of God, an urge to pray, To draw near unto him each day. What can the new year bring to me? More strength to meet adversity, A lamp of faith to light the way Of loved ones, lest they go astray. I hope the new year lets me see My great responsibility To live and teach the gospel plan And foster brotherhood in man. I hope it has in store for me More tolerance for humanity, More tact to guide the errant youth, More diligence in seeking truth. I pray the new year lets me know The joy that day by day will grow, If I but strive to serve the Lord And hearken to his holy word. I pray that it will bring to me The peace and sweet serenity That comes from knowing God is near To bless me in the coming year.

Communication Viola Ashton Candland Words are often feeble things When we have need to speak To one whose heart is troubled Or one whose faith is weak. If eloquence is needed, Hope is a message to impart, Let spirit speak to spirit And heart commune with heart. When Christ expressed his love for us, Mere words did not suffice; He spoke by spirit and by deed, And with the voice of sacrifice.

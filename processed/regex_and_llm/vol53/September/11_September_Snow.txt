FROM THE FIELD Roy Stake (Utah) Singing Mothers Present ''The Charms of Music'"' April 29, 1966 Glenda G. Thompson, President, Roy Stake Relief Society, reports: 'In celebration of our fifth birthday, we wished to do something special and really nice. We thought we would like to include all our families and friends to enjoy this special event with us. We presented a musical program "The Charms of Music.' This program was very well received, and was well attended. Ninetytwo sisters in the Singing Mothers chorus were wonderful, and the guest artists were outstanding. We were happy to have with us Sister Cleone Rich Eccles of the General Board of Relief Society. The excellent program was arranged by June Heiser, chorister, with Feola Barton as organist."

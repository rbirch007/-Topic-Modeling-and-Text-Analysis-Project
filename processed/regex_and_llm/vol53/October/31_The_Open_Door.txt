THE OPEN DOOR Verda F. Welch If God should close a door to thee, Another door will opened be. Our path is not illumined clear. Walk thou by faith, said he. I'll gently lead thee by the hand; I'll bless and comfort thee. The sorrow and the tears that flow; The trouble, and the loss Are part of life's refining fire To sift thee from the dross. The fire will but humble thee, And help thee see more clear The heavy load thy brother bears; His heartache, and his fear. In loving service thou shalt find New vistas, rich and rare; A recompense, a joy so sweet, And glory of heaven there.

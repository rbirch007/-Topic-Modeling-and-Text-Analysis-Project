DUMPLING SOUP Combine beef broth and chicken broth, cooked separately, from 2 good-sized pieces of brisket and a shin or knuckle bone, added to broth from one hen or rooster, each flavored with one onion and a bunch of celery leaves, parsley stems, and green onion tops tied into a tight bundle with thread and dropped into the soup the last two hours, and removed with the meat and bones. Broth should be skimmed of nearly all the floating fat. Add, and simmer until tender: 114 qts. Raw, sliced carrots 1 sliced parsnip, raw 1/4 tsp. Ginger. Add the dumplings to the simmering soup.

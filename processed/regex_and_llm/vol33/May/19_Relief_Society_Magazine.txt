What glowing Surge caused a writer's heart to move The shuttle of thought through warp and groove, Unfolding a woven field of printed thread Whose legacy of culture is before us spread; Creative wisdom and intellect imbued, Social service advanced and renewed; Spiritual guidance with faith convene, Lighted by hope and love serene, Wounded not by its own assurance, The typed facsimile claims endurance.

Charles William Penrose To fill the vacancy in the First Presidency caused by the death of John Henry Smith, a man was chosen who was a convert to the Church. He was another man of English birth, Charles W. Penrose, who was then seventy-nine years of age, but alert in body and mind. He served President Smith for seven years as second counselor.

However, during the remainder of the year when the shop 583 must depend largely on local trade for its support, it is regrettable that such trade is not always of adequate volume to provide the funds necessary to meet operating expenses. HIS brief review of the history of the shop and its worthy purposes should bring to us the realization that we, as members of Relief Society, are actually stockholders in this business. We have a personal responsibility toward this "child" of Relief Society, which we can discharge by patronizing the shop and by acquainting our friends and associates with its fine purposes, as well as with the beautiful and useful articles sold here.

Killpack BIG HORN STAKE, RED LODGE (MONTANA) BRANCH RELIEF SOCIETY BAZAAR Left to right: First Counselor Lois Cozzens, President Theda Lynn; Second Counselor Hazel Updegraff; work director Augusta Crow. This branch was organized January 28, 1948 and a year later held its first bazaar. There are thirteen members enrolled and there were on sale 200 articles. The picture shows only part of the beautiful displays which included a large assortment of crocheted and hand-embroidered articles, as well as children's clothing, layette articles, and many decorative items for the home.

Then I had jumped from the pony while she was still running, as my brother had taught me to do, and run headlong toward Tom. I had taken Betsy's rein and trailed slowly after him to the corral. Tom had been very angry. I had taken Betsy's bridle off and turned her into the pasture. Then I had leaned against the corral fence while Tom carefully unharnessed and fed the team.

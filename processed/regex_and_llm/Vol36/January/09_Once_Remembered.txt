ONCE REMEMBERED Wanda Greene Nielson I tried to ease my aching heart, And turned from love away; — I never once remembered you The first monotonous day! I schooled my heart and trained my thoughts So very well! At length I took a stroll down memory's lane, Just to test my strength. Weighted to a burden, Sharpened to a pain, Love with its hurting Is in my heart again. — FROM THE FIELD Margaret C.

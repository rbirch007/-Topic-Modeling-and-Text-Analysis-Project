Notes TO THE FIELD
Annual Stake Conventions Set
RELIEF Society annual stake conventions will begin in August and run through November. The conventions have been planned to occupy one day and will begin with a stake board meeting at 10 A.M., conducted by the home stake Relief Society president. Matters of importance to stake boards will be considered in this meeting and the discussion will be led by representatives from the Relief Society general board.
An officers and class leaders meeting will be held from 11 A.M. to 12:45 P.M. for stake board members, ward presidencies, secretary-treasurers, class leaders, and work meeting leaders. Members of the stake presidency, high council, and bishoprics are also invited to this meeting. Following the opening exercises and remarks from Relief Society officers, two departmental meetings will be held: (1) for presidencies, secretary-treasurers, magazine representatives; (2) for class leaders.
A general session is scheduled for the afternoon, to be conducted by the visiting stake Relief Society president. Stake and ward officers and class leaders are to attend the meeting, and priesthood representatives and visiting teachers are invited.

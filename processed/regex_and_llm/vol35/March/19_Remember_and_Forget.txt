REMEMBER AND FORGET Della Adams Leitner
Have I—have I forgotten? Stay, my heart; Be not so quick to censure careless ways, Daydreams and laughter and neglected tasks, The disregard of time, the drifting days, All so a part of adolescent youth, Trying the patience of the older mind; Oh, thinking back, remember—then forget The intervening years.... You will be kind.

PARTING Adeline R. Ensign
There always comes a parting time When we must say goodbye, When sadness fills the lonely hearts And teardrops dim the eye.
For time is ever passing on, Nor would we change the plan, For when God gave us life he knew That tears were good for man.
And so, as sorrows come along, Oh, give us strength, we pray, And give us faith and courage, Lord, Sufficient for each day.

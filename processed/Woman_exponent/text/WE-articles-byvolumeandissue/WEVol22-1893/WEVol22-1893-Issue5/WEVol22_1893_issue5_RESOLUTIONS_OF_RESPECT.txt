RESOLUTIONS OF. RESPECT.
	To the memory of Sister Elsie Booth, who
departed this life at Alpine, Utah July 10, 1893,
after a short illness, but passed away in a calm
sleep.
	Sister  Booth was born at Bedford Leigh,
Lancashire, England Dec. 21, 1825. She joined
the Relief Society here in 1867 and has been a
faithful worker ever since, she was a teacher and
secretary which offices she faithfully performed
the duties of for many years.
	Whereas our Heavenly Father has seen fit in
his Alwise Providence to call from our midst our
dearly beloved sister,
	Resolved that we the members of the Relief
Society feel that we have lost a true and faithful
worker, always ready and willing to do good.
	Resolved that we cherish her memory and re-
gard her wise counsel ever worthy of emulation.
	Resolved that these resolutions be recorded in
the Record Book ot the Society and a copy sent
to the EXPONENT for publi[---]
	In behalf of the Relief Society
Committee
MARIA ADAMS,
MARY E. BATERMAN,

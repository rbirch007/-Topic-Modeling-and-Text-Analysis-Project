	DOCTRINE AND COVENANTS.

THIS book, I deemed, a fitting gift,
	From friend to friend on Christmas morn,
Since, through the light these pages hold
	Christ to men’s souls is newly born,
And when your eyes these pages scan,
	Though far or near, by land or sea,
’Midst worthier thoughts,I trust you’ll find,
	Time for a kindly thought of me.
	E.J.

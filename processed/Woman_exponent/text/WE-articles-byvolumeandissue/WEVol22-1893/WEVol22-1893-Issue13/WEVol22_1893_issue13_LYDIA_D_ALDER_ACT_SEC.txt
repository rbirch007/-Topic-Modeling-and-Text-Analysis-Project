MME. ELISE HWASSER, who died re-
cently, was for forty years the greatest
Swedish actress. Mme Hwasser delineated
the heroines in Ibsen’s dramas, and also
played the leading Shakesperian roles.
She retired to private life in 1888.
	MISS MARTHA E. PARKER is secretary of
the New England Tariff Reform League.
Miss Parker became a clerk in the office of
the league four years ago, when the Hon.
Charles S. Hamlin, now an assistant
secretary of the treasury, was secretary.
Temporarily she undertook the duties of
the office when Mr. Hamlin resigned, and
the league, recognizing her efficiency and
familiarity with the work, have since made
her permanent acting secretary. She has
charge of the League headquarters Bos-
ton, where a number of clerks are kept
busy, and a large correspondence is carried
on.

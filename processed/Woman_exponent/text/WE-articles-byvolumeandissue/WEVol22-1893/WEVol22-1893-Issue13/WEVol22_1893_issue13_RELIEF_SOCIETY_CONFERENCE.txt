RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	THE Conference of the Relief Society of
this Stake of Zion will be held in the 14th,
Ward Assembly Rooms in this City on
Friday March 23, meetings commencing as
usual at 10 a.m. and 2 p.m. Each Society
in the Stake should be represented by ts
President or some other officers that all may
be benefitted. Bishops and brethren in-
terested are cordially invited to be present.
		M. ISABELLA HORNE, President.

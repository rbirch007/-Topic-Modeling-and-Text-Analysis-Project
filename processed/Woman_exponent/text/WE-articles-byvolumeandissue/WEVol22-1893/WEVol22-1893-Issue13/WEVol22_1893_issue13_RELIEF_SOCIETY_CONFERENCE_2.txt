RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	THE general conference of the Relief 
Society will be held in the Salt Lake As-
sembly Hall, in this city, on Thursday,
April 5th, commencing at 9 a. m., 2
p. m.—two sessions. It is very
desirable that the president of each Stake
organization, or one of the board should at-
tend this conference, as there will be mat-
ters of importance considered. 
	ZINA D. H. YOUNG,
	President.
	JANE S. RICHARDS,
	BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
	Counselors.

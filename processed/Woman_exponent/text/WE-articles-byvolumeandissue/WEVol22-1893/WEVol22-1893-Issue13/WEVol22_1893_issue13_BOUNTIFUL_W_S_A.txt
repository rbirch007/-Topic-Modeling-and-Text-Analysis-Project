	BOUNTIFUL W. S. A.

	FEBRUARY 12th, at 2 o'clock was the 
annual meeting of the Woman Suffrage
Association of South Bountiful. After
some able speeches from various ones, the
financial report was read and on motion
was accepted.
	Mrs. Lucy A. Clark President of Davis
Co. took the chair, while the organization
was completed with Mrs. Mary A. Page,
President, and Betsy J. Howard Secretary.

OBITUARIES.

	CENTER WARD—Has had a shadow of gloom
cast over it by the sudden demise of one of our
number, Maria Ann Simmons who departed this
life, Oct. 24, 1893 of typhoid fever, and leaves a
husband and six small children to mourn her loss,
she was a Relief Society Teacher, and always
ready to do her duties in all her callings. She was
born in Coney Heath Hatfield Hearts England,
Sep, 25th, 1855; and was baptized July 24 1865 by
by William D. Owen at Chamberwell, London.
Emigrated to Utah in the fall of 1881. May she
rest in peace.
			MARY A. WHITTAKER, Sec.

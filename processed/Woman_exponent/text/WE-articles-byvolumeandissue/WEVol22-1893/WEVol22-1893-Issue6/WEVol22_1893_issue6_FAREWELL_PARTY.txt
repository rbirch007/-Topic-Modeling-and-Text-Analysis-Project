FAREWELL PARTY.

	“WE the members of Center Ward Relief
Society, meet to pay our respects to Sister


Mary E. Cluff our President as she is going
to Arizona to live, we unitedly come to
thank her for her kindness in letting us
meet in her comfortable house and to thank
our Heavenly Father for the good spirit we
enjoyed when together, which will be long
remembered by us all, as a token of love
we all present you with a Book of Mormon
from willing minds, that within this kind
circle your heart may rejoice for blessings
are for you from every voice.”
	A pleasing repast was spread and with
suitable conversation partaken of by the
hostess and her friends.
	We subscribe our names as follows,

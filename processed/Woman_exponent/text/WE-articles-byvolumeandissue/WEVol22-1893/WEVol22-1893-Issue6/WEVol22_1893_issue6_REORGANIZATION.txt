REORGANIZATION.
REORGANIZATION of the Relief Society
of Granite Ward Oct. 18th, 1893. Meet-
ing called to order by acting President
Catharine M. Staker, Singing “Let those
who would be Saints indeed.” Prayer by
Sister Staker, singing “Now let us rejoice.”
Roll called—minutes of the previous meet-
ing read and accepted.
	The Bishop being present took charge of
the business of the meeting.
	Sister Catharine M. Staker was chosen to
act as President of the society. Sister An-
nie E. Hanson and Sister Susan Despain
were chosen Counselors. These sisters
were unanimously sustained to fill the
above named offices and were set apart to
act therein; good counsel and encouraging
remarks were received from the Bishopric
and other brethren. 
	Our society is not what we might call
first class as yet, owing to a great many dis-
advantages, but we hope to make it such as
soon as possible. Meeting was adjourned
for three weeks, singing “We thank thee
O, God for a prophet.”  Benediction by
Bishop Alva Butler.
			ANNIE B. DESPAIN, Sec.
Wasatch Oct. 1893.

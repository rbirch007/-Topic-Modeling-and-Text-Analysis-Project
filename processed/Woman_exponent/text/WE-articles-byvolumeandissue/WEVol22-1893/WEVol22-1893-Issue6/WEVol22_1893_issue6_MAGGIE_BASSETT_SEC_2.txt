			MAGGIE BASSETT, Sec.


IN South Australia, full woman suf-
frage is under consideration. On Aug.
15, the Minister of Education moved the
second reading of an adult suffrage bill.
On motion of Mr. Caldwell the debate was
adjourned to Aug. 17. We shall look
with interest for further advices. At the
last accounts the debate was in progress.

UTAH COUNTY W. S. A.
 
	THE annual convention of the Utah
county branch of the Territorial Woman’s
Suffrage association will be held in the
Provo meeting house on Thursday, Nov-
vember 23, 1893.  The executive session
will convene at 10 o’clock a. m.  The con-
vention proper will commence at 2 p. m.
The Territorial president Mrs. E. B. Wells,
will be here, also delegates from the various
organizations of the county.  All are
cordially invited.

	Mrs. ELECTA BULLOCK, President.
	REBECCA H. DOOLAN, Secretary

BLACK FRUIT CAKE—Cream together one
pound of brown sugar and one pound of
butter. Beat the yolks and whites of ten
eggs separately. Add the yolks with one
pound of seeded raisins, one pound of cur-
rants, and half a pound of sliced citron,
one-third of an ounce each of ground cin-
namon and nutmeg, and one-quarter ounce
each of ground mace and cloves, also one
pound of flour that has been slightly
browned. Add the whites of the eggs.
Mix and beat well. Turn into a mould and
bake for five hours in a moderate oven.
Before using, ice and decorate with candied
fruit.

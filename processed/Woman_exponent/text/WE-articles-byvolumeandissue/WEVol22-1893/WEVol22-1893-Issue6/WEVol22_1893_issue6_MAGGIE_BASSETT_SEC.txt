				MAGGIE BASSETT, Sec.


THE regular monthly of the W. S. A.
of Salt Lake Co. was held in the 13th,
Ward Assembly Rooms, Oct. 17th, 1893.
President Nellie Taylor in the chair: opened
by singing “Our Country,” prayer by Vice
President E. McFarlane. Sang “Oh, come
come away” &c. Roll called, minutes of
the previous meeting read and approved.
	A few excellent clippings were read by
the secretary. A duet was rendered by
Mr. T. Ashworth and Mrs. Maggie Bassett.
	Mrs. May Talmage read an appropriate 
selection. Mrs. C. C. R. Wells and Mrs.
Julia Druce each spoke encouragingly.
	President Nellie Taylor recited “The
Eagle Rock or A Mother’s Love.” Re-
marks were made by Vice President E. M.
Price, Mrs. M. I. Horne, Vice President
E. McFarlane and President Nellie Taylor.
Meeting was adjourned until the third
Tuesday in November.

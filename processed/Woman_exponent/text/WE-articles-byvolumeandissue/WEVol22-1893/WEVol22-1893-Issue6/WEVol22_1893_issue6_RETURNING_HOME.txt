RETURNING HOME.

Editor Woman's Exponent.
	I WISH to say through the columns of
the EXPONENT that my health is so far im-
proved that I expect to start home in a few
days to resume my labors in the St. Joseph
Stake of Arizona where I shall use my
influence in the interest of the EXPONENT
Deseret News and Young Ladies Journal
which I think should be the duty of every
officer in the Church organizations of women;
in our various reports from our stake we
should not omit to say that President
Layton has rendered that aid that was
necessary to enable us to make our visit by
giving us a pass on the stage line of which
he is proprietor and also attending the Con-
ference of the Associations and giving that
encouragement that tends to the growth
and development of the Stake over which
he is called to preside.

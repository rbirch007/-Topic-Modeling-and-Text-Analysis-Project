NOTES AND NEWS.
	MRS. LAURA ORMISON CHANT went
on to California from the Religious Con-
gresses in Chicago. She is filling several
engagements there.
	MRS. LUCY STONE is seriously ill. In
answer to many inquiries, we state that she
is much reduced in flesh and in strength,
but suffers no pain, and generally passes
comfortable nights. Any important change
will be reported in the Journal, A. S. B.
Woman's Journal Sep. 30th.

EDITORIAL NOTES.
THE good news from Colorada that the
vote of the state for woman’s suffrage was
6,000 majority comes like good tidings to
the country, and presages the good time
when political equality will no longer be a
by word and a jest, but an acknowledged
fact the civilized world over.

many readers to the display advertisment of
the Dunford Shoe Co. which appears in
this issue of the EXPONENT.  They have
just moved from their old location on first
South St. to the elegant store of Spencer
Clawson’s No. 57 S. Main St., half a block
north of Deseret National Bank.  Every-
thing in the line of footwear is carried by
this popular house and the prices are such 1
as to be in the reach of all  "Style, fit and
durability” at hard time prices is the motto

OBITUARY.
	THE Relief Society of Price has been called
upon to part, within the last four years, with four
of our beloved sisters who were faithful to all re-
quirements made of them  in living their
religion, they passed through the trials and
tribulations the saints have been called to pass
through from time to time, departed this life and
with the assurance of a glorious resurrection.
The last one was our beloved Sister Mary Bryner
who died Sep. 1st, 1893 of dropsy at her
daughter’s home, Sister Pauli e Pace of this
place. She left her home in Zurich Switzerland
for the Gospel’s sake, in company with her blind
husband and one child in the year 1856 and as a


mother in Israel has passed through many trying
ordeals; she has left to mourn her loss two sons
and three daughters, her second son is absent on
a mission at the present time to his native country
Switzerland; she also leaves twenty-one living
grandchildren and numerous friends as she was
beloved by all; she was cheerful and calm to the
last and died as she had lived a faithful Latter-
day Saint.
	SARAH G. COX, President.
Price, Sep. 21, 1893.

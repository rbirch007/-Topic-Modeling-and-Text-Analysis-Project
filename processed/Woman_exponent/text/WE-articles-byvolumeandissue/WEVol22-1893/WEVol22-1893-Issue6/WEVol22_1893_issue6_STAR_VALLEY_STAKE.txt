STAR VALLEY STAKE.
	The first Relief Society conference of'
the Star Valley Stake convened at Afton
Wyoming Monday, June 12, 1893, at 10
a. m. President Kittie E. Dixon presid-
ing. Meeting commenced by singing,
prayer by Prest.  A. V. Call, singing by
the choir. Opening remarks appropriate
to the occasion by Prest. Kittie Dixon. The
branches of the society were then represen-
ted as follows:
	Afton, Prest. Harriet Cazier, Grover,
Hannah Hepworth, Auburn, Catharine 
Holbrook, Glencoe, Elizabeth Turner.
All were represented as in good order.
The statistical and financial reports were
then read. Counselor S. A. Burton made
a few remarks in which she gave good in-
structions. Meeting adjourned by singing,
benediction by Secretary Lucy Call.
	Afternoon Session. Meeting opened by
singing, prayer by Sister Cazier, Singing
by the choir, Prest. Childs of Fairview, and
Sec. E. Walton of Cotton-Wood then re-
ported their wards as in good condition.
Some of the Sisters of the various wards
then made some very instructive remarks.
One of the Stake Presidency gave some
good counsel and encouraged the Sisters to
press on in the good cause in which they 
were engaged. Prest. Kittie Dixon made
a few closing remarks. Conference then
adjourned for six months. Singing. Bene-
diction by Bishop C. D. Cazier.
Kittie E. DIXON President.
Sarah A. Burton, 
Jane Parson, Counselors.
Lucy Call, Secretary.

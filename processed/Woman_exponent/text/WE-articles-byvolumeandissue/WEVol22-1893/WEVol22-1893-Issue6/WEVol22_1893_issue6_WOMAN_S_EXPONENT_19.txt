WOMAN'S EXPONENT


The Annual Convention of the W. S.
A. of Salt Lake Co. for the election of of-
ficers was held in the 13th, Ward Assembly
Rooms, Tuesday afternoon Nov. 21st, 1893.
President Nellie Taylor in the chair, opened
by singing, "Oh, ye fair ones,’’&c. Prayer
by Vice President E. McFarlane, continued
by singing "Do what is right.” Roll
called, the minutes of the previous meeting
were not read. The treasurer’s report for
the year was read and accepted. The
election of officers for the ensuing year was
then carried out, by ballot, Mrs. E.
McFarlane was chosed by unanimous vote for
President, Mrs. A. W. Eardley as 1st, Vice
President, Mrs. E. M. Price as 2nd, Vice
President, Mrs. Julia Druce as 3rd, Vice
President, Mrs. Mary E. Irvine as Sec’y
and Mrs. E. McCune as Assistant Secretary
and Mrs. E. W. Hyde as Treasurer. Mrs.
Nellie Little as chairman of the Executive
Board and Mrs. Mary P. Silver, Mrs. A. W.
Grant, Mrs. E. Lyon, Mrs. M. Holt as mem- 
bers of the board. A vote of Thanks was
tendered to the retiring officers, Mrs. E.
Mc Farlane took the chair. Territorial
President E. B. Wells made some very en-
couraging remarks, touched upon the
questions that were important to suffragists.
Mrs. Nellie Taylor, Dr. E. S. Barney, Mrs.
C. C. R. Wells each made a few interesting 
remarks.
    The membership fee was lowered by
 unanimous vote to 25 cts. a year, with privi-
lege to call for 10 cts. donation. Meeting
stood adjourned ’til the third Tuesday in
December.

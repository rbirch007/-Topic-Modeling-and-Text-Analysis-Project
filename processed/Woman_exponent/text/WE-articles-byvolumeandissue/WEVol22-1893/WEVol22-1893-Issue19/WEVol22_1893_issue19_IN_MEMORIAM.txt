IN MEMORIAM.

	CHLOE WOOLF, the beloved daughter of Isaac
and Ellen Woolf departed this life, June 6th, 1894,
with heart disease and dropsy, aged sixteen years,
and four days.
	Dearest Chloe thou hast left us
	And our hearts are sorely grieved,
	But there is great consolation,
	From suffering now thou art relieved,
	And we know our Father loveth,
	One so pure, so kind and true,
	And we feel if we are faithful,
	We may sometime, come to you.
COM.

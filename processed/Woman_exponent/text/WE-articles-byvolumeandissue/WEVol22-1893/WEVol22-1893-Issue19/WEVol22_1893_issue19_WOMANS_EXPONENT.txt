WOMANS EXPONENT


THE Chicago Woman’s Club has added a
woman suffrage section to its other depart-
ments.
	REV. DR. GUMBART, of Roxbury, Mass.,
preached a strong sermon in favor of
woman suffrage last Sunday.
	THE Prohibition State Conventions of
California and Illinois, and the Populist
State Conventions of California, Illinois and
Indiana endorse woman suffrage.
	AT Tuskegee, Ala., Normal and Indus-
trial Institute, every girl who graduates
this year has cut and made the dress which
she wears on Commencement day.
	“WHY Women Should Vote” is the title
of an admirable pamphlet by Rev. Dr.
Rainsford, of New York. It may be
ordered from Rev. J. R. Atkinson, 207 E.
16th Street, New York City. Price, ten
cents.

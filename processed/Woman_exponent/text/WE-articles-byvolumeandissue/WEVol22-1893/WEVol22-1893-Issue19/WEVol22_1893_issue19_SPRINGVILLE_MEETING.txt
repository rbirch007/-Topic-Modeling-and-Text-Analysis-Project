SPRINGVILLE MEETING.
	ON June 14th the  W. S. A. presided 
over by Sarah A. Boyer, held a meeting at
Springville at which Mrs. E. B. Wells,
Zina D. H. Young, L. John Nuttall, 
Brother Kelsey and others addressed the
audience on the question of woman and
her responsibilities.
	An interesting feature of the programme
was a recitation by Miss Nellie Boyer, "Our
Jamie,” which brought tears to many.
	At 2 p. m. the Relief Society presided over
by Mrs. E. Packard was addressed by the
above named ladies and Brother Nuttall,
after which a number took the 4:04 train to
Castila, returning at 3 next morning.
	The elegant supper, the baths, etc., were
highly appreciated by the company.

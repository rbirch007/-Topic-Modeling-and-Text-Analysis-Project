	A sumptuous supper was laid out in the
dining room which all partook of freely.
After supper there was music, songs and
dancing, the evening closed with prayer by
Brother David Sanders, and all retired to
their homes feeling they had been well
paid. M. P. K.
Erastus Arizona, May 1894.

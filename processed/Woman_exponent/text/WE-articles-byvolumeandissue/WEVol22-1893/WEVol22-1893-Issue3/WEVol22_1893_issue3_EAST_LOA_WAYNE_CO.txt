EAST LOA WAYNE CO.

Editor Woman's Exponent:
	DEAR SISTER—Thinking a few lines
from this little quiet and out of the way
place, would be of interest to you, we pen
you these few items.
	Our Relief Society was organized Nov.
the 3rd, 1892 with Sister Helen A. Max-
field as president; owing to the scattered
condition of the people also being working
people we have not made as rapid progress
as we would wish, our time having been
taken up with the sick a great deal, what
few have their names enrolled are very
energetic and trying to do the best they can.
We sincerely wish to have your valuable 
paper the EXPONENT in our Society and a
few of the sisters have donated together of
their little mites and as we cannot send the
silver, we hope you will accept a few
stamps, and send us your paper in the
R. S. You may send it to the president,
hoping this will be accepted, we remain
your Sisters and co-laborers in the Gospel.
Officers are as follows,
		Helen Maxfield, President.
			MARIA JAKEMAN,
Counselors.	SUSAN TURNER,

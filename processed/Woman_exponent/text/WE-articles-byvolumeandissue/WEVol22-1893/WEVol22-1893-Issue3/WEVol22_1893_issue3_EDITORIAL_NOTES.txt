EDITORIAL NOTES.

	OUR readers will please excuse the non-
continuance of the serial “In Rural
England” as this paper only contains
articles complete in one number and special-
ly designed for free distribution at the
World’s Fair.

	THE ladies of the American Fork W. S.
A., Mrs.Hannah S. Lapish, President, very
generously appropriated money to publish
a thousand extra copies of the EXPONENT,
September first, to be given away on Utah
Day at the World’s Fair in Chicago. They
have our most sincere thanks for this
liberal donation. The same Association
have in the past been very active in raising
money for many noble purposes. May they
continue to increase in good works and also
in numbers. Such energy deserves com- 
mendation.

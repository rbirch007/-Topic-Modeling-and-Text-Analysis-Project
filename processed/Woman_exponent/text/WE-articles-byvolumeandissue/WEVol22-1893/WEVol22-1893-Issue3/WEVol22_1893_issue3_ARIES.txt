OBITUARIES.

	SISTER ELIZABETH POWELL the beloved wife
of Brother Thomas Powell, departed this life at
Marriott Weber County, April 27th, 1893. She
was a native of Carmarthen South Wales; born
September 18th, 1822, was baptized in 1854, emi-
grated to Utah 1869. She soon after joined our
Relief Society; was a very honest upright, kind
and charitable woman, a faithfnl wife and kind
mother, a good neighbor and friend. When in
her native land she made a comfortable home for
the Mormon Elders, who preached the Gospel in
that land.
	After much suffering she died in full faith in
the Gospel of Jesus Christ and in hopes of a
glorious resurrection.
	We extend our sympathy to the bereaved
family.
				ANN BICKINGTON, President.

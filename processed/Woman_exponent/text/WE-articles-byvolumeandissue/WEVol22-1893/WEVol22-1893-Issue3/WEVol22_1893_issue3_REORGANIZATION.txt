REORGANIZATION.

Editor Woman's Exponent:
	As our Relief Society in the 9th, Ward
of this City has been reorganized, I
thought it would be well to apprise you of
the same.
	Owing to the death of our beloved Presi-
dent, Sister Sarah E. Groo, which occurred
June 22, 1892. since which time Sister
E.S. Webb has acted in that position until
June 6, 1893, when Bishop S. A. Woolley
and counselors were present at our general
meeting in order to organize us as a
society.
	Sister Mary Mair was set apart as presi-
dent with Sister Elizabeth A. Woolley as
first, and Sister Martha Barrow as second
counselor.
	A vote of thanks was tendered to Sister
Webb for the able manner in which she
had presided since the death of Sister Groo,
which was carried unanimously.
	MARTHA BARROW, Acting Sec.
9th Ward Relief Society.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	[To the memory of Mrs. Elizabeth Baldwin
Harrison born Oct. 15th, 1815, and died May 20th,
1893.]
	Sister Harrison has been a faithful Latterday
Saint over forty years and a member of the
Relief Society since its organization in 1868 and a
teacher many years we feel the loss of an active
teacher, in her demise.
	Whereas: Our Heavenly Father in His divine
dispensation has seen fit to remove from our
midst by death, our beloved sister who has borne
many afflictions, trials and lonely hours, having
left her native land for the Gospel sake, leaving
all that was dear to her, an only daughter whom
she was not allowed to bring, and in her seven-
tieth year returned to seek her and in a very
providential way found her, but she would not
return with her.
	Resolved, that we the members of the Relief
Society of the First Ward Provo City do cherish
her memory and feel to emulate her example in
the performance of her duties as a teacher.
	Resolved that we sympathize with the family
in the loss of a kind and affectionate wife and
mother.
	Resolved that a copy of these resolutions be
placed on the records of the Relief Society and
sent to the local papers for publication.
	MARY JOHN, MARY FARRER, AGNES FARRER,

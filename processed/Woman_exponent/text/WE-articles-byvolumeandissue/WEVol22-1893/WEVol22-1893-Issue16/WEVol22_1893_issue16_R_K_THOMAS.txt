R.K. THOMAS

	MRS. LIZZIE H. SHIPP, professor and
practical teacher of obstetrics, will open a
school of obstetrics and nursing in Salt
Lake City the first Monday in May. A
half yearly course will be taught for the
unprecedented low terms of ten dollars.
All persons desiring to avail themselves of
this opportunity will please consult me by
letter or in person at No. 34 S. 7th, East
Street, Salt Lake City.

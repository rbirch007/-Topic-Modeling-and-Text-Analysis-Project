UTAH W.S.A.
	THE Semi Annual Convention of the
Territorial W. S. A. was held April 4th,
1894 in the 14th Ward Assembly Hall, the
president Mrs. E. B. Wells in the chair.
Singing by the Suffrage Choir, prayer by
Mrs. Zina D. H. Young.
	Mrs. E. J. McFarlane, Mrs. Lucy A. 
Clark, Mrs. Hilliard, Mrs. M. A. Grover
and Mrs. Louissa Jones delegates from Salt
Lake, Davis, Weber, Juab and Beaver
Counties represented their respective as-
sociations.
	Mrs. E. S. Richards and Mrs. C. E.
Dye gave short addresses in favor of suf-
frage, Dr. Ellen B. Eerguson in an able
address showed the advantages to be
gained by making suffrage universal. 

	Mrs. E. B. Wells made brief remarks
pertaining to suffrage of an explanatory
nature and the meeting adjourned. 
	An executive session was held on the 
morning of the same day in the EXPONENT
Office. Mrs. Phebe V. Beatie in the chair. 
Business pertaining to the various organiza-
tions was discussed and transacted.

No, no, happy Springtime, linger,

HYACINTH.
May 1, 1893.


THE soul that does good to others grows
in goodness. He that is a medium of
blessing to others is himself blessed thereby.
Hence, selfishness is a folly, as well as sin;
for while it prevents our doing good to
others, in the same degree it prevents our
doing good to ourselves.—Jean Paul.

	Counselor E. Stevenson stated that she
had been at Grantsville, so that she had not
attended the last meeting, “the brethren and
sisters there were so anxious to attend the
meetings, coming sometimes twelve miles
in a wagon, yet, look at the empty benches
here today. It is true the nearer the church
the farther from God?”
	Sister Delamare spoke on the fulfillment
of prophecy, as seen in gathering hosts at
Washington, and bore excellent testimony.
Sisters Baubrick, Morris and Frances Smith
each bore instructive testimonies, and
Louie Stevenson and Sister Bregher.

WEBER CO. W. S. A. 
Editor Exponent:
	THE Woman's Suffrage Association of 
Weber County met in Ogden City on 
Tuesday, February 27th, 1894, for the pur-
pose of effecting a reorganization. Mrs.
Emily Richards of Salt Lake City was 
present and acted as chairman until the 
election of officers. The following officers
were elected - Mrs. Janie Nelsen, President; 
Mrs. Hilliard, First Vice President; Miss
Rose Canfield Second Vice President; 

[Column 2]
Mrs. Hendershort, Third Vice President;
Miss Aggie Herrick, Corresponding Sec-
retary; Miss Imogen Wilson Recording
Secretary; Miss Hattie Brown, Treasurer.
Members of Executive Board—Aggie
Herrick Chairman, Minnie Farr, Janie
West, Lizzie Anderson, Maggie Chambers,
Mercy Burton, Annie D. Taylor: Sarah 
West and Aggie Herrick as Auditors.
	After the organization was completed
Mrs. Emily Richards spoke very interest-
ingly on matters pertaining to Woman's 
Suffrage. Meeting adjourned for one 
month.
	AGGIE HERRICK, Cor. Sec.
Ogden Utah, April 10th, 1894.

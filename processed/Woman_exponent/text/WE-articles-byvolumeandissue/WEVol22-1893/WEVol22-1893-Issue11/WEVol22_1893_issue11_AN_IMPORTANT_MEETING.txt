AN  IMPORTANT  MEETING.

	AN assembly of women under the aus-
pices of the Woman Suffrage association is
to take place at the Fourteenth ward hall on
Saturday, February 3, 1894, at 3 o’clock
p. m.
	There will be brief speeches from some
of the most eminent ladies and gentlemen
of the Territory, intermingled with songs,
recitation, etc. Mrs. Zina D. H. Young,
Hon. J. E. Booth, Mrs. Emily S. Richards
and others will take part. All are invited to
attend.
		MARY WELLS,
		Chairman Pub. Com.

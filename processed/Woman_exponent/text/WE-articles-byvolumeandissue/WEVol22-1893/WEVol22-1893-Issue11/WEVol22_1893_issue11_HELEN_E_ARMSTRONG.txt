	HELEN E. ARMSTRONG.


“Do our sons in the law schools rise from
their studies of the invidious statutes and
opinions of jurists in regard to women with
a higher respect for their mothers? By no
means. Every line of the old common law
of England on which the American system
of jurisprudence is based, touching the in-
terests of women, is, in a measure, respon-
sible for the wrongs she suffers today.”
	THE Roman Catholic college of St.
Francis Xavier has just opened its doors to
women.

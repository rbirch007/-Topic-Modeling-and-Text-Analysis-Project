	S. A. PETERSON.


AGNES SMITH ARMSTRONG was born in Car-
lisle, England in the year 1818. Joined the Church
in 1836, was married in 1843 to William Arm-
strong. In 1848 she with her husband and two
children emigrated to America, locating in St.
Louis, Mo., at this place she buried her husband
and four children. In 1855 she came to Utah
locating in Salt Lake City until 1858, she came
to Ephraim, Sanpete Co, where she resided until
the time of her death, which occured Dec. 9th
1893. She leaves three children and eleven
grandchildren.
	Mother worked hard to support herself and
children teaching school for over twenty years.
When the Relief Society was organized in 1869,
she was chosen secretary, which place she
occupied until the time of her death; for the
Society she worked with an energy and faithful-
ness which is a credit to her memory. Mother
had many friends, but those who knew her best
loved her most.	Sincerely.

118					WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


MARIETTA HOLLEY maintains her pop-
ularity. Her last book, “Samantha at the
World’s Fair,” sold 40,000 copies the first
three weeks after its publication. 
	ANOTHER victory was won in the Massa-
chusetts House of Representatives last
Thursday. The municipal woman suffrage
bill was passed to be engrossed by the
decisive vote of 90 yeas to 79 nays and 28
pairs, making in all 118 to 107. An ad-
ditional section was added to the bill,
providing for a vote of the men at the
November election on the question: “Shall
municipal suffrage be granted to women?”
But the women will have the law, and the
expression will have no legal validity.

	SANPETE STAKE.

	The Relief Society Conference of the
Sanpete Stake of Zion convened in the
Spring City Meeting House, Dec. 16th,
L893. President M. A. P. Hyde presiding.
Conference opened by singing, “Prayer is
the soul’s sincere desire,” prayer by Brother
John Frantzen. Singing “For the strength
of the hills we bless thee.” Minutes of
the last conference read. President M. A.
P. Hyde prayed that we would have
a time of rejoicing during conference and
that the Spirit of the Lord would be poured
out upon all present. The branches of the
Relief Society of Fayette, Gunnison,
Sterling, Moroni, Chester, Mt. Pleasant,
Wales, Spring City and Ephraim were
represented, the sisters testifying of the
good that is being done among the poor.
Counselor Sarah Peterson was pleased
with the humble spirit manifested. Spoke
at some length on the storing of grain and
the culture of silk.
	President Canute Peterson advised the
sisters to gather wheat for it will be needed.
said “We have only a few years left until
the prophecies that have been uttered will
be fulfilled. I do not think thirty years
will pass away until all that has been
spoken of will come to pass and that the
earth will be purified from all evil, and that


Christ will reign here and be our King.
May God our Eternal Father bless us in
our associations, for we cannot do without
them. Whenever you pray and you want
it to have power, effect and virtue, ask it in
the name of Jesus Christ.”
	Conference adjourned for three months,
singing, “Guide us, O thou great Jehovah”
benediction by Bro. John T. Allred.
			MARY A. F. HANSEN Stake Sec.
Spring City Jan. 15th, 1894.

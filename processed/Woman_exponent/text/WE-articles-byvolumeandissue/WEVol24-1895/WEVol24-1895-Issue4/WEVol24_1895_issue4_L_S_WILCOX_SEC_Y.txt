	L. S. WILCOX Sec’y.


THE growing interest of women in pub-
lic questions was strikingly shown last
week in the National Republican League
Convention, at Cleveland, O. Populist
women take fully as active a part in the
conventions of their party, and Democratic
women are coming more and more to do
the same. It is a good thing for women of
all shades of political faith to co-operate
with the men holding similar views.---Ex.

REV. JEANETTE OLMSTEAD, of Gusta-
vus, O., and Rev. Arthu Ferris, of Scran-
ton, Pa., were married on June 12th, at the
residence of Drs. V. L. and E. T. Gilchrist,
Ashtabnla, O. Both bride and groom are pas-
tors of Congregational churches. They were
married by Rev. S. B. Hershey, pastor of
the local Congregational church. Miss Olm-
stead has preached acceptably for three years
at Gustavus, and has been active in suffrage
and temperance work.—Ex.

HUNTINGTON EMERY CO. 
Dear Editor:
	ON Thanksgiving Day, Nov. 28th, the
members of the Suffrage Association
gathered at the Huntington Hall, at 2
o’clock p. m. and spread a sumptuous table
with every good thing that the heart could
desire, turkey and pumpkin-pie included,
and about seventy-five people sat down to
the tables and enjoyed a good dinner; after
which we had some very excellent speeches,
toasts and sentiments from both gentlemen
and ladies. In the evening we had a dance
mingled with songs, recitations and a
dialogue on Wowan Suffrage, in which both
sides were shown and in which the ladies
came out victorious. The association is in
a fair condition, there being enrolled twenty
members; we hold meetings twice a month.
Wishing the Suffrage Association success,
we remain yours truly,
MARIA S. CHRISTENSON, President
C. R. RILEY, Vice-President.
BESSIE JOHNSON, Secretary.

IN MEMORIAM.
PERNECY F. ALLEN WILLIAMS.
	RESOLUTIONS of respect adopted by the officers
and members of the 12th Ward Relief Society, to
the memory of our beloved Sister Pernecy F.
Williams who departed this life Nov. 23rd 1895, at
Logan, Utah, where she had gone to comfort her
two daughters in sickness by her presence.
	Whereas: Our Heavenly Father in his wisdom
has seen fit to take from our midst our beloved
Sister Pernecy F. Williams.
	Be it resolved, that while we bow in humble
submission to the will of Him, “Who doeth all
things well,” we do recognize in her death, the
severe loss of a faithful, energetic and efficient
teacher, assistant treasurer and trustee, in our
society. A true friend to the needy, a comforter
in the hour of trouble, a devoted wife, and
mother, and a true Latter-day Saint, we also
recognize in her faithful labors and devotion to
the cause of Zion an example worthy of emula-
tion of each member of our society.
	Resolved, that we sincerely sympathize with
her bereaved husband, Brother T. V. Williams,
her children and friends in the loss of a faithful
wife, loving mother and sincere friend.
	Also that these resolutions be placed upon the
records of the Twelth Ward Relief Society and be
published in the WOMAN'S EXPONENT, and that
a copy be sent to the family of our departed
sister.
				JULIA A. DRUCE, President.
			HANNAH C. BROOKS, Secretary.

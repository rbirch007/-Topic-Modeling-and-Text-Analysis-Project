SNOWFLAKE STAKE.

Editor Exponent:
	PERHAPS you will be interested in the
change recently made in our Stake officers.
Sister Ruth Hatch, second counselor to
Sister Smith, has been residing in Utah
for some time, and has been uncertain about
her return, so she sent in her resignation at
our conference, Nov. 21, 1895. Sister
Mary J. West was chosen to fill her place,
and was sustained at the conference.
	During the past year Sister Smith and
her counselors have visited all the branches
of the Relief Society in the Stake. They
are doing well under the circumstances
Our faith and prayers are freely given in
behalf of those who stand at the head of the
Relief Society, and we try to be in readiness
to aid in any way we can.
	That the peace and blessing of the Lord
may attend you is the sincere desire of
your sister in the gospel.

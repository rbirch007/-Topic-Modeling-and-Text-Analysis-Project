JANET CRYSTAL SHELLEY.

	IT is with feelings of sincere regret that we
chronicle the death of our beloved Sister Janet
Crystal Shelley, who departed this life January I,
1896, of that dread disease diphtheria, aged 23
years and three months.
	Sister Janei was the beloved daughter of James
and Janet Crystal and the wife of Brother Ammon
Shelley, beloved by all within the circle of her ac-
quaintance. A true wife and mother and a faith-
ful friend. Her sudden departure was a shock to
the community, as she had been but a few days
prostrated by the loss of her little daughter
Geneva by the same disease, only a week
previous.
	May the Lord comfort the doubly bereaved hus-
band and the sorrowing parents, and give them
strength to say Father thy will be done, knowing
that she has gone where there is no sorrow nor
mourning, that she has filled her mission and
gone home.with honor. COM.

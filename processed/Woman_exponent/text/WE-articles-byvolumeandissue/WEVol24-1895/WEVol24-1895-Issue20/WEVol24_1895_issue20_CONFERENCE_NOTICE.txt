CONFERENCE NOTICE.

	The Sixty-sixth Annual Conference of
the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day
Saints will convene in the Tabernacle,
Salt Lake City, at 10 o’clock on Saturday
morning, April 4th, 1896.
	The officers and members of the Church
genarally are cordially invited to attend
the meetings of the Conference.
	WILFORD WOODRUFF,
	GEORGR Q. CANNON,

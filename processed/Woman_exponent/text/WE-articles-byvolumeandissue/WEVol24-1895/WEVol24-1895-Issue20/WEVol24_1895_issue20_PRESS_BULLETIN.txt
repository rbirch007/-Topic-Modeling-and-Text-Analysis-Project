	PRESS BULLETIN.
	WE have received from the Agricultual
College of Utah the Fourth Biennial Re-
port of the Board of Trustees to the Legis-
lative Assembly, and the Sixth Annual Re-
port of the Experiment Station.
	The Report gives an explicit statement of
tlie financial resources of the College, and
the relations of the national government
and of the State of Utah to the institution.
The function of the land-grant colleges is
plainly set forth, together with a summary
of the courses offered and the methods of
instruction pursued at the College.
	The book is beautifully illustrated with
half-tone engravings of the different build-
ings and rooms, and contains reproductions
of the pen drawings of students, showing
the regular work of the class room. The
pamphlet is an excellent souvenir to send to
your friends. It exhibits in a brief and
striking manner, the advantages of higher
and practical training—offered free in the
College to all qualified applicants.
	By sending a written request to the
Agricultural College, Logan, Utah, a copy
of the Report will be mailed to any address.

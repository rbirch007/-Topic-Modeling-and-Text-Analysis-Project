That in life’s darkest hours, we’ve met, 
	"Angels of Consolation"—yet—
Disguised in this our mortal state.

Worthies of old—have, entertained, 
	Angels of mercy, so we’re told.
And at this feast, we have at least;
	One Angel, true as earth can hold—
A Woman! Good as finest gold.

Salt Lake City, Jan. 31st, 1896.

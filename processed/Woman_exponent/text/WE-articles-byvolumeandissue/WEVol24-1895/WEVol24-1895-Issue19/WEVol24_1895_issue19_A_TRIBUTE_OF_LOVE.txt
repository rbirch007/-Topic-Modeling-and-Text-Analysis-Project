		A TRIBUTE OF LOVE.

SWEET friend, with eyes of Heaven's blue,
Where the love of God, shines gently, through;
Kind Patience restiug on thy fair young fair,
And noble traits that add their grace,
We bring an offering on this day, 
And Heaven’s blessing on thee pray.

Lift up your heart for God is love,
Look up the sky is blue above;
And far away in realms unseen,
Thy record stands, no blots between;
But traced in gold, on each fair line,
Read “Hannah you are truly mine.” 

The gentle sigh that heaves thy breast,
The longing thought for precious rest, 
Like incense rare ascends to Him,
Whose earthly eyes with tears were dim,
Thy hopes and fears are in His hand— 
The shepherd loves, his chosen band.

Then take Life’s burden boldly up,
Though mixed with bitter be thy cup,
This is the road thy Saviour trod,
The royal road that leads to God,
May music from the unseen shore, 
Sweet comfort bring forever more. 
						LYDIA D. ALDER.
Nov. 1895.

IN MEMORIAM.

	RESOLUTIONS of respect adopted by the officers
and members of the Orangeville ward Relief
Society, to the memory of our beloved Sister
Mary Catherine Anderson, who departed this
life Oct. 10, 1895, at her home in Orangeville,
Emery Co.
	Whereas: Our Heavenly Father in his wisdom
has seen fit to take from our midst our beloved
Catharine Anderson.
	Be it resolved, that while we bow in humble
submission to the will of Him, “Who doeth all
things well.” we do recognize in her death, the
severe loss of a faithful, energetic and efficient
teacher, in our society.
	A true friend to the needy, a comforter in the
hour of trouble, a devoted wife and mother, and
a true Latter-day Saint, we also recognize in her
faithful labors and devotion to the cause of truth,
an example worthy of emulation of each member
of our society.
	Resolved, that we sincerely sympathize with
her bereaved husband, Brother Swain Anderson,
her children and friends in the loss of a faithful
wife, loving mother and sincere friend. Also
that these resolutions be placed upon the records
of the Orangeville Ward Relief Society and be
published in the WOMAN'S EXPONENT, and that a 
copy be sent to the family of our dear departed
sister.
	RODHA E. ROBERTSON, Prest.

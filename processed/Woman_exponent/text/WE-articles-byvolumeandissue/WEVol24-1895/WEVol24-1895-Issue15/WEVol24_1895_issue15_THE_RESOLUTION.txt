After a week full crowded with renewed

with its dilapidated little church and its ad-
joining grave yard, the Indian Mound &c.
which aroused my childish curiosity, and
brought many remembrances of the past.
   The weather here is so changeable and
unpleasant that I have not been able to
visit as much as I might have desired.

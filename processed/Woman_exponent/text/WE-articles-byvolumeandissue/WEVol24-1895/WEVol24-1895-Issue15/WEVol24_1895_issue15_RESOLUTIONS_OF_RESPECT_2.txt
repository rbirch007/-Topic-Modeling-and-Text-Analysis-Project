RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	In loving remembrance of Dena Louisa,
daughter of Lars L. and Rendena Olsen, who de-
parted this life Saturday, November 16th, 1895, at
the residence of her parents in Santaquin; aged
fourteen years, eight months and eighteen days.
Summoned by the angel death away from the
loving arms of her affectionate parents, brothers
and sisters. She was beloved, by all who knew her
and in the Primary Association, she was always
ready to respond to every call.

Dear one we shall think of thee,
	For many years to come;
Thy smiling face we ne’er shall see.
	For God has called thee home.
Why do we mourn? She is not lost,
Beyond the river she has crossed,
And on that bright, celestial shore,
Our loved one waits the coming o’er.

	We as officers and members of the Primary
Association unanimously adopt the following
resolutions.
	We sincerely condole with the parents, brothers
and sisters in their heavy bereavement and we
tender them our heartfelt sympathy.
	Resolved that we adopt these resolutions as a
token of respect, that we present a copy to her
parents, and publish the same in the WOMAN'S
EXPONENT, and place a copy on onr records.
ELIZABETH WALL,
LAURA LEVANDER,
MARY ECKERSLEY.

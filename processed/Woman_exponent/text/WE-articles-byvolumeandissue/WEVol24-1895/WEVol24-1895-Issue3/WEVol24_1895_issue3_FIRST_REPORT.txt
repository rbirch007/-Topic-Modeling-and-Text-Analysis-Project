			FIRST REPORT.
	Fourteenth Ward Female Relief Society, 
organized on the 17th day of September,
1856. Commenced and carried on our
operations in a time of scarcity and under 
trying circumstances until, the 28th day of
March, 1858, about one year and six months,
during which time we received from mem-
bers of the society in the form of contribu-
tions of money, wearing apparel, Provisions,
etc, amounting to. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . $346.05
Quilts on hand and for Sale, . . . . . . . .100.00
Overplus for work and Propt. . . . . . . 101.12

LADIES’ SEMI-MONTHLY MEETING.

	MINUTES of meeting held in Fourteenth
Ward Assembly Rooms, Oct. 19th, 1895.
Counselor E. Stevenson presiding. Opened
by singing, “Welcome best of all good
meetings.” Prayer by Counselor E. Webb.
Continued by singing, “Come let us re-
joice in the day of Salvation.” Counselor
Stevenson read a note from President Horne
stating that she had a cold and could not
meet with us to day. Counselor E. Steven-
son said she was sorry that we had not our
Secretary present today. Sister L. D.
Alder was called upon to act in this capacity.

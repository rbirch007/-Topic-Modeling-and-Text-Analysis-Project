	Monday evening May, 13th, 1895. Con-
ference called to order by Miss Anthony in
the Convention hall at 8-30 p. m.
	Opening prayer by president Angus M.
Cannon. Miss Maude Pratt and the Cecelia
Club sang “The Psalm of Life” which was
beautifully rendered.
	Mrs. Wells: I am very proud to be able
to say to you that Miss Anthony will ad-
dress us in a very few minutes and I hope
the people will not spare their enthusiasm
tonight and I trust you will applaud her.

COLORADO WOMAN’S GREETING.
_________________________________
	THE women of Colorado who know
whereof they speak, send friendly greeting
to the women of Utah who have just been
enfranchised under the Constitution of the
new state. We heartily respond to this
cordial expression of their appreciation of
our full-fledged citizenship, and trust the
link that binds the women of the three free
states; Wyoming, Colorado and Utah to-
gether, may be as strong as the chain that


unites the rock-ribbed mountains of these
intermountain states, and as imperishable
as the pure metal with which these ever-
lasting hills abound.

“FOR, AND IN BEHALF.”

THE millions now behind the vail,
	In bondage, tears and grief,
Are waiting for these blessed words,
	Of comfort and relief:
The words are truth and surety,
	They mean no broken staff—
They’re words of joyous liberty!
	Yea, “For, and in behalf.”

Elijah’s power to bind and seal,
	By Heaven’s decree gone forth—
The father’s hearts are turning to
	Their children now on earth.
And praying them with tearful eyes,
	To strike their fetters off,
And listening eager for the words;

EUGENE FIELD. 
_________________
EUGENE FIELD the beloved poet and 
man of letters, died suddenly and without
warning, Nov. 4th, at day break; the news
made not only Chicago sad but thousands
throughout the country will mourn the loss
of one of the brightest geniuses of the
nineteenth century. The newspapers and
magazines of the month contain touching
tributes to the memory of this man of bril-
liant genius, from his friends and com-
panions, all of them, men who are them-
selves famous in literature.

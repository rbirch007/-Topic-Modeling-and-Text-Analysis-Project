		SAN LUIS STAKE.
Editor Womans Exponent:
	MINUTES of the Semi-annual Conference
of the Relief Society of the San Luis
Stake, held, September 15, 1895.  Present
on the stand were President Mortenson
and Counselors, Magdalene Funk and
Jane Crowther, and many of the leading
brethren and sisters of the stake. Meeting
opened with singing and prayer.
	Cornelia Mortensen made a few opening
remarks, was pleased to meet with the
saints on such a beautiful morning, prayed
for the spirit of God to direct us.
	Verbal reports were then called for,
which were responded to cheerfully. The
spirit of the speakers in general was good,
and the storing of grain, was made the im-
portant subject. The general and local
officers were presented and unanimously
sustained; after which a letter from Zina
D. H. Young was read. Before closing,
Bishop S. C. Berthelsen made a motion,
that the brethren sustain the sisters with
their means as well as by their faith, which 
was unanimously carried.
	Conference closed by singing and bene-
diction.
	LAURA J. M. PETERSEN, Sec. pro-tem.

OFEICIAL CONGRATULATIONS.
_____________________________
		DENVER, COLO. Nov. 12, 1895.
	Dear Mrs. Wells:
	The Colorado Equal Suffrage Asociation in ex-
ecutive session on November 9, 1895, instructed
its secretary to send congratulations to the newly
enfranchised women of Utah.
	May the granting of citizenship to the women
of Utah presage reforms manifold and beneficial;
and, in the march of progress, may your banner
ever float in the front ranks.
	We gladly adorn our suffrage flag with the
third star, and the women of Colorado wish you
God-speed as workers, and as citizens of this
great nation.
	Very cordially yours,

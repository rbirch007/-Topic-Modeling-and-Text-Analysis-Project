NOTES AND NEWS.

	Two ladies were elected members of
parliament in the reign of Edward III.
MISS MARY SNOW has been re-elected
superintendent of the public schools of
Bangor, Me.
	IN the death of the distinguished Scandi-
navian, Hjalmar Hjorth Boyesen, professor
of Germanic literature at Columbia College,
the principle of a free opportunity and
equal justice for women loses an influential
advocate.

CONFERENCE.

	THE Quarterly Conference of the Relief
Society of this Stake of Zion, will be held
in the Assembly Hall on Saturday, Sept.
21st. Meetings commencing at 10 a. m.
and 2 p. m. as usual. It is very desirable
that there should be an official representa-
tive from each branch of this Stake of
Zion and a full attendance of members is
requested.
	M. I. HORNE, PRESIDENT.

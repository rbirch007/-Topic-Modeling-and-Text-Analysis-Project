BIRTHDAY PARTY

	A Birthday party was held in St. John’s,
Idaho, March 23, 1896, in honor of
Eliza P. Williams.
	The members of the Relief Society of
St. John’s gave their President Eliza P.
Williams a surprise at about twelve o’olock.
The members gathered together at the
meeting house with their baskets filled
with good things which they transfered to
the table and when all was ready, Sister
Williams was invited to the meeting house.
When the door was opened and our Presi-
dent saw so many sisters and the tables
spread, she wept for joy. She was pre-
sented with two chairs and some other
small gifts.
	Singing and short speeches followed and
an enjoyable time was held.
ELVIRA A. HARRISON, Sec.
St. John’s, Oneida Co., Idaho.

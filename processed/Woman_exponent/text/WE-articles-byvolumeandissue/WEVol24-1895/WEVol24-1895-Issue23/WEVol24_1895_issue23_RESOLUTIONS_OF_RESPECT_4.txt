RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	RESOLUTIONS of respect adopted by the Of-
ficers and members of the Relief Society of South
Bountiful to the memory of Phebe N. Hatch who
died Feb. 9th, 1896; she was born Nov. 6th, 1850
in the fifteenth Ward Salt Lake City.
	Whereas, Sister Hatch one of our beloved
teachers has left this stage of action;
	Resolved that we recognize in the death of
Sister Hatch, the loss of a faithful and energetic
teacher, a friend to the needy, a comforter in the
hour of trouble, a devoted mother, and a faithful
Latter-day Saint.
	Resolved that we esteem her labors and devo-
tion to the interest of Zion as worthy the emula-
tion of every member of our society.
	Resolved that we condole and sympathize with
the bereaved children and friends in the loss of a
loving mother and sincere friend.  
	Resolved that these resolutions be recorded in
our records, a copy sent to the bereaved family
and also one be sent to the Woman’s Exponent
for publication.
	SARAH A. HOWARD, 
	MARY A. PAGE,		}Committee.

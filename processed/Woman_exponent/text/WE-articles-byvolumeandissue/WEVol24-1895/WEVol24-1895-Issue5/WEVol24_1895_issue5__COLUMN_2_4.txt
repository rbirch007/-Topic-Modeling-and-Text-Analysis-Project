	The gentlemen and ladies are all pro-

Pacific, Northern Pacific and Oregon rail-

sible kindness, courtesy and attention and
did all in their power to make the trip
agreeable.
	President Woodruff slept well while away
where the atmosphere was cool, but since 
his return feels the heat somewhat oppres-
sive.

done and were continually doing in the
community; and many good wishes were
expressed for their future success, prosperity
and happiness, Brother and Sister Steel each
responded neatly and gratefully to the kind
wishes, and expressions, and demonstra-
tions of love that had been made for them
and in a very interesting manner related
some of their early experiences, and the
company dispersed, feeling that we had
spent a very pleasant and profitable time to-
gether.

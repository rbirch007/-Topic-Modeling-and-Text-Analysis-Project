				AMY A. THOMAS.

Brother and Sister Steel are real Scotch
born near Glasgow and were married in
Galston Ayrshire Scotland on the 31st, of
January,1895,by the Rev. Robert McIndoe.
four and a half years before they became
members of the Church of Jesus Christ of
Latter-day Saints. Sister Steel’s brother
who is a wealthy grain merchant and mem-
ber of the City Council of Glasgow visited
his sister and spent a few days here in 1876,
he is a man of extensive cultivation has
traveled much in foreign countries.

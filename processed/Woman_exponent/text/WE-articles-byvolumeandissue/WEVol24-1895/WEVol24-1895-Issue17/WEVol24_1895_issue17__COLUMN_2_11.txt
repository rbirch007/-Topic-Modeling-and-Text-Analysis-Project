She joined the Church of Jesus Christ of
Latter-day Saints about 1838. She was
the mother of three children, Caroline
Augusta, born at Tomsriver, N. J., Georg-
ianna, born in New York City and died there
in infancy, Anthony W. Ivins was born at
Tomsriver in 1852. In 1853 they moved
to Utah, and 1861 came to St. George.
She was the first President of the Relief
Society, and has been Stake President of
the same society about twenty years.
Her life has been a very useful and respect-
ed one. She died on the 10th of January,
1896, of general debility, and passed away,
as she had lived, a faithful Latter-day Saint.
She leaves a husband, one son, eleven
grand-children and a host of friends to
mourn her loss.

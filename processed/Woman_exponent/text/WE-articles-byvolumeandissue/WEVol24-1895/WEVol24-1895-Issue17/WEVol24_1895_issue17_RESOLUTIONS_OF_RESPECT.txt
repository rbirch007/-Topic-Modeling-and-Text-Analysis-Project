		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
 
	Adopted by the officers and members of the
St. George Stake Relief Society, and her fellow-
laborers in the St. George Temple to the memory
of Anna L. Ivins, who died from the effects of old
age and general debility, Friday, Jan. 10th, 1896,
at her residence in St. George.
	Whereas:—God, in his infinite mercy and
wisdom, has seen fit to take unto himself our be-
loved sister and co-laborer, Sister Anna L.
Ivins.
	Resolved: That we mourn, in the death of
Sister Ivins, the loss of a faithful and much be-
loved president, both of the Fourth St. George
Ward, and the Stake Relief Society; a devoted
worker in the Temple, a friend and helper to all,
a loving wife and mother, and a sterling Latter-
day Saint, whose laudable example we might
all emulate with profit.
	Resolved: That we sympathize and sincerely
mourn with the aged and bereaved husband, the
children, grand children and friends in the loss of
a devoted wife and mother and a sincere and
charitable friend.
	Resolved: That these resolutions be read at
the anniversary meeting of the seventy-fifth
birthday of President Zina D. H. Young: that
they be spread upon the records of the Fourth
Ward and Stake Relief Society: that a copy be
sent to the bereaved family and that they be
published in full in the WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
	ANN C. WOODBURY, MARY ANN WOODWARD,
EMMA MORRIS, BARBARA MATHIS, Committee.

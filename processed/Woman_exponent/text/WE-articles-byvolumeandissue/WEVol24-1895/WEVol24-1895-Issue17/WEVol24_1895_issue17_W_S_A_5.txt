	When I was a girl we knew exactly
what the Lord created women for;
they were to take care of the household, at-
tend to the cooking etc. that was the cus-
tomary work of women. In fact as far as
cooking was concerned we believed it to be
our sphere, but you look out on the world
to day, who are the cooks from one end of
our nation to the other, there is not a wo-
man cook anywhere; the hotels, restaurants
and in the homes of the wealthy all are
men. Why is it? Let me give you a
receipt by which you can always tell.
	Whenever you pay a dollar and a half a
week for anything that is woman’s sphere,
always woman’s sphere no matter what it
is; but to day we pay to cooks from one to
ten thousand dollars a year. Some of the
cooks receive exactly the same salary as the
President of Harvard University. Now
when you pay from one to ten thousand
dollars a year for any kind of service that
is man’s work and the reason why men are
cooking to-day is not because it is man’s
work anymore than it is woman’s work, it
is because it pays, that is all; and wherever
work pays there you find men. Women in
that sphere are always called cooks but you
could not insult a man more than to call
him a cook, he is a Professor of the Culinary
art; one is a woman and the other is a man.

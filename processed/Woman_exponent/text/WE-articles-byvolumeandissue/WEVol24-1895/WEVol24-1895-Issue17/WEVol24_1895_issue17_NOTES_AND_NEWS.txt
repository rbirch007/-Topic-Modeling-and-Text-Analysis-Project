NOTES AND NEWS.

	IN the U. S. Senate, Hon. George F.
Hoar has presented a joint resolution for a
Constitutional Amendment admitting wo-
men to suffrage.
	MRS. CARRIE CHAPMAN-CATT has clear-
ed $140 for the National Organization
Committee by her Woman Suffrage Calen-
dars, which found a ready sale. We con-
gratulate her.
	LADY HENRY SOMERSET was the victim
of an attempted compliment at a recent
temperance meeting in London. She was
absent through illness. The lady who
took her place made this kindly but unex-
pected explanation: "Dear Lady Henry has
been overworked, and we must, of course,
be careful not to kill the goose that lays the
golden eggs."

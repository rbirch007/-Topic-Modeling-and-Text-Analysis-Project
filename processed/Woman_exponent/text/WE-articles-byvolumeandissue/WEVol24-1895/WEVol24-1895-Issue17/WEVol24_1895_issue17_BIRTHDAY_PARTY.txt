BIRTHDAY PARTY.

Editor Exponent.
	ON Feb. 4th the Relief Society of Mink-
Creek Idaho made a surprise for Sister
Ingrid Graham, the President of the Society.
About two hundred people were assembled


at the Town Hall. When Sister Graham
was brought to the Hall by Brother Gra-
ham, both of her Counselors came down
and escorted her to an upper room where
tables were set with the good things of the
earth. It being Sister Graham’s 57th birth
day many congratulations were accorded
her and amidst music and many good wish-
es she was escorted to the head of the table
where she was seated among her children
and grand-children on one side and the
Bishopric on the other; at the same time
she was presented with a rocking chair as a
token of respect for her services among the
sisters. After all had participated of the
good things set on the table, a program of
singing, music, recitations and speeches
indulged in till towards evening when all
separated pleased and happy with the gather-
ing, wishing each other many such gather-
ings in the future. Yours very respectfully,

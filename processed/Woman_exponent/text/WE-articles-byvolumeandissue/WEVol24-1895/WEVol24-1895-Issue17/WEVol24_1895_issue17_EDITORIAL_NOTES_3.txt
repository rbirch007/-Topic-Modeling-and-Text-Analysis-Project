EDITORIAL NOTES.
	REPORTS received to date are Bear Lake,
Cassia, Emery, Iosepa, Kanab, Maricopa,
Parowan, San Luis, Sevier, St. George,
Star Valley, St. Joseph, Snowflake, Cache,
Summit, Uintah, Wayne, Morgan, eighteen
in all.
	MISS LUCY E. ANTHONY was the guest
of honor at the Woman’s Suffrage Club
in the parlors of the Golden Gate Hall
in San Francisco on Tuesday Feb. 11th.
Miss Anthony is the niece of Miss Susan B.
Anthony of world-wide fame, and is a
very sweet mannered and lovable young
woman. She is also very capable in all
executive work, and when she does speak
has something of consequence to say. She
does not pose as a public speaker although
she possesses the ability and has a thorough
knowledge of the equal suffrage work

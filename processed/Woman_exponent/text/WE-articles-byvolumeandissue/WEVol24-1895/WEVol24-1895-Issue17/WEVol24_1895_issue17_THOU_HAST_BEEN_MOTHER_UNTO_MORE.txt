Thou hast been mother unto more
	Than thine own children, and the name
“Mother in Israel" is thine own
	And all who love thee share a claim.

Linked with this dispensation is
	Thy hist’ry from its earliest date,
In ev’ry Temple thou hast wrought
	Salvation for the small and great.
O, souls redeemed in other spheres!
	If thou hast knowledge of this day,
Join in our love and praise of her
	Who led from prison world thy way.

May mightiest love from higher, life
	Blend with our love and prayers below,
And heavenly power attend thy days
	While still on earth thy face we know;
Still drawing nearer to the source
	Thy listening soul learn holier love,
Till—passing—memory keeps thy name
	In Earth and Heaven, forever more.
AUGUSTA JOYCE CROCHERON.
Bountiful, Jan. 22nd, 1896.

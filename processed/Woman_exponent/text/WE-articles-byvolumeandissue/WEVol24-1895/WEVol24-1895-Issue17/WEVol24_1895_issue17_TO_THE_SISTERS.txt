TO THE SISTERS.
	SOME preparations have been carefully
made by Sister Emma Empey, wife of
Bishop N. A. Empey of the Thirteenth
Ward, to accommodate sisters from the coun-
try who desire to come and work in the Tem-
ple. The rooms are located just back of
the 13th Ward Assembly Rooms and are
neatly furnished for light housekeeping.
	Everything is new, clean and neat.
One dollar for each person per week
including coal and lights and conveniences
for cooking on a small scale.

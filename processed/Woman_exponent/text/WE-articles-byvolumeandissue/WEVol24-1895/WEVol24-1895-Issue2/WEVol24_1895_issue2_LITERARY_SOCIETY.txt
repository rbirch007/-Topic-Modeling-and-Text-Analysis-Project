LITERARY SOCIETY.

	THE American History and Literature 
Club was organized April 3, 1895, at the
home of James Howard, South Bountiful,
Davis Co., with the following officers:
President, Mrs. Augusta J. Crocheron; Sec-
retary, Caroline A. Crocheron; Assistant
Secretary, Jennie Howard, and Eveline
Parkin as Treasurer. Committee on pro-
gramme Nellie E. Parkin and Lucy J.
Howard.
	Our meetings are held weekly and much
interest is manifested.

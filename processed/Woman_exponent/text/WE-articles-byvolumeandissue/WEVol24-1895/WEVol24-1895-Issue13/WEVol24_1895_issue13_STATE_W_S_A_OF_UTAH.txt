STATE W. S. A. OF UTAH.

	THE W. S. Association in the several
counties, in this Territory are hereby re-
minded that the memberships are due this
present month, and the State President has
been notified that the entire membership
must be sent on to the treasurer of the
National American W. S. A. otherwise we
shall lose our votes in the Convention dur-
ing the year 1896. Officers are urged to be
as diligent as possible in collecting and for-
warding the money with the names and
addresses of all paid up members to the
Treasurer Ella W. Hyde 246 W. 2nd South
St. Salt Lake City. These lists of names
and addresses must be certified to by the
President and Recording Secretary of the
respective auxiliary W. S. A. The State
President also requires a concise report of
the year’s work in each county to be for-
warded to her address, duriug this month in
time to include it in the yearly report which
must reach the Secretary of the N. A. W.
S. A. before January 1st 1896.
	E. B. WELLS, President.

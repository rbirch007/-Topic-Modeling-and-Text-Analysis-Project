IN MEMORIAM.
	The following resolutions of respect were
adopted by the Pleasant Grove First Ward Re-
lief Society in memory of Sister Emeline Richards
who departed this life February 24th, 1895. She
was born June 27th. 1857 and leaves a husband
and six children to mourn her loss, the youngest
being only twelve days old.
	Whereas, God in His infinite wisdom has seen
fit to call home Sister Emeline Richards,—
	Resolved, that we deeply mourn the loss of an
active member of the Relief Society, who was
ever willing to assist in all good works, was very
generous, and a friend to the poor. She was a
devoted wife and mother and respected by all
who knew her. We feel to extend our heart-
felt sympathy to the bereaved husband, mother-
less children and relatives, and can only point
them to a higher source for consolation in their
hour of need.
	Resolved that these resolutions are entered in
our minutes, a copy presented to the bereaved
family and forwarded to the Woman’s Exponent
for publication.
	PATIENCE ARCHER, President.

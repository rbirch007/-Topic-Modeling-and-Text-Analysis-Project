RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	THE quarterly conference of the Relief
Society of the Salt Lake Stake of Zion,
will be held Saturday, December 14th,
1895, in the Fourteenth Ward Assembly
rooms in this city meetings commencing
at 10. a. m. and 2. p. m. It is urgently re-
quested that a representative from each
society in the stake be present, as verbal re-
ports are desired. A cordial invitation is
extended to the public.

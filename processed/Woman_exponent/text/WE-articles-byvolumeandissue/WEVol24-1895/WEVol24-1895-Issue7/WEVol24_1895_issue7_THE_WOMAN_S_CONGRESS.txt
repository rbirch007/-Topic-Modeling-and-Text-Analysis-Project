THE WOMAN’S CONGRESS.

	THE Woman’s Congress of the Association for
the Advancement of Women will be held this
year in New Orleans, Nov. 6, 7, and 8. It will be
preceded as usual by a Business Meeting of of-
ficers on the evening of Nov. 5. By special ar-
rangement a preliminary meeting of one day will
be held in Atlanta, Ga., Nov. 4.
	JULIA WARD HOWE, President.
	LILY LORD TIFFT, Secretary.

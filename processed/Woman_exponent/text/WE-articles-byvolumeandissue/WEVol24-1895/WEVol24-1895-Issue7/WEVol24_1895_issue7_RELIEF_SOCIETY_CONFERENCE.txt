RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	THE general conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Salt Lake As-
sembly Hall in this city, on Thursday,
October 3rd, 1895, meetings commencing at
10 a. m. and 2 p. m. as usual. It is earn-
estly desired that there should be a repre-
sentation from the Relief Society of every
Stake of Zion and from each society within
the several Stakes. All are cordially in-
vited to attend the conference, and especial-
ly the members of this important organiza-
tion.
	ZINA D. H. YOUNG, President
	JANE S. RICHARDS,
	BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
	Counselors.

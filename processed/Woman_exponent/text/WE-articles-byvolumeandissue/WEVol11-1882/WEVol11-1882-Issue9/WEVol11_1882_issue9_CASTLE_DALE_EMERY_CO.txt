CASTLE DALE, EMERY CO.

	DEAR SISTER:—With great pleasure I com-
municate to your very interesting paper the
following items.
	On the 12th and 13th of August, 1882, we
were visited by two of the Twelve Apostles, E.
Snow and J. H. Smith, who held two days
meeting at this place, during which time they
recommended us to organize a Relief Society
in all the settlements of this Stake.
	The sisters of this Ward met to organize
on the 24th. August 25, elected the following
officers: Annie N. Larsen, President; Hannah
Seely and Harriet A. Willcox, Counselors;
Lucinda Jeffs, Secretary, Ellen Mitler, Treas-
urer. They were blessed and set apart under
the hands of Pres. C. G. Larsen and Counselor,
who also made some excellent remarks, and en-
couraged the sisters to be active to the duties
devolving upon them in their high and holy
calling, as Saints of the Most High. Pres.
Annie N. Larsen gave good instructions, all of
which the sisters feel to appreciate. We held
our first meeting on the seventh of September.
	Praying God to bless you in your labor of
love, I remain, with kindest regards, your sister
in the Gospel,		LUCINDA JEFFS, Sec'y.

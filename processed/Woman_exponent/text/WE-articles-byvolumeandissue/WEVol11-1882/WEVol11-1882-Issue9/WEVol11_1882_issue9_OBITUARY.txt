OBITUARY.

	DIED: In Brighton Ward, Aug. 12th. 1882, of
cholera infantum, after an illness of many
weeks, Minnie Presinda, the dearly beloved
child of George W. and Agnes Squires Hunting
ton; age one year and ten months, lacking three
days.
	Little Minnie was an unusually bright and
interesting child for her age, also very large;
but from her birth she was always afflicted with
sickness of some kind, so that she was only a
little sufferer during her brief sojourn in this
world of grief and affliction.
	The angels have now taken her home to the
celestial kingdom of our Lord, where there is no
more grief and pain, but all is joy and peace for-
ever.

'Twas not in anguish nor in wrath,
	The Reaper came that day,
Bright angels wished the earth,
	And bore the flower away;
A lovely bud so young and fair,
	Called hence by early doom.
"Just come to show how fair a flower,
	In Paradise would bloom."
									A. C.

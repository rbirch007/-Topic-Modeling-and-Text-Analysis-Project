WRITTEN FOR THE PRIMARY.

	I wonder if we all realize what we were
placed on this earth for; what purpose the Lord
sent us here for, in these "the last days?" I
am afraid a great many of us are too apt to
think there is no higher aim on earth than to
spend all our time in seeking amusement and
recreation, instead of striving to gain all the
knowledge and wisdom that is to be obtained.
We should all live for a purpose; and have
some high standard of excellence to attain to,
before we leave this frail existence to go to
some higher sphere of action on the other side.
If we as a people fully understood the law of
the Lord as we should, I think we would all
try to do better, and seek to serve God more
fully than we do at present. That we may do
this, and strive to keep his commandments, is
the sincere wish of
						NELLIE SNOW.
Pine Valley, Utah, Jan. 3d, 1882.

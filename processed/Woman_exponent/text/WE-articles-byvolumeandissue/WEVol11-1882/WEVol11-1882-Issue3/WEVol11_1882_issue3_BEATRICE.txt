							BEATRICE.


AFTER the battle of arms comes the battle
of history.

	WOMEN at Work, a very ably edited mag-
azine, formerly published at Louisville Ky.,
will be hereafter issued from Brattleboro, Vt.
Success to the enterprise.

	MRS. Matilda M. Ricker has been admitted
to practice law in the court of the District of
Columbia. At the examination before the
committee of the Bar Association, Mrs. Ricker
stood higher than the nineteen men examined
at the same time. This speaks well for women,
as law is one of the professions in which it has
been said women could not excel.

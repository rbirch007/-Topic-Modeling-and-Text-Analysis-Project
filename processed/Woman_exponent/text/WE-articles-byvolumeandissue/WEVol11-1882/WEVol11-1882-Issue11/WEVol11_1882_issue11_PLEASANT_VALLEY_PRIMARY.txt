PLEASANT VALLEY PRIMARY.

	President David Williams assisted by his
Counselors, organized the children of this
branch into a Primary Association on the 3d,
of Sept. President Williams called upon Sis-
ter Tillie Bliss to give some instructions regard-
ing the object and character of Primary Asso-
ciations; which she did in a very interesting
and able manner. Counselor Robert Gillispie
proposed Sister Tillie Bliss as President of the
Association, who was unanimously sustained.
She chose as her Counselors, Sister Martha
Gillispie and Sister Mary Ann Lewis. Miss
Annie Eden as Treasurer, and Miss Martha
Reese Secretary. All of whom were unami-
mously sustained. President Williams then
made some very excellent remarks to the par-
ents, desiring them to take an interest in the
Association and send their children as he knew
they would be benefited by so doing. Counselor
Robert Gillispie followed urging the necessity
of education our little ones in the principles of
the Gospel. Thought the President had taken
a very wise step in organizing the children.
Thought they had made a very wise selection
in appointing Sister Bliss President, as she had
had considerable experience with children
understanding their different dispositions etc.

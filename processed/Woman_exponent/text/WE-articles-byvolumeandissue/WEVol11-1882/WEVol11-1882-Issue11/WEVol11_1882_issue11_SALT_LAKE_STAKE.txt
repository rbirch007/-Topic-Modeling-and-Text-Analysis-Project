SALT LAKE STAKE.

	Minutes of the Young Ladies’ Conference,
held in the Assembly Hall, Salt Lake City,
Sept. 23, 1882. Singing. Prayer. Singing.
Minutes of previous Conference read and ap-
proved; also reports. Sister Freeze was pleased
with the reports, but would like to have more
of the country wards represented. Pleased to
hear the testimonies that were borne, for she
believed with Sister Snow, testimony to be the
foundation of spiritual, improvement.
	The following resolutions of respect to the
memory of Counselor Clara Conrad, were read
by Coun. Louie Felt, and were unanimously ad-
opted by the Conference.

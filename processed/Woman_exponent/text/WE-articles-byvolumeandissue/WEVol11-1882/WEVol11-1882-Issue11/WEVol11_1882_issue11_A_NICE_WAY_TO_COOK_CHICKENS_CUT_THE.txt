A NICE WAY TO COOK CHICKENS.—Cut the
chicken up, and stew until done; make a thick-
ening of cream and flour, adding butter; salt
and pepper to taste; have made and baked a
pair of short cakes—rolled thin; cut in small
squares, and arrange on the dish, pouring
chicken and gravy over while both are hot.
This is much better than chicken pie, and more
simple to make.

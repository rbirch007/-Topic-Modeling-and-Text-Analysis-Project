CLASS IN OBSTETRICS,
BY special request of MRS. E. R. SNOW SMITH,
Dr. ROMANIA B. PRATT
Will commence her class in Obstetrics, November 1st,
1882, in her office over Godbe & Pitts' Drug Store. Lec-
tures, three per week for five months. During the course,
Drs. Anderson and Benedict will each deliver six prac-
tical lectures. Fee for course, $30.00; former stu-
dents, $20.00; books (3), $12.00, Manikin, skeleton, in-
struments and plates for illustration. Please send or-
ders for books early, as they have to be ordered from
the East. Lectures Commence at 3 p. m.

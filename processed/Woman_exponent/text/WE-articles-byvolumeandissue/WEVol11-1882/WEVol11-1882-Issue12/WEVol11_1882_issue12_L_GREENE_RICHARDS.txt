				L. GREENE RICHARDS.


Lady Hannah Shepherd Havelock recently
died at her residence in Kensington Palace
Gardens, London. In recognition of the ser-
vices of her distinguished husband, the captor
of Lucknow, Parliament settled upon her a
pension of 1,000 a year, and raised her to
the rank of a baronet’s widow, with the baro-
netcy renewed to their son.

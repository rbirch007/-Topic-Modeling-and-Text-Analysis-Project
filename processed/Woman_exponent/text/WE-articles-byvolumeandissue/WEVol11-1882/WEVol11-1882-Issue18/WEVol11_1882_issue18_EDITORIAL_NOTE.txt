EDITORIAL NOTE.

	MRS. W. East writes from Smithville, Arizo-
na, enclosing subscriptions to the EXPONENT,
that she hopes soon to be able to increase her
list for it, as they find the paper invaluable in
that far-off land, as the representative of our
organizations, and they feel to assist in the de-
velopment of the eternal truths contained in
its pages. She adds: "Success to our little pa-
per, which will soon be in its teens. May it
grow in power and influence with its years."
It is very gratifying to those who labor in the
interest of the EXPONENT, to know it is appre-
ciated, in the different settlements even outside
Utah. It shall be our constant aim to make it
interesting to its many readers.

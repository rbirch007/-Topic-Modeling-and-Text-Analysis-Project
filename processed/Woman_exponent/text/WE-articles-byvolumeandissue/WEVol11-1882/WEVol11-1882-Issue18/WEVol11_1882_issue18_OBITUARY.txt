OBITUARY.

	Died, in Evanston, Wyoming Territory, Jan. 17, 1883,
Eliza Martin, daughter of Hugh and Jane Watterson
Martin.
	Deceased was born in Motherwell, Lanark, Scotland,
June 17, 1866, and was in her 17th year at the time of
her demise. She was a faithful member of the Young
Ladies Mutual Improvement Association, also of the
Sunday School. She was kind and dutiful and beloved
by all who knew her. She leaves a loving father and
mother, four brothers and four sisters and many dear
friends to mourn her loss.
	NETTIE A. JONES.
Brighton, Salt Lake County, Jan. 21, 1883.

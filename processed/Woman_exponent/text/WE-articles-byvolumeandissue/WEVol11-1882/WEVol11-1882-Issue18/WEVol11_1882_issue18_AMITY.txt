	AMITY.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	I feel it my duty to give a brief report of
the organization of the Y. L. M. I. A. of
Amity, Arizona, which was organized Nov. 2,
1882, choosing Sister Julia A. Jenson presi-
dent. Our meetings are well attended, and a
great deal of good has been done since the or-
ganization. The young ladies show a desire to
press forward in this great work of the latter
days.	JULIA A. JENSON, Secretary.
		M. E. BIGELOW, Secretary.

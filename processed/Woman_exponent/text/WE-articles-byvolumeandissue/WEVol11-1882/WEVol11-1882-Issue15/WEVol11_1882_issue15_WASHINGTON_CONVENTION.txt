WASHINGTON CONVENTION.

	We have received an invitation to attend as a
delegate from Utah the National Woman Suf-
frage Association to be held in Washington,D. C.
January 23d, 24th and 25th. There will be
sessions daily at 10 a.m., and 8 p.m. The
afternoon will be devoted to the business of the
N. W. S. A., and its congressional work. It
is the wish of the officers of the Association
that delegates be present representing the wo-
men of every State and Territory in the
Union. Friends who cannot aid by their work
are solicited to contribute generously in money
to defray the necessary expenses of the Con-
vention. The call is issued by the Chairman
Executive Committtee and further signed by
Susan B. Anthony, Vice-President, and Ra-
chel G. Foster, Cor. Sec.

RESOLUTIONS.

	The following resolutions of respect to our
young and much beloved Sister, Julia V. Pack,
who departed this life May 22d, 1882; in the
Seventeenth year of her age, were unanimously
adopted by the Y. L. M. I. Association of Kamas,
Summit Stake of Zion.
	Whereas God in his divine wisdom has seen
proper to call from our midst, to a higher sphere,
one of our members, who was greatly beloved
and respected by us all, for her kind and gentle
spirit, her pure and virtuous life, her faithful
labors in our Society and many good qualities of
a saint which she possessed, and for the love
which we have for her, and the respect for her
parents, brothers, sisters and friends,
	Therefore, be it resolved that we as members
of the Y. L. M. I. A., do deeply mourn the loss of
our beloved Sister and co-laborer in this Society,
and that we condole with her parents and rela-
tives in their great loss intermingled with the
joy and hope of a glorious resurrection of the
departed,
	And be it resolved, that we present a copy
of these resolutions to her parents, and that a
copy be sent to the WOMAN'S EXPONENT for
publication, and that they be spread upon our
record book, that we may be reminded of and
emulate her virtues and inestimable good
qualities in our lives, that we may meet her in
heaven.

Dearest sister mild and lovely,
	May thy virtues we possess;
Farewell, sister may we meet you
	And be numbered with the blest.
		MARGARET A. LAMBERT, Prest.

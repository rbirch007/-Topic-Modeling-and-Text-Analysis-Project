SPRINGVILLE, Y. L. M. I. A.

	Resolutions of respect on the death of Sister
Janet Whitmore, adopted at a meeting of the
Y. L. M. I. A., March 15th, 1882.
	Whereas, our heavenly Father in his wisdom
has seen fit to call from our midst our much
loved and respected sister, and,
	Whereas, our departed friend and associate
was worthy our highest regard and affection, her
labors in our Association having been faithfully
performed, we feel that some proper recognition
of her virtues be had among us:
	Therefore, be it resolved that we, as members
of the Y. L. M. I. A., deeply mourn the loss of
so valuable and faithful a co-laborer.
	Resolved, that we hereby extend our heartfelt
sympathy and condolence to her husband and
family, and that we condole with each other in
this sad affliction.
	Resolved, that we present a copy of these res-
olutions to her relatives, and publish the same
in the EXPONENT, and the Ladies Speaker, and
and place the same on our record.—Com.

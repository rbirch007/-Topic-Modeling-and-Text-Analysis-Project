OH GOD SAVE NAUVOO.
When we pray for all blessings to equally flow,
For the gathering and kingdom of Christ here below,
For the good of all people, the Mormon and Jew,
For a more perfect union we’ll pray without
ceasing—
O God save Nauvoo.
When you pray for old Israel, now scattered afar.
For the nations and kingdoms degraded by war,
For the world in its blindness and wickedness too.
For redemption as promised, then pray without doubt-
ing—
	O God save Nauvoo.
When you pray for your foes, both without and within
For the captive in prison, the exile in sin,
When you enter your closet as Christ told you to,
And ask of the Father, then pray in the Spirit—
	O God save Nauvoo.

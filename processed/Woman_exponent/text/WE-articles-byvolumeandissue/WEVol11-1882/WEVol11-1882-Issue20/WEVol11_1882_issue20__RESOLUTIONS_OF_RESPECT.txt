RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Resolutions of respect to the memory of Sister Ellen
Condie, who departed this life Febuary 21st, 1883, aged
seventy years.
	The following preamble and resolutions were unani-
mously adopted by the members of the Fourth Ward
Relief Society.
	Whereas, an Allwise Providence has called from us,
by the hand of death, our beloved and respected sister;
	Resolved, that we recognize in the death of our Sister
Ellen Condie, the loss of an able, efficient and worthy
teacher and co-laborer in the Fourth Ward Relief Society.
	Resolved, that we esteem her past faithful labors and
devotion to duty as worthy the fellowship and highest
respect of us all.
	Resolved, that we condole and deeply sympathize


with the bereaved family and friends In the loss of &
hind wife, affectionate mother, worthy teacher and a
good and respected citizen.
	Resolved, that these resolutions be spread upon our
record, and that a copy be furnished the bereaved
family, also the WOMAN'S EXPONENT, for publication.
							MRS. SUSAN SPERRY.
							M. E. TAYLOR, Secretary.
	Salt Lake City, March 4th, 1883.

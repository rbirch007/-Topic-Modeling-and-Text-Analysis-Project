TO THE OFFICERS AND MEMBERS OF THE
	CHURCH OF JESUS CHRIST OF LATTER
	DAY SAINTS:
	Meetings will be held in the Large Tabernacle
in this city, on Thursday, April 5th, 1883, at 10
o'clock in the morning and 2 o'clock in the after-
noon, preparatory to the Annual Conference,
which will conmence on Friday, April 6th, at
10 o'clock in the morning, as per previous ad-
journment.
					JOHN TAYLOR,
					GEORGE Q. CANNON,
					JOSEPH F. SMITH,
	First Presidence of the Church of Jesus
		Crist of Latter-Day Saints.
	Salt Lake City,
			March 20th, 1883.

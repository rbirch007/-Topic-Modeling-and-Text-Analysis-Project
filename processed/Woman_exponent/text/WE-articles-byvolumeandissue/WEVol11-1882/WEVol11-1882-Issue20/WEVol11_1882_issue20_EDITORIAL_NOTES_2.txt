 AMONG other ways in which the Auerbach
Bros, celebrated the wedding of their niece, Miss

$50, and wishing the long continued usefulness
of that benevolent institution.


Evan Stephens’ Concert in the Salt Lake
Theatre, March 17th, which was repeated in
the Salt Lake Assembly Hall, April 5th, was a
grand success. The most beautiful sight one
could imagine was two hundred little choristers
on the platform, their sweet, fresh young voices
making harmonious melody in the most appro-
priate pieces. Mr. Stephens richly deserves the
congratulations that are showered upon him
from all sides. He has done a wonderful work,
in training the little ones in the beautiful science
of music, and he never wearies of his continuous
labors in that direction. Mr. Stephens is, with-
out doubt, a genius, and he composes music
with the greatest ease. He has ability and per-
severance, and with these he ought to excel in
the rank of composers.

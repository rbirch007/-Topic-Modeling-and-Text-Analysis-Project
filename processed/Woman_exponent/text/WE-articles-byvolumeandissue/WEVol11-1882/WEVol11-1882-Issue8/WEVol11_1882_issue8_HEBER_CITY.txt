HEBER CITY.

	The Conference of the Relief Societies of
Wasatch Stake convened in Heber City Hall,
Aug. 11, 1882. Present were, Pres. Emma
Brown, her Second Counselor, Mary Daybell,
the Pres. of the several societies, local author-
ities. Pres. Hatch, T. H. Giles, Bishop Mur-
dock and other brethren. After the usual op-
ening exercises, the minutes of the previous
Conference were read and accepted. Written
reports were read by the Secretary. The sev-
eral wards were then represented. East Heb-
er, Mrs. Rasband; West Heber, Mrs. Foreman,
Midway, Mrs. Gurney; Wallsbury; Mrs. Clif-
ton; Charleston, Mrs. Taylor; Center, Mrs.
Richardson. The Primary wes well repres-
ented, all seeming to be in a prosperous con-
dition. Conference adjourned until 2 o'clock,
singing and Benediction.

A LOVING TRIBUTE FROM LULA.

Blessings! Blessings! This we repeat:
From thy silver crowned head to the soles of thy feet,
On thy head, which hath weathered the storms and the
	heat,
With the prophets of God in their exiled retreat;
Thine eyes which have turned not from hardships and
	toil,
To view thy lost treasures, thine enemies spoil,
Thy cheeks which have glowed with the spirit of light,
From Him in whose blood are thy garments washed
	white:
Thy lips which have valiantly heralded truth,
In the scorners proud face, since the days of thy youth;
Thy neck, which hath bent not in servitude mean,
To the foes of thy God, howe'er threat'ning the scene.
Thy arms and thy shoulders, thy lungs and thy heart,
Thy body entire—yes, every part,
Be blessed and renewed, and strengthened to-day,
And with thee continue these blessings alway,
To bless thee, our sister, our mother, and friend,
Our voices and hearts here in harmony blend;
And thousands besides, whom thy goodness hath blest,
Will never forget thee, and sure is thy rest.
'Mong the jewels which Jesus will "make up" and claim
We shall see thy dear face, we shall read thy loved
	name.
Now, for all that thy soul can desire we pray,
With "many bright happy returns of the day,"
	September 7th, 1882.

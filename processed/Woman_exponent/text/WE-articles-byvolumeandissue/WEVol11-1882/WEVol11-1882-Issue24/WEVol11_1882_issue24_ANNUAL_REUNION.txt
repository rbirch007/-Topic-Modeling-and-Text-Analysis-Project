ANNUAL REUNION.

	THE yearly meeting of the Y. L. Association
of the First Ward, was held on April 25th, 1883,
A number of the leading Sisters were present,
Sisters E. R. S. Smith, M. I. Horne, M. Y.
Dougal, Margaret P. Young, M. A. Freeze.
	A dinner was prepared for them by the offi-
cers and members of the Association at Prest.
Sarah Graham's. Bishop Joseph Warburton
being present; it was a very pleasant time, all
seemed to enjoy themselves. After dinner all
went to the meeting house. where many more
were waiting. President Sarah Graham presi-
ded over the meeting. Some excellent instruc-
tions were given to the young and old, which
will never be forgotten. Counselor A. Steel
made a few good remarks, the Bishop closed
with some good counsel to all—an excellent
spirit prevailed.		LOTTIE E. ARGUST,
								Secretary.

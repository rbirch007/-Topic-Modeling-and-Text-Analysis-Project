EMERY STAKE.
	Report of the organization of the Relief So-
cieties and Primary Associations of Emery
Stake of Zion.
	Relief Society of Castle Dale Ward, organ-
ized August 24th, 1882, under the direction
of the President of the Stake, assisted by Presi-
dent Anna M. Larsen and Counselor Joana
Childs. Anna M. Larsen, President; Hannah
Seely and Harriet Wilcox; Counselors; Lucinda 
Jeffs, Secretary; Violet Wilcox, Assistant Sec-
retary; Ellen Miller, Treasurer; Caroline Lar-
sen, head teacher; and six visiting teachers;
Officers, thirteen; Members, twenty-five.
	Primary Association of Castle Dale Ward,
organized January 6th, 1883. Stena Olsen, 
President; Caroline Larsen and Annena M.
Justisen, Counselors; Sarah Seely, Secretary;
Franklin P. Larsen, Assistant Secretary; Mens
Petersen, Treasurer; Officers, six; Members,
twenty-nine.
	Relief Society of Orangeville Ward, organ-
ized August 29th, 1882, Caroline Curtis, Presi-


dent; Sarah A. Fulmer and Ellen Robertsen,
Counselors; Joanna P. Curtis, Secretary; Eliza-
beth Reid, Treasurer; Officers, twelve; Mem-
bers, thirty-seven.
	Primary Association of Moab Ward, organ-
ized February 21st, 1883, by President C. G.
Larsen and Council. Betsy Burnham, Presi-
dent; Laura Taylor and Sarah A. Stewart,
Counselors; Eunice Pritchett, Secretary;
George F. Burnham, Assistant Secretary; Ro-
sanna, Stewart, Treasurer; Officers, six; Mem-
bers, fifty two.
	Primary Association of Huntington Ward,
organized November 25th, 1882, by President
Anna M. Larsen. Margaret Crandall, Presi-
dent; Emma Leonard and Laura Neilson,
Counselors; John F. Wakefield, Secretary;
Hannah Johnson, Assistant Secretary; Susan
J. Wakefield, Treasurer; Officers, six; Mem-
bers, seventy-three.
	President C. G. Larsen and Counselors, also
the Bishops and Counselors of their respective
wards, were present at most of the organiza-
tions, and blessed and set apart the officers.
			ANNA M. LARSEN, President.
			ANNA E. SEELY, Secretary.

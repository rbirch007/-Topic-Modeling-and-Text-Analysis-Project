IN MEMORIAM

	Sister Cynthia Durfee died at her home in Beaver
Dams, Box Elder Co., Utah, February the 20th, 1883,
aged seventy three years, two months and twenty days.
Deceased was born in the State of Vermont, and was
baptized into the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day
Saints, in the year 1830, and has passed through a great
many trying scenes in the Church, but she has ever been
true and faithful to the Gospel, never doubting its truth.
She was the wife of Francillo Durfee, one of the Mor-
mon Battalion. Long may her good example live in the
hearts of her children and friends.
	Resolutions of respect on the death of Sister Cynthia
Durfee, adopted at a meeting of the Relief Society of
Beaver Dams, held March 1st, 1883.
	Whereas, God in his wisdom has seen fit to call from
our midst our much loved and worthy teacher, and, 
	Whereas, her labors in our Association have been
faithfully performed, we feel that some proper recogni-
tion of her virtues be recognized among us, therefore
be it,
	Resolved, that we as members of the Relief Society
do deeply mourn the loss of so valuable and faithful a
co-laborer.
	Resolved, that we condole with each other in this be-
reavement, and tender this token of respect and love to
her relatives and friends in this affliction.
	Resolved, that we present a copy of these resolutions
to her relatives, and have the same published in the
WOMAN'S EXPONENT, and keep a copy of the same on
our record.	SARAH A. DURFEE,
			President Relief Society.
Collinston, Utah.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	We the sisters of the Relief Society of the Second
Ward, Salt Lake City, unanimously adopt the following
Resolutions:
	Whereas, it has pleased an alwise Providence to call
from our midst by the hand of death, our beloved sister
and friend, Therefore,
	Resolved, that we do recognize in the death of our
Sister Caroline Iverson, the loss of an able, efficient and
faithful member of our Society.
	Resolved, that we esteem her past faithful labors and
devotion to duty as worthy the fellowship and emula-
tion of us all. 
	Resolved, that we condole and deeply sympathize
with the bereaved husband, children, relatives and
friends, in the loss of a loving wife, affectionate mother
and a true friend.
	Resolved, that these Resolutions be spread upon the
records of the Relief Society of the Second Ward, and
that a copy of them be sent to the bereaved husband,
also that a copy be sent to the WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
					SARAH WHEELER, President.
					SARAH MCGREGAR, Secretary.

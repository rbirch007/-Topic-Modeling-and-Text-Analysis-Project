								Sec. Y. L. M. I. A.

Addie now is sweetly sleeping;
	In the tomb she's laid to rest,
But her spirit watch is keeping,
	For she dwells among the blest.

Though her body now is resting
	Free from weary pain and care,
She rejoices in bright mansions
	With the just and righteous there.

Then cheer up and cease your weeping,
	She has reached that "happy shore,"
Where no grief or sickness enters,
	And there's rest forevermore.

Hooper,							JULIA BAKER.

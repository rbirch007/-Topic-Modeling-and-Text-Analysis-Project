IN MEMORIAM.

	Died, in Hooper City, Weber County, Utah, November
19, 1882, Adelaide Johnson, daughter of Theodore and
Sarah P. Cook Johnson.
Deceased was born in Salt Lake City, March 30, 1866,
and was in her 17th year at the time of her demise. She
was a faithful member of the Y. L. M. I.A., and loved
and respected by all who knew her. She leaves a de-
voted mother and brother, with many relatives and dear
friends, to mourn her loss. She was a kind and obedi-
ent daughter, and her last words were to comfort her
mother.
	Hooper.			JESSIE MANNING,

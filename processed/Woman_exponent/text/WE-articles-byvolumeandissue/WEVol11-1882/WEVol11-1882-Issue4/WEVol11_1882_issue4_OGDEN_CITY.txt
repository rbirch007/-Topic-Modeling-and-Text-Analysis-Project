OGDEN CITY.
	Dear Sister.-- The Ladies Relief Society of
Weber Stake held their Conference on June
8th.  The meeting was conducted by Prest.
Jane S. Richards.  Commenced with the usual
exercises of singing and prayer.  The minutes
of the previous meeting were read and adopt-
ed.  Sister Richards made a few excellent re-
marks, explaining the object of the gathering
and expressing great pleasure at having the
privilege of again meeting with her brothers
and sisters in conference.
	We also had the pleasure of Sisters E. R.
Snow Smith and Zina D. Young's company at
this time.  Both expressed great pleasure at

[Column 3]
being permitted to meet with this Stake of
Zion. Spoke at some length on various sub-
jects of great interest to us as a people, giving
us good and wise counsel.  They also related
some very interesting reminiscenes regarding
the experiences of the Saints in early days.
They blessed this stake of Zion, hoping it
would prosper and make progress in all things
pertaining to the kingdom of God.
	Bishop Robert McQuarie next made a few
brief and excellent remarks.
	The meeting then adjourned with singing
and benediction until two P. M.
	When it was continued there was a large
assemblage present;  all seemed to enjoy the
good spirit that prevailed.
	The conference was adjourned with singing
and prayer, for three months.
			Respectfully

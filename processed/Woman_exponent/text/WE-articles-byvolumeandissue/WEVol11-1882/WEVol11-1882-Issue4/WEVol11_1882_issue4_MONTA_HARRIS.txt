					MONTA HARRIS.

								May 23, 1882.
	Annual meeting of the Primary Associations
of South Cottonwood.  Meeting called to
order by our president Sister Elizabeth Davis.
Singing.  Opening prayer by Brother Christian
Steffeson.  Singing by the children.
	There were present as visitors, Sister E. B.
Ferguson, Bishop Joseph Rawlins and many
of the children's parents.  The meeting con-
sisted of recitations and songs;  and Sister
Ferguson gave a breif sketch of her life while
away from Utah.  Others gave good instruc-
tions to the children. In the afternoon there
was a picnic and party.
			Elizabeth Davis, President,
			Emily Brinton, Secretary.

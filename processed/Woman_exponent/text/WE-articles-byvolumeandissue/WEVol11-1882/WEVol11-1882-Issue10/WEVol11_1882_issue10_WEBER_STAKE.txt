WEBER STAKE.

	DEAR SISTER WELLS,—The ladies of the
Relief Society—including the Y. L. M. I., and
Primary Associations, held their Conference on
Sept. 7th, 8th and 9th, insts., which were all
well attended considering the busy season.
The Spirit of the Lord was indeed with us, and
we received much good and wise counsel from
those who addresed us, which, if practiced,
would make us better and nobler men and
women. The Conferences were adjourned for
three months. Please excuse a more lengthy
report this time. Respectfully,
						MONTA HARRIS.
	Ogden.

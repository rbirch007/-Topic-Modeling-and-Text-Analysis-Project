"LOVE PREVAILS OVER ALL"

In the fresh bloom of the apple tree,
	Robins sing merry this morn;
My hear is as happy as theirs can be,
	For love to me is just born.

The skies bend down with tenderest blue
	More golden seem the hours;
All nature takes a brighter hue,
	Deeper fragrance the flowers.

The rippling brook, and the babbling rill,
	Whisper tales of love to me.
the dashing waves bear the same sweet song,
	From ocean, or deep blue sea.

The voice of bird, and the flower's bloom,
	Mystic rustling of the pines
Breathes music, glad with my heart in tune,
	As I kneel at love's pure shrine.

The meadow is donning brightest robes,
	And down in the earth's dark mould,
Numberless agents, busy at work,
	Are producing green and gold.

Fairies are spinning gossamer threads.
	Weaving strange fabrics of art;
Love, a palace is rearing instead,
	Down in the depths of my heart.

The world is lovelier, fairer far,
	Than ever to me before;
Since in my heart has risen love's star,
	Why need I on earth crave more?

L. M. H.

		RELOLUTIONS OF RE

	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has seen fit to
call from our midst our worthy and beloved sister
and co-laborer, Caroline Detton, therefore be it
	Resolved, That we strive to emulate her devo-
tion to her home, her purity and honesty of pur-
pose, and her faith in the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
	Resolved, That we extend our heartfelt sympa-
thy to the stricken husband and family, and sin-
cerely hope our Heavenly Father will send the
comforting influence of the Holy Spirit to heal
the wounded spirits of those who are called to
mourn.
	Resolved, That a coppy of these resolutions be
sent to THE WOMAN’S EXPONENT and a copy of
them be sent to her family.

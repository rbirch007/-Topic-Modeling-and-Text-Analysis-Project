THE EDITOR.


Mrs. Wynne, wife of the new postmaster,
general, was married when very young-
and it is said that her first long dress was
her wedding gown.
	Miss Mary Scott, State Superintendent of
Public Instruction in Idaho, is vice-presi-
dent of the Inland Empire Teachers’ Asso-
ciation, and was prominent at the seventh
annual meeting lately held at Pendleton,
Oregon.

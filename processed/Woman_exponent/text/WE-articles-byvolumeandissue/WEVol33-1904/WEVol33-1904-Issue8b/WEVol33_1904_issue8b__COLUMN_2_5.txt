	Lesson 3rd. Economy and Industry.
	1. Economy and Industry are unfailing
sources of temporal prosperity. 2. Econ-
omy of strength, of time and of money is
absolutely necessary to success. 3. Thou
shall not be idle: For he that is idle shall
not eat the bread nor wear the garments
of the laborer. Doc. & Cov. Sec. 42-42.
Doc. & Cov. Sec. 72-29. Proverbs 19: 15.
II Thess. 3-10-11-12. 4. Begin early in life
to teach a child to love manual labor: the
educational value of work.
	Lesson 4th. Tithing and Fast Offerings.
	1. By the law of Tithing the land of Zion
may be sanctified unto the Lord. Doc. &
Cov. Sec. 119: 6. 2. Origin of the law of
Tithing, Lev. 27-30 to 34. Gen. 14 18-19
20. 3. Meaning of the term Tithing. Doc.
& Cov. Sec. 119, Mal, 3: 10-11, Prov. 3: 9-
10. 4. When and to whom Tithing should
be paid. 5. Promise to those who are
tithed. Doc. & Cov. Sec. 64-23. 6. Potency
of the law of Tithing. 7. Fast offerings. In
as much as ye impart of your substance to
the poor ye will do it unto me. Doc. &
Cov. Sec. 42. 30-31.
	Lesson 5th. Word of Wisdom and prepar-
ation of Food.
	1. Health, wisdom and knowledge are
promised to all those who observe the word
of wisdom and the destroying angel shall
pass them by. Doc. & Cov. Sec. 89.
Dan. 1: 6-21 inclusive. 2. The word of
Wisdom was given first as a principle with
a promise, but since that time it has been
given as a commandment. 3. Show the
injury and disadvantage resulting from non-
observance of this commandment. 4. Food
should be clean, wholesome, and well
cooked. 5. Avoid the use of fermented
liquors and strong spices in seasoning lest
an appetite be created in the youth for
strong drink.

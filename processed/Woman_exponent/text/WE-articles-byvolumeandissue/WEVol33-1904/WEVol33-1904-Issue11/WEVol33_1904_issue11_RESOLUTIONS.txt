			RESOLUTIONS.
	Whereas the Supreme Ruler of the universe has
called from our midst our beloved Sister Sarah
Jane Halliday; and whereas she was a faithful
teacher in the Santaquin Ward Relief Society,
performing that duty in her advanced age, never
tiring in well doing. Therefore we desire to
honor her memory. Her charity never failed;
she was ever willing to care for t e sick and to
help the widow and fatherless, thereby gaining
the goodwill and affection of her associates. Her
great desire was to remain true and faithful to the
Gospel of Christ. This she has done, and labored
devotedly for the advancement of the work of
God.
	Therefore, be it resolved by the sisters of the
Santaquin Ward Relief Society that we tender to
her bereaved sons and daughters our heartfelt
sympathy, and trust that they will be comforted
in the assurance that their mother is not dead,
but has gone to a brighter and better world; be it
further
	Resolved, that a copy of these resolutions be
enrolled upon the records of the Society, and also
published in the WOMAN’S EXPONENT.
			ALICE ELLSWORTH, Secretary.
	Santaquin, Utah, May 13 1905.

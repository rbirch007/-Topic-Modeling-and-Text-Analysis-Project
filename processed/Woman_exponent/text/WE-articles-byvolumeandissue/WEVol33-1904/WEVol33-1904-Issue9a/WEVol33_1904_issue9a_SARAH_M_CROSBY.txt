				SARAH M. CROSBY.

Lecture III.— What to teach our Boys.
	(a) Should teach them to have faith in
our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, that He
will hear and answer prayers, love for our
religion and other instructions. Teach
them purity, energy, and that good habits
are worth more than riches. (b) To have
an object or purpose in life and to work with
a will to accomplish that purpose. (c)
Honor, refer to the fifth commandment. (d)
That marriage is ordained of God unto man.
	(1st) Explain the action of work on de-
veloping morals. (2nd) Explain the action
of food on muscle and brain. (3rd) Ex-
plain the action of tobacco and liquor on
muscle and brain. (4th) Explain the action
of tea, coffee, high seasoned food. (5th)
Show how diseases of one part of the body
make all the body ill by illustration.

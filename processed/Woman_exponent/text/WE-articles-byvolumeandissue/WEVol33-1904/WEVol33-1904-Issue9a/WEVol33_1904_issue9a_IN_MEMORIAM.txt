IN MEMORIAM.

Whereas: Our Dear Sister Sophia T.
Nuttall has been called upon to mourn the
loss of her companion, Elder L John Nut-
tall;
Be it resolved; that we the members of the 17th
Ward Relief Society extend our love and sym-
pathy to herself and family praying that our
Heavenly Father will pour out His Holy Spirit in
abundance upon them to bless and comfort their
wounded hearts; may the husband’s and father’s
influence remain with them throughout their lives
to replace in part his presence.
Resolved that we as a Society do mourn his loss
on account of his devotion to Relief Society work
being ever willing to assist us with his worthy
counsel and noble advice:

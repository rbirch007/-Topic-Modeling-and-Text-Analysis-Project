EDITORIAL NOTES. 

	The minutes of the General Conference
of the Relief Society will be published in
the May number of the paper.

	Some othe other matter deaths, etc., that
are left over will be used next issue.

	Those who send articles for publication
in the paper will please put the name of the
writer, it is quite impossible to guess at
the right signature and each one writing an
article for publication should sign her own
name or nom de plume if she so desires.

AN EASTER SONG.

In Easter time the breezes blow,
In Easter time the lilies grow,
The sun gives forth a warmer glow.
In Easter time.

In Easter time the robins sing.
In Easter time the swallows bring
Into their nests a wee warm thing
In Easter time.

In Easter time young hearts beat true,
In Easter time fair lover’s woo, 
Awakening thoughts so old, yet new
In Easter time.

In Easter time the world is bright,
In Easter time earth breathes delight,
For past and gone is winter’s blight,
In Easter time.

A. W. C.

			LAURA M. RAPPLEYE.

Lecture VIII.—Obedience.
	Necessity of its Enforcements.
		From childhood to girlhood
		Maid mother and wife
		All need to be obedient
		To guide them properly through life.
	1st. Why should the lesson of Obedience
be taught in early childhood.
	2nd. How will it effect the progress of a
Latter-day Saint?
	3rd. Why is it necessary for our spiritual
progress?
	4th. How should Obedience be taught?

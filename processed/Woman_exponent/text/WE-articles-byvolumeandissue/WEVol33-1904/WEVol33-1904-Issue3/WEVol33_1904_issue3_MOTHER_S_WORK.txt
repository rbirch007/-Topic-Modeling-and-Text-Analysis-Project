MOTHER’S WORK.
		MARICOPA STAKE.
Lecture I.— Woman, Know Thyself:
	“Oh, Woman! Woman! know thyself,
		And know thy mission too:
	Thou comest from thy Maker’s hand
		A mighty work to do.”
							E. R. Snow.
	a—Necessity of studying well your own
character before undertaking responsibility
of wife and mother. Ref. Imp. Era, vol.
5, No. 3, p. 184. Young Woman’s Journal

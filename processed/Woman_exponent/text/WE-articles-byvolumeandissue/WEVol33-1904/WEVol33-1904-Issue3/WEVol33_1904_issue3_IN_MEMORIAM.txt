IN MEMORIAM. 
	Sister Mary M. Findlay Mathews was the daugh-
ter of Allen M. and Jeanetta Ireland Findlay. 
She was born in Salt Lake City April 4, 1862. She 
came to Panacca, Nev., with her parents in the 
year 1872. On February 20, 1884, she was married 
to Philip Mathews. In the year 1892 she was 
chosen as secretary of the Panacca Ward Relief 
Society, which position she filled with equity and
justice till her death, which occurred July 21, 
1904. She leaves a devoted husband, four chil-
dren, a brother and sister, besides a host of other 
relatives and friends to mourn her loss.

ON BEHALF OF PEACE AND ARBITRATION.

	The Demonstration for Peace set as an
Annual Festival to be celebrated on each
recurring 18th of May was last spring, for
good and sufficient reason postponed. This
Demonstration will take place on October
8th. A letter has been issued to the Presi-
dents of National Organizations affiliated
with the National Council and to Presi-
dents of Local Councils and to members
of the Committee which we hope will result
in the following preparations for the Dem-
onstration.
	First: Clergymen throughout the coun-
try will be asked to devote one service on
Sunday, October 2nd to some phase of the
general subject of Peace and Arbitration.

Boston on Monday, October 3d, the pastors

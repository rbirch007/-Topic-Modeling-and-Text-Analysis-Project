LECTURE IV.
D. CARE OF CHILDREN.
	1. Physical needs. (a) Hygienic laws
observed. (b) The word of wisdom a law
to all Saints. (c) Proper living lays the
foundation for strong character.
	2. Moral and spiritual training. (a) Obed-
ence essential to development of character-
(b) Cooperation of parents necessary. (c)
Qualities to be cultivated—love, patience,
cheerfulness, kindness, contentment, etc.
(d) The brother and sister in the home.

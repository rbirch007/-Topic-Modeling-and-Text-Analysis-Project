Death of body; not of spirit;
Ay! there is no death of soul.

Search the records and traditions,
Search the present and the past;
Science, logic, reason, wisdom,
Says this life is not the last.

Near God’s throne are lines of angels,
One would make their ranks complete;
So they came and took our brother,
There to live a life most sweet.

Is it safe to doubt the wisdom?
Can we always judge aright?
Be consoled, subm[-]t, don’t murmur,

Life is but a fading shadow
Passing swiftly year by year;
Then we’ll meet the one departed
In that high and holy sphere.

Grieve no more, and let not sorrow
Mar the soul you have to save,
There will be a joyous meeting,
For there’s life beyond the grave.

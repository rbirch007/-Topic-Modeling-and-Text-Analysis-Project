			WASATCH STAKE.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The half yearly conference for the Relief So-
ciety of Wasatch Stake was held in the Heber
Hall, May 6th. On the stand were Prest. Emma
Brown and Counselors, presidents of the various
branches, Prest. Abram Hatch and Coun.
T. H. Giles of the Stake Presidency, several
of our bishops and other brethren. Services
began by the choir singing, “God spoke the
word.” Prayer offered by Bishop R. S. Duke.
Minutes of former conference read and adopted.
Our Stake contains nine branch organizations,
seven of which were represented as in a flour-
ishing condition.
	Prest. Brown in her opening remarks gave
some well-timed instructions to the secretaries.
	Bro. Giles followed with the same subject,
and gave a few hints regarding the correct fill-
ing out of the blanks.
	Our conference was attended by a large num-
ber of sisters. The remarks were such as
tended to strengthen and encourage all who
felt desirous of being built up in the Kingdom

[Column 2]
of God. Several of the brethren also addressed
us, and theiir remarks were well appreciated.
	The conference then adjourned for six months.
The choir sang an anthem. Benediction by T.
H. Giles.

EDITOR EXPONENT:

	Knowing that you have not lately heard
from us, the young ladies of Moab, I thought
I would write you a few lines:
	We were organized a few years ago, but for
some reason did not prosper. We were there-
fore lately reorganized, and our society is now
in a flourishing condition, with Fanny Standi-
ford as President; Priscilla Warner and Dell
Darrow, Counselors; Clara Burnham, Secretary;
Rose Stuart, Treasurer. We are having nice
weather, the fruit trees at this date being all
in bloom. We have general good health, and
withal a splendid climate.
	The EXPONENT is a very welcome visitor, I
do not know what we would do without it.
				Yours,	
					FANNY STANDIFORD,

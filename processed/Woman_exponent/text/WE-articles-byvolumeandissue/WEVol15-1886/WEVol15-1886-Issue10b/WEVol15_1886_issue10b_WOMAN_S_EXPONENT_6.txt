WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


What we all fall in love with as a race, is in
most cases efficiency and ability. What we
fall in love with individually is, I believe, our
moral, mental and physical complement. Not
our like, not our counterpart; quite the con-
trary; within healthy limits, our unlike and
our opposite,—Grant Allen.

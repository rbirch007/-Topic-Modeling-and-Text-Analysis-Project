IN MEMORIAM.

	It is with feelings of great sorrow that we record the
death of our dear sister, Josephine, beloved daughter of
Neils M, and Mattie C. Peterson.  She was born Feb 15,
1869, and died Dec. 19, 1886.  She was counselor in the 
First Ward Primary Association of Richfield, Sevier Co.
Her great ambition was to gain knowledge, and during
her sickness, which lasted nine days, she bore a faithful
testimony several times to this being the true Church of
Jesus Christ.

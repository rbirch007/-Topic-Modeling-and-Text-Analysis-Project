104			WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


From the annual report of the New York
Exchange for Women’s Work it appears
that during the past year nearly $40,000 has
been paid to consignors of work in the several
departments, which include all varieties of
painting and fine needle and lace work. In
the order department 1,100 orders were re-
ceived and filled. The exclusive object of
this society is to assist reduced “gentlewomen."

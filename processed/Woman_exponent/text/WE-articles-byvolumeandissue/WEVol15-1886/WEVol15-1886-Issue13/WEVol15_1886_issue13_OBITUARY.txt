OBITUARY.

To the memory of Elizabeth Dutsen, who departed this
life of old age at Goshen, Utah Co, August 19th, 1886,
after laying upon her bed suffering with paralysis for one
year and ten months, during which time her daughter, Sis-
ter Mary Johnsen, our beloved President, waited upon her
with untiring zeal. Our aged sister was baptized by Presi-
dent Wilford Woodruff in 1840, in Herefordshire, England.
She emigrated to Utah in 1862. Was the mother of eleven
children, grandmother of forty, and numerous great-grand-
children. She endured many trials for the sake of the
Gospel; embraced the law of celestial marriage, and died
at the advanced age of eighty-three years in full faith and
hope of a glorious resurrection.
	We feel to acknowledge the hand of God in relieving her
of her sufferings, in taking her from this weary world to a
higher and better one.
	Resolved, That we deeply sympathize with her children
and friends; and further, be it
	Resolved, That these Resolutions be spread upon the
record of the Relief Society, and that a copy be sent to the
EXPONENT, and also her family.
						MARY DEDDS,
						LOUISA TAYLOR, Committee.

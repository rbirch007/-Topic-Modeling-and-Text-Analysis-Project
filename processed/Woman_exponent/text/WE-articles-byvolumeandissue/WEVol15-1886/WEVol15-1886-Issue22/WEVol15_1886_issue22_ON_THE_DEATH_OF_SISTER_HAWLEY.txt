ON THE DEATH OF SISTER HAWLEY.

	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father in His infinite wisdom
has seen fit to remove from our midst by the hand of death
our beloved sister, Jennie Hawley,
	Whereas, Our sister, by her kind, sympathetic nature,
and faithfulness to the cause of truth, has won the hearts of
all those who knew her worth, therefore, be it
	Resolved, That we, the members of the Mesa Relief
Society, do hereby tender our love and esteem to the mem-
ory of our departed sister, and we realize that in her we
have lost a true friend and a faithful co-worker in the ever-
lasting Gospel.
	Resolved, That we extend our heartfelt sympathy to the
bereaved husband and family of the deceased, and feel to
express an earnest hope that the Holy Comforter will im-
part to them consolation and comfort in this hour of their
deep sorrow.
	Resolved, That a copy of these Resolutions be sent to
the family of the deceased, a copy to the EXPONENT for
publication, and also placed on the Society records.
		ELIZABETH G. MCDONALD,
		ELEANOR MORRIS,
		MABEL ANN HAKES,
		SARAH M. POMEROY,
			Committee.

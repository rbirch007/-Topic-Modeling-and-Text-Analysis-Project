EDITORIAL NOTES.

	Dr. Elvira S. Barney exhibited in this office
a Tape Worm, 31 feet in length, taken this week
from a young man about 17 years old. She makes
this one of her specialties. This is an item
worth knowing when many have suffered for years
without knowing any means for relief, as did this
young man for five years. She says she will
guarantee a removal within 24 hours after com-
mencing treatment.

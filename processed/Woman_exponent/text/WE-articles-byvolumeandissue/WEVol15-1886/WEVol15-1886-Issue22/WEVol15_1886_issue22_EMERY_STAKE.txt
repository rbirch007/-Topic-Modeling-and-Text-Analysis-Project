EMERY STAKE.
	The Eighth Semi-annual Conference of the
Relief Society of Emery Stake was held at
Huntington, on Friday, April 1st, commencing
at 10 a.m., Prest. Anna U. Larsen presiding.
After singing, prayer was offered by Bro.
Johnson. Minutes of previous conference
read and approved.
	Prest. Anna U. Larsen, in her opening re-
marks, spoke of the duties of the Society in
caring for the sick, and doing all they could
for those who needed assistance. She advised
all to be careful in training their children, not
neglecting to teach them the principles of the
Gospel, and thought if we lived as we should
we would have no need to fear our enemies.
	The presidents of the different branches
gave verbal reports, showing that the members
were desirous of doing all in their power to
advance the cause of truth.
	A few of the older sisters related some of
their experience in the Church, and the trials
they had passed through; they could see the
sayings of the Prophet Joseph being fulfilled
every day, and their remarks, as well as those 
of the brethren who were present, were en-
couraging and replete with good counsel and
instruction, showing a determination to uphold
all the principles revealed for our exaltation,
and put their trust in the Lord.
	A good spirit was with us, and peace pre-
vailed, all feeling blessed in meeting together

[Column 2]

Conference adjourned till the 7th of October.
Benediction by Bro. Jensen.

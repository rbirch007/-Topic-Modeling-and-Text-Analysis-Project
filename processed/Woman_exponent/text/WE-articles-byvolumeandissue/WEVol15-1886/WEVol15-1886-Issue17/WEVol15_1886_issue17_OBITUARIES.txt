OBITUARIES.

	To the memory of Margaret Selmen, born July 23rd,
1814, at King's Wood, Gloucestershire, England. Was
baptized in 1850. Emigrated to Utah in the year 1860.
Was the mother of nine children, eight of whom are living,
five boys and three girls; two of them are in England.
Had forty living grandchildren. Deceased died in Payson
on the 5th of April, 1886, in full faith of the Gospel and
with a sure hope of a glorious resurrection with the just.

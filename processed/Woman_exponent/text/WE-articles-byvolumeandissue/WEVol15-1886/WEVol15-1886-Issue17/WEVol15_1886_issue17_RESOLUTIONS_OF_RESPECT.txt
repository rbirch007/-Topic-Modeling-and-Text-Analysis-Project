	RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT,

	Whereas, it has pleased God in His infinite wisdom to
remove from our midst, by the hand of death, our worthy
and beloved sister, Margaret Selmen,
	Whereas, the intimate relations held during many years
by her with the members of the Relief Society make it
fitting that we record our appreciation of her.
	Resolved, That the sad removal of our sister from our
midst, creates a sorrow that is felt by all members of the
Society in the departure of a good and noble sister.
	Resolved, That with the deepest sympathy, with the
family we express an earnest hope that God will impart to
them consolation and comfort in the hour of their bereave-
ment and deep sorrow.
	Resolved, That these resolutions be spread on the record
of the Society.

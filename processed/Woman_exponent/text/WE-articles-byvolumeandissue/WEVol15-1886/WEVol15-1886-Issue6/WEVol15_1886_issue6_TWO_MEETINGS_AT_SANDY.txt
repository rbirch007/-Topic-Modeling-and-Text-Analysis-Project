TWO MEETINGS AT SANDY.

	On Tuesday morning, July 26th, Sisters M.
I. Horne, B. W. Smith and E. B. Wells visited
Sandy, to attend the sisters' meetings.
	The annual meeting of the Relief Society
was held at 10 a.m., Mrs. Wilhelmina Olsen
presiding. Minutes and report for the year
were read and accepted. The meeting was
addressed by Sisters Smith, Wells and Horne
on the various duties of the sisters, and the
condition of affairs at the present time. Prest.
Olsen made some appropriate remarks, and
Bishop Holman addressed the meeting for a
few minutes, expressing his interest in the pro-
ceedings. Benediction by the Bishop.
	In the afternoon the children assembled in
the Primary Association, and after some very
sweet singing, and prayer, the following pro-
gramme was given:
	Bro. James Brown and daughters, a song;
Recitation, William Lewis; Recitation, Rachel
Brown; Recitation, Dora Castle; Recitation,
Lottie Castle; Select Reading, Mary Alsop;
Song, Emma Bowen; Recitation, Annie Oster-
mann; Recitation, Betsey Geltea; Dialogue,
Dora Castle and Nettie Labrum; Recitation,
Elizabeth Brown; Recitation, Robert Scott;
Song, Bro. Brown and daughters.  After this
the sisters from the city addressed the child-
ren.

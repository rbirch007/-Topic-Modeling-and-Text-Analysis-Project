SYNOPSIS OF MINUTES.

	The quarterly conference of the Relief So-
ciety of this Stake of Zion was held in the
Fourteenth Ward Assembly Rooms, Thursday,
Dec. 9th, 1886, Mrs. M. I. Horne presiding.
After the usual opening exercises, the Secre-
tary called the roll; sixteen presidents, six
counselors, one treasurer and three teachers re-
sponded, representing twenty-six branches.
Minutes of previous conference read and ac-
cepted, after which a very affectionate and
interesting communication was read by the
Secretary from the President, E. R. S. Smith.

To the officers and members of the Relief So-
	ciety of the Salt Lake Stake of Zion, in Con-
	ference assembled, President M. I. Horne
	presiding.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Resolved, That we, the sisters of the Glenwood Relief
Society, do tender our love and esteem to the memory of
our departed sister; we will miss her in her accustomed
place; and also her many good counsels; we feel she has
indeed shown us, both old and young, an excellent ex-
ample of faithfulness, worthy of emulating, and we feel to
say, "Peace to her soul." God help us all to hold out so
faithfully to the end.
	Whereas, We condole with her friends in their bereave-
ment, yet we would not wish her back. Oh, how sweet is
her rest after so long and tiresome a journey of life.
	Resolved, That we spread these Resolutions on the
minutes of the Relief Society, and also send them to the
family and WOMAN'S EXPONENT for publication.
	SARAH JACOBS,
	PATIENCE PALMER, Committee.

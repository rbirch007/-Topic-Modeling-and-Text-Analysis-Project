	EASTERN ARIZONA STAKE.
	
	The quarterly conference of the Relief So-
ciety of the Eastern Arizona Stake was held
at Snowflake, September 10th, 1886. Prest.
Emma S. Smith presiding. After the usual
exercises, Sister Smith made opening remarks,
after which verbal reports of the branches of
Snowflake, ShowLow, Erastus and Taylor were
given by Sisters Mary H. Lawson, Harriet
Whipple, Clarissa Wilhelm and Elizabeth
Hawkins. The reports showed some improve-
ment, but sickness had been prevalent, and so
many deaths occuring, caused the people to
feel somewhat downcast. The presiding offi-
cers of the Stake, and also of the branches,
were presented, and sustained by unanimous
vote. The statistical and financial reports of
twelve branches were read.
	Sisters Jemima W. Smith, Elizabeth Pulsi-
pher and Mary Savage addressed the meeting,
and gave much good counsel and encourage-
ment to the sisters. Felt that unless we are
humble and prayerful we will not be able to
pass through the trials which are coming upon
the Saints.
	Prest. Emma S. Smith spoke upon the
good results obtained from having Relief So-
ciety meetings, and gave some instructions

[Column 2]
in regard to the manner of conducting them.
Counseled the mothers to teach their children
the principles of the Gospel, to make their
homes pleasant and agreeable, and to endeavor,
by divine assistance, to faithfully discharge the
duties of wife and mother.
	Prest. Jesse N. Smith made some remarks,
Expressed good wishes for the sisters in their
various walks of life, and encouraged them to
continue faithful in the work. Regarded the
training of children as a very pleasant task,
yet one which requires considerable patience,
care and study. Prayed the Lord to abundant-
ly bless us in our labors.
	Conference adjourned for three months.
Singing. Benediction pronounced by Brother
Samuel H. Rodgers.
	DELLA FISH, Stake Secretary.

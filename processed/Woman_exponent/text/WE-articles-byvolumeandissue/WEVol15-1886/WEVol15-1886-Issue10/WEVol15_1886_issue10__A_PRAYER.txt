A PRAYER.

Mighty God of earth and ocean,
	Hear us from Thy blest abode;
While we in faith, in deep devotion,
	Praise our great and gracious God;
Help us humbly, we implore Thee,
	Countless are Thy mercies here;
Mighty Prince of peace and glory!
	Bless our Prophet and our Seer.

We our shepherd are bewailing, 
	In his exile from the flock,
Let thy power, oh, God! prevailing,
	Plant him still on safety's rock;
Who for freedom now would barter
	Purest gold, will still remain:
Oh! to see our living martyr,
	And to hear his voice again.

Every gift and every blessing 
	Comes, O Lord, from Thee above;
Life and liberty possessing,
	We the righteous giver own;
And with earnest supplications,
	Now we bow in humble prayer;
Great and mighty God of nations, 
	Shield our Prophet and our Seer.

Let Thine angels hover near him,
	Fill him with prophetic fire;
Send Thy minstrels forth to cheer him
	With the touch of seraph's lyre; 
Bless his bed and soothe his pillow,
	Charge his sentinels sincere;
Toss'd on persecution’s billow,
	Guard our Prophet and our Seer.

While Thy humble interceders
	Praise Thy name for mercies past, 
Grant to our beloved leaders
	Thy protection to the last. 
Let Thy Zion, now before Thee, 
	In her destined freedom stand;
Lord of liberty and glory,
	Sweep the tyrants from our land. 

Break their chain from Zion’s noble, 
	Save Thy servant from their scorn,
Bare Thine arm in days of trouble,
	Bid Thy children cease to mourn;
Then to Thee, with warm devotion,
	Shall our hearts and voices sing;
Honor be to Zion's chieftain,
	Glory be to Zion's King.

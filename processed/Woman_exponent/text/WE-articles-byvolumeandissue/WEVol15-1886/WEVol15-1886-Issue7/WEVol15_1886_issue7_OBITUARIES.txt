OBITUARIES.

	DIED, at Orangeville, August 17th, 1886. Zina Lavenia,
infant daughter of Parker Adelbert and Josie E. Childs,
aged eleven months, of inflammation of the bowels and
congestion of the brain.
	Our sweet little rose bud has gone
		Where there is no sickness or pain;
	Sweet is the rest she'll enjoy
		Until we can see her again.

	Short was her stay on this earth,
		Dear to our hearts she had gown.
	Sad, oh, so sad, now to part!
		Lonely, indeed, is our home.

	But in God's goodness we trust.
		And now, in taking her home.
	Help us, dear Father, to say.
		"Thy will, not ours be done."

								S. A. FULLMER.

	
	DIED, at Mill Creek, Alice Maud Gunderson daughter
of Thomas and Harriet Gunderson born March 31st 1879.

	
	DIED, July 27th 1886 of Diptheria. She was a member 
of the Primary Association, ever willing to perform her
part, and a steady attendant when circumstances would
permit. 
	When her sorrowful mother asked what could be done
for her, she calmly answered—pray for me. Everything 
was done for her that could he done but she had to go.
	It is hard to part with those we love, but we know if we
remain faithful we shall meet them again. We feel to ac
knowledge the hand of the Lord in all things. We sin-
cerely sympathize with the ber[-]aved parents and friends.
			Your sister in the gospel,

MISCELLANEOUS.

	If a man is faithful to truth, truth will be
faithful to him.  He need have no fears.  His
success is a question of time.—Prof. Phelps.

     When you have learned to submit, to do
faithfully, patiently, duty that is most distaste-
ful to you, God may permit you to do the work
you like.—Prof. Riddle. 

   The best of a book is not the thought which
it contains, but the thought it suggests, just as
the charm of music dwells not in the the tones,
but in the echoes of our hearts.—Holmes.

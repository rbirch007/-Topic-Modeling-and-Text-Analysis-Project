RELIEF SOCIETY NOTES.

	Missionary and Indian donations are included
in charitable disbursments.
	Wheat is computed by bushels.
	Nothing is to be reported except under one or
other of the printed heads.
	If Real Estate increases in value this increase
must be shown in the receipts for the six months.
	Presidents and secretaries are requested to at-
tach full name and address to reports.
	Reports are hereafter to be sent to Dr. Romania
B. Pratt. Office over Godbe and Pitts drug store,
Salt Lake City, and they must be sent in promptly
by the 15th of September and if latter than the
20th of September they cannot be used.
				MRS. S. M. KIMBALL, Sec.

NOTES AND NEWS. 

	Municipal suffrage for women has been de-
feated in the Nebraska Legislature by a small
majority. 
	The women of Kansas are registering in
large numbers, to vote under the new municipal
suffrage law. A circular, urging them to do
so, has been issued jointly by the Kansas Equal
Suffrage Association and the Kansas W. C. T.
U. Public jubilee meetings have been held in
a number of [-]owns, and great enthusiasm pre-
vails.


Patents have been granted to women for the

for Serving Guests in Restaurants.
	Catharine C. Griswold, New York, Corset.

flector.

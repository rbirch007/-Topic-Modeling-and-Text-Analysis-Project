			RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	In memory of our aged sister, Anna Maria Jasperson,
who departed this life Dec. 17th, 1886. She was born
Sept. 12th, 1809.
	Whereas, The Lord in His infinite wisdom has seen fit
to call from our midst our worthy and beloved sister, Anna
Maria Jasperson, and,
	Whereas, She was a true and faithful Latter-day Saint,
and a faithful member of the Relief Society of Goshen
Ward, and for many years honorably filled the office of a
teacher in the same, it is fitting that we record the high es-
teem and appreciation of her services. Therefore be it
	Resolved, That her counsel and example will be held in
grateful remembrance by our Society.
	Resolved, That we who have seen the ever willing and
cheerful spirit with which she performed every duty will
endeavor to profit by the same.
	Resolved, That it is the sentiments of our Society that
we spmpathize with the relatives, and that a copy of these
Resolutions be furnished them, one sent to the EXPONENT, 
and also placed on the minutes of this Society.

Sleep sweetly, dearest sister, from sorrow, grief and
	pain,
Thy labor is all over, it was not done in vain;
Thy faithful course here on this earth is worthy of imita-
	tion;
The day draws nigh when we shall see the Lord and His
	salvation.
					PIRENA JAMESON,
					MARTHA JENSEN,		Committee.

KIND FRIEND:

	Will you please call attention to the offer of
a Prize Banner to the New England State,
which reports the largest per cent of increase
in membership, including regular and honorary
members of the W. N. C. T. U., and the "Y's;"
also the middle, Southern and Western State,
which makes this report, with one for the state
beyond the Missouri, having the largest per
cent of increase in its membership, and one
for the territory that leads in this respect dur-
ing the present year- Thus, the weakest state
is placed on a level, at the start, with the
strongest. The banners will be presented at
Nashville, next autumn, at the annual meeting
of the W. N. C. T. U.
	Please print this note and plead for a more
thorough canvas for names. It means the
spread of the total abstinence propaganda, and
that means victory.
			Yours for prohibition,

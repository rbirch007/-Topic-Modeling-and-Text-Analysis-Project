EDITOR EXPONENT:

	It is with feelings of deep sorrow that we communicate
the death of our beloved young sister, Catherine E. Suther-
land, daughter of John and Ellen Sutherland, who de-
parted this life at the residence of her parents, Big Cotton-
wood, Salt Lake County, at 9 a.m., on the 22nd of Feb.,
1887, of fever and paralysis of the brain. She was aged
fifteen years, four months and twenty-eight days, and for
her age had great faith in the Gospel; was most amiable
and tender in disposition and manner. She was a regular
attendant at Sunday School, an assistant secretary, and led
the singing in the Primary Association; also a member of
the Y. L. M. I. Association. To know her was to love and
respect her. The funeral services were held at the ward
meeting house on the 24th of Feb., at 12:30. where a large
number met to pay their last respects.

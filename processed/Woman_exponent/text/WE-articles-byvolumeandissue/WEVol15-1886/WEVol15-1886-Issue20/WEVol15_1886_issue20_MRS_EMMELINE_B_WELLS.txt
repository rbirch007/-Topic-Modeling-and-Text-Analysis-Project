MRS. EMMELINE B. WELLS:

	DEAR SISTER:—It gives me pleasure to send
you a few lines from Arizona.
	We have been very greatly blessed, for
which we are very thankful. We have had
a very beautiful winter in the "sunny south,"
with general good health, but we are in a very
scattered condition, which is very inconvenient.
	The EXPONENT is a very welcome visitor in
our midst. May it ever prosper in its noble
efforts of doing good.
	May the blessings of God be with you, my
sister, and all who labor with you in the cause
of truth.
						A SUBSCRIBER.
	St. Joseph Stake, Arizona, Feb. 21, 1887.

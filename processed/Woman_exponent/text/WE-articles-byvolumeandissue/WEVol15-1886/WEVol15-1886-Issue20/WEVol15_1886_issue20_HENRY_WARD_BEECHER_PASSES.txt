HENRY WARD BEECHER PASSES
	AWAY.

	New York, March 8, 9:45 a.m.—Beecher is
dead.
		DEATH-BED DETAILS.
	New York, March 7, midnight.—A message
just received from Brooklyn says that a very de-
cided change for the worse has taken place in the
condition of Beecher, and that the members of 
his family are gathered about his bedside.
	Many telegrams were received during the day,
but none of them have been opened.
	10 o.clock p.m.—Mr. Beecher is steadily failing,
but we do not think he will die to-night.

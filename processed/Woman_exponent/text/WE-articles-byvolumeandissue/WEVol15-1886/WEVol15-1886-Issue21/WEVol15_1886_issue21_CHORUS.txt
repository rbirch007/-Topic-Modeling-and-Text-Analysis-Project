			CHORUS:
		Beautiful temple, Beautiful temple,
		Beautiful temple of God.

Where the life and the power, that Elijah inspires,
	Will bring forth the dust from the sod,
When the hearts of the children are turned to the sires,
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where the feet of the proud and the steps of the meek
	Are directed in one narrow road,
That will bring forth the union and love we should seek
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where the prayer of faith can arise from the soul,
	And ascend to Jehovah's abode,
And the humble and faithful are reaching the goal
	They aspire to in temples of God.
										Cho.

On the Ladder of which the Patriarch dreamed,
	The feet of the angels had trod,
The living are crowned and the dead are redeemed
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where the echoes of heaven respond to the heart,
	To dispel all the cares that corrode,
And the curse from the earth has begun to depart,
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where the bright Lamp of Life and the Tree in its bloom,
	With the oil of salvation's bestowed,
And the wise are preparing to meet the Bridegroom
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where dressed in the bright robes of glory we seemed,
	With the sandals of righteousness shod;
And fanned by the zephyrs of spirits redeemed,
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Like the odor of Aaron's anointing so sweet,
	Was the comfort and blessing that flowed
From the crown of the head, to the soles of the feet,
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where the pure balm of Gilead is soothing the breast,
	And wafting its fragrance abroad.
Bespeaking the glorious coming of Christ
	To the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where the weak and infirm are restored to their health,
	When with pain and affliction they're bowed,
And they offer their thanks for such priceless wealth
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where the jewels of worth can be laid up in heaven,
	When we walk by the bright iron rod;
Where the keys and the powers of the priesthood are given,
	In the beautiful temple of God.
										Cho.

Where the triumphs of Jesus will bring forth the dead,
	From the precious seed that is sewed,
And with glory and song the redeemed will be led
	Through the beautiful temples of God.
										Cho.

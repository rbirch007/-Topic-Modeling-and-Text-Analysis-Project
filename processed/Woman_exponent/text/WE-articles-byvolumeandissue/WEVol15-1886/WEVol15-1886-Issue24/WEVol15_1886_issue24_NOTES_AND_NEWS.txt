NOTES AND NEWS.

	There are 60,000 women farmers in Ire-
land.
	The Sultan of Turkey is happy in the pos-
session of 494 wives.
	The richest colored woman in America is
Mrs. Amanda Eubanks, of Rome, Ga., who
pays taxes on $400,000 worth of property, in-
herited by will from her white father.
	In Duruy's History of France, the writer
says: "The first king of France was Phara-
mond, an imaginary being, who had neve
existed. He was succeeded by his son."
	Mr. Norman W. Dodge, of New York, has
instituted a prize of $300, to be awarded at
each annual exhibition of the Academy to
the best picture painted in the United States
by a woman.
	A society to promote the higher education
of women has been founded in Japan, with
the prime minister as president. The whole
institution will be under the control of a
foreign lady principal.

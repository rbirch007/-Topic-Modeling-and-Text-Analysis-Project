	DRAPER.

	Owing to the death of our late President,
Harriet R. Burnham, we have been disorgan-
ized, but were reorganized September 11th,
with Mrs. Catherine Smith, President;  Mrs.
Mary Smith and Mrs. Hannah Rawlins, Coun-
selors; Mrs. Annie Burnham, Secretary; Mrs.
Kate Sanders, Ass’t Secretary; Mrs. Annie
Howard, Treasurer.
	The sisters all seem to feel well in the Gos-
pel and to go ahead with renewed energy in
keeping the commandments of the Lord, and
in assisting the poor and needy; in trying to
comfort those who feel bowed down and need
comfort, and in doing all the good they can in
every way.  We have a small house now that
we can call our own, where we can meet to-
gether and hold our sewing meetings without
having to wait until we can get the ward house.
Our school is held in that building, so that it
has been rather inconvenient for us to have our
meetings there.  We always feel blessed in
meeting together, and in talking one to another
in regard to our duties as Latter-day Saints,
and we feel to ask the Lord to bless His peo-
ple with such a sufficiency of His holy Spirit
that they may be able to stand firm and stead-
fast to the truth of this Gospel, no matter
what we may be called to pass through, and
that we may always be found in the line of our
duty, is the prayer of,
	Your sister in the Gospel,
	ANNIE BURNHAM, Secretary.

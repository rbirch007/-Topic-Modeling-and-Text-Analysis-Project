EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The late Sister Elizabeth James who was so recently
killed in Salt Lake City, by being thrown from her buggy,
was a very energetic member of the Young Ladies Associ-
ation of this Ward. She had left this ward lately to go to
Bountiful; her husband's occupation called him there.
	The day before she left she bore a strong testimony, and
desired to live the life of a true Latter-day Saint, and bade
her Associates a kindly good-by for a season as her home
is still in this ward.

	R. S. TEMPE, ARIZONA.

     Our ward and society are alive to the inter-
est of the Latter-day work, and we are trying
to live up to the requirements thereof.  There
is much sickness among the little children this
season.  Sister Delcena Babbitt lost her lovely
little daughter, one year old, on the 20th of
August.
	Ever praying for the welfare of Zion, I re-

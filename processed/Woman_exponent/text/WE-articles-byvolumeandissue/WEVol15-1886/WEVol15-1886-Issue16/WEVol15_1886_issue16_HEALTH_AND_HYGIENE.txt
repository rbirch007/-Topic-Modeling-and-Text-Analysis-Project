HEALTH AND HYGIENE.

	Muchardt, professor of hygiene at the Paris
Faculty of Medicine, declares that aged people
should have exercise for the benefit of all the
organs of nutrition and locomotion as well
as young people. He says that the tendency
to rest brings on a gradual diminution of
strength. Moderate exercise, particularly of
walking, is commended. It is well known that
the disuse of any organ impairs its powers.
Why, then, should not the aged lose the use of
their members the sooner from giving way to
an inclination for rest.

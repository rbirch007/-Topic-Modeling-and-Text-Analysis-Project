OBITUARIES.

	DIED, at Pima, Graham Co., A. T., Nov. 23rd, 1886,
Caroline Adrianna, beloved daughter of Heber and Caroline
Masham, born May 8th, 1872, at Santaquin, Utah.
RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT,
	Adopted by the Y. L. M. I A, of Pima, to the memory
of Caroline Adrianna Masham:
	Whereas, by the decree of an alwise Providence, our
worthy young sister, Caroline Adrianna has been taken
from our midst.
	Resolved, That we, her associates and co-workers, of

[Column 2]

the Young Ladies' Association, do fondly cherish her
memory, and while we sadly miss her; we feel to bow in
humble submission to the divine will of heaven.
	Resolved, That we condole with the bereaved family in
the loss of their dear one, not, however, without the con-
soling reflection that she has gone in her purity, and is
worthy to come forth in the morning of the first resurrec-
tion.
	Resolved, That these Resolutions be entered in the
Young Ladies' record; also a copy be presented to the
parents of the deceased, and one be sent to the WOMAN'S
EXPONENT for publication.
		LAURA NUTTALL,
		LUCINDA GUSTAFSON, Committee.

	ONE OF THEM


A floating church for the island of Arran,
Scotland, is the latest novelty, the Dnke of
Hamilton, who owns Arran, having refused
to allow any United Presbyterians a place of
worship on the island. The modern ark is to
be moored in Landlash Bay, one of the snuggest
anchorages on the Scottish coast, and the mem-
bers of the congregation will be pulled on
board from the shore in small boats when the
ship's bell rings at the masthead. The Duke
is an Arran(t) bigot, but his canny Scots ten-
ants have outwitted him.—Ex.

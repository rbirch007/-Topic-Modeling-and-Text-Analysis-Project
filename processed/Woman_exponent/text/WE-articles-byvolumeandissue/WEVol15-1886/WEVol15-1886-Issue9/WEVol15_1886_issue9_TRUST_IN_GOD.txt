TRUST IN GOD.

Trust in God, he'll ne'er forsake you,
	If you strive to do what's right;
Bear in mind, when grief o'ertakes you,
	Darkest nights bring morning light.
	
If in shadow lies your journey,
	Foot it bravely to the end,
Trusting God to send you sunshine—
	He will always prove a friend.

After rain the sun seems brighter,
	Then, despair not, who can tell?
For wise purpose He may smite Thee,
	For "He doeth all things well."

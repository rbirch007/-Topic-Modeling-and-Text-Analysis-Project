RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	To the memory of Mary Bronson, Secretary of the Re-
lief Society of Wasatch Stake, who departed this life on
the 9th of February, 1886.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father in His divine providence
has called away our beloved sister, Mary Bronson, after a 
long life of usefulness spent in His kingdom here upon the
earth.
	Resolved, That while we feel to bow to His most holy
will, we miss our sister in our association, and her wise,
motherly counsel in our circle of loved ones.
	Resolved, That we, as members of the Relief Society of
Wasatch Stake, will hold her memory sacred, and strive in
our lives to follow her example, who was always ready
to perform her duty as Secretary in this stake.
	Resolved, also, That we tender our heartfelt sympathy to
the bereaved husband and family in the loss of a kind and
affectionate wife and mother.
	Resolved, That a copy of these Resolutions be placed
upon record, a copy sent to the bereaved family and to
the WOMAN'S EXPONENT for publication.
					EMMA BROWN, President.
					SARAH ALEXANDER,
					MARY DAYBELL, Counselors.

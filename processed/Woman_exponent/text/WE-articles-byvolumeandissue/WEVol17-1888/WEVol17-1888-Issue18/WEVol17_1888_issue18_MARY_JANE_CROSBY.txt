MARY JANE CROSBY.

	On January 31, 1889, at 9:30 a.m., at her residence,
East Bountiful, Davis County, Utah, of lung disease and
pneumonia, Mary Jane, daughter of Samuel and Mary
Johnson, and wife of John K. Crosby, sen. Born August
28, 1816, in Milton, State of New York. She leaves a
husband, four children, one son and three daughters, and
15 grandchildren to mourn her loss.
	The deceased was an active worker, and for many years
was a counselor to President Barlow, until the reorganiza-
tion of the Relief Society of the East Bountiful Ward.
She died a faithful Latter-day Saint, and in the hope of a
glorious resurrection. Requiiscat in pace.

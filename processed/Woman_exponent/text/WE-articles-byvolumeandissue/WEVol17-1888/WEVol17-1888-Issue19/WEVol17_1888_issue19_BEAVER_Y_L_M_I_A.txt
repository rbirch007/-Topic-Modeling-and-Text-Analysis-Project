BEAVER Y. L. M. I. A.
	In memory of Sister Mona Schofield Olsen, who died
Jan. 26th 1889:
	Whereas, our sister, who has departed this life in her
early youth, was a faithful member of this Association for
a number of years, ever prompt in the performance of the
duties devolving upon her, therefore, be it
	Resolved, That we, as an Association, mourn the sud-
den death of our sister, that we miss her pleasant face and
sweet voice from our meetings, and that we extend our
sincere and heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved young hus-
band, to her little babe, deprived of a mother’s loving care,
and to her parents, brothers, sisters, and to all her former
friends and associates.
	Resolved, That a copy of these Resolutions be sent to
the sorrowing husband and parents, that a copy be sent to
the Utonian and the Woman’s Exponent for publication,
and also recorded in the books of this Association.
	E. J. BENNETT, Prest.

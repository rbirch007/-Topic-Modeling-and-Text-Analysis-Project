WOMAN SUFFRAGE ASSOCIATION.
 
	On Saturday at 2 p.m., Feb. 16, 1888, a
meeting of the ladies of Beaver County was
held in the meeting house, for the purpose of
organizing a Woman Suffrage Association.
Upon motion Mrs. E. J. Bennett was elected
chairman and Mrs. Hettie Gentry, secretary.
Prayer was offered by Mrs. R. W. Tyler. The
chair then stated the object of the convention
and the different offices to be filled. Mrs. J.
P. M. Farnsworth was there-upon elected Presi-
dent of the Woman Suffrage Association of

[Column 2]

Beaver Co., with Mrs. S. C. Maeser as First,
Ida Marshall Second, and M. A. Tanner as
Third Vice-president, Lu F. Mansfield, Corres-
ponding Secretary, Mrs. M. Johnson, Secretary,
A. W. Farnsworth, Assistant Secretary and
Sarah M. Dell, Treasurer.
	The Executive Committee is as follows: E.
J. Bennett, Chairman, Harriet Farnsworth,
Mary Fernley, Annie Gunn, Stella Barton and
A. Cannon. A committee to draft By-laws
was elected. While the committee was out,
Mrs. Farnsworth addressed the meeting.

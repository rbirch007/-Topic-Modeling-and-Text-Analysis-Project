CARD OF THANKS.
       	The committee having charge of the benefit re-
cently given in the Theatre for the Deseret Hos-
pital, desire to make public acknowledgment of
their appreciation of the generous response given
to the cause.  Especially are they grateful to the
Home Dramatic Club, who, by their laudable
efforts, did so much to make the affair the artistic
success which the performance proved. They
also tender their special thanks to the Orchestra
for their generosity, and the exquisite music ren-
dered, and to the managers of the Salt Lake
Theatre, and the Gas Company, as well as to the 
newspaper people, who were so liberal in the mat-
ter of advertising.  Multiplication of words is
needless; we are very grateful to all, who in any
way contributed to the benevolent cause for the
good of the institution. 
	EMMELINE B. WELLS, 
	MARGARET G. CLAWSON, 
	ELIZABETH HOWARD, 
	Committee.

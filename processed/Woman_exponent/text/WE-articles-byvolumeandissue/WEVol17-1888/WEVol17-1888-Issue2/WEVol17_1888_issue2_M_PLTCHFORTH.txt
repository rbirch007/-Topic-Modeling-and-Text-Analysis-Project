	Rest in peace, then, dearest sister,
		All thy earthly cares are o'er;
	O, the many loved ones waiting
		For thee on the other shore.

	Oh, what joy will fill thy bosom,
		Mingling with the good and true I
	Peace and rest will be thy portion,
		Heavenly blessings wait for you.

			M. P.

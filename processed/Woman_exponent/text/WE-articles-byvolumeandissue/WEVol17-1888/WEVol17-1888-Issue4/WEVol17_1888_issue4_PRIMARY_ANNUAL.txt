	PRIMARY ANNUAL.
DEAR EDITOR:
	The Primary Associations of Lewiston and
Coveville held their annual conference at
Lewiston, June 23rd. Meetings commenced at
10 a.m. and 2 p.m. Sisters Pike and Scrothers,
of Logan, were present. The forenoon exercises
consisted of recitations and songs by the
children. The different Associations were re-
ported improving.
	Sister Pike and Scrothers gave some very
good instruction in regard to the training and
education of the young; said that they should
be taught the first principles of the Gospel, so
that as they grew in years they would increase
in knowledge and understanding, and exhorted
all to be faithful in keeping the commandments
of the Lord.
	Afternoon session, 2 p.m.: After the usual
opening exercises, there were speeches, dialogues,
songs and recitations by the children.
	Some of the brethren spoke, and gave some
very good instructions. A good spirit prevailed,
and all felt well in the Gospel, with a desire to
help roll forth this great and glorious work.
	Conference was then adjourned for one
year. I remain,
	Your sister in the Gospel,

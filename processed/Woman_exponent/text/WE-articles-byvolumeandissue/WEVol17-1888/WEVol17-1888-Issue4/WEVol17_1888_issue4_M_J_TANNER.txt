Miss May W. Whitney takes the place held
by Maria Mitchell as professor of astronomy
at Vassar College.
	Our laws about dower, about divorce, about
defense against husbandly squandering of
estates, about the holding of property, will
never be what they ought to be until there are
a great number of women with severest legal
acumen, able to shape statutes and confront
courts on these questions.—Dr. Talmage.

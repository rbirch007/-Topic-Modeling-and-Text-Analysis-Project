GENEROUS DR. BUCKLEY.

	Scene, Heaven's Gate.
	St. Peter, addressing Frances Willard and
other Christian women: "Welcome, dear sisters.
Enter into the joy of your Lord."
	Dr. Buckley, jumping on a soap-box, push-
ing St. Peter away, and slamming the door in
the ladies' faces: "Every woman that gets in
here keeps out a man, to the disadvantage of
heaven.  I stand here to champion the holy
cause of woman!"		H. W.

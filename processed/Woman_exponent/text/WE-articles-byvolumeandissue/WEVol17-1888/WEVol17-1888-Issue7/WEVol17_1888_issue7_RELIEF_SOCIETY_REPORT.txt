RELIEF SOCIETY REPORT.	

To the Stake Presidents of the Relief Society:

	In order that there may be a full and com-
plete report, statistical and financial, of the
Relief Society, including all the several Stakes,
for the Semi annual Conference of the Church,
which will be held in October, the several
Stake Presidents of the Relief Society are
hereby notified to see that the Secretaries pre-
pare and forward a correct Stake report, and
direct to Dr. Romania B. Pratt, 206 n, Second 
West Street, Salt Lake City, not later than
the 15th of September.  
				E. B. WELLS, 
	Cor. Sec. Central Board of the Relief So-
ciety.

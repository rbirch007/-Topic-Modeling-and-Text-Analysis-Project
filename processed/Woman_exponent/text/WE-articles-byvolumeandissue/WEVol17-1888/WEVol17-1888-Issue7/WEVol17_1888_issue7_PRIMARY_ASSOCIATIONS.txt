		PRIMARY ASSOCIATIONS.

To the Stake Presidents of the Primary Asso-
	ciations:

	It is very desirable that a full report of all
the Societies be had for the General Conference
of the Church, which convenes October 6th.
Will the presidents please see that the Stake
Secretaries forward their reports so they will be
received not later than the l5th of September?
Direct to Mrs. C. H. Clayton, P, O. Box 306,
Salt Lake City, Utah.
			LOUIE B. FELT, President.

		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Whereas, it has pleased the great Creator to take unto
Himself, after a long and useful life, our much loved sister,
Sarah H. Crosby, and,
	Whereas, it is fitting that she be held in honorable re-
membrance, therefore, be it
	Resolved, by the ladies' Relief Society of Kanab, That
while we mourn, not as those who have no hope, yet we
realize that in the death of Sister Crosby we have lost a 
dear friend, and her family a wise and loving counselor,
in fact we will all miss her loving counsels, her happy face
and cheery voice. God bless her for evermore! she has
gone to receive a crown she has so faithfully waited for.
	Resolved, That we extend our deepest sympathy to all
the members of her family.
	Resolved, That these Resolutions be put upon the records
of the Relief Society, and a copy thereof be sent to the
family of our sister, and to the WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
								M. C LITTLE,
			    In behalf of the Relief Society of Kanab.

ANNIVERSARY.

	Salt Lake City, October 1st, 1888. This is the
anniversary of my father, Wm. Huntington, and
family of seven leaving their home in Watertown,
New York, to gather to Kirtland with the Saints.
There are still three of the number remaining in
the Church, living in Utah. Fifty years to-day
since we went into Adam-ondi-Ahman. Also the
same day of the month, in 1855, I moved into the
Lion House.
	Many are the incidents this day brings to mind,
but many more joys than sorrows on this our up-
ward march.

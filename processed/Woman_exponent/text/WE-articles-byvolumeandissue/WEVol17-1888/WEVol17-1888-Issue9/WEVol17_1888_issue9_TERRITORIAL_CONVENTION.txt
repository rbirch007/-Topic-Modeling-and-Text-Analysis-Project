TERRITORIAL CONVENTION.

	A Territorial Convention of the People's Party
s hereby called to convene at the City Hall, Salt
Lake City, on Monday, the 8th day of October,
A. D., 1888, at 12 o'clock m., for the purpose of
nominating a candidate for delegate to the Fifty-
first Congress.
	The number of delegates to compose the Con-
vention had been apportioned to the several coun-
ties as follows, to wit:
	Beaver County, 2; Box Elder, 3; Cache, 6;
Davis, 3; Emery, 1; Garfield, 1; Iron, 2; Juab, 2;
Kane, 2; Millard, 2; Morgan, 1; Piute, 1; Rich 1;
Salt Lake 16; San Juan, 1; Sanpete, 6; Sevier, 2;


Summit, 2; Tooele, 2; Uintah, 1; Utah, 9;
Wasatch, 1; Washington, 2; Weber, 6. Total,
75.
	The County Central Committees are requested
to take immediate steps in their respective coun-
ties for the election of delegates to the Territorial
Convention.
	By order of the People's Territorial Central
Committee.
		ELIAS SMITH, Secretary.

CLIFTON, IDAHO.

	Minutes of Relief Society meeting, held
May 3rd, 1888, for the purpose of reorganizing
our Society. Our worthy Prest., Jane M.
Howell, having resigned on account of her
having removed from the ward for a short
time. The visitors present were Stake Prest.
Elizabeth Fox, of Franklin, Sister Lewis and
Sister Crawshaw, of Oxford; local officers,
Bishop W. F. Garner, Counselors John S.
Bingham and Martin Hendersen, Prest. Jane
M. Howell and Counselors.
	After Prest. Howell had resigned her office,
the Society was reorganized, with D. E. Dudley,
President; S. E. Bingham and Janette Davis,
Counselors; M. A. Garner, Secretary; E. M.
Henderson, Ass't Secretary; Amelia J. Dixon,
Treasurer.
	We had an enjoyable time, and much good
and timely instruction was given by all the
speakers present.
		M. A. GARNER, Secretary.

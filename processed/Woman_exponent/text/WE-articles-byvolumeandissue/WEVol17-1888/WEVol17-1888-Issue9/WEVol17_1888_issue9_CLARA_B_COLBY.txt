CLARA B. COLBY.

	Sister Workers, in the cause
	Of God's mighty Truth and laws,
	May His Spirit guide and bless,
	Leading you to sure success.

	And it will, for Truth is strong,
	Right is right, and wrong is wrong;
	Error lifted ne'er so high,
	Must be crushed, must fall and die.

	Temperance, Purity and Love,
	Truth's own children, born above,
	Sent with Unity to earth,
	Hold the power of all true worth.

	And gainst these the world now fights,
	While in evil it delights,
	But Intelligence must win
	Power o'er ignorance and sin.

	Vile Intemperance must be bound,
	Liberty again be crowned,
	Freedom's enemies be slain,
	Truth and Justice rise and reign.

	Temperance Unity and Love;
	Virtue's offspring born above.
	These, the pure are fighting for,
	In the world's great moral war.

	Lo! the pathway must be paved,
	And our favored land be saved,
	Sister Workers, do your best
	GOD and Israel for the rest.

		LULA.

	In behalf of the ladies of the Latter-day Saints, Salt
Lake Assembly Hall, Tuesday, Sept. 25th, 1888.

EMMA TOONE.

And, is she dead?  'Tis true indeed she's dead
To this cold world of misery and woe,
Yet, living in a sphere that's bright
And glorious, and hard to gain
Except by those, who true to God and right
Have earned a title to that blest abode,
	Like she has done.

And, now she's gone, we would not call her back
Again, to suffer grief and pain,
But thankful feel, that she has been released
To dwell in that bright realm
Where God and angels are,
And there to await the Ressurrection Day
	Where Jesus reigns.

Zion's Home Monthly.  WM. WILLIS.

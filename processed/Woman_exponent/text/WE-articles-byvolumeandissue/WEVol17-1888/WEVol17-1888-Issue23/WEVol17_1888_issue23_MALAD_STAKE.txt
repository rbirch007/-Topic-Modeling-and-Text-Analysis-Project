		    MALAD STAKE.
	The Relief Society of this Stake of Zion
was organized in a stake capacity on the 16,
of June 1888. Officers as follows: Lucinda
Hoskins, President, Mary Stewart and Eliza-
beth Zundell, Counselors, Eliza A. Hall,
Secretary. We hold our conferences quarterly
and change the place of meeting, sometimes
holding conference in Malad City and some-
times in Portage.
	August 17, 1888, we held a Relief Society
conference in Portage. Present on the stand
Stake officers, President Oliver G. Hoskins,
Counselors Abraham Zundell and William H.
Gibbs; also the Stake officers of the Relief
Society, Mrs. Lucinda Hoskins presiding.
Meeting opened by singing “Glorious things
of Thee are spoken.” Prayer by Brother
Jesse Dredge, singing “Great is the Lord.”
Reports as follows: Malad, President Annie
Sawyer; Samaria, President Mary D. Davis;
Portage, President Jane Harris; St. John,
President Elvira Harrison. President O. G.
Hoskins addressed  the meeting; exhorted the
sisters to live their religion, and be true to
their integrity, attending to all their duties,
and gave good advice on several different sub-
jects.
	President Lucinda Hoskins made a few
closing remarks: urged the sisters to diligence
in looking after the poor, and advised them
to be humble and prayerful at all times.
After singing, beuediction was pronouneed by
Brother William Johns. Conference adjourned
for three months.
			   ELIZA A. HALL, Secretary.

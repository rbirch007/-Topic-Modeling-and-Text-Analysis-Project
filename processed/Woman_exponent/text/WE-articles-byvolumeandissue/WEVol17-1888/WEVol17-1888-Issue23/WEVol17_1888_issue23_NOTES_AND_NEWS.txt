NOTES AND NEWS.

     Missouri has just granted school suffrage to
women.
     The Arkansas Legislature has voted to
admit girls to the State University.
     Marietta Holley, "Josiah Allen's wife," has 
another  book ready for the press.
     Grand celebration in New York yesterday
April 30, anniversary of the inauguration one
hundred years ago.
     Mrs. Elizabeth Stuart Phelps-Ward, with
her husband,, Dr. Ward, is at her old home 
in Andover on a visit to her father.  Her
winter at the South has greatly improved her
health.
     A reception was given at the Kindergarten
for the Blind, Jamaica Plains, Mass., April 9.
Addresses were made by Rev. Phillips Brooks,
Hon. Leverett Saltonstall, and Rev. George A.
Gordon.
     Ramabai has opened her school.  By the
counsel of her advisory board in India, it has
been established in the Back Bay district of
Bombay instead of at Poonah.  Her reception
among her own people has thus far been cordial
and encouraging. EX.

			JAMES FREEMAN CLARKE.
	"Resolved, That the American Woman Sufirage
Association has sustained a great loss in the
removal by death of Rev. James Freeman
Clarke.  President for three years of the
Massuchetts W. S. A., and always our faithful
and judicious friend. Dr. Clarke's wise advo-
cacy of all just reforms made his adherence to
our cause a matter of great weight and impor-
tance. His presence has left us, but his influence
remains."

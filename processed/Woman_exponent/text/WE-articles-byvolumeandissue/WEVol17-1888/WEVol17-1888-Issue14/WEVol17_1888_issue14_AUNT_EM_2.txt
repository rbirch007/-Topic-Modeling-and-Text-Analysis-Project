						     AUNT EM.


The women of Ohio organized a State Coun-
cil at Columbus, October 11, with Dr. Martha
May Howell, of Cleveland, as President, and
Dr. Juliet Monroe Thorp as Secretary.
	In consequence of the death of Dr. Rachel
Bodley, Pundita Ramabai's book is placed in
the hands of the W.T.P.A. Orders should
be sent as follows: To Pundita Ramabai, care
Woman's Temperance Association, 161 La Salle
Street, Chicago.

PRIMARY FAIR.

	On the 16th of November a Primary Fair
was held at Hyde Park, Cache Co.  There was
a good representation of work, done by the
children, both at their homes and in the work-
ing meetings.  The work consisted of sewing,
knitting, crochet work and cooking by the girls,
both ornamental and useful articles being made;
also vegetables raised by the boys and orna-
ments made of wood, etc.
	A party was given to the children, and alto-
gether it was a very pleasant affair. The
children showed their willingness to do what
was required of them, by bringing in their
work.
					LYDIA DAVIS, Sec.
	Hyde Park, Dec. 5, 1888.

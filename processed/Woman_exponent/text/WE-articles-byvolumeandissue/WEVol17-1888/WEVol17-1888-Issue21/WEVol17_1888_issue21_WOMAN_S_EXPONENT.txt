WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


You may depend upon it that he is a good
man whose intimate friends are all good, and
whose enemies are characters decidedly bad.—
Lavater.
	People will, in a great degree and not with-
out reason, form their opinions of you upon
that which they have of your friends; and
there is a Spanish proverb which says justly,
"Tell me with whom you live, and I will tell 
you who you are."

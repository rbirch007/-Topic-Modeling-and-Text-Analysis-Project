		ANN C. WOODBURY


Harriet Beecher Stowe has made a most re-
markable recovery after a long illness which
even her nearest friends deemed fatal.
	Queen Victoria does not share the general
opinion that the gift of an opal is likely to
bring ill-luck to its owner. She has presented
each of her daughters, on their marriage, with
a parure of opals and diamonds. Her influence,
and that of her numerous offspring is said to
be doing much to rehabilitate the opal in the
good opinion of the public.

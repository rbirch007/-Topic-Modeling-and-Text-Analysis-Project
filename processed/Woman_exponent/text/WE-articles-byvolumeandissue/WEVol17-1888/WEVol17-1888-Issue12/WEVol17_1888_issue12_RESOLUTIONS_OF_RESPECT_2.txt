RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Adopted by the sisters of the 4th District Relief Society
of the Payson Ward. To the memory of our esteemed
sister Anna Scharren:
	Whereas, it has pleased God in his infinite wisdom, to
take from our midst, by the hand of Death, our worthy
and beloved sister, and,
	Whereas, we, her fellow-laborers in the Relief Society
feel that we have lost a friend and sister; be it therefore.
	Resolved, that we tender our love and esteem and heart-
felt sympathy to Bro. Henry Scharrer and family, and
condole with them in their bereavment while we submit to
the will of an alwise God who doethall things well.
	Resolved, that we spread these resolutions on the So-
ciety record; and present a copy to the family, and also to
the Woman's Exponent for publication.

	She has filled herearthly mission
		And is wanted up above;
	To continue in good labors
		Where all is peace and love.

	Sleep! Oh sleep dear Sister.
		Till Gabriel's trump shall sound.
	You'll awaken from your slumber
		To receive a righteous crown.

			MRS. JANE LEATHAM.
Wellsville, Oct. 30, 1888.

	Died, at Payson, Sept. 16, 1888, Anna, the beloved
wife of Henry Scharrer. Born Dec. 21st 1817. Heard,
and embraced the Gospel in the fall of 1857, being the
only one of a large family embracing it; emigrated with
her husband and two children to Utah in the year 1860,
In answer to a call, went with her husband and family to


assist in opening up the Mission in Southern Utah,
Passed through many trials and afflictions, and also much
suffering iu her last sickness, caused by liver complaint and
old age.
	She was the mother of three children two of whom are
living, and grand-mother to eighteen. She was very
modest and unassuming in manner, patient and uncom-
plaining, and resigned in trouble.
	Died in full faith of the Gospel and in the hope of a
glorious resurrection.

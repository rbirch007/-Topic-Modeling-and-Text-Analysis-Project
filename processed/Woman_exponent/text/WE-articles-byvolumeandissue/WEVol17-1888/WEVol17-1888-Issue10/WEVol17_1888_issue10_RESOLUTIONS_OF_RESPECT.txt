RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Resolutions, of Respect to the memory of Sister Martha
Ann Waite Owens, born at Lincolnshire, England,on the 
2nd of March, 1843; Was baptized when eight years old.
Emigrated to Utah in 1853, Died Aug. 8th, 1888. 
	Whereas, the Lord has been pleased to call Sister 
Martha Ann Waite Owens from our midst, a member of
the Relief Society of Willard and Ass't Secretary of the
same, 	
	Resolved, by the Relief Society of Willard, That we  
hold her name in remembrance, inasmuch as she was a 
faithful Latter-day Saints, a fond wife and mother.
	Resolved, That we tender our sympathy to her husband 
and family.
	Resolved, That we send a copy of these Resolutions to 
her husband and family; also a copy be sent to the EX-
PONENT for publicatation, and that a copy be put on the 
Relief Society record book.	
					MARX ANN HUBBARD, Prest.,

TERRITORIAL FAIR.

	The Fair for the Territory of Utah was held in
the new and handsome brick building, erected for
the purpose of the display, on the Tenth Ward
Square. The Fair was a very creditable showing
of all kinds of produce, cattle, horses, and almost
every kind of exhibit that the Territory affords.
It would be impossible in limited space to give
details of all, but we shall try to mention the
ladies' work.

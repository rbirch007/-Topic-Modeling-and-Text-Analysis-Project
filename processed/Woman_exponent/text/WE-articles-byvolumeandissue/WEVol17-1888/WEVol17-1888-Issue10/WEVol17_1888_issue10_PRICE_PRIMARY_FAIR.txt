PRICE PRIMARY FAIR.
	
	On the 21st of September a Primary Fair
was held at Price, Emery Co. Doors were 
opened at 10 o'clock. There was an exhibi-
tion, of needle and crochet work, home-made
woolen yarn, carpenter work, marbles, vege-
tables and corn, by children from four to six
years old.
	Meeting was called to order by the Prest.,
Jane Eldredge. Singing. Prayer was offered
by Bro. E. W. McIntire. Singing, after which


there were songs, recitations and dialogues, by
the Price and Spring Glenn Primaries.
	Bros. Hardman and McIntire spoke a few
encouraging words to the children.
	Singing. Benediction by Bro. F. M. Ewell.

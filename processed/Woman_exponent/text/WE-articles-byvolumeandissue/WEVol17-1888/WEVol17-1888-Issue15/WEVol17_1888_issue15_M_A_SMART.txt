			M. A. SMART.

	DIED, at East Bountiful, Davis Co., Utah, after an ill-
ness of two months, Sept. 23rd, 1888, Emma Maria, be-
loved wife of Daniel Wood. Deceased was born July 12,
1834, in Plymouth, England. Baptized Feb. 25, 1851, by
Charles Phelps. She embraced the order of plural mar-
riage, was the mother of six children and twenty-three
grandchildren. Sister Wood was a faithful member in the
Relief Society, and labored in the capacity of a teacher
for many years; was always prompt to her meetings and
fulfilling her duties honorably, and died in full faith of the
Gospel, and in the hope of a glorious resurrection.

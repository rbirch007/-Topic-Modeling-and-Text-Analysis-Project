OBITUARIES.

	DIED, at Union, Salt Lake County, Dec. 6th, 1888,
Mary Ann Alberta Cunningham, the beloved daughter of
Hyrum H. and Della A. Brady, of membranous croup,
aged one year, ten months and eight days.

	I laid my darling down with a sigh,
		In grief, for sorrow was too deep;


My anguished heart could only cry,
		"O, God in heaven, my darling keep."

	I know my darling's form will wait
		Beyond this world, where grief's dark night
	Enshroud my saddened life, at heaven's gate
		I'll meet my child where all is light.

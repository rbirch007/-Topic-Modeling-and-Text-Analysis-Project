ANOTHER W. S. A.

	THE following notice appears in the Richfield
Advocate of March 6th; this indicates the energy
and progressive spirit of the ladies in that locality.
ED.
	A meeting will be held, at Relief Society Hall,
March, 14th, at 2 p.m., for the purpose of organiz-
ing a Woman Suffrage Association in Sevier Co.
All ladies are requested to attend, irrespective of
church or creed.
		ELIZABETH BEAN,
		JANE SPENCER,

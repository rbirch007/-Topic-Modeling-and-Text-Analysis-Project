There are 3000 women in charge of post-
offices in the United States.
	There are fifteen States in the Union where,
if the wife was unchaste at the time of mar-
raige, unknown to the husband, proof of the
fact entitles the husband to a divorce. There
is only one State (West Virginia) where, if the
husband was unchaste before marriage, un-
known to the wife, proof of the fact entitles
the wife to a divorce. Even in West Virginia
he must have been notoriously licentious.—Ex.

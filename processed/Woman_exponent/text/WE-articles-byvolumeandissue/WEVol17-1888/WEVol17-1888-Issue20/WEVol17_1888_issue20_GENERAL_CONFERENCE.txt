GENERAL CONFERENCE.

	The Fifty-ninth Annual Conference of the
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, will
commence at 10 o'clock on Saturday morning,
April 6th, 1889, in the Tabernacle, in Salt Lake
City.
	The officers and members of the Church are
cordially invited to attend.
		WILFORD WOODRUFF,
	In behalf of the Twelve Apostles.

CLARKSTON PRIMARY.
	Primary annual meeting of Clarkston, held
Nov. 5th 1888, Prest. C. Thompson presiding.
Singing. Prayer by O. A. Jensen. Singing.
Roll called. Minutes of previous annual
meeting read and accepted.
	Coun. Sarah Shumway asked the Bible ques-
tions, which were answered in concert; William
Shumway, recitation; Dannie and Eddie God-
frey, recitation from card on good manners;
Sister E. J. Jardine asked Bible questions,

[Column 2]

which were answered by the following little
girls: Katie Godfrey, May Jardine, Melissa
Griffin, Sadie Shumway and Susie Atkinson;
Ellie Ogilvie sang a song; Tommy Griffin, reci-
tation; May and Johney Jardine, a song.
	Bro. A. G. White addressed the children a short time.
	Dialogue, Annie Looslie and company; reci-
tation, George Sparks; Ellie Harris, Essay;
Maggie Jensen, recitation; Lettie Shumway,
recitation. Prest. Mary Griffin addressed the
children on obedience. Katie Myler and Ellen
Clark recited Motto Card, No. 5; Johnny
Thompson and Lettie Shumway recited the
Lord's Prayer.
	Sister Agnes Jardine Spoke to the children
in a very encouraging manner.
	Coun. O. A. Jensen gave a brief address,
touching on many useful matters.
	Prest., C. Thompson said she was greatly 
pleased with the way in which the exercises
had been rendered; she would like to have seen
more of the parents present. Told the children
they must all come to the party at 2 p.m.
Singing. Benediction by Prest. C. Thompson.
		SYLVA THOMPSON, Secretary.

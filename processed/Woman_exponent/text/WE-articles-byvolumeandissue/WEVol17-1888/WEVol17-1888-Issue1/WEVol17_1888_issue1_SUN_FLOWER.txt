						SUN FLOWER.


Miss Edith Martineau, a daughter of Rev.
Dr. James Martineau, has just been elected an
associate member of the Royal Society of 
Painters in Water-colors.
	The Queen of Sweden has been ordered by
her doctors to get up early, make her own bed,
take care of her own room, work in the garden
and take long walks.  Under this regimen, her
health is visibly improving.

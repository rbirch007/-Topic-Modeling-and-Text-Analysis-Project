JULIA WARD HOWE POST CARD. 
	M,s. Julia Ward Howe has been made the
subject of a new post card, one of the most
artistic yet issued, It is pale brown and
bears a very pleasing portrait of Mrs. Howe,
the last verse of the “Battle Hymn,” and a
fac-simile of Mrs. Howe’s signature. It may
be ordered from the Msssachuttes Woman
Suffrage Association, 585 Boylston St. Bos-
ton. Price 5 cents apiece. Add one cent
or more for postage, according to the quan-
tity ordered.

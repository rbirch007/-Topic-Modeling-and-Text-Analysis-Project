RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	At the annual meeting of the Relief Society of
the Parker ward, Idaho, March 17th, 1909 the fol-
lowing resolutions were unanimously passed:
	Whereas, the eternal Father has seen fit in His
infinite wisdom, to call to himself our beloved
Sister Juliet Rice.
	Be it resolved, that the members of this Society,
sincerely mourn the loss of their cheerful co-
laborer, and faithful associate in the cause of hu-
man aid and fellowship.
	Also be it resolved, that a copy of these resolu-
tions be sent to the bereaved family. A copy
furnished the Woman’s Exponent, for publication,
and they be entered upon the secretary’s record
of the Parker Relief Society.
	As cheering as the sun’s bright ray,
		She shed her light upon our way,
	Her genial presence cheered our gloom
		As dewy roses waft perfums
	Dear friend, fond mother, faithful wife,
		Fit subject for eternal life!
								COM.

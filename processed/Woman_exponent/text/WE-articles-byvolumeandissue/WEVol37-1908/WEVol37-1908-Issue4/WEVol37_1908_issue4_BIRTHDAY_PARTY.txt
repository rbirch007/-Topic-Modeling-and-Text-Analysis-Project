BIRTHDAY PARTY.
	The members of the South Relief Society
of the West Weber Ward, North Weber Stake
of Zion, gave a very pleasant surprise party
on their president Sister Sarah Hunter on
her seventieth birthday, November 9, 1908.
The party gathered at her home at 11:30 p.
m. and at 12 o’clock some thirty brethren
and sisters were seated at a beautiful table
spread with the most delicious bounties of
life provided by the members of the Society.
The afternoon was spent in singing, speak-
ing by the brethren, comic readings, and
phonograph music; after which supper was
served before going home. The party was
in every way a success and a merry day was
spent.

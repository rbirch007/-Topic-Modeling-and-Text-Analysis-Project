CONTRACT.
	In consideration of obtaining instruction
at a reduced price in the General Relief So-
ciety Nurse class taught by Dr. Margaret C.
Roberts, I pledge to hold myself in readi-
ness to answer all calls made upon me by
the General Superintendent of Relief Society
Nurses, or the President of the Stake to
which I belong, to nurse for Charity for fifty
days, and in addition for whatever pay shall
be designated by my Superintendent for the
term of two years after date of graduation,
and I shall not be exempt from this duty
unless through personal sickness.
	And I also agree not to accept any case
of nursing on my own account, during the
said two years, without previously consult-
ing my Superintendent.
			(Signed.)............................
	It is our desire that Superintendents of
Stakes and Ward Presidents will be energe-
tic in presenting this matter to the class of
young women who would be desirable stu-
dents, as we are in great need of more train-
ed nurses for the people. It is important
that students, as far as possible, commence
with the class at its beginning, on the 21st
of September next. For further particulars
write to Mrs. Phebe Y. Beatie, 55 North
West Temple Street, Salt Lake City.
			BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
					General President.
			EMMELINE B. WELLS,
					General Secretary.
	EMMA A. EMPEY,
			Gen. Sup. of R. S. Nurse Work.
	PHEBE Y. BEATIE, Secretary.

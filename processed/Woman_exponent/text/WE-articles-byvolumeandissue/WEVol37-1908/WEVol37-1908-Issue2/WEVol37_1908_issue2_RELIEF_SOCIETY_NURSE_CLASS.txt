RELIEF SOCIETY NURSE CLASS.
	To those intending to take the nursing
course taught by Dr. M. C. Roberts under
the direction of the General Board of Relief
Society it is desired that they communicate
as early as possible with the Secretary of
that department Mrs. Phoebe Y. Beatie
55 North, West Temple Street, so that suit-
able homes may be provided for them dur-
ing their course of study where they can
either pay for their boa[-]d or earn sufficient
to cover that obligation.
	The class will start September 21st, due
notice will be given later in regard to the
plaee where class will be held and other
particulars.

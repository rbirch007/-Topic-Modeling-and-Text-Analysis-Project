R. S. REPORTS.
			BEAR LAKE STAKE.
	Minutes of Relief Society conference and
officers meeting held in tabernacle at Paris,
July 12, 1908. President Nancy E. Pugmire
presiding.
	Sisters Elizabeth S. Wilcox and Minnie H.
James being our visitors of the General Re-
lief Society.
	Meeting opened by singing and prayer
and other usual exercises.
	President Nancy E. Pugmire gave a few
opening remarks.
	Alton, Cokeville, Laketown and Bern Re-
lief Society organizations reported.
	Different business was attended to con-
cerning real estate and other land and pro-
perty of the several Relief Society wards.
	Sister Nancy Allred was honorably re-
leased with a vote of thanks from the posi-
tion of Stake Secretary, having another re-
sponsible position to fill.
	Sister Nina Michaelson was voted in as
Stake Secretary.
	Sister Margaret Stucki was also released
as Stake Treasurer with a vote of thanks.
	Sister Sarah Allred of St Charles was
voted in as Treasurer.

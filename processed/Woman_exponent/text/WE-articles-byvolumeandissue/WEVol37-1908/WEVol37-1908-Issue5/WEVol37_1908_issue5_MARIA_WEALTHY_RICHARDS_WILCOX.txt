MARIA WEALTHY RICHARDS WILCOX.

	Whereas our Father in heaven has seen fit to
call from us our beloved President, Sister Maria
W. Wilcox, whom we shall greatly miss for her
many sterling qualities, and one who was true to
every trust,
	Whereas the poor have lost a benevolent sister,
the distressed a comforter, having always carried
with her a sweet spirit, which was felt by those
she met, be it
	Resolved, that we, the members of the Relief
Society condone with the family in the loss of a
noble wife a loving mother, and considerate sister,
be it
	Resolved that these resolutions be filed in
the minutes of the Fourteenth Ward Relief Soci-
ety, Salt Lake City, and a copy be sent to the
family.
					Rida C. Taylor,
					Nellie D Woodruff,
Committee.			Louisa F. Brown,
					Sarah C. Folsom,

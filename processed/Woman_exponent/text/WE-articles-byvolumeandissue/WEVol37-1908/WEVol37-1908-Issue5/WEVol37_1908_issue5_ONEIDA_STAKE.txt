			ONEIDA STAKE.
	Relief Society annual conference held in
Preston, May 2, 1908. President Louisa B.
Benson presiding.
	There was present of the General Board
Sister Susan Grant and Annie Wells Can-
non, full Stake Board present.
	Singing, Prayer, Singing, Roll called, 19
wards represented.
	Minutes. Statistical and financial report
approved.
	President Benson welcomed all to our
conference, reported a new society viz. Win-
der, making the 20th in the stake.
	President Julia Jenson reported the 2nd
ward as building a new granary.
	Solo—Dora Geddes.
	Fairview, Weston and Franklin were re-
ported by their respective presidents.
	Sister Annie Wells Cannon addressed the
conference on The Broadness of Relief So-
ciety Work, said every woman in Zion should 
be an example of purity. Spoke of the du-
ties of teachers, power of organized charity,
looking after spiritual welfare of sisters and
of the watchcare we should haye over our
girls, keeping them from large cities etc.

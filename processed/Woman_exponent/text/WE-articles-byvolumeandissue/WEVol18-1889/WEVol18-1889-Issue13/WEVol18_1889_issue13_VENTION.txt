VENTION.

	The Twenty-second Annual Washington, D.
C., Woman Suffrage Convention will be held
in the Church of our Father, corner of L.
and 13th streets, on Tuesday, Wednesday,
Thursday and Friday, February 18th, 19th,
20th and 21st, 1890.  And at the Rigg's
House on the afternoon of Monday.  February
17th, will be an executive session of the
officers and delegates of the several states and
territorial suffrage societies, at which it is hoped
every one of the forty-two states and five
territories will be represented.  Persons wish-
ing copies of the National-American Constitu-
tion should address the Secretary of the
Committee of Arrangements, Rachel Foster
Avery, 748, North 19th, street Philadelphia,
Pennyslvania.

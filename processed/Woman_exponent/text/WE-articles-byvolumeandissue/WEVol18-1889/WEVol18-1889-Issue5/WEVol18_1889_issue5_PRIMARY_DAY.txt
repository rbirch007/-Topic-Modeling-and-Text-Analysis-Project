PRIMARY DAY.

	June 1, 1889, a lovely morning, the Primary
children of East Bountiful, met at the Taber-
nacle, with their little baskets to celebrate the
birthday of Brigham Young. The children
formed in a line and marched to the bench
under the shade of some fine trees, after the
children were seated, the meeting was opened
with prayer by Sister Easthope. Sister Lucie
Thurgood then addressed the children and told
them the reason why we met to-day. Spoke
of the great and noble deeds performed by
this man of God.
	Sister Dustin then spoke to the children,
said she felt well and happy, to be connected
with the Primary, and wished we could all visit
the grave of the prophet of the Lord. Sister
Easthope then spoke to the children. Carlos
Call recited, "When boys are men." We then sang
"We thank thee O God for a Prophet." The
children then had some games in which they
enjoyed themselves very much, and also in
gathering wild flowers. We then dismissed
the assembly with prayer by Carlos Call, and
returned to the Relief Society Hall; and after
asking a blessing upon the food, it was par-
taken of with a good relish.
	In the afternoon we had a dancing party
for the little folks, and all enjoyed themselves.
At the close, oranges were given to the children
and all left with smiling faces.
		LUELLA STOKER, Secretary,

OBITUARIES.

	It becomes my painful duty to record the death of our
beloved Sister Margaret Helen, daughter of Gibson and
Elizabeth Condie, and wife of Wm. Palmer, who died
November 3, 1889, leaving a husband and six small
children who deeply mourn her loss.
	Sister Palmer has only been married eight years, but she
was the mother of seven children having had two pairs of
twins. 
	She was a kind and affectionate wife, a loving mother,
and a friend to all who knew her. She was a firm Latter-
day Saint and has been President of the Y. L. M. I. A. of
Croydon ever since its organization. She was always
cheerful and had a kind word and good advice to give
every one, she always manifested in her labors a meek and
unselfish disposition; her precepts and examples will long
be remembered. 
		Heal the wounded-hearted husband,
			Who has been so tried and true;
		And protect that little infant,
			Whom its mother scarcely knew.

NOTES AND NEWS.
 
	Miss Helen Castle, of Honolulu, Sandwich
Islands, is taking a course at the Harvard
Annex.
	The lady who writes poems under the name
of E. Nesbit is Mrs. Edith Bland. She is a
vigorous socialist, and lives at Lee, one of the
suburbs of London,
	A young lady stands first in the com-
petiition for the six $800 scholarships at Cor-
nell University. What’s the matter with
the young men of this day and generation?
	Miss Clara Barton has completed her work
at Jamestown, and has returned to Washing-
ton, D. C. A public reception was given her
at Willard’s Hotel, Saturday Nov. 2. followed
by a banquet, to which more than two hun-
dred persons have been invited. Commissioner
Hines of the District will preside.

SILK WORMS.

	Mr. D. Graves has received another circu-
lar from the U.S. Department of Agriculture,
in which it is stated that persons desirous of
obtaining silk worm eggs can do so by apply-
ing to him and answering the following ques-
tions:
	Have you ever raised silk worms before?  If
"yes," state how many seasons you have raised
silk worms.
	Do you intend to use mulberry or orange
leaves in feeding the worms? 
	Have you a copy of Prof. Riley's pamphlet.
entitled, "The Mulberry Silk Worm" published
since 1885?

W. S. A. PLEASANT GROVE.

	Pleasant Grove organized a W. S. A. March 15.
House called to order by Mrs. E. Brown, choir
sang, prayer by the Chaplain Mrs. E. Harper.
Officers duly elected Mrs. F. E. Stewart, Pres.
Vice Pres. Emily H. Bullock, Secretary Mrs. L.
Lund, Cor. Sec. Miss Amy Bosza, Treasurer Mrs.
E. Thorn, Executive Com. appointed. A lecture
"The Advancement of Woman," by Mrs. Rose
Hays. The speaker portrayed the necessity of
striving for our just rights and fitting ourselves
for any sphere we might be called to occupy.
Recitation "Barbara Fretchie," by Miss Clara
Stewart, Essay "Woman's Work," by Mrs. Lydia
Lund, speech by Mrs. E. Brown, instrumental
music by Libbie Mayhew, a lecture by Mrs. Ella
Smith, "Woman's Mind Equal to Man's." A
stirring speech by Mrs. M. A. Phelps, song by
Miss Eva Frampton, a speech by Mrs. M. A.
Winters, replete with many truths plainly eluci-
dated. A speech by Pres. Stewart in which the
object, aim and purpose of the meeting was set
forth.
		F. E. S.

						L. D. MUIR,  R. S. Sec.

	Died at Brigham City, Saturday March 22, 1890. Eliza-
beth Chapple Smith, aged 74 years. Deceased was born
in Bishop's Hympton's, Devonshire, England May 4th,
1816. Received the Gospel in the year 1849: emigrated
with her family to Utah in 1871, her husband having pre-
ceded her two years to prepare a home for the family.
	It can truly be said of Sister Smith, that she was a Saint


n very deed, honest and up right in all her dealings and
generous to the needy.
	Having passed through many trials and privations in her
native land, to assist the Elders and gather to Zion, and
sad as may be the feelings of the dear ones left behind, we
cannot but rejoice to think that another faithful sister has
passed behind the vail, full of years, and good works, and
sterling integrity. She has now gone to enjoy the society
of the faithful saints, who like her have fought the good
fight and kept the faith, and endured through evil and
good report faithful to the end; resting in the glorious hope
of coming forth in the morning of the first resurrection.
		A. N. B.

TO THE SUFFRAGE LADIES.

					Beatrice Neb., Sept. 1889.
	DEAR MRS. WELLS:
	The Woman's Tribune will be published in
Washington. D. C. after the middle of Novem-
ber announcement in next Tribune. This will
enable it to be more helpful in national affairs,
will you kindly urge upon those whom you think
would be interested as you have opportunity their
need of the paper. I am glad that Utah is
getting such a good waking up on the Suffrage
question and that it has so able a representative
as the Woman’s Exponent.
				Yours cordially

				MRS. MARY ANN M. PRATT.
			Our love for you has brought us here,
		Although but few, we hold you dear;
			Your life has been a useful one.
		Continue till your work is done.

			Your many friends wish that your days,
		May not soon end, God bless your ways!
			And may life’s hours be long and sweet,
		To give you joy when e’er we meet.

			Your counsels, yea, we all have sought,
		As treasures dear that can’t be bought,


Your works of love unto the sick
And words of comfort to the meek.

	Are to your sisters, healing balm
Their troubled spirits you can calm;
	And many years may added be
As long as life's desired by thee.

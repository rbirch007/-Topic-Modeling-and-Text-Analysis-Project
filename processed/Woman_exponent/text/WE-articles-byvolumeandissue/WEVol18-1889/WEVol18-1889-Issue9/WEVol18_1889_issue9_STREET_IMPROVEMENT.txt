STREET IMPROVEMENT.
4—	The grades and lines of all streets, water
ditches and sidewalks should be permanently
established; the corners of blocks and grades of
streets indicated by suitable corner-stones, and all
improvements made to conform thereto.
	The streets and sidewalks should be perma-
nently graded and improved,with good material,as
fast as possible upon an equitable and definite
plan, and all bridges and culverts should be the
full width of the streets and sidewalks.

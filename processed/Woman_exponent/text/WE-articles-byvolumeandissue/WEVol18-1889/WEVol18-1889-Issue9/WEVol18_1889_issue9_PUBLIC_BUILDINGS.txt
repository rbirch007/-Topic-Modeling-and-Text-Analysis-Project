PUBLIC BUILDINGS.
5—	We favor the erection of public buildings
adequate for present and approaching requir-
ments. But in order that the taxes may not be in-
creased for this purpose, and that the ordinary city
revenues may be used for water and other press-
ing requirements, the city surplus lands not dedi-
cated to public use should be sold as provided by
law, and the funds appropriated for public build-
ings in common with other improvements.

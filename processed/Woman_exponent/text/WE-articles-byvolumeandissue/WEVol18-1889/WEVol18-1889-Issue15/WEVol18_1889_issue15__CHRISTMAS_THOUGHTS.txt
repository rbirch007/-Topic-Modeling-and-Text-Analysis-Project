CHRISTMAS THOUGHTS.

	Peace on earth good will to man, sung the
Angels;—for unto us a Child is born, unto us a
Son is given, and the government shall be upon
His shoulders. And His name shall be called
Wonderful Counselor, the Mighty God, the Ever-
lasting Father, the Prince of Peace.
	I sat in the quiet evening time and meditated on
the wondrous story of the incarnation of the Son
of God and of the love of God to man, and my
thoughts traveled back to the time more than
eighteen hundred years ago, and in imagination I
heard the angels sing “Glory to God in the
highest” and I could see the glorious star and the
Wise Men bending before the lowly bed of the
infant Jesus, and my heart throbbed with joy and
my spirit was filled with love as I contemplated
the glorious work of the Son of God,then I thought
of the Christmas approaching and how few while
in the midst of merriment and rejoicing would
remember Him whose birth they professed to
celebrate, and I knew that many would fill the
air with riot and drunkenness, and many would
blaspheme His Holy name; and my heart was
sad and I bowed my head and wept; and me-
thought a guide came to me and took me by the
hand and said, “Do you not know that these are
the Latter-days? And that the great work of the
Redeemer must and will be accomplished? Come
and look through the earth.” And I went, and
saw many things both in the abodes of the rich
and dwellings of the lowly, and I heard the merry
chimes of Christmas bells and saw troops of
happy children with hands filled with toys and
sweetmeats, and I saw houses and Churches
decorated with evergreens and great feasts were

[Column 2]
prepared; and many danced with glee and sent
gifts to one another; and some there were whose
hearts were filled with charity and love; and they
were pitiful to the poor and the oppressed and re-
lieved their wants with many good gifts, and they
caused the weary orphan to smile and the widows
heart to sing for joy because of the happy
Christmas time and the peace and joy they gave
returned fourfold into their own bosoms; for the
recording angel smiled because they would re-
ceive their reward from Him whose promise
never fails.; then I followed my guide to a large
and gloomy building enclosed with high walls,
and there were many men incarcerated therein
and some were criminals of the deepest dye, men
of violence and blood, men whose hearts were
hardened in sin, and others who were young with
the freshness of youth yet upon their brows, and
who were not yet hardened in crime; and some
were there who were guilty of no crime but were
imprisoned for conscience-sake, and they all
mingled together; the man steeped in sin and
wickedness, the youth, and the men who were
there for conscience-sake, and the friends of those
men whose hearts were filled with charity and
love sent them food and comforts in which all
shared and many good words were spoken to the
weak and erring and they were exhorted to seek a
better way and even the hearts of the prisoners
were made to rejoice because of the remembrance
of friends in the happy Christmas time and I know
that this also would receive a reward from Him
who said, I was sick and in prison and ye visited
me. . Then I thought I followed my guide to the
great cities of the old world and I saw many
Churches that were built some to St. Peter and some
Paul and to Saint Mary and Saint Jude, and there
were preachers preaching in them and tho’ they
sometimes spoke of the babe of Bethlehem; they
failed to warn the people that the second coming
of the Messiah was near at hand but instead they
said all things remain as they were from the be-
ginning until now; and some were asleep and
cared not, and some were fools and said in their
hearts, “There is no God.” And there were
secret combinations and society was shaken to
the foundation and great preparations for war
were being made,and there were strange diseases
and famine and earthquakes; then I thought of
the words of Him who was born, the babe of
Bethlehem. “When ye'see these things come to
pass know that the coming of the Son of man is
near at hand.”
	And then I thought we entered a building that
was not one of the Churches of the rich and
great, and many were congregated there and they
listened to the words of one, who had traveled
sea and land to bring to them the tidings that
God had again spoken from the heavens, and had
set His hand to bring about the restoration of all
things, warning the people to repent and be bap-
tized for the remission of their sins, and turn to
the Lord with all their hearts that they might be
prepared to meet the bridegroom. And some
there were that received this message gladly,
while others were exceedingly angry and said
“this fellow preaches strange doctrine;” we will
not hear a God speaking from the heavens, away
with him. And many men laid hands on him and
they beat him with many stripes and caused him to
flee unto another place, then I knew that the
same spirit that entered into Herod causing him to
murder the Innocents that he might destroy the
infant Jesus, was still upon the earth possessed
with great fury and determined to make war upon
the Saints because he knows that his time is short
and I also saw that the divine Spirit that filled
the hearts of the angels with love and joy when
they sang “Glory to God in the Highest;” on that
Christmas morning long ago, was still upon the
earth filling the Saints with faith and love and
enabling them to rejoice in the midst of trials and
persecution because they are looking and waiting

[Column 3]
for the second coming of Him who was born, the
babe of Bethlehem.
	Then with the eye of faith I was enabled to
pierce the future and I knew that the great work
of the Son of God would all be accomplished, and
not one of all the words that He had spoken
would fall to the ground, then I saw as in a vision
that all was changed and the earth no longer
groaned under a weight of sin and sorrow; for the
spirit that had made war so long against Him who
was once the babe of Bethlehem was bound with
a great chain and the earth was covered with
verdure and yielded her strength to man with
fruits of immortality, and in place of discord and
strife, there was peace and joy; for men had
thrown down their weapons of war and blood-
shed and had taken up the peaceful implements
of husbandry; and t y learned the art of war no
more; and deliverance was preached to the cap-
tive and the prison doors were opened. And
fathers and children rejoiced together; (and God’s
ancient people were restored), and instead of the
mosque of the Idolator a stately Temple was
raised to the name of the Most High and in it
they acknowledged the babe of Bethlehem as the
Messiah; and happy boys and girls were playing
in the streets of Jerusalem. And the whole
animal creation was subject to man as at the be-
ginning, and there was none to hurt or make
afraid for all nations acknowledged the reign of
the Messiah; who was once the babe of Bethle-
hem, and I understood how the government
would be upon His shoulders; and I heard my
guide softly whisper “Peace on earth good will
to man.”

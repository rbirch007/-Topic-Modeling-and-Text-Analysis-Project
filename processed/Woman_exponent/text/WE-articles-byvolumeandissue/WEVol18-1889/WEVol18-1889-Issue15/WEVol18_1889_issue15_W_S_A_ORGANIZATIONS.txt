W. S. A. ORGANIZATIONS.

	The Woman's Suffrage Association of Emery
Co., was organized at Castle Dale, Oct. 19th,
1889. Mrs. E. Howard of Salt Lake City was
chosen Chairman and Kathinka Andersen
Secretary pro. tem. Officers nominated and
sustained by vote. For Pres. Sarah Ann
Fulmer, of Orangeville; First Vice Pres. Mary
Ann Mc'Donough, of Castle Dale; Second,
Caroline A. Larsen, Castle Dale; Third, Adelia
Mackleprang, Huntington; Fourth, Paulina
Pace, Price. Secretary, Jenny Cox, Orange-
ville; Cor. Sec. Joanna, Curtis, Orangeville;
Treasurer, Marie Olsen, Castle Dale. Execu-
tive Committee, Amanda Shipp, Castle Dale,
Anne Pulsipher, Huntington; Jossia Childs,
Orangeville; Hannah Seely, Castle Dale;
Hannah U. Larsen, Castle Dale.
	All these nominations were solidly sustained.
Spirited speeches were delivered by several
of the ladies, including Mrs. Howard who ex-
plained the legal condition of woman clearly.
Mr. Wm. Howard made a very eloquent
speech on the subject also, all tending to stimu-
late the cause of the right and value of Wo-
man Suffrage in Utah and all the United
States.
	KATHINKA ANDERSON, Sec. pro. tem.

SCOFIELD.
Editor Exponent:
	I thought perhaps a few lines from this out
of the way place might be of interest to some
of the readers of the Exponent. Our Relief
Society is in a tolerably fair condition. We
hold two meetings monthly and are trying to
do all the good we can. The majority of the
Sisters work energetically,there are a few who
do not take so much interest.
	Our Relief Society was organized in Aug.,
1882, with the following officers: Harriet Wal-
ton, Pres. Ella G. Ingles, First Coun. Agnes
Russell, Second Coun. Rachel Ann Gatherum,
Secretary, and Mary Ann Morgan, Treasurer.
	Your Sister and Co-laborer,
		Rachel Ann Gatherum, Sec.

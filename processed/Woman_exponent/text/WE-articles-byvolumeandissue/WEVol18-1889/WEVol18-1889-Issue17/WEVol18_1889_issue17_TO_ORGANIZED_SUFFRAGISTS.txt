TO ORGANIZED SUFFRAGISTS.
To all Organized Suffragists Throughout the
Territory:	
	It is especially desired that you send, im-
mediately, a report stating the number of
members, and the work accomplished during
the past year; that our delegates may be able
to report the same to the National Convention
to be held in Washington next month.
			SARAH KIMBALL,
						PRESIDENT.
			RACHEL EDWARDS,
						SECRETARY.
Address:  55 N, W Temple St.

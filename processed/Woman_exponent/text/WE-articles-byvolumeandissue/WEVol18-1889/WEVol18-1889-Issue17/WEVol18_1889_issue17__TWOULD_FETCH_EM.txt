'TWOULD FETCH 'EM.

If registrars were ladies,
With eyes as black as Hades,
	Fair and petite,
	With laughter sweet,
As many a Buckeye maid is,

A simple invitation
To come for registration
	Would bring all men
	With ink and pen
With trifling hesitation.

Whate'er the voter's trade is
Or his complexion's shade is, 
	He’d go and sign
	In rain or shine,
If registrars were ladies.
				Columbus Dispatch,

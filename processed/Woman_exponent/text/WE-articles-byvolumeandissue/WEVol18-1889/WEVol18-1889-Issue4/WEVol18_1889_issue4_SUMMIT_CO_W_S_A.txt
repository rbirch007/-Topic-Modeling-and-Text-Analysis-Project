SUMMIT CO., W. S. A.

	A meeting was held at the Tabernacle, Coal-
ville, Thursday, May 30, to organize the Summit
Co., branch of the U. W. S. A. Mrs. Cluff was
called to the chair, and Miss Brown was made
Secretary protem. The meeting was addressed
by Mrs. S. M. Kimball of Salt Lake City, who
spoke ably, and earnestly of the advantages to be
derived from such an organization. She referred
to the intellectual stimulus such a society would
be to its members; preparations for its meetings
would involve study and mental improvement.
	Hon. W. W. Cluff, also addressed the meeting
advocating the organization of such a society. 
	The Summit County W. S. A.. was then
organized with forty members, five more have
since been added. The following officers were
chosen.
	President, Mrs. Boyden. First Vice-President
Mrs. Rheade. Second Vice-President, Miss
Brown. Third Vice-President, Mrs. Hudson.
Executive committee: Miss Ball, Mrs. Han-
nah Eldredge, Miss Nora Evans, Miss May
Cluff, Miss Lena Allison. Secretary, Miss
Maggie Salmon. Corresponding Secretary, Mrs.
Edith Cluff. Treasurer, Mrs. Lavinia Beard.
	Considerable enthusiasm  was manifesred. Mrs.
Kimball, on taking leave of the President, re-
marked that she felt gratified and encouraged at
the result of the meeting.
				M. BROWN, Secretary, protem.

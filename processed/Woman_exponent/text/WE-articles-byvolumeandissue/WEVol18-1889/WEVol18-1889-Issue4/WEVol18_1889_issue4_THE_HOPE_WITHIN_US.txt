THE HOPE WITHIN US.

	Young ladies, do we all realize our position as
members of the Church and Kingdom of God?
Are we fully alive to the necessity of preparing
ourselves for future usefulness? In the revela
tions of the Lord to us, given in these last days,
He tells us to “let the solemnities of eternity rest
on our minds.” Though the things of time are
small compared with those of eternity, is there not
enough in the prophecies and promises concern-
ing the things of time to fill our minds with a de-
termination, to let the perishable things of this
world be only a secondary consideration.
	The one main thing which we, as young men
and women in Israel, lack, is a proper apprecia-
tion of the duties which devolve upon us.
	If we knew that it was at the risk of our lives to
get drunk or swear, would we do so? If we were
in the presence of a great earthly king whom we
knew would take our lives if we committed such
offences,would we, to gratify a false taste or whim,
throw away our lives? No, indeed; but accord-
ing to that word which we all as Saints, believe
in, many of us break the laws of that King who is
able to take not only our lives, and end our earthly
existence, but has all power over our souls and
controls our destinies through all eternity.
									S. P.
	Manuscript Paper, Nephi.

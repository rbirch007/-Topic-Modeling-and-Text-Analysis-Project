IN MEMORIAM.

	In memory of Sister Elizabeth Stocking, who died April
9th, 1889, at South Jordan.
	Deceased was the daughter of the well known Henry
Arnold of Salt Lake City, who emigrated from England


when she was about two years old, her mother having died
previous, born June 19th 1847.	
	We the Sisters of the Y. L. M. I. A. unanimously adopt
the following resolutions to the memory of Sister Elizabeth
Stocking.	
	Whereas it has pleased bur Heavenly Father to call
from our midst by the hand of death our beloved Sister
and co-worker, therefore be it,
	Resolved that we recognize in the death of our Sister the
loss of a true friend and former counselor in the Associa-
tion. 
	Resolved that we esteem her goodness and uncomplain-
ing endurance, may we emulate her noble example as
sisters of the Association.
	Resolved that these resolutions be put on record and
that we condole with the family who are called to mourn
the loss of a kind and loving mother, eight children being
left orphans.
	Rosolved we send a copy to the family also one to the
EXPONENT for publication.
							MARIA HOLT,
							SOPHIA WARDLE,
							NAOMI V. OLIVER,
							THURZA CASE,
										Committee.

VISIT TO PRIMARIES.

	On the 6th of May, Sister Helena Madsen,
President of the Primaries of Sanpete, accom-
panied by Sister Louie B. Felt, President of
the Central Board of the Primary Associa-
tions, Counselor Lucy Berglund, and Stake
Secretary Sarah Metcalf, started to visit the
Primaries of this Stake, holding the first meet-
ing in Gunnison. There are 18 Primaries in
the Stake, which have been holding meetings
continuously, except where sickness hindered.
The meetings were exceedingly well attended;
notably in Fountain Green.
	The children in their exercises and be-
havior, manifested excellent progress; their
officers, in general, being energetic and solici-
tous for the welfare and advancement of their
precious charges.
	Sister Hulda C. Hjorth, President of the
Indianola Primary, furnishes a remarkable in-
stance of perseverance in the Primary cause.
She lives four miles from the meeting house;
in fact she lives in Utah County, about a mile
over the boundary.

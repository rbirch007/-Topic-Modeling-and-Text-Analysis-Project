NOTICE.

	The Sixtieth Semi-Annual Conference of the
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, will
commence at 10 o’clock, on Friday morning,
October 4th, 1889, in the Tabernacle, Salt Lake
City.
	The officers and members of the Church are
cordially invited to be present at the meetings.
				WILFORD WOODRUFF,
				GEORGE Q. CANNON,
				JOSEPH F. SMITH,
						First Presidency.

MILLARD COUNTY W. S. A.

	Meetings of the Millard W. S. A. have been
held in Meadow and Kanosh, Mrs. M. A. Y.
Greenhalgh, presiding.  Addresses were de-
livered by the President, and Vice President
Mrs. D. Olsen, Mrs. Gates, of Scipio, Mrs.
Thompson, &c.  Many of the sisters en-
rolled their names, and promised their sup-
port to the cause of woman's freedom.  Meet-
ings will shortly be held at Holden, Scipio,
Leamington, Oak Creek and Deseret, when,
doubtless, the members' roll will be largely in-
creased, so that the W. S. A. of Millard
County will compare favorably with the other
counties.						M. A. Y. G.

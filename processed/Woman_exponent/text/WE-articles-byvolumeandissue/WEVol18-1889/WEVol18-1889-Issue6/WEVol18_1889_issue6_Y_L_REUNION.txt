Y. L. REUNION.

	Report of the reunion meeting of the Y. L.
M. I. A. of Union Ward held June 17th,1889,
commencing at two p. m. Meeting opened
by singing, “All nature invites, etc." Prayer
by Bishop I. Phillips. Singing, “Improve
the shining moments,” etc. After which the
minutes of the last two meetings were read
and approved; roll called giving credit of four
officers and thirty-five members.
	Programme as follows: reading of the
manuscript paper by Lizzie Walker, it con-
tained some very interesting and instructive
contributions from the members of the associa-
tion; a letter from Sister M. A. Ball, was also
read by Lizzie Walker; song, Martha R.
Brady and Martha E. Cole; correspondence,
Laura Denney and Ann M. E. B. Evans;
organ solo, Annie Stone; recitation, Mary
Anderson; essay, M. A. Smart; select reading,
Emily Oborn; President Sarah Buxton then
spoke a short time. Song, Annie Montier;
recitation, Emma M. Burgan; recitation, Sarah
A. Brady.
	The speakers were Bishop I. Phillips, Coun-
selor M. H. Brady, John A. Barrett and
Brother Middleton. President Sarah Buxton
made a few closing remarks. Benediction by
Counselor M. H. Brady.
	Refreshments were next in order, including
strawberries and ice cream were then passed
around.

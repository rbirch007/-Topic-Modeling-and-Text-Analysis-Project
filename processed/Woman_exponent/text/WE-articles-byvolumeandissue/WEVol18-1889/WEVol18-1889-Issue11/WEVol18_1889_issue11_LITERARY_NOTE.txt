LITERARY NOTE.
	The National Magazine for November will con-
tain among other articles “Comparative Philolo-
gy,” by Professor Schele de Vere, Ph.D., J. U. D;
of the University of Virginia; Political Science,’
by Professor Raymond Mayo Smith, A. M., of
Columbia College, and “Shakespeare,” by F. W.
Harkins, Ph.D., Chancellor of the new National
University of Chicago, whose instruction by mail
and University Extension System for non-resi-
dents now meeting with such favor, will also be
explained in this number.
	In the future numbers will appear a symposium
comprising articles by prominent scholars and
statesmen giving their opinions on leading ques-
tions, such as “Darwin’s Theory,” “The Chinese
Question,” “Socialism,” and “Should Immigra-
tion be Restricted?” Published the first of each
month, at 147 Throop Street, Chicago. Subscrip-
tion price, $1.00 a year. Sample copy 10 cts.

PRIMARY AT MILL CREEK.

	On Tuesday June 4th, we had the pleasure of a
visit to the Primary Associations of Mill Creek
Ward, the four districts having come together, as
is their usual custom to hold an annual meeting.
There were present of visitors President Ellen C.
Clawson and her Counselor Mrs. C. Cobb also Dr.
R. B. Pratt. The meeting house presented an
inviting appearance with the beautiful children in
summer attire, and the vases filled with roses and
fragrant flowers from the field and garden. The
exercises were varied and a credit to the Associa-
tion. Each President had charge of the children
from her own district. The meeting opened with
singing, "All nature invites her praise to God."
Prayer by Elder Phillip Carlisle, singing, "Nearer
my Savior to Thee." Minutes read by the Secre-
tary and approved, also the yearly report. Pro-
gramme first district Mrs. Ann Lemon presiding;
Gertrude Cook, Washington Lemon and Mary
Fuller each gave a recitation, singing by all the
children "The Word of Wisdom," President
Clawson spoke a few words on this subject. Pro-
gramme second district, Mrs. McAllister presid-
ing exercises as follows: Lizzie Bowden, recita-

girls. Rhoda Ann Boam recitation, Etta Jenson

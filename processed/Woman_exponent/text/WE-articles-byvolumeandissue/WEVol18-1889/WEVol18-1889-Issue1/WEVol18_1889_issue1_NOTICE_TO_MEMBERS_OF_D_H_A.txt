NOTICE TO MEMBERS OF D. H. A.
	The month of May is the regular time to re-
new the membership to the Deseret Hospital
Association, and it is to be hoped the brethren
and sisters will come forward and pay the
annual fee of one dollar, and in this way help
to sustain a worthy and benevolent institution.
There are about nine hundred names on the
book of memberships, though but very few of
them have paid regularly since the first year.
Therefore it was thought best by the Execu- 
tive Committee to remind them of the time for
payment. New members are also earnestly
solicited to join.
				     E. B. WELLS, Secretary.

PRIMARY ANNUAL.
	Report of the Annual Meeting of the 20th.
Ward Primary held April 19th, 1889. Meet-
ing opened by singing “Beautiful Zion,”prayer
by Bishop Geo. Romney. Singing “Love at
Home.” Minutes of the last Annual meeting
read and approved. Programme as follows’
Address, Albert Romney, “The Primary.’:
Recitation, Katie Sansome; Recitation, Levi
Richards, “The Death of Sister Toone.” Song,
Pearl and Cathie Culmer; Essay Walter Rom-
ney, “The Latter-day Saints.” Recitation,
Isabelle Romney; Dialogue between three small


boys. Organ Solo, Katie Romney; Recitation,
Richard Evans; Dialogue between six of the
members. Recitation, Hazel Culmer; Song,
Lennie Savage; Recitation, Willie Salmon; 
Recitation, Pearl Culmer; Essay, Katie Hall
“Patience.” Song, Mary Salmon and Alice
Clowes; Recitation, Ray Savage; Reading,
Plet Lambourne; Dialogue between four little
girls. Song, Rosie Romney; Essay, Albert
Romney, “How to Succeed.” The speakers
were Sisters Jane Miller, L. G. Richards, Lilie 
Freeze, Louie Felt, L. A. Wells, P. L. Kimball,
E. C. Clawson, M. A. Freeze, and Brothers C.
W. Stayner, W. Willes, L. W. Richards, C.
R. Savage, J F. Simmons and Bishop Geo.
Romney. Counselor Maggie Sharp thanked
the children for their good behavior. Closed
by singing “Marching Homeward,” benedic-
tion by Brother Savage. Refreshments were
then passed around.
					S. PUZEY, Secretary.

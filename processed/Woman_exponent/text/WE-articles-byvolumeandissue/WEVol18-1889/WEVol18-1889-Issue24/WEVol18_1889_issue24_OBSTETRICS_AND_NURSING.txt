OBSTETRICS AND NURSING.
	Mrs. Isabella M. Lunn of Mill Creek, Salt Lake
Co., a student from Dr. Maggie C. Shipp's Obstet-
rical Class, passed a very satisfactory and rigid
examination and has also been set apart by some
of the Apostles to go forth and labor and assist
her own sex in their noble mission, in the line
marked out by her course of study.

IN MEMORIAM. 
	We are called upon to chronicle the death of a beloved 
sister and friend. Martha Ashby wife of W. H Ashby 
and daughter of Albert and Lettie Stevens.
	She was born April 12th 1867, at Holden, Utah, and 
passed peacefully away Nov. 7, 1889. She leaves a daughter
six weeks old and many relatives and friends to mourn 
her absence.
	We feel to say to those who mourn:
		Weep not for comes soon the drawing
		Of that bright and happy day,
		When we may receive the bodies 
		That have long been laid away.

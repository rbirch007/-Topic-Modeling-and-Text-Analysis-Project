OBITUARIES.	
	Died at Losee, Garfield Co. Utah Sunday Dec, 1st 1889,
of cancer in the breast, Eliza Jane Losee Cox; aged 47
years 3 months and 21 days. Deceased was the daughter 
of Isaac and Sarah Losee, and the widow of the late Or-
ville Cox. Was a true Latter-day Saint. and departed
this life in full faith of a glorious resurrection. 
					Respectfully,
							JOHN M. DUNNING. 
	At Morgan City Morgan County, Utah, Jan. 8th, 1890,
of neuralgia of the heart, Eliza Lissette Ursenbach Comp-
ton, beloved wife of George Compton. Deceased was the 
daughter of William and Phebe Durrant and was born 
July 6th, 1844, at Deanshanger Northamptonshire England.
Emigrated to Utah in 1896.
	She lived the life of a Latter-day Saint and peacefully 
fell asleep in full hope of a glorious resurrection.
										    P.D.

OBITUARY.

	It becomes our painful duty to record the death of our
beloved sister Anna Maria Westergard, born May 14,
1856, in Bedsted, Thidsted Co., Denmark. Was baptized
in 1865, came to Utah with her parents in 1866.  Married
Bro. J. Westergard Aug. 25, 1886, who had then only a
few months before buried a kind and affectionate wife.  She
came as an administering Angel to share the trouble of
a sorrowing father and five small children the youngest
only a few months old, which she cared for very tenderly,
and brought in time an addition to the sorrowstricken father
another little boy.


Sister Westergard was lady-like in appearance, a woman
of good judgment, and very sincere in her religion, a true
Latter-day Saint, and encouraged her husband and child-
ren to be faithful, and bore her testimony to the last.
During her short stay in Harrisville she was beloved by all,
she had acquired a good deal of useful knowledge and was
willing to impart it to others
	Truly we have lost a good teacher, a noble sister in our
Society.  But our consolation is, we have but buried her
remains, her spirit has gone to mingle in the society of the
Saints forever.
					MARY HEGSTED, President.
					CHRISTENA HEGSTED, Sec.
	Harrisville, Weber Co., Utah.

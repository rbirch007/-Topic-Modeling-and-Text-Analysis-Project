				RACHEL EDWARDS, Sec.

	There will be a General Conference of the Re-
lief Society held in the Salt Lake Assembly Hall
in this City Monday April 7, 1890. Meetings
commencing at 10 o'clock a. m. and 2 o'clock p. m.
the officers and members of the Relief Society
and all interested in its welfare and advancement
are cordially invited to attend. It is expected
that representatives will be present from each of
the Stake organizations and report at the meet-
ings. By order of the President,
						Zina D. H. Young.

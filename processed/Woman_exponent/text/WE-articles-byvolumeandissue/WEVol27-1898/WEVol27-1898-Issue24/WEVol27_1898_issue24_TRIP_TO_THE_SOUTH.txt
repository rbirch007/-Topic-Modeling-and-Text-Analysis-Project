TRIP TO THE SOUTH.

	President Lorenzo Snow contemplates a
visit to southern Utah commencing today,
May 15, that will extend over a period of
two weeks or more.
	The party, that will consist of Presidents
Lorenzo Snow, Jos. F. Smith, and Frank-
lin Richards, Apostles F. M. Lyman, Rudger
Clawson, together with Elders Seymour B.
Young and Bishop W. B. Preston, will
leave Salt Lake City on Monday 15, and
journey by train to McCune station, below
Milford, where the party will be met by
the Presidency of the St. George Stake and
several prominent citizens of St. George.
From this point they will drive to St.
George, where they will arrive by Tuesday
evening, all being well.
	After a brief stay in that city the entire
party will visit and hold meetings in the
following settlements ere they return to
Salt Lake City:
	Kanarra, Cedar, Parowan, Beaver,
Meadow, Kanosh, Fillmore, Holden and
Scipio.

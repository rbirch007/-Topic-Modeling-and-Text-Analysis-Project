CALL FOR PEACE MEETING.

	Pursuant to a call made upon the ladies
of Utah by the American member of the
International Peace Committee, arrange-
ments have been made to hold a public
mass meeting of citizens, under the auspices
of women’s organizations in Utah, in the
Salt Lake Assembly Hall, Monday evening,
May 15, 1899, simultaneously with women’s
meetings throughout this and other countries.
Held’s famous band will be in attendance to
inspire the audience with sweet music, and
five-minute speeches from prominent men
and women on the peace subject, with some
good vocal music, will make an attractive
program. Gov. Wells is expected to be
present and preside. Tae public is most
cordially invited.
			EMMELINE B. WELLS,
			ELMINA S. TAYLOR,

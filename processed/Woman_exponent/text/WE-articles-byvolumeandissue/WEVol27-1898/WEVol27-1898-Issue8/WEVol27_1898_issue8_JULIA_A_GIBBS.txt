	JULIA A. GIBBS.
	Sister Julia A. Gibbs departed this life August
22, 1898, after a short illness of two days. She
died as she had lived a faithful Latter-day Saint
and loved by all who knew her. She was a mem-
ber of the Relief Society of Portage since its first
organization, holding the office of first counselor
for twenty-one years. She was full of charity
and a worker that will be missed by the sick and
the poor who had learned to love her. She
leaves six children and a host of grandchildren to
mourn her loss. As a socie[-]y we deeply sympa-
thize with her family, and feel to commend them
to our Heavenly Father who alone can comfort
them.
	E. A. H.

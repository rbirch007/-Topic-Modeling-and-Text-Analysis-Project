RED TAPE.

	[The following from a Chicago paper illustrates
the too frequent course of events in volunteer,
service.]
	He started to war in splendid shape,
	But they held him in with a piece of tape,
				Red tape.
	He fought with canister and grape
	As well as he could.They were tied with tape,
				Red tape.
	He lived on what he could rake and scrape
	For his regular grub was tied with tape,
				Red tape.
	And many and many a narrow escape 
	He had because he was hampered with tape, 
				Red tape.
	He sickened. They laid his wasted shape
	On a nice, soft cot that was made of tape,
				Red tape.
	He died. His form they proceeded to drape
	In a winding sheet that they made of tape,
				Red tape.
	Let not the symbol of mourning be crape;
	Let the door knob simply be wrapped with tape
				Red tape.

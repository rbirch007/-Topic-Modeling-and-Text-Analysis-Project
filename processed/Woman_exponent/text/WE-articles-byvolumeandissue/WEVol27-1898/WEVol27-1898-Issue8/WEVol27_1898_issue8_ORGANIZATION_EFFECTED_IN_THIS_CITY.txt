ORGANIZATION EFFECTED IN THIS CITY.
	At a meeting of the Wasatch Chapter of
the Daughters of the Revolution held
Wednesday, Nov 16, in room 208 Templeton
building, the Wasatch Society of the State
of Utah, Daughters of the Revolution, was
organized and the following officers elected:
	Regent, Emmeline B. Wells; first vice
regent, Susa Y. Gates; second vice regent,
Clarissa S. Williams; recording secretary,
Julia Farnsworth; corresponding secretary,
Lucy W. Smith; registrar, Phebe Y. Beatie;
treasurer, Mary A. H. Cannon; historian,
Alice S. M. Horne, and chaplain, Maria Y.
Dougall.
	The newly elected regent expressed her-
self as being well pleased that the State or-
ganization had been effected and voiced the
sentiment of every R. D. present in wish-
ing prosperity to the society and a steady
growth of its membership.
				L. W. S., Sec. pro tem.

RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	The General Conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Assembly hall,
Salt Lake City, Wednesday, October 5,
1898, meetings commencing at 10 a. m.
and 2 p. m. as usual. It is desirable that
each Stake organization of the Society
should be represented by the president or
an authorized representative. All members
of the Society and of the Church are most
cordially invited. The meetings of the con-
ference are open to all interested in chari-
table or philanthropic work.
			ZINA D. H. YOUNG,
						President.
			JANE S. RICHARDS,
			BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
						Counselors.
	E. B. WELLS,
			Secretary

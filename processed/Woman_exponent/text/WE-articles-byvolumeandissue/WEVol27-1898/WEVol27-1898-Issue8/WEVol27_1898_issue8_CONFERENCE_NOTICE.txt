CONFERENCE NOTICE.

	The Sixty-ninth Semi-Annual Confer-
ence of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-
day Saints will convene in the Tabernacle
on Thursday morning, October 6, 1898, at
10 o’clock a. m. Officers and members of
the Church are invited to be present.
			LORENZO SNOW,
			GEORGE Q. CANNON,
			JOS. F SMITH,
				First Presidency.

	The business meeting of the National
Woman’s Relief Society will be held in the
Salt Lake Assembly Hall in this city on
Monday, October 3, at 2 p. m. A punctual
attendance members is very desirable.
		E. B. WELLS, Secretary.

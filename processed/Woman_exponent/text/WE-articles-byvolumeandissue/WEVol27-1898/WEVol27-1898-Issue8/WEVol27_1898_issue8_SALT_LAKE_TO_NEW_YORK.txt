SALT LAKE to NEW YORK.
	By a recent change in schedule the
Overland Limited of the Union Pacific
and North-western in connection with
the L S & M. S. and N. Y. C. & H. R.
R. R., now makes the unparalleled
time record of only three days to New
York. Train leaves Salt Lake 7:00 a.
m., Ogden 8:10 a. m. daily, arrives Chi-
cago 7:45 a. m. and Grand Central
depot, New York City, 10:35 a. m.,
second and third days respectively. 
Only one change of cars and twelve
hours quickest time.
	For purchase of tickets and reser-
vation of berths, call at the “Old 
Stand,” 201 Main Street, or address
H. M. Clay, General Agent.

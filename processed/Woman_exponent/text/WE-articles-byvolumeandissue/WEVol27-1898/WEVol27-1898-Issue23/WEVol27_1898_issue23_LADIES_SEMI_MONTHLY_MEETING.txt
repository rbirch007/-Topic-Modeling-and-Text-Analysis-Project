LADIES’ SEMI-MONTHLY MEETING.

	Semi-monthly meeting held April 1, 1899,
with President M. I. Horne presiding.
Singing, “Come, come ye Saints.” Prayer
by Counselor E. Webb. Singing, “O,
say what is truth.” Minutes read and
approved.
	President Horne said she was pleased
to meet with the sisters again, as she
had not been able to be present for so long.
Thought we needed to live near unto
the Lord, the adversary was trying hard
to creep into the hearts of the people. We
must maintain our integrity to the truth
and be faithful and true. The young peo-
ple were being tried today, there were
many who had not been tried among
us, the children had grown up with such
abundant blessings, they had not been
tried as those who came across the plains
and met those hardships. We should
be careful of the reading matter we give
our children. When the people keep the
commandments of God He always blesses
them abundantly. We should prepare for
the coming of the Son of Man, not be like
the five foolish virgins; we must be united

MANSON JESSE WOODWARD.
	At Kansas City, Mo., on the evening of July 18,
1898, Manson Jesse Woodward, after a long
illness entered into rest, aged seventy-seven years 
and three months, born April 21, 1821, in Peters-
ham Worcester Co., Mass. Impressive funeral
services were held at the house Wednesday 20
burial in Mount Vernon, at Atchison, Kansas,

Tenderly folded in mother earth’s breast,
All that is mortal is now laid to rest;
Long suffering thro’ day, watching thro’ night
Ne’er more can trouble the worn spirit’s flight.
Out from the border-land-life’s fitful dream,
Into life infinite where peace reigns supreme.
Faith points away from the flower-decked sod,
To Soul’s release with a merciful God.
	 L. M. H.

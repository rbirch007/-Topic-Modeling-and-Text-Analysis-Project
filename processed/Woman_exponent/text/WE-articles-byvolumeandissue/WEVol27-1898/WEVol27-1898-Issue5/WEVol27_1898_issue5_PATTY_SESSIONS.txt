PATTY SESSIONS.
	Patty Sessions was born in Bethel, Ox-
ford County, Maine, 1795, was married to
David Sessions, June 28, 1812, and settled
in the adjoining town of Newry.
	She was a member of the Methodist
church for seventeen years, and was bap-
tized into the Church of Jesus Christ of
Latter-day Saints in 1834. She was the
mother of eight children and survived them
all but two; she has fifty-three grandchil-
dren, one hundred and thirty-seven great
grandchildren and twenty-two great great
grandchildren, making in all two hundred
and fourteen posterity.
	They sold their property and left their
native land in June 1836, to share in the
troubles and persecutions of the Saints.
They traveled two thousand miles, camp-
ing out all the way, to Caldwell Co., Mis-
souri, they then purchased land and re-
m[--]ned in that place until 1838, when they
were driven out, they then went to Han-
cock Co. Illinois.

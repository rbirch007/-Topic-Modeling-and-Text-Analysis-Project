	Dr. Margaret Roberts said she wanted to
do all she could to help the young people,
felt the Relief Society work was a great one
and felt we were greatly blessed as a peo-
ple. She said her son was one who had
volunteered to go to war, it was only a
mother who understood how she felt at this
time, said the remarks that had been made
about the Lord protecting His people were
very comforting to her.
	President Horne said she had enjoyed the
meeting very much, said she knew it had
been beneficial and made beautiful closing
remarks. Said she felt to say that the
young men who went to war and who had
lived their religion would be all right and
would return in safety.
	Meeting adjourned for two weeks. Sing-
ing, Doxology. Benediction by Sister

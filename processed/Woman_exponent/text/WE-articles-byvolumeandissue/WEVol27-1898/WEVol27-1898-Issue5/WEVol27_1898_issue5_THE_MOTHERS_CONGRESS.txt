THE MOTHERS' CONGRESS.

	Program of the Mothers' Congress to be
held in Salt Lake City, June 30, July 1
and 2.
	Thursday evening, June 30—Reception
to guests from the Denver Biennial and
others.
	Friday morning session—Some Prin-
ciples in Discipline. The Problem of the
Child's Freedom. The Children's Mother.
The Reconciliation of Duties.
	Friday afternoon session—Symposiums,
Cultivation of Moral qualities. Ten min-
ute talks on Truthfulness, Obedience,
Gentleness, Helpfulness, Reverence, Rights
of Others. The Mother as a Confident.
	Friday evening session—Platform meet-
ing, general subject, Motherhood as a Pro-
fession; Relative Importance among Pro-
fessions.
	Professional Training Associations—The
clubs, congresses, etc. The Father's Re-
sponsibility in Child Training.
	Sister Professions—The School and
Home. The Washington Congress.
	Saturday morning session—Hints in the
Care of Young Children. The Children's
Contact with Nature. The Sick Child.
Wider Motherhood.
	Saturday afternoon session—Children's
Stories, Children's Music—Illustrated.
The Mother's Incentive for Public Spirit.


All club women attending the Biennial
in Denver are requested to wear upon their
arrival a knot of light blue ribbon on the
shoulder, for identification by the reception
committe, members of which will wear the
colors yellow and white

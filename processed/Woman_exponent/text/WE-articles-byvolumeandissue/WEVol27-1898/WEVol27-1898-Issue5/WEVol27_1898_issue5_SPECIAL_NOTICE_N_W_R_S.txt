SPECIAL NOTICE N. W. R. S.

	The Summer Conference of the Nation-
al Woman’s Relief Society which was
postponed from June until August, will
not be held this season on account of ex-
isting circumstances that were not antici-
pated at the time the appointment was
made at the April conference last spring.
The General conference of the Society
will be held as usual in October, of which
due notice will be given.

ZINA D. H. YOUNG, General President.
E. B. WELLS, General Secretary.

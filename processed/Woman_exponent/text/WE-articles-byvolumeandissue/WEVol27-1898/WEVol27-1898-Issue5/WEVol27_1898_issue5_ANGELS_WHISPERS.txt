ANGELS' WHISPERS

Sweeter than the breath of roses
	Lingering on the dewy air,
Wafting as the daylight closes
	To my heart bowed down with care,
Blessed thought that Father loves me,
		And doth hear my fervent prayer.

Gentle words in softest accent,
	Whispered in my listening ear:
Weep not for thy boy though absent,
	Dry that secret burning tear;
Thy Creator knows and loves thee!
	Lift thy soul and do not fear.

Guarding e'er with unseen power
	Sheltering him so far away,
Though where shot and shell doth shower,
	He the missiles flight can stay,
Ever trust the God of Battles!
	Live thou near Him day by day.
					ELLIS R. SHIPP. 
July 23, 1898.

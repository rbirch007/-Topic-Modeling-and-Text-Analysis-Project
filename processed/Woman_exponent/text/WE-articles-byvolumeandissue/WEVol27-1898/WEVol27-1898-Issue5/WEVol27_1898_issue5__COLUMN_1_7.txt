OFFICERS MEETING,
	The officers’ meeting of the general con-
ference was held in the Assembly hall in
this city April 9, 1898, President Zina D.
H. Young presiding. Meeting opened
with singing “O, my Father.” Prayer
was offered by Dr. R. B. Pratt. Singing
“We thank Thee O, God for a prophet.”
	The president said, “We are all happy
and gratified to be able to meet together to
see each others faces, and especially are we
glad to see those from a distance whom we
seldom see, and I thank my Heavenly
Father that we enjoy so many blessings.
I would like to hear from Sister Rhoda
Hinman from Canada.”
	Sister Hinman said, “We have the Holy
Spirit with us. I remember when the Re-
lief Society was first organized; this work is
increasing from North to South and from
East to West.” Sister Hinman made
other interesting remarks.

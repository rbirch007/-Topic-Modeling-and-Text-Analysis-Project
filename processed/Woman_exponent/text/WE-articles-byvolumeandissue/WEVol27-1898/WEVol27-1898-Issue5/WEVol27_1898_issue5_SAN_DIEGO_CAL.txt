		SAN DIEGO, CAL.
	The San Deigo branch of the Church of
Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints having
increased to such a goodly number Presi-
dent E. H. Nye of the California mission
and Conference President G. F. Harding
effected a Ladies Relief Society, May, 11,
1898.
	The following officers were set apart un-
der the hands of the above named presi
dents and Elders F. B. Platt, J. A. Love-
less and W. E. Stoker, traveling Elders in
San Diego City: Sister Amelia Jewell, 
president; Sister Mary N. Gabrielson, first
counselor; Sister Ida E. Hunt, second coun-
selor; Sister Louisa Edwards, secretary;
Sister Anna Buhorn, treasurer; Sisters
Louisa Swanson and Adelia C. Kingsbury,
teachers.
	Three meetings have been held during
the month, each meeting was well attend-
ed and the Spirit of God was enjoyed. The
work is being carried on very successfully.
The prospects for the future are bright for
accomplishing a good work.

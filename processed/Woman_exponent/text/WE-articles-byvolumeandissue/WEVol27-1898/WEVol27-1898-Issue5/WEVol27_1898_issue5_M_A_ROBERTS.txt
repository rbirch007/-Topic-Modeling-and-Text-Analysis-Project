					M. A. ROBERTS.


Mrs. Walworth, director general of the
Woman’s National War Relief Association,
is in Washington attending to the inter-
ests of that association. She has been in
conference with the presidents of the asso-
ciations and also with the surgeon-general
of the army and navy. She spent one day
at Camp Alger visiting the division field
and regimental hospitals. She reports that
conditions are improved at Camp Alger,
and that the sick men look comfortable
under their musquito nets, which relieve
them from the torment of the troublesome
flies.

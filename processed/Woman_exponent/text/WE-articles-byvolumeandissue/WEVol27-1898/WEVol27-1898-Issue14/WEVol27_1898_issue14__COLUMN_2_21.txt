	There are at present six sisters laboring
in the Hawaiian mission, and we are striv-
ing in our weak way to accomplish the
purpose for which we were sent. The Lord
has greatly blessed us while laboring in
these lands and we are thankful that we are
able to spend a portion of our time in His
vineyard, in striving to raise the people to a
higher plane of morality. Our labors con-
sist in taking care of the Mission Home,
working in the Relief Society, Sunday
School, Mutual Improvement Association,
Primary Association; also teaching the
children music. We are striving to ac-
quire the Hawaiian language and feel
grateful that the Lord has blessed us with a
portion of it. Peace and contentment is in
our midst and at the dawning of another
new year everything seems to be in a pros-
perous condition.

the cause of truth we are,

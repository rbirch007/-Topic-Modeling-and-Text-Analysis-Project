	ELLA BRANCH, Sec.


At the state convention of the Daughters
of the American Revolution at Norfolk, Va.,
Mrs. Herbert Doyle Brackett of Alex-
andria, reported that $41,000 of the amount
required for the building which the Daugh-
ters propose to erect in Washington was
now in hand. It was decided that further
contribution to the building fund would be
asked at each chapter. The building is to
be the national headquarters for the society,
and will be known as Continental Hall.

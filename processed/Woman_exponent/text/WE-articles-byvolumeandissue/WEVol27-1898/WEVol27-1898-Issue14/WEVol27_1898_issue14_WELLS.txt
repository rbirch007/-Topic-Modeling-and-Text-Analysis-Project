	The following interesting and carefully
prepared program was rendered: Poem on
Christmas, Mrs. M. A. Freeze. Christmas
story, Mrs. E. B. Wells, also one by Mrs.
Ruth M. Fox. Christmas reminiscences
by Mrs. Maria Francis. Poem, Mrs. L. D.
Alder. Several beautiful musical selections
were rendered, after which the hostess and
her charming daughters served delicious re-
freshments. A short time was spent in
social converse after which the club ad-
journed to meet in the new year of 1899,
Jan. 31, at 7:30 in room 208, Templeton.

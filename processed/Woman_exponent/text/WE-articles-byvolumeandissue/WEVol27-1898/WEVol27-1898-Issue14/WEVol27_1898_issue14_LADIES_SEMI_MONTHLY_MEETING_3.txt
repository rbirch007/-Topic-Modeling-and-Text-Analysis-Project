LADIES SEMI-MONTHLY MEETING.

	The Ladies’ Semi-monthly meeting was
held in the 14th ward hall Saturday, Dec.
17, 1898, Counselor E. J. Stevenson presid-
ing in the absence of President Horne.
Singing, “For the strength of the hills we
bless thee.” Prayer by Counselor Bath-
sheba W. Smith. Singing, “Welcome
best of all good meetings.” Minutes read
and approved.
	A sister, name unknown, bore a faithful
testimony. Counselor E. Webb spoke of
the spirit of meekness and love and said we
should dedicate ourselves to the Lord in
the morning and evening, be careful and do
our duties.
	Sister Lydia D. Alder said this was the
first of the year when our hearts were
drawn out to one another, and our thoughts
are upon Him whose birth we celebrate.
She did not think we could realize the love
He bears for His children. He was an ex-
ample of humility and obedience. “Let us
love one another and help one another.”
Another sister spoke on honest tithing and
donations.

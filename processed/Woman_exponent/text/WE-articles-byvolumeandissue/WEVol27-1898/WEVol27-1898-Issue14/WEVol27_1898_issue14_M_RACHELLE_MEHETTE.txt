			M. RACHELLE MEHETTE.


Mrs. Mary A. Wright was elected to the
Idaho House of Representatives from
Kootenai County at the recent elections, in
addition to Mrs. Clara Campbell of Ada
County, and Mrs. Harriet Noble of Boise.

	Mrs. Ellen C. Johnson, superintendent of
the Sherborn prison for women, has been
appointed by Governor Wolcott as a dele-
gate from Massachusetts to the Convention
of the National Prison Association to be
held at New Orleans, Jan. 21 to 24. She
is announced to address the convention on
“Prisons for Women.’’

THE NEW YEAR.

Tho' clouds of disappointmen
Ma g[-]ther in life's sky,
Do not despair, the sun is there,
And soon will be so bright and fair,
And then those dearest hopes of thine
Like sunlight in thy path will shine;
For now the new year brightly dawns,
The old year with its fear is gone,
	And joy awaits thee.
Look not backward with regret
To the faded year—
See, the star of hope is bright,
It will guide thee to the light,
Better days are yet in store,
Tho’ the days that are no more
Like a tender flower lay,
Folded in the heart away,
	But joys awaits thee.

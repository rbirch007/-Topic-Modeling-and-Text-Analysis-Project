U. W. P. C.

rooms of Dr. Ellis R. Shipp, Friday eve-
ning, Dec. 30, 1898. After opening prayer
by Lydia D. Alder the secretary called the
roll, responses being given by quo-
tations on Christmas from favorite authors.

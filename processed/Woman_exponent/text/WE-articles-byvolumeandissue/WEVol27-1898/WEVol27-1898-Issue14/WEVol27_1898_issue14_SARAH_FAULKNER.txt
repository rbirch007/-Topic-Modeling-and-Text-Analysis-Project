SARAH FAULKNER.

	On Monday, Dec. 5, 1898, Sister Sarah
Faulkner passed over to the great beyond.
	Sister Faulkner was born in Chadwick,
Worcestershire, England, Feb. 6, 1830,
and was sixty-eight years old. She came
to Utah in 1853 and has labored very faith-
fully ever since, not only being an active
member in all her Church duties, but she
desired to aid in making our city attractive
as far as lay in her power, by beautifying
her surroundings.
The deceased was a teacher in the Relief
Society for many years and she did her
work faithfully in sun or shine. She was al-
ways charitable and kind to the needy. Mrs.
Faulkner was a very estimable lady and
leaves numerous friends to mourn her death
besides one son, her other five children
having gone before her. The cause of her
death was a malignant cancer, from which
she has been a sufferer the past two years. 
	ELLEN KELSEY, Sec.
Springville, Utah.

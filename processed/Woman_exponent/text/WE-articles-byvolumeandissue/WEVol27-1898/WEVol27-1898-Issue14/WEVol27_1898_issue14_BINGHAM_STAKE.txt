BINGHAM STAKE.
	The quarterly conference of the Relief
Society of Bingham Stake convened at
Lewisville, December 17, 1898, President
E. J. Bennett presiding. Opened by sing-
ing “The morning breaks.” Prayer by 
Bishop Jardine. Singing “Now let us re-
joice.” President E. J Bennett made a
few opening remarks, minutes of the pre-
vious forenoon conference were read and 
approved.
	President James E. Steele explained to
the sisters the necessity of sending in
their charitable reports to the Bishops.
	President E. J. Bennett said we should
give what we have to give through the Re-
lief Society that we may have credit on the
books, and then forward our half yearly re-
ports to the Bishops that the sisters may
have credit for what they do, and when 
the teachers go around they should try and 
find out if there are any who are in need,
and those who may need assistance should
not be too proud to receive help. We should
be careful not to make our business too 
public so as to hurt any one’s feelings.

		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Resolved, That as our heavenly Father in His
wisdom has seen fit to call home our sister to a
better life, w  the officers and members of the 6th
ward Relief Society express our sincere sympathy
to the bereaved husband and family in their loss,
feeling, also, that they have been greatly blessed
with her wise and faithfu[-] counsel and example. 
Be it further
	Resolved, That these resolutions be placed up- 
on our society records, also a copy sent to the
“Woman’s Exponent” for publication
				ISABELLA WEST, Pres.

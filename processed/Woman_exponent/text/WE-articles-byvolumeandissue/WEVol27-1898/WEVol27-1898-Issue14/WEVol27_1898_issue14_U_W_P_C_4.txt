U. W. P. C.

	The U. W. .P. C. met in the “Ex-
ponent” office February 28, 1899. The
president being absent the vice president,
Mrs. C. G. R. Wells presided. Opening
prayer by Dr. E. R. Shipp, after which the
secretary called the roll, responses being
given by quotations from the poet Moore.
Mrs. Francis proposed the name of Mrs.
Ida Cannon as a member. Mrs. Cannon
was unanimously elected. Mrs. L. D.
Alder read a communication from the
General Federation of Women’s Clubs,
Atlanta, Ga., and one from Chester, Penn.,
regarding organizing Federation traveling
clubs, and one from the Utah Federation re-
garding badges. The following interesting
program was then given:
	Poem by Dr. Ellis R. Shipp; Current
Events by Dr. Romania B Pratt; Tid Bits
by Mrs. H. V. Kimball, followed by dis-
cussion of members. Before the benediction
was offered all present joined in extending
well wishes and congratulations to Mrs. E.
B.Wells, this being the anniversary of her
birth, or nearly so, as she was born on the
29th. May she meet with us for many,
many years to come.

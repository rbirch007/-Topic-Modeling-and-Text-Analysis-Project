REORGANIZATION.
	Minutes of the annual meeting of the 8th
ward Relief Society held February 4th,
1899, Vice President S. J. Chamberlain
presiding. The secretary called the roll,
after which the vice president stated that
the business of the meeting was to hear the
annual reports and the election of officers,
(on account of the death of the president,
Kathrine K. Palmer) for the ensuing two
years. The reports were read and approved.
Sarah J. Chamberlain was elected president;
Ann Cartwright, 1st vice president; Martha
A. Shelmerdine, 2nd vice president; Abby
A. Piggott, secretary; Elizabeth C. Frost,
treasurer, the vote in each case being
unanimous. Martha Morris was elected to
fill the vacancy in the board of trustees
caused by Martha A. Shelmerdine being
chosen vice president. Anna E. Hunter
and Louie Chamberlain were received as


members of the society. It was moved and
seconded that the minutes be accepted as
read; carried.

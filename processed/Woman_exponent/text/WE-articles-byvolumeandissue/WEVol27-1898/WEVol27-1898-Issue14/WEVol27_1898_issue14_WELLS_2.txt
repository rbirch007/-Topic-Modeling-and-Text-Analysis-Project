address of the State Regent, Mrs. E. B. Wells.
	Monday, January 16, the D. R. Society
met in the Templeton and the regent gave
her address and the Constitution was unani-
mously adopted. A State Council was
appointed, and committees on Press and
Printing and Decoration.
	The members of the Council are Mrs.
Carlie Young Cannon, Mrs. Isabel M.
Whitney Sears, Mrs. J. P. Murdock Farns-
worth, Mrs. Sarah Farr Smith, Mrs. Myra
Young Rossiter, Mrs. Ella Wilcox Hyde.
COMMITTEE ON PRESS.
	Mrs. Alice S. M. Horne, Miss Catharine
Wells, Miss Claire Clawson.
ON DECORATION.
	Mrs. Phebe Young Beatie, Miss Mabelle
Snow, Mrs. Ella Wilcox Hyde.
	The next meeting of the society will be
held in the Templeton, room 208, on Fri-
day, February 16, at 3 p. m., when busi-
ness pertaining to the annual meeting will
be transacted, and it is desirable all members
should be present and participate in the pro-
ceedings. It is the intention of the society
to incorporate shortly under the laws of the
State.

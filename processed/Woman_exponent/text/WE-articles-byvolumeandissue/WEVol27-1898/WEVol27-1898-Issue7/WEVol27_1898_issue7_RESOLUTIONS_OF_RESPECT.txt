		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Whereas, The hand of Divine Providence has
removed our beloved sister from a scene of her
temporal labors, we are desirous of testifyin our
respect for her memory and expressing our
earnest and affectionate sympathy with the house-
hold deprived of her earthly society; therefore be 
it
	Resolved, That while we bow with humble sub-
mission to the will of the Most High, we do not
the le s mourn for our sister who has been taken
from us.
	Resolved, That we lament the loss of an active
member of the Society, whose utmost endeavors
were exerted for its welfare and prosperity; a
friend and companion who was dear to us all, and
whose life was a standard of emulation to her co-
laborers.
	Resolved, That the heartfelt sympathy of this
Society be extended to her family in their be-
reavement.
	MARY BAXTER, President,
	BERTHA NIELSON,
	CHRISTANIA CHRISTENSEN, Counselors,
	MAGGIE KING, Secretary.

			ELLA BRANCH, Sec.


He who lends money to the poor is often
better than he who gives them alms.

	To do well is to be well. Persevere in the
thought, “I shall be better tomorrow,”
and it will help you to become so. It has
been said that no man ever died without his
own consent. Never get your own consent
to dying. Resolve to live; resolve to be
well. You yourself must make the effort;
you must work out your own salvation.
—Charles Kingsley.

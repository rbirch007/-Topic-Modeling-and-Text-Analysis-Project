To our great Washington?


Ah! may the Fortress wa[-]ls
	Of Ceuta be broken down,
While tyranny deeper falls 
	Till none shall bow to the crown.
May shackle and chain and cell
	Yield forth to Liberty,
And our children live to tell—
	America keeps the Key!
		AUGUSTA JOYCE CROCHERON.
Salt Lake City, August, 1898.

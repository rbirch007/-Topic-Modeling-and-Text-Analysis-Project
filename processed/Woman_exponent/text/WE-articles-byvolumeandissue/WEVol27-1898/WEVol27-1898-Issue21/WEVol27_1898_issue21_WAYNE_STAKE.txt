WAYNE STAKE.
	The Relief Society conference of Wayne
Stake was held at Loa, January 31, 1899.
The presidency of the Stake Relief Society
and a number of brethren were present and
an excellent spirit prevailed.
	The Loa choir sang “Come O, Thou
King of Kings.” Prayer, Bishop W. E.
Hanks. Singing, “Let us pray.”
	President Jane S. Coleman presiding,
spoke upon the magnitude of the work we
are engaged in, said much could be accom-
plished by the Relief Society when they
were active in their labors. She had re-
ceived reports from the local presidents,
showing satisfactory results with regard to
gathering up grain for storing. Read the
statistical and financial report for the year
past, and said our average should be higher.

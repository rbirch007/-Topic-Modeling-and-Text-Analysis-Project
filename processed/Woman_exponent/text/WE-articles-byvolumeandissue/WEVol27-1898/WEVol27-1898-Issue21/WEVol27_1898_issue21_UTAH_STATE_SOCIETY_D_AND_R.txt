UTAH STATE SOCIETY D. AND R.

Daughters of the Revolution:
	Trusting, ladies, that you will believe
that I fully appreciate the honor you have
conferred upon me in electing me State
Regent of the Utah Society Daughters of
the Revolution—let me assure you of my
devotion to our State, to the country, its
institutions and its welfare present and
future, and promise in so far as I am capable
with your help to maintain the dignity of
this society as a part of our fair common-
wealth, and hope and believe that in good
faith all who have united in this society
will share the responsibility which devolves
upon us all in this new organization, of
making it a success a sa part of the General
Society, and a credit to our own State for
whose honor and fair name we are all so
justly ambitious.

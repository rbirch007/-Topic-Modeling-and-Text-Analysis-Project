A WORD OF ADVICE.

	We celebrated the 17th of March and had
a good program rendered, two pieces were
read from the “Exponent,” and our presi-
dent, Sister Lucy Wilson, spoke in favor of
the “Exponent” and advised the sisters to
sustain that paper. Sister L. Liljenquist
also spoke in behalf of the “Exponent.”
	I have been thinking that if our by-laws
could be changed so as to have our annual
meeting come on the 17th of March instead
of the first Saturday in February, I believe
that all the sisters in the north would ap-
preciate that change, as the first Saturday
in February is always so cold, and it seems
it is a very bad time for mothers to get to
meeting. If it could be changed to March
17th, at 2 p. m., and it was made our an-
nual day for voting in our officers, I think
we would have a much better time at this
celebration, as we in Hyrum celebrate the


9th of May, it being the anniversary of the
organization of our Relief Society, it seems
like three annuals so near together is too
many.  I thought I would just make this
suggestion and perhaps those who have the
authority to make the change would think
it wise to do so in the future.
	Wishing the “Exponent” success, Iremain,
		Your sister in the Gospel of Christ,

WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


The annual convention of the Daughters
of the Revolution will be held at the Hotel
Stratford, Philadelphia, on April 24.
While the actual date of the business ses-
sion is Monday, April 24, the meeting will
extend over the entire week, beginning on
Sunday 23, with a special service for the
Daughters at Christ Church.

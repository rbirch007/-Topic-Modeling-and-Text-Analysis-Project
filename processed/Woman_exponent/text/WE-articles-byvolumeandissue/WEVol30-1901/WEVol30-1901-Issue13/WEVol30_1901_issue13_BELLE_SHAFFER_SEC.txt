			BELLE SHAFFER, Sec.

	Since the writing of the above a class for
Gospel instruction has been formed under


the auspices of our society, with Sister
Susa Young Gates, who intends remaining
in New York City for some time, for the
teacher. The class has had two lessons,
each on Faith. The class will meet once a
week, every Thursday, fourteen attended
last week. The class is open for names,
whether society members or not.
		B.S.

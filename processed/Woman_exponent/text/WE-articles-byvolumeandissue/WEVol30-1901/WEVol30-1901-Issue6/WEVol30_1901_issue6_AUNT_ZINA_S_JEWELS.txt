AUNT ZINA’S JEWELS.

Sisters, we have lost our Mother,
	Honored president and friend!
She who bade us love each other, 
	And the rights of Truth defend.
She who ne’er was known to falter,
	In the days of sore distress,
Placing all upon the altar,
	In the cause of Righteousness.
Let us follow her example,
	Place her jewels in each breast,
Chastity, with impulse noble,
	Valor, to endure each test,
Truth and trustfulness in Father,
	Hope, Humility and Love,
Bound in one bright chain together,
	Linking hearts with God above.

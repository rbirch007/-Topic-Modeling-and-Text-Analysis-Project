RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT
	Resoved, That we, the officers and members of
the Relief Society, feel the loss of such a faithful
worker in the cause of the Lord in our ward.
	Resolved, That we extend our sympathy to the 
bereaved husband, father, mother, brothers, sis-
ters and relatives, and earnestly pray that the 
holy Spirit of God may rest down upon them and
comfort them.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
sent to the husband and family, one be inserted in
our records and one be published in WOMAN'S
 EXPONENT. 
		Mary E. Hanks.

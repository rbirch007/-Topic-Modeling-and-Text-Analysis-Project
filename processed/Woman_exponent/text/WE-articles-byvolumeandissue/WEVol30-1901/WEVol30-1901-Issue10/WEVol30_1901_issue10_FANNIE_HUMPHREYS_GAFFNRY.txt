FANNIE HUMPHREYS GAFFNRY.


Mrs. Kate Upton Clark, the well known
story writer and popular lecturer to club-
women, has prepared for the D R. and
D. A. R. chapters a lecture called “An
Obscure Heroine of the Revolution,” deal-
ing with the life of her great grandmother,
Bridget Munroe Maxwell, and taken from
forty or fifty of her great grandfather’s
letters.

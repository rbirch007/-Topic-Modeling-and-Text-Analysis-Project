RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Resolved, That the members of the Parowan 
Ward Relief Society appreciate the Christ-like
example of Sister Gurr, and desire to profit by her
worthy example and humbly acknowledge that

[Column 2]
an over-ruling Providence doeth all things well. 
	That a copy of this remembrance be placed
upon the records of our Relief Society, one be 
sent to her devoted and worthy husband and chil-
dren and one to the Woman's Exponent for 
publication.

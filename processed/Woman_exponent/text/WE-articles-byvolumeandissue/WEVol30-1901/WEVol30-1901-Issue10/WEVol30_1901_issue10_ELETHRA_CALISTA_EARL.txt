		ELETHRA CALISTA EARL.
	In memory of Sister Elethra Calista Earl,
daughter of Edward and Emily Abbott Bunker,
who died June 30, 1901, was born November 9,
1859, at Ogden, Utah.
	She leaves a husband, several children and
numerous relatives and friends to mourn her loss.
She was a good mother and obedient to the cause

[Column 2]
of truth. She was chosen secretary to the Relief
Society, November 19, 1890, which position she
held until the time of her death.

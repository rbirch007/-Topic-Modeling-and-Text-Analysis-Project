ELIZA JOHANNAH HALL.
	Sister Eliza Johannah Hall was born February
3, 1857, at Fort Supply. She was the daughter of
Eliza and Eli Tracy, of Huntsville, Utah. She 
married Orson Hall in 1877, and came to Uintah Stake with her husband and family and has re-
sided at Mountain Dell ever since. 
	She was president of the Relief Society of 
Mountain Dell at the time of her death, was 
a sincere and earnest worker in the cause of truth,
a virtuous and loving wife and a kind and faithful
mother.
	She was the mother of eleven children, of whom
seven are now living. She died at Mountain
Dell, November 14, 1901.

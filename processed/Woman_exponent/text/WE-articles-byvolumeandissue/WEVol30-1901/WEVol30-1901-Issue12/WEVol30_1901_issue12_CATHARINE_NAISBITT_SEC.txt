	CATHARINE NAISBITT, Sec.


Under instructions from President Roose-
velt, a general order in the interests of
social purity has just been issued from the
War Department to all our officers and
soldiers, “especially those serving in the
tropics and away from home.” The com-
mon sense and practical sagacity of its rec-
ommendations will be recognized by all
persons of experience. From Maine to
California women and good men will
be grateful to the President for this action.

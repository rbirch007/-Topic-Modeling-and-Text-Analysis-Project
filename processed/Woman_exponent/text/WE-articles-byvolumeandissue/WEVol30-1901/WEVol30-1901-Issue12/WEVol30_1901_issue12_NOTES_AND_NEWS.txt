NOTES AND NEWS.
	To no class of women is the subject of
equal suffrage of such vital importance as
to the working women. To them it be-
comes an industrial as well as a political
question.—JANE ADDAMS.
	Mrs. Roosevelt has received through
John Berrett, delegate to the Pan-
American Congress, a gift from President
Diaz of Mexico, consisting of three pieces
of exquisitely drawn linen. They are said
to be the most beautiful specimens that
have ever come to this country, and were
sent to Mrs. Roosevelt as a special recog-
nition of her encouragement to the linen
workers in Porto Rico.

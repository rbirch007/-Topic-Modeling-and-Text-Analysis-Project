TOOELE CELEBRATION.
	The Tooele Relief Society celebrated the
sixtieth anniversary of the organization of
the Society with two very interesting and
well attended meetings, the meeting house
being tastefully decorated with bunting,
etc. Space will not allow for the publica-
tion of the two pleasing and instructive
programs, but it is safe to say all felt re-
paid for having come out to hear them.
	At night a dance was given in the Social
Hall by the sisters, and thus ended a day
long to be remembered by the residents of
Tooele.

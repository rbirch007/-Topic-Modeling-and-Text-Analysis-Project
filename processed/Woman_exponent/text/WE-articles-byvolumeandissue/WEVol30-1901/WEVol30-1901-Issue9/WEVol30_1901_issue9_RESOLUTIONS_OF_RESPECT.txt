RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Whereas, our Heavenly Father has seen fit to
call from our midst our sister, Henrietta Holla-
day, who was treasurer of the Relief Society
of Santaquin when first organized, and a
faithful worker, and one who always had a kind,
cheering word for all, therefore, be it
	Resolved, that we, the members of the Santa-
quin Relief Society, do hereby express our heart-
felt sympathy to the bereaved family.
ALICE ELLSWORTH.
Santaquin, Nov. 5, 1901.

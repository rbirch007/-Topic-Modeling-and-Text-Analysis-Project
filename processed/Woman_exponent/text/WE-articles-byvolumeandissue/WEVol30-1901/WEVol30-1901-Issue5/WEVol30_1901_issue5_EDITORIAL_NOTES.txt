EDITORIAL NOTES.

	Through letters received and messages
sent we have been notified of Memorial 
Meetings, in honor of President Zina D. H.
Young, being held by many branches of
the Relief Society in the several Stakes of
Zion. Everywhere her loss is deeply felt
and her memory fondly cherished; for more
than fifty years her name has been a house-
hold word in the homes of the Latter-day
Saints. Let the children of today and of
coming generations be taught to reverence
her name as a Mother in Israel whose hero-
ic example and noble life can never be for-
gotten.
	Visits have been made by the General
Board recently to Stake conferences and
meetings. Kanab Stake, Sister Elizabeth 
J. Stevenson; South Sanpete and Granite
Stakes, Sister Lydia D. Alder; to Relief
Society conferences in Southwestern
mission, St. John, Jay and Kansas City,
Sisters Sarah Jenne Cannon and Annie T.
Hyde; St. George Stake, Sisters Mary Alice
Lambert and Margaret A. Caine; to Woodruff
Stake, Sisters Susan Grant and Lydia D. 
Alder; to Weber Stake, Sisters Emmeline
B. Wells and Emily S. Richards; to Juab
Stake, Sisters M. Isabella Horne and Julia
C. Howe, and to Cassia Stake, Sisters
Elizabeth J. Stevenson and Louisa L.
Greene Richards. Everywhere the sisters
have been warmly received and meetings
and conferences have been generally well
attended and great interest manifested in
the work of this benevolent organization.

RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.
	The General Conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Salt Lake
Assembly Hall in this city, on Thursday,
October 3, meetings commencing at 10 a.
m. and at 2 p. m. It is desired that each
Stake organization should be represented
by its president or some one of its officers,
or a member authorized to report. A
cordial invitation is extended to all mem-
bers of the society and to the Bishops and
brethren interested in its welfare to be
present, also to the Y. L. N. M. I. A. and
Primary organizations.
JANE S. RICHARDS,
Acting President.
BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
SARAH JENNE CANNON,
Counselors.
EMMELINE B. WELLS, Secretary.

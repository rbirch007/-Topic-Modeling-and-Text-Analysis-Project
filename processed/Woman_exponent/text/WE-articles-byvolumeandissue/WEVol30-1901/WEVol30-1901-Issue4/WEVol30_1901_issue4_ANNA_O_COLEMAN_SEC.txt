ANNA O. COLEMAN, Sec.

	I will say that in Wayne Stake Sister
Mary A. White, of Giles, raised 16,300
cocoons, weight 52 lbs. N. J. Neilson, of
Giles, has planted an acre of mulberry
trees. We have also three acres planted at
Torrey and three fourths of an acre at
Caineville. It has taken effort to accom-
plish what we have done in this direction.
The Lord has blessed our labors, and we
expect to be successful in raising silk in the
future.

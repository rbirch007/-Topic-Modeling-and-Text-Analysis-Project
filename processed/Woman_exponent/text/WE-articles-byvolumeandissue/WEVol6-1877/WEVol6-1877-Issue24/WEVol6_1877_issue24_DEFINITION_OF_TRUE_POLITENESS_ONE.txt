	THE novel which has attained the larg-
est circulation during the life of its author,
and the novel which has brought its author
the largest profit, were both written by wo-
men, viz., "Uncle Tom's Cabin," which is
believed to have sold to the extent of a
million copies, and "Daniel Deronda," which
has yielded £16,000.

	FOR some time after the fall of Napoleon
III., the Royal Family seemed not to un-
derstand how to treat the Bonapartes. The
Queen did not even visit her old friend the
Empress for nearly a month. But now the
friendship is established very safely. The
Prince of Wales and Prince Louis Napoleon
are great friends, and a constant series of
visits kept up between Chiselhurst and
Marlborough House.

AND ALL MY DEAR SISTERS IN ZION.

		"All the mothers are abroad to-day
		Guiding tiny feet along the way,
		Crowning tender brows with garlands gay.

		"But among the mothers we shall meet,
		Pausing oft to favor little feet,
		None will be so blest as I, my sweet.

		"None among the children is so fair
		As my baby with her golden hair
		Like the aureoles the angels wear."

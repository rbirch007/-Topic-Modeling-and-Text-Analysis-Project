	VIRGIN CITY, KANE CO., Feb. 15, 1878.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	In our Relief Society the sisters are do-
ing the best they can under the circumstan-
ces. We cannot raise much wheat here,but
still we wish to store up all we can for Zion-
The sisters are now busily engaged in mak-
ing quilts, and as a general thing feel well,
and try to assist in all good works, such as
donating means for the building of Temples
and also helping to feed and clothe those
who work on them. The health of the peo-
ple of our little settlement is good, with
the exception of a few who have bad colds
We are having a heavy rain storm, the
first for a long time.

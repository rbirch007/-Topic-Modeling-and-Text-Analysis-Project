EDITOR EXPONENT:

	The following is the Annual Report of
our Society, for the year beginning June,
1876, ending 1877:

	Receipts $ 277 10
	Disbursed to the poor 42 75
	Misionaries 99 65
	St. George Temple 81 00
	Repairs on Meeting House 43 70
	Total, 267 10
	Remaining in treasury 10 00
	In teasury, at present date, 110 35

	The society is in a thriving condition;
peace and union prevails, the meetings are
well attended, and all seem anxious to carry
out the wishes of our late President, par-
ticularly in regard to storing grain.
	Wishing success to your valuable paper,
and all the interests of Zion, we remain
your co-workers in the great Latter-day
work.
	Officers: Ruth W. Tyler, President; Eliza
W. Hawkins, and Ann Lewis, Counselors.

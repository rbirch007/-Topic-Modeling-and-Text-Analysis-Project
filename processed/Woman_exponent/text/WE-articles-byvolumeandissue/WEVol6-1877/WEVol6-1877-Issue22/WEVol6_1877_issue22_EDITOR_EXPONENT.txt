EDITOR EXPONENT:
   
	Report of the Young Ladies M. I. A., of
this ward, since its organization by Sister
Eliza R. Snow, Feb 7th, 1878. Officers:

	M[-]ss Delila Gardner, President,

	"	Elizabeth Cutler, Secretary,
	"	Rebecca Cutler, Assistant Secretary,
	"	Elizabeth Cooper, Treasurer.
	We meet once a week. The number of
members in our association is 81, and all
seem to enjoy the spirit of the Lord in our
meetings. They attend punctually and do
whatever they are called upon to do. We
have a paper edited every week which is
interesting and instructive to all. We also
have select readings, recitations etc. Our
hope and prayers are that we may increase
and not decrease and that we may help the
great work along. Some of the sisters of
the Relief Society visit us and give us
very good instructions. We will close by
wishing the EXPONENT success, we re-
main, Yours, etc.

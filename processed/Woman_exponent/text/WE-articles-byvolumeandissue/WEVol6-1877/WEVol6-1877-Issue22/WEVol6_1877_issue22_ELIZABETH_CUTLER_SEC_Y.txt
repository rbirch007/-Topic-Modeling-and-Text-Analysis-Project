		ELIZABETH CUTLER, Sec'y.

	On Wednesday, March 20th, 1878, a spe-
cial meeting of the Young Ladies' Re-
trenchment Association of the 20th Ward,
(this city) was held in the Ward School
House. Sisters E. R. Snow, Z. D. Young,
M. I. Horne and a number of other ladies
were present by invitation, also President
Joseph Young and other brethren, by
whom much valuable instruction and en-
couragement was given. On motion of
Sister Snow, a unanimous vote was taken
to change the name of our Society to Mu-
tual Improvement Association.
	At the close of the meeting, which was
in all respects a very excellent one, the
visitors and some of the officers of the So-
ciety repaired to the residence of Brother
and Sister C. W. Stayner, where a most
enjoyable repast was partaken of.
		MRS. SARAH HOWARD, President,
		MISS SELINA SABINE, Secretary.

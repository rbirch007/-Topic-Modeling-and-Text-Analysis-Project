								A FRIEND.

	No flower so fair, but it must fade and die;
		No star so bright but it must sink to rest;
	No gift so precious from the worlds on high,
		But it may pass from those who love it best.

	But blessed hope, sweet promises bestowed
		On hearts that else must break, but for this cheer;
	We'll find our darlings in a safe abode,
		Nor lose again as we have lost them here.

	Oh! cherished girl, fair, gentle and refined,
		Beloved of those who love the pure and true;
	Beseech our God, that they may be resigned;
		And comforted, sweet one, who mourn for you!

	Remembering he can no injustice do,
		"Too wise to err, too good to be unkind."
												LUL.

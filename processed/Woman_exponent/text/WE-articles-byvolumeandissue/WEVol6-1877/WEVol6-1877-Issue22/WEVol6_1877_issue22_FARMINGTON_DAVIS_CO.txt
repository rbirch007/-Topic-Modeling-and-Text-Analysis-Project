			FARMINGTON, DAVIS CO.,
						April 3, 1878.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	A meeting was held to-day, commencing
at 11 o'clock a. m., for the purpose of ef-
fecting an organization of the Relief Socie-
ty of this county. There were present on
the stand Wm. R. Smith. President of this
Stake of Zion, and his Counselors, C. Lay-
ton and A. Call; also Miss E. R. Snow and
the Presidents of each of the branches of
the Relief Society of this county.
	Wm. R. Smith was elected Chairman,
and Mrs. Lucy A. Clark, Secretary, after
which the following officers were elected
by unanimous vote:
	Mrs. Sarah I. Holmes, President.
	"	Mary S. Clark,	}	Counselors.
	" 	Nancy A. Clark,	}
	Miss Minerva E. Richards, Secretary.

HYGENIC NOTES.

	A practicing physician says that many of the
deaths which occur among our children, are the
result of the use of stoves in sick rooms; that
pure air and free ventilation are of far more im-
portance than medical skill: and that open fire-
places are the very best ventilators.

	A lady ran a rusty nail in the bottom of her
foot, from which lock-jaw ensued. Raw beef
scraped or pounded and applied to the wound, re-
lieved the lock-jaw.

	An excellent remedy for scalds or burns is to
apply olive oil freely, and then cover with, and
bind on new cotton batting.

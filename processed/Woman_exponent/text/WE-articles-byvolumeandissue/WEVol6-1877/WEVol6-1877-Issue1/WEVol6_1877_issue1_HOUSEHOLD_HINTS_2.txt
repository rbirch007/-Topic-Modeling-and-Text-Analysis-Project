HOUSEHOLD HINTS.

	To preserve winter clothing from moths, the
very best method is to wrap each article in brown
paper. It has been proven one of the best pre-
ventives from moths in the world. Stiff seam-
less sacks are excellent to keep out moths, and
put in the sack some camphor and tobacco.
	Dough-nuts: One pint of flour, one table-spoon-
ful of sugar, one teaspoonful of cream of tartar,
half teaspoonful of soda, and two eggs. Mix
well with milk; roll out the paste, and cut it out in
any shape. Fry in boiling lard until a nice brown.

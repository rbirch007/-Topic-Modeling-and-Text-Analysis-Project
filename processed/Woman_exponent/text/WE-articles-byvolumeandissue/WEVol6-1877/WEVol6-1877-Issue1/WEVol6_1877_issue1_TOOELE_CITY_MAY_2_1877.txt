			TOOELE CITY, May 2, 1877.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The Seventh Annual Report of the Re-
lief Society of Tooele is as follows:

	Total amount donated in the
		year 1876.............................. $ 41 90
	Property in Treasury, cash
		and other things...................    51 65	Five shares in sheep herd, $25
		per share...............................  125 00
	Disbursed to the needy, and for
		other practicable purposes... 158 43.

	This last season we united with the
Library Association in building a large So
cial Hall to belong exclusively to the Re-
lief Society and Library Association, which
we have no doubt will be of great benefit to
our young people. We have contributed
between twelve and thirteen hundred dol-
lars already, and expect, if all is well this
summer, the inside of the building will be
finished. Our Society is in a good condi-
tion; the sisters feel determined to do their
duty in helping to build up the kingdom
of God, realizing that God's purposes are
being fulfilled, and that His kingdom will
last forever, whether we prove ourselves
worthy citizens or not. We rejoice very
much in the teachings given through the
EXPONENT:
	The good, the joy from it may spring
				Eternity can tell.
	On Friday, the 6th of May, we were fav-
vored with a visit from Bishop Wm. G.
Young, who gave us valuable instruction in
regard to training our children, and several
other items of great interest to benefit and
encourage us to walk in the path that
leads to eternal life.
	That we may be kept humble and prayer-
ful before the Lord, and secure the prize
that is before us, is the prayer of your sis-
ters in the Gospel convenant,
					MRS. MEIKLEJOHN.

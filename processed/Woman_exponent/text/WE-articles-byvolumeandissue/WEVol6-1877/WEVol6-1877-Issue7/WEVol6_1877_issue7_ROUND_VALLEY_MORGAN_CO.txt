	ROUND VALLEY, MORGAN CO.,
		Aug. 22d, 1877.EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We still continue to hold our meetings once
in two weeks, and good feelings are manifested
among the sisters. We read the EXPONENT,
and desire to carry out the counsel and in-
structions that are given to us from time to
time. We intend to lay up all the grain we
can this fall, and with the help of our Heaven-
ly Father we will do all we can for the salva-
tion of ourselves and families, by taking care
of our grain against a time of need.
	Praying for the success of your very valuable
paper we remain,
	Your sisters in the Gospel of peace,
		MARTHA TONKS, President,
		FANNIE OLPIN, Sec'y pro tem.

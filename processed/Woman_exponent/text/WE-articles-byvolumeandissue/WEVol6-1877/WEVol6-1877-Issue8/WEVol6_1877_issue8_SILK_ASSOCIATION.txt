		SILK ASSOCIATION.

	A meeting of the Utah County Branch of
the Deseret Silk Association was held at
the meeting house, American Fork City,
Saturday, Aug. 25th, as per notice given.
Quite a number of the silk raisers were
present, and considerable business enacted
pertaining to the organization. Constitu-
tion and by-laws were adopted for the gov-
ernment of the Association, and upon mo-
tion of D. Graves, Esq., it was carried
that the same be printed in due form, to be
distributed among the members. It was
suggested by S. Cornaby, Esq., that items
be kept of the success and failures in the
enterprise, for future information. Bishop
L. E. Harrington was present and aided
materially in the transaction of business.
		Respectfully.
			EMMA FEATHERSTONE,
							Secretary.

	M. E. R. L.

	DIED IN Salt Lake City Jan. 25, 1878, Eli-
za, widow of Stephen Lees, in the seventy-
third year of her age. Deceased was born July
7th, 1805, at Doncaster, Yorkshire, England.
She embraced the G spel in May 1848, at Ash-
ton Under-Lyne, and emigrated to Utah in
1853. Sister Eliza Lees has ever been true to
the Gospel, and while in England did much
good in assisting the Elders and making a home
for the missionaries. She contributed liberally
of her means to the Church. She was devot-
edly attached to her faith, punctual to the du-
ties of a Latter-day Saint. She delighted in
talking upon the principles of the Gospel in
which her soul was engrossed. She was a con-
stant attendant at public worship and was one
of the few who regularly attend the Fast Meet-
ings. She had no fear of death, and passed
from earth as peacefully as a child falling
asleep. She was a member of the Relief So-
ciety in the 13th Ward, where she resided, and
took great pleasure in attending the sisters'
meetings. She was the last of her family.

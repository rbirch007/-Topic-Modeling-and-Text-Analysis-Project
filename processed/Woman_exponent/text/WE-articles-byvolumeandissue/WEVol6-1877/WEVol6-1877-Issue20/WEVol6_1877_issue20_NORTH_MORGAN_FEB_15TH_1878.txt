		NORTH MORGAN, Feb. 15th, 1878.
EDITOR EXPONENT.
	We send you a few items from this place
in regard to our Relief Society.
	Before the re-organization of the Church,
we all belonged to South Morgan. A spe-
cial meeting was called, for the purpose of
dissolving our Society at Morgan City into
two Wards. We were re-organized Oct. 13,
1877, by Bishop W. M. Parker and his

[Column 3]
Counselor, Olif B. Anderson, with twenty-
eight members. Since that time others
have been added. Having no place of our
own, as yet, we hold meetings twice a
month, at the residence of Sister E. A. Par-
ker, which are generally well attended. We
have a Young Ladies' Mutual Improvement
Society in connection with the Relief Soci-
ety, and they with us are trying to carry
out the counsel of our beloved President,
Brigham Young, in Storing Grain. We
held our first Quarterly meeting Jan. 16,
and the report was as follows:
Received............................................$57. 05
Disbursed to the Poor.....................  10. 00
In the Treasury.................................  29. 80
Salt Lake City Temple......................  17. 25
Wheat on hand 38 ½ bushels.

	Names of officers are:
	Elizabeth Worlton, President.
	Amanda Rollo,	} Counselors.
	Eliza Ann Parker,	}
	Elizabeth Rock,	} Secretaries.
	Harriet Clawson,	}
	Adelgonda Heiner, Treasurer.

	Two of the sisters here take our good lit-
tle paper, the EXPONENT, and it is read by
many more.
	We should be pleased to receive a visit
from the sisters of Salt Lake City any time,
and receive instruction from them, for we
feel our own weakness in this great under-
taking, but with the help of our Heavenly
Father, we are determined to do the best
we can. We have good, active teachers,
who visit us once a month. We truly feel
to rejoice, if we have not much of this
world's means we are rich in spirit, blessed
with health, kind hearts and willing hands,
that are ready to help build up the kingdom
of God.
	May God bless and prosper the Relief So-
cieties, that they may accomplish that for
which they were organized, is the prayer
of yours truly,

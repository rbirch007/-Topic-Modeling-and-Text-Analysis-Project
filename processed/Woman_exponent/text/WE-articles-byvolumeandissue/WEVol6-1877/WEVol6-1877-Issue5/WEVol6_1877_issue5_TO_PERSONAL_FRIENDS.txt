TO PERSONAL FRIENDS.

	A second little daughter was born to me on
the 24th of June of this year. We named her
Mabel Greene. I was blest with her sweet,
happy presence only three weeks and three
days. Although to all appearance strong and
healthy from the first, she left us on the 18th
of July, afrer a few hours' suffering, to join
her sister and other blessed associates in the
spirit world.	LULA.

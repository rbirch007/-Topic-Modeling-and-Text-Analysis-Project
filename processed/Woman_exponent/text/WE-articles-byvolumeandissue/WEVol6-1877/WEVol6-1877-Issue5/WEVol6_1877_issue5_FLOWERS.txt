FLOWERS.

	As every thread of gold is valuable, so is
every moment of time.
	KIND words do not cost much. They
never blister the tongue or lips, and we
have never heard of any mental trouble
arising therefrom.
	THE history of the world teaches this
that the only safe guide of a great intellect
is a pure heart; that evil no sooner takes
possession of the heart then folly commen
ces the conquest of the mind.

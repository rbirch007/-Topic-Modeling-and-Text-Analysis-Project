	ELIZABETH A CUTLER..


WE have had a very pleasant call from
an old school-mate, Aden E. Haskell, Esq.,
who is on his way to California in business
interests; he spent a day or two in the city
looking around and visiting points of in-
terest; expressed himself as pleased with
the improvements made in the country, and
considerably surprised at the success of 
home-manufactures and co-opertive insti-
tutions.

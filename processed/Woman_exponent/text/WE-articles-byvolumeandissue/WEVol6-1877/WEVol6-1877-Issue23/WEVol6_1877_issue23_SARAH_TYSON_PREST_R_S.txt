	SARAH TYSON, Prest. R. S.

	JOHNSON, KANE Co., April, 1878.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The sisters of Johnson were called to-
gether by Bishop S. E. Johnson, Feb. 11,
1878, and a Relief Society was organized
with Sisters Jane C. Johnson as President,
Mary A. Johnson, and Louisa Shumway,
Counselors. The Bishop and Counselors
gave us excellent instruction concerning

[Column 2]

the duties of Relief Societies, and exhort-
ed the sisters to be united and prayerful
that the Spirit of the Lord might be with
them to lead and guide them. There are
several copies of the EXPONENT taken by
the members of the Society here and the
instructions are read with interest.
	As ever, your sister, etc.

NOTICE.

	We are pleased to announce to the Ladies'
Medical Class of Salt Lake City, and other
ladies who may wish to join the class, that Mrs.
Zina D. Young, will meet with them, to aid and
assist them in the pursuit of the study of Obstet-
rics.	The class will meet at the office of the
WOMAN'S EXPONENT every Wednesday, at
4 o'clock p.m., commencing July 11th. The
ladies will please bring their books, and it is
hoped they will receive mutual benefit. A
punctual attendance is desired.

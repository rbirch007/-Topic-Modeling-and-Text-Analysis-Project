		ST. JOHN, MALAD VALLEY,
					Dec. 27th, 1877.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	A meeting was called on the 24th of
November, for the purpose of organizing a
Relief Society; Bishop Dunford and Pre-
siding Elder Lewis and other Elders were
present. The following officers were cho-
sen.
	Mary Ann Kent, President.
	Mary James,			} Counselors.
	Elizabeth Williams,	}
	Ann Dishan, Treasurer.
	Two Secretaries and six teachers have
also been chosen. Bishop Dunford gave us
some excellent counsel and spoke of the
evil of tale-bearing, and bade us be faith-
ful and true to all our callings. Elder Lewis
and Counselor Rees have since met with us,
and given us some excellent counsel with
regard to her duties.
	We are a small branch, but by exertion
and faithfulness we shall, we trust, increase
in numbers and good works. We intend to
read your paper in our meetings; I receive
mine from the Malad Branch, but hope soon
to subscribe for more and receive them
here.
	May God bless you and every faithful
saint.

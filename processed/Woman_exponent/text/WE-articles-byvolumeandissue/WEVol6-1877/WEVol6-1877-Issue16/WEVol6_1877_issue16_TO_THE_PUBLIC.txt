TO THE PUBLIC.

	CANCER: I feel it my duty for the sake of
humanity, to say to all who may be troubled
with this dreadful disease, that my wife was
severely afficted with cancer under the right
arm, extending towards the brea[-]t and down
the side. I was recommended by several par-
ties who knew of the successful treatment of
cancer by Phineas H. Young. I accordingly
submitted the case to him, and after forty-
seven successive days of his treatment she is
quite free from all cancerous affections.
	I am confident that no case of this kind was
ever treated with more skill, or came to a
more favorable issue than this.
		JOHN SEARS.
	11th Ward, Salt Lake City.
	Witnesses: Bishop A. McRae, Mark Barnes,
Charles Edwards.

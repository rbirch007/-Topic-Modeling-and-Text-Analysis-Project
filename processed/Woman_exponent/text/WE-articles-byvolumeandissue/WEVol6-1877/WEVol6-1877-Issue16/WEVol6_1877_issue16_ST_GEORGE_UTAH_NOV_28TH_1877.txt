	ST. GEORGE, UTAH, Nov. 28th, 1877.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Since the organization of our Second
Ward Young Ladies Mutual Improvement
Association, we have regularly held our
meetings. Latterly, persuant to our Bish-
op’s suggestion we have also held joint
meetings with the Young Men’s Mutual
improvement Association of the Second
St. George Ward.  At these joint meetings
we have addresses and lectures deliverd
by our seniors.  We have now determined
upon the publication of a Monthly Maga-
zine in manuscript to contain original es-
says and articles from the Young Men’s and
Young Ladies’ Associations.  Prior to the
establishment of the joint meetings we had
published and successfully carried on a
Monthly Magazine in our Association, the
contributions being solely from the pens
of the sisters who were members; now,
however, we expect to see an enlargement
of, and a greater variety of articles in the
joint Magizine, the first number of which
is to be read at the joint meeting next Tues-
day, the 14th of December.  We in unison
with the daughters of Zion, take sincere
pleasure in reading the EXPONENT and
gratefully acknowledge the benefit we de-
rive from reading its pages.  May success
attend its efforts to advance the cause of
righteousness, is the desire of your sister,
		LILLIE BLEAK, Ass't Secrtary.

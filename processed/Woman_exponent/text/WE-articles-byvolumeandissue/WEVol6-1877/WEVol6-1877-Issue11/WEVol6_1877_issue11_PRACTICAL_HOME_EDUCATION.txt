		PRACTICAL HOME EDUCATION. 

	The necessity of young ladies being edu-
cated in household departments, has been
so long and so thoroughly ventilated, that
it is now a well established and almost uni-
versally acknowledged fact, that every girl
should learn to be a good cook and house-
keeper.
	And what about the boys? I will an-
swer, that in my opinion, every boy should
also be taught enough about housework,
that he may always know how to make him-
self useful about home. And to under-
stand the true dignity of labor so well, that
he would be more ashamed to sit idly watch-
ing his mother and sisters trudge about
hour after hour, than to be found assisting
in the household duties which are performed
for his comfort as well as theirs.

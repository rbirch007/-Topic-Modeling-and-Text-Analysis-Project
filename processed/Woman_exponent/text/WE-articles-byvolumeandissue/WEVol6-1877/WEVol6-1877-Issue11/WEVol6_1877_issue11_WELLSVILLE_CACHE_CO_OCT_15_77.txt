	WELLSVILLE, CACHE Co., Oct. 15, '77.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We send you a report of our Ninth Annual
meeting:
		TREASURER'S REPORT.
	Donations................................	$51 36
	Quilting...................................	  11 85
	Wheat stored for Zion, 30 bushels.
	Given to the needy...................	  57 10
	Given for Relief Society House,	  26 40
	Cash and store order in treasury... 	    9 65
	Merchandise............................. 	  61 55
	In Cash Mercantile Store..........	  22 50
	In Co-op Relief Store...............	  46 00
	Interest on shares in R. S. Store...	  17 00
	A good spirit prevailed in the meeting.
We are striving to do something for the
cause of Zion and the more united we are
the more good we can do. We were visit-
ed with the grasshoppers this year, but ex-
pect to respond to the counsel of our late
and esteemed President Brigham Young,
and do more in storing up grain another
season. Our sincere prayer is for the pros-
perity of Zion in all her institutions and
our desire is to be co-workers in her enter-
prises. We read the EXPONENT with in-
terest,	LUCY E. HASLAM, Sec'y.

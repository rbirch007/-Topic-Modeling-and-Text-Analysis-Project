OBITUARY.

﻿	Died in Wellsville, Cache Co., Oct. 1st, 1877,
Seline, beloved wife of Joseph S. Redford, and
daughter of James and Margaret Dinsdale.
Deceased was born June 9th, 1852, at Bradford,
Yorkshire, England. After her arrival in the
valley she resided in Ogden two years, since


which time she has lived in Wellsville. She
suffered a most agonizing death during her ac-
couchement, after having endured the most in-
tense pain for forty hours. Medical aid was
summoned and all was done that was possible
under the circumstances, but all to no avail.
She leaves a husband and four young children
to mourn her loss; the eldest but seven years
old. She was a good wife, a loving mother,
and a faithful saint, and was beloved and es-
teemed by all who knew her, and is sincerely
mourned by a large circle of friends and ac-
quaintances who followed her to her last rest-
ing place, expressing sympathy and affection.
She was a useful member of the Relief Socie-
ty. Thus has passed away another young and
promising wife and mother, to await the morn-
ing of the First Resurrection.
	[Utah papers please copy; also Millennial
Star.]
			ELLEN WHITON,
				President Relief Society.

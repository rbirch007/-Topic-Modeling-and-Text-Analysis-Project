	EPHRAIM, SANPETE Oct. 9th, 1877.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Under the able administration of our
President Sarah A. Petersen, the counsels
of our lamented President Brigham Young
in regard to storing grain, are well respond-
ed to. The sisters of the Relief Society
have gleaned 70 bushels of wheat, beside
this, we have five hundred bushels stored
for Zion, and intend to add as much as we
possibly can. We have had rather a sickly
season in Ephraim, but the health of the
people is improving. The boys in this set-
tlement (God bless them) have hauled poles
enough to fence our Relief Society lot, and
the sisters in return made the boys happy
by a picnic supper and dance.
	With best wishes for the EXPONENT and
yourself.
	Respectfully your sister,

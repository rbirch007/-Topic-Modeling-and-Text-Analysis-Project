NOTES AND NEWS.

	APROPOS OF the reported projected mar-
riage of King Alfonso, of Spain, with one
of the daughters of the Duc de Montpen-
sier, it may be of interest to say something
of the Orleans family. This family now
consists of 29 individuals.
	The eldest is the Duc de Nemours, 63
years old, and who has four children: (1) The
Comte d’Eu, married to the daughter of
Pedro II., Emperor of Brazil, and has one
child. (2) Le Due d’Aleneon, born in 1844,
married to the sister of the Empress of
Austria, and has two children, the Princess
Louise, and the Prince Jean Emmanuel.
(3) The Princess Marguerite, born in 1848,
married to Prince Czartoryski. And (4) The
Princess Blanche, born in 1857.
	The Prince de Joinville, born in 1818, has
two children: (1) The Princess Francoise,
married to her cousin, the Duc de Chartres.
And (2) the Duc de Penthiere, born in 1845.
	The Duc de Aumale has no children.
	The Duc de Montpensier has six children:
(1) The Princess Isabelle, married to the
Comte de Paris. (2) The Princess Amelie.
(3) The Princess Christine. (4) Prince
Ferdinand. (5) The Princess Mercedes, the
future Queen of Spain, born in 1860. And
(6) Prince Antoine.	
	Louis Philippe Comte de Paris, born in
1838, has three children: (1) The Princess
Amelie. (2) Prince Louis Philippe. And
(3) the Princess Helene.
	Robert d’Orleans Duc de Chartres, has four
children: (1) The Princess Marie. (2) Prince
Robert. (3) Prince Henri. And (4) the
Princess Marguerite.
	There is besides the Princess Clementine,
Duchess of Saxe Coburg Gotha, born in
1817.

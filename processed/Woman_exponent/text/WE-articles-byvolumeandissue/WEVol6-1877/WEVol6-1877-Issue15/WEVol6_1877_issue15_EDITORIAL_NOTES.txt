EDITORIAL NOTES.

	WILL MRS. Haitland please call at the of-
fice of WOMAN'S EXPONENT?

	WE HAVE received from R. Mathews &
Co. Prof. Barfoot's Balsam of Indian Root,
[Peah-Nat-Suah.] If we find it as valuable
a remedy as we anticipate, we shall take
pleasure in recommending it to the public
in our columns hereafter.

	WE DESIRE to call the attention of our
patrons to the advertisement of Bro. B. F.
Cummings, published in this issue. The
Latter-day Saints should be earnest, and en-
ergetic in looking up genealogies, and this
is an excellent opportunity for New Eng-
land people. There is a vast labor connect-
ed with the work for the dead, and the
time is hastening. Procrastination on this
subject may be a grevious sin.

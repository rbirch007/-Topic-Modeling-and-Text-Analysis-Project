NOTES AND NEWS.

	HON. GEORGE Q. Cannon, Delegate from
Utah to Congress, had a hearing before the
House sub-Committee on Territories, Mon-
day, Jan. 28. His speech was in opposition
to the Utah elective bill. Dr. Mary Walk-
er and Mrs. Sara J. Andrews Spencer both
protested against Congress disfranchising
polygamists. These two ladies spoke hero-
ically, and expressed their own views of
the subject—but we prefer to take the sa-
cred phase of the practice of plural mar-
riage to any other. "It is understood the
Committee will soon report THE BILL."

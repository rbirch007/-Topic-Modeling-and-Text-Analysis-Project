	KANOSH, MILLARD CO, Dec. 13, 1877.
EDITOR EXPONENT.
	In compliance with the notice published
in the EXPONENT, we send you the follow-
ing report of grain stored in this city.
	We have stored wheat to the amount of
one hundred and thirty-one bushels, and
have more owing to us from the Sevier
River, which we expect shortly; we have
also one hundred and sixty pounds of flour.
We have a little means in quilts with which
to purchase more when an opportunity
offers.
	We have been pleased to read the reports
of wheat; it is quite encouraging to see
how energetic the sisters have been in stor-
ing grain for Zion, and our prayer is that
the Lord will bless their labors.
	We remain your sisters in the labors of
Zion,			ADELIA A. KIMBALL.

WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


JOY.

Oh joyful visitation
	Come Jesus' voice obey, 
To gain His approbation
   We'll work and watch and pray. 

The faithful saints are rearing
   Their Temple to the Lord, 
The quick and dead 'tis cheering
   Through truth to earth restored. 

In every nation laud it;
	The Savior soon will come,
And then the welcome plaudit
   Will be to saints "well done,"

Christ comes, the Lord's Annointed
	To comfort those who weep,
Fore-ordained and appointed;
	A Shepherd for the sheep. 

Then glory! Hallelujah!
	To Jesus we will sing;
He comes to reign in glory, 
   The Saints Eternal King.

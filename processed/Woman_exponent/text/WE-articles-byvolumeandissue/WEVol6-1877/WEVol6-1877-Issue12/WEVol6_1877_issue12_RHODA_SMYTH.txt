						RHODA SMYTH.


A SERVANT who prided herself on living
in a "genteel" family defined it, on being
questioned, as one "where they keep a car-
riage, have three or four kinds of wine, and
never pay a bill the first time its called for."
	A SCOTCH editor illustrates the prevail-
ing extravagance of the present day by
calling attention to the costly perambulators
in use now; while, when he was a baby,
they hauled him around by the hair of his
head.
	"Two and two never make more than
four;" said a public speaker. "Yes, they
do," cried a boy in the audience. "Per-
haps our young friend will kindly tell us
when two and two make more than four,"
blandly said the speaker; whereupon the 
boy cried, "When they're side by side, you
old stupid—then they make twenty-two,
don't they?"

EDITOR EXPONENT:

	Snowville is a new settlement, situated in a
large and beautiful valley on the north of the
Great Salt Lake with good facilities for a large
town.
	On Jan. 10, our respected Bishop organized a
Relief Society, when the following officers
were unanimously elected; Mrs. Mary Good-
liffe, President; Ellen Robbins and Mary Ann
Osterhout, Counselors; Esther Goodliffe, Secre-
tary and Mary Patter, Treasurer. Six Teach-
ers were also appointed. The meeting was re-
plete with good instructions and a good spirit
was manifested. The brethren present gave
us good teachings, and the sisters expressed a
thankfulness that we conld now meet together
in the capacity of a Relief Society, to enjoy
the blessings to be derived therefrom, as we,
one and all, know it is our duty to do all we
can in the great cause of truth.
	Ever wishing the success of the EXPONENT
and your happiness, we remain your sisters.

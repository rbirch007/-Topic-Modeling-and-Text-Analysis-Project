EDITOR EXPONENT:

	Our Relief Society has been reorganized:
This is a very small place, but we are both
willing and anxious to do all the good we
can. Our President is Mrs. Margaret L.
Reese, Counselors, Mrs. Jane Edmunds,
and Mrs. Jemima Dans, Miss Maggie
Reese, Secretary, Mrs. Eliza Reese, Treas-
urer. We had a young ladies society here
for some time which resulted in good.
Our meetings were interesting, but as there
are now but three young girls unmarried,
we have joined with the Relief Society, 
that we may be useful, and move forward
in an organized capacity.
		MISS MAGGIE REESE, Secretary.

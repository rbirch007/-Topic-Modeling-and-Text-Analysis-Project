WOMAN'S EXPONENT.		165


PROVO, March 21.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Being often in receipt of letters from various
parts of the Territory, concerning the culture
and manipulation of the silk-worm, 'tis my
intention to publish, from week to week, in
the "News," prior to the time of attending
the same.		D. GRAVES.

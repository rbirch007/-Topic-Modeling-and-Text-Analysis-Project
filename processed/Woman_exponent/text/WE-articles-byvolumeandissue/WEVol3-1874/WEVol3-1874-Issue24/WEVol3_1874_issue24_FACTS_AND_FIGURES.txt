FACTS AND FIGURES.

	ENGLAND has 800,000 more women than
men.
	A sixteen-pound cauliflower is the latest
astonisher in San Bernardino.
	EMIGRANTS to the number of 34,255 have
arrived in California overland since the first
of April last year.
	OVER 100 houses are contracted for in
Santa Rosa, all to be erected during the
coming summer.
	THE Steele Bros. of San Luis Obispo
have sold $200,000 worth of land within
the last ten months.
	THE shipment of oranges and lemons
from Los Angelos this season will average
in value $40,000 per month.
	FRANCE has 123,000 manufacturing es-
tablishments, using steam-power of 500,000
horse power and giving employment to
about 800,000 hands.
	DURING January 8,890 gallons of free
soup were furnished in Boston to 10,739
families, comprising 50,365 persons, at the
expense to the city of $1,823.

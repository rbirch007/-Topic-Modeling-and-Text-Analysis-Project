A NEW ADDITION.

	Our many friends, personal and otherwise,
will doubtless be gratified to learn of our con-
tinued good fortune; and every true woman,
especially hopeful young mothers, upon read-
ing this, will be able to sympathize with us in
the pleasure and gratitude we feel in making
the following announcement. To-day—we write
upon on date of Saturday, June 27th—at twen-
ty-six minutes past five a. m. a new addition
was made to the editorial family of the WO-
MAN'S EXPONENT in the form of a fine little
daughter. We also deem that we will be con-
sidered excusable for presenting to our readers
the following lines, addressed to our worthy
husband, Mr. Levi Willard Richards, upon
this occasion.

			TEACHER.

      Applicants will answer the following
			questions.
1. What is your age?
2. Where educated?
3. What branches can you teach? (Under-
        score branches in which you are specially
	proficient.)
4. Have you taught?
5. If so, how long and where?
6. Are you married or single?
7. If a church member, of what denomina-
	tion?
8. What salary do you expect?
9. The lowest you will accept?
10. Have you preference as to locality?
11. When can you commence?
12. What certificates or recommendations do
	you hold?
13. What references can you give? (Give
	names and address in full.)
14. For how long will you engage?
  FORM OF APPLICATION FOR A TEACHER.
	Applicants will answer the following
			questions. 
1. The name and locality of school?
2. What branches to be taught?
3. Is a lady or gentleman wanted?
4. Have you preference as to age?
5. What is the average number of pupils and
	their age?
6. What salary will you pay?
7. How is it to be paid?
8. Is board included?
9. If without board, what does board cost?
10. Must the teacher be a church member; if
	so, of what denomination?
11. How long will you engage?
12. When must the teacher commence?
13. Will you pay traveling expenses?
	Too full particulars cannot be given.
  Applicants will remain three months on
the register of the Bureau, unless sooner filled.

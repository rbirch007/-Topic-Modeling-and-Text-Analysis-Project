EXPLANATION.

	We hope our kind readers will readily excuse
the lateness of the present issue, when we offer
in explanation that we, in consequence of ill-
ness, are now unable to write except by dicta-
ing to another, and we were disappointed in
not having editorial assistance upon which we
had depended. Added to this Miss Horne, the
Business Manager, is still suffering from a se-
vere jar caused by the upsetting of a coach on
which she was riding. These causes may affect
the punctuality of the succeeding number, but
we hope not, for, regretting the delay as much
as our readers, we mean to have it out to time
if possible.

NEW EXCHANGE.

	We take pleasure in placing the “Beaver
Enterprise” on our list of exchanges. It is
edited by Daniel Tyler not David, as incor-
rectly stated, through misinformation, in our
notice of the recent editorial change at the of-
fice of that paper. As a regular visitor, we feel
to welcome the “Enterprise” and trust it will
prove a success as long as it aims to be an able
advocate for, and defender of the best interests
of the people.

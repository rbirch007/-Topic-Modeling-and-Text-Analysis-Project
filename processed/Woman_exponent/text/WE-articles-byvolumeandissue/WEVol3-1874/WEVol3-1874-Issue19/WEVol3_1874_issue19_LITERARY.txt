			LITERARY.

	Darwin has a new book in press, which
treats of the insectivorous and climbing
plants......A new dictionary by Dr. H. Dun-
bar of the Edinburg University is soon to
be circulated......The Millennial Jubilee of
Iceland, has suggested to Rev. P. C. Headly,
the writing of a book entitled "The Island
of Fire, or A Thousand years of the Old
Northmen's Home," which is reported to be
a highly interesting work.

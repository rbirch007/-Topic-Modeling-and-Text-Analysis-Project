			POLITICAL.

	It is now reported that disturbances have
arisen at Pekin, China, on the question of
the successor to the throne......The country
of Darfur has been annexed to Egypt......
The Senate has rejected the Reciprocity
Treaty with Canada.......In the Pennsylvania
Legislature, the Committee on Vice and
Immorality report in favor of the repeal of
local option......The Ways and Means Com-
mittee in the House of Representatives,
have rejected in full, the proposition for a
restoration of the duties on tea and coffee
......Seven States, California, Connecticut,
Iowa, Kentucky, Maryland, New Hamp-
shire and Oregon hold no Legislatures this
winter. The Legislatures of the other
thirty States are now in session......The in-
surgents in Peru lately tried to take the city
Arequipa, but were defeated......In the
French Assembly, a bill for the creation of
a senate has passed......In the Maine Sen-
ate, Rev. Mrs. Hanes lately officiated as
Chaplain, being the first woman who has
ever acted in that capacity in the Legisla-
ture.

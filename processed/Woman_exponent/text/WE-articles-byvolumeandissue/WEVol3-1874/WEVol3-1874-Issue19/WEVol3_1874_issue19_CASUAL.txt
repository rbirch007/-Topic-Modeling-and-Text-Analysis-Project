			CASUAL.

	Many injuries to sailing craft were re-
cently occasioned by a terrific gale accompa-
nied by storms of rain, on the British 
coast......There has been a great flood in the
river Seine; the water has been so high at
Paris that steamers could not pass under the 
bridges......The Railroad bridge on the Sus-
quehanna has been burned......Losses by fire
in New England during the month of Jan-
uary, amounted to $1,023,457.

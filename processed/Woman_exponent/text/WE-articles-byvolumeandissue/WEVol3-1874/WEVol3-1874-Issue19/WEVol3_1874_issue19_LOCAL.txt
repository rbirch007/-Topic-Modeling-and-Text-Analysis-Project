			LOCAL.

	Professor Riggs, the Territorial school
Superintendent, says that there are more
than thirty three thousand children in Utah,
of the proper age for attending school......
President Young and company arrived in
this city, from St. George, on the 19th ult.,
in good health.....The Firemen's grand, dress
ball, given on the 22nd ult.—Washington’s
birthday,—was a note worthy affair, not
soon to be forgotten by the participants......
The construction of railroads in Utah is con-
stantly and rapidly increasing, but no credit
mobiliering is expected from the honest and
honorable gentlemen concerned in them.

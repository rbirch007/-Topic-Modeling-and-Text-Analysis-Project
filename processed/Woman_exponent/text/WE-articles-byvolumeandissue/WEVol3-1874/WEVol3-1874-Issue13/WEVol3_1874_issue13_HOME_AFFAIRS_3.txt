HOME AFFAIRS.

	THE ANNUAL conference of the Weber stake
of Zion was held recently at Ogden.
	AMONG THE events of the Holidays
are recorded several cases of drunkenness,
theft, and other disturbances of a similar na-
ture which have transpired in this city. "It
was not always so," but, alas! it is to-day.
	THE OLD time honored custom of "Wish a
merry Christmas and a Happy New Year" has
been well considered by many of the "Well-to-
do" in this city this season, by suiting their ac-
tions, to the words, they have caused much re-
joicing among their less favored friends.
	PRESIDENT GRANT has nominated Samuel
B. Axtell, of California, for Governor of Utah;
the nomination has been confirmed, and George
A. Black is re-nominated and re-confirmed to
the Secretaryship. The people need not antici-
pate any worse governmental jurisdiction than
they have been accustomed to, we think; and
they may be favored with something bet-
ter.

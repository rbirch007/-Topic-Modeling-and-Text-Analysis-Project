	Whether God requires of us the fulfill-
ment of this commandment in its fullest
sense and meaning I know not; at present,
to us it seems impossible.
	We know that to look upon bright spark-
ling waters, to behold others partaking
freely, will not quench our burning thirst;
nor will it satisfy the knawing hunger to
gaze upon the board spread with plenty.
But we fall far short of even a partial fulfill-
ment, and there is much, very much to do
before He could in any wise say, you have
loved your neighbor is yourself.
							HOPE.

NOTICE.
	Will our Agents and subscribers please take
notice that on and after the first of January,
1875, the postage on newspapers must be pre-
paid, and at the office where they are mailed
instead of at the place of delivery; and send,
or make arrangements with their postmasters
to have sent to us, ten cents postage for each
yearly subscriber? This new postal law does
not effect subscribers within the princinct of
this county.

						R. M. JONES.


WHAT man must have his glass before he
can do a day's work?—A glazier.
	IF a colt's pistol has six barrels, how many
barrels ought a horse pistol to have?
	WHEN a lady stands at the hymeneal altar
with her intended, you may know she is
about to draw her beau into a knot.
	"Gracious me!" exclaimed a lady in a
witness box, "how should I know anything
about anything I don't know anything
about?"

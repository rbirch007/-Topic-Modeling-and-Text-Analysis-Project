	NO sunny side? Nonsense! Everybody
has something to love—something to live 
for, hope for and labor for. Your gloomy
feelings are occasioned by one of two things:	
you exercise too much or too little; either
will make you disconsolate, so avoid both.
If the former error has been yours; stop and
rest, and replenish your wasting strength;
if your guilty of the latter failing, go to
work. In either instance you will become 
healthy and happy.

		CORRESPONDENCE.

	SALT LAKE CITY, April 5th, 1875.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	My remarks at the meeting, March 6th
1875, concerning  the St. George Temple,
were misunderstood.
	It should read:—The last rock to make it
ready for the roof; and after the roof is put
on, seventy-three cords of red rock will be
used to complete the walls and pinnacles,
the roof being nearly flat, will be concealed
from the observer on the ground by this ad-
ditional wall.		Respectfully Yours,

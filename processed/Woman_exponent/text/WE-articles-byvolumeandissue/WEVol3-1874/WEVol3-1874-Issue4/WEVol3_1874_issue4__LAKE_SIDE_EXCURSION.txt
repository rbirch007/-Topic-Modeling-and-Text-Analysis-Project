LAKE SIDE EXCURSION.

	There will be an excursion to Lake Side on
the twenty-eighth inst. The Retrenchment
Associations and Relief Societies are invited to
attend; they can secure tickets for the round
trip for one dollar each. This will no doubt be
an enjoyable time, as there will be music, danc-
ing, and preparations for bathing in the Lake,
with an abundance of shade, and plenty of
pure cold water.
		Committee of Arrangements:
						MRS. W. EAST,
						MRS. E. DAVIS.
	We thank the sisters for a complimentary,
and sincerely hope they will have a time of
pleasant enjoyment.

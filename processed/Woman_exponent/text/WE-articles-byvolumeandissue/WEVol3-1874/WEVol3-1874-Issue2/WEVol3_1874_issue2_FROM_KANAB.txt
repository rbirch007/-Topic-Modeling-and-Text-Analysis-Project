FROM KANAB.

				Wednesday, May 13th.
DEAR EXPONENT:—
	Brother John R. Young and company re-
turned home yesterday. They had no
trouble with the Indians, and the Lord ruled
everything for their good; for which we
feel very thankful. It certainly looked as
though they were sure to have some diffi-
culty; the Navijoes are very angry. We
have had a day of Thanksgiving to-day. A
few people met at the Bishop's this morning
and cooked dinner for the brethren who
have been over the river, and have been
brought back through all the dangers, by
the hand of the Lord,		H. M. B.

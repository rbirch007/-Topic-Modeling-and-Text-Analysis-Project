			LITERARY.

	"The Crusader," published by the women
crusaders in New York, promises to advo-
cate every reform calculated to make people
wiser, better, healthier and happier. It will
need a world of wisdom, not to make mis-
takes in judging of right and wrong......One
of the oddest “typographical errors” we
have lately noticed was that in which a con-
temporary in speaking of a man whom it
wished to designate as being “well favored,”
was made to represent him as being “well
flavored”......Kate Field, well known lit-
erary lady, is said to be studying for the
stage......A Crescent city, Cal. editor, claims
to publish the most westerly paper in the
United States, as he says the water of the
Pacific Ocean at high tide, reaches within
ten feet of his office door.........The oldest
newspaper man in Iowa is Hon. George
Paul, who helped to print the first issue of
the “Patriot,” at Fort Madison in 1837......
The “Orchard and Vineyard” is a newsy,
and to us a new paper, which we welcome
as an exchange from Grand Island, Ne-
braska.

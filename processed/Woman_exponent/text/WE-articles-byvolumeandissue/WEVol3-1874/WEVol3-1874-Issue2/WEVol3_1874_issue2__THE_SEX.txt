	EVERY flower may be supplied with its
favorite soil by the exercise of a little pa-
tience and observation. A light soil suits
nearly all descriptions. Almost any piece
of earth which is thoroughly dug and
dressed yearly, may be made suitable to
the production of flowers. All bulbs, car-
nations, pinks, auriculas &c., love a mixture
of sand.—ENGLISH LADY.

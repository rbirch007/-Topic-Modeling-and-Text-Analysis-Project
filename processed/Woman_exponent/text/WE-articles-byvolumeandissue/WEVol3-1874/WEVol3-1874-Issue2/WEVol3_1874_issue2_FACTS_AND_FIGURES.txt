	PERU has lately discovered new deposits
on guano, which will more than pay her
foreign debt of $180,000,000.

	THE world uses 259,000,000 pounds of tea
and 718,000,000 pounds of coffee every year.
China furnishes nearly all the tea, and Bra-
zil nearly all the coffee.

	IT is calculated that in Alameda county,
Cal., $1,000,000 is invested in the manufac-
ture of beet sugar alone. Last year the
yield was about 20,000 tons, from which
about 3,800,000 pounds of saccharine matter
was produced. It is expected that this sea-
son's crop will be much greater.

	MR. Silas Cooper of Lincoln county, West
Virginia, is claimed to be the oldest man in
the State. He was one hundred and six
years old on the 9th of last March. Mr.
Cooper is as quick of foot as many men at
fifty, and able to stand a great deal of exer-
cise. His wife died a few months ago, at 
the age of one hundred and ten.

	ACCORDING to statistics derived from the
"Bureau of Education," it is calculated that
out of the 38,558,371 of the population of
the United States of all ages, 12,135,799, or
31.47 per cent. of the total population,
must be excluded from the number who are
able, through reading and writing, to ex-
change information with others for the
guidance of their conduct.

	AHMED, an old gentleman living in Tre-
bizoned, Asia Minor, is said to have reached
his 138th year. He enjoys excellent health
and spirits, though he has long survived all
his relatives. From his youth upward he
has been a strong advocate of bodily exer-
cise as a preservative of health, and until
quite lately has been in the habit of taking
long "constitutionals" daily. Ahmed is
not the only instance of remarkable longev-
ity at Trebizoned. Another old man has
just died who had reached his 125th year.

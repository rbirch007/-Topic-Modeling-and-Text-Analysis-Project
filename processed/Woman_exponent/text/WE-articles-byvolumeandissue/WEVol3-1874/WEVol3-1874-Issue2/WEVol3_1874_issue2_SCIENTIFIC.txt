			SCIENTIFIC.

	It is the opinion of Sir William Thomp-
son that the needles of mariner’s compasses
in general use are far too large, and that
they should be only one-fourteenth as long
as they are.........Mr. Newall of Gateshead,
England, is said to have the most perfect
and most powerful telescope in the world;
the diameter of the object-glass is twenty-
five inches........A lake recently discovered
in Turkestan, called Lake Issykul, which
appears to have been quite unknown a cen-
tury and a half ago, is said to have buildings
visible from the surface, at its bottom, and
there is evidence of former existence of vil-
lages on the present lake site......In the
eleventh annual report of the Massachusetts
Agricultural College, one of subjects treated
upon is the amount of pressure in the sap
of plants; The highest pressure recorded
was in the case of black birches.

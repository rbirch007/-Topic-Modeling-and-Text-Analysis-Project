			FOREIGN.

	The Pope is ill......On the occasion of the
birthday of Queen Victoria, the bank of
England and London Stock Exchange were
closed.......It is estimated that France ex-
ports to England one and one half millions
of eggs per day......In Anatolia, Asia Minor,
the famine continues to rage, many having
starved to death........England decides to
erect her several dependencies on the Gold
Coast into a colony to be presided over by a
governor......The Roman Catholic Church,
it is reported, is now persecuted in nearly
all the countries of Europe.

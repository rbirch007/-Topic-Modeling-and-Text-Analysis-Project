TO R. S. PRESIDENTS, AND AGENTS.

	At a meeting of the EXPONENT Committee
of Consultation,  held at this office, May, 30th,
it was considered that large towns, such as
Ogden and Provo, should have one or more
agents, for each ward or precinct. Miss C.
H. Horne was directed to write to the Presi-
dents of the Relief Societies, in the above
named cities, requesting them to appoint the
requisite agents. It was also decided that
Presidents of Relief Societies, in places where
there are no agents, should be requested through
the EXPONENT to appoint capable agents who
will take an active interest in promoting its
circulation. The list of agents formerly pub-
lished for this city, having proved not fully
satisfactory, it was deemed advisable by the
committee to publish the following revised list:

     Every decision which has been rendered
by Chief Justice McKean, and reviewed by
the Supreme Court of the United States, so
far, has been reversed......The city is at this
season favored with many pleasure seeking
visitors from different parts of the world. ...
A little boy had the misfortune to stumble
across the street car track near this office,
the other day, and spill some yeast which
he was carrying; of which a gentleman, be-
ing a witness of the scene, made the rele-
vant remark that the yeast couldn’t be very
“strong,” as it didn’t appear to rise.........
Among other distinguished tourists, Cannon
Kingsley, the noted English lecturer and
divine, has lately visited this city......Dr.
Mary H. Barker, of this city, recently per-
formed a very nice operation upon the eye
of Mr. Aaron West of Ogden. The opera-
tion which was for the formation of an arti-
ficial pupil, was successfully executed, and
without accident, Mr. West, who has been
blind for about eight years, will be able to
distinguish objects quite distinctly.

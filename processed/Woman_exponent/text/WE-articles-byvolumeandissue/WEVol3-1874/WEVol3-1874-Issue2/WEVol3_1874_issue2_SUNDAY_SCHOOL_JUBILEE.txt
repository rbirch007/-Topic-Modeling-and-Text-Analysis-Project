SUNDAY SCHOOL JUBILEE.
	We are requested to remind the many friends
of the Sunday School Union, that the Jubilee
will take place on the Twenty-fourth of July,
in the New Tabernacle. The time is drawing
near, and all who are desirous of participating
in the Jubilee are respectfully solicited to send
in their names, secure their tickets and their
song books, tune their voices and be prepared
to join in the united song of praise.
	In what way could the "Twenty-fourth,"
for 1874, be better celebrated than by bringing
together multitudes of children to sing praises
to the Great Giver of all good? He has blessed
the earth for His people's sake. He has caused
the desert to blossom, and the field to yield its
fruitful increase. He has protected His people
and has confused their enemies. Should not
the Saints and the children of the Saints unite
in singing His praise and in giving thanks to
Him? We heartily wish the jubilation may
prove in all respects, to all interested, a success.

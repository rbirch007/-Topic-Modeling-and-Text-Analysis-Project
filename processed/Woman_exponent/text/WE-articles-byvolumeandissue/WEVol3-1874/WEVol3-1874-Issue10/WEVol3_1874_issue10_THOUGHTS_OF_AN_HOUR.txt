	THOUGHTS OF AN HOUR.

Give back, give back thy genial beams.
To cheer life's path of grief and pain,
Those blissful though illusive dreams,
O star of hope give back again!
Give back the promises of joy,
That gleam along the clouds of woe,
How much of grief can they destroy,
Though never realized below.

Give back the sunny smiles of calm,
That whispered peace amid the storm;
Dispense again the healing balm,
And miracles of good perform.
Thine are the promises of heaven,
Or latent memories of the soul;
Some straying beams of glory given,
To lead us to the blissful goal.

Dear star, the heart unlit by thee
Is but a charnel house of gloom,
Where nothing lives but misery,
And no sweet flowers of promise bloom.
Give back, give back thy genial beams,
To cheer life's paths of grief and pain;
Thy blissful though illusive dreams
O star of hope give back again!
Salt Lake City,			RICH SMYTH.

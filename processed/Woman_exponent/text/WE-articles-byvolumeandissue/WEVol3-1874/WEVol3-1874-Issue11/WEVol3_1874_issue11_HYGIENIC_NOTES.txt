HYGIENIC NOTES.
				For the EXPONENT.
	For Chillblains.—Oxgall, olive oil or
coal oil, spirits of turpentine and camphor,
equal parts.
	Cancers.—When a cancer first appears,
it may be killed by the application of oil of
winter-green. It is also good to take a
little of the oil internally.
	For Burns.—Never forget salt for burns.
If the skin is broken, put a piece of thin
cloth over the flesh before putting on the
salt, and drip a little water on occasionally.
	To harden tender feet.—Strong brine
or salt and water is an excellent thing for
hardening tender feet. It is also good for
sponge bath, as it invigorates the whole sys-
tem. A swallow of salt and water in the
morning is good to prevent sick head ache.
	For sudden Deafness.—Syringe the ear
with warm milk and water increasing the
heat as you can bear, it some five or six
times; then with a smooth tweezer bole or
something of the kind, remove whatever
has accumulated. This often gives instant
relief.

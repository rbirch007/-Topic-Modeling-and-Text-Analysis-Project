EDITORIAL NOTES.

	"DEMON WORSHIP," is what a prominent
New York paper calls Spiritualism, which
it says is making giant strides and is rap-
idly becoming the national religion(?) of the
United States. This is certainly, not very 
flattering to a great nation which prides
herself on her christianity and civilization; 
sends missionaries abroad to convert the
heathen; and frowns upon the Latter day
Saints because they do not appear without
spot or blemish in the sight of her pure
eyes. 
	THE PRINCIPAL officers of the California
Woman Suffrage Society are: Mrs. Sarah
Wallis, President; Mrs. Laura DeForce
Gorden, (Editor, Stockton Leader,) Vice
President; Mrs. Mathews, Recording Sec-
retary; Mrs. Watson, Corresponding Secre-
tary, besides one Vice President for about
half the counties in the State and a Board
of Directors.

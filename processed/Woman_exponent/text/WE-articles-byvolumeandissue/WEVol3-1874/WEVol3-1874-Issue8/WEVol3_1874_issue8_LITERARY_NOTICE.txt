LITERARY NOTICE.
	
	"The Bible and Polygamy" is the title of
another neat publication issued from the Des-
eret News establishment. It is a republication
of the celebrated disscussion, between Prof.
Orson Pratt and Rev. Dr. J. P. Newman on
plural marriage; also of three sermons on the
same subject by Prest. Geo. A, Smith and El-
ders O. Pratt and G. Q. Cannon. This work is
for sale at the Deseret News Office or will be
mailed from there on receipt of the price: 50
cts.

SPENCER'S LETTERS

	The fifth edition of the above standard work
has just been published at the Deseret News
Steam Printing Establishment, where it is for
sale. Bound in cloth, embossed, $1.25.
	This excellent publication, though well
known by many, should be widely circulated
among the Latter-day Saints. Showing as it


does, the most prominent doctrines of the
Church in an interesting manner, no better
book could be placed in the hands of the young;
or of any person desirous of obtaining a cor-
rect understanding concerning the principles
it advocates.

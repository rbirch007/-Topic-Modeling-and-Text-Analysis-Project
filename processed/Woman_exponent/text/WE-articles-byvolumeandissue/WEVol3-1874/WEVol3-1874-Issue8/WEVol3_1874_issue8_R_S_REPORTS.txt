R. S. REPORTS.

	An organization of the young ladies of
Grantsville, Tooele Co., was formed on Thurs-
day, Sep. 17th, at a meeting in that place,
several sisters from this city being present.
The names of the officers of the association
are as follows:
		Miss Rachel Hale, President,
		Miss Harriet C. Bates,	}
		Mrs. Wilda McBride,	}
		Miss Augusta Elison,	}
		  "	Matilda Anderson,	}Counselors.
		  "	Mary A. Hunter,	}		  " 	Lena Lee,			}
		  "	Bertha Sabins, Secretary;
		  "	Mary Worthington, Ass. Secretary.
	The names of seventy-two young ladies
were entered as members of the society;
and Miss Eliza R. Snow remarks concerning
them that a finer group of girls she has never
seen.

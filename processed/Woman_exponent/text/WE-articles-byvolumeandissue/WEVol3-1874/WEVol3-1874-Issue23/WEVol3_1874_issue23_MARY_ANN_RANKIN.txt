		MARY ANN RANKIN.


"IT's well enough for you to name your
boy Elias," said Aunt Hepzibah, "but for
goodness sakes don't name him Alias, 'cause
those Aliases is always cuttin' up bad.
Here's Alias Jones, Alias Brown, Alias
Thompson, Alias One-eyed Jake all been
took up in New York for robbin' and steal-
ing."

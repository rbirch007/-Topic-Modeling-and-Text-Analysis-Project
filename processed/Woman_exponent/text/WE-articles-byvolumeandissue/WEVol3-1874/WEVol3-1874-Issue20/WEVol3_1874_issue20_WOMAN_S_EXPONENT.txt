WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


For the EXPONENT
			THE UNITED ORDER.
					__
		BY JACOB E. TERRY. ROCKVILLE.
					__
	Come let us join our hearts and hands,
		In the United Order;
	Likewise our houses and our lands,
		And all within our border.
	For it will help to make us free
		From bondage and oppression;
	When through our faith we all can see,
		And all do take possession.

CHORUS. For this is the truth we will maintain,
		Until our dying day, Sir,
	That Jesus Christ our King shall reign,
		And righteousness bear sway, Sir.

	The prophets longs ince did foretell
		The beauties of the Order;
	When Jesus Christ with saints shall dwell,
		And peace reign through their border.
	Then come ye Saints both great and small.
		And understand the sequel,
	That when we have put in our all,
		We all will then be equal.

CHOURS. For this is the truth, &c.
	
	We'll heap up gold and silver too,
		And treasures that are hidden.
	For we believe the prophets true,
		And thus the Lord hath bidden,
	That all the Saints benearth the sun,
		Should bring their wealth together,
	And that will make us all as one,
		With the blessings of our Father.

Chorus. For this is truth, &c.
	
	And when the Saints through faith behold
		The glories of the union;
	And by their faith and works lay hold
		Upon the blest communion;
	We'll bind old Satan fast in chains,
		And cast him into prison.
	And peace and righteousness shall reign,
		And earth become a heaven.

Chorus. For this is truth, &c.

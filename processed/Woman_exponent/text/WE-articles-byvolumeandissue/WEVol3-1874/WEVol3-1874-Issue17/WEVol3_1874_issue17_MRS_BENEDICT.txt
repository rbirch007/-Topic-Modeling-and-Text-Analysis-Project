MRS. BENEDICT.

	Mrs. Phebe Benedict, of Antioch, Cal.,
has solved the question, "What can Woman
Do?" Some two years since she took out
papers as sole trader, gained the consent of
her husband to allow her complete manage-
ment of the farm and transact all the busi-
ness of the place, and at a time when the
farm was mortgaged for several hundred
dollars, and farming implements out of re-
pair. Now, by economy, close attention to
business and shrewd management, she has
paid off the mortgage and interest, purchased
a new cultivator, plows and other imple-
ments, treated herself to a sewing machine,
repaired the buildings, and will shortly loan
money; and Mrs. Benedict is, beside this, a
leading advocate of suffrage for women.

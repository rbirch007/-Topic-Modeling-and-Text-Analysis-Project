		AMERICAN FORK, Jan. 20th, 1875.
DEAR SISTER RICHARDS:—
	Enclosed, find money for postage on EX-
PONENT. The sisters who take the paper
here, prize it very highly for the good coun-
sel it contains.  Hoping your health and
that of your family is good, and wishing
you continued success and increasing pros-
perity, I am your sister in the Gospel,

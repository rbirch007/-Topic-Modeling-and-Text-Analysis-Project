TO SUBSCRIBERS.
	Campbell & Patterson who were general
agents for the EXPONENT, previous to the pres-
ent Volume when we dropped their names,
have become bankrupt. Those who subscribed to
them for the current numbers, doubtless feel dis-
appointed at not receiving their late papers, but 
as we have received neither names nor money
we cannot tell who have subscribed till the af-
fairs of the firm are settled. While the EXPO-
NENT will thus suffer a serious loss, we intend
to make matters as satisfactory as possible to
those who subscribed to Campbell & Patter-
son while they were our agents, and if those
who hold receipts from them will send us the
same we will try and supply their full number
of papers.

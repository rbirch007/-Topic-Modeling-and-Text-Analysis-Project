LAKE SIDE EXCURSION.
	We are requested by one of the committee to
state that the excursion to Lake Side, noticed in
our last has been postponed to the 25th of the
present month. This change in the time has
been thought advisable in consideration of the
large excursion advertised to go to Cache val-
ley soon, and also the violent storms so fre-
quent of late. By the 25th inst. the ladies hope
the weather will have become so settled that
the original programme can be carried out to
the enjoyment of all who participate.

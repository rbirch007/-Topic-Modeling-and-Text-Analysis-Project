UTAH GAZETEER.
	We have received a copy of the "Gazeteer
of Utah and Salt Lake City Directory" edited
by Edward L. Sloan, Esq. It is of convenient
size, the binding seems to be durable and the
print clear. From a brief examination we
judge the contents to be well arranged and of
value to every one who is engaged in business
or who wishes for information concerning City
and Utah affairs. For sale at the Herald Of-
fice, price $5.

	CURE FOR CORNS AND BUNIONS.—Squeeze
out the juice of two good lemons; put it in
a bottle, add one dozen small pearl buttons—
old ones will do as well as new; shake the


bottle well a few times until the buttons are
all dissolved, when is is ready for use. Ap-
ply with the finger to the corn or bunion
twice a day until a cure is effected. I have
never known it to fail of accomplishing the
desired result. It is very penetrating.
Keep closely corked.

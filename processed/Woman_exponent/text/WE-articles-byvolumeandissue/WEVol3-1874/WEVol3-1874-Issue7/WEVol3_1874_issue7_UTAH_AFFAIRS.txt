UTAH AFFAIRS.	
	We acknowledge receipt of a neat pamphlet
bearing the title of "Utah Affairs. Congress
and Polygamy. Including the Poland Bill,'
which, on examination we find to be a series
of letters that appeared in the Des. News, over
the signature of "Veritas." The author says
that he "has used, very generally, the langauge
of prominent and distinguished parties, know-
ing that such matter has the greater weight."
Those who desire information on the foregoing
subjects will doubtless find this work well wor-
thy of a perusal. For sale at the Deseret News
Office. Price twenty-five cents.

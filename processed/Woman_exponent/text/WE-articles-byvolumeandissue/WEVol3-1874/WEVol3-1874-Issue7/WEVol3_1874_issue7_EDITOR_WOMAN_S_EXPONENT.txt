EDITOR WOMAN'S EXPONENT:

	Since the organization of the Relief So
ciety in this place a good many poor have
been relieved by it; and money has been
gathered to assist the emigration of the
poor Saints in foreign lands. A good spirit
prevails in our meetings, but they are not
always well attended. Much more good
could be done if the sisters were more
punctual in attending.
	I have reflected much upon the United
Order which has been organized at St. George
and accepted in many places. I feel to thank
my heavenly Father for His goodness in
giving to us this holy Order, and strive to
renew my diligence that I may ever be
ready to receive the word of the Lord and
obey it.
	With many wishes for the success of your
valuable paper, I subscribe myself your
sister in the Gospel.				E. S.

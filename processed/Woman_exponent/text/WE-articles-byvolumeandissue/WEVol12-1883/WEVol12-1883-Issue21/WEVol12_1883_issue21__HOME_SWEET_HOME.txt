"HOME, SWEET HOME."

Straight is the gate and narrow the way,
And weary the journeying day by day;
Tempations and trials on every hand,
But soon we shall reach the promised land;
And angels in heaven will sing as we come,
"Well done, faithful servants, come home, come home."

The weary journey we will not regret,
Temptations and trials we'll soon forget,
And strike on our harps a louder strain,
As we join in gladness our sweet refrain;
The angels in heaven sing as we come,
"Well done, faithful servants, come home, come home."

Ah, when we have reached the promised land,
And taken our place mid th' angelic band,
How gladly we’ll welcome each day some friend,
And holy and sweet our voices will blend
In the song the angels sing as they come,
"Well done, faithful servants, come home, come home."

The angels that watched round the tomb we'll see,
The "chosen twelve" and the "Mother Mary;"
Elias and Moses will be in the throng.
To share in the bliss and join in the song
The angel band as a welcome sings,
"Well done, faithful servants, come home, come home."

Jesus our Savior, and God on the throne,
Will be there to greet each purified one;
The portals of heaven will echo and ring
With praises to God, our priest and our king,
Who bids His angels the welcome sing,
"Well done, faithful servants, come home, come home."
										E. S. D.

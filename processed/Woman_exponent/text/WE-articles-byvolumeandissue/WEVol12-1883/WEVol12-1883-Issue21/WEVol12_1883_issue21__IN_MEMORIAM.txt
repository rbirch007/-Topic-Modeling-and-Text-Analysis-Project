IN MEMORIAM.

	DIED, at Hyde Park, Feb. 17th, 1884, Louisa King,
wife of William Beddingfield. Deceased was born July
28th, 1827, at Allsaints, Suffolk, England. She came to
Hyde Park at its first settlement; has been a member of
the Hyde Park Relief Society since its organization
and acted in the capacity of teacher for the same until
prevented by the sickness that terminated her existence
here.
	She was loved and respected by all her acquaintances,
those who have known her since childhood testify to
her pure and exemplary life.
	She leaves a husband and a host of relatives to mourn
her loss. She has been the mother of two children, who
both died in infancy. She has been a great sufferer for
many years, but passed away peaceful.
							M. E. GRIFEETH,
							Secretary Relief Society.

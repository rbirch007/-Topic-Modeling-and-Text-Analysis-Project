RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Whereas it has pleased the Great Creator to take un-
to himself, after a long and useful life, our much loved
sister, Caroline Crosby, and,
	Whereas it is fitting that she be held in honorable re-
membrance,
	Therefore, be it resolved by the Ladies' Relief Society
of Beaver City, That while we mourn, not as those who
have no hope, yet we realize that in the death of Sister
Crosby we have lost a valued assistant, the poor a
friend and her family a wise and loving counselor.
	Resolved, That we extend our deepest sympathy to
all the members of her family.
	Resolved, That these resolutions be put upon the rec-
ords of the Relief Society, and a copy thereof be sent to
the family of our dear sister, and to the WOMAN'S EX-
PONENT.				DELILAH F. COX,
						S. M. Dell,
						HARRIET E. SHEPHERD,
										Committee.

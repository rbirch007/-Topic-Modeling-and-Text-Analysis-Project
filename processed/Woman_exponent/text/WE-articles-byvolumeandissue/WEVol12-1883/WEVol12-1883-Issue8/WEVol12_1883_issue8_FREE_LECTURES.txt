FREE  LECTURES.
	Dr. Barney says she will give a number of
free lectures upon the subject of Medicine, or


the Laws of Life, and those interested in the
prevention of disease and prolongation of life,
and wish to become acquainted with some of its
laws are invited. Her first lecture will be
given in the 13th Ward Hall, October 9th, at 7
o'clock p.m., to to the Young Ladies' Mutual
Improvement Association.

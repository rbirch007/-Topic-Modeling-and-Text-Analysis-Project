TO MY PATRONS:

	Owing to an unfavorable accident, and con-
sequent delay, the picture is not ready to de-
liver as I expected it would be before this time.
However, it will not be long now before you
receive both picture and book, which are in
progress in the hands of the publishers. I felt
that some must be growing anxious regarding
the matter, and that an explanation is due my
kind, benevolent friends.
	Yours very sincerely,

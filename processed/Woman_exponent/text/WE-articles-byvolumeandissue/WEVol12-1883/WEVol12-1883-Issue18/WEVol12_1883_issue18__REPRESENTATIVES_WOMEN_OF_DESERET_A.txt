	"REPRESENTATIVES WOMEN OF DESERET," a
book of 140 pages, is for sale at this office, $1.00
per copy. The book is by Mrs. Augusta Joyce
Crocheron, and contains a sketch of Sister E.
R. Snow Smith, Zina D. H. Young, Phebe
Woodruff, Presendia L. Kimball, M. Isabella
Horne, Hannah T. King, Elmina S. Taylor,
Bathsheba W. Smith, Helen Mar Whitney and
others.

	THE secretaries of the several branches of the
Relief Society in Salt Lake Stake, also the Y.
L. M. I. A. and Primaries are requested to pre-
pare their reports for the quarterly conference of
these societies, which will convene about the
middle of March.

	THE Secretaries of the several Stakes of Zion
of Relief Society, Y. L. M. I. A. and Primary
Associations are requested to prepare their var-
ious reports and send to the secretary of the cen-
tral organization of the Relief Society and the
other Associations.

EDITOR EXPONENT:
	During the past two months the presidency
of the Relief Society of Juab Stake, in con-
nection with President Paxman and party,
have visited the different branches of Relief
Society of this stake.
	Found the sisters alive to their duties, and
laboring earnestly for the advancement of
Zion's cause. Also visited Juab Station, Oct.
30th, and organized a Relief Society and
Primary Association in that branch. The fol-
lowing officers were elected for the Relief So-
ciety: Mrs. Hannah Kinder, President; Mrs.
Susan Witbeck, and Mrs. Mary E. Taylor,
Counselors; Mrs. Mary F. Bigler, Secretary;
Mrs. Christena Jensen, Treasurer.
	The Quarterly Conference of the Relief So-
ciety of Juab Stake will be held at Nephi,
Dec. 15th and 16th, 1883.

[---] GRAHAM CO., ARIZONA.

	This ladies of the Relief Societies of the St.
Joseph Stake held their Quarterly Conferences

[Column 2]
at Pima, Friday, Feb. 29th, 1884, President
Willmirth East presiding. After the usual
opening exercises the minutes of the previous
conference were read and accepted; also stake
and ward reports. The presidents of the vari-
ous branch societies gave verbal reports of
their respective wards, showing them to be in
a lively and prosperous condition; all desirous
of performing their part in assisting to build
up the Church of Christ here upon the earth.
	President East and Counselor Mary Ransom,
also President Christopher Layton and Patri-
arch Philemon Merrill, of the St. Joseph
stake, addressed the congregation. The sub-
jects treated upon were The Word of Wisdom,
home industry and the training of the youth
of Israel. President Layton said he felt
pleased with the proceedings of the conference;
asked the Lord to bless and prosper us in our
labors.
	Conference adjourned for three months, to
meet at St. David. Closing hymn, "The
Spirit of God like a fire is burning." Bene-
diction by Bro. Mc. Rae.

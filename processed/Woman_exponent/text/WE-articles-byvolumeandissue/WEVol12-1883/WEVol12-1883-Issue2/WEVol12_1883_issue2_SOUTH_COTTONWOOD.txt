SOUTH COTTONWOOD.

	Minutes of the Primary Association of
South Cottonwood Ward, May 23rd, 1883.
Meeting called to order by President E. Davis.
Opened by singing, "Gather them into the
fold." Prayer by Charles Shepperd. Singing,
"Don't kill the little birds." The forenoon
exercises were mostly composed of songs and
recitations, and the manner in which the young
folks acquitted themselves of their parts was
really noteworthy.
	Afternoon meeting. Called to order  by Presi-
dent E. Davis at two p.m. Singing. Prayer
by Brother Thomas Wheeler. Singing, "Do
what is right." Sister E. Clawson addressed
the children; told us she could see a marked
improvement since her last visit, and was
pleased to see so many present.
	Sister Emmeline B. Wells addressed the
meeting in words of love and encouragement. She
was pleased to see so many boys present, and
closed her few remarks, leaving a general re-
gret that she did not occupy more time.
	Sister Louie Felt, Sister Mary Freeze, Sister
Liddell and Sister Mary Rawlins, each gave
an expression of the interest they felt in our
Primary meetings, and closed, leaving their
blessings for the boys and girls of Israel.
	At four o'clock p.m. the exercises closed,
each and every one being pleased with the
entertainment. Benediction by Sister Wheeler.
						E. M. BRINTON,
								Secretary.

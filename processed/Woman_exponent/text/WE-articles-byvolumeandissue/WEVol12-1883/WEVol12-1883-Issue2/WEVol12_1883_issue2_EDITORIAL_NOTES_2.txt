EDITORIAL NOTES.

	THE Relief Society Conference of this Stake
of Zion will be held in the Fourteenth Ward
Assembly Rooms on Friday, June 22nd, and
a prompt attendance of officers from the sever-
al branches is desired. Brethren and sisters
are cordially invited.
	THE Primary Associations of this Stake will


hold their Conference Saturday morning, in
the Salt Lake Assembly Hall, commencing at
ten a.m. By request of the President, Mrs.
Ellen C. Clawson, we invite the several Associ-
ations in the city and throughout the Stake
to bring as many of the children as can con-
veniently come. Every Association should be
well represented.
	THE Y. L. M. I. A. will hold their Confer-
ence Saturday afternoon, in rhe Assembly
Hall, and Mrs. Mary A. Freeze, the President
would be glad to see all the Associations well
represented by members, and most cordially
extends an invitation to all to attend, especial-
ly the young people of this city, and Salt
Lake Stake.
	MRS. Lockwood's speech occupies so much
space that we are obliged to leave other matter
over until next issue, but we think it eminent-
ly proper that the women of Utah should
know how forcibly she has spoken in the their
behalf.
	THE preparations for the two grand Con-
certs in the large Tabernacle on the 24th of
July are going forward under the able direc-
tion of Brother Evan Stephens and Elder
George Goddard with the Committees appoint-
ed for the purpose. Mrs. Priscilla Jennings
acts as chairman of the "Ways and Means
Committee" to represent the Deseret Hospital
interest in the affair, these ladies have the cos-
tumes and colors to arrange to represent the
different nationalities.

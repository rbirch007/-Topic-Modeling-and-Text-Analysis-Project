OBITUARY.  
     Died. in Salt Lake City, Utah, December 17th., 1883,
of inflammation of the bowels, Jemima, the beloved
wife of the late Charles Brown, and daughter of John
and Dinah Offes.  Deceased was born at Loring, County
of Norfolk, England, April 8, 1814.  She joined the
Chnrch of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in early
days and passed through many trying events, but was
ever true to her covenants, and died as she had lived, a
faithful Latter-day Saint.				Com.

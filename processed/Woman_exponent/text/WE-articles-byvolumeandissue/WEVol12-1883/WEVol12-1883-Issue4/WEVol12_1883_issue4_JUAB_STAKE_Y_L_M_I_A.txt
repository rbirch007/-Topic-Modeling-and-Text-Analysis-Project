JUAB STAKE Y. L. M. I. A.

	On the 23rd of June, at ten a.m., the Young
Ladies Association of Juab Stake organiza-
tion met in conference in the Nephi meeting
house, President M. E. Teasdale presiding.
	After the opening exercises the reports of
the various wards were read, and verbal reports
given, showing the growth and improvement
of the association. The Young Ladies Jour-
nal, a manuscript paper, was read and highly
approved by the speakers. The time was nearly
all taken up according to the programme, by
the members of the association, in recitations,
songs and short speeches; after which Brother
Charles Sperry, President Paxman's counselor,
spoke encouragingly to the young ladies.
Bishop Warner, Udall and Haws and several
other brethren were present. Much good coun-
sel and instruction was given, both by the
brethren and sisters, and a good spirit prevailed.
Singing. Benediction.

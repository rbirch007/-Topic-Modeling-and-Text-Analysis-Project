DEAR SISTER:
	The ladies of the Relief Society of Weber
County held their conference in the Ogden
Tabernacle, Dec. 7th, 1883. After the usual
devotional exercises the minutes of the previ

[Column 2]
ous conference were read and accepted. The
presidents of the different branch societies gave
verbal reports of their respective wards, show-
ing them to be progressing in every way; the
members all striving to do their part in the
Kingdom of God.
	Sister Zina D. Young Smith then spoke to us
upon a variety of subjects pertaining to the
Gospel, explaining each, as only a true Saint of
God can explain His divine will, giving much
good and wise counsel to her brothers and sis-
ters present.
	Counselors Hattie C. Brown and Sarah Her-
rick and Apostle F. D. Richards also address-
ed the congregation, each expressing high and
noble sentiments in regard to the work of God
upon the earth in these days, which was lis-
tened to with great interest by the large con-
gregation.
	The conference adjourned for three months.
Closed by singing “The Spirit of God like a
fire is burning.” Benediction by Apostle F.
D. Richards.
	P. S. Please excuse this short report, and
me for not sending sooner, as I should have
done.			Respectfully,

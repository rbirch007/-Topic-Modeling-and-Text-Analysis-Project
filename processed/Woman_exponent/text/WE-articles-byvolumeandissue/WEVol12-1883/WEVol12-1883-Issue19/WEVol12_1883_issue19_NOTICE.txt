NOTICE.

	To the Stake Presidents of Y. L. M. I. A: It
is very desirable that we have full and correct
reports from each stake. Will the Secretaries
please send them in at once, in order that the
Secretary of the Central Board may be prepared
to make her report in due time for the Annual
Conference in April proximo. The reports
should be forwarded so as to reach here not later
than March 15th. Address, Louie M. Wells,
Salt Lake City. B x 5[-]5.
			ELIMINA S. TAYLOR, President.
			LOUIE M. WELLS, Secretary.

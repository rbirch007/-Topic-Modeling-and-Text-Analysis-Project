NOTES AND NEWS.

	"Mrs. Mary A. Livermore is addressing
large audiences in the West, and as usual win-
ning golden opinions."
	"Miss Helen Taylor is to write the biogra-
phy of John Stuart Mill for the series known
as 'English Philosophers'"
	"Mrs. Ann Greene Phillips, wife of Wendall
Phillips, has declined to act as executrix of her
husband's will on account of ill health, and
has requested the Judge of Probate to appoint
some one in her stead."
	"The Hoar Polygamy Bill" was passed over
in the senate on Tuesday, Feb. 26th, on ac-
count of that Senator being absent with an in-
vestigating committee in New Orleans.

DEAR EDITOR:
	The Relief Society of the 8th Ward is in a
prosperous condition. January 15th the officers
met at the residence of Sister E. B. Fletcher as
a surprise on her 61st birthday. They also pre-
sented her with a suitable present, which was
highly appreciated by one who is held in great
esteem by the ward. A bounteous repast was
spread, and partaken of with pleasure by all
present.			SARAH A. HAWKINS,
							Secretary.

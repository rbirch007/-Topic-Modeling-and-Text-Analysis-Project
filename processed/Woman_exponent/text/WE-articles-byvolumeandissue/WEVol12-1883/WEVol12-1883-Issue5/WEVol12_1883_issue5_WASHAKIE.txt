WASHAKIE.

	Minutes of the organization of the Primary
Association by E. R. S. Smith:
	"We thank Thee O God for a Prophet" was
sung by the children, after which Sister Snow
offered up prayer. The children sang, "In
our Lovely Deseret." Sister Fulena H. Zun-
dell was chosen President and unanimously sus-
tained; Eliza Ward and Elmira Pall, as Coun-
selors, were unanimously sustained; Phoebe
Zundell, as Secretary, was also unanimously
sustained; Sister Haddon T. Ward was sus-
tained as Treasurer of the Primary Associa-
tion. Number of names enrolled, 23.
	Sister Zundell gave out notice of the next
meeting, to be held on Wednesday, at four
o'clock.
	The congregation sang "O my Father," and
the benediction was pronounced by Elder Al-
phonso H. Snow.				P. ZUNDELL,
								Secretary.

OBITUARY

	DIED, at Centerville, Oct. 13th, 1883, of general debil-
ity, in the 79th year of her age, Ann, wife of John Hol-
land, daughter of John and Sarah Rennall. Deceased
was born in Maldon, Essex, England, Aug, 4th, 1805;
was baptized into the Church of Jesus of Latter-day
Saints June 13th, 1852; emigrated to Utah with her hus-
band and family, and arrived in Salt Lake City Sep. 2nd,
1860. The following spring moved to Centreville, where
she resided till her death. She was for years a teacher
in the Relief Society; she early learned and observed
the Mormon Creed, and died as she had lived, an exem-
plary Latter-day Saint.
	In the silent tomb she's gone to rest,
		Till the righteous dead in glory rise,
	The pure, the meek, the good, the blest,
		To meet her Savior in the skies.	COM.

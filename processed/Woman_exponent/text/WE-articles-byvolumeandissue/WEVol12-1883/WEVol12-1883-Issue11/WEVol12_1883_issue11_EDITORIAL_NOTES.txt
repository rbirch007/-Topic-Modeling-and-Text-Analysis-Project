EDITORIAL NOTES.

	WE take pleasure in hastening to correct a
slight mistake in our last issue, where we
made it appear as if Mrs. Barney had given
an original lecture to the Y. L. M. I. Associa-
tion of the 8th Ward. The truth is she sim-
ply read through courtesy, Dr. Pratt's lecture,
which an imperative professional call intercept-
ed Dr. Pratt's presenting herself.
	MISS KATE FIELD will give her Musical mon-
ologue in the Salt Lake Theatre, Monday even-
ing, November 5. Those who wish to have a 
musical treat should be sure and attend. Lon-
don and American papers speak very highly of
her remarkable talent for a public entertainment,
and knowing a little of the lady's versatility of
talent we cannot help thinking her appearence
at the theatre will be a success professionally
and we hope it will be financially.

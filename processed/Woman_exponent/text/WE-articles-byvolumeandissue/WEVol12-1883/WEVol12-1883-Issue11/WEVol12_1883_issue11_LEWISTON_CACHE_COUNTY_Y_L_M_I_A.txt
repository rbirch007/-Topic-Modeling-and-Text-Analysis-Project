	LEWISTON, CACHE COUNTY Y. L. M. I. A.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	A meeting was held for the reorganization of
our association on the 29th of June, 1883.
The following officers were elected: Mrs Re-
becca Rawlins, President; Mrs. Anna E. Funk,
First Counselor; Miss Louisa Waddoups,
Second Counselor; Mrs. Sarah A. Orchard,
Secretary; Miss Emma Kemp, Assistant Secre-
tary; Eliza J. Stephenson, Treasurer and Li-
barian. We have thirty-four names enrolled;
our average attendance is twenty-two; we hold
our meetings once in two weeks, and have very
good meetings. The young sisters all take a
part in helping to make our meetings interest-
ing, and try to do whatever they are called up-
on. As an association we are trying to im-
prove our minds, and learn the ways of the
Lord. Our meetings consist of speaking, reci-
tations, essays, songs readings, etc.
	Wishing for the success of the EXPONENT, I
remain your sister in the Gospel,
	SARAH A. ORCHARD, Secretary.

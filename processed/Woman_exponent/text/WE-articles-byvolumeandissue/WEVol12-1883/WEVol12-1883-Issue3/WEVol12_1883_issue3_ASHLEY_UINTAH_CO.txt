ASHLEY, UINTAH CO.
	On the Fourth day of March, 1883, we met
and organized a Primary Association with Sis-
ter Abigail Oakes as President; Ella Reynolds
and Mary A. Bodily, Counselors; Sister Mary
A. Shoffer, Secretary; Sister Annie Brown,
Treasurer. They were blessed and set apart
under the hands of Bishop Hatch, Bishop
Shoffer and Counselors. These brethren made
some excellent remarks and advised the sisters
to take such care and interest in these meet-
ings as would encourage the children and be a
benefit to them through life. On the First
day of May we met and had a very interesting
meeting and picnic, songs were sung, and
pieces recited, and we enjoyed ourselves very
much indeed. President Abby Oakes gave us
some very good instruction which we highly
appreciated. May the Primaries prosper, is
the heartfelt wish of your sister in the Gospel,
	MELVIE BASSETT GLINES,
		Corresponding Secretary.

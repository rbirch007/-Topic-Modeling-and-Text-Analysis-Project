EDITORIAL NOTES

	THE Utah Legislature convened in the City
Hall, Monday, January 14th.; nearly all the
members present. After some preliminary cer-
emonies the necessary officers were elected, Hon.
W. W. Cluff was chosen President of the Coun-
cil; C. W. Stayner, chief clerk; Hon. James
Sharp, Speaker of the House; and Junius F.
Wells, chief clerk. The oath of office was ad-
ministered by Secretary Thomas.
	ON Tuesday morning, Jan. 15, a joint session
of both Houses of the Legislature convene[-]
and His Excellency, Governor Murray, was in-
troduced by Hon. W. W. Cluff, and delivered
his message to the Legislative Assembly.

SIGNS OF THE LAST DAYS.

	Hark! ominous sounds come from afar! Dis-
asters by sea and by land.  Shipwrecks—cy-
clones—tornadoes—earthquakes—fires—explo-
sions—wars—bloodshed—murder most horrible—
suicides—secret combinations, accidents of all
kinds, nation against nation, husbands killing
their wives, mothers killing their children, bro-
ther against brother, sickness and death stalking
through the land. Surely the word of the Lord
is being fulfilled in the last days, and His judg-
ments are going forth upon the whole face of


the land. And the end of the wicked draweth
nigh. All eyes are turned towards Zion. The
President of the United States counsels against
the people of God. All sects are united in the
one object—annihilating the Mormons; but ac-
cording to the signs of the times they will have
plenty to do at home without interfering with
this people. And God grant that all the honest
may be gathered out from the nations before
the land is laid in waste And may He help
the saints to live more righteous, that they may
be worthy of His protecting care, and of the en-
joyment of those blessings that flow from His
bounteous hand. That they may be prepared
to meet Him when He comes to dwell on Mt.
Zion and enjoy His presence forever and for-
ever.							E. DOW.
	Salt Lake City, Jan. 13th, 1884.

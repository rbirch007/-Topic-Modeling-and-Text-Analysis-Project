NEPHI PRIMARY.

	The Nephi Primary Fair, held in the meet-
ing house on Sept. 21 and 22, 1883, was largely
attended by admiring visitors. The Fair ex-
hibited a variety of cooking, needle work, cro-
chet, etc., executed by the girls, vegetables and
a little wood work done by the boys.
	This is the third Fair the children of Nephi
have had, and there is a marked improvement
visible between each; not only in the number
of articles handed in for view, but also in the
execution and variety of them.
	On Sept. 27th the Primary had their Confer-
ence, which was held in the meeting house. It,
also, was largely attended by the Primary
Children, their parents and friends. The time
was occupied by the children's exercises and
addresses from officers and visitors.
			L. ADDIE SCHOFIELD, Secretary.

OBITUARIES.

	I grieve to have to record the death of Sister Lucinda
S. Curtis, who died March 24th, of childbed fever, aged 32
years, 9 months and 13 days; she leaves a husband and
seven children to mourn her loss. She died as she had 
lived, a faithful Latter-day Saint, and had acted as Sec-
ond Counselor in the Relief Society since last June.

								LIZZIE LAYTON.

	DIED, at Malad City, March 26th, 1884, Mary Davis,
daughter, of Zaphonie and Caroline Jose, aged 24 years
and 6 months, leaving a husband and six little children,
(two of whom are twin girls nine days old) with many
relative and friends to mourn her loss. Sister Mary Da-
vis was a member of the Y. L. M. I. A., which she filled
with credit, possessing a meek and humble spirit she
strove to live the life of a Latter-day Saint, although
surrounded by trying circumstances. Having taken the
right course to secure her little ones in the morning of
the first resurrection she now rests in peace, to awake
when the just are called forth at the sound of the
trump, to associate with those that have washed their
robes and made them white in the blood of the Lamb,
clothed with immortality and eternal life.
									ANNE SAWYER.
	Deseret News and Millennial Star, Please copy.

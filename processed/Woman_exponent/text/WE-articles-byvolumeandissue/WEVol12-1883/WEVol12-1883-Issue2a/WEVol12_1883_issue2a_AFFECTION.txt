AFFECTION.

God bless Sister Horne;
	I love to hear her voice;
I love to hear her warn
	The people of God's choice.

Her words are most discreet,
	Her actions correspond;
She fain would guard our feet
	From weaknees and from wrong.

Oft have I sat and listened,
	Yea, many years ago,
And wondered how such wisdom
	From woman's lips could flow.

Her words unlike "weak woman,"
	But like God's servant brave:
Called forth to preach the Gospel,
	Such words of life she gave.

She has served her God in youth,
	And honored Him in age;
Has stood by right and ruth,
	Yet humble, meek and safe.

God bless all her associates,
	I love them everyone;
They are as noble women
	As heaven ere smiled upon.

There are none in ancient story
	Excelling them in deeds;
They give to God the glory,
	And help the orphan's needs.

God bless His worthy handmaids,
	Yea, bless each hoary head:
I love to hear their counsels,
	I love the truths they spread.
								M. L. M.

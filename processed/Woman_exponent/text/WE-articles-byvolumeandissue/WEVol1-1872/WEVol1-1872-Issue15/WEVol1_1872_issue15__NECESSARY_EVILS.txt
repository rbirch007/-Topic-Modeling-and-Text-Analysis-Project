NECESSARY EVILS.
					—
	The other evening, being out in company,
I heard a gentlemen (?) declare that women
were necessary evils. Now was not that
brilliant? Quite original. In down East
phraseology, let me exclaim “due tell.”
Being only a woman, I was covered with a
due sense of my degraded position. A
necessary evil!
	Will anybody define man’s position on
this sublunary sphere?
	Let me whisper in your ear—this gentle-
man has had three wives—think he is
rather fond of said evils, but don’t know.
	Wonder if his sons with such training
won’t become unmitigated causes of regret to
some poor necessary evils.

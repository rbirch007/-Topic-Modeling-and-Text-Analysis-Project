FACTS AND FIGURES.   
 			——
 CHICAGO now has a population of 380,000.
 FORTY-nine operas failed in Italy last
year.
 THE Shah of Persia is building a new ten-
acre palace.
 TWENTY-one editors have recently been
elected Mayors in Illinois.     
 BROADWAY, N. Y., is to be widened at a
cost of $3,800,000.
 SENATOR Pomroy owns 120,000 acres of
land. 	
 DES MOINES is to have a $50,000 opera
house. 	
 No fewer than 175 almanacs for 1874 have
been published in Paris.
 THE new United States Hotel at Saratoga,
will have a mile of piazzas.
 MICHIGAN has 148 legislators, not one of
whom was born in the State. 
 OHIO GIRL students play base ball, and
the newspapers talk of it. Well? 
 TEXAS is improving rapidly.   She has
944 convicts in her penitentiary now,
against 21 in 1862.
 SIX members of the German royal family
make up an aggregate stature of thirty-six
feet and two inches.
 LADY Burdett Coutts supports a sewing
school of two hundred pupils in Spitalfields,
and a night school in Shoreditch.

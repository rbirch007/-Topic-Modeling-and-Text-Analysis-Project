BIRTHDAY MUSINGS.
 		  —
	TO A FRIEND.
		  —
	We are falling,
	We are falling,
In the sear and yellow leaf,
	Time is calling,
	Time is calling,
Telling us that he's the thief
	That is stealing,
	That is stealing,
All our youthful form away;
All the time he keeps revealing,
He is making us his prey.

	Hair is shewing,
	Hair is shewing,
Silver threads within each braid;
	Eyes are growing,
	Eyes are growing,
Dimmer in our later grade.

	Steps are slower,
	Steps are slower,
As the downward path we tread
	Voice is lower,
	Voice is lower,
Bow’d is oft the weary head.

	Shall we sorrow,
	Shall we sorrow,
That our life is in decline?
	There’s a morrow
	There’s a morrow
"Where eternal suns will shine!

	My Mother Earth
	My Mother Earth!
In thy bosom we shall sleep;
Thou alone and not another,
Shall those sacred vigils keepl
	Till the morning,
	Till the morning,
When the resurection, comes—
Then with all a Brides’ adorning
Christ will take us to our Homes—

	Ready waiting,
	Ready waiting,
All prepar’d by Beauty’s hand,
	All dilating,
	All dilating,
On the scene I now command.

	God of Heaven!
	God of Heaven!
Hold us that we never fall!
	All forgiven,
	All forgiven,
God and we be All in All

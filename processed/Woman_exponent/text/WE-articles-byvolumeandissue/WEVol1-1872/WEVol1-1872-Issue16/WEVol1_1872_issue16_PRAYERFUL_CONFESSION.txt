		PRAYERFUL CONFESSION.

	O God, when grief and pain beset,
	When hope and joy my soul forget,
		I bring my woe to Thee;
	I come to Thee and humbly pray
	That Thou wilt smooth my thorny way,
		And bid my sorrows flee.

	I ne’er forget to breathe Thy name,
	When o’er my pathway grief and shame
		In dreary darkness brood;
	I cry to Thee when in distress,
	Ask Thee to lift from off my breast
		Its weary, weary load,

	But when Thou healest all my woe,
	And when my soul is all aglow
		With golden beams of peace,
	My fickle heart, contented well
	'Mid transient gayeties to dwell,
		Bids all devotion cease.

	Dear Lord! assist my feeble faith,
	That not alone it prove its worth
		'Neath sorrow's blighting dew—
	That not alone I seek for aid,
	When treading paths of fearful shade,
		But make me grateful too.

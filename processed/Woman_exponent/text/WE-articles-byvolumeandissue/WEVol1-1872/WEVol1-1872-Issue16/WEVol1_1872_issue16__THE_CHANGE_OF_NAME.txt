THE CHANGE OF NAME.

	The Relief Societies of the following named
settlements have sent us reports containing
accounts of their properly renouncing the prefix
“Female” in connection with the name of the
Society: Smithfleld, Farmington, Ogden, Wil-
lard City, Brigham City, Kamas Prairie,
Alpine City, Santaquin, Salem, Nephi, Big
Cottonwood, 1st Ward In Provo City, Bounti-
ful, Ephraim, Wanship, Logan, Cedar City,
Mendon, Heber City, Fillmore, Beaver, and
American Fork—twenty-two. And the newly
organized branch at Riverdale, which takes the
correct term without making use of the super-
fluous word, makes the number twenty-three.
But these do not constitute a majority of the
branch Societies throughout the Territory,
Send on your minutes, Secretaries; we are
anxious to leave the “F” off the heading of the
Society reports, but cannot do so legally while
only a minority of those concerned have sanc
tioned the proceeding.

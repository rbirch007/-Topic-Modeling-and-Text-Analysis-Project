F. R. SOCIETY REPORTS.
	Mrs. Amy L.Bigler, Secretary of the F.R.
Society of Nephi, Juab county, sends us the
following, dated August 29th:
	It is now four years since the organiza-
tion of our Female Relief Society. Since
that time we have been trying to do all we
could to assist the poor, and to do our duty
in blessing those who have required a word
of counsel from us. We have one of the
best of Presidents, Mrs. Amelia Golds-
brough. We have not handled much means,
but have tried to do the best we could with
what we have had. We own one house and
lot in the north part of town, a building
spot on Main street, in the centre of town,
and a good portion of material for building
on the lot; have the foundation laid, and
expect to put up a good house this fall.
Have given $400.13 to the poor, and $30 to
the Emigration fund. Have received in all,
since the Society was organized, $836.63.

EDITOR WOMAN'S EXPONENT:—
    As a lover of Truth, I take great pleasure
in advocating the same. I have been in the
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
twenty-two years, and have had much
experience in that time.  It is over twenty
years since I left father, mother, brothers
and sisters for the Gospel.  I am a believer
in polygamy, have lived in it sixteen years
and have never once wished to be out of it.
I thank God my Heavenly Father, for His
goodness in enlightening my mind to know
the Truth when I heard it; and that I have
embraced it.  And I do know that the Lord
has heard and answered my prayers many
times.  I take the EXPONENT and like it
much; think two dollars well spent in sub-
scribing for it, and would not be without it
for anything.
						      M. E. W.
Salt Lake City.

		    SECOND WARD, S. L. City,
				February 13th, 1873.
EDITOR WOMAN'S EXPONENT:—
    At our meeting on the 2nd of January,
1873, we were favored with the presence of
our Bishop, Brother Leach, and his Counsel-
ors; also sisters Zina D. Young, Elizabeth
Howard and Martha B. Young; who ad-
dressed the meeting, exhorting to faithful-
ness and perseverance in good works. Our
financial report gave a good showing for the
year 1872. A motion to drop the word
“Female” from the name of the Society
was presented, and the vote to sustain the
proposition was unanimous.
    We held another excellent meeting on
the 6th of February, at which sister Sarah
Wheeler was appointed and unanimously sus-
tained as President of the Society, in the
place of sister Nicolona Oleson, who has been
faithful and energetic in her calling, but has
lately left the Ward.
			JANE RICKARD, Secretary.

A ﻿SONG FOR THE TWENTY-FOURTH.

	All hail the glorious Twenty-Fourth !
		The birthday of the true and free; 
	The breaking light that ushers in
		Th’ eventful dawn of liberty,

	Hail to the ragged Pioneers !
		Weary and dusty though they be
	'Tis the tread of empire at its birth
		The herald footsteps of the free.

	Oh, Father rain thy blessings down!
		Thus prayeth every heart and tongue,
	On the chosen of the people’s hearts,
		Truth's noblest champion, Brigham Young.

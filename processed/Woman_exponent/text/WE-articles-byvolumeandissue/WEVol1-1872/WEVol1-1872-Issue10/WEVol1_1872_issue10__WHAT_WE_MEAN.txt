WHAT WE MEAN.
				    ——
	Queries are still frequently made concerning
the object of the EXPONENT; whether it is de-
signed to merely vindicate the cause of woman,
her aspirations and prerogatives; or whether its
contents are calculated to be strictly religious,
with no diversity of sentiments and principles;
if it sustains all theories advanced by the Pro-
phets of God in these and former days, or if it
has no other aim than the advocacy of “Plu-
rality of wives.” Notwithstanding the concise
yet full explanation given on these subjects in
the Prospectus to the EXPONENT, which so far
has been published on the last page of each
number of the journal, we willingly comply
with the request to endeavor once more to ex-
plain our intentions definitely and comprehen-
sively.
	The EXPONENT does not claim to support
any one particular cause, practice or opinion.
But being an advocate of everything which is
calculated to interest, instruct, or in any way
benefit woman, its columns are open for the re-
ception of all communications of a suitable
nature, from the pen of any of the ladies of
Utah, whose views and practices it is chiefly
designed to represent.

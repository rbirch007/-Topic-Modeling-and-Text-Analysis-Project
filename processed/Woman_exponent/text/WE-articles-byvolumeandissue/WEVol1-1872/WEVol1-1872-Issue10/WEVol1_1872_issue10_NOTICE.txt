NOTICE.
			    ——
	As the word “Female” is useless in "Female
Relief Society,” we wish to drop it, which can
legally be done with the concurrence of a
majority of ladies composing the various branch-
es of the Society throughout the Territory.
It must be done by vote. We therefore request
all of the Presidents to take an action on the
subject, at their first meeting, and report the
result to the Editor of WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
							ELIZA R. SNOW.

NOTICE.
				     —
	Subscribers will please remember that the
present number commences the second half
year of the Exponent, and they who subscribed
for six months will understand that their sub-
scriptions have expired. Ladies in this city,
who have paid for having their papers carried,
and do uot receive them, will please notify the
editor and the matter will be rectified. City
subscribers who have not paid the carrier, can
obtain their papers at Campbell & Patter-
son’s.

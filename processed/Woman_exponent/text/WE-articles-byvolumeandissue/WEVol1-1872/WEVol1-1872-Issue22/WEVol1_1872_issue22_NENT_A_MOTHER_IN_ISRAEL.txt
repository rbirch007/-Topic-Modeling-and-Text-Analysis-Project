NENT.		   A MOTHER IN ISRAEL.


﻿DUNLOP says that in all European litera-
ture there are not more than three hundred
distinct plots, and two hundred and fifty of
these are earlier than Christianity, and had
their origin in Asia. Almost all the news-
paper jokes have reached a venerable age;
the Irish bulls are Greek.
	NEVER give up when you are right. A
frown is only a muscular contraction, and
cannot last long. A laugh of derision is but
the modified bark of a cur. If you can
laugh out of good, or the good be laughed
out of you, you are weaker in intellect than
the fool, whose argument is guffaw and
whose logic is a sneer.

NOTICE TO STAKE SECRETARIESr
	The Stake secretaries of the Relief
Society are reminded that the yearly re-
ports are to be made for the year 1905,
from January 1, to December 31, and it
will be a good thing to give notice to all
the branches in the respective stakes so
that there may be a full and complete
record, noting all the changes in officers,
new branches organized, condition of
grain stored as well as the amount on
hand; number of buildings in the stake,
owned by the Society, etc. Address
General Secretary, Mrs. E. B. Wells,
Templeton Building, Salt Lake City,
Utah.

MY SENTIMENTS.
Lord grant me grace from day to day,
And strength to do Thy will,
Oh may I walk in wisdom's way,
And all Thy laws fulfill.

And when my days on earth expire,
Dear Lord, remember me,
For its my soul's sincere desire
That where Thou art, there I may be.

And join with all the heavenly host,
To sing sweet songs of praise,
To Father, Son and Holy Ghost,
In never ending days[-]

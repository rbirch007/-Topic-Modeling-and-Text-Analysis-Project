WOMAN’S EXPONENT


[Column 1]
			IN MEMORIAM.
			MARY WALKER.
	Mary Walker, wife of George Walker, who died
June 20th, 1905, at Mendon, Cache County, Utah,
was born in England, September, 1813.
	Sister Walker was a true and faithful Latter-
day Saint, a faithful wife and loving mother; was
a counselor in Relief Society for some years, and
was always devoted to the cause of truth.

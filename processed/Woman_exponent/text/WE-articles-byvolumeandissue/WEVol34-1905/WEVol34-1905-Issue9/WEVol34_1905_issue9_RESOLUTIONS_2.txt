			RESOLUTIONS.
	Whereas, our Heavenly Father, in His infinite
wisdom, has seen fit to remove our dear sister to
a higher sphere; therefore be it
Resolved, that we mourn her loss as a dear sis-
ter, a faithful worker in the cause of truth, and as
a faithful President in the Relief Society for
many years, and that we desire to emulate her
worthy example.

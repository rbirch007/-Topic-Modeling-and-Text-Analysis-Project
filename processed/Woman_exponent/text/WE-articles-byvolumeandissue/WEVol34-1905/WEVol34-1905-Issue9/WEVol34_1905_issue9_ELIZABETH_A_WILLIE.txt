		ELIZABETH A. WILLIE.
	Elizabeth A. Willie, wife of James G. Willie,
who died Jan 25th, 1906, at Mendon, Cache Coun-
ty, Utah, was born in New York, December 3rd,
1818.
	Sister Willie was a true Latter-day Saint, a
faithful wife and mother, devoted to her duties;
was President of the Relief Society at its organiz-
ation in the Mendon Ward, and remained in that
position for upwards of 20 years; her influence
was always felt for good in this benevolent work.

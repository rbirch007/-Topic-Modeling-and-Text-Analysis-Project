“WHOM THE GODS LOVE DIE YOUNG.”

	The festive scene, the light, the gay,
		The tales of old forgotten long;
	The glimm’ring light that fades away,
		The notes that wail some dead love song.

	It’s the way of life that happiest moments flee
away, songs and laughter die upon the air, and
desolation falls upon the fairest scenes. Music
throbs out its life and life itself vanishes away.

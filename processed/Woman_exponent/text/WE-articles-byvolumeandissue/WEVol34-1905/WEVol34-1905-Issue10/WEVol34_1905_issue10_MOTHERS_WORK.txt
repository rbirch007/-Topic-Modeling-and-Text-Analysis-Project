MOTHERS’ WORK.
		SAN LUIS STAKE.
	Lecture 1.— Obedience. Reference:
Gen. 6, 7 and 8 ch., Gen. 22 ch.; Ex. 16
ch., 27 to 31 v; Eph. 5 ch., 22, 23 and 33
v., 6 ch., 1, 2 and 3 v.; 1 Nephi, 16 ch.,
9 to 16 v.; 1 Nephi, 17 ch., 7 to 19 v., 18
ch., 1 to 4 v.
	Lecture 2.—Patience. Matt. 27 ch.;
Doc. and Cov., sec. 98, 23 to 26 v., sec.
101, 5 v.; Romans 5 ch., 2 and 3 v. Read
or recite poem, “Send the children to
bed with a kiss.”
	Lecture 3.—Self Denial. Reference:
Matt. 10 ch., 16 to 39 v.; Matt. 26 ch., 53
v.; Doc. and Cov. sec. 103. 27 and 28 v.,
119 ch. on Tithing sec. 72, 15 v.
	Lecture 4.—Domestic Economy. Or-
der and system in the home. Economy
of time and means. Cleanliness and care
of personal appearance.
	Lecture 5.—Evil Effects of Patent Med-
icine. Reference: Era (Editor’s Table),
June, 1902. Appetites for drugs and li-
quor formed by their use, also injury to
health.
	Lecture 6.—Our Home. Unity, love
and reverence in the home. Provide our
children with amusements. Enjoy the
society of your children as time passes.
Extend sympathy in childish troubles.
Reverence for the Sabbath day and the
Priesthood.
	Committee:
			EMMA SMITH,
			LAURETTA PEHRSON,

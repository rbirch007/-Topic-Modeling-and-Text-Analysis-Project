		NIGHT. (OREGON.)

Mountains! the grandest that God ever made!
Shrouded in snow and with moon-light bathed, 
Millions stars, set in azure sky, 
Looking down from their home so high;
Lowing of cattle astray on the hills,
The solemn tolling of convent bells,
Forests of stately old fir tree,
A river winding its way to the sea,
A distant city's glimmering light,
Reigning o'er all,-a queen-the Night.

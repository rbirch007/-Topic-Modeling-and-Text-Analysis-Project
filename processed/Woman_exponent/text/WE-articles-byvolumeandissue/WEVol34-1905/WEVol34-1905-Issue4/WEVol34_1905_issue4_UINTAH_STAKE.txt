			UINTAH STAKE.
	Minutes of 36th Relief Society Confer-
ence of Uintah Stake of Zion held at Jen-
sen Ward Aug. 24, 1905. President Sarah
M. Colton presiding, Pres. Colton in her
opening remarks said the reason we
are holding conference in the different
wards is to get more acquainted with our
sisters and to give those that are not able
to travel a chance to attend conference,
she reported the Stake in a fair way to
progress.
	Recitation Sister Zina Dudley, Jensen
Ward, song Sister Eden Powell, Naples,
Reports from presidents Vernal and
Naples Wards, Lecture Sister Kate Cal-
der, Vernal, report in writing from Mt.
Dell as it was too far for them to attend.
Essay written by Zola S. Calwell, Mt.
Dell and read by the sec’y Phebe A.
Merkley.
	Conference adjourned until 2 o’clock
Singing, Now let us rejoice, Benediction,
Sister Sarah Pope.
	Afternoon Session: Pres. Sarah M.
Colton presiding, singing and prayer and
other usual exercises.
	Sister A. K. Bartlett was pleased to
meet with the sisters once more.
	Sister Abigail Oakes read a lecture from
the EXPONENT then commented upon it.
	Pres. Colton said we had, had a great
deal of good advice if we will only live
up to it, advised the mothers to do all
that was possible against the use of bad
language.
	Next Conference to be held at Mt. Dell
closed with singing, benediction.
	Sisters A. K. Bartlett, Abbie Goodrich,
Minnie Davis were chosen as assistants
in the Uintah Stake Relief Society at the
last quarterly conference of the Stake.

GENERAL CONFERENCE RELIEF SOCIETY.
	The General Semi-Annual Conference
of the Relief Society will be held in the
Salt Lake Assembly Hall in this city on
Thursday, Oct. 5, 1905. Meetings com-
mencing at 10 a.m. and 2 p.m. as usual.
There will be another session of the Con-
ference held on Monday, Oct. 9 at 2 p.m.
in the same place.
	It is hoped there will be a representative
from every Stake organization of the
Society and a large attendance of the
members.
	The First Presidency of the Church,
Apostles, Patriarchs, Council of Seventy,
Bishops and other brethren, also the
sisters of the Y. L. M. I. A. and Primary
are invited to attend the Conference.
	(Notice of Officers meeting will be given
later).
	The General Board of the Relief So-
ciety will give a reception to the Presi-
dents of Stakes and missions or their
representatives at the Bee Hive House,
Thursday evening, Oct. 5 from 7:30 to 11
o'clock. General Officers of the Y. L.
M. I. A. and Primary Association are in-
vited to attend this reception.
			BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
					General President.
				ANNIE TAYLOR HYDE,
			IDA SMOOT DUSENBERRY,
							Counselors.

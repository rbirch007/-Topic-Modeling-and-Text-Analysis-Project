PANGUITCH STAKE.
The Relief Society conference of the
Panguitch Stake convened in Escalante,
May 25, 1905, commenced in the usual
manner; Counselor Alice J. Cameron pre-
siding. Six wards were represented.
Sisters Minnie Loveland Snow and Jo-
sephine West, who were visiting the
Primary conference, both spoke encour-
agingly to the sisters; Sister Alice J. Cam-
eron gave a few closing remarks. Con-
ference then adjourned until some future
day, and closed in the usual manner.
	This is a very short report, but on ac-
count of so many meetings to be held at
that conference we were only allowed
one short meeting.
	MARGARE H. HAYCOCK, Secretary.

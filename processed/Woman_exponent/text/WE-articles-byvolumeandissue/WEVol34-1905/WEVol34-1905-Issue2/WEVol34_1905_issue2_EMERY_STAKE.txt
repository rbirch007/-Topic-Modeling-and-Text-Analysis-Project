EMERY STAKE.
The Emery Stake Relief Society con-
ference was held at Cleveland June 6,
President Mary A. Sorenson presiding.
There were present all the Relief Society
stake officers and Sisters P. Y. Beatie and
P. Smith from the General Board. Pres-
ident R. G. Miller, Bishop Oveson and a
number of other brethren were also pres-
ent.
	There was also a good attendance of
the sisters from the several wards, ten
Relief Society branches being represent-
ed. Excellent reports were given by the
respective presidents.
	Two lectures from the mothers' work
were given by Sisters McIntyre of Price
and Ruth McRae of Huntington, entitled
"Reverence" and "Girlhood."
	Brief, though very instructive, address-
es were given by Pres. Mary A. Sorenson,
Sisters Beatie and Smith, Bishop Ove-
son, President R. G. Miller, and Sisters
Maria Allen and Maria Guymon on sub-
jects relating to Relief society work.

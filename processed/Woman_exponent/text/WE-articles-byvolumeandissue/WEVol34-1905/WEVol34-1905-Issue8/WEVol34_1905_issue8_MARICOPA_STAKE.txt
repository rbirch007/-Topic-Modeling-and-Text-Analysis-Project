		MARICOPA STAKE.

	Lesson 1.—Sacredness of Home.

	(a) What does it mean? (b) How to
get the children’s confidence. We should
never lose it, and thus let it become nec-
essary to regain it. (c) Uproot evil by
planting good.

	Lesson 2.—Social Purity.
	
	(a) How shall we create a higher social
standing? (b) Provide reading matter
for the children that will entertain while
it instructs; stories of noble men and
women, and brave and noble deeds. (c)
Provide plenty of innocent amusements,
healthful exercises and agreeable employ-
ment for our young people, and mingle 
with them in their amusements.

	LECTURE V. 
Hygienic Food. 
"In what thou eatest, seek from thence due
	nourishment, not gluttonous delight."
	I. Bread. What are the effects of gra-
ham, white, rye, yeast-powder, and soda 
bread  on the system? Which is prefer-
able? How best prepared? No. 2. Ce-
reals - best way to prepare whole-wheat,
crackers, wheat, rice, oatmeal, etc. No.
3. Meats. Word of Wisdom. Ref. Doc.
and Cov., Sec. 89. Beef, mutton, pork,
poultry and fish. Give best way of prep-
aration to be most wholesome and nutri-
tious. No. 4. Vegetables- value as food.
Effects of eating between meals. Effects
of pastry. No. 5. Fruits- why essential 
to health? No. 6. Substitutes for meat,
beans, peas. Explain their value as food.

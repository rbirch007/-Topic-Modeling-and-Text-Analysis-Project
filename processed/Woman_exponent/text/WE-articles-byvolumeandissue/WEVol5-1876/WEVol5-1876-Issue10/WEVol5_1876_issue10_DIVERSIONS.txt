DIVERSIONS.

	WHAT word will read backwards and for-
wards the same?—Level.
	WHAT flower is emblematic of a fop ad-
dicted to fighting?—The dandy-lion.
	WHY are we sure to have justice done us
after dinner?—Because we get our dessert.
	WHAT word in the English tanguage be-
comes shorter by having a syllable added?—
Short.
	WHAT word of one syllable becomes a
word of two, by having two letters taken
away?—Plague.
	IT was said of a certain judge that he
was so reserved in his manners that one
would never suspect he had any.
	WHEN Richard III. was "himself again"
of course we knew who he was; but who
was he when he was not himself?
	WHAT word is it, the first two letters of
which are male, the three first female, the
first four a brave man, and the whole word
a brave woman?—Heroine.
	DEFINITION of a baby:—A palpitating
tiny bunch of nothing rolled up in flannel,
with the one faculty of almost automatic suc-
tion.

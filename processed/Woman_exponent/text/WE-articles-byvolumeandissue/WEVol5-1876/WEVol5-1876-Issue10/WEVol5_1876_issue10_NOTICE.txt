NOTICE.

	The Relief Society Woman's Co-operative
Association or Woman's Commission Store is
now open at the Old Constitution Buildings
opposite Z. C. M. I. The Superintendent and
those interested with the management of the
organization are prepared to do business on
commission in all departments of dry goods
home productions. They have for sale many
useful and ornamental articles, and their
supply is rapidly increasing. They have near-
ly all the varieties of church books, including
"Book of Mormon" and "Doctrine and Coven-
ants" new edition, and Mr. E. W. Tullidge's
new book "The Life of Brigham Young, or
Utah and Her Founders." This enterprise is a
most laudable one and the Association labor
under many inconveniences in starting out in
this new direction. We sincerely hope the
Latter-day Saints will interest themselves
practically in this praiseworthy undertaking,
and by their patronage thus promote the es-
tablishment of more successful and extended
home-industries, and thus advance the inter-
ests of economy in devising and practising
such measures as shall result in a thorough
system of self-support throughout this com-
munity.

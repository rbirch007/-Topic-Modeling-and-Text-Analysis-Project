	THE woman of work sweeps everything
before her, while the woman of fashion
may be said to sweep everything behind
her.
	WHAT are our young men doing? (Ans-
wer.) Trying to lead a five pound exist-
ence on a two pound salary.
	BOGGS recently invested in a box of cigars.
He calls them tickets to a course of lectures
to be given by his wife.
	BROWN PERPLEXED.—Mrs. Brown tells
her husband not to sit in his shirt sleeves
or he will catch cold. How can a man sit
in his shirt sleeves?
	MRS. PARTINGTON expresses her appre-
hension that the people of the gold regions
will bleed to death, as the papers are con-
stantly announcing the opening of another
vein.


PATIENT, anxiously to doctors after con-
sultation:—"Tell me the worst gentlemen;
am I going to die?" Doctor: "We are divi-
ded on that question, but there is a majori-
ty of one in favor of your living."
	ONE GRAND object accomplished by the
visit of the Prince of Wales to India will be
the substitution of the Sanscrit, Arabian
and Persian languages for the Latin and
Greek classics at Oxford for the benefit of
Hindoo students and candidates for the In-
dian Civil Service.

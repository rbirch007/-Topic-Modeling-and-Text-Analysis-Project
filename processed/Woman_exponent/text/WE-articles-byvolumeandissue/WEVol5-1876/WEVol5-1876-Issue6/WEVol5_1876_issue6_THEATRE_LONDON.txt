THEATRE, LONDON:—

	A benefit was tendered to the old and
celebrated Comedian, Buckstone of the
Haymarket Theatre, London, it came off on
the 8th of June-"The School for Scandal,"
was the play chosen-every character even
to the guests, being played by first class
characters, in fact the elite of the stage
came forward to honor their old friend; the
result-was one of the most wonderful per-
formances ever known. The net profits of
that single performance amounted to the
large sum of twelve hnndred pounds."
						H. T. K.

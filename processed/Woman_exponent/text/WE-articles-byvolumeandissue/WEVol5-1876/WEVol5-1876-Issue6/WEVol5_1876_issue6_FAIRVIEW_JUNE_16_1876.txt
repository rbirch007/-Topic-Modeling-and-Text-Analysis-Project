	FAIRVIEW, June 16, 1876.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	By request of our president, Mrs. Mary
A. Pritchett, I send you the Eighth Annu-
al report of Fairview Relief Society. The
Society convened in the meeting house on
the 14th of June, 1876. Opened by sing-
ing—prayer by Bishop Tucker. Report
read by the Secretary, as follows: From Jan.
21st, 1876, till June 14th.
	Disbursed to the needy................... $ 20
	To St. George Temple....................	    38
	To Salt Lake Temple......................     16
	In Treasury.....................................     12
	Spirited remarks were made by our Bish-
op and others. Our President, Sister Prit-
chett exhorted the sisters to be united, said
she was pleased with what they had
done in so short a time. We have a great
mission to perform in helping to build up
the kingdom of God, and it is for us to be
up and doing. Several other sisters spoke,
and all seemed to enjoy the spirit of the
Lord. Dismissed by Bro, Sanderson.
	M. A. PRITCHETT, Pres.

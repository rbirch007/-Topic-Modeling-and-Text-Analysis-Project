	WELLSVILLE, CACHE Co.,
		July 11, 1876
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	On the 17th day of June, 1876, the young
ladies of Wellsville were organized into a
Retrenchment Association with the follow-
ing officers:
	Miss Mary E. Maughan, President,
	"	Martha Eckersley,
	"	Martha Woodward,
	"	Lucy Haslam,
	"	Annie Leavitt,
	"	Mary J. Owens,
	Mrs. Martha A. Murray, Secretary,
	Miss Euphamia Nibley, Treasurer.
	We meet regularly, and speak of the
things pertaining to the work in which we
are engaged. Our President is an amiable
young lady, who is trying together with
her counselors, to honor her calling. We
have forty-seven members. We have ta-
ken a vote to refrain from round dancing;
we read selections from the EXPO-
NENT at every meeting and gain much in-
struction therefrom.
	Wishing the paper success, and all insti-
tutions of improvement, I remain your
Sister &c.,

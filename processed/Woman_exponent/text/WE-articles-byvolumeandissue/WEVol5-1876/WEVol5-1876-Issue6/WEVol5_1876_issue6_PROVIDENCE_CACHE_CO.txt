PROVIDENCE, CACHE Co.
	July 26, 1876.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Our Relief Society was organized April
27, 1868, under the counsel of Bishop W.
Budge. From that time up to the present
it has progressed very favorably. We have
endeavored to do all in our power to comfort
and bless the poor, the widow, the father-
less and the sick. We hold regular month-
ly meetings beside special ones which are
called on certain occasions. The sisters

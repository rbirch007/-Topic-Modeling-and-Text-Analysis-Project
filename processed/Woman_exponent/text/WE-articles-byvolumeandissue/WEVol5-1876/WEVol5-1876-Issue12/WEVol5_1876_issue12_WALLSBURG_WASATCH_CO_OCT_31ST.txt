WALLSBURG, WASATCH CO., Oct 31st.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	On Friday, Sept. 29, we had a pleasant
visit from Sisters E. Howard and E. S. Bar-
ney, who, by their teachings and instruc-
tion, greatly encouraged the sisters of this
little burg. Our Relief Society is in a
flourishing condition, and a majority of the
sisters are trying to live their religion.
	Wishing success to every enterprise for
the good of Zion, and the blessings of God
to attend you in your labors, as well as all
who are working for the spread of truth,
we remain your sisters in the Gospel,
	NANCY WALL, President,
		SARAH GLENN, Secretary.

WOMEN IN LITERATURE.

	The above heading is that of an article
in "The Woman's Journal" of Oct. 28th,
read at the recent Woman's Congress. We
have copied a few scattering paragraphs
from it as illustrative of our own ideas on
the subject. The article in its complete
form is an excellent one, is highly interest-
ing and contains many excellent sugges-
tions, and plain and truthful statements in
relation to the subject.

TEMPLE SONG.

Shout for joy, and sing this glory!
	For the Temple now is done;
Tell all nations this glad story,
	That redemption's work's begun
For the dead and for the living.
	Who these blessings will receive,
And rejoice, that God is giving
	Pow'r eternally to live.

Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
	Praise the Father and the Son!
God of Abram, Isaac, Jacob—
	Day of joy on earth begun.
And the brightness of its dawning,
	Shows the breaking of the day,
That the time is fast approaching,
	When all sin will flee away.

Glory! glory! glory! glory!
	How our hearts with rapture swell,
That these blessings we're receiving,
	Who on earth at present dwell.
Thanks to God our Heav'nly Father,
	For His mercies kindly giv'n,
For the Priesthood, and a Temple,
	For the foretaste here of Heav'n.

Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
	Praise the Father and the Son!
God of Abram, Isaac, Jacob—
	Day of joy is just begun;
And the brightness of its dawning,
	Shows the breaking of the day,
For the time is fast approaching.
	When all sin will flee away.

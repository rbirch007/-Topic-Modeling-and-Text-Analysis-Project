PRAISE.

Give praise to Jehovah, His watchcare is over
	His people, though myriads seek to destroy.
Be prayerful, then, brother and sister and mother,
	Your time in the work of redemption employ.

God will not forsake us, but blessings await us,
	If humbly we seek for His spirit to guide;
Our cares it will lighten, our prospects 'twill brighten,
	And comfort afford us, whatever betide.

Then heed every warning, God's kingdom adorning,
	That you may be ready, when Jesus the Lord
In glory appearing, while sinners are fearing,
	To greet him, when earth is as Eden restored.

Then reigning in splendor with Christ our defender,
	We heavenly blessings will surely obtain;
If we have been seeking, God's laws to be keeping.
	The joys of Celestial life we shall gain.

DEAR SISTERS:
	Feelings assured that a few lines from us
would be acceptable to you, and feeling in-
terested in every good organization or en-
terprise calculated to benefit or increase the 
happiness of the saints in Tooele, although
a distance of over six thousand miles inter-
venes between us, yet the memories of
past associations are ever with us, and give
us pleasure in our reflections, and it is very
happifying to us as we hear occasionally of
your progress, and the success attending
your labors in assisting to erect a suitable
hall for the benefit of your society, and to
enable the young, and others who are fond
of amusement to procure the same under
proper management, namely, the Holy
Priesthood. We know that the Relief So-
ciety is a great help in government having
seen your good acts in administering to

THE NEW VOLUME.

	This number ushers in the fifth volume of
the "WOMAN'S EXPONENT." On this occas-
ion (one of infinite interest to us) we would
tender our sincere thanks to all, who by their
patronage, influence, or correspondence help
to sustain the Woman's paper. The WOMEN
of Utah at the present time occupy an import-
ant position and should therefore be diligent
in availing themselves of every avenue open
to them for improvement in home-literature as
well as in every other department of home-
culture. Hoping that we may receive from
our numero[-]s friends and patrons the encour-
rgement in the future that has been accorded
us in the past, we dedicate the present volume
together with all the ability and power we
may be able to bring into requisition, to the
best interests of Zion at home and abroad.

built a $30,000 school-house, by contribu-
tions of $10 apiece......The "New York
World" has been sold by Morton Marble, to
William Henry Hurlbut......Thirty thou-
sand troops including three cavalry regi-
ments, are under orders to sail for Cuba on
the first of September......Thomas Jeffer-
son's favorite grand daughter died recently
in Boston—Coral is coming into fashion
again......A very destructive hailstorm took
place in Morristown, N. J., May 22d......

	SOCIETY:

MRS. PACK, Dear Siste[-]:
	With heartfelt thankfulness and feelings of
gratitude for the kindness shown to our dear
mother, we beg to return our sincere thanks
to all the ladies of the Relief Society; their
kindness will never be forgotten by us as a 
family. My brother and sister join with me in
thanks.
	Believe me your sister in the Gospel of
Peace.

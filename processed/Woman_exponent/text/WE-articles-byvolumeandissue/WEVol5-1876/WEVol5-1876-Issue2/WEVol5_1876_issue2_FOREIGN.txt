				FOREIGN.
	Queen Victoria's new title of Empress of
India, meets with approval in Russia......
The King of Dahomey has been fined by
the British government for permitting the
slave trade in his dominions......The Pope
has replied to King Alfonzo's letter, thank-
ing him for the same, but wishing to avoid
discussion, and that Spain need fear noth-
ing from the new constitution......Advices
from Rio Grande City report Gen. Diaz
as retreating, suffering from scarcity of wa-
ter; and desertion of daily occurrence......
Ninety members of the House of Commons
have signed a memorial, asking the release
of the Fenian prisoners.

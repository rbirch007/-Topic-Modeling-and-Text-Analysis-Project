EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The Retrenchment Association here was
organized on the 17th of June, 1875, with
Mrs. Hannah Cloward, President, and Mrs.
Anna Swenson and Miss Artie Draper,
Counselors; Miss Annie Irons, Secretary,
and Miss Ellen Munson, Assistant Secretary.
	Bishop G. W. Bradley and Counselors met
with the sisters of the Relief Society to or-
ganize the Retrenchment Association—they
gave us good counsel. Our Society is in a
flourishing condition, and we are trying to
do all in our power to aid in rolling on the
great work of the Latter-days. I think we
all should feel interested in the woman's
paper and help to sustain it. With sincere
feelings and best of wishes for your future
success, I remain
			Respectfully,

GRACE and glory are inseparably joined—
he that gets the one may be certain of the
other.
	THE best estimate of the population of
the American nation at the beginning of
its first century was 2,750,000. It has
now 44,675,000. The area of county has been
extended from 800,000, to 603,844 square
miles. Manufactures have advanced in val-
ue from 20,000,000, to 4,200,000,000. There
are only three nations which surpass the
United States in extent of surface; the
British, Chinese and Russian Empires.

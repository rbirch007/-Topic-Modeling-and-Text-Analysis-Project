WOMEN'S COMMISSION HOUSE.

	This house established recently upon a self-
sustaining principle, without capital, for the
sale of home products and manufactures, thus
giving encouragement and further promoting
facilities for home industries, ought to receive
the patronage of every person who has the
best interests of Zion at heart. It is one of
the noblest schemes ever devised and those who
are laboring so diligently to establish it should
receive the most hearty commendation. It is
an enterprise which will be the nucleus of a
great culminating system of financiering with-
in the Territory. Many hands make labor
light, and union is strength. Then let the wo-
men of Utah prove to the world by their unit-
ed co-operation in this and other matters that
they are equal to any enterprise they may un-
dertake.
	The Woman's Store recently received a beau-
tiful assortment of artificial flowers suitable
for hats, bonnets, head dresses caps etc. The
patronage of the ladies of Utah and their in-
fluence is solicited for the Woman's Commis-
sion House. Ladies please call and examine,
see what Utah is capable of producing at home.

			HARRISVILLE, Jan. 11, 1877.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Our Relief Society has been re-organized,
and held their first meeting Thursday, the
4th, of this month.
	The former president resigned her office,
and we have started anew with a determi-
nation to do all in our power to be one with
all the other branches of this great and
grand institution, which the Lord has
deemed requisite to establish upon the earth,
to assist in carrying out His wise and holy
purposes, for the re-generation of the hu-
man family. The following officers were
appointed.
	Mrs. Ellen C. Fuller, President.

	"	Ann Newton,	}
	"	Hattie E. Laney, Secretary.
	"	Amanda Daly, Treasurer.

PREEXISTENCE.

	Beloved friends! I knew ye well:
	Our spirits did together dwell
	In regions where all pure and bright
	Immortal beings live in light.
	We saw the nations joys and tears,
	Upon this earth thousands of years;
	And yet with full consent we come
	To earth and left a happier home,
	That we in trembling, love and fear,
	Might work out our salvation here;
	Might share in sorrows, grief and woe;
	And all the contrast fully know.
	And thus receive wisdom to gain
	Eternal lives, and power to reign
	With Jesus, in that blessed day,
	When sin from earth is done away.
	We knew the TRUTH must be brought forth
	In Latter-days upon the earth,
	And that we might this glory share,
	And with the noblest ones prepare,
	Temples and towers, wherein might shine
	The glorious rays of light divine,
	When God His purpose should fulfill,
	Subjecting all things to His will.
	We in the Gospel light rejoice
	And listen to the "still small voice,"
	That we a crown of life may win
	Regenerated from all sin.
								S. E. PRATT.
S. L. CITY, Sept. 19, 1876.

HOME AFFAIRS.

	WE HAD a very pleasant call on Saturday
last from Mrs. A. J. Dunniway, Editor of
the "New North West," Portland, Oregon;
this lady has been well known to the public
for some years as a lecturer, an author, and
an editor. She is on her way to Philadel-
phia to deliver a centennial address at the
Woman's Convention, on taxation without
representation.

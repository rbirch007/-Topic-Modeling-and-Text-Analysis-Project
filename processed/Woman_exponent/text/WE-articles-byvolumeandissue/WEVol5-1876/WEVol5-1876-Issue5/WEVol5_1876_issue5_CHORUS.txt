		    CHORUS

	Saints and angels shout Hosannah,
		"Peace on earth good will to men"
	Hear the message of salvation,
		Christ will come on earth again.

See the mighty angel flying
	With the message to declare,
Fear your God and give Him glory--
	For His judgment now prepare.

	Chorus.

See the fig tree's leaves are budding;
	Know this token–summer's nigh–
Hear the word which God hath spoken
	The day of vengeance draweth nigh.

Hark! the watchman's loudly calling,
	Awake ye saints! awake prepare!
Trim your lamps and keep them burning
	You have oil but not to spare.

Clothe you with the wedding garment
	Come unto the feast prepared;
You have listened to the warning,
	Now you'll have the great reward.

Jesus comes in power and glory,
	Comes to wed His ready Bride,
Robed in white and wreathed in glory;
	No more to be crucified.

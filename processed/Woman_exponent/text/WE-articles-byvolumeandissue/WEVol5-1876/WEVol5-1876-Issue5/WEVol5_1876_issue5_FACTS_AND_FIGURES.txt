FACTS AND FIGURES.

	FRENCH statistics show that woman is
six times less criminal against person, four
times less criminal against property, and
twice less hardened a criminal than man.
The recently published record also proves
that maternity is a better shield than pater-
nity against bad life. Of 1,000 female
criminals 261 are mothers, and of 1,000
male criminals 324 are fathers.

	IN England and Wales there are 810 co-
operative societies with a membership of
357,821; a capital of £4,151,634; annual
sales amounting to about £14,000,000; with
profits £1,070,000; expenses of manage-
ment £601,000. In Scotland, 216 societies
with 54,431 members, £338,946 capital,
£2,062,000 sales, £155,000 profits, and £76,-
300 expenses of management.

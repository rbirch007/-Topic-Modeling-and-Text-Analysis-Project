BELOVED SISTERS:
	I wish to call your attention to the mission
given to all Branches of the Relief Society by
the General Conference held in the Temple in
St. George; which mission is, THE RAISING OF
SILK. This important branch of Home Indus-
try has been long neglected, although through
the efforts of a few, sufficient proof has been
given of the practicability of its success.
	Millions of additional trees should be pro-
duced. Now is the time for setting cuttings
which should be attended to immediately, to
take advantage of this year's growth. What
the sisters cannot do with their own hands,
they may bring to pass as Solomon "built the
Temple."
					More anon.
	Your fellow-laborer in the great cause—the
establishment of Zion.
						ELIZA R. SNOW.
	Salt Lake City, April 10, 1877.

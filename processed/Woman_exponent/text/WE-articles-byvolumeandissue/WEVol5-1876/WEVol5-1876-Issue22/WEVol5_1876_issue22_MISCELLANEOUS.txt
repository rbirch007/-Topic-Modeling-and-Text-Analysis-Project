MISCELLANEOUS.

	INVENTIONS have taken from women the
distaff, the shuttle and the loom, only to
call her genius into wider work.
	GIRLS in their teens should learn the
worth of money and the judicious use of it.
If poor, they should earn it honestly, and
then expand it in gaining an education, or
saving it for the emergencies of the future.
				—Sarah M. Perkins.
	RUSSIAN leather is the skin of the horse
or the calf tanned with the bark of the
birch, which gives it that peculiar smell
which is so grateful to the senses, and seems
to preserve it from the attacks of insects.
	THE number of pilgrims who assembled
in 1875 at Mecca, is stated by Consul Beytz
in his commercial report on Jeddah, for that
year to have been 140,000. Of these 40,000
went by sea, and the remainder by caravans
across the continent.

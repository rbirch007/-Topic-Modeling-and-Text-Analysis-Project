PEN AND SCISSORS.

	WHAT goes first in at the church door?
The key.
	WHAT is that which is often found where
it is not? Fault.
	THE longest periods in a boy's life.—
Those between meals.
	"Borrow as you go" is popular, but "bor-
row all you can and then go" is more so.
	AN old author quaintly remarks, "Avoid
argument with the ladies. In spinning
yarns among silks and satins a man is sure
to be worsted and twisted, and when worst-
ed and twisted he may consider himself
wound up."

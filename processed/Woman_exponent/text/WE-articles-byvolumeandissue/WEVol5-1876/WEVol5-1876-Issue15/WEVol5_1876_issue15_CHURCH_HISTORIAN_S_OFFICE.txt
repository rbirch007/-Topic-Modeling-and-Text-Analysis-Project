CHURCH HISTORIAN'S OFFICE,
	Dec. 19, 1876.

To Sisters Bathsheba W. Smith, E. R.
	Snow, and all others who contributed in
	any way to the present to the Historian's
	Office:

	Please to accept of our hearty thanks and
gratitude for your Christmas present to our
office, in the shape of a handsome home-
made carpet. Wishing you every blessing
the Almighty can bestow, together with a
Merry Christmas and Happy New Year,
	ORSON PRATT, Sen.,
	GUS M. CLARKE,

COMPLIMENTS.

The following came by Deseret Telegraph:
	"ST. GEORGE, Dec. 24, 1876.
"MY FAMILY AND FRIENDS AT SALT LAKE
AND ELSEWHERE:
	"The compliments of the season; wishing
you all a 'Merry Christmas' and 'Happy New
Year.' May you live to accomplish the work
given you to do of our Heavenly Father, and I
pray you may do it faithfully, that you may be
prepared to dwell eternally in the heavens.
May God grant it. Amen.

WOMAN'S VOICE.

	Sisters do we understand why we are
placed here upon this earth? Do we fully
realize the responsibility resting upon us?
The duty of woman upon this earth is to
"bear the souls of men," and prepare them-
selves for a future existence. Each per-
son has to work out his or her own salva-
tion. How necessary it then is for us to
live in obedience to the laws of God as giv-
en through the mouth of His prophet. If
we cannot live in obedience to those laws
here, how can we expect to live in heaven
and enjoy His presence. It is necessary for
us to lay a firm foundation while we dwell
here in time, that we may have something
to build upon in eternity. Teach our child-
ren by example as well as by precept, train
them with all diligence to walk in the
ways of the Lord.
	We know that light, knowledge, intelli-
gence and wisdom are ours if we seek them
diligently in humility. We also know that
the end draweth nearer and nearer, and the
hour of trial for all who dwell upon the
earth is near at hand, and will be terribly
severe; and that His work will be cut short
in righteousness.
	If we do all we can that is right, learn
all we can that will benefit us now and
hereafter, we will not have done more than
God will require of us. Let us keep the
spirit of God continually in our hearts,
having charity for each other's failings,
striving hard meanwhile, to overcome our
own short comings, and with the help of
God if we do these things, we will conquer
in the end.
	D. E. DUDLEY.
Clifton, Idaho, March 8, 1877.

			HOME AFFAIRS.

	C. W. [-]ENROSE, editor of the Ogden Junc-
tion, and S. W. Sears, produce and grain mer-
chant, are gone on a business trip to San Fran-
cisco.
	WE HAVE recently received, through the
courtesy of the editor who was in Salt Lake
City a short time ago, as an exchange, "The
Watchman," published in Boston, Mass. It is
the second oldest newspaper in the State.
	HON. GEORGE Q. Cannon, the people's Del-
egate to Congress, left this city for Washing-
ton, D. C., Tuesday morning, Nov. 27th. We
wish him a pleasant trip and success in his
efforts to maintain the national rights of the
people of the Territory of Utah.

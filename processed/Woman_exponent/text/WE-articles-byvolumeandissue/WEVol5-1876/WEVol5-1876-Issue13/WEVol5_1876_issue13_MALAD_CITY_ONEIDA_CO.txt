		MALAD CITY, ONEIDA Co.
					Nov. 1st, 1876.
EDITORS' EXPONENT:
	Our Society is in a prosperous condition,
the sisters wish to do right so as to keep
the spirit of the Lord with them. We feel
the need of teachings from the Fountain
Head, as there are not many here that have
been in a society before, and we are but
young in experience. We will write again
soon.
	From your sisters in the Gospel,
					JANE PRICE, Prest.

						HANSINA SANBURG.

	DIED, at Clifton, Oneida Co., Idaho, Nov.
29th, 1878, after a lingering illness of liver
complaint, Jason Elihu Howell, aged 39 years,
2 months and 28 days. Deceased was a faith-
ful member of the Church of Jesus Christ of
Lotter-day Saints and well respected and be-
loved by all who knew him. He leaves a large
family and circle of friends to monrn his loss.

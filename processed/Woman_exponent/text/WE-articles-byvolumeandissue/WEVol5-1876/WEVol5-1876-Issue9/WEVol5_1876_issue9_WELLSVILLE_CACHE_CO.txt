		WELLSVILLE, CACHE CO.,
						Sep. 12, 1876.
EDITORS' EXPONENT:
	The Annual Meeting of the Relief So-
ciety of this place, was held July 11th '76.
Our financial matters for the past year are
as follows:

	Temple clothes, and Sundries..     91 30
	Shares in Co-op Institutions.....   118 25
				Total....................... 215 00
	We had a good meeting, the Spirit of
God prevailed in a marked degree. Act-
ing Bishop Jardine who was present, en-
couraged us by his wise exhortations to
continue faithful in our honorable and
holy mission, in striving to alleviate suf-
fering among the children of our Great
Parent. Several of the Sisters then arose
and bore testimony to the truth of the Gos-
pel as taught by the Latter-day Saints.
We expect to finish our Relief Society
House this fall, it was commenced last
Spring and the outside work is now com-
pleted, thanks to the brethren and sisters
who generously donated for that purpose; it
is a splendid structure, being composed of
three rooms; a large one running east and
west, 30 by 15 ft., and a room at each side
14 by 12, the large room will be dedicated
to meetings and quiltings. One of the
small rooms is intended for a store, the
other for various purposes. This good piece
of work was the suggestion of our worthy
Bishop, Wm. H. Maughan ere he went on
his mission.
	Success to the EXPONENT and every en-
terprise that has for its object the good of
the human family.
	Yours in the Gospel of Peace,
			ELLEN WHITON, Pres.

	SUGAR HOUSE WARD, Apr. 7, 1877.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	I enclose you a brief report of a meeting
of the Relief Society held in the Society
Hall, Feb. 7th 1877, President Mrs. S. B.
Gibson presiding. After the usual exerci-
ses of singing and prayer the financial re-
port for the year 1876 was read. Amount of
donations received eighty-seven dollars
and fifty cents; We have also on hand
about fifty bushels of wheat, and a consider-
able quantity of flour which we hope to keep
adding to as long as the years of plenty
last.
	On March 23, the Sisters of the Relief So-
ciety had a social party to help in getting
means to finish their new Hall. They had
a splendid time all feeling to appreciate the
efforts of the committee to make it a suc-
cess. The proceeds of the party amounted
to forty-eight dollars and seventy-five cents.
	With kind regards I am yours
				Respectfully,
					SARAH WHEELER,
							Sec'y.

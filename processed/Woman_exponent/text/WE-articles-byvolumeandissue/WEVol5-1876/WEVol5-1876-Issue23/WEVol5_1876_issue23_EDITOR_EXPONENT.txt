EDITOR EXPONENT:
	On the evening of Tuesday, March 20th,
a surprise birthday party was tendered Mrs.
Elizabeth Stickney, President of the Santa-
quin and First President of the Spring
Lake Relief Societies and the teacher of 
our precinct school,—by the misses of her
school. A number of her friends were in-
vited and all who participated seemed to 
enjoy themselves. She was the recipient
of several presents.
	The Relief Society here, under the pres-
idency of Mrs. Elizabeth Broadbent is 
steadily growing and all the sisters seem to
take a lively interest in helping to make
the Society a blessing. We have not been
able yet to lay up much GRAIN, but expect
the present year to make a good start in
this direction.
							D. E. B.
	Spring Lake, March 21st, 1877.

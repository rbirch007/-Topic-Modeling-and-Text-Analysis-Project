			FOREIGN.
	An Art and Industrial Exhibition for
North Wales is to be held at Wrexham......
A Shakespeare Memorial Theatre and Pic-
ture Gallery are to be built in Stratford......
Mr. Spurgeon it is said will visit America
next Autumn...The Emperor has given 3,000
marks towards a monument for Luther in
Eisleben, his native town......In Shetland
the ministers are expected to be as hardy
as ponies and to live almost as cheap..........
Mrs. Craik, the author of "John Halifax,
Gentleman," is erecting a drinking fountain
near her residence in Kent with the addi-
tion of penny loaves and a money-box......
Twenty-four women burned to death in a
carpet manufactory in Ayr, Scotland, on
the 16th of June......Australia will exhibit
ninety-three varieties of apples at the Cen-
tennial Exhibition at Philadelphia......The
Emperor William of Germany when trav-
elling is unwilling to receive any distin-
guishing marks of attention.

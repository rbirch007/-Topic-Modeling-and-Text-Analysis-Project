			SMITHFIELD, CACHE Co.,
						June 6, 1876.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We held our Annual meeting May 12th,
which was well attended, the sisters feeling
well and much interested in the welfare of
the Society. The Treasurer's report was
read and the financial account was found to
be in a prosperous condition; we have dis-
bursed during the past year $97.58 in help-
ing the poor, educating children, assisting
Emigration and temple funds; having now
funds in the hands of the Treasurer amount-
ing to $322.97. We are maki[-]g prepara-
tions to build a house for a store, and a
large room attached for meetings. Bishop
Roskelly gives the sisters all the encour-
agment in his power, and I believe we
shall be able to accomplish the object con-
templated, namely, a good house for the
use of the society.
		Yours in the Gospel,
				JANE S. COLEMAN,
							Secretary.

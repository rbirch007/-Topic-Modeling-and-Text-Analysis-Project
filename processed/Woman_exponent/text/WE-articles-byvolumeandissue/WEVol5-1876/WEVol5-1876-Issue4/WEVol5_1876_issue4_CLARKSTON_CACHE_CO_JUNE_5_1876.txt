	CLARKSTON, CACHE Co., June 5, 1876.
	Minutes of a meeting held by the Presi-
dent, Counselors and Teachers of the Clark-
ston Relief Society, for the purpose of or-
ganizing a Young Ladies' Retrenchment
Association.
	Meeting opened by singing and prayer.
President Ma[-]y Griffin and Counselor Ann
Smith spoke at some length, giving the
young people some very interesting and
instructive counsel. The Retrenchment
Association was then organized, with the
following officers:
	Miss Thurza Smith, President.
	"	Henrietta Smith,	}
	"	Merinda Homer,	}	Counselors.
	"	Stena Rasmusen,	}
	"	Lizzie Godfrey,	}
	"	Mary A. Homer, Secretary.
	Mrs. Martha Griffin, Treasurer.
	These officers were all unanimously ac-
cepted.
	Sister Grover then made a few excellent
remarks. Benediction by Sister Keep.
				MARY A. HOMER,
						Secretary.

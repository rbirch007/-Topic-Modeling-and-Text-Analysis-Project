			R.S. REPORTS.

	The meeting of the Senior and Junior
Retrenchment Association, held in the
Fourteenth Ward Assembly Rooms, Satur-
day, July 8th, was of a most interesting
character. Counselor Mrs. Zina D. Young
presided in the absence of President Mrs.
M. I. Horne. Counselor Mrs. Zina D.
Young and Counselor Mrs. Sarah M. Kim-
ball, Mrs. Presinda Kimball, Mrs. E. S.
Barney and Mrs. Dunkinson each addressed
the meeting for a few minutes in succession,
also Mrs. Mary S. Clark of Farmington.
The subjects were HOME INDUSTRY, the silk
culture, the spirit of the present day, wo-
man's work, &c. Some other sisters bore
their testimony. The sisters seem alive in
the things pertaining to the kingdom of
God.

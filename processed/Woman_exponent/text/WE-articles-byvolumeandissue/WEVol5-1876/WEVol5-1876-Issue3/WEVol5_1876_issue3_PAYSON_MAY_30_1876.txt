PAYSON, May 30, 1876.
Editor Exponent:
	The Relief Society of this place held their
8th annual meeting May 24th. On the
stand were President Jane Simons, her
counselors Mrs. Agnes Douglas and Mrs.
Mary Moore, Bishops Tanner and Halliday.
Several visitors, by invitation, from Santa-
quin, Goshen and Salem, represented their
Relief Societies.
	Home Industry was well represented.

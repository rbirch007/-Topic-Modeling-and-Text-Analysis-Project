		SPRING CITY, SANPETE Co.,
						May 23, 1876.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	A Retrenchment Society for Young La-
dies was organized in this place by M. A.
P. Hyde, President of the Relief Society,
and her counselors, on the 3rd of December
1874, with the following officers:—
MISS ANNENA LARSEN, President,
"	GEORGIANA CRISP,	}
"	JULIETTE BIRDIC,		}
"	MARGARET JUALISEN,	} Counselors,
"	OLIVE ALLRED,		}
"	EMMA ADLER,		}
"	ANNA LARSEN,		}
"	FIDELIA KOFFORD, Secretary,
"	OLIVE ALLRED, Assistant Secretary,
MRS. MARIA BUCHANA, Treasurer.
	We hold our meetings regularly, and speak
of the things pertaining to the work in
which we are engaged. We desire to over-
come everything that would prevent us
from gaining eternal life in the kingdom of
our God. Our President is an amiable
young lady, who is trying, together with
her counselors, to honor her calling. There
are sixty-three members of the society, and
all manifest a good spirit when they meet
together. We read pieces from the EXPO-
NENT in almost every meeting, and by so
doing, we feel much edified; and we wish
it success, and the blessing of God to attend
it, and all institutions of improvement.
				P. E. KOFFORD.
						Secretary.

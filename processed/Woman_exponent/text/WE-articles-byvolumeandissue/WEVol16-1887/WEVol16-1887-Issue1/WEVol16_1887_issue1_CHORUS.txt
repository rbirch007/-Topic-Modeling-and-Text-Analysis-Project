			CHORUS.
		For there is sweet peace in prayer,
			For we know Thou art near;
		There is sweet peace,
			There is sweet peace in prayer.
 
	Thou knowest our hearts, dear Father,
		We know in whom we trust,
	For Thou art kind and merciful,
		And we know that Thou art just.
										Cho.
	We are Thy children, Father,
		And Thou hast sent	us here;
Then whisper words of comfort
		When our trials are severe.
										Cho.
	We are Thy daughters, Father,
And as mothers, too, and wives,
	We desire to prove faithful
And gain eternal lives.
										Cho.

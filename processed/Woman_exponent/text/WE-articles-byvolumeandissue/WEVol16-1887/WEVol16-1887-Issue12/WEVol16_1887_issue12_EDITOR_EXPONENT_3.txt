EDITOR EXPONENT: 
	It is with feelings of great sorrow that we record the
death of Sister Mary Stahle, beloved daughter of John and
Susan Stahle, born Nov 17th, 1865, and died Aug 11th
1887, of brain fever, at Bountiful, Utah.
	She was always cheerful and pleasant, and her bright
smile won her friends wherever her lot was cast. She died
as she had lived, a faithful Latter-day Saint.
RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father in His wisdom has see


fit to call from our midst our much loved and respected sis-
ter, Mary, and,
	Whereas, Our departed friend and associate was worthy
our highest regard and affection, her labors in our Associa-
tion having been faithfully performed, also in the Sunday
School, we feel that some proper recognition of her virtues
be had among us. Therefore, be it
	Resolved, That we, as members of the Association, deep-
ly mourn the loss of so valuable and faithful a co-laborer.
Resolved, That we hereby extend our heart-felt sympathy
and condolence to her parents, brothers and sisters, also to
her promised husband, in the loss of so true a companion,
and we condole with each other in this sad affliction.
Resolved, That we present a copy of these Resolutions
to her relatives, that one be sent to the WOMAN'S EXPON-
ENT for publication, and also placed on the Young Ladies'
record.
	Oh, Mary! thou art gone
		To a bright and happier shpere;
	Although thy spirit's departed
		Thy memory we cherish dear.

	True, you were your mother's darling,
		Also your father's pride, 
	But God saw fit to take you
		Ere you became a bride.

	But, Sister Mary, never mind,
		A work for thee is done;
	Your husband, in God's Temple, 
		Received thee as his chosen one.

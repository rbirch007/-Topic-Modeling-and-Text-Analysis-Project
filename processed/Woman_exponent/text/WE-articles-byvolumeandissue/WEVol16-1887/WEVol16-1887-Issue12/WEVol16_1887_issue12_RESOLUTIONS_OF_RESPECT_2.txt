RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	To the memory of Miss Zella Webb, who departed this
life September 28th, 1887, at Provo City, Utah County,
Utah.
	Whereas; In the providence of an Alwise God, He has
seen fit to call from our midst our beloved sister, Zella
Webb, who has suffered with patient endurance, and,
Whereas, We are called to mourn the loss of a dear
friend and co-laborer in the work of Mutual Improvement;
one who has always been a faithful member, willing to re-
spond to every requirement made of her by the Associa-
tion; and,
Whereas, Her long suffering has been purifying and en-
nobling, and, as an example to all, will ever be one worthy
of imitation. Therefore, be it
	Resolved, That we, as members of the Y. L. M. I. A.
of the First Ward, Provo City, realize the loss of so faith-
ful a member.
	Resolved, that we extend our sympathy and condolence
to the sorrow-stricken family and friends, hoping that the
Spirit of the Almighty will comfort them in their sad hour
of affliction.
	Resolved, That a copy of these Resolutions be fur-
nished the family, Territorial Enquirer, WOMAN'S EX-
PONENT, and kept in the records of the Association.
		HANNAH BILLINGS,
		EVA BILLINGS,
		GRACE BEE,
			Committee on Resolutions.

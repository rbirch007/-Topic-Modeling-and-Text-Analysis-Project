		“THE MAORI VILLAGE
	"Is Taranaki, the largest native village in
New Zealand, and the home of Ti Witi, the
great Maori Prophet, who claims to be equal
with Christ, and was visited in March, 1885,
by Elder Amasa Aldrich and myself, as the
first two Mormon Elders to present to him the
only true Gospel. On that occasion we made
a trip of 600 miles in six weeks, mostly on foot,
and slept seven nights on cold mother earth,
and we were blest of the Lord, and trust the
seed we in our humble way endeavored to
plant will yet spring forth and bring rich fruit
to the honor and glory of God.
	“The ferns are a few of the 160 kinds to be
found in Maoriland, or New Zealand. Fern
Alley is one of the lovliest places in the world.

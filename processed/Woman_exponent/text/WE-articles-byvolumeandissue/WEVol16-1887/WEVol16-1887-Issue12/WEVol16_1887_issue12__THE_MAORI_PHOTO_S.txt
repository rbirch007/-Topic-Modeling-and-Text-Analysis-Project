		"THE MAORI PHOTO'S.
	"In the middle of the frame is Prest. Ihaia
Hopn, a distinguished Maori chief, and first
among the Maoris to receive the holy Priest-
hood, and President of the first branch, Te
Ore Ore. He died firm in the Gospel, April
27,1886. On his left is his wife, Erihapata;
in the centre of the bottom is Piripi Te Maari,
Chief of the Rakai Whakairi Tribe, and
President of the Kohunni Branch; on his left
is Sister Horiana Hopn, daughter of Ihana
Hopn, in ball dress; in centre of second row is
Paratane Tui, President, and Aporo Kumeroa
First and Arapata Te Maari Second Counselors
of the Waitapu Branch, and the others are
brothers and sisters in the Gospel, whose gener-
ous hearts and hospitable acts are not to be ex-
celled on mother earth.

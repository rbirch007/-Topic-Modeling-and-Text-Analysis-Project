DR. MAGGIE C. SHIPP'S CLASS.

	The following ladies, who have attended the
lectures of Dr. Shipp during the last six months,
passed very successful examinations, held by Drs.
Anderson and Bower, who complimented the
ladies very highly. The ladies who received their
certificates were: Mrs. Miranda Bateman and
Mathilda Olsen, Misses Carrie Jensen, Emma
Evans, all of Salt Lake County, Miss Jennie
Lloyd, of Cache County, and Miss Esther C. Par-
kinson, of Oneida County. Dr. Shipp intends to
commence a class about Oct. 1st. Any ladies wish- 
ing to study obstetrics would do well to communi-
cate with her before that date. She has given
such good satisfaction in the study of obstetrics,
that she should certainly receive patronage from
those desirous of gaining information upon this
necessary and important branch of the department
of medical science.

		HANNAH JEX, Sec.


﻿DIED. Elizabeth Barber, aged 78 years and two months,
at the house of her son-in-law, Saml. Pike, on the 1st of
August. Her remains were buried in Logan Cemetery on
Wednesday 3rd, inst. She embraced the Gospel in the
early days of the Church at Birmingham, England, and
emigrated in 1870. She endured much suffering from in-
flammatory rheumatism and dropsy for many years with
great fortitude and patience. She was a mild, wise woman,
and left five children to mourn her loss.
		EMMA PIKE.
Logan City, Utah, Aug. 5th, 1787.

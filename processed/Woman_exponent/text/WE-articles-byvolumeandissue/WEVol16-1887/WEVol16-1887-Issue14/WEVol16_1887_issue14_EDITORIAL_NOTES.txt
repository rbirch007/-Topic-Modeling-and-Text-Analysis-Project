EDITORIAL NOTES.

	We take pleasure in acknowledging the receipt
of a novel and handy little calendar, for 1888, from
Pope Mfg. Co., Boston, Mass,	The calendar


proper is in the form of a pad, containing a leaf
for each day in the year, with appropriate quota-
tions on each. A portion of each leaf is left
blank for memoranda, so arranged that the mem-
orandum can be turned to at any time. The pad
rests on a portable stand, and when placed on the 
desk or writing table, the entire surface of the date 
leaf, furnishing date and memoranda, is brought
before the eye.

			RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has seen fit in His wis-
dom to call from our midst our much loved and respected
sister, Elizabeth Parkin;
	Whereas, We are called to mourn the loss of a dear
friend and co-laborer in the Relief Society, one who has
always been a faithful member, willing to respond to every
requirement made upon her;
	Resolved, That we, the members of the Relief Society of
South Bountiful, realize the loss of so faithful a member.
	Resolved, That we extend our symyathy to the bereaved
family and friends.
	Resolved, That a copy of these Resolutions be furnished
the family and the WOMAN’S EXPONENT, and placed on
the Relief Society record.
						PHEBE ATKINSON, President,
						MARY ANN PAGE, Secretary.

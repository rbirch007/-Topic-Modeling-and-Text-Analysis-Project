EDITORIAL NOTES.
	OUR readers will readily understand that as we
published the Epistle of President Woodruff and
Council, that there is little room for Relief Society
Reports or other general matter. The Juab Stake
minutes of R. S., Y. L. M. I. A. and P. A., and
also Box Elder, Summit, and Tooele Stakes are
on hand, and will have place in the coming
issues of the paper.


THE report of the Utah Commission has been
publised in full in the Deseret News and Salt Lake
Herald, and we think every woman
as well as every man should read it and see for
themselves just how the "Mormon" question is
viewed by the Commission and how needful they
think it to have further legislation. In consider-
ing this subject it seems impossible for men with
human hearts to ask for further persecution of a
people already, as it were, pushed to the extreme;
how much more the Commission think can possi-
bly be borne one cannot even imagine.

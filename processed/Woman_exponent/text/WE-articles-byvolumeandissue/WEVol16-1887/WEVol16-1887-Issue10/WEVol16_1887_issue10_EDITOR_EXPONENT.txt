EDITOR EXPONENT:

	Thinking you would like to hear something
from our Relief Society in far off Mexico, I
will give you a brief account of our organiza-
tion.
	Our Relief Society was organized July 7th,
1887, Sister Elizabeth Hawkins, Presi-
dent; Sarah A. Corden and Phebe Fife, Coun-
selors; Elizabeth Haines Secretary, and Salena
Corden, Treasurer Apostle Erastus Snow was
present and assisted in organizing, also Sister
Minerva W. Snow was with us, and gave many
good instructions. We hold our meetings
semi-monthly. We have thirty-nine names
enrolled, and fifteen average attendance. Most
of the people enjoy good health, and appreci-
ate the peace we enjoy here.
			ELIZABETH HAWKINS, Prest.

		SAN JUAN STAKE. 

	The conference of the Relief Society and
Y. L. M. I. A. of San Juan Stake was held at
Mancos, Colorado, Aug. 27th, 1887, Prest.
J. M. Walton presiding. After the usual open-
ing exercises, the different wards were reported,
all of which were in good condition. Sister
Martha Hammond was chosen Second Counselor
to J. M. Walton, and was unanimously sus-
tained, and was set apart by President F. A.
Hammond. All the sisters manifested a good
spirit, and desired to live their religion. 
	The Young Ladies occupied a portion of the
time, Prest. Moselle Hall presiding. The
Young Ladies’ Associations of the different
wards were reported in good condition, and all
feeling well. Singing. Benediction by Bishop
Jens Nielson.
			JANE M. WALTON, Prest.,

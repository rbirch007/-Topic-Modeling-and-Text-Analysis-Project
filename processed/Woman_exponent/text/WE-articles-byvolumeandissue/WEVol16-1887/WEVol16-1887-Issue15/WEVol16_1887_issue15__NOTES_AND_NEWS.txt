NOTES AND NEWS.

	Stone bullets were used in 1514; those of
iron are first mentioned in 1550.
	Miss Phoebe Couzins has announced herself
as an independent Prohibition candidate for
Governor in Missouri.
	Miss Amelia B. Edwards, the English novel-,
ist and archaeologist, has been lecturiug on
"Old Egypt" to crowded houses.
	David Dudley Field takes exercise at the
age of eighty-two years by walking eight mile


every day and riding horse back for an hour
besides.
	It has been estimated that the number of
youug ladies who find regular and profitable
employment as type-writers in New York city
alone reaches beyond two thousand.
	Mrs. H. M. Tracy Cutler rejoices in the fact
that she is now a great-grandmother, while her
own mother still lives, hale and healthy, al-
though more than ninety years of age.
	During the year ending October 31st, there
were 19,226 visitors to Burn's cottage at Ayer.
The visitors are from all parts of the world,
most of those from abroad being Americans.
	The building for the Ramona Indian Girls
School at Santa Fe, N. M., commemorating
Helen Hunt Jackson, will cost $30,000. It
will accomodate one hundred and fifty pupils.
	The memorial in favor of granting degrees
to women at Cambridge University, England,
had more thau 500 signers, of whom 40 were
head mistresses of publje schools, 211 assist-
ant mistresses in public schools, and 42 profess-
ors and lecturers.

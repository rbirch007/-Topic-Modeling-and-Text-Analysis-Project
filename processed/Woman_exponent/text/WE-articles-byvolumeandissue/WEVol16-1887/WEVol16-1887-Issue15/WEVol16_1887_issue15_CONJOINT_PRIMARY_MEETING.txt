CONJOINT PRIMARY MEETING.

	Special meeting of the Primary Associations
of Manti, held in the Council House, Wednes-
day afternoon, Dec 14th, 1887, Olive Lowry
presiding. Singing. "Beautiful Zion." Open-
ing prayer offered by Bishop H. Jensen. Sing
ing "Joseph Smith's first prayer." Exercises
as follows: Reading by Cyrena Hanson,
entitled, "What boys should learn;" Song,
"Footsteps on the stairs," by Vernie and Addie
Snow; Recitation, entitled, "Are the children
at home?" by Gertrude Jensen; Song by seven
little girls, entitled, "Sing me a song," Recita-
ton, "The Christmas party," by Howard Cox.
Song by Lillian and Sofrona Ludvigson, organ
accompaniment by Annie Ludvigson; Recita-
tion by Annie Ware; Class exercise, subject,
"Martyrdom of Joseph and Hyrum Smith,"


by a class of little girls, conducted, by Eliza
Keller. Song, "Hard times come again no
more," by Gena and Leah Reid; Recitation by
Eddie Bench, entitled, "Two little squirrels,"
Recitation by Emily Keller, "Three days in
the life of Columbus," Song, "Down by the deep
sad sea," by Ethel Lowry, Organ accompani-
ment by Dora Lowry; Recitation, "The old
hat," by Lawrence Lowry; Organ music by
Rosetta Hougard.
	Sister Emmeline B. Wells addressed the chil-
dren in a very interesting manner. Expressed
her pleasure in listening to the exercises which
had been rendered. Advised the children to 
encourag all their playmates to attend Prim-
ary meetings and they would then be perform-
ing missionary labors. Related several interest-
ing stories, and asked many questions which
are very beneficial for children.
	Sister Mary A. Hyde made some appropriate
remarks after which meeting adjourned. Sing-
ing "Dare to do right." Benediction by Prest.
J. B. Malben.

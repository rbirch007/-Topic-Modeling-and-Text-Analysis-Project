EDITORIAL NOTES.

	"JACK, THE FISHERMAN," by Elizabeth Stuart
Phelps, is a pathetic story of the net woven by
the demon alcohol to entrap the unwary. The
tale is told with a depth of pity and tenderness,
that should 'waken a responsive chord in the
heart of every person, to work for the rescue of
precious souls from the horrors of intemperance.
This little book is bound to be a powerful aid in
such a work. The price places it within the reach
of all. May it be widely read. It is pretiily
bound and fully illustrated. For sale at James
Dwyer's Book Store, price, 50 cts.

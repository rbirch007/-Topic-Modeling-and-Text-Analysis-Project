RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has in His infinite
wisdom, taken from our midst our worthy and esteemed
fellow laborer in the Payson Relief Society, Sister Eliza-
beth Lant, and
	Whereas, The intimate relations held during her long ser-
vices both in the District and Ward Societies of this branch
of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, we,
the officers and members of the Relief Society deem it
proper as a mark of respect that we record our apprecia-
tion of her; therefore be it
	Resolved. That the wisdom and ability which she had
shown in assisting to promote these worthy enterprises for 
many years will be held in grateful remembrance.
	Resolved, That the sudden removal from our midst of
such a sister will be deeply felt, especially in the district
over which she presided, and will prove a loss to the gener-
al public.
	Resolved, That with deep sympathy we condole with
the afflicted husband and children and friends of the de-
ceased, and express our earnest hopes that even so great a
bereavement may be overruled for good.
	Resolved That these resolutions be spread upon the Re-
cord of the Ward and District Societies of Payson, and that
an Engrossed copy be given to the bereaved family, and
that a copy of the same be published in the Territorial
Enquirer and the WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
				JANE A. DANIELS,
	Committee.	CLARISSA MOORE,

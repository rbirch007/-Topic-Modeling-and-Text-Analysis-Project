EDITOR EXPONENT:

	We wish to give publicity through the
columns of our paper, that at our last Stake
Relief Society Conference, held Sept. 2nd, 
1887, President Smoot set apart Sister M. A.
Till as President of the Silk Association of
this Stake, and Sisters Sarah Saunders and
Sarah Goodman were appointed as her Coun-
selors.
	On account of the demise of the late Presi-
dent, Sister Hester Beebee, the Association has
been inactive. We now resume the business, 
and desire the aid of those previously inter-
ested, and hope those who have not heretofore
lent their endeavors towards the silk industry,
will assist by planting mulberry trees and
raising cocoons.
	Respectfully your sister in the Gospel,

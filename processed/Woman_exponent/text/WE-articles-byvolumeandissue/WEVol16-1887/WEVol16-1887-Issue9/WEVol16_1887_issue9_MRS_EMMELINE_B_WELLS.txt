MRS. EMMELINE B. WELLS:

	DEAR SISTER:—We thought perhaps a small
contribution on our part might be acceptable
to our valuable paper.
	Our Relief Society is steadily plodding along,
and as a general thing a good feeling prevails.
We are in a scattered condition, which makes
it very inconvenient. The EXPONENT is a
welcome visitor in our midst. May it ever
prosper in its noble efforts of doing good. We
remain,
	Your sisters in the Gospel of Christ,
		ELIZABETH DILL, Prest.,

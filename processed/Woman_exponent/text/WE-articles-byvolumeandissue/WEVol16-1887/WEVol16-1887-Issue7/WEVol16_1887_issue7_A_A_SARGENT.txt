 		A. A. SARGENT."

	This letter was written in 1878, but Senator Sar-
gent has ever since advocated these same liberal
views. He would not, we are confident, have
voted to have the suffrage taken from the women
of Utah, for he believed in the progress of women,
not in hindrances put in the way.
	Such men should be remembered with gratitude;
they have enriched the world by living in it, and
their deeds will live lifter them.

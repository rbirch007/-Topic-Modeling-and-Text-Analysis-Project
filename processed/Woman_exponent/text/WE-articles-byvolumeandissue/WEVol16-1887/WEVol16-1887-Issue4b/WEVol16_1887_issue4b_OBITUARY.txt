OBITUARY.
	DIED, at Gunnison, Sanpete Co., Utah, October 17th,
1887, Sarah Copley, daughter of John and Ellen Gilmer,
and widow of John Copley. She was born May 10th,
1818, in Lanarkshire, Scotland; was baptized in 1847, by
Elder Wm. Livingstone, at Ardry, Scotland; kept an open
house for the Elders in Dundee Branch from 1850 until she
emigrated to Utah in 1863; moved to Gunnison in 1866.
Was President of the Gunnison Relief Sociery from June
28th, 1875, until the day of her death   To the sick and the
needy she has made herself especially endeared; through
her sympathy for the sufferings of others, she had a keen
eye to observe where help was needed, and her kind heart
and willing hand administered it with Saintly discretion.
She died a firm believer in all the principles of the Gospel.
										H. M.
	Gunnison, Oct. 28th, 1887.

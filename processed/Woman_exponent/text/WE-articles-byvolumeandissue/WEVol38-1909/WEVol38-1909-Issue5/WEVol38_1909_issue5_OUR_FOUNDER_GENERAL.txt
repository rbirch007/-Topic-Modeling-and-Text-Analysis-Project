		OUR FOUNDER GENERAL.
	Expressions of love and esteem to the memory
of Annie Taylor Hyde, who departed this life
March, 1909.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has called from
our midst our beloved Founder-General and
first president, Annie Taylor Hyde, who through
religion and loyalty, founded this Society of
Daughters of Utah Pioneers, obeying willingly
the commandment to honor thy father and thy
mother, and
	Whereas, We shall miss not only her dear,
sweet face, her gentle voice and pleasant smile,
but her many beautiful ideas, her high standard
of honor and duty and exeecutive ability, and her
great influence in making parental honor a per-
petual suggestion in our society, therefore be it
	Resolved, That, while we say Thy will be done,
we deeply regret that she has been taken from us,
for her life was a benediction to us; that we feel
full sympathy for her family. May the home
which she blessed never lose her influence, and
may her children hold as sacred her precepts
as she did those of her parents, and
	Resolved, As Daughters of Utah Pioneers, that
we will honor father, honor mother, also the
memory of Annie T. Hyde while life lasts, and
make this society a living monument to their
names.
	Resolved, That these resolutions be sent to the
family of deceased as a mark of respect, and a
copy be recorded in our minute book.

		Annie’s hands have labored,
			Sharing love and life.
		Daughters we must follow,
			In this noble strife.

		Onward, pioneer daughters,
			Raise our standard high,
		Honor parents’ mem’ries 
			Let this be our cry.

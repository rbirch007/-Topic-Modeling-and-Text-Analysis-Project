R. S. REPORTS.
 		JUAB STAKE.
	A conference of the Juab Stake Relief
Society convened in the Tabernacle at
Nephi, March 25, 1910, President Unity
Chappell presiding. The speakers were
President Chapppell, Counselor Priscilla
Lunt, Presidents E. H. Grace and J. W.
Ord, and Sister Ann Garrett, the latter speak-
ing in the gift of tongues. The speakers all
told of the great progress and growth of this
Relief Society, since it was organized; and
the support and benefit it had been to the
Church, and felt that with them there
was no such word as fail.
	Each ward in the stake was represented,
and very good reports were given by all.
	President Chappell read a letter from
President Bathsheba W. Smith, which was
full of love and encouragement to the sis-
ters.
	Sister Chappell urged the young mothers
to take hold of this work, as it would surely
fall upon their shoulders to carry it on.
	A full choir was in attendance and furn-
ished some beautiful music, and a very
profitable time was enjoyed.
				SADIE COLE,
					Stake Secretary.

GENERAL RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERFNCE.
	The Semi-Annual October Conference of
the Relief Society will be held in the Salt Lake
Assembly Hall in this City, Thursday Oct.
7, 1909, meetings commencing promptly at
10 a. m. and at 2 p., as usual. All mem-
bers of the Relief Society are cordially in-
vited, also the Presiding Authorities of the
Church, Bishops and brethren interested in
the work. The General Board of the Y.L.N.
M. I. A., and of the Primary Associations
are included in the invitation.
	There will also be an Officers’ meeting of
the Relief Society held previously on Tues-
day, October 5, at 2 p. m., in the Salt Lake
Assembly Hall, and it is desirable that all
presiding officers who can conveniently,
should attend this special session of the
Conference. 
		BATBSHEAA W. SMITH,
			General President.
		IDA S. DUSENBERRY, 
			Counselor.

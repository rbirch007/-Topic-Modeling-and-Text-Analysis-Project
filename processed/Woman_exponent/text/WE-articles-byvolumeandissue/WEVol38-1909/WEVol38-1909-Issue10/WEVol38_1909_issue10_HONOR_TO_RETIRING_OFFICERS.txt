HONOR TO RETIRING OFFICERS.
	A grand reception and banquet was given
March 5, 1910, from one to 5 o’clock, at the
Heber Amusement Hall, in honor of the
retiring officers of the Relief Society of the
Wasatch Stake, who were honorably re-
leased at the quarterly conference, Jan. 15,
1910, after many years of excellent service.
Fully five hundred people partook of dinner
after which a fine program was rendered,
and each retiring officer presented with a
beautiful chair, as a token of love and es-
teem from all the members of all the wards
in the stake. It was given under the man-
agement of the newly-sustained stake offi-
cers, with the assistance of the ward officers.
It was one of the grandest social events ever
held in the stake and one long to be remem-
bered. The Stake organization now stands
as follows: President, Joannah E. Jensen;
First Counselor, Margaret Murdock; Second
Counselor, Sophia Luck; Secretary and
Treasurer Carlie C. Tidwell.

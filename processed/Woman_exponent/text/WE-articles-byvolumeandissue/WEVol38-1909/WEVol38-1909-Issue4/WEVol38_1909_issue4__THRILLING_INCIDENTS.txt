“THRILLING INCIDENTS.”

	A book has been issued during the pres-
ent year by Solomon F. Kimball containing
incidents and experiences in his own life,
that are very extraordinary, and to those
who are interested in spiritual matters of
prophecy, visions and manifestations, this
book will appeal with very great interest.
It is a wonderful work, and vividly portrays
the depth of religious feeling with which the
author has been inspired, not only while 
writing the book, but in passing through
the peculiar circumstances so feelingly given
in its pages.
	Brother Solomon Farnham Kimball is
the son of Heber C. and Vilate Murray Kim-
ball, both well known as early members of
the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day


Saints, and greatly beloved by all our peo-
ple while living, and the memory of their
faith and integrity will stand forever, as a
waymark to the Latter-day Saints.
	Solomon Kimball, the author, has many
of the sterling and noble qualities of his par-
ents, and the book has been written out of
the fulness of his own heart with a genuine
desire to do good to those who are wayward
and “sometime disobedient,” that they may
walk in the paths of truth and righteous-
ness and not yield to temptation. It is, as
its title indicates, thrilling in the extreme.
The book is of a character to give the reader
much food for thought. One is reminded
of the startling sermons of Heber C. Kim-
ball and also of the conversion of the Apos-
tle Paul. We are told the book is having a
ready sale, and we hope and trust it may
do much good among the young people.

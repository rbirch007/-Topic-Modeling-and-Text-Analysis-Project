RESOLUTIONS OF APPRECIA TION.
	Whereas our former president and fellow work-
er, Sister Melissa Olson, has moved from our com-
munity, and
	Whereas, circumstances have arisen which nec-
essitate her absence from us for an indefinite per-
iod, and
	Whereas she has seen fit to resign her position
as president, and
	Whereas, she has always been a diligent and
faithful worker, it is fitting that we, the members
of the Relief Society of Fillmore, record our ap-
preciatioa of her work among us. Therefore, be
it
	Resolved, that we tender her a vote of thanks
for her labor among us, and be it further
	Resolved, that by purity of life and conversa-
tion, by kindness of spirit and grace of conduct,
by patience and fidelity in the discharge of her
duties she has inspired nobler views and has won
the love and confidence of her associates who will
follow her with their earnest prayers in her new
field of labor.
	Committee: Clara M. Swallow, Hattie Robinson,
Julia Day, Hattie Partridge.

IN MEMORIAM.
	Expressions of love and respect to the memory
of Sister Martha M. Miller who departed this life
April 26, 1909.
	Resolved, that we mourn her as a faithful work-
er, a devoted mother and wife, a true Latter-day
Saint.
	That we the Granite Stake Relief Society extend
our heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved husband,
family and friends and pray God that his com
forting influence may be with them.
	Resolved that we send a copy of this memento
to the bereaved family and one to the Woman’s
Exponent for publication.
			LYDIA A. THATCHER.
	We the sisters of the Granite Stake Relief Soci-
ety have been bereft of our beloved and ever faith-
ful coworker.
	Dear Sister Lydia A. Thatcher departed this
life June 9, 1909.
	In commemoration of her good words, pure and
humble life, we pray that our Heavenly Father
will bless and comfort with His Holy Spirit her
bereaved family in their sorrow and help them
and us to emulate her worthy example.
	Through our heartfelt love for our dear sister we
resolve to present a copy of this memoriam to the
family of the deceased and send one to the Ex-
ponent for publication.

RELIEF SOCIETY REPORT.
		ST. JOSEPH STAKE.
	Report of the Relief Society conference,
St. Joseph Stake, held at Thatcher; Decem-
ber 3rd, 1909. President Elizabeth Layton
and Counselors, all members of the Stake
Board, and a splendid representation from
the wards in the Stake were present The
singing was congregational, led by Mrs.
Effie R. Montieth, who also sang with Mrs.
Knudsen an appropriate duet.
	A program for the two meetings had been
prepared, which consisted of talks on differ-
ent themes relative to Relief Society work,
interspersed with a recitation from Sister
Emma Merrill and a solo from Miss Pris-
cilla Layton.
	We were honored by a visit from Sisters
Susa Y. Gates and Emma Romney, who oc-
cuped a share of the time and helped to
make it one of the most interesting and in-
structive conferences we have ever held.
	A peaceful influence characterized the
meetings, and all who attended felt amply
repaid for coming.
		ELIZABETH LAYTON, Pres.,

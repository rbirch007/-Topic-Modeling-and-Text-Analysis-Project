MRS. JOSEPH SMITH AT CONFERENCE—BABY 
	HOME MAY BE LOCATED IN PORTLAND.
	Two noted women, one, Mrs. Julina
Smith, the wife of Joseph F. Smith, head
of the Mormon church, and the other, Mrs.
Clarissa S. Williams, sister to the late
noted John Henry Smith, wife of State Sen-
ator W. N. Williams, came all the way from
Salt Lake City to Portland last week, to
participate with the women of the local
Mormon church in the organization of a
Northwestern States Relief Society.
	These two women, who were accom-
panied by Miss Emily Smith, a daughter
of Joseph F. Smith, attended the several
conferences held last week, at the Mormon
church at East Tenth and Sherman streets,

[Column 2]
and through their efforts, the organization,
which is a branch of the largest and
wealthiest Woman Relief society in the
world, was perfected.

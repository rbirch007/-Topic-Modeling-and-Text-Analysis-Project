Salt Lake City, Logan, Farmington, and
other places joined those of the Weber
stake, bringing with them tokens of love
and sympathy in floral offerings and tender
expressions.
	The speakers at the funeral were Presi-
dent Joseph F. Smith, Charles W. Penrose,
David O. McKay, Emmeline B. Wells,
Patriarch McKay, Bishop McQuarrie and
Sister Rebecca Monch, all of whom spoke
of the good and faithful work of Sister
Richards with great feeling and eloquence.
	President Smith’s beautiful tribute to her
motherhood in his reference to her noble
and loved posterity was most touching. Her
life will always be an inspiration not only
to those who call her mother but to hun-
dreds of those who have received from her
bounty her love and her wise counsel, and
to those who will yet learn of her good
works, for like those other great women of
the Latter-day work, her deeds will live
after her and not only her own children but
the children of the Saints “will arise and
call her blessed.”

THE UTAH WOMAN’S PRESS CLUB.
	The Utah Woman’s Press Club, at
their annual meeting held an election of
officers and discussed informally the coming
year’s work. Mrs. Amanda Done was elect-
ed president, and Mrs. May W. Cannon,
vice president; Mrs. Cora Leaver, secretary
and treasurer.
	The loan fund which is to be used to as-
sist in the publishing of books by the mem-
bers, is steadily increasing and the collection
of books for the library was also reported
favorably.
	An interesting feature of the meeting was
the proposal by Miss Kate Thomas, that
the society endeavor to get Aunt Em to
publish her long promised book, “Great
People I Have Known.” This proposition
was followed by a discussion which resulted
in the decision that for each meeting during
the year a chapter of the book be prepared
and read by the author. Certainly the many
friends of Mrs. Wells will be delighted with
this idea and we trust it will be carried out.
	The new officers are full of zeal for their
work, and everything points to a most suc-
cessful and profitable year.

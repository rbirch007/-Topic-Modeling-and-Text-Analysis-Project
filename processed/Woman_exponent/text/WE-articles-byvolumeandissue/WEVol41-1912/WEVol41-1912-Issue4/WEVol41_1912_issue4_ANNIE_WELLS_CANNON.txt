E. Homer, one of Utah’s four Presiden-
tial electors, is said to have announced that
he will cast his vote in the Electoral Col-
lege for Mrs. Margaret Zane Witcher of
Salt Lake City for Vice-President on the
Republican ticket. He will also endeavor
to persuade the other three electors of the
State to do the same. Mrs. Witcher is serv-
ing her second term as county clerk of Salt
Lake County. She is a daughter of C. S.
Zane, former Chief Justice of Utah—Ex.

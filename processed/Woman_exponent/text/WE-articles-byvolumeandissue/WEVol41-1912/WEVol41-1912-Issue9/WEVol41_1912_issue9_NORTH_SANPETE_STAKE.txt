		NORTH SANPETE STAKE.
	Relief Society conference of North San-
pete stake held in Mt. Pleasant, June 29,
1913, President Williamina Ericksen pre-
siding.
	Roll was called, showing 75 visiting offi-
cers and members present. Minutes of
previous conference were read and accepted.
	President Ericksen welcomed the sisters
and was indeed pleased to see so many
present; was also pleased we had a sister
from the General Board to visit us.
	The General Board and local officers were
sustained.
	Sister Larsen from Spring City and Sister
Guymon from Fountain Green each report-
ed their wards as doing the best they could
and preparing for the calls made of them in
various ways.
	Sister Sarah Fowles reported the Fair-
view ward as also doing a good work. In
the last four years had gathered Sunday
eggs amounting to $2,000. Had assisted the
bishop in the building of the meeting house,
with donations and Sunday eggs, $3,000.
	Sister McLellan from the General Board
was indeed pleased to be present, and
thought it a great pleasure to the president
to see such a large attendance from the
adjoining wards. She had learned to love
the Relief Society work since she had been
called to work with the sisters. We learn

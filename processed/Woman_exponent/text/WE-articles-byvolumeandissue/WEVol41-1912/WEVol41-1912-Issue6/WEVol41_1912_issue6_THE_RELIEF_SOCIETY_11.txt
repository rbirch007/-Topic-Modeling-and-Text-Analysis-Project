the program had been arranged to do honor

	A vocal solo was rendered by W. D. 
Roberts.
	Sketches of the lives of the three guests
of honor were given by Mrs. Pearl Daniels
Thomas, Mrs. Melissa Bunnell Williams,
and Mrs. Zella Farrer Stubbs.
	Sisters Daniels, Bunnell and Farrer were
each presented with a post card album, and 
were showered with post cards bearing sen-
timents of the greatest esteem. The sisters
thus honored responded briefly, and related
many incidents of their experience in the
early days of Relief Society work. The 
musical exercises, in charge of stake Relief
Society choir director Mrs. Salt, were a
very pleasing feature of the program. 
Stake President Joseph B. Keeler added
a few words of appreciation of the labors
of Relief Society workers. After the bene-
diction refreshments were served.
			MARY E. ASHWORTH,
			Corresponding Secretary.

	RELIEF SOCIETY REPORTS.
		PAROWAN STAKE.
	The Parowan Stake conference convened
at Cedar City May 27, 1912, presided over
by President Mary E. Ollerton, with her
counselors and Stake board all present.
	Sisters Emily S. Richards and Julia P.
M. Farnsworth of the General Board were
present and gave some excellent counsel,
nstructions and encouragement to the sis-
ters.
	Fine musical numbers were rendered
during the three meetings held, also some
very good papers read.
	Several Other good speakers occupied a
short time.
	The Stake was reported in good condi-
tion.
	After the forenoon meeting an excellent
luncheon was served at the ward hall by
the sisters of Cedar City.
	A good spirit prevailed during the day
and evening, and the conference was very
much enjoyed by all present.
			LENA C. MORRIS,
					Stake Secretary.

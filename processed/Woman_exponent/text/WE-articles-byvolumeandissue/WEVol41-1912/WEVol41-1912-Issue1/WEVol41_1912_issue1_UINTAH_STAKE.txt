		UINTAH  STAKE.
	The conference of the Uintah Stake Re-
lief Society was held in Jensen ward June
2, 1912.
	There were present on the stand Stake
President Don B. Colton and First Coun-
selor David Bennion, Patriarch Nelson
Merkley, members of the High Council, the
Ward Bishopric and many others besides the
Stake officers of the Relief Society. After
the opening exercises and the administering
of the sacrament the meeting was turned
over to the Relief Society, Stake President
Nancy M. Colton presiding.
	Sister Colton reported the Stake work
and gave good instructions to the sisters.
She drew attention to the Sego lily, which
was used in profusion for the decoration and
referred to the time when the people in dire
distress had found that the root of this plant
contained nourishment and had probably
prevented suffering when the people had
used it for food in the early settlement of
the state.
	A sketch of the life of Eliza R. Snow was
given by Mrs. Annie K. Bartlett. Instruc-
tive and entertaining remarks were made
by Mrs. Zina Dudley, President Don B. Col-
ton, Bishop Archie Richardson and others.
	During the services a fine musical pro-
gram was rendered.
			PHEBE A. MERKLEY,
							Secretary.

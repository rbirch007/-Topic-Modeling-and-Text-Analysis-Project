BOOK REVIEW.
	We have received from Dillon, Montana,
a new book entitled “Poetic Gems to the
World I Love,” by Mrs. Louise Coulson.
The author’s picture is the frontispiece. The
collection is somewhat fragmentary, con-
taining poems that breathe sweetness and
purity and touch a sympathetic chord in
the hearts of those whose minds tend to the
spiritual side of life, thus bettering human-
ity and the home.
	Mrs. Coulson resided here in Utah for
some years, and became well acquainted
with our people. She both spoke and 
wrote in their behalf.
	We trust this little volume will prove a
blessing to its readers, and bring success to
its author.

CAKE WITHOUT EGGS.
	1 1/2 cups of apple sauce, strained and
sweetened.
	1 cup of sugar.
	2 of flour.
	1 cup fruit and nuts.
	1/2 cup of butter.
	1 tablespoon hot water.
	2 teaspoonsfull of soda
	1 teaspoonfull of cinnamon.
	1 teaspoonfull of cloves.
	This recipe makes a very delicious and
inexpensive cake.

NOTICE.
	While expecting to make the change in
the form of the EXPONENT to a magazine
and preparing for the same, a number of
articles about the March Annual were re-
ceived which we had hoped to be able to
publish, but as our readers are aware there
was no issue of the paper from May until
the September number, when this present
volume begins, the hope of the magazine be-
ing deferred for the present; and it would
be out of place to publish at this late day
these notices. However, we thank the sis-
ters for sending them and hope in the regu-
larity of the paper henceforth to be able to
publish these interesting items in the season
thereof.
	We would again insist that all notices,
obituaries, and minutes of conferences, par-
ties, etc., sent for publication be written on
one side of the paper only, as it is impossi-
ble for the printer to set his copy correctly,
unless properly prepared

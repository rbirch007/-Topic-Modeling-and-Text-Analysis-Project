FRUIT CAKE.
		(WITHOUT EGGS.)
	2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 cup
raisins, 1 teaspoon cloves, 1 cup currants,
1 teaspoon soda, 1 cup water, 1/4 teaspoon
salt, 1/2 cup butter, 1 teaspoon lemon ex-
tract, 1 cup sugar.
	Candied peel and nutmeats may be added
if desired.
	Boil all ingredients (except flour, soda
and lemon), together for a few minutes.
When luke-warm, add flour, soda and lem-
on extract.  Mix thoroughly and bake in
slow oven, having oven hotter at first.

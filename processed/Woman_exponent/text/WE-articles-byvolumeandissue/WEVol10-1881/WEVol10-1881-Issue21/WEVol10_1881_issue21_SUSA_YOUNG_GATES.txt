					SUSA YOUNG GATES.


MRS. Emily Charles is the editor of the
"National Veteran" a new paper published in
Washington.
	The House passed the law allowing woman
to practise as attornys at law in the United
States.
	The Legislature of the State of Iowa have
passed through both branches a bill granting
suffrage to women from the Constitutional
Committee. The bill reads. "That the con-
stitution of the State be amended by striking
out the word "male" from Section 1 Article 2
of said constitution and that the same be re-
ferred to the Legislature to be chosen at the
next general election of members of the Gen-
eral Assembly and that the Secretary of State
cause the same to be published for three months
previous to the day of said election, as provied
by law."

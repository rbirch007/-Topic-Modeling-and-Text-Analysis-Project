OUR GUARDIAN ANGELS.

Angels ever hover round you,
	Guarding every step of life;
They who've lived on earth before you,
	Fought life's battle, conquered strife.

They whose thoughts are pure and holy
	Strive to fill your minds with light,
Knowing naught but good can follow
	Those who live a righteous life.

Angel forms unseen surround you,
	Guard your steps where'er you stray;
They will watch, protect and guide you,
	Till from earth you pass away.

When your mortal life is ended,
	And your spirit takes its flight,
They will lead you up and onward
	To a purer, higher life."

	"Are they not all ministering spirits, sent
forth to minister for them who shall be heirs of
salvation?" Heb., 1st chap., 14th verse.
	"Oh, what an unspeakable blessing is the
ministry of angels to mortal man! What a
pleasing thought, that many who minister to
us, and watch over us, are our near kindred—
our fathers who have died and risen again in
former ages, and who watch over their descend-
ants with all the parental care and solicitude
which characterize affectionate fathers and
mothers on the earth." Key to Theology.

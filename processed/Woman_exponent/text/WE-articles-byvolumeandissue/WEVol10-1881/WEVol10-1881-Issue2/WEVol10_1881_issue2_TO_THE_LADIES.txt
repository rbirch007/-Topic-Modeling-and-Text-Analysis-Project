TO THE LADIES. 

	The ladies of this Territory who are native-
born or naturalized citizens, and who wish to
exercise the privilege of the ballot, should attend
the meetings of their respective Primaries and
get a correct understanding of the matter in
question, viz., the nomination of officers to be
voted for at the August election.  Every woman 
who values liberty should be punctual at the
polls, and cast her vote intelligently for the can-
didate of her choice.

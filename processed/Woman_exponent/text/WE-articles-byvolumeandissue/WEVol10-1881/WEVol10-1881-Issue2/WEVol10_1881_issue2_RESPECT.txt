RESPECT.
				  -----
	A surprise party took place June 27 at Bro.
Jones’ residence Hoytsville in honor of his
daughter, Sister Emma Webb, who is about to
leave this Ward. She has been President of
the Young Ladies Association for a long time
and Assistant Sec'y. of the Relief Society and
a faithful teacher in the Sunday School ever
since its organization. The Young Ladies and
and sisters of the Relief Society met and spent
a pleasant evening, we had singing and a re-
citation and the sisters expressed their good
wishes and thanked her for past services. The
President of the Relief Society presented her
with the book of Sister E.R. Snow’s Poems,
and the young ladies with other presents to
which she responded gratefully, and exhorted
the young ladies and rising generation to be
faithful, honest, truthful and virtuous. We
closed by singing and prayer at ten o’clock.

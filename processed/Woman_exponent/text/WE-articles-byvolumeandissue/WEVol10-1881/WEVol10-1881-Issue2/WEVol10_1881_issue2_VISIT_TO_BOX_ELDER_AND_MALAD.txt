VISIT TO BOX ELDER AND MALAD.
	The Ladies Conference in Ogden City closed
on the evening of the 11th, of June.  On the 12th, 
in company of Sister Jane S. Richards, attended


meeting in Plain City, returned to Ogden, (after
administering to some sick) and in the evening
went to Brigham City.
	On Mon. the 11th, in company of Sister H. A
Snow, Stake. Pres, of the Relief Society of Box-
Elder Co., Went to Harperville, met with the
Relief Society at 11 A. M., and in the afternoon
organized Primary Associations—one in the
East and one in the West Districts.
	On the l4th attended Box EIder Conference
with the Relief Society, and on the 15th with
the Y. L. M. I. A., and the P. A.  All well at-
tended. 16th met with Relief Society at 10 A.
M., and at 2 P.M. organized a P. A. in Honey-
ville.-- 17th Went from Brigham City to West
Portage and on the 18th to Malad City. 19th
met with  the Relief Society at 2 o'clock P.M. At
half past 7 met with the officers of the three
organizations R. S., Y. L. M. I.A., and P.
A.--had a very interesting time.  
	On the 20th went to Samaria met with the P.
A. at 9 A. M., and the Relief Society at 10--dined
and went to West Portage, met with the P. A. 
at 4 and the Relief Society at 5.  21st called at
Br. Zundell's on the "Indian Farm" -- from there
to Br. Standing's--met with the R.S. full meet-
ing, and returned to Brigham City.

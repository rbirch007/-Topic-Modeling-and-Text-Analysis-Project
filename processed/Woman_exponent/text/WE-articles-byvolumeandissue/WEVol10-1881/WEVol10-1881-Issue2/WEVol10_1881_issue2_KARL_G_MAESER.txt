				KARL G. MAESER.


Mrs. Sarah Andrews Spencer has organized
a Social Science Association at Washington,
D. C. Mrs. Spencer is a woman of the great-
est magnanimity of character. Her benevo-
lence and generosity are almost without par-
allel. In the clevation of woman and the cause
of woman suffrage she has labored earnestly
and courageously for years against prejudice,
ridicule and all sorts of obstacles. We bid
her God speed and offer our prayers for her suc-
cess in benefitting the condition of woman.

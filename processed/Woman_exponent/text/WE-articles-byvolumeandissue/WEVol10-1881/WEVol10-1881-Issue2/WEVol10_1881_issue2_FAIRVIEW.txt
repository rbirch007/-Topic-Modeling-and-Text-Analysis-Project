FAIRVIEW.

	The Relief Society of Fairview held their
13th annual meeting on June the 14th, com-
mencing at 10 o,clock A. M. The forenoon
was occupied with the usual exercises, reading
of reports, voting in officers. A few cheering
remarks from Bishop Tucker, Counselor Lar-
son and others. The programme gotten up by
the committee was carried out and we had a
most enjoyable time, ended with a picnic, in
which all were invited to participate. A danc-
ing party was given in the afternoon and even-
ing.
	One of the enterprises in which our sisters
are engaged is making cheese on the coopera-
tive principle which so far has been a success.
	On the 19th of May, Sister M. A. P. Hyde
and Sister Kofford of Spring City and Sister
Petersen of Ephriam with several other sisters,
visited our place and held meetings with the
Relief Society and Primary Association in the
afternoon and met with the Young Ladies in
the evening. The Spirit of the Lord attended
them and they gave much good counsel which
we hope to see carried out. Such visits from
our sisters come like refreshing dew and we
pray that in blessing they may be blessed.
				M. A. PRITCHETT, Pres.

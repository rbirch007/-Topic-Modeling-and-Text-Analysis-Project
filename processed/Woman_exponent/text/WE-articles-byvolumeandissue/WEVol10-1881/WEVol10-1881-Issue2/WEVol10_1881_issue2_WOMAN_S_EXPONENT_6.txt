WOMAN'S EXPONENT.﻿


IN the university of Kansas a woman fills
the chair of Greek language and literature,
Miss Kate Stephens, she has held the position
three years.
 JENNIE June has gone to spend the season in
Europe. Miss Sprague "The Earnest Trifler"
has gone abroad for the summer. Bishop Hav-
en at the American Woman's Rights Meeting
at Des Moines, Iowa said "Only the ballot in the
hands of women can save this nation from
bankruptcy in finances, minds and morals. As
a nation of drunkards they will entail heredi-
tary diseases and vitiated tastes not upon sons
oply, but upon the daughters, the mothers of
men also.”

ST. JOSEPH, APACHE CO.

EDITOR EXPONENT:—At the request of our
President I write to inform you that we have a
Relief Society organized here with Sister Mary
Richards as President and Sister Jane Allen
and Lois A. Bushman as Counselors. We have
eighteen members all young in experience, but
who endeavor to do what they can to progress
this great and glorious work in which we are
engaged. We meet regularly once a month
ana hold sewing meetings whenever necessary.
We have made several quilts, have at present
$4.15 in the treasury—we live in the Order
and have no poor and a good Spirit prevails in
our midst—our Sunday School and day school
are both well attended. We take the Expon-
ent and appreciate the good instructions it
contains and say success to the Exponent and
to its contributors. Our prospects for the com-
ing season are moderately good.
		Lois A. Bushman.

				A. LOUISE HASLER, Sec'y.

	The family and friends of Sister Jane Baily,
President of the Relief Society Moroni, gave her
a surprise party on her 71st birthday July 4, 1881,
the tables were loaded with the bounties our
heavenly Father bestows upon his children. 
Songs, speeches and some very good instructions,
and many presents were given her as tokens of
respect for her faithful labors. One beautiful
chromo with the following inscription "There is
no place like home." Sister Baily thanked all
very kindly who had aided in making her 71st
birthday so pleasant and hoped that the Lord
would bless them. All enjoyed themselves
throughout the evening. Some verses were
written for the occasion by Sister A. M. Nielson.

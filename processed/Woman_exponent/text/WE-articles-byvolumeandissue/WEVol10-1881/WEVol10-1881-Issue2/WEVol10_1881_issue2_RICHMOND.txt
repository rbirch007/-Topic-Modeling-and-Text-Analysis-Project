RICHMOND.

	Our organization has at last been completed.
At our last monthly meeting Sister Frances
Teasdale was voted in as second Counselor to
President Myra Merrill, and Sister Rosina
Hyers as President of the Teachers. We have
the rock foundation laid for our house and hope
to have it built and finished ere winter sets in,
where we can hold meetings more comfortably
than we are now doing altho' we enjoy our-
selves very nicely in our Meeting Hall. On
the 4th of June we held our annual meeting
when we had the pleasure of having our es-
teemed friend Sister Libby Benson, President
of Relief Societies of Cache Valley Stake, pre-
sent with her first Counselor Sister Louie Ben-
son; had the comfort and blessing of our sisters
which made us rejoice. I am pleased to say
there is very little sickness here tho' we have
had very serious times, having had six deaths
occur within a short time of each other lately.
		Yours in the truth,

INDUSTRIAL PRIMARY FAIR.

	The Children's Fair for the Primary Associa-
tions of Salt Lake County will be held in the
Social Hall in this city, commencing on Thurs-
day, the 22d inst., and continue three days. It
will be held under the auspices of the President,
Mrs. E. C. Clawson, and her assistants. On


Tuesday and Wednesday, September 20 and 21,
it is expected all the articles prepared for the ex-
hibition will be brought in, ticketed with the
name and age of the child, and the Ward, or
place, to which he or she belongs. Such articles
as bread, cake, pie, preserves, etc., also vegeta-
bles or flowers, must be brought in on the last
day. It is anticipated that the President, or one
of her Counselors, of each Association, will take
charge of her respective department in the Fair,
in order to prevent any confusion. Price of ad-
mission will be, adults ten cents, children five
cents.

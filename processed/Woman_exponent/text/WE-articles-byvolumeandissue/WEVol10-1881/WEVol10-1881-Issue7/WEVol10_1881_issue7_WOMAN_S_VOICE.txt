WOMAN'S VOICE.

	Shall we not rejoice even in affliction? Do
we not see the mighty hand of God stretched
forth every day in our behalf? See the glorious
fields of grain, the sweet-smelling hay, the gar-
dens, the fruits; the flowers, all proclaim ye are
God's people! I have gathered, you from the
four quarters of the earth, and I will bless you!
I will pour forth into your laps the rich treas-
ures of the earth for ye are mine! Oh, be faith-
ful ye handmaidens of the God of heaven for
ye are indeed the blest wives and daughters of
the noble sons of Ephraim, who are ever seeking
for the welfare of Zion! let us help bear the
banners of mercy truth and justice in all meek-
ness, in all sincerity, let us do and dare in our
sphere of action: strive to bring forth a noble
race of sons and daughters, who will acknow-
ledge the hand of God, who will be more dili-
gent than we are in serving the Lord our God,
for they are heirs of truth, and will be lovers
of every good. They will go on conquering
and to conquer every influence that is not of
God, if we will live near unto Him who ruleth
the Universe. Then let us rejoice for we have
a glorious future before us, upward and ouward
be our motto! side by side with our husbands,
be our watchword! That we may receive the
blessed assureance that will be given to all who
strive to serve God and keep His command-
ments—"Well done good and faithful servants
enter thou into the glory of thy Lord."
	B. M.


THE American Woman Suffrage Association
will hold its next annual meeting in Louisville
Kentucky, Oct. 25 and 26. This will be very
soon after the Woman's Congress in Buffalo.
The Western and Eastern ladies who go to the
Congress in Buffalo will be able to attend the
Convention in Louisville on their return with-
out extra expense.

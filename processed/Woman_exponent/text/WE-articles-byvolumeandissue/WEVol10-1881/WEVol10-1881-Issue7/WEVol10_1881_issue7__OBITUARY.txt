OBITUARY.

	Died, in the 10th Ward, Salt Lake City, Au-
gust 18th, 1881, of cholera infantum and teeth-
ing, Don Carlos Bailey, son of S. H. B. and
Mary C. Smith, aged 9 months and 27 days. His
last words were “mamma, mamma.”

Don Carlos, thou wert dear and precious;
	Thou hast gone in peace to rest.
But again I'll have my treasure
	When “mamma” is spared the best. 

Don Carlos, now I say farewell;
	My love for thee death cannot sever;
And when we meet in heaven to dwell,
	We'll meet to part no more forever.

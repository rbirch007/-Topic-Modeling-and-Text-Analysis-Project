SECRETARIES Y. L. M. I. A.

	The Stake Secretaries of Y. L. M. I. A. are
earnestly requested to send in complete reports
(which will comprise each branch) to the Secre-
tary of the Central Board, as early as the middle
of September, that an accurate report of all the
organizations may be presented to the October
Conference.  This has never yet been done, be-
cause Stake Secretaries have failed to send in
proper reports.  Those who have reported in full
from date of organization need only send from
date of former statistics.  It is an important
matter and should be promptly attended to.
The Secretary of the Central Board is expected
to hand in the full report two or three days pre-
vious to the October Conference.  Bear Lake
Stake, Box Elder and Weber sent very credia-
ble reports last year, but there were not any
from several other localities, so it would have
been very unsatisfactory to attempt a complete
report, and was therefore laid over to the pres-
ent year.  Be sure and state number of branches
in the Stake, as well as number of members, and
all other items worthy of mention,  not forget-
ting the editing of manuscript papers. Direct all
reports to the Secretary, Louie Wells,  Office
WOMAN'S EXPONENT, Salt Lake City.

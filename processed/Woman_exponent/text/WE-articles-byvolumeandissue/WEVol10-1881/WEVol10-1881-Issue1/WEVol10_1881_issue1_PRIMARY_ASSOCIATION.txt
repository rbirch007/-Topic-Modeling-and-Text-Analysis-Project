PRIMARY ASSOCIATION.

	The following notice will show what Primary
children can do for Zion.
	On the 23d of April, 1881, the Primary Asso-
ciation of East Bauntiful held their second an-
nual meeting. The children met at half past
one o'clock. After the opening exercises the
time was taken up by the children. Songs were
sung, recitations given, also select reading and
dialogues. Brother Anson Call then addressed
the children and gave them some very good
counsel and expressed himself very much pleas-
ed with the proceedings. A few of the parents
were present. After the meeting closed Presi-
dent Ann Dustin, with her assistants passed
round refreshments to the children, oranges,
candy, cakes, etc., all partook cheerfully and
were well satisfied and left the tabernacle much
pleased. 
	On the 16th of March previous the Primary
Association gave an entertainment in the


tabernacle, the proceeds, $22,30 was donated for
the benefit of the missionaries.

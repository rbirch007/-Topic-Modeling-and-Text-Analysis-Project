PRICE WARD WASHINGTON COUNTY.

	The Sisters of this place were organized into
a Relief Society on the 21, of Feb. 1879,
Sisters Minerva Snow, Anna Ivins, and Hannah
Romney, of St. George being present. Sister
Hannah M. Miles, President, Lydia Blair, and
Lenora Gardner, Counselors; Ruth Fawcett,
Secretary; Margaret Andrus, Treasurer. We
have two teachers and 18 members enrolled;
hold our testimony meetings every fast-day.
We are all trying to do the best we can, we are
knitting socks, making quilts, which we donate
to the Manti Temple. We donate monthly
for the P. E. Fund.
	Sisters E. R. Snow Smith and Zina D. Young,
visited us Feb. 5 1881, held meeting at 10, A.
M., which was highly appreciated by all. Met
at 2 P.M. Sister Eliza R. Snow then organized a
Primary Association with 30 members enrolled.
Our meetings are held Saturday afternoons
which are well attended; the children highly
appreciate their meetings and will never forget
those honorable sisters, and if we strictly hearken
to their counsel it will prove a great blessing to
us both here and hereafter.

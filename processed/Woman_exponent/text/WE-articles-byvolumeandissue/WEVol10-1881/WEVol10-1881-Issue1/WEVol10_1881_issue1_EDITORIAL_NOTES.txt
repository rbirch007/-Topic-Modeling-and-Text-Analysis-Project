EDITORIAL NOTES.

	SISTERS E. R. S. Smith, E. C. S. Clawson
and L. B. Felt went to Granite yesterday to or-
ganize a Primary Asssociation in that Ward.

	Sister Zina D. H. Young and M. Isabella
Horne left for Richfield May 23d to attend the
Sisters' Conference of Sevier Stake, which
was expected to convene the 27th ult. They in-
tend to visit through the settlements of Sevier
County before returning.

	Ladies Conferences: Weber Stake Conference
will be held in Ogden, 9th, 10th and 11th of this
month. Tooele Stake Conference will be held
in Grantsville on the 11th and 12th of June
Box Elder Conference in Brigham City the 13th
and 14th of June. Utah Stake Conference was
held the 27th of May; Sisters Eliza R. Snow and
E. S. Taylor, of this city attended.

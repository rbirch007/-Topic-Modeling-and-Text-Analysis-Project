OBITUARIES.

	DIED: in Evanston, Wyoming Territory, April
13th, 1881, of heart disease, Mary Ann Schopp,
daughter of Thomas and Ann Hewitt, and wife
of Phillip Schopp, born Febuary 4th 1827, at
Sutter Leasdel, Derby, England. Baptized July
14th, 1849. Was the mother of 11 children 8 of
whom are now living: deceased was a faithful
member of the Relief Society and died a true
Latter-day Saint. 

	DIED: in Evanston Wyoming Territory, May
8th, 1881, of Paralysis, Sarah Whittle aged 74
years one month and 11 days; she emigrated
from Botton Lancashire, England, September
13th, 1876. Sister Whittle died in full hope of a
glorious resurrection; was a faithful teacher of
the Relief Society of the Evanston Ward, was
always found in the path where duty called her,
and when the message from the Master came,
she was ready with her lamp trimmed and
burning.
		MRS. H. BURTON President R. S.
		MRS. MAUD BURTON, Secretary.

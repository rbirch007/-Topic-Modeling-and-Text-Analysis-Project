LADY OFFICERS.

	Having been often inquired of in regard to
the names of ladies holding offices in the Presid-
ing Board of Latter-day Saint women's organi-
zations, it has been thought proper to publish
them, for the benefit of those living at a dis-
tance, that no mistakes need occur.
	RELIEF SOCIETY CENTRAL BOARD.
Eliza R. Snow Smith, President.
Zina D. Young,				Counselors.
Elizabeth Ann Whitney,
Sarah M. Kimball, Secretary.
M. Isabella Horne, Treasurer.

OBITUARY.
	DIED, at Bellevue, Kane Co., September 28,
1881, of consumption, Christine Sylvester, wife
of Joshua W. Sylvester, Bishop of Elsinore,
Sevier Co., while on a visit at the house of her
father-in-law, Mr. James Sylvester. Deceased
was a native of Mygdal, Hjorring, Denmark,
born April 19, 1847; baptized in the year 1855,
and emigrated to Utah in 1862. She was Presi-
dent of the Relief Society of Elsinore, and was
firm in the belief and practice of the principles
of the Everlasting Gospel. She was a true and
loving wife, a kind and affectionate mother, and
was beloved and respected by all those who,
through duty and circumstances, had made her
acquaintance.  She leaves a husband and seven
children and a large circle of friends to mourn
her loss.

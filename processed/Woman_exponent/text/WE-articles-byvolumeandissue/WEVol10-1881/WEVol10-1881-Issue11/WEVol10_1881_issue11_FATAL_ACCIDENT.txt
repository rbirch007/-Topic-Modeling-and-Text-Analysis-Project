FATAL ACCIDENT.

	About 11 o'clock on Nov. 3, Maggie Rebecca,
daughter of George and Jane S. Coleman, of
Escalante, Iron Co., fell, at that place, from a
wagon loaded with adobies and being driven by


the father. The girl was run over by one of the
hind wheels, crushing the life right out of her.
All was done that could be to restore vitality,
but to no effect—the spirit had fled. About a
month previous to her death, the child fell into
the well and came near being drowned. Little
Maggie was three years old the 5th Of October.
Being her mother's youngest and a fine, promis-
ing child, she was the joy and sunshine of her
home.’ We deeply sympathize with our dear
and valued friends, the parents and family, in
this sudden and sorrowful bereavement.
								LULA.

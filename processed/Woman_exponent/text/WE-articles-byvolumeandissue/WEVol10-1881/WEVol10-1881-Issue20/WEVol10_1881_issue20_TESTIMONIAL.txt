TESTIMONIAL

Of respect and esteem to the memory of our be-
loved sister, Sarah Ann Needham.
	Whereas, It has pleased God, our Heavenly
Father, to release our sister from affliction and
suffering, therefore,
	Resolved, That we, the Sisters of the Relief
Society of the 8th Ward, have lost in her a kind
and affectionate sister and true friend, and that
we sincerely sympathize with her bereaved hus-
band, children, and family.

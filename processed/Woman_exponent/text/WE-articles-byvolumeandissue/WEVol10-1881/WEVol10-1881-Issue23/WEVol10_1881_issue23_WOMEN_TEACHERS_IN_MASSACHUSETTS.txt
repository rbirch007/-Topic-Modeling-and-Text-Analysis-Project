WOMEN TEACHERS IN MASSACHUSETTS.

	Of teachers in 1879, in the public schools of
Massachusetts, there were: Males, 1,133
Females, 7,482; total, 8,615.
	Of the above, 494 were teachers in high
schools.
	The sexes are not indicated in the above
figures, but deducting male principals, about
200, most of the remainder were females, show-
ing a decided majority of female teachers in the
high schools also.—Ex.

EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Before the Stake organization the East,
West, and South Bountiful sisters, met in one
Relief Society.
	Jan 26th, 1878, our Society was organized
in a Ward by our Bishop. Mrs. Phebe Atkin-
son President; Elizabeth Brown. Jane Hatch,
Counselors; Rebecca W. Brown Secretary;
Harriet Ellis, Treasurer. With a quorum of
twelve teachers,who strive to faithfully perform
their duty. Our Society when first organized
consisted of fifty-six members, of whom nine
have removed and three died.
	Jan. 1880, our board of teachers was organ-
ized, Mrs. Rebecca Moss, Presiding Teacher;
Hannah Arnold, Sec’y. Our financial report
is as follows, from our organization until
Oct. 1881.
	Receipts, cash $152.50; property 97,93; dis-
bursed, meeting house in cash, $53.00; in pro-
perty $74.35; temple, in cash, 21.00; poor in
cash 78.50; in property 16,33. On hand 7,25.

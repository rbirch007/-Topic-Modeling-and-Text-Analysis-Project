MBS. ELMIRA P. TUFT.

	DEAR MADAM: Your letter and the one from
Prof. Barfoot, apprising me that specimens of
your vast wealth of minerals had been forward-
ed for the benefit of the Coffin Re-union, were
duly received. The box arrived the following
day, and the specimens appear to be in very
good order. Please accept through me (as far
as I am able to express the sentiments of the
association,) its profound gratitude to you for so
kindly remembering it in so generous a gift.
	I am very truly yours,
				ALLEN COFFIN,
						Secretary T. C. R. A.
	P. S.—I personally very much regret that we
shall not have your presence and that of your
family at the Re-union, but we shall remember
you kindly.

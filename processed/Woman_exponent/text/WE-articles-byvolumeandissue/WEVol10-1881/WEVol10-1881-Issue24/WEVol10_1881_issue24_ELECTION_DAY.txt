ELECTION DAY.

	Let no person forget the election on Monday,
the 23rd of May. This people should show their
strength in the vote cast for the Constitution of
the proposed State. The Lord has in many in-
stances blest in a visible manner the efforts
made by this people to obtain justice, even
though the rulers of the nation refused and re-
jected their intercessions with scorn. It may be
so now. "Our cause is just," and the event is
in the hands of One, who doeth all things
well.

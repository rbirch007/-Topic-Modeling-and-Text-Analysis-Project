ST. GEORGE.

	The Quarterly Conference of the Relief
Societies of the St. George Stake of Zion, held
at St. George, Friday March 17th, 1882. Coun-
selor Anna L. Ivins presiding.
	After the usual opening exercises, the min-
utes of our last Conference were read and ac-
cepted. The different wards were represented
by their respective presidents. The financial
condition of the societies throughout the Stake
were reported in a better condition than usual.
President McAllister gave some very good
general instructions. The meeting was also
addressed by Sisters Anna L. Ivins and Lucy
B. Young; and a good spirit prevailed through-
out the whole Conference.
			ELIZABETH P. BENTLY,
				Corresponding Secretary.

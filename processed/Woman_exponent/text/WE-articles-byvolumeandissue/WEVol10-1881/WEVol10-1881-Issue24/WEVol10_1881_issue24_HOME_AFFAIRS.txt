HOME AFFAIRS.

	The Eighth Ward Relief Society met according
to appointment, for reorganization, May 7th,
1882, at the meeting house. Present, Bishop
Sheets, Counselors McMurrin, and Brockbank,
and Sister M. I. Horne. Opened by singing and
prayer minutes read and accepted. Resolu-
tions of respect of our late President and Coun-
selor, were read and adopted. The Bishop then
made some remarks, and nominated the officers.
Sister Esther B. Fletcher, President; Catherine
Palmer, First Counselor; Jane Cowen Second
Counselor; Sarah Hawkins, Secretary; Jenette-
McMurrin Assistant Secretary; Ruth Ensign
Treasurer; Annie Cross, Prosiding Teacher.
Sister Horne gave us good instructions. Meet-
ing adiourned. The Bishop invited the sisters
to his home to dinner, we had a very enjoyable
 time with his family.	E. B. FLETCHER.

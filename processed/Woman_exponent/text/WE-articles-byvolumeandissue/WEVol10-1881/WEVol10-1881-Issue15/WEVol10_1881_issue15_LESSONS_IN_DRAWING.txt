LESSONS IN DRAWING.

	DR. H. C. Norton, will teach perspective
drawing in crayon in twenty lessons. Office at
the residence of Mrs. H. M. Whitney, North
Temple Street, half a block east of the Temple.
We have seen some specimens of the lady's
work and consider them very fine. Terms fif-
teen dollars for twenty lessons.

Relief Society of Beaver Stake, very politely re-
quests us to correct the report of the Relief So-
ciety as published in Nov. 1st issue of the EXPO-
NENT. She states that instead of cash $37.40,
property, $99.20, and wheat 24 bushels, they
have on hand cash, $[-]7.40, property in treasury,

REPORTS.

	Minutes of a meeting held in the 5th Ward
Meeting House Salt Lake City, Feb. 6th,
1882, for the purpose of organizing a Primary
Association. Sister Eliza R. Snow Smith
presiding.
	Singing by the children; prayer by Sis-
ter Horne. Sister Eliza asked the children
if they prayed and asked blessings on their food
told them that in the time of Joseph Smith, the
children had little meetings and bore testimony
and had the good spirit with them. Gave some
good counsel on obedience to those placed over
them, and on politeness to their companions.
She then proceeded to organize the Society, the
following persons were sustained as officers:
Myra Winter, President; Elizabeth Clark,
First Counselor; Sarah Page,Second Counselor;
Esther Davey, Secretary, Emmeline Pettit,
Asst. Sec., Edwin Pettit, Treasurer. Number
enrolled as members sixty-four.
	Sister Horne then addressed the children in
a very interesting manner. Singing by the
children. Prayer by Sister E. R. S. Smith.
						E. DAVEY, Sec'y.

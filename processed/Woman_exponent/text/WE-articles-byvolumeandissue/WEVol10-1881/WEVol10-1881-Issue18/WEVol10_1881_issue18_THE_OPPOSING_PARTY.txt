THE OPPOSING PARTY.
			------
	What meaneth the sound of tumult--this
array of words, trying by the force of their
power to bring trouble upon us? The spirit of
the evil one inspires the wicked, and they are
tools in his hands in this, his last struggle for
power and supremacy. His days are numbered,
his time  short, and with increased force and
venom he raises his hand against the people of
the Lord, the Latter-day Saints. He stirs up
the hearts of his followers to warfare with the
living God. It is not polygamy they seek to
exterminate, but by that as a handle seek to
exterminate the truth and drive it from the
earth, as once before it was driven. Will they
succeed? No, never! they cannot do it, for the
Lord has promsied, and it will endure.
	Howl on, corrupt politicians! Howl on, ye
who ought to be ministers of peace, but who are
ministers of war! The honest in heart will 
stand back aghast at your proceedings, and in
their hearts will condemn your works. Evil
spirits may rage, and Satan may plan, all all
will be confounded, for the Lord has spoken
and His word is true.
	Celestial marraige, that gives us power over
death, that unites in heaven as well as on earth,
that has no dissolution in it, is the present tar-
get. Celestial marraige, that brings the bless-
ings of the Lord, that is a commandment form
heaven, is what they pretend to aim at. Really
it is the kingdom of God, the truth from heaven,
and the people who obey the commandments of
God, against whom their hatred exists. The
same God who heard the prayer of Hezekiah
and smote the army of Senacharib will hear
our prayers and smite those who oppose us.
The same God that delivered Daniel from the
lion's den will deliver us. In Him we trust.
The power of the wicked will be broken, and
righteousness at last prevail.

OBITUARIES.
				-----
	We regret to chronicle the death of our es-
teemed and beloved sister, Eliza A. Thomas,
who departed this life Jan. 14, 1882, after an ill-
ness of one week with scarlet fever.  Deceased
was born in Heber City, Utah, Nov. 9, 1863.
She was baptized when eight years old, re-
baptized October 2, 1881. Was a member of the
Sunday School and Y. L. M. I. A., and well
known for her sterling qualities, and the news
of her death cast a feeling of sadness over the
entire community.
	The funeral services were held in the 4th
Ward, Provo.  Karl G. Maeser and V. L.
Halliday were the speakers on the solemn occa-
sion; members of the Young Men’s Association
acted as pall bearers.  We sincerely condole
with the relatives and friends of our departed
sister.

					ELLEN M. JONES.

   Brother Bracken said he well remembered
"the wrongs of Missouri" and "the fate of Nau-
voo," and spoke by the spirit of inspiration; he
was followed by Brothers R. L. Lloyd and
John Grey. Singing, "How have the nations
grown corrupt." Then the following sisters
bore testimony to the truth of this work: Sis-
ters Jones, Ann Snow, Amanda Burgess and
Sister Jacobson. Singing, "O Saints have you
seen." Sisters Sophia Burgess, Jane Gardner
and Cynthia Gardner each spoke a short time.
   We had without exception one of the most
enjoyable times we have ever had the pleasure
of witnessing. The Spirit of God was richly
enjoyed by all present. Singing, "O ye moun-
tains High." Benediction by R. L. Lloyd.

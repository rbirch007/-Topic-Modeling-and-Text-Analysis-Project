NOTES AND NEWS
                               -----
The Woman's Silk Culture Association has
held its first exhibition in Philadelphia.

 MRS. Ednah D. Cheney has written a chap-
ter on "The Women of Boston," in Mr. Win-
sor's Memorial History of Boston.

	Mrs. Mary E. Haggart, of Indianapolis, on 
Tuesday, the 25th of January, addressed the 
Kentucky Legislature on the rights of women.
The same day a bill was introduced to give
property rights, and rights to children and to
women in a degree never known in Kentucky
before.

PRIMARY ASSOCIATIONS OF UTAH
			STAKE.
	The Primary Associations of Utah Stake are
progressing nicely, their numbers being in-
creased continually. Great interest seems to
be taken by the parents in regard to the
meetings of their children and this gives en-
couragement to those who have the responsi-
bility of carrying on this organization.
	During the summer just passed, President
Annie K. Smoot and others visited the various
associations throughout the county holding
meetings and instructing the officers. The
spirit of God prevailed in each meeting and
at some the gifts of the Gospel were made
manifest. In instances where all public in-
terest, with reference to meetings of the people.
had died out the Primary children were still
holding meetings, anxious to progress.
	The Stake Conference was held in Ameri-
can Fork. There an excellent programme was
rendered and instructions given by Coun.
Lillie Freeze and President Annie K. Smoot.
			ANNIE K. SMOOT, Stake Pres.
			MABEL PRATT, Stake Sec’y.

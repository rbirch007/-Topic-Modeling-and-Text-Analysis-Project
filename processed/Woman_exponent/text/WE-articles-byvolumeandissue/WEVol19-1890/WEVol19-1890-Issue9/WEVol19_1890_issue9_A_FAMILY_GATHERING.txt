A FAMILY GATHERING.
	
	Our worthy postmaster Brother John Pym was
honored Sunday 14th with a family gathering at
his residence in St. George, in honor of his 75th
birthday.
	Brother Pym was one of the first settlers of St.
George; his kindness of heart is well known to
every one in the community, and willingness to
oblige any one in need; his uprightness and integ-
rity cannot be excelled, he is especially kind to the
aged and widows.
	Sister Donnell his wife’s mother, who is well
and favorably known to the Elders who have been
in Scotland, for her kindness and generosity, and
who we do not doubt would likely be pleased to
hear that she is remarkably well; and her mind
clear and bright in regard to the Gospel.
	She was also surprised on her 90th birthday
by her children and grandchildren with a very
nice supper and many tokens of love and esteem.
	Brother Pym has been a faithful worker in the
St. George Temple from its commencement. May
his life be long spared is the wish of his friends
who are legion.
								A. C. W.
Sept. 19, 1890.

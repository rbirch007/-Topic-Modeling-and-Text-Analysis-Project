RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	The second annual conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Salt Lake Assembly
Hall, in this city, on Tuesday, April 7, 1891.
Meetings will commence at 10 o'clock a.m. and
2 o'clock p.m. respectively.
	The officers and members of the Relief Society
and all those interested in this benevolent work
are cordially invited to attend the Conference. It
is expected that able representatives from each
State organization will be present and make brief
reports of the methods of work and results
achieved.
		ZINA D. H. YOUNG,
			President.
		JANE S. RICHARDS,
		BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
			Counselors.
	E. B. WELLS, Cor. Sec.

NOTES AND NEWS.

	The population of Alaska, as per corrected
returns, is 30,000.
	The cap and gown are paraphernalia
usually foreign to a Yale student, but this
year the graduating class will don that garb.
	Mrs. Jennie C. Croly, "Jennie June," has
been made honorary president for life of
Sorosis.
	Mrs. Nellie Blessing Eyster, president of the
Woman's Press Association of the Pacific
Coast, is a grand-niece of Barbara Frietchie.

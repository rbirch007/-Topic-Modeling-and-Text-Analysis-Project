		RELIEF SOCIETY ORGANIZATION.
	Meeting held for the purpose of organizing
a Relief Society in the Cleveland Ward,
Emery Co., March 12, 1891. Bishops Charles
Pulsipher of Huntington, and L. P. Overson
of Cleveland; Annie Larsen,  Stake President
of Relief Society and Counselor Ann Pulsi-
pher, were present. Meeting opened by sing-
ing "Praise ye the Lord, 'tis good to raise."
Prayer by H. H. Ovaitt. Singing "I know that
my Redeemer lives."
	The following officers were then appointed
and sustained by unanimous vote: Annie M.
Alger, President; Ann Eden and Louisa
Overson, Counselors; Harriett Eden, Secre-
tary; Agnes Ovaitt, Ass't. Sec.; Margaretta
Johnsen, Treasurer.
	President Annie Larsen expressed her
pleasure at meeting with the sisters of the
ward. Spoke at length on unity and prayer.
Gave general instructions to the officers in
their different positions.
	Coun. Ann Pulsipher was pleased to witness
an organization here, and said it was the duty
of all to have the Spirit of the Lord with
them.
	Annie M Alger, Ann Eden, Bishop Pulsi-
pher, H. H. Ovaitt, Thomas Farish and
Bishop Overson, all expressed themselves


pleased with the meeting, and gave words of
counsel and encouragement.
	Choir sang "Doxology." Benediction by C.
Pulsipher.

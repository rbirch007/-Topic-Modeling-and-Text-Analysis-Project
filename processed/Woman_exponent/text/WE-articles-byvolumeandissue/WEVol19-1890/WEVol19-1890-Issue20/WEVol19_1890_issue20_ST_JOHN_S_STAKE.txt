			ST. JOHN'S STAKE.
	Conference of the Relief Society of the St.
John's Stake of Zion, convened in the upper
room of the Tithing Office, at St. John's Mar.
7th, 1891, Pres. E. L. S. Udall presiding.
Congregation sang, "Now let us rejoice in the
day of salvation." Prayer by Counselor
Frances White. Congregation sang, “How
firm a foundation ye saints of the Lord.”
After reading reports and presenting officers,
the remaining time was occupied by the fol-
lowing speakers: Pres. E. L. S. Udall, Mary
E. B. Farr, Mary Farr, Counselor Margaret
E. Patterson, E. N. Freeman and Wm. 
Gibbons, of the Stake Presidency, and Coun-
selor Frances White.
	The subjects spoken of were prayer, the
vision of Nephi, bearing of testimony, influ-
ence of mothers over their children, condition
of woman among the nations of the earth
showing the difference between us and them,
regarding our advantage over them. Pres.
E. S. Udall after making some timely remarks
regarding the duties of R. S. presidents, read
an article from the Gazette, on the "Woman
Suffrage Bill” now before the Territorial Leg-
islature. The meeting was a very interesting
and instructive one, the remarks being ac-
companied by that Holy Spirit which brings
peace and joy to the soul.

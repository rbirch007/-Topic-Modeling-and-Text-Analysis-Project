IN MEMORIAM.

	Mrs. Sarah M. Blair who departed this life
July 8th 1890, was the daughter of E.W. and
Willmirth East and was born Dec. 31st, 1840 in
Washington Co. Texas. She was baptized by
Seth M. Blair, August 1854 in Milam Co. Texas,
and came to Utah with her parents in 1855, was
married to Seth M. Blair in Sept. 1856. She was
the mother of five children two sons and three
daughters two of whom survive her. She was a
woman of moral character and sterling worth.
She lived and died a faithful Latter-day Saint in
the hope of a glorious resurrection.

		Angel Sarah do not leave us;
			Though your body’s from us borne,
		Yet in spirit be thou with us
			Whispering loved ones do not mourn.

		Dearest mother do come near us.
			Bless us with that heavenly love,
		Tell us of the joys you've tasted
			In your blissful home above.
						Her	Mother,

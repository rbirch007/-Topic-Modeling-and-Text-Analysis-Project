			PAROWAN P. A.
	Minutes of Primary Conference held at
Parowan Aug. 9, 1890. Singing from Primary
Hymn books “Gladly meeting,” opening prayer
David Mathewson, singing “I think when I
read that sweet story of old,” minutes of last
conference read and accepted, followed by a
lengthy programme from the children, consist-
ing of songs, dialogues, readings, essays and
recitations, many of the parts deserving of
great praise.
	Sister Ellen Lunt told the people assembled
she was pleased to meet them, she had been
detained therefore did not hear all the parts
rendered, enjoyed the little she had heard,
prayed God’s blessing upon all.
	Sister Holyoak was gratified to have the
visiting sisters, Mrs. E. B. Wells and Mrs. J.
S. Richards, with us, asked the parents to en-
courage their boys to come to the meetings, as
they seemed to be more backward than the
girls.
	Sister Morris,Pres. P. A. Parowan Stake re-
marked we had a lengthy meeting, hoped what
had been done would be beneficial, could readily
perceive an improvement in the children;

[Column 2]
﻿thanked each one who had assisted in making
up the programme of exercises.
	Closed by singing “We thank Thee O God
for a prophet.” Benediction by Pearl Wat-
son.
	E. A. CRANE WATSON, Stake Sec. P. A.

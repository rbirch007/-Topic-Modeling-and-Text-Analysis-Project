		AGRICULTURAL COLLEGE OF UTAH.

	The Agricultural College of Utah will be open
for the reception of Students on Tuesday, Sept.
2d. On Sept. 4th, at 10 a. m., it will be dedicated
to the Higher Education. Industrial Education
will be a prominent feature of its work. The
College has organized five courses of instruction
namely: Science, Agriculture, Domestic Arts,
Mechanical Engineering and Civil Engineering.
	The speakers announced below will represent
the several courses of Study pursued in the Col-
lege.
	Piano Solo, Mrs. R. W. Sloan; Invocation,
Apostle Moses Thatcher; Song, Mr. W. S.
Lamoreaux; Address, Hon. Arthur L. Thomas,
Governor of Utah; “Education in its Relation to


Agriculture," Bishop William B. Preston; Song,
Mrs. R. W. Sloan; Address, "The Higher Edu-
cation of Women," Mrs. S. W. Eddy; "Educa-
tion its Relation to the Mechanical Industries,”
George G. Bywater; Song, Mr. R. C. Easton;
Brief Addresses by the trustees of the College
and by Citizens; Duet, Mr. R. C. Easton and Mr.
W. S. Lamoreaux; Benediction, Rev. E. W.
Greene.

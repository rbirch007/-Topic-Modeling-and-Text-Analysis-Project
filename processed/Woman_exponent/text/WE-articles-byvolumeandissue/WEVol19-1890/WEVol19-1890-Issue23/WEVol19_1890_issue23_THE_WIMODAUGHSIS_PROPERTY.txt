THE WIMODAUGHSIS PROPERTY.
We have made a deposit on the house and
lot numbered 1319 L. St. N. W. for Wimo-
daughsis and shall take possessson on the first
of November. The lot has been considered by
the board for some time and is very desirable
for our immediate purposes, being 29 feet front
by 164 feet deep to a 30 foot alley. The cost is
$13,000 and it is estimated that our first issue
of stock which is 25,000 would put on a hall
on the war of the present lot and pay for one
purchase. This would enable us to secure
rentals and good quarters for present purposes.
Then when we can do so we can put up a
handsome front which will be a credit to the
women of the country. An urgent appeal is
made to every one to send in their stock sub-
scriptions that the payments on the lot may be


made and improvements begun. The stock
is five dollars a share and is non-assessable and
full paid.
				E. M. GILLETT, Treasurer.

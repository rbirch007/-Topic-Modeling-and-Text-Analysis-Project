INMEMORIAM.

Sister Christiana Christenson of Redmond, Sevier Co.


Utah, departed this life March 18th, 1891 and inasmuch
as she was a teacher, and a faithful co-ladorer in the Relief
Society we as sisters feel that, we owe to the memory of
our departed sister, that we should give expression to the
feelings of our hearts concerning some of the good works
of her life. Sister Christenson was forty-eight years of
age; she leaves a husband and seven children, five of them
to whom she was a step-mother. She was a kind, patient
wife and mother, and a true Latter day Saint, and will
be greatly missed in the family circle, as well as in the
Relief Society. She nobly filled her mission, and no
doubt has gone to that heavenly region, where there is no
sorrow and where friends never part.
	For her husband and her children we offer an earnest
prayer and extend heartfelt sympathy,and we say to them
be comforted, and prove worthy to join her in the kingdom
of God's glory.
		MATILDA PETERSON Pres. of R. S. Redmond.

	THURBER, UTAH.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Our regular annual meeting was held at
the meeting House Jan. 1, 1891. Pres. Sarah
G. Meeks presiding, after the usual exercises
including reading of minutes, financial report
of the past year and so on was approved,
Bishop Wm. Meeks spoke, encouraging the
sisters in their labors thought they had done
well under the circumstances, all felt well,


determined to do better in the future than in
the past. Meeting adjourned for two weeks,
closed by singing, "Lord dismiss us with thy
blessing," benediction by Bishop Wm. Meeks.
			SARAH G. MEEKS, Pres.

FEBRUARY 22-25, 1891.

	Before September 1st, 1891, it is hoped that
this volume of about three hundred pages (con-
taining the report of the eight public, and three
business sessions of the Council, also the por-
traits of the retiring and the in-coming Board of
Officers), will be ready for sale.
	It is desirable that the Officers of the Council
know as soon as possible how large an edition of
this permanent report it will be necessary to
issue.
	Subscriptions should be forwarded at once to
the Corresponding secretary, price $1.00.
		RACHEL FOSTER AVERY,
			748 N. 19th Street, Philadelphia.

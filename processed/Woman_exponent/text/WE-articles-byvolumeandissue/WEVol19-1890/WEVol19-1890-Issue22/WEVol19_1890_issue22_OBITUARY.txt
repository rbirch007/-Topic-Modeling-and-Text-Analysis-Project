OBITUARY.

	There's another Bud removed,
		Ere it felt the blight of sin
	Through the gates the Angels made,
		Our baby has passed in.

	Died at Oakley, Cassia Co. Idaho March 28, 1891, Effie
Louisa, daughter of L. J. Robinson and Sarah E. Richards
Aged 11 months 21 days.

	"Our's the loss and, not our Darling's
		Our's the pain and her's the joy
	Our's the mourning and the weeping
		Her's the bliss without alloy.

	Her's the little feet that patter
		Making music as they go,
	Her's the little hand that beckons
		Ever from the radiant shore.
	Angel of the Lord to lead us
		Where we'll never lose her more."

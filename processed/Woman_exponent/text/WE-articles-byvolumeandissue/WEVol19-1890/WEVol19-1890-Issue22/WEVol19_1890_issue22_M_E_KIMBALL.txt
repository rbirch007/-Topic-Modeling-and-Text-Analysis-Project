Chief Juctice Green, sitting as District
Judge at Guthrie, Oklahoma, has rendered a
decision that women are eligible to public
office, Miss Cora E. Diehl was elected
register of deeds, but the former register re-
fused to surrender the office, on the gronnd
that a woman was not eligible. The judge
held that Miss Diehl was entitled to the office
and, further that a woman may, in the absence
of a statute, hold any office not incompatible
to her sex.

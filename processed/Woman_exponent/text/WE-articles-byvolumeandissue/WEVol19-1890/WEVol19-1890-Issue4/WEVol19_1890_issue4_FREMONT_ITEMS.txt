FREMONT ITEMS.
				June 17th, 1890.
DEAR SISTER WELLS:
	Thinking that some of our friends who read
the EXPONENT, would like to hear from their
sisters up here in the tops of the mountains, I
thought I would write a few words in their be-
half.  The Saints here, as a general thing, are
striving to live their religion.  Our Sunday
schools and meetings are well attended, and
also an increase in our Relief Society meetings
of at least one third.  We have had a great
deal of sickness here during the latter part of
winter, and through the spring, but we have
great reason to thank the Lord that He has
answered our prayers in  behalf of the sick, and
for the manifestation of His goodness to us in
many ways  The EXPONENT is a welcome
companion in my house, as it gives us many
valuable instructions, and also gives us an op-
portunity to hear from our sisters in different
parts of the world.	Yours truly,
								S. J. M.

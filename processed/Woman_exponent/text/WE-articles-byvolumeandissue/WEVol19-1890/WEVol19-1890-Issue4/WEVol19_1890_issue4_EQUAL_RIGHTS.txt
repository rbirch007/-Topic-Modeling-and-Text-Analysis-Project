EQUAL RIGHTS.
TUNE, HAIL COLUMBIA.
	Rise Columbia's daughters rise;
	Heaven has surely heard your cries,
	Yet, to the world we must appeal;
	Arise, ye Mothers of the race?—
	Enjoy your heaven appointed place,
	Demand the rights the world accords—
	Freely to “Creation’s Lords”
	Now let woman's watchward—be—
	“Equal Rights, and Liberty."

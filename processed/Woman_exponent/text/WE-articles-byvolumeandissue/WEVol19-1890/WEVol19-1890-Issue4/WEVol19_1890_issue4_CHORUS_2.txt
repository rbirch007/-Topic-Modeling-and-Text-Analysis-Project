CHORUS:
	“Equal Rights," for small and great;
	"Equal Rights,” whate’er our state—
	No more, no less than this we claim;
	Let others think 'tis woman's fate—
	Always submissively to wait,
	For equity we’ll still contend—
	And work to gain the wish'd for end;
	Take courage, friends, and don't forget—
	That “Equal Rights" await us yet.

						E. A. CRANE WATON.


Minutes of Relief Society Conference, held
at Mesa, June 19th, 1890. Meeting opened
by the usual exercises. Pres. Sarah M. Pom-
eroy presiding. Pres. C. I. Robson, with 
quite a number of the brethren were present,
altogether a very good attendance.
	The reports brought in were of an encour-
aging nature. Pres. M. A. Hakes of Mesa,
Lizzie MacDonald, Lehi, Ann Klienman,
Alma, Susan Savage, Papago, and Esther Open-
shaw of Nephi wards, all felt encouraged and
made some excellent remarks.
	Pres. C. I. Robson, with a number of the
brethren, gave some good and timely advice
and instructions in their addresses to the peo-
ple. A general good spirit prevailed.
	Pres. Sarah M. Pomeroy and Sister Emma
Hill made a few closing remarks.
	Benediction by C. H. Allen. Adjourned 
for three months. 		F. E. Robson, Secy,

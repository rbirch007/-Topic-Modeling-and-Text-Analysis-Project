	TUNE, STAR-SPANGLED BANNER.

Where the mountains eternal, lift up to the sun
Their summits snow-crowned and unchanging forever,
Where the streams leaping forth to the valleys below
Bring harvest and joy from the Father and Giver;
There’s a light shining forth there’s a voice we can hear,
Through the dark o’er the earth, oh how sweet and how
	clear;
'Tis the spirit of God and where’er they may roam,
He is gathering the pure to our own mountain home.

Not for riches of earth would we barter away
The title of Saint and the joy of our mission.
Though the world may oppose, all our trust is in Him
Who hath lighted our souls by each glorious vision.
Let the truths we have heard in the Temples' still peace
Fly with wings of a bird to each spirit’s release;
'Tis the voice of our God and where'er they may roam
He is calling the pure to our own mountain home.

MILL CREEK PRIMARIES.

	The 10th, annual meeting of the Primaries of
Mill Creek met in the Mill Creek Ward house
June 3rd, 1890 at ten o'clock. The weather was
very unpleasant but there were assembled a large
number of children, and also parents and friends,
who greatly enjoyed the exercises. There were
present on the stand Bishop J. C. Hamilton of
Mill Creek, Pres. Ellen C. Clawson, Counselors
Camilla Cobb and Lydia Ann Wells, of Salt Lake
Stake, Sisters E. Davis and M. Cahoon of Cot-
tonwood, and Pres. Mary McAllister, Ann Lemon,
M. A. Birch and Frances Hanson of Mill Creek.
	The forenoon session was presided over by
Pres. Hanson; the programme consisted of songs,
solos, recitations, etc. After which, good instruc-
tions were given by Pres. Clawson, Sisters Wells
and Cobb. After singing all retired to the vestry
where tables were spread with the bounties of the
earth. The children enjoyed an intermission of
two hours. Then meeting commenced, Pres.
McAllister presiding. The programme was well
rendered, and all appreciated the labors of the
presidents in their endeavors to make a pleasant
and instructive day for parents and children.
	Good and encouraging remarks were made by
Pres. Clawson, Sisters Cobb and Wells and
Bishop Hamilton. The day was well spent and
will long be remembered by the little ones.

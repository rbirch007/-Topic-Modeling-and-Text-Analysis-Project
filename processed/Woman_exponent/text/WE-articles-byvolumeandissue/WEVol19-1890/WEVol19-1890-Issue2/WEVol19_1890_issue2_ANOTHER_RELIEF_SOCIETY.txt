ANOTHER RELIEF SOCIETY.

	On May 1 1890 at Lawrence, Emery Co.
Utah a meeting was held for the purpose of
organizing a Relief Society under the direc-
tion of President Annie Larsen and counselors.
The following names of officers were presented
to the congregation and accepted.
	Catharine E. Moore, President, Lydia
Mc Niven First Counselor, Amy Roper Second
Counselor, Mary E. Bunnel Secretary, Lucinda
Hill, Assistant Sec. Emma Tanner, Treasurer.

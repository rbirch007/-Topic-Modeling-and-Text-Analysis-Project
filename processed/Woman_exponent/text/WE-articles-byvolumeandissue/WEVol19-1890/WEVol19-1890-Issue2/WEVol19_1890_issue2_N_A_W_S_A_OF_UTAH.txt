N. A. W. S. A. OF UTAH
	A Meeting of National American W. S. A.
of Utah was held May 18, 1890 in the 14th
Ward Assembly rooms, with Mrs. S. M.
Kimball in the chair.
	Opening prayer by Mrs. M. Y. Dougall.
	Minutes of the previous meeting were read 
and approved.
	Pres Kimball next introduced Hon. F. S.
Richards to the assembly who delivered a 
lecture upon the Municipal government of 
Salt Lake City.
	Miss Hindman's petition to Congress was
read by the secretary, after which Mrs. E. S.
Richards spoke of the lady as being personally
acquainted with her.
	Mrs. Nellie Little made a few remarks, a vote
of thanks was tendered Hon. F. S. Richards.
Meeting adjourned for two weeks. 
	RACHEL EDWARDS.
	Sec'y of W. S. A. of Utah.

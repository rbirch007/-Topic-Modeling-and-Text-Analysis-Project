SUPLICATION.

Lord thon who see’st all my heart,
	And know’st my every thought:
Help me to do Thy holy will
	And serve Thee as I ought. 

And when my path seems dark and wild,
	And hope burns low within;
Look down upon Thine erring child,
	And keep my heart from sin.

Help me to bear my daily cross'
	Though it be hard to bear:
Oh! may I feel through every loss
	I've still a Father's care.

And when at length my day’s are passed,
And all the world grows dim
Oh! may I anchor safe at last
	And be at rest with him.

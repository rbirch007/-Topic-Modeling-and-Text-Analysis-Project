					M. A. Y. GREENHALGH,

Meadow, July 11, 1890.

	Died at Payson, April 3rd, 1890, Annie Elizabeth, be
loved wife of John Henry Box; deceased was born July
11th 1813 in Switzerland; embraced the Gospel and emi-
grated in 1876. Lived and died in full faith of the Gospel
and in the hope of a glorious resurrection.
	Resolutions of Respect adopted by the Relief Society of
the 4th District—Payson.
	To the memory of Sister Annie Elizabeth Box;—
	Whereas; it has pleased our Heavenly Father by the
hand of death to take from us our beloved Sister we bow in


humble submission to the divine will of Almighty God,we
realize in her death we have lost a good woman.
	Resolved; that these resolutions be entered in the Relief
Society record, also a copy be presented to the bereaved
family and printed in the WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
		LAMECIA A. SCHARRER.
		ANNA JONES,

OBITUARIES
	Died at Meadow, Millard Co. Utah,July 8,1890, Sophia
wife of E. A. Beckstrand. She was born at Oland,Sweden,
Oct. 9, 1833, embraced the Gospel and was baptized into
the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in 1855.
Emigrated to Utah 1861, and was married the same year
to Brother E. A. Beckstrand, in Salt Lake City.
	She was a good wife, affectionate mother and a sincere
friend, and sister. She died as she had lived, a faithful
Latter-day Saint. She leaves a husband and four children to
lament her loss, her eldest son is on a mission to Sweden.

	Death softly came, and bore away
		A sister true, and tried.
	In the calm twilight of her days,
		In faith and hope she died.

	She suffered long, then calmly bent
		To meet death’s fatal blow;
	An arrow sure, in mercy sent,
		To sufferers here below.

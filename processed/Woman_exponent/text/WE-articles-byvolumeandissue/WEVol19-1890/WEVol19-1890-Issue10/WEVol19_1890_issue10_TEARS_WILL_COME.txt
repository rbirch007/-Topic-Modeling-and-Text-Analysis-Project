TEARS WILL COME.
When the heart feels its stay is gone
	And confidence is rudely torn,
Where we had trusted much and long,
	Tears will come !

When no tender word is spoken,
	Lips keep the truth no more unbroken,
Give no smile of peace the token,
	Tears will come:

When love's flower pales and withers,
	'Til the soul with anguish quivers,
None to comfort or deliver,
	Tears will come.

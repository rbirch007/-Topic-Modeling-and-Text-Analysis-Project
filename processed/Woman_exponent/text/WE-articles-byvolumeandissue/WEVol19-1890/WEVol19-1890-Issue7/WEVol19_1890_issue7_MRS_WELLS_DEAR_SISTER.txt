	MRS. WELLS: DEAR SISTER:
	I am much interested in the story of
Hephzibah, and I think I recognize some of
the characters.  Had I been as courageous
as she was,I might now be with the Church;
don’t know when I will get there again.  My
health is tolerably good, but my eyes and
ears are badly affected; fear sometimes I will
get entirely deaf and blind.  I hope and pray
that I may not.
	*		*		*		*		*
	Accept my best wishes and remember me in
your prayers; give my respects to Elvira.have
been looking for a letter from her; have re-
cieved none of late.
			Yours Affectionately
								L. B. T.
	Osceola, Ill. Aug. 12, 1890.

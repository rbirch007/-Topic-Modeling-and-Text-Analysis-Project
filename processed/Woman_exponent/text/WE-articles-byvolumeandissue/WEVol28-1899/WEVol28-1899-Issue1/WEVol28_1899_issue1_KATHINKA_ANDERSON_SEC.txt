KATHINKA ANDERSON Sec.


MRS. MARY A. LIVERMORE has just de-
stroyed the manuscript of a hundred and
twenty original poems written by her.
She is going over her papers and ruthlessly
burning up all her old compositions, essays,
and even the manuscript of her lectures. A
whole regiment of scrapbooks, full of her
written comments on the books that she
has read during the last half century, have
lately gone to feed her furnace fire, and
still the work of destruction continues.

EDITORIAL NOTES.

OUR recent visit to the New England
hills, old scenes, associations, relatives,
and friends, will appear in the June num-
ber of the paper.

	The Primary Associations are to have a
Jubilee early in the month of June, which
is expected to. be quite a unique affair.
General-President Louie Felt and mem-
bers of her Board are making elaborate
preparatione and planning to have it repre-
sentative in every way of this association of
the children of Zion.

	All trains of the Oregon Short Line out
of Salt Lake City, start directly from this
city, and are consequently always on time.
You can depend upon them to the minute.
	This feature is the most satisfying one
in railway travel, and, in part, accounts
for the well deserved popularity of the
Short Line’s local passenger service.

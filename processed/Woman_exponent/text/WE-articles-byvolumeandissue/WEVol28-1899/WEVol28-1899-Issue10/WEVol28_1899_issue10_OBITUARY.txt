OBITUARY.
	CORNELIA PRATT DRIGGS.
	Died at her home, Forest Dale, October 7, 1899,
Cornelia, wife of Bishop A. G. Driggs, and
daughter of the late Apostle Parley P, and Mary
Wood Pratt. The deceased was born in Salt Lake
City. Sep 5, 1848, and lived even up to woman-
hood in the old homestead on South Temple St.
She married A. G. Driggs, Oct. 5, 1867, and took
up her abode at Pleasant Grove, Utah Co., where
they lived a few years. Came to Sugar House in
1870.

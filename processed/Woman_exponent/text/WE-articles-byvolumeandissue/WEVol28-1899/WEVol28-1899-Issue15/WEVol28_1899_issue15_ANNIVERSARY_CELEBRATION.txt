	ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION.

    On March 17th last the Relief Society of
Forest Dale Ward convened for the purpose
of celebrating the first organization of the
Relief Society, Louisa Stevenson presiding.
    After singing and prayer, the minutes of
the first organization of the Relief Society
were read, after which the following pro-
gram was rendered:
    Sketch of the life of Sister Bathsheba W.
Smith, by Stake Counselor Mary P. Young.
Duet, by Miilie Kimball and Maggie
Summerhayes. Select reading, by Sister
Belle Hansen. Recitation, “The Changed
Cross,” by Sister Nellie Fowler. Song, by
Sisters Libbie Pratt and Maggie Summer-
hayes. Guitar selection, by Hazel and
Geneva Love. Song, by Hannah Gates
and Rhoda Laird.
    There was a good spirit manifest and all
rejoiced exceedingly in the work of the
Lord.

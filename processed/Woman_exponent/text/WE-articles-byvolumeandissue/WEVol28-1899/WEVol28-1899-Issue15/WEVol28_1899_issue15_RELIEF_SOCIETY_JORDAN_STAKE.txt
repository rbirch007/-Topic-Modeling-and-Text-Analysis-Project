RELIEF SOCIETY JORDAN STAKE. 
	At a conference held at East Jordan,
January 21, 1900, for the purpose of or-
ganizing the Jordan Stake of Zion, it was
proposed that the following officers of the
Relief Society of the Jordan Stake be
sustained: Marinda Bateman, president;
Hedvig Huldah Larson, first counselor;
Agnes Cutler, second counselor. 
	The appointments were sustained unani-
mously, and Marinda Bateman was set
apart January 27, 1900, by Francis M.
Lyman; Hedvig H. Larson, same date, by
Anton H. Lund; Agnes Cutler, by Francis
M. Lyman; Elizabeth Gardner, chosen as
secretary on February 10, 1900, set apart
on the same date by Francis M. Lyman;
Emily White, chosen as corresponding
secretary on February 10, set apart March
10, by Orin P. Miller; Mary Aggie Cushing 
chosen as treasurer on February 10, set
apart March 16 by Hyrum Goff.

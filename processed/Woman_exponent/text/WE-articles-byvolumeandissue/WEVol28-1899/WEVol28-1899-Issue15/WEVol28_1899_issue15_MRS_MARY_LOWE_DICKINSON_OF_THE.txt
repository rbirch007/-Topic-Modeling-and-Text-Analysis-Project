MRS. MARY LOWE DICKINSON, of the
National Council of Women, finding that
there was danger of a deficit in the treas-
ury, promptly contributed $450, which put

	THE South African war is costing
England about $5,000,000 a week. If the
economical and housekeeping sex had the
Parliamentary vote, it is likely that they
would object to so large a butcher’s bill.

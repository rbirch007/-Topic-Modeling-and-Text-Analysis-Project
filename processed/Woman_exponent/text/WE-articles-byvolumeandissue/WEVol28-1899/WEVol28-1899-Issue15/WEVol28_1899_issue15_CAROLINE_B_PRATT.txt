			CAROLINE B. PRATT.


A FEW months ago it was announced
that both Houses of Parliament in West
Australia had passed a resolution in favor
of full suffrage for women. This was an
earnest that they would pass a bill to that
effect as soon as the necessary red tape
could be untied. Now the news comes
that the bill has finally passed both
houses. So, one by one, the Australian
provinces join the procession.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	To the memory of Sister Julia Anna Ivins
		Pace, who departed this life Jan. 17, 1900.
	Whereas, In the dispensation of Providence,
God has seen fit to call home one of his daughters
who labored long as corresponding secretary for
the Relief Society of the St. George Stake of Zion;
and
	Whereas, she has proved herself an efficien
worker in all her various duties, and was one
whose kindness and generosity of spirit brought
sunshine to many hearts, helping the needy in
time of want, and was in her own life a fitting em-
bodiment of her constant prayer, that she might be
humble and united in all that is good; therefore,
be it
	Resolved, That we, her sisters, united with
her in the presidency of the St. George Stake, do
deeply feel her loss in connection with her earnest
effort and constant work; nevertheless, in this our
loss, we feel to bow to the mandates of an Alwise
Creator who doeth all things well.
	Resolved, that a copy of these resolutions be
sent to her family, also that one be sent to the
WOMAN’S EXPONENT for publication. 
			ANN C. WOODBURY, President.
			EMMA MORRIS, First Counselor.
			JANE F. BLEAK, Second Counselor.

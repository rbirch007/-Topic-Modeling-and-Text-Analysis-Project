MOTHER’S BASKET.
Babies and books. Women and work, 
	There is room in this world for us all.
The sunbeams that dance and the shadows which
		lurk, 
	All answer to nature’s call.

Nature requires both sunshine and storm,
	So we’ll shrink not from heat nor from cold;
But cheerfully, lovingly strive to perform
	All the work that our baskets hold.

OFFCIAL NOTICE.
At a special meeting of the Board of
Directors of the National Woman’s Relief
Society, held March 7, 1899, in room 208
Templeton building, (more than the re-
quired number for the transaction of busi-
ness being present) when the subject of
ways and means was being, considered, a
motion was made by Mrs. Martha A.
Cannon, of Ogden, and seconded by Dr.
Ellis R. Shipp, of Salt Lake City, that all
money raised by local branches of the Relief
Society on the 17th of March, over the ten
cent membership of each member enrolled
in the society, should be retained by the
respective society. Motion was put by
President Zina D. H. Young and carried
unanimously.

(Signed)

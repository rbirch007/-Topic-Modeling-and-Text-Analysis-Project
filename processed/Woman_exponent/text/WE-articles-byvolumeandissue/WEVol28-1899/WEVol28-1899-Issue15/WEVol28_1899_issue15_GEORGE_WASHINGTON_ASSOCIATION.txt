GEORGE WASHINGTON ASSOCIATION.

The George Washington Memorial Asso-
ciation held its annual meeting at Washing.
ton, D. C., December 13, 14 and 15, 1899.
There was a large attendance from the
several organized States and much interest
was felt by those who could not be present,
as the voting of several hundred proxies
showed. The first session was devoted to
reports of officers,and the annual election of
new officers—Mrs. L. D. M. Sweat, of Port-
land, Me., president of the Mt. Vernon

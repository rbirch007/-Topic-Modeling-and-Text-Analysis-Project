LANTERN LECTURE.
	On Tuesday evening, November 28, an
interesting lecture, illustrated by innumer-
able limelight views, was given in the
Town Hall, by Elder Ezra T. Stevenson, of
the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day
Saints.
	The hall was comfortably filled by an
interested audience, not a few Maoris from
the Te Aroha Pah being among
those present. The pictures were well
produced and the lecture listened to atten- 
tively. A great many of those present left
the hall with a much healthier view of
Mormonism than they previously had.—Te
Aroha and Ohinemuri News.

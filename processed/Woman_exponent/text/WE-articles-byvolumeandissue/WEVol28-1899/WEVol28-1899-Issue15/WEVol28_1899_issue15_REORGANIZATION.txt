REORGANIZATION.
	At the ward conference of the Forest
Dale Ward of the Salt Lake Stake of Zion,
held Tuesday, October 22, 1899, the re-
organization of the Relief Society of that
ward was affected, Mrs. Louise Stevenson
being elected and set apart to fill the vacan-
cy caused by the death of President Cornelia
Driggs.
	President Louise Stevenson selected for
her counselors Mrs. Agnes Young and Mrs.
Ella Love. Mrs. Agnes Rumel was elected
secretary, Della Young assistant secretary,
and Mrs. Litha Hendry treasurer.

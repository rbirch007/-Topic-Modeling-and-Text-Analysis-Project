HONOR TO WHOM HONOR IS DUE.
Last Thursday, March 29, Sister Agnes
Cutler was tendered a very pleasant sur-
prise party at her home in East Jordan.
For the past three years Sister Cutler has
been the active president of the ward Re-
lief Society, but with the organization of
the Jordan Stake, she was chosen second
counselor to the Stake president of the Re-
lief Society, Sister Marinda Bateman.
The surprise party was given to show
the respect and appreciation in which Sister
Cutler is held, and to wish her God-speed
in her more exalted calling. The time
was spent enjoyably, singing, reciting and
partaking of the bounties of this life, after
which Sister Cutler was presented with a
handsome present, consisting of a piece of
household furniture. Over seventy Relief
Society workers were present, among the
more prominent being Sisters Marinda
Bateman and Hilda Larsen, of the Stake
presidency, and Sister Emily White, assis-
tant Stake secretary.

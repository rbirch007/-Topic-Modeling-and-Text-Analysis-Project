U. W. P. C.
	The U. W. P. C. held its regular
meeting in the EXPONENT office, on March
21, 1900, the president, Mrs. Schiller, pre-
siding. Opening prayer, Ruth M. Fox.
The roll was called, members responding
with original sentiments, after which the
business before the club was considered.
	The second circular on the Industral
Problem was read, and the president re-
ported from the club council. The Con-
sumers League was then discussed. A
communication was read regarding the edu-
cational work.
	Mrs. Wells announced to the club that
Mrs. Charlotte Perkins Stetson would be
here on the 25th of April. A recommend-
ation was read asking for the names of the
club, after which the following program
was carried out:
	A poem composed by Lucy A. Clark,
entitled “Wedding Bells,” was read by
Ruth M. Fox. Phebe C. Young read a
paper on Manilla, and Aimee C. Schiller
read Current Events.
	It was decided that the club hold a special
meeting during the month, invite the
Reapers’ Club to meet with us and have
Emma J. McVicker talk on education. 
	Prayer, Rebecca Doolan.

OBITUARY.
RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
To the memory of Sister Martha B. Taylor, who
	departed this life December 25, 1899.

	Whereas, in the dispensation of Providence, God
has seen fit to call home one of His daughters,
who labored long among her sisters as a counselor
in the Relief Society of the 21st ward of the Salt
Lake Stake of Zion, and 
	Whereas, she has proved herself an efficient
counselor and an effective member of the organiz-
ation in all its various duties, and was one whose
kindness and generosity of spirit brought sunshine
unto many hearts, helped the needy in time of
want, and was in her own life a fitting embodi-
ment of her constant prayer, that she might be
humble and united in all that is good, therefore, be
it
	RESOLVED, That we, her sisters, united with her
in the Relief Society work, do deeply feel her loss
in connection with her earnest effort and constant
work; nevertheless, in this our loss we feel to bow
to the mandates of an Alwise Creator, who doeth
all things well.
	RESOLVED, That a copy of these resolutions be
entered on the minutes of the 21st ward Relief
Society, and that a copy be sent to her family and
one to the WOMAN’S EXPONENT.
		ELIZA BARTON, President.
		FRANCES S. CASTLETON, Counselor.
		M. A. TUDDENHAM, Secretary.

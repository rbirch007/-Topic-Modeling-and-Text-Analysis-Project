				JANE S. COLEMAN.


AN aged lady getting into a cab in Dub-
lin, said to the driver, “Help me in, my
good man, for I am very old!” “Begor,
ma’am,” said he, “no matter what age you
are you don’t look it.”
	A VILLAGE schoolmaster entering school
one morning, read on the blackboard the
words: “Our teacher is a donkey!”
The pupils expected nothing less than, a
cyclone or an earthquake; but the philo-
sophic pedagogue contented himself with
adding the word “driver,” and opened the
school as usual.

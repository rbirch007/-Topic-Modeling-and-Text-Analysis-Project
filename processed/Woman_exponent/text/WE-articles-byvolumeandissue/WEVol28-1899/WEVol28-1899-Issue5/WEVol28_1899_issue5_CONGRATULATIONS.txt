CONGRATULATIONS.
	The EXPONENT cannot refrain from ex-
pressing its heartfelt congratulations to
Mr. and Mrs. John Q. Cannon upon the
arrival in the family of beautiful twin boys.
We are pleased to say that mother and
babes are doing nicely; the father is as
proud as can be, and justifiably so. The
little “Cannons” are as bright and up-to-
date additions to the general’s family equip-
ment as any one could desire, and from
present indications will grow up to be
valuable and honored members of Utah’s
hero-band, for many years of future useful-
ness and progress. May they live to fulfill
the most ardent, loving wishes of their
families and friends.

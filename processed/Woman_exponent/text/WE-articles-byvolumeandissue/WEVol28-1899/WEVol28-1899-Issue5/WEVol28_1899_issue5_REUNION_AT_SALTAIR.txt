REUNION AT SALTAIR.
	A pleasant remembrance in the minds of
the U. W. P. C. is the successful reunion
held at Saltair, Thursday, July 12, 1899.
	The members and friends boarded the
4:15 p. m. train, arriving at the beach in
ample time for a dip in the lake. Those
not caring to venture in the water, set the
banquet table, which fairly groaned under
its weight of tempting viands.
	The absence of our esteemed Honorary
President, Mrs. E. B. Wells, who was in
England, was keenly felt.
	At 8:15 p. m. the club returned home,
extending a vote of thanks to the committee
in charge and many good wishes for the
success of the U. W. P. C.

GENERAL RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.
	The General Conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Salt Lake
Assembly Hall in this city, Thursday,
October 5, 1899, meetings commencing at
10 a. m., 2 p. m. and 7 p. m. It is desir-
able that there should be a representative
from each Stake of Zion to report, and all
officers and members, also Bishops and
brethren, are most cordially invited to
attend the meetings of the conference
of this great organization.
ZINA D. H. YOUNG,
President,
JANE S. RICHARDS,
BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
SARAH J. CANNON,
Counselors.

THE CAGE. 
When song-bird thoughts within his heart
Make melody sublime,   
The Poet snares them by his art
Into a cage of rhyme. 
And there the captive fancies beat
Their wings against the bars, 
The music, soft and low and sweet, 
Ascending to the stars. 
Yet evermore they long to be 
Back where the surges roll,
Untamed, unfettered, wild and free.
Within the Poet's soul.

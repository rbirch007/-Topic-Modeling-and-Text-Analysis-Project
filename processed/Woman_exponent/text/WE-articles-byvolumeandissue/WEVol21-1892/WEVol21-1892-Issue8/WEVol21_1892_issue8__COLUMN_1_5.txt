COLUMBUS DAY IN CHICAGO.
---
	THE commencement of the dedicatory
ceremonies of the Columbian Exposition be-
gan in Chicago on the 19th of October with
the formal inaugural reception to the guests
of honor.
	It would be utterly futile to attempt a
description of the magnificence of the prepa-
rations and paraphernalia though only cul-
ling from dispatches, so we shall only say,
there was a great procession of official digni-
taries, and distinguished guests foreign and
native born, and the decorations included
carloads of flowers and smilax, and besides
the stars and stripes,the colors of Spain and
Italy and the flags and banners of all
nations.

grand orchestra a grand march was led by

throng merged, and floating into a quad-

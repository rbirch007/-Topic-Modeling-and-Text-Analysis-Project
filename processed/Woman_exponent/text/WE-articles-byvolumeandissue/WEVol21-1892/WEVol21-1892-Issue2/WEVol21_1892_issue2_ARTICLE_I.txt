ARTICLE I.

	Par. I. A regular meeting of the club
shall be held at the EXPONENT parlor on the
last day of each month; unless the date or
place of meeting be changed by a two-thirds
vote of the members present at any regular
meeting.
	Par. 2. The October meeting of each
year shall be the annual meeting. At this
meeting officers shall be elected for the en-
suing year.

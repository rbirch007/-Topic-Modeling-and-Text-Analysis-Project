ARTICLE III.

	Par. I. Annual dues shall be one dollar
payable in October and become delinquent
sixty days after the annual meeting.
	Par. 2. No member whose dues are not
paid, shall be entitled to vote.
	Par. 3. Every appointee of the regular
programme who fails to fulfil the part as-
signed her shall be fined twenty-five cents.
	Par. 4. Every member present or ab-
sent except honorary, shall be taxed ten
cents per month to pay for refreshments.

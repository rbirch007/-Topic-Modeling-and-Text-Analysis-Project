	HONORED.

	A VERY pleasant social gathering was
held in the Manti Temple on the 22nd of
March in honor of the seventy-first an-
niversary of the birth of Sister Minerva
W. Snow.
	Her sweet and gentle spirit and her 
integrity to duty has endeared her to all
who know her and especially to her co-
laborers in the Temple.  Desiring to show
our love and appreciation for her, a few of
us sisters planned a surprise for her and in
cousulting Presidents Lund and Maiben,
they invited us to have it in the Temple.
We gladly consented and the following is
Sister Snow's description of it, written to a
friend:
	"We had assembled as usual at the
Temple and Prest. Maiben, at the close of
his remarks in the morning service, ex-
pressed a wish to meet the workers in an
upper room, after the day's work was done.

with a few intimate friends who had been
invited, assembled there. Of course care

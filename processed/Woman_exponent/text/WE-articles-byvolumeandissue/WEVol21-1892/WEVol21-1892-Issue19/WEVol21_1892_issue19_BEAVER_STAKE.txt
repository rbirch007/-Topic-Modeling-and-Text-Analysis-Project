BEAVER STAKE.
	THE quarterly Conference of the Relief
Society of this Stake was held in the L. D.
S. Meeting House, Beaver City, Aug. 6th,
1892.  President Lucinda Howd presiding.
	After the usual opening exercises the
local Presidents reported their respective
branches of the Relief Society as being in
good condition and doing all they could to
fulfill their mission.  The statistical and
financial reports were then read.  The bal-
ance of the time was mostly occupied by
Mrs. Elizabeth Howard and Mrs. Ardella
Eardly.  Such topics as:  keeping minutes
properly, punctuality, the necessity and


efficacy of prayer, cleanliness, taking the
EXPONENT,sustaining the Deseret Hospital;
and teaching our children obedience to the
priesthood and the law of tithing, Word of
Wisdom, observance of the Sabbath and
fast days, were treated by these sisters in a
very pleasing and instructive manner.  We
enjoyed the visit and their instructions very
much and hope they will come again soon.
	President C. D. White was pleased with
the counsel we had had.  Thought if it
were carried out our lives would be happier
and more useful.  The Stake officers were
unanimously sustained by the conference as
follows:  Lucinda Howd, Pres.  S. M.
Dell, 1st Coun.  Lydia White, 2nd Coun.
Louissa Jones, Sec.
		LOUISSA JONES, Stake Sec.

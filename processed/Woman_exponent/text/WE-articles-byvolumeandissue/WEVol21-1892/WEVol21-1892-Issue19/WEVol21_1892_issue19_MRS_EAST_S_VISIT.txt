MRS. EAST’S VISIT

	THE many friends of Sister Willmirth
East, President of the Relief Society of St.
Joseph Stake, will be glad to learn that she
arrived in Salt Lake City, April 1st, enjoy-
ing good health, and buoyant in spirits, and
finding her loved ones waiting with tear-
dimmed eyes and open arms to meet her.
She has enjoyed herself very much in the
meeting with dear old friends during the
conference, and with joy exceeding and full
of glory for the privilege of attending the
dedicatory services of the most holy and
beautiful Temple, in honor of the God of
srael, which is glorious beyond description.
She attended the general conference of the
Relief Societies, and gave a favorable report
of the labors of the sisters, and also atten-
ded a meeting of the officers of the Y. L.
M. I. A. held April 5th, and reported the
associations to be in a flourishing condition.
Said since the visit of President B. H.
Roberts, there had been a decided improve-
ment and have felt more of the spirit of the
Lord than for years past, reported the
health of the people of the St. Joseph Stake
to be better than it ever had been previously
since the valley was settled. She will visit
the central board and attend the conference
of the Primary Associations while she is in
Utah, and other meetings wherever she is
invited and able to go. Her friends and
especially the sisters are delighted to see
her and hear her welcome voice once more.

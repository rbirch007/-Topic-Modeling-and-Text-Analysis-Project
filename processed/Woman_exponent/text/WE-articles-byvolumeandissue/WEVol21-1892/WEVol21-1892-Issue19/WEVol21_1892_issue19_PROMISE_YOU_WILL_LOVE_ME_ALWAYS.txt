PROMISE YOU WILL LOVE ME ALWAYS.

		I will ask of you a promise
			Now while I am young and strong,
		’Tis that you will truly love me
			When the years have rolled along.
		When life’s cares and trials leave me
			With deep lines upon my brow,
		And my brown hair turns to silver, 
			Can your heart love then as now?

		Now I’m young and have life’s pleasures,
			Gay young friends to cheer each day,
		And your love will be much dearer,
			When the years have passed away.
		When these friends that now are near me
			With my youthful days have flown,
		Tell me, can you love me truly
			Then when I have older grown.
	
		If your love to me is precious
			When each day with pleasure rings
		Oh, how much my heart will need it
			When old age unto me clings.
		When my steps are slow and feeble
			Hushed the merry sound of glee,
		After years of life together 
			Will you, can you still love me?
 
		When I’ve gone beyond the river
			Left on earth all pain and care.
		And you too have passed the portals, 
			And we dwell together there,
		I will want your love in Heaven
			When our circle is complete;
		Promise now that you will love me
			When beyond this life we meet.

OBITUARIES.
	Hannah Oleson, or better known as Hannah
Neilson, died at her home in Hooper, Weber Co.
of old age, Feb. 7th, 1893. Deceased was born
in Bosarp, Malmo Sweden, Oct. 16th, 1816. She
was the daughter of Anders Oleson and Ellen
Jipson. She first heard the Gospel in the sum-
mer of 1857 and on the 26th, day of December of
the same year she was baptized into the Church
of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, by Elder
Neils Johnson her only bed was often vacated
for the weary Elders and she divided her last
crust of bread with them and washed their
wounds when they had been mobbed. She left
her native land in the spring of 1862, bound for
Zion; she crossed the Ocean in a sailing vessel

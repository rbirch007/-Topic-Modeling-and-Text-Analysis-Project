RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Resolutions of respect to the memory of Sister
Clara Marshall, who departed this life on Tues-
day August 25th, 1891. Aged 49 years and 8
months.
	Whereas,—Our Heavenly Father has in His in-
finite wisdom, removed from our midst our be-
loved sister Clara Marshall and
	Whereas, —That we the officers and members
of the Mill Ward Relief Society do mourn her loss
realizing that we have lost a noble sister whose
hand was ever ready to help the sick and
afflicted.
	Resolved,—That we the officers and members
of the Mill Ward Relief Society do mourn her loss
realizing that we have lost a noble sister whose
hand was ever ready to help the sick and
afflicted.
	Resolved,—That we tender our heartfelt sym-
path to her bereaved children.
	Resolved,—That a copy of these resolutions be
placed on the records of the Relief Society, and a


copy be sent to the bereaved family, and the WO-
MAN'S EXPONENT.
		SARAH COLTON,
		MELLISSA REYNOLDS,

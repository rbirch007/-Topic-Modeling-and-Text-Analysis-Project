MISCELLANEOUS.

	A noble deed is a step toward heaven.
	It is a thousand times better to suffer than
to sin.
	Afford others the same indulgence you would
wish to receive.
	One knows the value of pleasure only after
he has suffered pain.
	It is far less dangerous to slip with the foot
than with the tongue.
	Prayer will make us leave off sinning, or
sinning will make us leave off praying.
	A bitter jest, when it comes too near the
truth, will leave a sharp sting behind it.

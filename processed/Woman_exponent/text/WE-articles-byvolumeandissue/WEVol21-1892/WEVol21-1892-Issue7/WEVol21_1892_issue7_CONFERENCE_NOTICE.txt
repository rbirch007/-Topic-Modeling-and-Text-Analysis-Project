CONFERENCE NOTICE.

	THE Sixty-third Semi annual Conference of the
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints will 
convene in the Tabernacle, Salt Lake City, at
10 o'clock on Thursday morning, October 6th,
1892.
	The officers and members of the Church gen-
erally are cordially invited to attend the meetings
of the conference.
				WILFORD WOODRUFF,
				GEORGE Q. CANNON,
				JOSEPH F. SMITH.
							First Presidency.

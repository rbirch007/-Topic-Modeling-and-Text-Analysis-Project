OBITUARY.

	Sister Orelia Christine Hanson was born June
22nd, 1849, in Elling, Swenborg, Denmark.  She
died at Colonia, Diaz, Chihuahua, Mexico.
	Sister Hansen emigrated to Utah about nine
years ago, residing for a time in Newton, Cache
County, afterwards removing to Manti, where she
labored in the Temple for nearly two years.  She
was also a teacher in the South Ward Relief
Society, performing all her duties faithfully and
well.
	She was for many years a great sufferer from
lung disease, but she lived and died a faithful
Latter-day Saint, enduring all her sufferings with
patience and fortitude.
						ELIZABETH CASTO,
		President, Manti South Ward Relief Society.
						REBECCA WAREHAM,
			President North Ward Relief Society.

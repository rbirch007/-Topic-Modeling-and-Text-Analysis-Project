SALT LAKE STAKE.

	The quarterly Conference of the Relief
Society of this Stake was held in the Salt
Lake Assembly Hall in this city June 17,
1892, President M. I. Horne presiding.
Meeting opened with the hymn "O happy
souls who pray," prayer was offered by
Mrs. Elizabeth Howard, continued with
singing "O, say what is truth;" minutes of
previous Conference read and approved.
Mrs. Home made the opening address in
which she urged the sisters to cultivate
spiritual gifts, and perform the duties that
pertain to their spiritual welfare as well as
those of a temporal nature, to be saints in-
deed as well as to profess the name of Saint,

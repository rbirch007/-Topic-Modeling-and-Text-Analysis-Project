APPRECIATIVE.

	The following is from a gentleman in the
South who received a complimentary of our
paper recently:
Mrs. E. B. Wells:
	This is only intended to convey to you the
information that the WOMAN'S EXPONENT came
to hand by the last mail and rest asured I am much
pleased with the work, and sincerely wish that a
copy of it was in the hand of every person in-
terested in the welfare of the human family.
And in the mean time believe me, most respect-
fully,
			N. P. L.
July 26, 1892.

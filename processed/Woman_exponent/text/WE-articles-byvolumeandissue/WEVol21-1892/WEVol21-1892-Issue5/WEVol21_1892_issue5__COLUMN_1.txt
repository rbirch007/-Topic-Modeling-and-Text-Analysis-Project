R. S. REPORTS.
JACKSON.
	Report of the organization of the Relief
Society of the Laklata Branch, of the Burn-
ham Ward, organized Mar. 27th, 1892,
as follows: Sister Lydia Taylor was chosen
to act as President, Sisters May Rogers,
and Agnes Smith Counselors, Sister Mary
Taylor Secretary, Miss Lillie Taylor assist-
ant Secretary, Sister Amanda Taylor
Treasurer. The brethren who officiated in
setting apart these officers, were President
F. A. Hammond, Bishop L. C. Burnham,
Elders F. A. Young, J. S. Smith, and D. J.
Rogers.

	MR. EUGENE FIELD has been formally
dubbed Master of Arts. To his heirs and
posterity the sealed document may be of
value, but to his contemporaries Mr. Field
has long been known as Past Grand Mas-
ter of many arts. He can weave stories
like cloth of gold, and he can play upon
the pipes of Pan, but best of all his gifts is
the largess he bestows upon his friends
from out his masterful heart. 		ELITE.

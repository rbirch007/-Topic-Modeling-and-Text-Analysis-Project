N.A. W. S. A.

[To the Friends and Members of the N. A.
		W. S. A.]
	During the Columbian Exposition, daily
noontide meetings in the interest of equal
suffrage and the advancement of the cause
of women will be held in an auditorium in
tile Exposition gronuds.
	These meetings which are, of course, to
be free are to be under the management of
the Columbian Exposition Committee: you
are asked for aid. You will see that in
carrying on such an extensive work, we
cannot undertake to pay speakers either for
expense or any fee, so that we must appeal
for help to all speakers upon subjects con-
nected with the “Woman’s Question” in
the most comprehensive meaning of that
phrase.
	Will each interested person who reads
this notice kindly make it a point to send
to me at once the names and addresses of all
speakers of her acquaintance who have ex-
pressed an intention to visit the fair, to-
gether with the dates and length of time
which they will be there. These lists will
help the committee to arrange its program,
and the former will be doubly valuable if
received promptly.
			RACHEL FOSTER AVERY,
		Ch. Columbian Exposition Committee,
	Somerton Philadelphia,

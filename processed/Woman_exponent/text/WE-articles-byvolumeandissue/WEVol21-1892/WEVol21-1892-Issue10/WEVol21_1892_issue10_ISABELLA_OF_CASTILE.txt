	THE light that had begun to dawn upon
the minds of men caused them to be con-
vinced in other ways besides religious mat-
ters; a new hemisphere was to be discover-
ed; “A land choice above all lands;” a
land destined to be a land of liberty and a
refuge for the oppressed of all nations; one
that had been kept hidden for centuries,
but the time had come for it to be brought
to light and chosen instruments were raised
up, fitted to preform the work. Christopher
Columbus a native of Genoa supposed to be
bom about the year 1436, marrying the
daughter of a great navigator in the
Portuguese service and obtaining through
her many valuable charts and other things

THE MASTER’S STROKE. 

Touch Thou with gentle strokes divine, oh Lord!
Discordant tendrils of life’s harpsichord;
O, let the vibrant echoes thus awoke
Within men’s souls, enobling thought evoke. 
Let sweetest accents from the worlds unknown,
Change solemn moan to gentle patient tone.  
Sublime, triumphal anthems fill the air, 
To lift all human hearts from mirage of despair.

Let every throbbing pulse in nature’s tune
Accord with thine for aye. Oh very soon,
Dear Lord, may waves of music from above
Inspire in mortal lives unselfish love,
Till through the universe from shore to shore 
No discords will be heard forevermore!
Till as themselves all love their fellow men— 
The worlds all sing in praise the grand amen.

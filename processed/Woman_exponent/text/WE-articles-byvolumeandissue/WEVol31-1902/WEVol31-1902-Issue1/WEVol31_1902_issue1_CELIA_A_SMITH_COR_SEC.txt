In 1902, New South Wales gave full suf
frage to women, and full national suffrage
with the right to sit in Parliament, was ex-
tended to all the 800,000 women of Fed-
erated Australia.
	Miss Elsie Reasoner has been appointed
Eastern press representative of the St. Louis
Exposition in place of the late Julian Ralph.
Originally Miss Reasoner was Mr. Ralph’s
assistant. She is a native of Leavenworth;
Kan. Her father is a retired editor. Under
his direction she began newspaper work in
this city, and was well known here.

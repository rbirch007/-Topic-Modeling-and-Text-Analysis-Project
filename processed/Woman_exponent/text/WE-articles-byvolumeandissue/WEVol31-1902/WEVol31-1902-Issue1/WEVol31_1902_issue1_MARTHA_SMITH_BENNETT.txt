MARTHA SMITH BENNETT.

	It becomes our painful duty to record the death
of our beloved sister, Martha Smith Bennett. She
was born June 9, 1817, at New York, St. Law-
rence, Stockholm. She was the daughter of Asa-
hel and Elizabeth Shellinger Smith; was baptized
into the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day
Saints by her cousin, Hyrum Smith, and confirmed
by David Whitmer, was married to Hyrum Ball
Bennet, August 17. She was the mother of six
children, three of whom survive her, she was presi-
dent of the Meadow Ward Relief Society from its
first organization, it being in the year 1870, till 1897,
when she resigned feeling it too great a responsi-
bility in her old age, she passed to the great be-
yond, Oct. 12, 1902.

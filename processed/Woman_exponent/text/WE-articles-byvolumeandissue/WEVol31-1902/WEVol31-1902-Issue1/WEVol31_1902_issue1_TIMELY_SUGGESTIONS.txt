TIMELY SUGGESTIONS.
	We take pleasure in publishing for the
benefit of the Relief Society Mothers’
Classes elsewhere, the plan of work adopted
by the Granite Stake, which has been suc-
cessfully carried out in the several wards in
that Stake, by giving one lecture a month
followed by discussion.—EDITOR.

	Motto of Relief Society Mothers’ Class,
“Woman know thyself and know thy mis-
sion too.” Build well. 
	To women, the builders of the temples of
life:

“And then in your building, build wisely and well,
How ye have builded the future will tell,
Then if thou hast builded for ambition or fame. 
Oh! woe to the builder, confusion and shame
Shall follow thy building; but honored shall be,
The builders who build for time and eternity.”

	What shall we do to have our babies healthy
and happy: Study child culture.

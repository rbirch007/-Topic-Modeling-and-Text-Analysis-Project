THE RIGHTS OF THE CHILD IN THE HOME.

	I. Exchange of confidences.
		a Attention to proper questions.
		b Respect for the child’s feelings.
	“Too much indulgence has spoiled many
a child, too much love not one.”
	Love brought the child into the world,
and the more love we give them now, the
more we have for ourselves. Love goes
most to those who give most.

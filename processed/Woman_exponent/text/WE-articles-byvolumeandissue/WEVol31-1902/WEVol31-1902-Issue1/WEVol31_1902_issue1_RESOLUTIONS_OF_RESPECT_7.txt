RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
Resolved, that we, her colaborers in the great 
work of love, mourn her loss as a wise and faith- 
ful worker in the cause of truth. 
	That we express our sincere sympathy for her 
bereaved family, and pray our Heavenly Father 
that His Holy Spirit may comfort them in their 
loss, that they may realize she has gone to enjoy a 
rest prepared for the faithful, and will come forth 
in the glorious resurrection of the just. 
	That a copy of these resolutions be placed on 
the record of the Payson First Ward Relief Soci- 
ety, one be sent to the family and one sent to the
WOMAN'S EXPONENT for publication.

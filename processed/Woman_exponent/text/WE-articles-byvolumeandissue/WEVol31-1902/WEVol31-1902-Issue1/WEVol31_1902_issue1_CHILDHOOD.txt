			CHILDHOOD.

	Childhood begins about the third year and ends
about the twelfth. The world that the child in-
habits is not just that around its mother’s knee, but
a widely extended world; it makes new and
wonderful discoveries; it forms new acquaintances
outside of the home circle; it comes in contact with
other influences, some good and some bad. As
judgment and reason are yet immature, there is
need of constant vigilance on the part of parents
during this critical period.

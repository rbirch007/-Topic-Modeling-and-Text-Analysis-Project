MARICOPA.

The Relief Society conference was held
at Mesa, in the R.S. Hall, March 12, 1903,
President Mabel A. Hakes presiding. Stake
Presidents C. R. Hakes and Isaac Dana and
other brethren were on the stand.
All the Stake officers of the Relief Soci-
ety were in attendance and nearly all from
the several wards. Subjects were, guarding
the youth of Zion, see that they kept good
company, and read only such literature as
would be of an elevating nature, the right
kind. Several appropriate hymns were
sung, interspersed with speaking, making
the meetings very interesting.

MARY HOFFMAN ELMER.

A Token of Love and Remembrance.
	We, the members of the Relief Society of the
First Ward of Payson, desire to show our respect
for our departed sister.
	Sister Mary Hoffman Elmer was born April 16, 
1827, in Ohio. Was married to Hyrum Elmer
June 9, 1845, and was baptized at Council Bluffs,
Iowa, 1848. She left us for another sphere, De-
cember 10, 1902.

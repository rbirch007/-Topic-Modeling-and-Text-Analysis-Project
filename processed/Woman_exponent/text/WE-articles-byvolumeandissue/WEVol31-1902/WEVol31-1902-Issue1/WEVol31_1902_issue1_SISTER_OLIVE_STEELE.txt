SISTER OLIVE STEELE.
 
	Sister Olive Steele Bywater, wife of James Bywater Jr.,
departed this lite at Corinne, Box Elder Co Utah, Dec.
1, 1902, after an illness of one week.
	Sister Olive was born in Logan, Cache Co., September
16, 1871; was the daughter ot Brother and Sister David
Steele and granddaughter of Brother and Sister Cyrus
Card, who brought her up as their own daughter, her
mother having died when she was one year old.
	On December 19, 1889, she was married to James By-
water, Jr. She was the mother of six children, two of
whom have preceded her to the spirit world.
	She was the secretary of the Woman's Suffrage Associa-
ation, and in March, 1899, was set apart as corresponding
secretary of the Stake Board of the Relief Society of Box
Elder Stake, and in that capacity filled her duties ac-
ceptably.
	She was a lovely woman and was highly esteemed by all
who knew her. The funeral services were held in the
Second Ward meeting house, Brigham City, and all who
spoke paid a high tribute to the character of the diseased.

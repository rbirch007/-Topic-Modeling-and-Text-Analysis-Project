		PREPARATORY SCHOOL.

	A Preparatory School is maintained
which gives preparation for the courses in
general Science, Liberal Arts, Mining and
Electrical Engineering, Law, Medicine and
Business.
	The proximity of great mines, reduction
works of various kinds, power houses for
the generation of electricity, afford advan-
tages for thorough and practical work in
mining and electrical engineering not en-
joyed by any other school of mines in the
United States.

GENERAL CONFERENCE RELIEF SOCIETY
	The General Conference of the Relief
Society of the Church of Jesus Christ of
Latter-day Saints will be held in the Salt
Lake Assembly Hall, in this city, on Fri-
day, April 3, 1903,, meetings commencing
at 10 a. m. and at 2 p. m. It is desired
that each Stake should be represented by
its president or a counselor or an authorized
representative.
	A cordial invitation is extended to the
First Presidency, the Patriarch, Apostles,
Bishops and brethren generally to be pres-
ent and give instructions and encourage-
ment. it is hoped there will be a large
attendance of members.
		BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
				General President.
		ANNIE T. HYDE,
		IDA SMOOT DUSENBERRY.
				Counselors.

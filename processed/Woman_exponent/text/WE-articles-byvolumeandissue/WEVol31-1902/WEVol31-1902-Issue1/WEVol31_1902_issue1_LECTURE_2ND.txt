			LECTURE 2ND.
	The motto of the Greeks. “A sound mind
in a sound body”—Moral Need—Should be
taught the laws of personal purity—Study
of special physiology; pure thoughts and
actions—No liberties with the opposite sex.
	Mental Needs: Selection of good books
and companions—Should be taught early in
life the scientific truth “that every thought
and action influences the body. But vice
and hatred how they mar the countenance
and form of man.”
	Study of L. D. S. Church history.

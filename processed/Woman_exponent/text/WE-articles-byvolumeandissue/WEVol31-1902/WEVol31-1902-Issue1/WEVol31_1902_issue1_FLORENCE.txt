FLORENCE. 
Tis heart-beats count, not distance great, 
Love ne'er was measured by the sea, 
It boundless is, wide as the world, 
More strong than Death 'tween you and me. 
Twill ever bind though all things lose, 
Its chains reach to Eternity, 
Will cling to those that long are gone, 
Who dwell beside the Silent Sea. 
THIRTEEN TODAY. 
Good wishes for thy birthday!
God’s blessings from above 
Rest on thee, May, my baby, 
Till birthdays dawn above, 
Good bye to play and dollies, 
A maiden now art thou; 
O, shun all earthly follies, 
Twine wisdom on thy brow. 
At Jesus’ feet sit lowly, 
How blessed there to be !—
So graceful droops the lily—
O, love, so may’st thou be.

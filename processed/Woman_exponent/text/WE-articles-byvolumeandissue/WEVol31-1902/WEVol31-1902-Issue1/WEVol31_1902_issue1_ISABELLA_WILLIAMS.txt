ISABELLA WILLIAMS.

	It has become our painful duty to announce the
death of one of our beloved sisters of the Syracuse
Relief Society, Isabella Williams, wife of E. H.
Williams, aged thirty-nine years, who passed
away Sunday morning, April 27. She had been
troubled for several years with leakage of the
heart, but was confined to her bed only a few
days.
	The deceased was a faithful member of the
Relief Society since its first organization and filled
the office of visiting teacher. She was a model
wife and mother and endeared herself to those
around her by her kindness in times of sickness.
She possessed an affable, pleasant nature and won
the esteem and good feeling of her many acquain-
tances. She leaves a husband and seven sorrow-
ing children, who will feel the loss of an affection-
ate wife and loving mother until that joyful time
when they will again meet and enjoy her society
in a state far more happy and glorified.

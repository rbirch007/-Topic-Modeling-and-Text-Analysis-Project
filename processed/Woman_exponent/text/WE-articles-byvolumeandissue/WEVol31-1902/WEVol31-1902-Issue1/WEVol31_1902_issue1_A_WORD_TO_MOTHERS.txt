A WORD TO MOTHERS.
A child is the most helpless of all God’s
creation. The first thing for parents is to
look after the health of their children. In
doing so, dress them with warm flannel
next their bodies and have them eat at
regular hours, little meat, if any, and
plenty of milk and fruit
When old enough to run about they
should have plenty of outdoor exercise, in
cold weather as well as warm, carefully
dressed according to the weather.
Parents cannot bestow too much affection
upon their children. Children should never
be trifled with, but when speaking to them -
let them know that you mean what jou
say. Rule with love and kindness I
think a good plan for parents would-be to
learn to control themselves before trying to
control their children. It is wrong to pun-
ish in anger, it is better for children to obey
through love than fear, and to be taught
that they have a good Father in heaven
who sees them, and He watches over them
as well as their earthly parents.
Parents should teach their children the
principles of the Gospel, they will be held
responsible if they do not. They should
also teach them the object of their mission
on earth, how to control their passions and
appetites. Mothers should watch their
children very closely, find out their little
troubles and actions, encourage them to
ask questions and never fail to answer them
to their satisfaction.
Teach them the Word of Wisdom: that
it is their duty to prolong their lives upon
the earth. Children should be taught to
listen to the promptings of the Holy Spirit,
and to believe that it is their privilege to be
guided by it in all the affairs of life. Teach
them noble principles, that to be truly great
they must be truly good. Teach them to
be industrious early, in life and to exercise
every faculty they possess. Prayer in the
season thereof, also secret prayer.

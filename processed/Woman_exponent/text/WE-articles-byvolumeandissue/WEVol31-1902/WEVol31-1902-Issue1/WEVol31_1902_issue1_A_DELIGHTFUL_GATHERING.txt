A DELIGHTFUL GATHERING. 
	Our Relief Society of Parowan have
taken up a very commendable labor, that
of taking a meeting to the aged or en-
feebled, not able to attend the regular
sessions They arrange a program and
take refreshments, and if necessary do
sewing for the parties they visit. Very
happy results are realized, and in this way
it becomes established that our organiza-
tion is a power for good.

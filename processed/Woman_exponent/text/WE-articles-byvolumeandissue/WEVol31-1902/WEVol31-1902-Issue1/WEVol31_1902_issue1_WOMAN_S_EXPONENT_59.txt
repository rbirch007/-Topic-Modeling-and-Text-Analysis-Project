	R. S. REPORTS. 
	PAROWAN.
	The Relief Society conference of the
Parowan Stake was held on Sunday in the
Summit Ward. Being cold and stormy,
but few representatives from the other
wards were present; the reports, however,
were very encouraging. Accompanying
the report from the Kanarra Ward was a
lovely letter from Sister Ford, president of
that society, in which she said the sisters
there had made during the summer seventy-
five yards of carpet for their new meeting
house, and were also buying lamps for the
same building, besides having donated
twenty dollars to assist their missionaries.
This was considered an excellent showing
for a Relief Society with a membership of
but twenty-one.
	The sisters of the Summit Ward are well
up in Relief Society and M. I. A. work and
are an example to some of the sister wards
when we consider that they are so few in
number. The Stake officers received a
warm welcome and many kind invitations
of hospitality that they were unable to ac-
cept in so short a visit

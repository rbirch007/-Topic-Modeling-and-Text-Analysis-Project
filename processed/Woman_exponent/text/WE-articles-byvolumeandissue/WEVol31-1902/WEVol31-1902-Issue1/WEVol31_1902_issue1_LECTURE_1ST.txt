			LECTURE 1ST.
	“Overcome evil with good.”
	Physical Needs: Food, fresh air and sun-
shine—Cleanliness, exercise, habits.
	Moral Needs: Encourage virtue and
vice will disappear.
	Baby or Childhood: Confidence, character
building—Sacredness of physical organs—
Watch growth and beauties of nature—
Spiritual needs.
	Physical Education: Prefix the character
of the child which is out of temptation—A
due reverence for God—“The parent who
neglects the child’s religious training, neg-
lects that which is most important for its
success.”
	Physical Needs: Boy, Girlhood and Pub- 
erty—Outdoor exercises, freedom of dress
—“Good habits form the character.”

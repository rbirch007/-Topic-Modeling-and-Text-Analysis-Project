SARAH ANN REESE.
It becomes our painful duty to record the death 
of our beloved sister, Sarah Ann Reese. 
	She was born in London, England, April 21, 
1839, joined the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter- 
day Saints in the year 1868, and died November 
7, 1902, being 63 years, 6 months and 16 days old. 
She was married to James Reese in 1870; was the 
mother of eight children, six living and two dead, 
also twelve grandchildren. Her husband pre- 
ceded her to the spirit world. 
	She was a faithful and active worker in the Re- 
lief Society. She labored as a teacher for eight 
years, also labored as president of the industrial 
class for a number of years, until she was pre- 
vented by sickness from which she suffered long. 
Yet she was always hopeful and patient, trusting 
in the Lord and thankful for his goodness to her. 
She was always ready to help the poor and the
needy.

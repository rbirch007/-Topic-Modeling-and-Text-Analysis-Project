RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Whereas God nr His infinite wisdom has seen fit

[Column 2]
to call home Sister Elizabeth P. Harper of the
Pleasant Grove Relief Society, be it
Resolved, That we deeply mourn her absence,
a faithful worker, a comforter in the hour of need,
a devoted wife and mother, and extend our sin-
cere Sympathy to the bereaved family in this their
hour of trial and admonish them to look to a divine
source for comfort and consolation. We regard
her many virtues as worthy of emulation.
Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
sent to the family; that a copy be sent to the
WOMAN’s EXPONENT; and that the same be placed
upon the records of the Pleasant Grove Relief
Society.
SARAH A. FOUTZ,
President
EMILY C. BULLOCK,
PAULINE E. BROWN,
Committee.

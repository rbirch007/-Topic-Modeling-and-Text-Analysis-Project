	 SISTER KATHARINE K. PALMER, Presi-
dent of the Eighth ward Relief Society,
was pleasantly surprised on April 6th, by
the officers and teachers of that Society and
three daughters and a daughter-in-law of
Sister Palmer calling on her at the close of
the afternoon session of Conference, it
being her eightieth birthday. After par-
taking of supper the evening was spent in
reciting, singing and pleasant converse.
	Sister Palmer has a twin sister who is
still living, and who was also surprised on
that day at her residence in New Jersey by
many relatives who reside in the Eastern
states.

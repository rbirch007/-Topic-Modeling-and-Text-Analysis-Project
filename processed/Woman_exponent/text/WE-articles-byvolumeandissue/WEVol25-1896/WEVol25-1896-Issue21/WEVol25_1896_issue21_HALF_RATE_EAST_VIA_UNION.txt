HALF RATE EAST VIA UNION
PACIFIC.
	For the accommodation of a large num-
ber of teachers and their friends, the Union 
Pacific as usual will make rate of one fare
for the round trip to Missouri River, Chicago
and St. Louis. Tickets on sale June 5th,
6th and 7th, good until Sep. 5th. Call at
Union Pacific ticket office, No. 201 Main
Street for any additional information.


At the “Ladies Club” in Paris, France,
the voting is done in secret session. The
club is open for one month to the aspirant
for membership. If her conduct is ap-
proved she is then voted in; otherwise she
is refused admittance.

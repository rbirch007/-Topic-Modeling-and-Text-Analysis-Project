LADIES' SEMI-MONTHLY MEETING.
	MINUTES of the Semi-Monthly Meeting
held in the 14th Ward Assembly Hall
May 1, 1897 Singing “Who are these
arrayed in white;” prayer by Sister Mar- 
garet Steele; singing “How will the Saints
rejoice to tell;” minutes of the previous
meeting read and approved, Counselor E. J.
Stevenson presided.
	Counselor E. J. Stevenson said it was
very pleasing to have Saturday come
around again for these meetings; was sorry
to see so few here on this beautiful day.
Regretted the absence of Sister Horne and
spoke of her great loss in the death of her
noble husband; told us of the last time
she saw him, and how comfortable he
seemed to feel, which was about two weeks
before his death. “Does it not stand us all
in hand to be ready when the call comes?”

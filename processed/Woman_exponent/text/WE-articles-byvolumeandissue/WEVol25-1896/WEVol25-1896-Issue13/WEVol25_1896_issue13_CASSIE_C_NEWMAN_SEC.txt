		CASSIE C. NEWMAN, Sec.

	Minutes of S. L. W. S. held in the WO-
MAN’S EXPONENT office, Nov. 17, 1896.
Song “The reason why;” prayer by Mrs.
McFarlane; song “Columbia’s Daugh-
ters;” minutes of last meeting read and ac-
cepted. Resolutions of respect to Mrs.
Fewson Smith read, and approved.
	Mrs. Wells, State President, made some
suggestions in reference to the Association
and the matter of continuing or uniting
with the State Association, which subject
was discussed and finally a motion was
made and carried that the members unite
with the State Association and discontinue
holding separate meetings.
	Mrs. Silver spoke as to the
efficient work done by the president of the
association. Mrs. McFarlane and Mrs.
Grames also spoke of, and thanked the
president for her devotion to the work.
	Treasurer’s report read by the Secretary
and accepted. Mrs. Silver moved that the
funds on hand belonging to the County
Association be given to Mrs. Wells for the
use of the office in holding meetings.
	RESOLVED, That the county Association
present the pictures of Mrs. Stanton and
Miss Anthony to the State Association.
Decided that the literature of the Associa-
tion be left at the EXPONENT office for the
use of the members. Prayer by Mrs.
Silver.

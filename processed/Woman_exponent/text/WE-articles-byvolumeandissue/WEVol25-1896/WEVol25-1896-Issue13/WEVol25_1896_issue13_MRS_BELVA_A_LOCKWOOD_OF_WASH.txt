MRS. BELVA A. LOCKWOOD, of Wash-
ington, who was one of the delegates from
the United States to the second Internation-
al Congresses held at Geneva, Switzerland,
in September last, to consider the adminis-
tration of public charities and the protection
of children, has made an instructive report
to the Department of State.
	MISS MARY F. FIELD, a daughter of the
late Eugene Field, is making a beginning
as a public reader. She reads only from
her father’s writings.

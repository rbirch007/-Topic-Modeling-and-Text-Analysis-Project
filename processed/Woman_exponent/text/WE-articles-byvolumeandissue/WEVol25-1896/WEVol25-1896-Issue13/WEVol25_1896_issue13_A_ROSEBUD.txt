A ROSEBUD.
		  _____
Tenderly, lovingly.
		Lay it away,
		Where the light of day,
		Sheds no kindly ray,
		On its bright array,
		Now faded and gray,
		Lay it away,
Carefully, silently.
Thoughtfully, hopefully,
Lay it to rest,
		For its tender breast,
		By a thorn was pressed, 
		That pierced the crest, .
		Of its petal’d nest
		Lay it to rest,
Peacefully, quietly.
Tearfully, feelingly,
		Over it weep; 
		For a soul doth sleep,
		in its grave so deep,
		Where no eye can peep,
		And no hand can reap, 
		Over it weep,
Mournfully, soothingly.
Watchfully, willingly,
		Guard it with care; 
		’Twas a life so fair, 
		Yielding fragrance rare,
		That this world so bare,
		Will forever wear.
		Guard it with care,
Faithfully, sacredly.
					Hyacinth
Year’s Eve, ’96.

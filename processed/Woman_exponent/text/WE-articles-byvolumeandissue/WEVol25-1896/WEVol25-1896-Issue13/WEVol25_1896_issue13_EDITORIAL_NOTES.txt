EDITORIAL NOTES
_________________________________
	THE secretaries of Stake organizations of
the Relief Society are hereby officially noti-
fied to send reports for the year r896—not
later than Feb. 1897—to Mrs. E B. Wells
Constitution Building, Salt Lake City
Utah.
	WE are very sorry to be obliged to state
that the Book of Poems which we had
hoped would be issued Christmas has been
unavoidably delayed but will, unless
something unforeseen transpires be ready
in February of this year. We shall be very
glad to receive orders for the book from any
who would like to have a copy, as not
nearly enough subscriptions have been
taken to defray the expense of publish-
ing. The title of the book will be ‘ Mus-
ings and Memories,” Poems by Emmeline
B. Wells, and will contain steel engraving
of the author ; price of cloth binding $1.25
—leather or Art binding (new style) $2.00.
	
	ALL agents of the WOMAN'S EXPONENT
and all Presidents of Relief Society in all
the several branches throughout the Stakes
of Zion, and others who are interested in
the woman’s paper are most respectfully,
but urgently requested to use their best en-
deavors to further the interests of the EX-
PONENT by striving to increase its circula-
tion, and also to use their influence in
helping to collect back subscriptions,
remembering that the EXPONENT is really
the official organ of the Relief Society, and
contains a complete record of all the General
Conferences of that important organization,
and is the means of communication to all
Stakes of Zion and through them to each
other, besides doing much efficient work
for women in various other ways.

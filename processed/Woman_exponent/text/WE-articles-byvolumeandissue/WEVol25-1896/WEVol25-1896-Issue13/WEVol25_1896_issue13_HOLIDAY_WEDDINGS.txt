HOLIDAY WEDDINGS.
___________________________________
THERE'S bliss beyond all that the minstrel has
	told, 
When two that are linked in one heavenly tie,


With hearts never changing and brow never
		cold, 
   Love on thro' all ills and love on till they die.”
   It is always pleasant and gratifying to
have young people marry happily, and in
the three weddings noticed here all the
circumstances are favorable and everything
indicates “solid happiness,” if one may
judge by appearances and acquaintance
with the young people. In our belief we
have not only a desire for union here, but
for the eternal marriage in which we most
firmly believe, and undoubtingiy hope that
all the young people in whose marriages we
are so deeply interested may be so happy,
that they may continue throughout the
eternal ages to come.

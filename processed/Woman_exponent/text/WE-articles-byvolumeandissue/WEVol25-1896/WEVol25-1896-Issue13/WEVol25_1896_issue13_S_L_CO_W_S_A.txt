S. L. CO. W. S. A.

	MINUTES of meeting of W. S. A. held in
the WOMAN’S EXPONENT office Oct. 19.
Song “Wyoming;” prayer Mrs. E. Price;
song “Help the working women;” minutes
read and accepted and roll called. Presi-
dent Grosebeck read a letter from Mrs.
Fewson Smith in which she sent in her
resignation as chairman of the executive
committee of the association, on which
action was defered for one month. Mrs.
McFarlane read from the new State laws of
Utah a chapter on “what constitutes a
voter,” and the penalties of disregarding it.
	Moved that Mrs. Groesbeck and Mrs.
McFarlane form a committee to draft
“Resolutions of Respect” to Mrs. Fewson
Smith for past services. Adjourned to
meet one month from that day at the same
place and time. Prayer by Mrs. Oswald.

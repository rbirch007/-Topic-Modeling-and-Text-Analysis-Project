U. W. P. C. 

	JAN. 30, ’97. The U. W. P. C. held its
regular meeting at the office of the WO-
MAN’S EXPONENT. Pres’t. Ruth M. Fox
called the meeting to order at 7.30 p. m.
After prayer was offered, the roll was
called, the members responding with senti-
ments from Edwin Arnold. Minutes of
last meeting read and approved. Motion
was made and carried that club pay twelve
dollars a year for room rent, beginning Oct.
31, ’96.
	Report on membership book by Dr.
Pratt. Remarks made by Pres’t. for the
good of the club. Program as follows:
	“Late Kate Field” Dr. Pratt; “Cuban
War’’ Mrs. Harker; “Action of United
States on Cuban Question’’ Mrs. Hyde.
Each paper in its turn discussed by mem-
bers of the club.
	Motion made by Dr. Pratt and carried that
minutes be read and approved at end of
meeting.
	Motion made by Mrs. Dickinson and
carried that the treasurer pay the rent of
room for six months. Program for next
meeting read, minutes of evening read and
approved. Benediction by Mrs. Phebe C.
Young; adjourned.

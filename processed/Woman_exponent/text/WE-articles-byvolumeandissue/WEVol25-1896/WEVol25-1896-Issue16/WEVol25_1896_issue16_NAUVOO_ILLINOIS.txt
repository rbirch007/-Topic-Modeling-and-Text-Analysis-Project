NAUVOO, ILLINOIS.
	AS very many of the sisters have proba-
bly never seen this report, and because it is
well to preserve it on record in our own
woman’s paper—we herewith give a true
copy as published in the “Times And
Seasons” at Nauvoo, Ill., August 1, 1843.
We hope our sisters everywhere will keep
it as a memento of the small beginning of
this great and grand organization. —Editor.

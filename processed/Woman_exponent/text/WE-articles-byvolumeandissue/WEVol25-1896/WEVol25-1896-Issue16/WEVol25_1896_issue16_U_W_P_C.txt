U. W. P. C.

	FEB. 27, ’97. The U W. P. C. held its
regular monthly meeting at the home of
Mrs. Ruth Fox, 261 W. 2nd South St.
	Meeting was called to order by the Pres’t.
and prayer was offered by Mrs. E. B.
Wells. Roll call, members responding
with quotations from Mrs. Emmeline B.
Wells. Report of Federation committee on
revision of Constitution read by Sec’y
Motion by Mrs. Wells that Federation be
held as formerly, in May.—Carried.
	Motion by Mrs. Dickinson that report of
Committee on Constitution be accepted as
read.—Carried.
	Program for the evening: Talk by
Mrs. Wells on her Eastern trip.
	Paper, “Three Lakes,” Mrs. Kimball.
	Poem, “A Woman’s Heart,” Mrs.
Lydia D. Alder.
	Dr. E. R. Shipp in behalf of the Press
Club presented Mrs. Emmeline B. Wells
with a birthday gift as a token of the esteem
and love felt for her by the club.
	Mrs. Wells made a fitting response.
Minutes read and approved, benediction by
Mrs Clark. Adjourned.

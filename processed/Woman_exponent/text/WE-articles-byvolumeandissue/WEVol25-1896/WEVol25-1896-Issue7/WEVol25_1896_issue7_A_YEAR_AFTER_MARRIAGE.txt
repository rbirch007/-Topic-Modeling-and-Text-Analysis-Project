A YEAR AFTER MARRIAGE

A year ago today, we knelt
	Before the sacred shrine;
And only peace and joy I felt,
	To link my fate with thine.

You ask me now if I regret— 
	Had I the power to show
What value on your love I set,
	I need not answer, no ! 

Though fond and tender then you seemed,
	And I no scruples knew;
With all my faith I had not dreamed,
	Of one so wholly true.
							LULA

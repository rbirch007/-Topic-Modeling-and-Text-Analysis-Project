SURPRISE PARTY.

	At 2 o,clock June 22nd, the members of
the Relief Society met at the ward meeting
house, to do honor to their beloved sister
and Secretary, Sarah Eggersen Cluff, who
has recently removed to the 2nd Ward.
After opening exercises Pres. M. M. Wat-
son expressed her appreciation for Sister
Cluff and the good work she has been
enabled to accomplish while residing in the
4th Ward. Sister Watson was followed by
Stake President, Mary John, Counselor
Marilla Daniels, Secretary, Caroline Daniels
Mary M. Boyden, Caroline Pratt, Cor.
Secretary, Barbara Silck, Hannah Arrow-
Smith, Sarah Baker, Mattie Keeler and
Brother J. E. Booth, who all testified to
the estimable qualities of our sister. By
special request Bro. S. E. Eggersen and
Sister Fannie S. Taylor recited. The
exercises were varied by congregational
singing. President M. M. Watson in an
appropriate speech presented Sister Cluff
with an autograph album. Sister Cluff
expressed her gratitude in a few well chosen
words, after which all retired to an adjoin-
ing room, and feasted on strawberries, ice
cream and cake.
	All join in wishing our dear sister much
joy and happiness in her future career.
A FRIEND.
Provo 4th Ward Relief Society.

NOTES AND NEWS.

	THE address of Mrs. Elizabeth Cady .
Stanton for the summer will be Peterboro,
Madison Co., N. Y.
	THE death of Jules Simon causes another 
vacancy in the French academy. There is
good authority that Emile Zola will not be
elected to fill the place.
	MISS ELIZABETH UPHAM YATES, of
Maine, who has been in the Suffrage cam-
paign in California, visited our City on her 
way home, but much to our regret could
only remain two days. Miss Yates is a
very charming woman.

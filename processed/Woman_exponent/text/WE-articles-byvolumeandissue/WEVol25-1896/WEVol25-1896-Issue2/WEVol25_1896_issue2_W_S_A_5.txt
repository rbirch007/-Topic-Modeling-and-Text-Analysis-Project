	Of all the officers of the County not one
could be missed more than our “Ella”
beside being our efficient Secretary, she has
been an earnest worker in every way that
she could to forward our cause.
	We fully realize our loss, but are happy
in knowing that Beaver (where the bride
makes her future home) will have added to
her corps from our ranks, a noble promoter
of all good causes.
	Mrs. Pheobe Evans, a suffrage member
and worker, was married to Mr. Frank
Westbrook the day of the ball, although
they intended it to be a quiet affair we dis-
covered the fact in time to divide the hon-
ors with them.
	May unbounded joy and prosperity ever
attend these worthy pairs is the fervent
prayer of,
E. CRANE WATSON,

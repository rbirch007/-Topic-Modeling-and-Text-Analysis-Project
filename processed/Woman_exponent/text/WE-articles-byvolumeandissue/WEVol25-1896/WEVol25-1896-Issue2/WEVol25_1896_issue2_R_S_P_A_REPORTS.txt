R. S. & P. A. REPORTS.

	THE Primary Conference of Bear Lake
Stake convened at St. Charles, May 30,
1896, President Annie Laker presiding.
Verbal reports of the different associations
throughout the stake were listened to with
great interest, showing that the work is in
a prosperous condition. After which an
interesting program was rendered by the
children. The general instructions were
good. Large attendance present.

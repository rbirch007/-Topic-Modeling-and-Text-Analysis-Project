RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	THE General Conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Salt Lake
Assembly hall in this city, on Saturday,
April 3rd, meeting commencing at 2 p. m.
and at 7.30. There will also be a meeting
on Monday, April 5th, at the same place.
It is desirable that there should be as large
a number as possible present of the officers and
members of this important organization, al-
so that every Stake of Zion should be
represented by the President, or some one
authorized to act for her.
	A cordial invitation is extended to the
Bishops and all brethren interested in the
welfare of the Society.
	ZINA D. H. YOUNG,
				President,
	JANE S. RICHARDS,
	BATHSHEBA W. SMITH, 
					Counselors,
	EMMELINE B. WELLS,
					Secretary.

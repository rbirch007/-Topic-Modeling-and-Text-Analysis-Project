	SISTER SARAH A. PITCHFORTH.

	WHEREAS, Death has come into our midst
and taken from us our honored sister whom we
all loved, and an earnest supporter and teacher of
the society, be it
	RESOLVED, By the members of this Society that
we tender to the bereaved family our sincere
sympathy, and earnestly desire that God will
comfort them in their affliction.
	RESOLVED, That a copy of these resolutions be
sent to the family, a copy placed on record, and
one forwarded to the EXPONENT.
		E. R. UDALL,
				     President,
		M. A. GROVER,
		K L. PAXMAN,
						      Committee.

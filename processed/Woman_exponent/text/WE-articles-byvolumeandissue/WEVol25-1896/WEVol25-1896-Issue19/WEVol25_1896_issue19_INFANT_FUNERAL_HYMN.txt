INFANT FUNERAL HYMN.
(“Tune Nearer My God To Thee”)
PEACE to thee, little one,
	Gone from our fold!
Quickly thy course was run,
	Thy story told.


Peace to the hearts that mourn,
Sadly bereft and torn—
Christ hath all sorrows borne—
	God’s will be done !

Let not our thoughts remain,
	Neath the cold sod,
Where thy fair form is lain,
	Lead us to God;
Where thy pure spirit bright,
Basks in eternal light,
Where there is no more night,
	No death nor pain.

Though from thy mother’s breast,
	Death carries thee,
By angel arms caressed,
	Now thou art free,
In glory like the sun—
God’s loving will be done!
Peace to thee, little one—
	Rest, Darling, Rest!

PHILADELPHIA Oct. 23, 1896.
Editor Woman’s Exponent:
	Will you kindly publish the following
proposed amendment to the Constitution of
the National American Woman Suffrage
Association:
	In Article III., Section I, strike out the
words “to attend all its meetings, to par-
ticipate in all discussions that may arise,
and.”
	Offered by Laura Clay, Lexington, Ky.

	THE QUEEN OF DENMARK, who al-
though she is seventy-eight years of age, is
still pretty and retains her vivacity and
charm, declares that the only way she
keeps young is by keeping young people
about her. She always has a house-full of
them, and likes nothing better than to be
surrounded by her grandchildren and their
friends. She affirms that she is aunt to all
of Europe, to which she is not grand-
mother.

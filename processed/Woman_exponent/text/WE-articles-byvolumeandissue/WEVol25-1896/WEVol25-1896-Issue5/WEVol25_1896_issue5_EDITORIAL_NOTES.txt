EDITORIAL NOTES.

	The Relief Society of the Salt Lake
Stake will hold their Quarterly Conference
on Saturday, September 19, in the Assem-
bly Hall in this city, meetings commencing
as usual at 10 a. m. and at 2 p. m. It is
desired there should be as large an atten-
dance as possible from the members of the
society throughout the city and county
and that each Presidemt be present, for
send an authorized representative. The
public are cordially invited.
	The Woman’s Co-operative Store has
been removed from Main St. to the Nauvoo
Building, on First South St., West, where
they have commodious sales-rooms, and


have also been purchasing new good; and
are very desirous of the patronage of the
ladies. The store makes a speciality of its
millinery and of burial clothing. The sis-
ters of the Relief Society and others would
do well to call before purchasing elsewhere,
as it is hoped the store will be a success
even though the times are a little hard.

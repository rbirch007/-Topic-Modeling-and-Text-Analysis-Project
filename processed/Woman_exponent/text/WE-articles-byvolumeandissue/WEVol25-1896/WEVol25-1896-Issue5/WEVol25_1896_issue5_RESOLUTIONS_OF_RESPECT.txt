RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	ADOPTED by the officers and members of the
Thomas Fork Relief Society in memory of our
beloved sister, Samantha Adeline Hoops, who de-
parted this life July 15, 1896, at her home in
Thomas Fork, Bear Lake Co. 
	WHEREAS, Our Heavenly Father in His wisdom
has seen fit to take from our midst our beloved
sister, Samantha Hoops, be it
	RESOLVED, That while we bow in humble sub-
mission to the will of Him “Who doeth all things
well,” we do recognize in her death the loss of a
faithful member in our society, a true friend, a
devoted wife and mother and a true Latter day
Saint.
	We also recognize in her faithful labors and de-
votion to the cause of truth, an example worthy
the emulation of our Society.
	RESOLVED, That we sincerly sympathize with
the bereaved husband, Brother Francis M.
Hoops, her. children and her friends in the loss
of faithful wife, a loving mother and sincere
friend.
	Also that these resolutions be placed on the
records of the Thomas Fork Relief Society, and
be published in the WOMAN’S EXPONENT, and
that a copy be sent to the family of our dear de-
parted sister with these lines selected:
	A gentle woman’s gone to rest;
		A gentle mother’s voice is still;
	Sweet children and a husband kind
		Bow humbly to the Father’s will.
	The loss we feel is sister’s gain— 
		A Heavenly bliss is her reward,
	For while on earth she ever strove
		To faithfully obey the Lord.
	And those who mourn for mother, wife
		May they find peace from God above;
	And if they lead as pure a life,
		Again they’ll know a mother’s love.

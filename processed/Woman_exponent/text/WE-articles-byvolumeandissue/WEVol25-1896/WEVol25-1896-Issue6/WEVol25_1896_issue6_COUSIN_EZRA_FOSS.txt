COUSIN EZRA FOSS:
Our dear mother (Rhoda F. Scammon) passed
peacefully from earth to Heaven yesterday morn-
ing, 12 30 a. m. the 18th inst.
About a year ago she had a partial stroke of
paralysis which resulted so she could not walk
for about two months; she got better from this
but never was strong, was very feeble, and finally
died from age and weakness.
She, like your mother, was one of the best wo-
men. Her eventful life was one that made every
one who knew her better. She was a good
Christian woman and a faithful mother.
She thought much of your mother and aunt
Phebe Woodruff and only two weeks ago she was
talking of them and saying their example had
made many good young men and women.
She was always a great friend and defender of
all our relatives in Salt Lake City.
Will you be kind enough to inform cousin
Lizzie Fox and all the other cousins and
friends.
I have just written a letter to uncle Woodruff
which will go in this mail.
Thought I had better announce by a personal
letter to him.
Hoping to hear from you in the near future I
am.
Respectfully your cousin

E. A. SCAMMON, M. D.

The notice of the demise of Mrs. Scammon we
deem a just tribute of love to her and to Sister
Phebe W. Woodruff who was a good friend and
patron to the EXPONENT as long as she lived, and
was a devout worker in the Relief Society her
name should be remembered by the daughters of
Zion and they should study her life and emulate
her noble self sacrifice for the Gospel.
Sister Rhoda Foss her sister was also a valiant
sister in every good word and work, and has left
her name on record as an upright Latter-day
Saint.
Following is the formal notice of the funeral.

NEW PUBLICATIONS.

	We have received the Jan. No. of "The Illus-
trated Journal," a periodical printed on fine
tinted paper, said to be better for the eyes than
the clear white paper in general use. The en-
gravings are excellent. It contains an inter-
esting, illustrated article, on "Utah and her
Institutions." "The Popular Science Monthly"
a work valuable to the professional and ama-
teur scientist. Also "St. Nicholas," a maga-
zine full of good pictures and pleasant reading
for the young. The above works are for sale at
Jas. Dwyer's, Main Street.

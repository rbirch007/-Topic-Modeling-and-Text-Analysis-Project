			     AMELIA GOLDSBROUGH.


HARRISVILLE, January 9th 1874.
	I wish to write a few words,to mingle my
voice with those of my sisters. I feel that
we ought to be the most thankful women in
the world. Where else do women have the
rights and blessings that we enjoy? We have
the privilege of associating with the Saints
of God, and of living in the Order of Celes-
tial marriage, which gives us the privilege
of marrying the men of our choice, also of
marrying good men; which I esteem among
the greatest of all blessings. I have lived
in polygamy twenty years, as the second
wife; and have nine chiidren and one grand-
child born and have never for a moment re-
gretted my choice. And I am thankful that
the women of the Saints have the opportu-
nity offered them of speaking for themselves,
through the columns of the EXPONENT.
This I regard as a great advantage and ever
pray that we may justly appreciate and wise-
ly use the benefits afforded us.

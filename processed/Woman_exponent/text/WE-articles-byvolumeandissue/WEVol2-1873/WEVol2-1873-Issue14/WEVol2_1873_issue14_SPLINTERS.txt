SPLINTERS.

	Mrs. Livermore arraigns "The Hub" city
for spending, last year, $41, 000 for refresh-
ments for the city government while only
$4,000 was paid for soup for the poor......A
young girl, of Peoria, Ill., lately became
hopelessly insane through over study and
anxiety to secure a first prize at school...The
college at Greencastle, Ind., which admits
women, lately received $100,000, donated by
a citizen of that place......The king of Siam
has established two English schools in his
dominions where the sons of his nobles are
to be educated......The three great Moslem
sovereigns, the Sultan of Turkey, Shah of
Persia and Viceroy of Egypt were born in
1830......Nilsson patronized Worth, the Paris
dressmaker......Mrs. H. Pendleton, the au-
thor of "Human Development" is President
of a Woman's Medical College......The late
Mrs. Alfred Gatly, of England, produced a
considerable number of very delicately exe-
cuted and tasteful etchings which were
published in book form......Sir Edwin Land-
seer, the celebrated English artist, was late-
ly buried in St. Paul's Cathedral. A large
number of members and associates of the
Royal Academy, artists, literary men, and
lovers of art attended the obsequies......The
Emperor William of Germany intends to
present a painting of the grand review of
the Prussian troops at Potsdam, to the king
of Italy who was present. The picture is to
be executed by Prof. Menzel......The death
is announced of the celebrated Italian sculpt-
or, Constantino Corti......In Brooklyn, New
York, travel on the horse railways is said to
have greatly diminished...It has lately been
discovered that iron filings are put into tea
as an adulteration, not so much to increase
the weight as to give color to the infusion
......In the southern part of the Russian Em-
pire, a female Savior is said to have appeared,
who claims to be the daughter of God, and
to have come to suffer for the redemption of
her sex, as Christ did for the world.

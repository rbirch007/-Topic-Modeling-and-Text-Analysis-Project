  OF THE PROPHET JOSEPH SMITH.

	Tuesday, the 23rd instant, will be the 67th
anniversary of the birthday of the Prophet
Joseph Smith, first President of the Church of
Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. On the
evening of that day a Social Party, under the
supervision of Mrs. E, Davis, will be given in
the 14th Ward Assembly Rooms in this city, in
commemoration of the day. Dancing will be
interspersed with appropriate songs and short
speeches from some of the senior Elders of the
church, who were associated with the Prophet
during his life. The proceeds of the party will

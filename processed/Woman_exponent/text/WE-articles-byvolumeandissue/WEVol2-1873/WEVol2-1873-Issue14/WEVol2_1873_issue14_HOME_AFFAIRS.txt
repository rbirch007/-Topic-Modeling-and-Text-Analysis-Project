HOME AFFAIRS.

	LATE COMMUNICATIONS from Cache Val-
ley, inform us, that very severe cold weather
has been experienced there during the past
few weeks,and that a greater depth of snow
has fallen than has been known for several
years.
	PRESIDENT YOUNG has again retired to
his Sonthern home in St. George, where,
we are pleased to learn, he is in the enjoy-
ment of good health.
	CHRISTMAS is again "at the door," and
will have made his cheery call and passed
by before another number of the EXPONENT
is issued. So we take the present opportu-
nity of wishing our friends, one and all a
"Merry Christmas."

SPLINTERS.

	Seven lives were destroyed by a late
prairie fire near Omaha......Memphis has
received $15,000 donation from Cincinnati.
......The cashier in the Treasury Department
of Albany, has robbed the State of $300,000,
to speculate on Wall street......The ladies of
Portland, Me., have been signing petitions
to the Governor in favor of Woman Suffrage.
......A melon growing on a cucumber vine
is one of the latest curiosities in the vege-
table kingdom......A sassafras tree 120 feet
high, 50 feet to the lower branches, is a
novelty in Painsville, Ohio......Less hours
of work on the farm, less produce and
higher prices for it, is the talk of some
Western farmers.........Eighteen thousand
Americans went abroad this year......Jet
ornaments have again come in favor with
the fashionable.........Paris has 150,000 pau-
pers......The theatre of Strasburg, destroyed
by the bombardment in 1870, has been re-
built and reopened......The old Mexican
General, Don Antonio Lopez De Santa
Anna, is passing a miserable life in com-
plete poverty, having sunk from opulence.
......There is a literary society in the
Maryland Inebriate Asylum, named after
Oliver Wendell Holmes......Miss Sarah E.
Fuller of New York has received an award
of merit from the Vienna Exposition for
excellence in wood engraving......Mrs.
Eliza H. Hill has been held in $5,000 bonds
at Worcester, Massachusetts, for shooting
her husband......Memphis is better and her
nurses from New Orleans have gone home.
......The "State Journal" office, of Harris-
burg, Pennsylvania, was lately burned, loss
$100,000......White sealskin is a new fur
which promises to be much worn.........The
prize fighters, Heenan and Sayers, have
both died of consumption.........The well
known English author, Sir Henry Holland
is dead......Parties engaged in a politico-
religious conspiracy have been ordered
arrested by the Russian Government......
A panic lately occurred on the Bourse in
Vienna, and the Government is trying to
settle the financial dicffiulty......The Carlists
are reported to have fired on a Spanish
man-of-war and killed one of the crew......
News from France indicate closer approah
to monarchy.........A Chicago negro wife
murderer, named Andrew J. Peetet, has
been sentenced to be hanged.........Stokes
has been convicted of manslaughter and
sentenced to four years imprisonment and
hard labor.

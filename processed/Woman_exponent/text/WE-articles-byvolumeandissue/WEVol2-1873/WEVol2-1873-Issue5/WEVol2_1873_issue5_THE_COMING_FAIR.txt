THE COMING FAIR

	The Deseret Agricultural and Manufacturing
Society, offers suitable premiums for the finest 
specimens of workmanship and home industry
which may be presented at the Territorial Fair,
to be held in this city on the days of the 2nd, 
3rd, 4th 6th, 7th and 8th of October next; a
considerable portion of which are designed to
particularly interest the ladies and aid in
eliciting their broadest endeavors to do their
best in assisting to render the forthcoming
occasion a brilliant success.

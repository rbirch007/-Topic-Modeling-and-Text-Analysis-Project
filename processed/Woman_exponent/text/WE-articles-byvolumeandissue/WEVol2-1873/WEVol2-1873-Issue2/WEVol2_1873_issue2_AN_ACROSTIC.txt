AN ACROSTIC.
				——
When women unite and each other invite,
"Oh come and subscribe for our paper."
May a blessing attend upon all who defend,
And help us to prai e our Creator;
Now, we are but a few yet we great things can do,
Sisters come join heart and hand,
Every one take delight and contribute her might, the
'XPONENT to send through the land
People all the world round think us trodden down,
Oppress'd and degraded, but know,
No existence as yet out of our Deseret
E'er the blessings of peace could bestow;
Nothing here can compare with our husbands, to
	share
The great gifts eternal, began here below.
							MRS. E. L. Hyde,
13TH WARD, Salt Lake City.

WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


﻿Who shall succeed Salmon P. Chase,
as Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of the
United States? Bulwer makes his “Riche-
lieu” say “The mate for beauty should be
a man and not a money chest;" so the choice
for Chief Justice of the United States, should
be a jurist, not a politician; and a jurist of
the highest capability and honor, calm, con-
servative, of sound judgment, swayed ever
bya consciousness of the vast responsibilities
placed upon him.

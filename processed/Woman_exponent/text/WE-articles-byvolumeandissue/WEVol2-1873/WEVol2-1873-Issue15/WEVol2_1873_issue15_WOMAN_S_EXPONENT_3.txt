WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


A large and beautifully ornamented "birth-
day cake" bearing the date of birth, and name
of the Prophet—made for the occasion by Mrs.
E. Davis—was presented to the company. And
every individual present, had the privilege of
being personally satisfied, that the splendid ap-
pearance of the cake was fully equaled by its
eating qualities.

PROGRESSIVE.

	A short time ago we clipped and published a
call for a woman's congress, which appeared in
the "Woman's Journal." In a late number of
the same paper is given a notice specifying the
time set for the meeting of those women whose
names had been forwarded in answer to the call
which is the 14th, 15th, 16th and 17th of the
present month. The list of names appended is
a long one, and it may be anticipated that good
results will follow this meeting of earnest re-
formers.

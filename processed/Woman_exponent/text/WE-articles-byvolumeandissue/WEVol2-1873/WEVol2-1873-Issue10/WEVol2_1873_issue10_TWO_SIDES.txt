TWO SIDES.

	Everything weak, timid, delicate, soft,
silly and inefficient is called feminine or wo-
manish, while all things strong, noble,
hardy, courageous and active are masculine
or manlike; and there are many of the femi-
nine sex who are rightly termed silly and
weak, but there are masculines who are so
soft and yielding that they cannot resist a
proffered wine-glass or a challenge to a game
of cards, though they know that the accept-
ance of either will lead to their eternal ruin.
And have you never seen a lord of creation
move about with the meek submissiveness
of a well-trained member of the canine fam-
ily, while his weak, (?) delicate (?) better-
half reigned (and sometimes stormed)
throughout his domicil?—Ex.

	My thoughts have been awakened, while
my soul sickened with the news of a beloved
friend having strayed into darkness and
wandered away from the Truth! And I felt
that the sisters should be on their guard and
watch themselves closely, that they say
nothing and allow no thoughts to remain
with them which may grieve away the
good spirit. But they should converse and
write freely about those things which per-
tain to eternal life and happiness much more
than most of them do; and they should
strive to reason and reflect understandingly
of their own condition and the condition of
the world; and pray always, that they be not
led into temptation and drawn away from
the light. I hope that all my sisters may be
led to realize the great responsibilities which
rest upon them, and strive diligently to live
in a manner, becoming Saints of God, that
His Spirit may guide them into all truth
and righteousness.
	This is my first attempt at writing for
the press, but, however, it may not prove
my last, for I feel it is well for us to try to
build each other up, and thus by united
efforts to sustain the Priesthood.”

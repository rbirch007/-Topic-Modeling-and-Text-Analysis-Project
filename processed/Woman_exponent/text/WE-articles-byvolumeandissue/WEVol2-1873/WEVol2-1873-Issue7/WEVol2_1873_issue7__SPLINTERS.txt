in England......Carlton College, Michigan,
has received a donation of $100,000 from

appear and seek to reconcile their difficul-
ties before they are permitted to commence
a suit at law..... Tea merchants in England,
are being arrested, and paying fines for
selling adulterated teas.... The Sandwich
Islands social system is begining to look

says the nation is given over to an early and
dishonorable grave.... Late advices charge
McLellan, the postal agent between Wash-
ington and New York with only $50,000
stolen and also say he has not been arrested
nor is likely to be......Indians and engines,
or Indian raids and railroad quarrels have
greatly assisted in keeping up the sensa-
tional interests of daily newspa-
pers for some months past, but seem to be
subsiding somewhat now....The Duke of
Brunswick willed all his land and personal
estate to the city of Geneva.....The Balti-
more "American" celebrated its one hun-
dredth anniversary last month.....The
"Sunday Morning News" is the title of a
new paper just commenced in Stockson,
California......A veteran of 1812 was lately

injuries received by the fall causing his
death.....A school teacher in Vermont,
punishes bad boys by making one turn a
grindstone while another bears on it with a 
stick....Prussian troops are vacating France.
......The Shah is said to be so dainty about
his diet that nothing but certain kinds of

dom of opinion but in associations and
vocations as well......Emily Faithful gives
occasional receptions at her residence in
Hyde Park, London...A miner in Pioche had a

eight hours afterwards was alive and in

spinal column was dislocated and the lower

setts' Republican State Convention will be

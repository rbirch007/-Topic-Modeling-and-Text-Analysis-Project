R. S. REPORTS.

	A Young Ladies Retrenchment associa-
tion was organized in the 16th Ward of this
city on Saturday the 24th ult. Miss Mary
Williams was elected President. The occa-
sion was one of interest. Eliza R. Snow,
Mrs. Horne, Mary E. Cook and others spoke
words of instruction and encouragement ap-
propreate to the occasion. Their (the young
ladies) motto is “Improvement.” May their
efforts be crowned with success.
							S. M. K.

	MISS M. E. COOK'S GRADED SCHOOL.

We had the pleasure of visiting this admir-
ably conducted institution, on Friday, Jan.
23d, and witnessing some of the closing ex-
ercises of the second term of the year. We
were much gratified with the degree of ad-
vancement that was manifested in analyzing
principles of penmanship, and in elocution-

[Column 3]
ary exercises. The school is divided into
Senior, Junior and Primary grades with the
following list of efficient teachers: Louisa
Ashby, Emma Wells, Clarissa Smith, Lu-
cretia Heywood, May Wells and Lilly Tay-
lor.
	In addition to the above, Eliza R. Snow
assists in giving lessons in composition and
elocution. The third term of the school
year commenced on Monday, 26th of Jan.

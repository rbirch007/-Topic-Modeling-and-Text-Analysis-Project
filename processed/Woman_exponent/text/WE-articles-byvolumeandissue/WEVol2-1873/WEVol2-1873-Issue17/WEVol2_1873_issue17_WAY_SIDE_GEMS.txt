		WAY SIDE GEMS.

	Cease young sister, young and fair,
	All thy useless thought and care;
	Follow not in follies train,
	All her senseless arts disdain.

	Gather for thy future life
	Gems with precious knowledge rife;
	Gems of endless beauty rare,
	Gems, not for thy glossy hair,
	Gems whose splendor will outvie
	All that,s bright of earth or sky:

	Gems of untold, countless cost,
	Gems too precious to be lost
	In the giddy whirl of life,
	'Midst its scenes of jealous strife!

	Treasures rare, which polished bright,
	Gleam with everlasting light:
	All that's good and pure of earth
	To these treasures owe their birth.

	Go then, fill thy youthful mind
	With all things noble and refined.
	Not the richness of the dress,
	But what hearts and minds posses

	True respect and love will win—
	Not all without nor all within;
	But when nicely blent with care—
	All fair within, without is fair.

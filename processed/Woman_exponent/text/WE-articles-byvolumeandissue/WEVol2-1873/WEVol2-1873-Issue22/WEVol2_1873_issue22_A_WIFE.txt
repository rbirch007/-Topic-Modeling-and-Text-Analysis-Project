A WIFE.

	HUBERT. 		Now thou art mine!
My love, my beautiful, thou art mine own!
Henceforth the partner of my every joy,
Which sharing, thou wilt double.

	EDITH. 		Nay, my Hubert!
Thou art but envious. Am I not thy wife—
And shall I be content to share thy joys?—
In every sorrow, too, I'll have a part,
And sharing makes each less than half its weight.

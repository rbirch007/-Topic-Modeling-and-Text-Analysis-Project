R.S. REPORTS.

	Secretary Miss M. A. Jenkins reports the
sixty-eighth meeting of the Senior and
Junior C. R. A., held October 11th, 1873,
in the 14th Ward Assembly Rooms. The
secretaries of the Young Ladies Associations,
of the 4th and 8th Wards read reports of
meetings held. Pride and extravagance
were spoken of, and the sisters were warned
against them. Improvement in knowledge
and grace was the principal subject upon
which instructions were given.

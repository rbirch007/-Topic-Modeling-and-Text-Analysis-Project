FROM DIXIE.

		ST. GEORGE, Feb. 25th, 1874.
	WOMAN’S EXPONENT, truly valuable and
ever welcome, comes to us in the “Sunny
South,” full of interest and good counsel.
I derive much pleasure and satisfaction
from the perusal of its columns; and in the
thought that I am associated with those
who are endeavoring to teach the principles
of truth, and bring about a reign of righ-
teousness upon the earth. The people of
Southern Utah appreciate, and are enjoying
very much the society and teachings of our
worthy President. We also feel highly fa-
vored in having the society of our much
esteemed Sister B. W. Smith, who meets
with us frequently in the Relief Society,
and imparts much valuable instruction.
	The Society is increasing in numbers, and
a lively spirit of interest is manifested.

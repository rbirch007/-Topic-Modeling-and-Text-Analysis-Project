EDITOR EXPONENT:

	Sorry to learn, through the kindness of
Mrs. Harriet Snow, that an account of the
Relief Society Stake Organization has not
reached your office, of which I forwarded a
copy immediately after the organization of
the same, which was effected May 14, 1878.
Bishop John Welch presented the follow-
ing names for officers of Relief Societies
of Box Elder Stake of Zion, who were
unanimously sustained:

	Harriet Snow, Presiden[-]
	Susanna Boothe,	} Counselors,
	Mary Stark,
	Mary E. Snow, Recording Secretary,
	Amy Madsen, Corresponding Secretary.

	Despite the reverses which the Lord in
his infinite wisdom and goodness sees fit to
send upon us, we are doing much good,
both spiritually and temporally, toward
the promotion of home industries. As a
public servant our President could not be
surpassed. In superior adaptability to the
work.
	Wishing success to the cause of truth, I
remain yours &c., T
			M. E. SNOW, Rec'g Sec'y.

				PEOA, Sept. 23d, 1878.

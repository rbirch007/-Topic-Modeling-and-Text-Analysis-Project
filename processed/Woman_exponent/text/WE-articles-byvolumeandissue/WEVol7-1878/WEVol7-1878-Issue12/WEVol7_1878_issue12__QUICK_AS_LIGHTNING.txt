"QUICK AS LIGHTNING."

	Lightning travels nearly a million times
faster than thunder. The speed of light-
ning is so great that it would go 480 times
round the earth in a minute. Whereas the
sound of thunder would go scarcely thir-
teen miles in the same space of time.
Thunder will take a second to travel 380
yards, hence a popular method of approxi-
mating the distance of a thunder cloud is
as follows: Immediately you see the light-
ning flash, put your hand upon your pulse
and count how many times it beats before
you hear the thunder; if it beats six pulsa-
tions the storm is a mile off, if twelve pul-
sations it is two miles off, and so on. In
the case of elderly people five pulsations
would measure nearly the same period of
time as six in the case of young people.

				A MEMORIAL.

To the Senate and House of Representatives of
	the U. S. A.

	We, the undersigned, women of Utah, pray
your honorable body to enact such legislation
as will make effective the anti-polygamy law
of 1862.

	After the adoption of this, a circular letter
was read by Mrs. Col. Froiseth, which it is the
intention to send to every minister in the
United States, the letter to be accompanied by
copies of the address and petition. Here is the
letter:

EDITOR EXPONENT:

	The Relief Society of this place was or-
ganized June 23, 1878, by our worthy
Bishop, George Lake and Counselors, with
the following officers:

	Matilda P. Killian, President,
	Morilla Bates,	} Counselors,
	Martha Stratton,	}
	Mary A. Adams,	} Secretaries,
	Annie Woods,	}
	Mary K. Overson, Treasurer

	There were also eight Teachers chosen.
	The Society numbers twenty-nine mem-
bers. As we are living in the UNITED OR-
DER we have no poor to look after, but there
are many ways that we can do good; we
can look after the sick and take care of
them, and help one another in various ways.
We feel anxious to do something to for-
ward the work which we are engaged in.
It is a general time of health with us, for
which we feel truly grateful to our Heav-
enly Father, as well as for every other
blessing we receive from His bountiful
hand.
	Ever praying for the welfare of the
Daughters of Zion, we remain your sisters
in the Covenant of Truth,
			M. P. KILLIAN, Pres't.

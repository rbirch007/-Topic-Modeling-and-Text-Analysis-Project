OBITUARY.

	DIED, at the Sulphur Springs in Ogden
Cafion, Oct. 15th, 1878, of lung fever, after an
illness of five weeks, Miss Caroline Amanda
Winslow, aged 14 years, 2 months and 7 days,
beloved and only daughter of John H. and
Caroline Marchant Winslow, of Morgan City,
Morgan Co., U. T. Deceased was the Organist
for the Choir and a teacher in the North Mor-
gan Sunday School, and as such she was punc-
tual and ever ready to perform the duties as-
signed her. "Blessed are they that die in the
Lord."—[Millennial Star please copy.].

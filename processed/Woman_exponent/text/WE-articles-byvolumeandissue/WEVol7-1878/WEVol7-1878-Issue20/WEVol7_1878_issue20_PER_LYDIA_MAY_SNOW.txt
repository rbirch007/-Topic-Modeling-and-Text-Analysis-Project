Faith evermore overlooks the difficulties
of the way, and bends her eyes only to the
end. She looks back to the Gross and is at
peace; and forward to the crown, and pants
for its possession.—Bishop Hall.
	If our religion is not true, we are bound
to change it; If it is true, we are bound to
propagate it.—Whately.

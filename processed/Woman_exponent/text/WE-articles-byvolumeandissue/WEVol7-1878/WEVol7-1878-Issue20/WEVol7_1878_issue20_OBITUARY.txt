OBITUARY 
	DIED, at South Cottonwood, Feb.—, 1879,
Mrs. Sophia Godfrey, wife of James Godfrey,
leaving a husband and large family to mourn
her loss. Sister Godfrey was baptized into this
Church when quite young, and emigrated to
Utah with her father and mother in the first
hand-cart company in 1856. The father was
taken sick on the plains, and she, although a
very young girl, aided her mother very materi-
ally during his illness; he died at the Sweetwa-
ter, and they continued their journey across

HOME AFFAIRS.

	CLASS IN OBSTETRICS.—Having had several
applications to join my winter ob[-]te[-]rical class
rather late, I propose commencing a new class,
beginning May 1st, and ending Nov. 1st, if a
sufficient number apply. Any one desiring to
join this class please advise me by April 15th.
	Further particulars can be had by applicants
by letter.

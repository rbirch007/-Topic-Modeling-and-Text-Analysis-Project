				HAPPINESS.

	What happiness supreme we humble mortals gain,
What power to redeem, by faith and works obtain,
If faithfully we seek the straight and narrow way,
And God's commandments keep, and never go astray.

Yes, surely as our day, our God will grace supply;
His promise is our stay, when clouds obscure the sky;
Our faith deliverance brings in sorrow's darkest hour;
Omnipotent to save, we know Jehovah's power.

No longer doubt and gloom our souls need fear oppress,
For truth and beauty bloom, increasing happiness;
With joy our lives abound, tranquility and peace.
May we be faithful found, till sin and sorrow cease.

God's chosen ones declare "this is the eleventh hour"—
Our King will soon appear, majestic in his power:
The Spirit whispers oft how glorious it will be,
Its music, sweet and soft, says Zion shall be free.
	Salt Lake City.				ELLEN H. WALLACE.

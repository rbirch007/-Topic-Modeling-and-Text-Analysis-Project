					ANNIE D. DAVIS.

 In the 13th Ward of this city, Sept. 25th,
1878, Miss Charlotta Elise Landgren, of typhus
fever, after a most severe illness of three weeks
Deceased was born in Sweden, June 14th, 1859,
and at the early age of 16 years left her parental
home and early associations to gather with
the Saints, arriving in the valley July, 23d;
1875, in C. G. Larsen’s company; and while
here had been earnestly laboring to save
money to send home  help her family towards
emigrating.

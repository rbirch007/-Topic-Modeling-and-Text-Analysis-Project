EDITOR EXPONENT:

	The sisters were together in the meeting
house on the 17th of April, 1878. Bishop
Rasmussen and Counselors were present,
and instructed the sisters in regard to the 
organization of the Relief Society. The
Bishop gave the sisters the privilege of 
choosing their president, and Christene
Keller was chosen. Emma Rasmussen was
selected First Counselor, and Christene
Peterson Second Counselor; Catherine Kel-
ler, Secretary, and Christene Neilson, Trea-
surer; we have sixteen members enrolled,
we meet twice a month, and a good spirit
generally prevails. We have made a quilt
and donated it to the Temple in Logan.
We are not very rich, but we wish to help
build up the kingdom of God. The EXPO-
NENT is always welcome to us, and we wish
to continue it.
	We remain your sisters,

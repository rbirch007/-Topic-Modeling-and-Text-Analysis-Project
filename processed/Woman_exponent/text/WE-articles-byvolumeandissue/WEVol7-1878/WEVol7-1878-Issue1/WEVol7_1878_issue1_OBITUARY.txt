OBITUARY.

	DIED AT North Ogden, Weber Co., April 30,
1878, of chronic asthma and dropsy, after an
illness of five years, Anne Austin, wife of Sam-
uel Knowles. Deceased was born at Bough-
ton, Aluph, Kent, England, March 7th, 1812.
She was baptized in the Church of Jesus Christ
of Latter-day Saints, May 22d, 1852, by Elder
George Nichols; emigrated to Utah with her
husband and family, Nov. 11th, 1873. She
was a loving wife, a kind and indulgent moth-
er, and in a[-]l her affliction was never heard to
murmur or comp[-]ain; she died as she had lived
a faithful Latter-day Saint.

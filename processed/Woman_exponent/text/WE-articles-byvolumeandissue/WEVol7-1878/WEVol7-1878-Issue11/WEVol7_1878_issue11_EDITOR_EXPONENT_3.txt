EDITOR EXPONENT:

	We wish to mingle our voice with the
rest, and let you know how we are getting
along as a Young Ladies' Association in
Tooele. We meet twice a month on week
days, and every Sunday evening alternately
with the Young Men's Association. Our
officers are very energetic in their endeav-
ors to carry out the instructions of the
Presidency. We have been several days
gleaning this season, and are fully alive to
the necessity of preparing for the troubles
that have already commenced. We feel
very thankful that we are here safe from
the pestilence and famine that is spreading
upon this continent. We can see the pro-
phecies are being fulfilled very fast, and we
desire to be wide awake, that we may ac-
complish some good in our generation. We
are favored with officers who are constantly
on the alert. We read the EXPONENT with
great interest.
		MRS. ELIZABETH TATE, Pres't.

	MISS EVE CHARLSON.

	The night before Elder Thaine went to visit
them she dreamed she saw two large pillars,
and between them a very high place, and a
man standing upon it with an upraised sword
in one hand, and a great multitude of people
standing around looking and listening to him;
and on each pillar was inscribed in large letters
these words:
SACRED TO THE MEMORY OF JOSEPH SMITH.
	Mount Pleasant, Canada.

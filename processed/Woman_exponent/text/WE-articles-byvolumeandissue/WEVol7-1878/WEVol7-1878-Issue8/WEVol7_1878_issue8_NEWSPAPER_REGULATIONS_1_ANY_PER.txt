NEWSPAPER REGULATIONS: 1. “Any per-
son who takes a paper regularly from the post-
office, whether directed to his name or anoth-
er’s, or whether he has subscribed or not. is re-
sponsible for the payment.
	2. If a person orders a paper discontinued, he
must pay all arrearages, or the publishers may
continue to send it until payment is made,
and collect the whole amount whether the pa-
per is taken from the office or not.

OBITUARIES.

	DIED, in West Jordan, Feb. 2, 1879, of in-
flammation of the lungs, Mary Ann Leather,
wife of William Leather. She was born in
England, March lst, 1814; was baptized into
the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day
Saints on the 24th day of December, 1850. She
emigrated with her husband to Utah in the
year 1866. The fune[-]al services were held in
the Social Hall in this place, where a large
concourse of people m[-]t and then followed the
remains to the grave.
	Sister Leather was a member of our Relief
Society. She lived the life of a true Latter-
day Saint, and died in full faith of the Gospel.
								M. G.

	DIED, in Evanston, Wyoming Territory, on
Tuesday, Feb. 11. 1879, aged 27 years, 5 months
and 11 days, Charlotte Elizabeth Edmisson,
daughter of William and Ma[-]y Ann Gingell,
born in Camden, Cook Co., Australia. She
embraced the Gospel in 1853 and emigrated to


Utah with her parents in 1857. She was an
active member of the Relief Society of Evan-
ston Ward since its organization in November
last. Her loss is deeply felt by her fellow-
members of the Society. She leaves a husband
and seven children.

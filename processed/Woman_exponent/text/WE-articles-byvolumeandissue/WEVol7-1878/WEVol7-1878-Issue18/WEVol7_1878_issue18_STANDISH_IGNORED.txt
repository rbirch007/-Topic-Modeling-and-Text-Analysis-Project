STANDISH IGNORED.

	There was recently given in this city a
grand dinner in honor of the landing of the
Pilgrims and the colonization of New Eng-
land. To this dinner came some two hun-
dred men. At this dinner various speeches
were made, and from the beginning to the
end of the feast there was not a word said
to indicate that women had any existence
in the world, or were in any way concerned
in that famous landing on the bleak Ply-
mouth rock two centuries and a half ago.

OBITUARY.

	DIED, at West Jordan, Jan. 16, 1879, Abigail
Bradford, wife of Bishop A. Gardner, aged 66
years and 4 months. Sister Gardner joined
the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
at an early date in its history, shared in the 
persecutions of the Saints, and emigrated to
Utah in the year 1847. She was always firm
in the faith, and was a zealous advocate of the
principles of the latter-day work. "Aunt Ab-
bie," as she was familiarly called had many
noble qualities of both heart and mind. She
was a member of the Relief Society, and an
earnest laborer in all good works. Her loss
will be felt not only by her relations, but by a
host of intimate friends and aasociates. Her
remains were inter[-]ed in Salt Lake City ceme-
tery, by the side of a number of her kindred.
"Blessed are the dead who die in the Lord,
their works do follow them.

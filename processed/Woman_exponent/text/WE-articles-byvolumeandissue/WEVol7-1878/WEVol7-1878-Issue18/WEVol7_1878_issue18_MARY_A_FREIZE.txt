								MARY A. FREIZE.

	The 22d of February last was the Birth-
day of Sister Amanda Smith, when she
reached the goodly age of three score and
ten. A select company of her sisters went
as a surprise party to her house, and carry-
ing with them many good things, as well
as blessings for this aged sister, made the
occasion an enjoyable one.
	It is well known to the Saints that Sister
Smith has passed through much persecution
in times past, which has enfeebled her
health. Her husband and son were both
killed, and another son left for dead, at
Haun's Mill Massacre, in Missouri.

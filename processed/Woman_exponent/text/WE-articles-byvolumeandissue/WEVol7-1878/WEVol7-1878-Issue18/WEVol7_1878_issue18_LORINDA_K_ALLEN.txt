LORINDA K. ALLEN.


REFINEMENT.—Refinement is not fas-
tidiousness. It is always allied to simplic-
ity and a judicious and tasteful employ-
ment of the means for good and happiness
which it has at command. It seeks to di-
vest itself of superfluities, and aspires con-
tinually to the utmost possible purity.
	MOTHERHOOD will be woman's crowning
joy, her life's purest enthusiasm and most
perfect expression.

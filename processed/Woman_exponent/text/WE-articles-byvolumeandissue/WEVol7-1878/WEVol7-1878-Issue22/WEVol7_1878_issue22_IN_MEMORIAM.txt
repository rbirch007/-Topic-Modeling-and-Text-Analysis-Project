IN MEMORIAM.

	It is with feelings of deep regret we record
the demise of one of our beloved sisters, Isa-
bella Catherine Maxwell, daughter of Ralph
and Isabella Maxwell of Peoa, Summit Co.
Deceased was born Jan. 4, 1864; she departed
this life Feb. 11, 1879, aged fifteen years, one
month and seven days. After great and con-
tinued suffering she at last quietly fell asleep.
She has left loving and affectionate parents
and friends to mourn her loss, and while we
sympathize with the bereaved family and feel
the loss of our dear sister, we can look forward
to the day when the faithful dead shall hear
the angel's trump, and rise to shine as the sun
forever. We do not mourn as those without
hope. No, my sisters. Let us prepare our-
selves while here on earth to dwell with our
Father in heaven. Our dear sister whose death
we record was a member of the Y. L. M. I. As-
sociatiation in Peoa; she was ever anxious to
do good, and she was loved and respected by
all who knew her.—Com.

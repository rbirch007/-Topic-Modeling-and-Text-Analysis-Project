ST. GEORGE STAKE.

	Our Second Quarterly Conference was
held March 14. The officers of the Stake
were all present; the several wards of St.
George were represented by the presidents,
who all gave good reports. President Mi-
nerva Snow feels the spirit of her mission;
she arose and addressed the meeting, speak-
ing words of comfort and encouragement
to the sisters. Mrs. Anna S. Ivins bore her
testimony to the truth of the work. Presi-
dent J. D. T. McAllister was present for a
short time, and advised the sisters to adopt
neatness and simplicity in their style of
dress, and not to follow the fashions of the
world. Rockville was represented by Sis-
ter Smith, and Toquerville by Sister Slack;
both manifested a humble spirit and a de-
sire to do right.

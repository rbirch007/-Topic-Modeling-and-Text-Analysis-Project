ENGLISH PICKLE FOR TONGUE. - For two
tongues make a brine of two ounces of salt-
peter, half pound brown sugar, one pint of
barrel salt, one teacupful of molasses, water
enough to cover them.  Let them stand in
a crock, well kept under the brine, for ten
days.  When ready to use boil two or three
hours, skin and eat cold.  This brine can
be used as long as it keeps sweet, and is
good to keep ham or dried beef in.

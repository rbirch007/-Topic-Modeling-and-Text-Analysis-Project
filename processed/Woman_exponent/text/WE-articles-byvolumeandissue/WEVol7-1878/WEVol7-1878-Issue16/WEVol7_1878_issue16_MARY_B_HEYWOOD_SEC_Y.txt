	DEWEYVILLE, Nov. 4th, 1878.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We held our Third Annual meeting Aug.
6, 1878. There were present from Brigham
City President Harriet A. Snow, Susan
Booth, Mary Snow and Sisters Alice and
Abagail Rosenbaum, who all gave us good
instructions. Bishop Dewey and his Coun-
selors were present and gave words of en-
couragement to the sisters. We had a good
meeting and enjoyed the Spirit of God. A
party was given in the evening for the
benefit of the Society, which was well at-
tended, and a spirit of sociability prevailed.
	We hold our meetings every Thursday,
working and speaking meetings alternately.
We have donated to the Logan Temple $20
in quilts and socks; we have 65 bushels of
wheat bought with the proceeds of our
Sunday eggs, and 22 bushels donated by the
Society; sundry articles now on hand worth
$10. Number of meetings held in one
year, 29. The WOMAN'S EXPONENT is a
welcome visitor in our meetings.
	May the blessings of God attend the Re-
lief Societies, is the prayer of your sisters,
	MARY DEWEY, Pres't.

				BE FIRM.

	My sisters be firm! If trials we have
		Either in pleasant or stormy weather
	Let us c[-]ing to each other in union and love
		And go hand in hand together

	Oftimes we are sad when clouds appear
		Yet cheerful when days are bright,
	The Spirit of God our hearts will cheer
		And show us the truth and the light.

	If chastened in love let us kiss the r[-]d
		And face all kinds of weather,
	Be true to each other, ourselves and our God
		And walk hand in hand together.

									—M. W. W.
Salt Lake City.

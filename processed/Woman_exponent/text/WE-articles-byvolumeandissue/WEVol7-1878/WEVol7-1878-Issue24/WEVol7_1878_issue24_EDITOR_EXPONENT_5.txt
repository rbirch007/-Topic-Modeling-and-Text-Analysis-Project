EDITOR EXPONENT:

	In behalf of the Panguitch Ward Relief
Society, we send you the following report:
There has been donated since our last Semi-
Annual Conference (aside from anything
paid to our Relief Society house) the sum
of $62 58.
Disbursed to Manti Temple.............$26 75
Missionary..........................................  17 60
Needy..................................................    9 95

	At a recent meeting of our R. S. our
President said, although we were a little
behind the times she would like to have
an expression of the sister's feelings in re-
gard to polygamy and the opposition that
is manifested against it. It was carried
unanimously that we heartily endorse and
sustain the sentiments of our sisters in Salt
Lake and other places with regard to the
anti-polygamy movement of our enemies.
			MYRA HENRIE, Pres't.

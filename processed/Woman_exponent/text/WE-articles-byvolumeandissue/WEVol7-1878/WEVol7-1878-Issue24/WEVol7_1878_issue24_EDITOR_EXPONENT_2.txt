EDITOR EXPONENT:

	We were organized into a Relief Society
Oct. 3, 1870, by Bishop Petty and Elder
J. C. Snow. The following officers were
elected unanimously:

	Jane C. Snow, President,
	Susan A. Barton,		} Counselor.
	Emeline M. Johnston,	}
	Hannah M. Henrie, Secretary,
	Almira Bunce, Treasurer.

	Two Teachers and six members enrolled.
Since then our numbers have gradually in-
creased, and we hope and trust that we
may ever be alive to our duties and respon-
sibilities that rest upon us in common with
our sisters in such a great and glorious
cause.
	Yours very respectfully,

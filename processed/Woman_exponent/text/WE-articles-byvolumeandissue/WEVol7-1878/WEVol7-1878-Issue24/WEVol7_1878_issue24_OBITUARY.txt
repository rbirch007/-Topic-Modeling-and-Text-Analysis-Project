OBITUARY.

	Died on the 5th of April 1879. Mrs. Amanda
Susanna Chamberlain, wife of John A. Cham-
berlain. Deceased was the daughter of Wm. and
M. A. E. Watmough, the latter a lady well
known in this community; was born in Wi-
gan, Lancashire, May 18, 1844, and, her moth-
er being a Latter-day Saint, was baptized and
confirmed when eight years old, by Bishop
Cairns.
	She moved with her parents to Boston in
1856; remained there for six years, and gradu-
ated with honors from the Welles School. In
the June of 1862 she came to Utah and was
married on the 29th of December, 1864. They
resided in Cedar Fort, Utah County, ten years
and then moved to a remote district about ten
miles from Deseret, on the Sevier. She was a 
faithful Saint, a true wife and mother, and 
leaves a husband and three little children to
mourn her loss.

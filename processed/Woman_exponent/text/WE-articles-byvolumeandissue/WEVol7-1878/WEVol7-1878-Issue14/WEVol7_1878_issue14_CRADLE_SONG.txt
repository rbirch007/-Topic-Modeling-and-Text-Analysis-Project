CRADLE SONG.
Softly croning a lullaby song,
Softly singing the whole day long,
Softly murm'ring, mother-love strong,
Baby is sweet,
Baby is sweet,
Sweetest blue eyes,
And dimpled feet.
Busily working, now swift, now slow,
Busily planning how baby should grow,
Busily thinking- while soft and low-
Baby is sweet,
Baby is sweet,
Sweetest blue eyes,
And dimpled feet.
Lovingly folding the muslins down,
Lovingly kissing away each frown,
Lovingly smoothing the tresses of brown,
Baby is sweet,
Baby is sweet,
Sweetest blue eyes,
And dimpled feet.
Forgetting that time will swiftly fly,
Forgetting that years will surely sweep by,
Forgetting that blossoms will sometimes die,
Baby is sweet,
Baby is sweet,
Sweetest blue eyes,
And dimpled feet.
Praying so earnestly God's sweet smile,
Praying with heart so free from guile,
Praying so trustfully all the while,
Baby is sweet,
Baby is sweet,
Sweetest blue eyes,
And dimpled feet.
Goodby, little mother with daintiest grace.
Goodby, little baby with flow'ret face,
Goodby, these words time cannot erase.
Baby is sweet,
Baby is sweet,
Sweetest blue eyes,
And dimpled feet.

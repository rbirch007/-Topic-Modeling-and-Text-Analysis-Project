			HENRIETTA STOUT, Pres.


MADAME CAZAMJOU, the eldest sister of
"George Sand" has just died in France.
The death is also announced of the Com-
tesse de Saint Andres, the widow of the 
celebrated Spontini. She was a woman dis-
tinguished both for her wit and her beauty.
Chateaubriand spoke of her as a "woman
who of all others best knew how to say
amiable things with grace and noble famil-
iarity."

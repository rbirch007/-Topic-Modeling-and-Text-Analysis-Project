EDITOR EXPONENT:

	The Young Ladies' Mutual Improvement
Association of Harrisville was organized in

[Column 3]
the year, 1875, Nov. 11th, under the direc-
tion of Miss E. R. Snow, with the follow-
ing officers;

	Miss Leonora Rawson, President,
	"	Cyntha Shurtliff,	  }
	"	Polly Rawson,	  }
	"	Almeda Taylor,	  }	Counselors,
	"	Sariah Rawson,	  }
	"	Margaret Pritchett }
	"	Lovina Shurtliff,	  }
	"	Sarah Dixon, Secretary,
	"	Mary Wilson, Assistant Secretary,
	"	Harriet Daybell, Treasurer.
	We hold our meetings as regular as possi-
ble, and have a good attendance, consider-
ing our scattered condition; we read your
excellent paper in our meetings, and feel
blessed thereby. We feel that we have a
work to do as well as the elder sisters, and
by thus commencing in our youth, we shall
be more capable as we grow older than we
could be if we had not this privilege. It
is a great privilege the young enjoy in these
organizations, and we should esteem it as
such and improve our time. Hoping that
God will bless all who are trying to serve
Him, we remain
	Your sisters in the Gospel,
		LEONORA RAWSON, President,
		SARAH DIXON, Secretary.

EDITOR EXPONENT:
	A Mass Meeting of the ladies of Sugar House
Ward, was held in the Relief Society Hall,
Dec. 4th, 1878, President Mrs. S. B. Gibson
presiding. Many sisters expressed their feel-
ings, and all felt to uphold and sustain the
principle of plural marriage. The preamble
and resolutions passed by the ladies at the
Theatre on Saturday, Nov. 16, were read and
unanimously adopted.

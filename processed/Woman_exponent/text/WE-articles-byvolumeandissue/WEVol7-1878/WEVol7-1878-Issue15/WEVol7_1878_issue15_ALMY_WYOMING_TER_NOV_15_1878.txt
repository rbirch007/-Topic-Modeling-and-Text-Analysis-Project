	ALMY, WYOMING TER., Nov. 15, 1878.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Sisters E. R. Snow and E. Davis visited
this place on Nov. 3d, and at one of our
meetings on that day they organized a
Young Ladies' Mutual Improvement Asso-
ciation at this place. We can truly say
that our hearts rejoiced to see so many step
forward to become members of the organ-
ization.
	We held our first meeting Nov. 7th, and
we had a good attendance and received
same good instructions from Bishop Bouns
and others of the elder brethren and sisters.
They advised us to be orderly, obedient
and united, and we should see the time
that we should bless the day that Sisters
Snow and Davis paid us a visit. We num-
ber thirty-eight members. The following
are the names of officers:
	Mary Ann Henery, President,
	Mary Jane Ball,
	Elizabeth Bruce,	Counselors,
	Mary Brown, Secretary.
		Very respectfully,

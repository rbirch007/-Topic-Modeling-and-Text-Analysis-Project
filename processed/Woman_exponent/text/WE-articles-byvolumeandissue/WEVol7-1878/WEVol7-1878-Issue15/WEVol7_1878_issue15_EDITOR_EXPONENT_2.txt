EDITOR EXPONENT:
	At the meeting of the Relief Society of Cen-
treville, on the 11th of December, each speaker
bore testimony to the truth of the principle of
Celestial Marriage. The Preamble and Reso-
lutions of the ladies of Salt Lake City at the
mass meeting in the Theatre on the 16th of
Nov., were read, and a vote was taken to en-
dorse them in full, which was carried unani-
mously; the addresses of the ladies on that oc-
casion expressed our sentiments.
	The members of the Relief Society here feel
well in helping to build up Zion.
	Yours respectfully,
		MARGARET RANDALL, Pres't R. S.
Centreville.

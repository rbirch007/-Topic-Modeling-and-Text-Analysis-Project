AUGUST.

The August born should
Wear a Sardonyx, or for thee
No conjugal felicity.
The August born without this stone,
'Tis said, must live unloved and lone.

This is a reddish yellow stone, nearly allied
to onyx, which is commonly a light brown
and white. This is the mineral used for
Cameos, the figure being cut in one layer,
the other layer funishing the back-ground.

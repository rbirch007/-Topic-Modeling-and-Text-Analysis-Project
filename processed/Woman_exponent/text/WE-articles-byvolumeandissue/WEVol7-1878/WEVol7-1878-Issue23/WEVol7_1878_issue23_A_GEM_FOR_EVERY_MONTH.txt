A GEM FOR EVERY MONTH.

	We have been reading recently in "The
American Home," some articles which were
very interesting on the subject which forms
the heading to this chapter, from which we
select the poems for each month. "The
American home is a pleasing weekly jour-
nal published in Chicago, and contains many
items of special interest, letters from
Washington, and articles on floriculture, in
fact a great variety of literary matter that
must certainly benefit those who read it
with care.

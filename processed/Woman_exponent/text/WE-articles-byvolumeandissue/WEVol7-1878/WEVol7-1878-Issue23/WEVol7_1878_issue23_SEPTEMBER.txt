SEPTEMBER.

A maiden born when autumn leaves
Are rustling in September's breeze

[Column 2]
A Sapphire on her brow should bind,
'Twill cure diseases of the mind.

The oxyd alumina comes in the form of
emery. This is granular. It appears also
in crystalline forms, and when clear, in all
its beauty and rarity, is the gem known as
sapphire, which is of various colors, though
it is only the blue crystals that generally
receive this name; a red crystal of it is call-
ed the Oriental Ruby, and a yellow one the
Oriental Topaz.

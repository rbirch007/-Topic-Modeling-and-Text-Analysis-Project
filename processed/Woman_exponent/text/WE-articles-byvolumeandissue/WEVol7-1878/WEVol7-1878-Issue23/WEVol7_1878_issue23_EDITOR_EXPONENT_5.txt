EDITOR EXPONENT:

	The Y. L. M. I. A. of East Mill Creek
Ward was organized March 30, 1879, by
President Mary A. Freeze and her counsel-
or, Sister Clara Young Conrad. The officers
elected were:

	Sarah A. Skidmore, President.

	Eudora V. Ranck,		} Counselors.
	Barbara M. Moses,	}

	Harriet S. Stillman, Secretary.
	Delia B. Neff, Assistant Secretary.
	Lydia Osguthorpe. Treasurer,
	Ann E. Neff, Superintendent.

	There are forty-four names enrolled in
this Association. Meetings are held once
in two weeks on Sunday evening, at seven
o'clock, with a general attendance.
	Respectfully,

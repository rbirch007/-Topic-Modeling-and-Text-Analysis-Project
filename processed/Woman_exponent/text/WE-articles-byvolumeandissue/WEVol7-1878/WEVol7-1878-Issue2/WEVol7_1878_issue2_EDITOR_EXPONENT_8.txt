EDITOR EXPONENT.

	As a Society we are decidedly in a pros-
perous condition; we are increasing in faith
and confidence in each other and in the
Lord, for we know that He is at the helm
and not only hears, but also answers the
prayers of His children. We have proved
this of a surety, in thousands of instances:
during the past quarter two of our sisters
whose names have been enrolled since the
first, of our organization, have departed this
life, their labors are ended here, and they
are removed to a higher life of usefulness
and progression. When we think upon the
precious promises that belong to the faith-
ful, how necessary that we should strive
diligently while it is called day, that we
may secure enteral salvation.
	With kind love to yourself and the sis-
ters, we remain yours in the Covenant,
			MARY MEIKLEJOHN, Pres't.

EDITOR EXPONENT:

	Some time has elapsed since the readers
of your esteemed paper have heard from
this place. We are trying to do what we
can to build up Zion. We have had a very
pleasant visit from Sisters Eliza R. Sonw
and E. Davis. It is the first time that Sis-
ter Eliza ever came to this place, and we
feel thankful for the good instructions she
gave us. We held two meetings while
they were here, one with the Relief So-
ciety, and one with the young ladies in the
evening.
	Wishing the EXPONENT success, I re-
main your sister in the Gospel,
								S. M.

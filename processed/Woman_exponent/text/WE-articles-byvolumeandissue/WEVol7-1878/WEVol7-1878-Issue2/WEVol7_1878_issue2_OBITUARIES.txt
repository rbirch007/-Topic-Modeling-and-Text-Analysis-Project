OBITUARIES.

						April 19th, 1878.
	MRS. MARY Annie Harris Bowen, the be-
loved wife of Bro. Lewis Bowen, aged sixty-
five years. She was baptized in the Abersy-
chan branch of the Herefordshire Conference
in 1853, and emigrated to Utah in 18[--]. She
was the mother of twelve children, seven of
whom are living, six sons, and one daughter.
The home of Bro. Bowen in Wales. was ever
open to the Elders on missions, and in add-
tion to the care of a large family, Sister Bow-
en took great pleasure in administering to their
wants for the Gospel's sake. Her faith was
firm in this Latter-day work; she bore her
sickness with great calmness and serenity, and
fell asleep in the full hope of a glorious resur-
rection.							COM.

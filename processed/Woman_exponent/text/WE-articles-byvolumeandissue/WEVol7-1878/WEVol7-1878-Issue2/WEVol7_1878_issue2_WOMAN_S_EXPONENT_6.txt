WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


For the EXPONENT.
OBEDIENCE TO PRINCIPLE.
Oh, may the Lord inspire our hearts.
Help us to yield to His decree;
His Spirit now to us impart,
That we obedient may be.

The time will come when we'll rejoice.
Though p'raps with sorrow now bowed down:
Oh, listen to the still, small voice,
The conqueror shall wear a crown.

Then surely, sisters, we'll not shrink
From what our Father says is pure;
Oh, let us now begin to think
How great our joy, if we endure.

If we are faithful, we shall stand
With those who've nobly borne the cross
If we oppose, we'll understand
To disobey will be our loss.

Then; sisters, let us lift our hearts
To God, who hears and answers prayer:
Call on His name, trust in His grace,
That we the victor's crown may wear.

THE APPROACHING ELECTION: On Monday
Aug. 5th inst. the sisters who are qualified vot-
ers should deposit their votes in the respective
precincts to which they belong, making prop-
er use of the "elective franchise," which has
been conferred upon the women of this Terri-
tory,and which should be held as a sacred right,
never at any time esteeming lightly the oppor-
tunity given through this, of representation.
The Hon. Mr. Courtney, member of the House
of Commons, in a speech before that Honora-
ble body June 19, 1878, upon women holding
office,said, "We must look at results in order to 
arrive at just conclusions, and of all the reports
made of the effect of women holding responsi-
ble positions, the evidence proves that woman
has performed her work creditab[-]y and thor-
oughly; one instance only to the contrary, and
the only testimony which could be produced,
when the question was asked, why was it not
successful, was "Because," which being inter-
preted, meaneth no reason at all.

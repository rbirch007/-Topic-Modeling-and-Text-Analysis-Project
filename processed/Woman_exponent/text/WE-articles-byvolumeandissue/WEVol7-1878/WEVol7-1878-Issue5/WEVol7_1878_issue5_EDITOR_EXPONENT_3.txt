EDITOR EXPONENT:

	We hold our meetings once in two weeks,
and a spirit of union prevails. On the 24th
of June Miss E. R. Snow and Mrs. Zina D.
Young of your city, Mrs. M. A. Hyde of
Spring City, and Mrs. Amelia Goldsborough
of Nephi, paid us a visit and held meeting.
The Spirit of God was poured out upon
them, and we had truly a time of rejoicing
in listening to the good counsel that flowed
from their lips. They were indeed wel-
come visitors, and we hope their first visit
will not be their last.
	Our financial condition is as follows:

	Wishing you all success in the good work
you have begun, and may God bless all His
faithful Saints, is the earnest wish of your
sisters in the Gospel.

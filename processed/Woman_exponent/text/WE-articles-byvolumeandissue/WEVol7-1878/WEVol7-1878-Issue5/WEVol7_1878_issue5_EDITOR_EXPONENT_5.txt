EDITOR EXPONENT.

	The second Quarterly Report for the year
1878. We meet once in each month and

[Column 3]
proceed with our meeting in the usual
manner. All who attend meetings seem to
take a lively interest in them, and express
a determination to do all they can for the
advancement of this great work. Very
few take the EXPONENT, but they are de-
termined to subscribe for it, as it is a good
enterprize. We had an excellent treat on
the 19th. Sisters Horne and Heywood
were out here, and gave us some very good
counsel and instructions, as they always
do.

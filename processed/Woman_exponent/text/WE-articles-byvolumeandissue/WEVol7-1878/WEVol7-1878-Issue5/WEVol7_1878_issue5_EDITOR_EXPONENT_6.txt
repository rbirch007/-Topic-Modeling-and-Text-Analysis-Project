EDITOR EXPONENT:

	Though somewhat tardy, on account of
inability to write through laming my hand,
I send you an account of our Quarterly
Conference as regards business matters.
The sisters meet every two weeks, and we
have splendid meetings. Our President,
Mrs. S. A. Petersen, is always on hand for
duty, and has a willing band of officers to
carry out her counsels, which are always
for the building up of the kingdom of God.

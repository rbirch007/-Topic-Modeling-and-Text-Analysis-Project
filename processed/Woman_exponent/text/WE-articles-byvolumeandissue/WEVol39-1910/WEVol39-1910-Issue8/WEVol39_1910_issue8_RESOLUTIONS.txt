RESOLUTIONS.
	Resolutions of respect, by the officers and
members of the Paris Second Ward Relief
Society, to the memory of our departed sis-
ter, Sarah Lindsay, who died March 4th. 1911:
	Whereas, our heavenly Father has seen fit
to take to Himself our beloved sister, who
was always faithful in the cause of right, and
full of charity and humility; and,
	Whereas her position as a counselor in this
Society in the nearly thirty years makes it
fitting for us to record our appreciation of
her untiring labors, therefore,
	Be it Resolver, that we deeply mourn the
loss of a devoted laborer and tender our
sympathies to the children and relatives of
the deceased. 
	Resolved, that in our natural sorrow for her
death, we find consolation in the knowledge
that “all is well with her,” she was prepared
to enter the realms of our Father.
	Resolved, that a copy of these resolutions
be sent to the family and a copy recorded in
the records of this Society.—Com.

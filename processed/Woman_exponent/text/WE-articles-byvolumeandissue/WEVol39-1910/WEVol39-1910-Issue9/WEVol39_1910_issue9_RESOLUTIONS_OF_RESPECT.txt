		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Whereas, our heavenly Father, in His wis-
dom, has taken from our midst our aged and
beloved sister and co-laborer, we wish to
emulate her virtues and noble qualities; there-
fore, be it
	Resolved, that the wisdom and ability and
labors of our beloved sister be held in grateful
remembrance. Also resolved that a copy of
these resolutions be copied in our record of
Beaver West Ward Relief Society, and one
given to the bereaved son, William Pearson,
Beaver, Utah, and one to the Woman’s Ex-
ponent for publication.
				Betsy S. Goodwin,
				Ellen M. Skinner,
				Francis L. Jones,
							Committee.

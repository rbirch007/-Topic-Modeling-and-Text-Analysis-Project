IN MEMORIAM.
			Mary H. Farrar.
	At Beaver, March 27, Mrs. Mary Hayward
Farrer died, after a short illness. She was
born at Derwent, Derbyshire, England,
March 17, 1822, and was the daughter of Jo-
seph Hayward, and Mary Bridge Hayward;
was married to Jonathan Pearson in 1842, and
was the mother of six children, all being dead
except one son, William, who is a resident of
this city. She was baptized on Nov. 6, 1848,
and crossed the plains in John R. Murdock’s
company in 1862, coming to Beaver, where she
has lived until now. She entered into mar-
riage with James Farrer, Dec. 7, 1867, and as
long as her physical condition permitted she
took part in the religious affairs of the com-
munity, being a prominent aid in Relief Soci-
ety work.
	The funeral services were held on Thursday,
March 30.

			EDNA M. TRIMBLE, Secy.


Party At Black foot.
	Last Thursday, March 2, 1911, a very
pleasant surprise was given our President,
Mrs. Catherine Hesse by the members of
the First Ward Relief Society, it being the
anniversary of her birthday.
	After the regular meeting, all were in-
vited into the main hall of the Latter-day
Saint Chapel where a long table was spread
with everything that “goes to make the
heart glad.” A splendid spirit prevailed;
toasts, sentiments and songs made the hours
pass pleasantly.
	Mrs. E. J. Priestly, Counselor to Presi-
dent Hesse in behalf of the members in a
neat speech presented President Hesse with
a beautiful hand painted cracker jar as a
token of the love and esteem in which she is
held by her co-laborers.

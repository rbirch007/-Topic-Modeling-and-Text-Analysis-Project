SOCIAL NOTES FOR FEBRUARY.
	The Daughters of the Revolution gave a
very pretty party and social on the evening
of the 11th in the Assembly Room of the
Bishop’s Building.
	The affair was partly in honor of the
junior society, recently organized and to
bring together the two organizations, the
seniors and juniors, and also a patriotic
party to honor the birthdays of the two
greatest Americans—Washington and Lin-
coln. The decorations were extremely beau-
tiful, the national colors gracefully draped
around the room, and standards and em-
blems of liberty hung over on the walls.
Dancing followed a short program and re-
freshments were served.

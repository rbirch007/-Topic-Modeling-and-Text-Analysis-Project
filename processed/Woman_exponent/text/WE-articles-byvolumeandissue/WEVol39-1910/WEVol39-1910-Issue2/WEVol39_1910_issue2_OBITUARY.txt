OBITUARY.
	Died at Cannonville, Utah, July 6, 1910, Mrs.
Lydia Ann Johnson, beloved wife of Seth Johnson
Sr. She was the daughter of the late Thomas P.
Smith, and his wife Mary Aagard, and was born
Feb. 17, 1847, at Worcester, England, came to
Utah in the early fifties with her parents. Her
mother died when she was eight years old. She
was married to Seth Johnson Nov. 11, 1861, and
was the mother of fourteen children, thirteen of
whom survive her, seven sons and six daughters.
Her love for her family knew no bounds and self-
ishness had no place in her heart. She was pres-
ident of ward Relief Society for 20 years and in
that labor won the greatest love from her associ-
ates. She was a nurse among the sick for years
and acted as teacher in Sunday School and presi-
dent of Primary Association. and was faithful in
all her labors.

				Chorus:
		Glory, glory, hallelujah!
		Glory, glory, hallelujah!
		Glory, glory, hallelujah!
		The light is shining on.

The father and the mother shall unite for truth
	and right:
A little child shall lead them forth to visions fair
	and bright;
The home will be more holy and the world have
	less of night,—
		The light is shining on.

Equality and freedom is the gospel of today,
Throughout the world it’s spreading with an
	ever widening ray,
Fraternity the guiding star that leads the Christ-
	like way,—
		The light is shining on.

Then keep the watch-fires burning for the long 
	and dreary night.
Will change to golden morning, lo! the dawn is
	breaking bright,
And soon the noon of glory will flame out for
	human right,—
		The light is shining on.

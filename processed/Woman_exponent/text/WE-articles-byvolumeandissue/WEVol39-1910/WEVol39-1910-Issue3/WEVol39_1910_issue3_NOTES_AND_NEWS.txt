NOTES AND NEWS.
	Mrs. O. H. P. Belmont sailed for Eng-
land June 29. She plans to study the ad-
ministrative methods of the three largest
Suffrage Associations.
	The bill giving universal municipal suf-
frage to women, which passed the lower
house of Parliament in Norway, 71 to 10,
has now passed the upper House, 24 to 7,
and has been signed by the king.

THE LIGHT IS SHINING ON.
		By Mrs. Carine L. S. Rose.
	Tune: “Battle Hymn of the Republic.”

I have seen the grand uprising that is spreading
	through the land,
It heralds the good tidings that the kingdom is
	at hand,
The day when men and women in full comrade-
	ship shall stand,—
		The light is shining on.

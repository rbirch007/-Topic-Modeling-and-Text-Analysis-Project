RECOMPENSE.
I care not when night comes on
	Or how the storm may rage,
If God my Light in darkness is;
	His peace my soul engage.
		He will be there!
I care not when sorrows come
	And grief to rend my soul—
If He my comfort e’er will be,
	And keep me in His fold.
		My cross He’ll bear!
I care not when sickness racks
	And fills my days with pain.
If He with hope will buoy me up
	Perchance of health again,
		His will to do.
But if His wisdom rules it so,
	My chastening shall extend—
I’ll love and trust Him thro’ it all
	And to His will I’ll bend.
		O keep me true!
I care not when death draws near
	If only I can know
His hand will safelv guide me o’er
	And keep me while I go.
		He will sustain!
And when at last, my journey o’er
	I’m safe from pain and sin,
I care not, if I can be
	Beside Him in Eternity;
		My God my King!
				—Hattie C. Jensen.

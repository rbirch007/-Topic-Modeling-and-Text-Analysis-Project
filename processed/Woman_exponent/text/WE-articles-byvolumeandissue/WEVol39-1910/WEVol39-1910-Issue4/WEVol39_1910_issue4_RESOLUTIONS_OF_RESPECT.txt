RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	It having pleased our Heavenly Father to
call home our beloved Sister Bathsheba W.
Smith,
	Be it resolved, That we, the members of the
Relief Society of the Seventeenth ward, Salt
Lake City, while feeling to bow to His will,
extend to the family of our dear sister our sym-
pathy in their loss. We feel that their loss is
ours.
	In the ward in which she lived and labored,
first as Treasurer and afterwards as President
of the Society, she was honored and beloved.
	Her presence was ever welcomed with joy,
and her advice was an inspiration to all to
follow in her footsteps and work for the good
of humanity.
					Fidelia S. Jacobs,
					Johannah Broberg,
					Mary Garrett,
					Lydia Y. Merrill,
					Abbie H. Cowley,
					Harriet Y. Morris,					
					Elizabeth A. Hayward.
Oct. 8, 1910.

NOTES AND NEWS.
	Mrs. Carrie Chapman Catt continues to
improve. She has left the hospital for her
home, at 2 w. 86th Street, New York City,
and she expected to be able to dispense with
a trained nurse about the time this paper
will reach our readers. The latter part of
July, if she is strong enough, she hopes to
go away to some couutry place for the sum-
mer.

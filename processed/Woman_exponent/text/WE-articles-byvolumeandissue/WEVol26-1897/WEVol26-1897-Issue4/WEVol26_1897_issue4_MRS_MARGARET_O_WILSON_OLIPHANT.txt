MRS. MARGARET O. WILSON OLIPHANT
author, born in 1828, who died June 26,
was buried in Eaton cemetery almost under
the shadow of Windsor Castle. Queen
Victoria sent a wreath of flowers and the
inscription “A mark of admiration and re-
spect from Victoria, R. I.” Shortly before
her death Mrs. Oliphant completed a
personal life of the Queen. Mrs. Oliphant
generally wrote at night after her family
and guests had retired, and she frequently
wrote until early in the morning.

OUR NEAR NEIGHBORS.

	POCATELLO seems to be advancing along
the lines of moral reform. When it was
announced that the anti-gambling law
does not apply to drinks, nickle-in-the-
slot machines and other devices for fleecing
the unwary, the city council of Pocatello
promptly passed an ordinance prohibiting
them.
	GRADUALLY women are beginning to
come to the front in matters pertaining to
the education of children. In Bear Lake
County three women have recently been
elected school trustees. Mrs. W. C. Cole
was elected in Paris; Mrs. Joseph Jones in
Montpelier, and Mrs. James E. Hart in
Bloomington. We sincerely hope these
good women will show that they have a
higher appreciation of the responsibilities
attaching to the office of school trustee than
most men have. Men often do not become
enthusiastic workers in an office which
pays nothing but honor.

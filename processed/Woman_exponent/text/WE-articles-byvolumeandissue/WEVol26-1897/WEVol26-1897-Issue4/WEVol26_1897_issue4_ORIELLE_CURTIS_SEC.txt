			ORIELLE CURTIS, Sec.


MISS MARIA LOUISE POOL is one of the
small band of New England women writers
who in the past few years have done so
much strong and original work. Her first
novel “Roweny in Boston," showed skill-
ful handling, and its local coloring made it
a story of unusual interest. Miss Pool was
born in Rockland, Mass.
	THE medical department of the Illinois
State University has decided to open its
doors to women.

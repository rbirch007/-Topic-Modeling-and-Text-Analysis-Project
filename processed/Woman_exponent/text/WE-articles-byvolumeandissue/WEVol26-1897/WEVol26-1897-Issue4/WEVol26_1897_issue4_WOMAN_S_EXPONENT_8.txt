WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


MISS JESSIE LIVERMORE, of Chicago, is
a successful designer of art glass. In a
competition of seven of the leading artists 
of the country, she has secured the work of
designing the art window for the elegant
new residence being erected by Mr. and
Mrs. Gougar at La Fayette, Ind.
	THE Business Committee of the N. A.
W. S. A, at its meeting held last week at
Adams, Mass., transacted a good deal of
important business. It was decided that a
large National Suffrage Bazaar should be
held in the fall of 1898, to raise funds for
the work. The place was not definitely 
fixed, but will probably be New York
City.

	MR. HENRY F. FERNSTROM and Miss
Pearl Russell were married on Thursday,
Jan. 4, 1898 at one p. m. in the Salt Lake
Temple by Bishop John R. Winder. Both
bride and bridegroom are quite young and a
very handsome couple. The young man is
the son of the City Councilman, F. S. Fern-
strom, and the girl is the daughter of the
late Robert Russell and his wife, Myra
Young Russell. Miss Pearl Russell is the
granddaughter of the late Joseph Young,
President of the Seventies.
	A few relatives and friends assembled at
the residence of the bride’s mother where a
fine and delicious dinner was served and
congratulations were tendered the happy
young people. The bridegroom expects to
leave on a mission to Scandinavia on the
22nd inst. The “Exponent” tenders
hearty and sincere congratulations and every
good wish for future happiness.

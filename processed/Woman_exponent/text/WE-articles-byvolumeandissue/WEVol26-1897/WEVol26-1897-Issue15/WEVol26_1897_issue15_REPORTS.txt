REPORTS.

	THE Secretary of each Stake organizations
of the Relief Society will please send statis-
tical and financial report of her respective
Stake for the year 1897 as soon as possible
the General Secretary, Mrs. E. B. Wells,
Templeton, Salt Lake City. Branches of
the Relief Society not included in any of
the Stakes of Zion will also send annual
reports so that the secretary may be able to
prepare a complete and correct report of the
Society in good time for the April con-
ference.
			E. B. Wells, Secretary.

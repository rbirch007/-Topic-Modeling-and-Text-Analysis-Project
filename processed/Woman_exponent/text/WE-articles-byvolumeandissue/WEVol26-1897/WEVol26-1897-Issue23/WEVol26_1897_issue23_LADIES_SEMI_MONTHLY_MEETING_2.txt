LADIES’ SEMI-MONTHLY MEETING.

	MINUTES of the Ladies’ Semi-Monthly
Meeting held in the 14th Ward Hall,
Saturday May 27, 1897, President M. I.
Horne presiding. Singing “ Once more
we come before our God;” prayer by Sis-
ter Agnes M. Jones, singing “Welcome
best of all good meetings;” minutes of
previous meeting read and accepted.
	President Horne felt glad to be with us
today, and was sure those who stayed
away were the losers. Heard a married
man say he did not want his wife to give
up her public duties for they made her a
better woman. No one can take a bless-
ing away from us which we earn, therefore
we should be comforted, for God is just.
Said we should be kind to one another and
spoke on charity. We should always be
humble and if we injure any one go to that
person and make it right, or if any one in-
jures us, to do the same; by doing this we
place ourselves in the hands of the Lord;

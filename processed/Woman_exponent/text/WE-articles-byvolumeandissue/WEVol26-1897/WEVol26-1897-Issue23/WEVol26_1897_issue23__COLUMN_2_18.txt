	THE commission of the Nashville Cen-
tennial Exposition have designated October
25, as Woman’s National Press Association
Day. It proposed not only to have the W.
N. P. A. send a delegation of speakers, but
women’s press clubs are invited to have
excursions to Nashville at that time, and
take part in the exercises.

learns how best to help himself.

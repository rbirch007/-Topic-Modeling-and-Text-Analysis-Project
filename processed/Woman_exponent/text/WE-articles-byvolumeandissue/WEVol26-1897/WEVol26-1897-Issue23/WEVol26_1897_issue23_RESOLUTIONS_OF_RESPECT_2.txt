Co., State of Maine, on the 28th of September

She always paid her tith s and offerings even to
within a few days of her death. She was a wo-
man of much faith, both for herself and others.
She was healed man  times by the power of faith,
and once when she had been given up by phy-
sicians and it had been reported that she was
dead. She was the mother of nine children,
fifty-five gran children and fifty-one great-grand-

	RESOLVED, That we feel to extend our sym-
pathy to her family in their loss, but we feel

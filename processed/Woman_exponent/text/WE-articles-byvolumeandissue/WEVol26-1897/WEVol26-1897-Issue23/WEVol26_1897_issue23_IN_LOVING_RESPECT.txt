IN LOVING RESPECT.

	To Sister Betty Walker, an old resident of this
place, and a faithful worker in our Relief Soci-
ety, who died June 5, 1897. after a long spell of
sickness. Sister Walker with her husband came
to Ut h in the year 1849, and to Cedar City in the
year 1851, residing here almost entirely ever
since. She has left a large family of growing
sons and dau hters, and had lived to become a
great great grandmother, being eighty-five years
of age. She was true to her religion and her
covenants, looking forward with joyful anticipa-
tion to her reunion with her husband and her
beloved sister who had preceded her to their
home of rest.
		J. H. MACKELPRANG, Cor. Sec'y
Cedar City, June 15.

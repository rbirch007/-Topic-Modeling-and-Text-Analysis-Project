Yet this brave and gifted leader
	Was tender in his love,
 In his care for wife and children
	He was gentle as a dove,
In his character was mingled
	The stern, the fierce, the kind,
Defiant when beset by wrong,
	Yet graceful and refined.

He knew not fear but fear of God,
	Which garnished all his life, 
Where’er that led him there he walked,
	However fierce the strife,

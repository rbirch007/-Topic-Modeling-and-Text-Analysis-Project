NEVER DESPAIR.

WORK on e'en though the way be dark and drear,
And heavy clouds around us gathering,
Cause us to doubt success and fear despair,
Have faith, God holds in trust a better day,
And surely, sunshine is beyond the c ouds;
They soon will break and let the sunshine
	through;
A glorious vision then we'll surely see,
When peace and comfort all the world will find;
And rum no more shalf rule God's beauteous land.
Oh! how we'll sing our hallelujah then,
And make the glad world with the good news
ring.
Each one de[-]pairing weakens other's hope,
And makes it harder still to win the cause,
Fight on and never give a good cause up,
Until the battle we can win and shout,
A glorious victory for the cause of God.
I would not have you fight with sword or gun,
That is a barbarous way of olden times;


But God has taught us better, surer ways;
If we obey His orders and commands,
He then will teach us how to win His cause,
	God ne’er forsakes, if man will just believe,
Have faith and trust and work along with Him,
And then in time His cause is sure to win,
If we’ll but keep our shou der to the wheel,
With patience work until God sees the time
Has come, when we sha l victory proclaim.
But never lose our faith and cease to work;
For that would but delay the happy day,
When we can shout and make the whole air ring,
Glo y to God, our righteous cause is won.

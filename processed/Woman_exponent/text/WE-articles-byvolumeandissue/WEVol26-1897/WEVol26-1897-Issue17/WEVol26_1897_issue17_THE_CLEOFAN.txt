THE CLEOFAN.

	ON Wednesday afternoon the Cleofan
was highly entertained by Mrs. Annette
W. Culmer who gave an interesting topic
on the life and character of Edmund Spen-
cer, also a bright and instructive paper on
the chief qualities and influences of his
poetry was read by Mrs. Cora Eldredge.
The club adjourned for one week.
	CLARA T. FELT, Cor. Sec. pro tem.

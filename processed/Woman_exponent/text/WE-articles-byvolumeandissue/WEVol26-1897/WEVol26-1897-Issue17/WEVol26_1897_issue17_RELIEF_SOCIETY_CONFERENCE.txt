RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	THE general conference of the Relief So-
ciety will be held on Tuesday, April 5,
1898, in the Salt Lake Assembly Hall in
this city, meetings commencing at 10 a. m.
and 2 p. m. as usual. It is very desirable
that there should be representation from
each Stake organization of the Society, eith-
er the president or one of her counselors
should attend or authorize some one to act
for the board, All members of the Society
are cordially invited, also the Y. L. M. I. A.
and Primary. Presidents, Bishops and
brethren will be most welcome.
	There will be an officers’ meeting on
Saturday, April 9, at the same place, com-
mencing at 10 a. m. and all officers of the
Society are urgently requested to attend.
ZINA D. H. YOUNG
President.
JANE S. RICHARDS
BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
Counselors.

REEIEF SOCIETY ENTERTAINMENT

	ON Feb. 22 an entertainment was given
in the 8th ward under the auspices of the
Relief Society in honor of the president,
Catharine K. Palmer, to which all the adult
members of the ward were invited. There
was an excellent program under the direc-
tion of J. M. Chamberlain. The Bishop
and his counselors were present, also sever-
al who were once members of the ward.
Refreshments were served and a very en-
joyable time was spent.

ON HER SIXTIETH BIRTHDAY, FEB. 8, 1898.
Sixty years! And still so active;
	Trudging cheerfully about;
Making Gospel Truth attractive,
	By dispelling gloom and doubt.
This, today, we witness here,
In your presence, sister dear.
Exercising faith and meekness,
	Hopeful ever—doubly blest;
Charity for other’s weakness—
	First and greatest, last and best;
In your life all these we find,
Sister well beloved and kind.
Your affectionate friend,

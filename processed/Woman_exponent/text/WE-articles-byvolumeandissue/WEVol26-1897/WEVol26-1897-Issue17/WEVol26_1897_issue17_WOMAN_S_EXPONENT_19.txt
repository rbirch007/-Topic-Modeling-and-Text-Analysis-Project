WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


I HEARD the gentle murmur
   Of voices strange and sweet;
And the indistinct low music 
   Of hosts of marching feet.
And then I heard the murmur
   Of a thousand voices say;
“All hail sweet sacred morning!
   Rejoice! ’tis Easter day?
Awake! awake, O nature!
   O joy-bells loudly ring!
Awake for Christ is risen,
   And praise is due our King”

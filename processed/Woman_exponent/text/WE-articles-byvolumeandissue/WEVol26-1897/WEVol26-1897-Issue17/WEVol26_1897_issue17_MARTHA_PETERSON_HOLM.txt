MARTHA PETERSON HOLM:
	Sister Martha Peterson Holm, daughter of
Peter and Mari Ane Christesen, was born March
21, 1849, in Hytter, Denmark. She emigrated to
Utah in 1873, and was married to Andrew Holm
at Salt Lake City Nov 11, 1873 and lived at Lake
Shore where she resided at the time of her death,
March 9, 1898. She was a teacher in the Relief
Society of Lake Shore, a faithful Latter-day Saint
and was loved and respected by all who knew her.
She leaves a kind husband and five children, four
daughters and one son to mourn her loss.

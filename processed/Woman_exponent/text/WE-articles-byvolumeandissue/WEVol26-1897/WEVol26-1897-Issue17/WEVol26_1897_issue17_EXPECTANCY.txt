EXPECTANCY

LET not the arms of expectancy,
Fold me in embraces of ecstacy,
That proves to be on’y a fantasy—
That fills my heart with a vacancy,
'T would long in its woeful exigency,
To soar to a clime of complacency,
And there heal the heartfelt discrepancy,
By love, which completes all deficiency,
And brightens the chain of dependency,
And over all things gains ascen[-]ency.
Think not there’s a dearth of pudency,
Because of a want of potency.
Bnt trust and believe in my fervency,
For therein rests all my quiescence C.
	HYACINTH.
Feb. 21, 1898.

UTAH HiSTORICAL SOCIETY.

	THE following constitute the officers and
board of control of the Utah Historical
Society: Franklin D. Richards, president;
Isabella Cameron Brown, vice-president;
Jerrold R. Letcher, recording secretary;
James T. Hammond, corresponding secre-
tary; Lewis S. Hills, treasurer; Antoinette
B. Kinney, librarian, and Joseph Geoghe-
gan, Electa Bullock, C. C. Goodwin, R. C.
Lund, L. W. Shurtliff, H. W. Lawrence,
G. W. Thatcher, Joseph T. Kingsbury and
John T. Caine.
	The standing committees are:
	Membership—R. W. Young, John Henry
Smith, Emma J. McVicker, H. G. Whit-
ney, and Angus M. Cannon.
	Ways and Means—Henry W. Lawrence,
John E. Dooly, Heber J. Grant, Robert C.
Lund and G. W. Thatcher.
	Printing and Publishing—John T. Caine,
C. C. Goodwin, John Q. Cannon, Alfales
Young and B. F. Cummings Jr.
	Library and Museum—Nat M. Brigham,
Spencer Clawson, Emmeline B. Wells,
George Q. Coray and Chris Diehl.
	Grounds, Buildings and Property—Heber
M. Wells, Robert N. Baskin A. O. Smoot,
Elias A. Smith and Charles R. Savage.
	Lectures, Historical Papers and Address-
es—Joseph T. Kingsbury, Harry C. Hill,
Ellen B. Ferguson, Willard Done and C.
W. Penrose.
	Memorials—O. F. Whitney, Aquilla
Nebeker, Eurithe K. La Barthe, Joseph
Geoghegan and Alexander C. Bishop.
	Special Committee on Historical Jubilee
Relics, etc—John Q. Cannon, Heber M.
Wells, Antoinette B. Kinney, Spencer
Clawson, Edward F. Colborn, Ellen B.
Ferguson, and H. G. Whitney.

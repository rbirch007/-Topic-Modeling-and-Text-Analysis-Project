Spoke of the necessity of each one building

warded for that which we do ourselves and
not for that which our fathers and mothers
do. Let us open up our hearts and not be
afraid to offer kind and encouraging words

	President William Budge said that in a
conference of this kind our minds were
drawn out towards God and His work, and
for a time we forget those things of less im-
portance. Said the sisters should economize
their time that they may attend their meet-
ings and thereby receive a blessing. Spoke
at some length upon influence and related
circumstances showing the necessity of pos-
sessing the right spirit, that our influence
may be felt for good.
	Minutes were read and approved. Sing-
ing “O say what is truth.” Benediction by
President William L. Rich.

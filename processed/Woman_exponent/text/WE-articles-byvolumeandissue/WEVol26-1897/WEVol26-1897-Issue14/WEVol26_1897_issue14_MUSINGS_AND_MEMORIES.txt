MUSINGS AND MEMORIES.

	MRS. E. B. WELLS book of poems so
long promised has been issued and is now
ready for distribution to those who have
subscribed, and is also for sale at the office
of the “Woman's Exponent,” Templeton
Block, opposite the Deseret News Building
on South Temple.
	The book contains over three hundred
pages and a steel engraving of the author.
Musings and Memories is handsomely
bound and the Sego Lily on the outside
gives an artistic finish to its appearance. It
will make an appropriate Christmas or New
Year’s gift. The prices are, for leather
bindings $2.00, cloth in lilac or green,
white and gilt $1.50, cloth in one color
$1.25. All have the steel engraving and
the sego lily. As there is only a limited
number in the edition those who wish them
should purchase immediately.

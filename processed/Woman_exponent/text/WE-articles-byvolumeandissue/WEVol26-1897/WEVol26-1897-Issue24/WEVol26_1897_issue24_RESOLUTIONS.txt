RESOLUTIONS
	Resolutions of respect passed by the Snowville
Relief Society in memory of their beloved presi-
dent, Sister Mary Goodliffe.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father in His a wise
providence has seen fit to take from our midst by
the hand of death our beloved president, sister
and co-laborer, Sister Mary R. Goodliffe, be it
	Resolved, That we the members of the Snow-
ville Relief Society extend our sympathy to the
bereaved family of our worthy sister in this hour
of affliction and bereavement.
	Resolved, That we strive to emulate her many
noble deeds and cherish in our hearts that love
for her which she so truly won.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
presented to the bereaved family, a copy be in-
serted in the records of the Society and one sent
to the “Woman’s Exponent” for publication.

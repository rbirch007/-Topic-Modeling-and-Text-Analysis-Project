RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has seen fit in
His alwise providence to take from our midst by
the hand of death our beloved sister, Sarah A
Sheffield, be it
	Resolved, That we the members of the Relief
Society of Kaysville extend our heartfelt sym-
pathy to the bereaved family in this sad hour of
trial.
	Resolved that we strive to emulate her many
noble deeds and cherish in our hearts that love
for her which she so truly won.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
presented to the bereaved family, one be placed
upon the record of the Relief Society and one
sent to the “Exponent” for publication.

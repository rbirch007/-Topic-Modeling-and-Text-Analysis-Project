DELEGATE FROM UTAH.
	Mrs. Hannah Sorensen, director of Moth-
ers’ Conference in Salt Lake City, Utah,
made an interesting address on the “Physi-
cal Side of Motherhood.” She said she
regarded woman, in her mission as a moth-
er, as the mediator between God and man
in the greatest function of creation. The
young women and the men of today who
marry and take upon themselves the vast
responsibilities which are to last not only
through this world, but through eternity,
have no conception of what they owe to
God and to themselves. Marriage is em-
braced without an iota of knowledge of the
physical environments that make mother-
hood a glory and a song. Girls should be
educated to a proper knowledge of the re-
sponsibilities they are to undertake when
they embrace matrimony. The physical re-
demption of women would mean the glori-
fication of mankind.

SARAH A. SHEFFIELD.
	Sister Sarah A. Sheffield, daughter of Samuel
and Mary Willmer was born at Olney, Bucking-
hamshire, England, Jan. 12, 1831, joined the
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the
year 1848, and two years later was married to
James Sheffield. In 1873 they emigrated to Utah
and located in Brigham City where they resided
for eight years, they then moved to Salt Lake
City remaining there for two years and in 1884
moved to Kaysville where she resided until the
time of her death, having died March 12, 1898.
She leaves a husband and six children and a
host of loving friends to mourn her loss. She
was a faithful Latter-day Saint and for a number
of years was treasurer of the Relief Society of this
place, which office she held at the time of her
death.

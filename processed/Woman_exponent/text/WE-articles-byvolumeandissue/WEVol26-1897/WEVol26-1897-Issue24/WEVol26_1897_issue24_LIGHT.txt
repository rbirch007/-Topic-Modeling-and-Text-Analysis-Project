Till we, too, shall reply to the roll call one day.
And there with the ransomed, the pure and the
holy,
With Christ and the angels forever to dwell,
We’ll greet Frances Willard and dear Mrs. Bailey,
And the white ribbon sisters we all love so well.
CHORUS.
Then let us, oh, let us be faithful my sisters,
And prove ourselves worthy of leaders so true.
JENNIE AMES PACKARD.
East Winthrop, May 3, 1898.

And then ’tis thy boast, thou canst muster a host
	Of such boys so true-hearted and brave,
Who never know fear, and when danger is near,
	Would fight till they died thee to save.

Thou art honored above all the world, we can
		prove,
	For here dwell the wisest and best, 
Who commune with the Lord and receive of His
		word,
	And an Ensign have raised in the West.

And to thy peaceful home, Zion’s children will
		come,
	And all nations shall hear of thy fame,
Highly honored art thou, to thy mandates we’ll
		bow,
	And our hearts shall exult in thy name.
								E. B. W.
Great Salt Lake City, Dec. 1850.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Whereas, Our beloved sister and co-laborer,
Sister E W. Hawkins, has been called from our
midst by the hand of death, and
	Whereas, She was a faithful member and teach-
er in the Relief Society, performing her part
cheerfully as long as her health would permit,
therefore be it
	Resolved, That we mourn her loss as a kind-
hearted a[-]d generous member in our Relief Soci-
ety and a noble woman in our community.
	Resolved, That we cherish in our hearts a lov-
ing remembrance of her many virtues and strive
to emulate her good example.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
placed upon the record of the Relief Society, a
copy given to the bereaved children and one sent
to the “Woman’s Exponent” for publication.
ALICE M. T. TANNER,
MARY F. WHITE,
Committee.
Beaver City, Utah, 1898.

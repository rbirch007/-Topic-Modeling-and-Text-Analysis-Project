A PLEASANT GATHERING
A surprise party inn honor of the thirty-
eighth anniversary of the birthday of Sister
Margaret Ballard, President of the Second
Ward Relief Society, Logan, Cache Co., was
given April 14th, at her residence.  She was
also presented with an elegant album and a
pair of vases, in behalf of the teachers of the
Relief Society.  Sister Ballard in terms of
gratitude expressed her heartfelt thanks and
appreciation for the honor and esteem which
the sisters entertained for her:  Then followed
songs, recitations and speeches, all of which
were appropriate for the occasion, and a very
enjoyable time was spent which will be long
remembered by all.
				EMELINE E. JAMES
May 9th, 1884.

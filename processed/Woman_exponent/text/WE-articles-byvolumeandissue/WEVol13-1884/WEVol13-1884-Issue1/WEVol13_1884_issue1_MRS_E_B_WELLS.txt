MRS. E. B. WELLS:

	The enclosed copy of a dream is received in
response to a request of mine, after hearing it
related.
	Although not much of a dreamer, and being
rather subject to attribute less importance to
dreams than many others, I was much interest-
ed in the one in question, and solicited a copy
for publication in the EXPONENT. Of course
you will act your pleasure in regard to it.
	Lovingly,

	PLESSINGTON, SECORA CO., NEW MEXICO.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We are a small settlement here, and there
are but few of us. This is a very nice little
place. We have a Relief Society, also a Prim-
ary Association, and the children have im-
proved a little, but there is plenty of opportun-
ity for improvement. We have regular meet-
ings and Sabbath Schools. We have no school
house but there is one started, and we are in
hopes of celebrating the Christmas holidays in
it. This town is improving in buildings. There
is nice farming land here; we raise everything
that is put in the ground in the shape of eat-
ables, all except fruit.
	Your loving sister in the Gospel,
	IDA I. WARDEN.
	November 17, 1884.

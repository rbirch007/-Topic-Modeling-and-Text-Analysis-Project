KOOSHAREM, Jan. 11, 1885.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Thinking that you would like to hear from
us in this remote valley, we will give you a
brief sketch of what we are doing.
	Our Society is in as good a condition as we
can wish for; we have a good substantial log
house and a granary attached to it, which will
accommodate us for several years, which has
been dedicated. The sisters all seem to be
alive to their duties and are willing and on
hand to help roll forth this great and mighty
work which we are engaged in,
	Wishing the EXPONENT success, for it is a
welcome visitor in our midst.
	Respectfully,
	MARGARET ANDERSON, Pres.

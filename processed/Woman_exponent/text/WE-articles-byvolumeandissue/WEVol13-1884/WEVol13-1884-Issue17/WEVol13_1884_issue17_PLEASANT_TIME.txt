PLEASANT TIME.
	On Monday, January 26th ult., a very enjoy-
able time was spent at the residence of Sister
Ware’s. Many sisters of the 19th and other
wards met together to celebrate her 79th birth-
day, it having been the custom to do so on that
occasion for the past ten years. The time spent
there together was profitable and instructive.
	The afternoon was spent in singing, prayer
and speaking of the goodness of God to His peo-
ple in days that are past and up to the present
time, when all bore a faithful testimony to the
truth of the Gospel. It was a time long to be
remembered.
	After the meeting was over all sat down to a
bountiful repast. The picnic was brought by
the sisters.
	I will just say I wrote a poem, the words of
which past through my mind the previous
evening, and I penned them down as they came
to me, and read them at the party.

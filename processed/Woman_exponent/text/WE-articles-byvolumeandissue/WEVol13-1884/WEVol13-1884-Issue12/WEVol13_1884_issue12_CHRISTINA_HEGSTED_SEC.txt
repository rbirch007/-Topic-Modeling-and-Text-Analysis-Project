﻿“The Boston Art Club have decided to give
$2,000 in prizes at current exhibition; $350 for
the best picture by a Massachusetts artist, and
the same for the best by an American artist;
three prizes of $150 each for the three next
best works, and five of $70 each for other meri-
torious pictures, all by native artists. The
balance will form a fund toward the donations
of prizes for next year."


﻿“Women are formed for attachment. Their
gratitude is unimpeachable. Their love is an
unceasing fountain of delight to the man who
has once attained it and knows how to deserve
it. But that very keenness of sensibility
which, if well cultivated, would prove the
source of a man’s highest enjoyment may grow
to bitterness and wormwood if he fails to attend
to it or abuses it.

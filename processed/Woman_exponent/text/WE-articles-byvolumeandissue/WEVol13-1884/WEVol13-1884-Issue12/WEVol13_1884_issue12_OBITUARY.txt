OBITUARY.

	DIED, at Harrisville, Weber Ce., Oct. 25, 1884, Maria,
beloved wife of Neils P. Lee, born January 6, 1847, in
Denmark; baptized by Elder S. Sorenson June 3rd.,
1866; immigrated to Utah 1867, and entered into the
celestial order of marriage May, 1868.  She leaves a hus-
band, seven children, and a host of friends to mourn
her loss.  She was an affectionate wife, a kind and ten-
der mother, a very true and earnest Latter-day Saint,
and a faithful member of the Relief Society, always
ready to sacrifice her own comfort to relieve others.
	The funeral services were held at the schoolhouse,
where apropriate remarks were made by the Bishop
and other brethren, aided by the Spirit of God, comfort-
ing the bereaved family.
	Resolutions of Respect to the memory of Sister Maria
Lee by the Relief Society of West Harrisville:
	Whereas it has pleased our Heavenly Father to take
from our midst by the hand of death our beloved sister,
we feel that we have lost a faithful and worthy member
of the Relief Society.
	Resolved, That we esteem her past faithful labors and
great devotion to duty as worthy the fellowship and
highest respect of us all.
	Resolved, That we deeply sympathize with the be-
reaved family in the loss of a kind wife and affectionate 
mother.
	Resolved, That these Resolutions be spread upon our
records and a copy of the same be presented to the be-
reaved family, also the WOMAN'S EXPONENT for publi-
cation.	MARY HEGSTED, President,

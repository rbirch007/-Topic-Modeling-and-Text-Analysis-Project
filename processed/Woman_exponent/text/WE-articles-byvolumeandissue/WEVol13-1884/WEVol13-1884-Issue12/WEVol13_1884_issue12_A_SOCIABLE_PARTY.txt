A SOCIABLE PARTY.

    The Y. I. M. I. A. of the Eighth Ward
held a social picnic and meeting as a surprise
to the Secretary, in honor of her birthday, Oct.
29th, 1884. After the opening exercises the
President arose and stated the object of the
meeting and presented a quilt to the Secretary
made by the officers and members. Miss S. A.
Hawkins then returned her thanks to all pres-
ent. The names of the committee are as fol-
lows: Counselor S. J. Chamberlain, Chairman,
Lucy Chamberlain, Jennie Smith, Jennie Mor-
tensen, Annie Lynch, Lucy Oakey, Mattie
Sheets, Mary Shank and Mrs. Jennie Young.
Misses Lucy Chamberlain and S. A. Hawkins,
invitation committee.
    After a short programme the following
named sisters made some very good remarks:
Sister E.R.S. Smith, Prest. M.A. Freeze,Sis-
ters Elizabeth Howard and Mattie Horne Tin-
gey, and E. B. Fletcher Prest. of the Relief
Society.
    After meeting those present partook of a
bounteous repast prepared by the young ladies.
			MRS. M. B. LEAVER, Prest.

EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Dr. Ellia R. Ship was the recipient of a very
pleasant surprise party, on the occasion of the
expiration of the first term of her class in ob-
stetric, Saturday Dec. 20th. The lady expressed
herself as being completely surprised for once
in her life.
	The party was managed by the members of
the class. The time passed quickly and pleasant-
ly in social chat, songs, recitations, select read-
ing, essays, etc.
	During the evening a handsome album was
presented to Dr. Shipp, by one of the members,
in behalf of the class, to which she responded in
a few well chosen words of thanks. All then
adjourned to the dining room and partook of a
goodly array of vianda, spread upon the table
by those taking charge of that part of the pro-
gramme. The exercises closed by the reading
of the contents of a budget box, filled with origin-
al pieces, humorous, serious, and all ex-
pressive of the esteem in which the teacher is
held by the students.
	At twelve o'clock the party broke up, all ex-
pressing themselves satisfied with the success of
the undertaking, and each wishing the other a
Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.

EDITOR EXPONENT:
     Through the medium of your valuable paper
I wish to make it known and to testify to the
power of God and the gift of healing.
     For many years I have been a sufferer from a
diseased leg, and at different times have sought
relief from various sources, but without any
beneficial results.  A few months ago my leg
became so much worse that my friends feared
for my life, and I was advised by people again
to seek for medical advice and relief, but having
lost faith in the wisdom of man, I determined
to rely wholly upon the power of God; accord-
ingly the Elders of the Church were called in,
anointed me with oil and administered to me;
the result of which is manifest, for from that
time my leg improved and I am now fully res-
tored to health and am anxious to testify to the
world of the goodness of God towards me.
     It is a consolation for us to know that the gifts
and blessings that existed in the primitive
church are again extant upon the earth and we


have the right to avail ourselves of these privi-
leges. How much more earnest we should
strive to be in promug[-]ing the principles of the
Gospel, so that others also might enj[-]y these
blessings.  My heart is filled with joy and re-
joicing,and with gratitude to God for this mani-
festation of His power.
     Stray copies of your interesting paper some-
times come this way and are read with avidity
by all the members of this small branch of the
Church. We are few in numbers, but are doing 
the best we can; and though we have a bitter
spirit of opposition to contend with, are enjoy-
ing a rich portion of the Spirit of God, and are
looking forward to the time when the Lord will
gather us to the valley, that we may assemble
with His people. and learn more fully of His
ways.
 Ever praying for the welfare of Zion, I re-
main your sister in the Gospel,
				MARY ANN SIMPSON.
Witnessed by
E.S.T. SIMPSON,
ARTHUR PORTER,

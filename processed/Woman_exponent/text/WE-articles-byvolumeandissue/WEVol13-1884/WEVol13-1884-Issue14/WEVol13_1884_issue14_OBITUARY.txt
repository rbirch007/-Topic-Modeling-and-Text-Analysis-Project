OBITUARY.

	It is with a feeling of regret that I write to acquaint
the readers of the EXPONENT of the death of our sister,
Rebecca Jane Hall, who died at Enterprise, Morgan Co.,
November 22nd, 1884, of remittent fever. Deceased was
the mother of seven children, six of whom are living;
the youngest two weeks and four days old.
	She was a member and the Treasurer of the Relief
Society, also Counselor in the Primary Association,
and a useful member of our community.
	She was a faithful wife, a loving mother and a true
friend, and we deeply feel her loss. Though her path-
way has been strewn with thorns she has won the vic-
tory, and has gone to rest. Friends will miss her, but
a bright future is before them at the resurrection morn.

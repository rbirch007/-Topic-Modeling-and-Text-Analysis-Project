RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT

	To the memory of Margaret T. Smoot, (wife of
President A. O. Smoot) Stake President of the
Relief Society of Utah County, who departed
this life September 1st. 1881.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father in His divine
Providence has called away our beloved Presi-
dent, Margaret T. Smoot, after a long life of use-
fulness spent in His Kingdom here upon the
earth, to join those behind the veil;
	Resolved, That while we feel to bow to His
most holy will, we miss our sister from our as-
sociations, and her wise, motherly counsels from
our circles.
	Resolved, That we, as members of the Relief
Society of Utah Stake, will hold her memory
sacred, and strive in our lives to follow her
teachings, and remember her wise counsels, in-
asmuch as she was ever a comforter to those in
distress and a ministering angel at the bedside
of the sick, ever striving to allay suffering and
sorrow wherever she found it.
	Resolved, That we, the Relief Society of Utah
County, do tender our heartfelt sympathy to the
bereaved husband and family in the loss of a
kind and affectionate wife and mother.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
placed upon the records of each society in the
county, and a copy sent to the Territorial En-
quirer and WOMAN'S EXPONENT for publica-
tion.		MARY JOHN,
		RHODA LYMAN,
	C. DANIELS, Secretary.

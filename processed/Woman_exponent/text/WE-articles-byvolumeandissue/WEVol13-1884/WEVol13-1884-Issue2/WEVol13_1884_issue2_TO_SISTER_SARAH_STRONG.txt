TO SISTER SARAH STRONG.

	Decaesed was born September 1st, 1806, York County,
Pennsylvania, was married to Jacob Strong February
28th, 1822, Strongstown, Indiana County, Pa. She
joined the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
under the hands of Apostle Erastus Snow, Oct. 20th,
1836, in Strongstown, Indiana Co., Pa. Immigrated to
Nauvoo, Illinois, Sept. 24th, 1839, was driven with the
Saints to the wilderness, arriving at Salt Lake City,
Utah, Oct., 1849, resided in the Tenth Ward up to the
time of her death, which took place May 9th, 1884, at
the hour of 5.52 p.m
	Whereas, It has pleased the Great Creator to take un-
to Himself our beloved Sister Strong, who had been
President of the Tenth Ward Relief Society for twelve
years, a devoted friend to the poor, and wise counselor
in her family, and,
	Whereas, It is fitting that she be held in honorable
rememberance, therefore be it
	Resolved, By the sisters of the Relief Society of the
Tenth Ward, that while we mourn, not as those who
have no hope, yet we realize that we have lost a faith-
ful President, who died as she had lived, in the hope of
a glorious resurrection.
	Resolved; That these resolutions be put upon the re-
cords of the Relief Society, and that a copy thereof be
sent to the family of our dear sister, and also to the
WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
				ELIZABETH PAUL,
				JEANNIE McLEAN,	Committee.

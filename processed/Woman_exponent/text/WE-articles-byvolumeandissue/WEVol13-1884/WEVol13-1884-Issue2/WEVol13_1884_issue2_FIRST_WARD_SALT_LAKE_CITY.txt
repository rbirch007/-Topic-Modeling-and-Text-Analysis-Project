FIRST WARD, SALT LAKE CITY.

	The young ladies of the First Ward held
their yearly meeting on Wednesday, April
16th, 1884. Some very interesting remarks
were made by Sisters Zina Young, Elmina Tay-
lor, Mary Freeze, Elizabeth Howard, Sarah
Graham, Margaret Steel and Annie Brown.
About forty sisters met at the house of Presi-
dent Graham and partook of a bounteous re-
past, preparatory to starting to meeting.
				FLORA MUSSER, Secretary.

RESOLUTIONS.

	The following Resolutions of Respect to our beloved
sister were unanimously adopted and sustained by the
Young Ladies' Mutual Improvement Association of Vir-
gen City:
	Whereas, God, in His infinite wisdom, has called from
our midst our sister, who was greatly beloved and res-
pected by us all for her kind and gentle disposition, her
pure, virtuous and exemplary life, faithful labors in our
Society, and the many good qualities she possessed,
	Therefore, Be it Resolved, That we, the members of
the Y. L. M. I. A. of Virgen City, do deeply mourn the
loss of our beloved sister, Eveline Short, and that we
condole with her husband and friends in their great loss;
but while we mingle our tears with theirs, we would
point with joy to the hope of a glorious resurrection
which all true Latter-day Saints possess.
	And, Be it Resolved, That we present a copy of these
Resolutions to her husband.
					ELEANOR OWENS President,
					BETSY A. PARKER,
					AGNES RICHARDS, Counselors.

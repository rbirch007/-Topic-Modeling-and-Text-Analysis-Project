OBITUARY.
It becomes our duty to again record the death of one
of our beloved sisters.  Ann Robinson, wife of Charles
Whittaker, departed this life Sept. 18, 1884, at American
Fork, Utah Co., Utah.
	Deceased was born Sept. 19, 1819, at Sawley, Derby-
shire, England.  Embraced the Gospel Dec., 1859; emi-
grated from Nottingham in 1863.
	She was a faithful and efficient teacher in the Relief So-
ciety of this place, and though sorely afflicted for a
number of years, she bore her afflictions with fortitude,
and attended cheerfully to her duties in the Society
whenever her health permitted.
	She leaves a husband, one daughter, several grand-
children and a number of friends to mourn her loss.

OBITUARY.

	DIED, at Fremont, Piute Co., Utah, Augusta L. Tay-
lor, dughter of James A. and Lousia Taylor, Feb. 10,
1885, aged six years, six months and fifteen days. At a 
meeting of the Fremont Primary Association the fol-
lowing resolutions of respect were adopted:
	Whereas, by an irrevocable decree of an alwise provi-
dence, our worthy young sister, Augusta L., has been
taken from our midst,
	Resolved, That we condole with the bereaved family
and friends, in the loss of their dear one, not, however,
without the consoling reflection that she will come
forth in the morning of the first resurrection. 
	Resolved, That this be made a matter of record in the
Primary Association, and a copy be presented to the
parents of deceased, and also sent to the WOMAN'S EX-
PONENT for publication. 
	And, whereas she was always a willing and active 
member of our Association, we, the members of the
same, feel to make and accept these resolutions of re-
spect and condolence.
				MARGARET J. TAYLOR, President,
				SARAH A. ALDER, Secretary.

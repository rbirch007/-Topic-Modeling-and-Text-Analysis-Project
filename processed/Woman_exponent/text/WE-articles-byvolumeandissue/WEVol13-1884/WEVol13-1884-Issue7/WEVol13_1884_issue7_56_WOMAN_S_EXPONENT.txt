56	WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


Secretaries of Relief Society are notified to
send in semi-annual reports by the 15th of Sep-
tember.
	The Stake officers of Y. L. M. I. A. are
hereby officially notified to prepare semi-annu-
al stake reports carefully, upon printed blanks,
and forward them as early as the 15th of Sep.,
that a complete and satisfactory report may be
made to the General Conference in October.
Direct the reports to Mrs. E. S. Taylor, P. O.
Box 629, Salt Lake City, Utah.
					LOUIE M. WELLS.
				Secretary Y. L. M. I. A.

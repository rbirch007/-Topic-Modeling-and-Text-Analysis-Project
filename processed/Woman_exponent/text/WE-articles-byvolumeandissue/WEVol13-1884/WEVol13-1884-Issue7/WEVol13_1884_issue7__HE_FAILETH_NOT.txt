"HE FAILETH NOT."

I have tried love, and I have known love fail;
Have trusted friends, and found that friends forgot;
Sought help from my own heart without avail—
				"He faileth not."

Neither by day or night, in age or youth.
In poverty, and in the fairest lot,
In sorrow and in joy, His word is truth—
				"He faileth not."

If I should let all other comforts go.
And every other promise were forgot.
My soul would sit and sing, because I know
			"He faileth not."

I cannot tell what winds of God may blow,
What safe or perilous ways may be my lot;
But I have little care, for this I know—
				"He faileth not."

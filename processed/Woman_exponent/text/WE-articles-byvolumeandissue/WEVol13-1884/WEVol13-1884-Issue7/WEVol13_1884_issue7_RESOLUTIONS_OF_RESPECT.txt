			RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	We, The members of the Relief Society of Minersville,
unanimously adopt the following resolutions:
	Whereas, It has pleased our Heavenly Father to call
from our midst our beloved sister and friend,
	Therefore, Be it resolved, that we esteem her faithful
labors, and devotion to duty, as worthy the emulation
of us all.
	Resolved, That these resolutions be placed on the
records of the Relief Society, and that we condole and
sympathize with the husband and children, relations
and friends in their bereavement.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be presen-
ted to the family, also that a copy be sent to the EX-
PONENT for publication.
				MARY E. LIGHTNER,
				LYDIA BLACKBURN,	Committee.

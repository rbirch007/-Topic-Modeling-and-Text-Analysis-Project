	BY ROSE ELLEN WHITE. 

Children, mourn not for your mother, 
	For her home is now with God, 
But strive to follow in her footsteps, 
	And hold fast the "iron rod." 

She has filled her earthly mission
	And is wanted up above
To continue in good labors
	Where there is but peace and love. 

Then bow to God and ask for comfort, 
	And that blessing you will gain; 
For your mother's in His keeping, 
	And is free from earthly pain. 

Children, mourn not for your mother, 
	Even though 'tis hard to part, 
For you'll meet again in heaven
	And joy be great within each heart.

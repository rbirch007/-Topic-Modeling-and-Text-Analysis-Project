VERSES COMPOSED BY SISTER HANNAH CORNABY.

Elizabeth has gone and left
	Her bright example here,
And those who knew her faithful life
	Will hold her memory dear.


﻿Although her mortal life was short,
	And much of pain she knew,
In patience she her sufferings bore
	And still was firm and true.

The love of God within her heart
	Made all her duties light,
Now in far happier scenes she dwells,
	And walks in robes of white

With those, who like her, kept the faith
	The Gospel light restored,
Having gained the victory over death
	Through Jesus Christ our Lord.

Oh, happy thought! we, too, may gain
	The presence of our God
If we, by strict obedience here,
	Hold fast the iron rod.

Our sister does not need our tears;
	She seems to say to all,
"Be up and doing, death may come,
	Be ready for his call."

We bow to God our Father's will,
	And humbly ask that He
May comfort all her mourning friends
	That they His hand may see.

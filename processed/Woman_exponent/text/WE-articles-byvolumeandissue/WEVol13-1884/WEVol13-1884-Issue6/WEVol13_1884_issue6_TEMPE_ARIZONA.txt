TEMPE, ARIZONA.

	On the 18th of January, 1884, the Primary
Association of Tempe was organized under
the direction of Prest. D. T. LeBaron with
Vilate E. LeBaron, President; Geneva John-
son and Mamie Hawley, Counselors; Winnie
Johnson, Secretary and Treasurer. We have
fifty names enrolled, with an average attend-
ance of thirty-five. Our programmes are well
carried out, although the members are mostly
under ten years of age. The children are all
willing to do their best. We meet once a week
and all seem to feel well in their labors with
the Primary.
			Your sister in the Gospel,

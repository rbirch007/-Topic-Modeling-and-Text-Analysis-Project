						MAMIE HAWLEY.

	On the 30th of January, 1884, the Y. L. M.
I. A. of Tempe was organized as follows, and
the officers set apart under the direction of
Prest. D. T. LeBaron: S. E. Russell, Prest.;
Zina LeBaron and Mary Johnson, Counselors;
Nettie Johnsen, Secretary; Amelia Rogers,
Treasurer; Jennie LeBaron, Librarian.
	We have twenty names enrolled, with an
average attendance of fourteen. We meet
once a week, and conjointly with the Y. M. M.
I. A. once a month. We have a manuscript
paper, which has been very good; our pro-
grammes are well carried out, and although
our numbers are small we have good meetings
and realize partially, if not fully, the import-
ance of preparing for the work which is ready
and waiting for the Saints to perform.
		Your sister in the Gospel,
			NETTIE JOHNSON, Secretary.

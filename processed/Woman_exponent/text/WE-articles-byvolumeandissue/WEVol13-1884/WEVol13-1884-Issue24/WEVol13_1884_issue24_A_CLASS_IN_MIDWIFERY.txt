A CLASS IN MIDWIFERY
Dr. Maggie C. Shipp
	Will commence a class in her office on May 1, 1885
a term consists of ten weeks.  Two lectures every day,
Saturdays excepted.
Tuition a Term, $10.00.	Books, $13.00.
	Dr. Shipp will remain in the city, and can be found at
her office and residence, 66 S. First West Street.

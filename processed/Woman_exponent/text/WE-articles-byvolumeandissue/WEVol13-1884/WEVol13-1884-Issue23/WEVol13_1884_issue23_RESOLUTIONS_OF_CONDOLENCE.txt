		  RESOLUTIONS OF CONDOLENCE.

	Whereas, Providence has seen fit to call from our midst
our much esteemed sister and co-laborer in the cause
of truth, to finish the labor so nobly begun on this earth
in a higher sphere, we, the members of this Association,
offer our heart felt sympathy to the bereaved parents
and freinds of the deceased, and although her loss is
keenly felt, both as a faithful member of the Associa-
tion and a devoted daughter, we trust the parents will
feel reconciled, knowing that she was taken from a
world of sorrow by a just and merciful God.
	Resolved, That a copy of these Resolntions be placed
upon the records of the Association, and that one be
presented to the bereaved parents.
				M JAMESON,
	Committee.	M. A. MADESN,

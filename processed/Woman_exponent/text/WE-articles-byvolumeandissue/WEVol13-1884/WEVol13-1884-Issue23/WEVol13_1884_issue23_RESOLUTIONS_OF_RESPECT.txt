		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	We, the members, of the Payson Ward, Relief Society,
Utah Stake, U. T., unanimously adopt the following:
	Whereas, God in His wisdom has seen fit to call from
our midst by the hand of death our much loved and 
worthy Secretary, of the 3rd District, and,
	Whereas, Her labors in our Society have been faith-
fully performed, we feel to recognize and respect her
virtues among us; therefore, be it
	Resovled, That we do recognize in the death of our
sister, Fanny Coombs, the loss of a faithful member in
our Society.
	Resolved, That we condole and deeply sympathize

[Column 2]

with the bereaved husband, children, mother, sisters,
relatives and friends.
	Resolved, That these Resolutions be spread upon our
records, that a copy of them be sent to the bereaved
husband, and also the WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
		J. B. SIMONS,
		AGNES DOUGLAS,

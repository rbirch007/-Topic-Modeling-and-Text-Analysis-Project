IN MEMORIAM.

	DIED, at her residence, in Payson Ward, Utah Co.,
U. T., Fanny Coombs, wife of Isaiah M. Coombs, March 
14th, 1885, aged forty-two years, nine months and eight 
days.
	She received the Gospel at Cincinnati, O., where she,
with mother and sisters, were baptized by Elder John
Banks, April 24th, 1855. Deceased was married to
Isaiah M. Coombs July 23th, 1858; was the mother of 
fourteen children, all living except three, who died in
infancy. She embraced and lived up to every principle
that God has revealed for the exaltation of woman
Was endeared not only to her family, but to all who
knew her. Had strong faith in the ordinances of the
Church, and when in the agonies of death sang, "We
thank Thee, O God, for a Prophet."

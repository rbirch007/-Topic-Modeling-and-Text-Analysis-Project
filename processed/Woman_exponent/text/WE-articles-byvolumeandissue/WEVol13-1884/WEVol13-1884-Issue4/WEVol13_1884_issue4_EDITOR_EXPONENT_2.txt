EDITOR EXPONENT:

	Our Primary was organized May 8th, 1883,
Sister Louisa Harper, President; Sister Letitia
E. Jones and Louellia Patterson, Counsel-
ors.
	We hold our meetings every two weeks. Our
President is doing all she can to encourage the
children to attend their meetings. We have
testimony, and also sewing meetings. The
children all take great delight in reading, re-
citing, bearing their testimony, etc. They also
take an interest in this great and glorious work,
and we feel very thankful to our Heavenly
Father that He has permitted us to live in this
day and age of the world, that we might grow
up to be honored mothers in Zion, and do a
great and mighty work in the Kingdom of God,
and to know and comprehend the work that
has to be done on earth.
	This is a small branch, but there are quite a
few children to attend the Primary. We are
improving rapidly. There are twenty-one
names enrolled as members.
		LOUEA B. HARPER, Secreatary,
		MARY J. STEELE, Correspondent.

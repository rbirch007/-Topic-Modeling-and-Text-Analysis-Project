ANNUAL MEETING.

All hail, this day, the First of June,
	Which gave a prophet birth;
Among the noblest ol God’s sons;
	His name shall ne’er have dearth.

The name, oh, yes, of Brigham Young
	Yet in our hearts shall live,
In memory of the wise advice
	He to the saints did give.

Our Primary was organized
	Upon this natal day.
On which we meet to celebrate
	It with harmonious lay.

Then welcome to the First of June,
	Young brothers and sisters dear,
And may we meet from time to time
	With many a happy cheer.


May union join us heart and hand,
	And may we live to see
Yet many celebrations,
	And then have our Jubilee.

OBITUARIES.

  It is with feelings of sorrow that we record the death of
our sister and co-worker, Sister Sarah M. Hawkins, who
died suddenly, May 2nd, 1885, aged forty-eight years and
two months.  Although she had been sick most of the
winter, yet she recovered so far as to be enabled to go to
the Logan Temple to do some work for her dead, and it
seemed to satisfy her that she had been there.  She leaves
a husband, a large family and many friends to mourn her
loss.
	At her funeral the speaker paid her the highest tribute
that could be given to woman, by saying she had been a 
pure, consistent woman in every phase of life.

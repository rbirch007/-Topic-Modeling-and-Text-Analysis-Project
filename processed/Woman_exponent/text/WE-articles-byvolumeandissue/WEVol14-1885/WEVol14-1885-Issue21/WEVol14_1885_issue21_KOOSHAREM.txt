KOOSHAREM.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We thought a few lines from this out of the
way place might be of interest to your valua-
ble paper. Our Relief Society is in a pretty
fair condition and the sisters are all striving
to serve the Lord and are alive to their duties.
we are enjoying good health and feel well in
the Gospel of Christ. We know that our ene-
mies are persecuting us on the right and on
the left, but this will only make Latter-day
Saints more humble and live nearer to the
Lord. It is necessary that trials come in order
that we may be proved, for the Lord says He
wants a tried people. We know the Lord is
on our side, so we have nothing to fear; if we
do our part He will do His, and He will help
us.
		MARGRETTE ANDERSON, Prest.
		JOSEPHA O. WRIGHT, Sec. pro-tem.

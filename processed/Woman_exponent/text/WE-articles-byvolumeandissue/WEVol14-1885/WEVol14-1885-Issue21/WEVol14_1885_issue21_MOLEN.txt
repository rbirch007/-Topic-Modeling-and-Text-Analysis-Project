MOLEN.
	A Relief Society was organized in Molen
Ward, Emery Stake of Zion, Feb. 7th. Fol-
lowing are the names of the officers. Ann
Rasmussen Prest.; Mary L. Hansen and
Dicintha Stringham, Counselors; Esther A. 
Fieldsted, Secretary, Emily Cook, Treasurer.

[Column 2]

All were set apart by the presidency of the 
Stake.
		E. A. FIELDSTED, Secretary.

OBITUARY.

	DIED, at Wanship, Summit Co., Utah, March 24th,
1885, Maria Horton, wife of Edward Horton,aged seventy-
two years. She was born at Batsall, Warwickshire, Eng-
land; joined the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day
Saints in 1851; emigrated to the United States in 1855;
came to Utah in 1860. She died in full faith of the Gos-
pel.

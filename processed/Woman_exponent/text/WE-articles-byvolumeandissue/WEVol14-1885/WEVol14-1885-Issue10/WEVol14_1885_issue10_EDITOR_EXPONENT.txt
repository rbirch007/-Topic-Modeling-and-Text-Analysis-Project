EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We held our Primary Fair at Goshen, on
the 26th of September, which was very inter-
esting, and a credit to those who had worked
so diligently to bring their various articles of
work for examination, and also to those who
took part in the proceedings of the meeting.
The following will give the readers of this
valuable little paper an idea of the work pre-
pared:
	Baskets made of different materials, knit
and chrochet lace and work of all kinds, hair
and wool flowers,artificial strawberries,mottoes,
quilts, drawings, cushions, rugs and mats of
various styles, also cooking and the produce of
the garden and farm, and rakes, forks etc.
from our little boys.
	Prest. Annie K. Smoot, Sisters May Smoot
and Grace Smith visited us. Prest. Annie K.
Smoot gave us some excellent instructions.
There were also songs, recitations, dialogues,
etc., rendered in a pleasing manner by mem-
bers of the Association.
		SARAH ROBERTS, President,
		JULIA M. JASPERSON, Secretary.

		SLATERVILLE.

	The Slaterville Relief Society was re-organ-
ized Nov. 27th, 1885. There were present, the
Bishop and his Counselors, Apostle F. D.
Richards, Sister Richards, Sister Brown, Presi-
dent and Counselors of Weber Stake; also the
Relief Society of the ward. The former
president, Sister Mary Field, having resigned
on account of her great age, the following
officers were appointed: Sister Mary Karney,
President; Mary Jane Allred and Helen Perry,
Counselors; Isabella J. Allred, Secretary;
Charlotte Bartholomew, Assistant Secretary;
Hannah Dasney, Treasurer.
		Respectfully your sisters,
				M. A. KARNEY, Prest.,

AS SEEN BY HOMESPUN.

	One of the commonest and tritest of sayings is,
that there are two sides to a picture. Nothing
could be truer; and as one of my friends remarked
the other day that I saw but one side, and that
generally in an exaggerated form, I shall prove to
her by this letter that I am quite capable of seeing
both sides, if I only choose. So I invite you to

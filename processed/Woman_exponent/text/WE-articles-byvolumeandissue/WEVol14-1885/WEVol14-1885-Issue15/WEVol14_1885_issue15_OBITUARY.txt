OBITUARY.

	In memory of Hyrum Frederick adopted son of Geo. M.
and Margaret A. White, who died December 15th, 1885,
at Mill Creek, aged nineteen months and twenty days.


He was a sweet and interesting child, and while we feel to
mourn his loss, we acknowledge the hand of our Father
and hope to live faithful, that we may again meet our
darling. 							MARY.

	In memory of Bathsheba, youngest daughter of J. F.
and Elizabeth Snedaker, who passed away on the morning
of the 29th of November, 1885, aged eight years and one
month. She was a lovable girl, always interested when
talking of the things of her Heavenly Father, and lived
long enough to receive the blessings of baptism, and all
other blessings it was her right to receive. She was a
faithful member of the Primary, and was ever willing to
respond when called to do anything. For the fair she
worked a beautiful pincushion. Her little associates miss
and grieve her loss. This is the fifth child which has been
taken from their family since June with the same dreadful
disease, diptheria.

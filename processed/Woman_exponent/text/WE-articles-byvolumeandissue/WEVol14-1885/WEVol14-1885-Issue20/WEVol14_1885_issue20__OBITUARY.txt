OBITUARY.
	DIED, at Riversdale, Oneida Co., Idaho, Feb. 20 1886, 
of dropsy, Emma C. Vail, daughter of Peter and Mary
Ann Preece, aged 22 years and 14 days. She was born in 
Franklin, Oneida Co., Idaho, 6 of Feb. 1864, and leaves
one little girl, one month and 24 days old. She was a 
member of the Relief Society and was assistant secretary 
in the same. She died in full faith of the gospel and in 
the hope of a glorious resurrection.

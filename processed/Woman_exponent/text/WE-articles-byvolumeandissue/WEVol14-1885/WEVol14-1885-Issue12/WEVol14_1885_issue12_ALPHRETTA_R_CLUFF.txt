					ALPHRETTA R. CLUFF.

		We must be prepared to meet her,
			And our loved ones who are there—
		There, there is no sin nor sorrow
			In that land so bright and fair.

		But oh, this world is full of sorrow—
			Nothing here but bitter woe;
		But we have a work to finish
			Here on earth, before we go.

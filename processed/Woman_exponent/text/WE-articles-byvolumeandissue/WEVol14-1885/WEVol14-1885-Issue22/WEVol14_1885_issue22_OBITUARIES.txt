OBITUARIES.

	DIED, in Ovid, Bear Lake Co., Idaho, March 19th,
1886, after an illness of twelve days, Mary Jane Skinner
Petersen. She was the eldest daughter of John and Jane
Skinner, and wife of John Petersen. Sister Petersen was
born in Genesee Co., State of New York, Feb. 22nd, 1861.
She leaves a husband and three children, the youngest
only twelve days old, and many relatives and friends to
mourn her loss.
	She was a member of the Y. L. M. I. A., and respected
by all who knew her. She died in full faith of the Gospel,
and in the hope of a glorious resurrection.
		NETTIE S. JOHNSON, Prest.,

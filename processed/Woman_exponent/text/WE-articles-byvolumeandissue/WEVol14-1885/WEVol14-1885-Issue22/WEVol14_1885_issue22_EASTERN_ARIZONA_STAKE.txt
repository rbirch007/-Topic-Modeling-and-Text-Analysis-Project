EASTERN ARIZONA STAKE.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The quarterly conference of the Relief So-
ciety of Eastern Arizona Stake was held at
Snowflake, March 12th, 1886, Prest. Emma S
Smith presiding. After singing and prayer,
the following ward societies were represented
by their respective presidents: Taylor, Show-
low, Woodruff, Snowflake Camp, Erastus, St. 
Johns and Snowflake. All were in a flourish-
ing condition. The sisters were united, and
felt a desire to build up the kingdom. The
statistical and financial reports were read,
showing the different branches to be in a bet-
ter condition than heretofore.
	Coun. Emily Lewis then spoke to the sisters,
giving good counsel and advice on subjects
of interest.
	Prest. Emma S. Smith spoke in regard to
the teachings children should receive from 
their mothers. Was grateful for the good re-
ports, and felt the Spirit of the Lord had been 
with us. 
	After singing, conference was adjourned for
three months. Benediction pronounced by
Bro. Oscar Mann.
		DELLA FISH, Secretary.

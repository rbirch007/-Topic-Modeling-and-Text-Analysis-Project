HOUSEHOLD HINTS.

	For rough hands use lemon juice. 

	Tepid milk and water without soap will clean oil cloth.
	
Mince Meat Without Liquors.—Use three howls of meal,
six of apple, one of suet, one of raisins, one of citron, 
three of molasses, one of appl[-] jelly or any other sour
fruit juice, and spices. 

Rice Pudding Without Eggs.—Take two quarts of milk, 
from one and a half to two teacupfuls of rice, one cupful
of sugar, a cupful of raisins, a piece of butter the size of
an egg, and a little nutmeg.  Bake the pudding two hours.


Cologne Water.—Use oil of lavender one drachm, oil
of bergamot one drachm, oil of lemon two drachms, oil of
rosemary two drachms, fifty drops of tincture of musk,
eight drops of oil of cinnamon, eight drops of oil of cloves,
and one pint of alcohol.
	Fruit Cake. Two cupfuls of butter, three of sugar, and
three and one-half cupfuls flour, eight eggs, one pound of
raisins, one pound of citron, one teaspoonful of cloves, a
teaspoonful of cinnamon, one nutmeg or a little mace, and
one gill of brandy or milk.
	Tapioca Pudding.—Soak two-thirds of a cupful of
tapioca over night, in one quart of milk; then add three
beaten eggs, five tablespoonfuls of sugar, butter the size of
an egg, one whole lemon, grated; after baking for three
quarters of an hour, serve the noodles with milk or
cream.

R. S., Y. L. M. I. A. & P. A. REPORTS.

	MOAB.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Thinking that a brief report from the Relief
Society and Primary Association of this ward
would be of interest to your many readers, I
submit the following:
	On the 7th of November last a meeting was
called by our esteemed Bishop, R. H. Stewart,
for the purpose of re-organizing the Relief So-
ciety and Primary Association of this ward,
which was made necessary by the death of the
President of the former, Sister China Lutz, and
the removal from the ward of the President of
the latter. The following organization was
effected for the Relief Society: Sarah M.
Burnham, President; Priscilla Warner and
Sarah J. Stewart, Counselors; Amanda E.


Taylor, Secretary; Julia A. Pierce, Treasurer.
For the Primary Association, Anna McBride,
President; Clara M. Burnham and Rose Lutz,
Counselors; Lizzie Roberson, Secretary; Ros-
anna Stewart, Treasurer. The officers were
then set apart by the Bishopric.
	It seems the determination of the officers
and members to press on in the cause of truth
and assist in bearing off the kingdom of God.
Although bemeaned and persecuted for the
Gospel's sake, we know in whom we trust, and
that He will wreak vengeance on those who
fight against Zion, and that we will get our re-
ward by yielding obedience to the requirements
of the Gospel.
	Praying for the success of the EXPONENT,
we subscribe ourselves,
		Your sisters,
	SARAH M. BURNHAM, Prest.,

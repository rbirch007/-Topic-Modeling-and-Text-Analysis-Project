MRS. EMILY SCOTT, OF OHIO.

  Your sensible letter, in the EXPONENT of
August the 1st, was interesting to many beside
myself. I was well pleased with it, for I
thought you were a true Democrat and could
see no crime nor disgrace in plural marriage.
You say you think, sometimes, of going to the
Mormons and writing your lecture about the
Gentiles. I would be sure to go, and if you
are afraid of the persecution going on there
now, you can find some very good Mormons in
Alabama that would be glad to see you. Write
again for the EXPONENT, or address,
JOHN M. RUSSELL,
Alabama, August 14, 1885.

OBITUARY.

  It is with feelings of sorrow that we record the death of
Sister Mary Ann Brown Warr, of the First Ward of this
city, who departed this life on Sunday, August 30th, 1885,
in her forty-first year, and we recognize in the death of
our sister the loss of a worthy member and efficient teacher,
and we deeply sympathize with the bereaved family in the
loss of an affectionate wife and mother, and faithful Latter-
day Saint.
Now she's gone, we'd not recall her
	   From a paradise of bliss,
Where no evil can befall her,
	   To a changing world like this.			 COM.

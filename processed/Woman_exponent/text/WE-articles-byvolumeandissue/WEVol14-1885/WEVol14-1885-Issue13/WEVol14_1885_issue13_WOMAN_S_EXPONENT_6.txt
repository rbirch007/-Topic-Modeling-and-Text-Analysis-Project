WOMAN'S EXPONENT


It is proposed to erect a memorial church
to the memory of Mrs. Elizabeth, Fry, the
mother of modern philanthropy.  Plasket
Park, in Essex, a few miles out of London,
was her residence at the time of her memorable
visit to Newgate, and it is proposed to build
the church on the site where her garden once
stood. A large sum has been already contribu-
ted by friends and admirers of Mrs. Fry."

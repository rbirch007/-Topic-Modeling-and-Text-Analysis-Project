EDITORIAL NOTES.

	THE Quarterly Conference of the Relief Society
of Salt Lake Stake will be held in the 14th Ward
Assembly Rooms on Friday, December 11th, com-
mencing at 10 a.m. and 2 p.m. It is earnestly de-
sired that there should be a good representation
from all the wards. Verbal reports are expected
from the respective presidents.


THE Conference of the Y. L. M. I. A. of this
Stake will be held in the Salt Lake Assembly Hall
on Saturday, Dec. 19th, commencing at 10 a.m.
and 2 p.m. This arrangement to have the whole
day for the Conference is in order to give more
time for speaking and spiritual improvement, or
exercises that might be of benefit to all. One
meeting is almost entirely occupied with business
and reports, and therefore leaves very little time
for general instruction. A general invitation is
extended to all interested to attend.
	THE Conference of the Primary Associations
of this Stake will be held in the Assembly Hall
on Saturday, Dec. 12th, commencing at 10 a.m.
and 2 p.m. Preparations are being made in the
Associations in the several wards to make it an
interesting event for all. Special care has been
taken to have the little folks well trained in sing-
ing and other exercises, and it is to be hoped
there will be a large attendance, not only of the
children who are enrolled in the Associations, and
those who have charge of them in the organiza-
tions, but of the parents, to encourage the child-
ren and those who labor for their advancement,
and certainly they are interested in the spiritual
progress of the youth of Zion.

HYGIENIC NOTES.
					---
	WITCH HAZEL.--(Medical properties and uses of the
bark and leaves.)  For neuralgia, take a large teaspoonful
of the dried and pressed leaves, as obtained at the drug
store, and sleep like common tea; using cream and sugar
according to taste.  Try it one night and one morning, and
note the result.
	"Astringent, tonic and sedative, used mostly in decoc-
tion, which is good as an astringent in diarrehea, dysentery,
and in bleeding from the lungs, stomach, and urinary
organs, taken freely interanlly; as a wash to old and foul
ulcers; as an injection in Leucorrhoea, flooding, and falling
of the womb, and as a gargle and wash in sore mouth and
throat.  Dosed of the decoction, from half to a teacupful
three or four times a day."

THE SECOND WIFE TO HER HUSBAND IN PRISON.

Dear Friend, when last, a little space,
Yourself and I stood face to face,
My heart was full of fear and pain.
My eyes o'erflowed with bitter rain—
You laid your hands upon my head
And low and fervently you said.
	"God bless you!"

Oh, sweet and low the words did flow, 
As summer breezes gently blow
But broad as earth, deep as the sea,
And high as heaven they were to me;
As pure as truth, as sweet as love—
I know they live and shine above—
	"God bless you!"

And God will bless me for your sake,
My grief and pain will wings betake,
And I in grace and truth shall grow,
Shall serve Him faithfully below,
And side by side with you shall rise
To greet our Savior in the skies—
	"God bless you!"

O servant of the living God,
Pursuing now the ways He trod.
Approaching step by step the door
Through which to share His throne and power,
Accept from me this echo sweet,
As I your precious words repeat—
	"God bless you!"

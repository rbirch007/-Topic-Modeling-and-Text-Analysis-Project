OBITUARY.

	DIED, in the First Ward, Salt Lake City, Nov. 28th,
1885. Sister Elizabeth Lovett, born Oct. 21st, 1824. She
was a loving and indulgent mother, and a most affection-
ate wife to an invalid husband, whom she nursed with the
tenderest care for five long years, and survived him just
ten years. Of our departed sister it can truthfully be said,
she has come up through much tribulation and washed her
robes white in the blood of the Lamb. She was a teacher
in the Relief Society a consistent Latter-day Saint, and
died as she had lived, in full faith of the Gospel, and in
the hope of a glorious resurrection.
						M. F. STEEL, Prest.,

EDITOR EXPONENT.
	The following Resolutions of Respect for our beloved
sister, Esther H. Davis, who departed this life December
21st, 1885, in her thirty-eighth year, were unanimously
adopted and sustained by the Primary Association of Big
Cottonwood Ward:
	Whereas, Our esteemed sister was formerly President of
the Primary Association of Big Cottonwood Ward, where
she was greatly loved and respected by every member of
the association, and,
	Whereas, In the loss of this our much esteemed sister,
we have parted with a willing worker, at all times, and one
who was ever devoted to the cause of truth and righteous-
ness,
	Resolved, That we condole with the bereaved family and
relatives in their great loss, and while we mingle our tears
with theirs, we look forward with the hope of meeting
her again, in the morning of the first resurrection of the
just.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be sent to the
bereaved family, that a copy be placed upon the minutes
of the association, and that one be sent to the WOMAN'S
EXPONENT for publication.
				ELLEN SUTHERLAND, President,
				MARY QUIST,
				EMILY E. STEVENSON, Counselors,
				MARY E. SUTHERLAND, Secretary

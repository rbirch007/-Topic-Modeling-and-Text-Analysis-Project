	FRIENDSHIP, LOVE AND TRUTH.

Three stars are shining on me from the WEST;
Three gifts of God, the brightest and the best;
Three strong impulses that have turned me round;
Three beacons placed upon a flowery mound;
Three guardian angels, pointing out the way;
Three voices urging me to "watch and pray;"
Three "silver trumpets," God's own heralds sound;
Three "muster rolls" that summon ALL around;
Three whispers echoed from the voice of God!
Three scourges ready, should I need the rod;
Three pearls dissolved in life's oft bitter cup;
Three sweets that urge me on to drink it up;
Three index hands, that point me to the goal;
Three magnets that attract and fix my soul;
Three paths, that lead direct to Zion's hill;
Three alchymists that gold, for me, distil;
Three messengers of peace, that calm my fears,
And fondly urge me thro' this vale of tears.
					HANNAH T. KING.
Dernford Dale, England.

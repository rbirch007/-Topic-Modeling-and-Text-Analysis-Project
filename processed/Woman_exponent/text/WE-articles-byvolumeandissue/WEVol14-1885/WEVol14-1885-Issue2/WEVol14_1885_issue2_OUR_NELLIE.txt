			OUR NELLIE

	Spirit immortal, sweet spirit blest,
	Gone from all pain to the bright land of rest;
	Leaving a void that no other can fill,
	And a grief in our hearts that no power can still.

	Save He who did give and hath taken away
	Our Nellie to mansions of eternal day;
	And oh, may His Spirit heal up each sore heart,
	'Til we meet her again never, never to part.

	No purer e'er came from heaven to earth,
	To gladden the home and the family hearth;
	Silently weaving a mystical chain
	Of the chords of affection, for aye to remain.

	The Father hath gathered her back to His fold,
	And her dear face no more shall our sad eyes behold,
	'Til life's journey is ended, and we reach the shore
	Where sorrow and sadness and partings are o'er.

	Oh, choice the home circle gathering there,
	Unclouded, unstricken by anguish and care,
	And sweet will the meeting be there bye and bye,
	In a blessed reunion, where love cannot die,

	Of father and mother, Buddie and all,
	Who have gone at the voice of Jehovah's call,
	And now, oh! our darling, the call was for thee -
	Oh! help us, our Father, Thy purpose to see,

	And oh, speed the day when Thy kingdom shall come,
	When throughout our sad earth Thy will shall be done;
	When the grave shall give back our loved ones again,
	And Thine own great victory Christ shall proclaim.

										HOPE.

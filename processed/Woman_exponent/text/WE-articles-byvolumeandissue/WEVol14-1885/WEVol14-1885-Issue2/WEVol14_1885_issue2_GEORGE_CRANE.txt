			GEORGE CRANE.

DIED, at Mill Creek, June 7th, 1885, David, son of 
John F. and Elizabeth Snedaker, aged twelve years, eight
months and twenty-three days.
	The following resolutions were adopted at the Third 
District Primary of Mill Creek Ward.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has seen fit to call from
our midst our dearly beloved young brother and associate,
be it
	Resolved, That we, the members of the Association, do
sympathize with Brother Snedaker and family in the loss of
their loved son and brother. David has the promise of a 
life beyond the grave, where sickness and death has no
power, where all is bright and beautiful.
	He was a faithful member of the Association, always
ready to respond to every call. We will miss him from 
our society, though we feel to say, "Thy will be done, O
Lord."
	Resolved, That we place this on our records, and pre-
sent a copy to the bereaved family, and one be sent to the
EXPONENT for publication.
		FRANCIS HANSON,
		ISABELL CARLISLE,
		KINDNESS WRIGHT,
			HADIE CARLISLE, Secretary

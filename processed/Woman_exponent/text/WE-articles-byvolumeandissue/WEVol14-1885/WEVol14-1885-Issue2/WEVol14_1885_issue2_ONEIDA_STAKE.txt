ONEIDA	STAKE.
	The Relief Society of Oneida Stake was or-
ganized in October, 1884, Apostles F. M. Ly-
man and John W. Taylor being present and
assisting the Presidency of the Stake in the
organization. Mrs. Elizabeth Fox of Franklin
was elected President; Mrs. Ann Hales of
Gentile Valley, First Counselor; Mrs. Mary
Hawkes of Franklin, Second Counselor; Mrs.
Annie Hatch, Secretary, and Mrs. Josephine
Mayberry, Assistant Secretary.
	The Relief Society of Oxford, Oneida Stake
was reorganized on the 28th of June, 1885,
Mrs. Polly Boyes, the former president, having
resigned her position. Mrs. Mary Jane Hend-
ricks was chosen President, with Mrs. Lewis
and Mrs. Crocker as Counselors.

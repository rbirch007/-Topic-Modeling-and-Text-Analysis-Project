SOUTH COTTONWOOD PRIMARY.

 The Annual meeting of the Primary Asso-
ciation of South Cottonwood wsa held May
23rd, in the ward meeting house, President
Elizabeth Davis presiding.
	A full attendance of children with many of
their parents, also Sister E. C. Clawson from
Salt Lake City, were present, on the occasion.
After singing prayer was offered by Brother
Wheeler, followed by singing "In our lovely
Deseret." Minutes and report of last annual
meeting read and approved, which gave credit
to the association. The programme was
promptly responded to, and encouraging and
interesting remarks were made by Sister E.C.
Clawson, Sister M. Rawlins, Brother Wheeler,
Brother Howe and President Elizabeth Davis.
All seemed to enjoy the meeting. The closing
prayer was offered by Bro. R. Howe.

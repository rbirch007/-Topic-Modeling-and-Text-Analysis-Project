TAYLOR, ARIZONA.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We held our annual review of the Primary
Association May 21st. 1885. There were pres-
ent upwards of seventy children, and quite a
number of their parents. We had a very en-
joyable time. Meeting opened with singing
by the Primary, under the direction of Bro.
Harrison Pearce, who is ever willing to assist
us. Prayer by Bro. Wm. Solomon. Singing
by the Primary. Opening speech by President
Magdalena Pearce. The programme consisted
of songs, recitations, select reading, dia-
logues and Bible Questions and Answers. We
must especially mention a recitation by four
little boys—"Give the little boys a chance,"
and also an exercise song by six very little
girls, and a song, "Look on the bright side," by
Misses Maria and Rebecca Standford and
Mable Pearce.
	Then our Stake President, Elizabeth Haw-
kins, read to the parents of the children an
eloquent address made by our beloved sister,
Eliza R. S. Smith, at Weber Stake Conference,
March 12th, 1885, after which Brother Wm.
Solomon and Merrill Willis made some en-
couraging remarks to all. Singing. Benedic-
tion by Bro. Joseph McCleve.
	In the afternoon we had a dancing party for
the children, and they enjoyed themselves, and
we feel to bless all the children of Zion, and
all who are engaged in building up our Father's
kingdom.
	Wishing for the success of the EXPONENT
and all those who are trying to help forward
the great work, we remain,
	Your sisters in the Gospel,
		MAGDALENA PEARCE, Prest.,
			ANN DATTON, Cor. Secretary.

IN MEMORIAM. 

	We, the officers and members of the Fourth Ward
Y. L. M. I. A., do tender these Resolutions of Respect to
the memory of Sister Clara Erickson, who departed this
life April 30th, 1885, aged fourteen years, six months and
twenty-one days.
	Whereas, She was loved and respected by all her asso-
ciates, and,
	Whereas, She was a faithful member of the association,
therefore, be it	
	Resolved, That we esteem her faithful devotion to duty
as worthy the emulation of us all.
	Resolved, That we condole with the bereaved parents
and relatives in the loss of their dear one, not, however,
without the consoling reflection that she will come forth in
the morning of the first resurrection.
	Resolved, That a copy of these Resolutions be sent to
the bereaved fam[-]ly, that another be retained in the Asso-
cition, and that a third be sent to the EXPONENT for
publication.
						ANNA WILCOX,
	Committee,			ANNIE THACKERAY,

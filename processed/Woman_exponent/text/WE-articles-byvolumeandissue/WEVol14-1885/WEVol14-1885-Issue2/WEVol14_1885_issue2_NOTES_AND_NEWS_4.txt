NOTES AND NEWS.

	"Mrs. Elizabeth Eaglesford is the first
woman admitted to the bar in Indiana."

	London, July 13.—A mass meeting of
women was held at Princess Hall this afternoon
to consider ways and means to protect young
girls from the horrible pitfalls of London, as
revealed by the Pall Malt Gazette. Mrs. Booth
and other ladies made eloquent addresses, and
the meeting resolved that the laws on the sub-
ject should forthwith be made more efficient:
denounced the state regulation of vice demand-
ed a total repeal of the contagious diseases act,
and that the age of consent be raised from 13
to 18. The meeting also voted to send a re-
cord of its proceedings to the House of Com-
mons.

SALT LAKE PRIMARY.
	The Quarterly Conference of the P. A. of
Salt Lake Stake was held in the Assembly
Hall, June 27th, at 10 a.m., Mrs. Ellen C.
Clawson presiding. Opened by singing, "Did
you think to pray," etc. Prayer by Sister
Smith. Singing, "In the chambeas of the
mountains." Minutes of previous conference
read and accepted. Verbal reports were given
from several wards, and two little girls from
the 16th Ward sang, "What is fairer than the
rose." Some other wards reported and Miss
Dora Boreman of the 19th Ward recited a 
piece entitled "The Martyrs." After some
more reports, Miss Irene Daynes sang a song
entitled "Grandpa's Darling," accompanied on
the organ by Master Josie Daynes, who after-
wards gave an organ solo. The 17th Ward
was then reported, and a very little girl from
the 7th Ward gave a recitation. Three other
wards reported, and a recitation was given by a
boy from the 10th Ward. Other wards were
reported, and a recitation given by a little girl
from South Cottonwood Ward, and after some
other wards had reported, Fanny Day of the
12th Ward sang a song. Mrs. Aurelia S.
Rogers, President of the Primary of Davis
Stake, made a few very interesting remarks to
the children.
	Conference adjourned for three months.
Singing, "The Lord's Prayer," by the children
Prayer by Coun. Lydia Ann Wells.

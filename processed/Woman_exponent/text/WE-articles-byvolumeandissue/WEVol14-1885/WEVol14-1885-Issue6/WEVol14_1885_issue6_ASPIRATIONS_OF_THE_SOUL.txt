ASPIRATIONS OF THE SOUL.

Teach me to know the mystic art
	Of drawing near to Thee!
Teach me to know eternal life,
	By being one with Thee.

Teach me the knowledge how I may
	Indeed behold Thy face!
The vision of my mind expand,
	That I may see the place
Appointed in the realms above
	For me, and all that's mine—
I only ask that we may be
	With Jesus—the DIVINE.

I worship Him—He seems so near
	His footprints I can trace—
This very earth. He's walked upon,
	And I can paint His face!

But there's another, higher still,
	Whom Jesus "Father" called,
Will mortal ever see revealed
	The God of all the world?

Oh! mystery of mysteries,
	Oh! how my soaring soul
Yearns for a glimpse of that grand Sight,
	Whose word the worlds control.

He'll fill the longings of my soul,
	He'll set my spirit free—
My brain and limbs, yea, all I am,
	Enfranchised then will be.

Savior, I bring my soul to Thee,
	Disrobed from things of time;
Touch me but with Thine holy touch,
	And seal me ever thine.

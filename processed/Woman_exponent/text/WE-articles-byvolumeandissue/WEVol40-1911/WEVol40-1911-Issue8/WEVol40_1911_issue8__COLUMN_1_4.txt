	We, therefore, the officers of the National
Woman’s Relief Society assembled, repre-
senting and acting in the name of 40,000
women, organized by the Prophet Joseph
Smith for the relief of the distressed, the
amelioration of the oppressed, in the interest
of education, the best citizenship, and for
true charity; do hereinafter bring forward
ten suggestions, upon which our united
strength would be a mighty power for good
upon questions the solution of which seem
at this time to be especially expedient and
essential, inasmuch as they are calculated
to have a bearing upon the promotion of
public health, of civic beauty, of maintaining
the highest standard of purity in the home,
of greater faith, of the use of individual
gifts, a broader intelligence, and a more
deeply rooted spiritual growth, and will
thereby create opportunities for higher liv-
ing.

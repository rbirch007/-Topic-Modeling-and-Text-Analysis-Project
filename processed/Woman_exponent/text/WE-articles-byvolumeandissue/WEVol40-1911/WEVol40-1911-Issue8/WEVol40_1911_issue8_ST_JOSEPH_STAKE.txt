		ST. JOSEPH STAKE.
	The semi-annual conference of the Re-
lief Societies of the St. Joseph Stake con-
vened at the Layton meeting-house, March
10th, 1912. The morning session was of a
business character, relating to our statis-
tical and financial affairs. Reports from the
different wards showed an increase of at-
tendance and interest since the New Year
began. The lessons from the Primer of
Sanitation are welcomed among the dif-
ferent societies, and a great deal of enthu-
siasm exhibited. Other subjects were taken
up and discussed, viz.: The storing of grain

SNOWFLAKE STAKE.
	At the quarterly conference of the Snow-
flake Stake, held June 3 and 4, 1911, Stake
President of Relief Society, Mary J. R.
West, her counselors and board were hon-
orably released. These sisters have served
long and faithfluly.
	A new organization was effected as fol-
lows Nellie M. Smith, President; Belle H.
Flake, first counselor; Anna S. Shumway,
second counselor; Maud F. Ramsey, secre-
tary, and Lulu J. Smith assistant secretary
and treasurer.

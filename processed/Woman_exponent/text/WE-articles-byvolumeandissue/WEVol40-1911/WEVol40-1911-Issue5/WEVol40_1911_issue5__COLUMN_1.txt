		WE ALL LOVED HER.
	[Dear Aunt Em:—This sketch was lost
for about three years, and has just come to
light again. It is offered to the Exponent
now with the thought that many readers
will appreciate the beautiful life lessons
given in its portrayal of a fine and noble
character. It was written for and read at
the funeral of Sister Elizabeth O. Decker,
which was held in Salt Lake City, Dec. 13,
her death having occurred Dec. 8, 1908.
Salt Lake City, Dec. 9, 1911.	L. L. G. R.]
			A TRIBUTE.
	The sister workers of the Salt Lake Tem-
ple highly esteem the privilege of contrib-
uting a few minor jewels and modest blos-
soms to the beautiful garland being formed
of thought-gems and flowers of affection
as a parting tribute to the memory of “Aunt
Lizzie Decker.”
	By this name she has been familiarly
called of late years by her near friends.

NOTES AND NEWS.
	All the New Jersey suffrage organiza-
tions will unite to open permanent head-
quarters in Newark. They will use for this
the money cleared by Mrs. Pankhurst’s lec-
ture, about $700.
	The Prime Minister of Austria has as-
sured a deputation of women from Bo-
hemia and Southern Austria that he thor-
oughly believes in the principle of woman
suffrage, and will use his influence in its
behalf.

			THE ROSE.
[Image of a rose]
	Undoubtedly the author of this exquisite-
ly tender poem of passion and sentiment has
entered, as it were, into the very heart of
nature and the philosophy of life.
	There is a richness and depth of feeling
in the poem itself, that cannot be expressed
in words, and is apparent only to the soul
of one who has the appreciation of heights
and depths, of joys and sorrows, of mortal
life, verging, as it were, upon the spiritual
and the unknown.

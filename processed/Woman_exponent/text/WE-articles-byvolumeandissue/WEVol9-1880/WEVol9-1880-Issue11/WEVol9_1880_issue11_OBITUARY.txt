OBITUARY.

	DIED: Prove Sept. 30, 1880. Julia A. Fosset,
wife of Thomas T. Holdaway, of childbirth.
aged 23 years and three months. Deceased was
born and brought up in Provo. Was a faithful
Latter-day Saint and an active member of the
Relief Society. She left a husband and three
small children, a father, mother and a large
circle of relatives to mourn her loss. She was
taken sick about midnight and died at four
o'clock in the morning. Her loss is mourned
sincerely by all who knew her.

She has gone, but our hearts in forgetting
	Can never forget she was dear,
Like a gem that is lost from its setting
	Her name and her place are still here.
								M. J. T.

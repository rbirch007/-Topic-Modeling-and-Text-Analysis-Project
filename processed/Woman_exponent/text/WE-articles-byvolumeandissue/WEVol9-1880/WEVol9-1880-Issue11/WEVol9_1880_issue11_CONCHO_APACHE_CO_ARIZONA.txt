CONCHO, APACHE CO., ARIZONA.
	The Relief Society of this place was organ-

[Column 2]

ized on the 14th of March, 1880, by Elders
John W. Young and Lorenzo H. Hatch, with
the following named officers: Matilda P. Kil-
lian, President; May Hettabell Brady and
Elizabeth J. Pulsipher, Counselors; Lucinda
J. Clemons, Secretary, add Charlotte Monk,
Treasurer.
	We are getting along very well; our Presi-
dent is an energetic woman, and one that is
qualified for the position she holds, and is al-
ways prompting the sisters to be up and alive
to their duties. We meet often and are united;
hold speaking meetings every fast day. We
read the EXPONENT and pray for its success.
	May the Lord bless the Relief Societies
throughout Israel. Your sister in the Gospel,

PIMA, PIMA Co., ARIZONA.
	The sisters of this place were organized into
a Relief Society on the first day of July,1880,
by our President, J. K. Rogers, with Sister
Mary L. Ransom, President, Sisters Josephine
Rogers and Caroline Teeples, Counselors. We
have thirty-four members enrolled, and are do-
ing very well for the short time we have been
organized; we hope to be able to send in a fa-
vorable report as soon as we have had time to
see what we can do.
	We ever pray for the welfare of all our sis-
ters and for the prosperity of all their right-
eous undertakings.
	MARY L. RANSOM, Pres't,

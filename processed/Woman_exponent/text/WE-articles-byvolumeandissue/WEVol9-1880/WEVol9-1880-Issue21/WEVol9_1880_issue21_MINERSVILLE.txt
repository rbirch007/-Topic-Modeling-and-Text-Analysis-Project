MINERSVILLE.

	Our Improvement Association was organized
October last, with Lydia Blackburn as Presi-
dent, Bathsheba Grundy and Ida Marshall as
Counselors, Harriet Eyre, Treasurer. We
have forty-eight members enrolled. We have
very interesting meetings, and a good spirit
seems to prevail.
				HETTIE DOTSON, Sec'y.

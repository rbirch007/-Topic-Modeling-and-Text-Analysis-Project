OBITUARY.

 Sister Maria Normington, wife of Bishop John
Parker died at Virgen City, Kane Co., March 19,
of jaundice,after an illness of four months,during
which time  she had two  paralytic strokes, the
first in her left  arm  five  weeks, the  other the
whole of her left side five days before her death.
She was born at Burnley,  Lancashire, England,
Dec. 25th, 1820.  Embraced  the  Gospel in  the
year 1839, emigrated with her family in the last
hand-cart company in 1856, buried  her husband
and two children on  the  plains,  and  suffered
much herself.  Was married  to John  Parker in
1857, in Salt Lake  City;  moved  to  Virgen  City
in 1863, was a teacher in  the  Relief  Society for
two years;  was a faithful  Latter-day Saint, ever
ready to  bear her testimony to the  truth  of the
latter-day work and gave good counsel to all her
associates. She quietly went to sleep in the hope
of a glorious ressurrection.

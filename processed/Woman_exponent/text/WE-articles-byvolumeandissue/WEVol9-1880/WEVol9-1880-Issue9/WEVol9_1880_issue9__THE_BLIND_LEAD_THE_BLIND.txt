"THE BLIND LEAD THE BLIND."

	It is astonishing to what a degree some of
"Our Father's" creatures can becloud their own
vision, and still imagine themselves far-seeing
individuals. The case of the late contest entered
into for the purpose of disfranchising the women
of Utah, by certain parties, exhibits this moral
deformity to an unusual extent. It seems that
no amount of experience can open the eyes of
these certain parties in regard to their own folly.
They are pleased to style themselves "Liberals,"
and perhaps they think that they are really try-
ing to act liberally. But if they could only open
their eyes to the truth, they would at once see
that there is neither justice nor even honesty in
their doings, to say nothing of generosity or lib-
erality. They would also see that as in the past,
their own and their co-workers' unjust actions
toward a people who seek to attend to their own
concerns have ever been productive of good
where they have sought to bring evil, so it will
be in the future. It is only a new shot at the
old mark they have so often missed. And they
still waste their ammunition, for they will miss

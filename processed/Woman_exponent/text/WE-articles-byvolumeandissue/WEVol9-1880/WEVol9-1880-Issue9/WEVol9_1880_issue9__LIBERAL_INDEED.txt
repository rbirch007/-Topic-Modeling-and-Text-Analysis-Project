"LIBERAL" INDEED!
The attempt to take the  franchise  from  us is
indeed  a  peculiarly "Liberal"  movement!  To
have suffrage taken  from  us, after  our  having
proved ourselves loyal to its privileges,  would be
cruelty in the extreme.  Have  we not suffered
indignities enough from out enemies already?
And have not endured  hardships  enough to
merit this blessing?

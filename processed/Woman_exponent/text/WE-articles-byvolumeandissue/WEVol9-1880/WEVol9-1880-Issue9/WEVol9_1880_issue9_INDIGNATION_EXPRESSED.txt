INDIGNATION EXPRESSED.

	I cannot but protest against the proceedings
of the so called liberals in our midst who are
seeking to take away our rights and privileges as
citizens. It arouses a feeling of indignation in
my heart, and my New England blood rebels
against such [--]nk injustice. How I rejoiced
when the suffrage was extended to us here in
these valleys and felt that it was a God given
privilege which all good and true women ought
to enjoy, and prayed earnestly that the women
of America, who so ardently desired this power
for good might also obtain equal rights as cit-
izens.

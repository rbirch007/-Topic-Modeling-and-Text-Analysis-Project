	Relative to the  hearing  from  the  dead,  in
Isaiah,  the  8th  chap.,  we  read  the  following:
"Should not a people seek unto their God for the
living to the dead?"  This  must  be  the  gift of
God, "the beholding of angels  and  ministering
spirits" spoken of in  the Book of Mormon.

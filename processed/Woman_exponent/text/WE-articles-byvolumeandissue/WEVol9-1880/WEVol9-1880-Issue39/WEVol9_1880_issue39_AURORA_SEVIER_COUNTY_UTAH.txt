AURORA, SEVIER  COUNTY, UTAH,
					March 15th, 1881.
The sisters of this place were organized  into a
Relief Society on  the ninth  of  January, 1880.


Sister  Clarissa  A.  Morgan, President Celestia
Durfee and Sabrina Howard, Counselors,Agatha
M.  Baxter, Secretary, Mary E.  Colby,  Assistant
Sec'y, Emma Broadhead,  Treasurer.  We have 6
teachers,  29  members,  enrolled,  we  are  doing
very  well  under  the  circumstances,  hold  our
testimony  meetings  every  fast-day;  we  had a
call from Sisters E. R. Snow  and B. W. Smith,
on or about the  first  of October last,  they  gave
us  some  good instructions, may  God  preserve
them long on the earth for our good.  We raised
53 bus. of wheat 5 bus.  and l9 lbs.  of oats.  We
held our annual meeting on the 5th of Jan., 1881;
there were present Bishop Jensen, of Salina, and
Sister Jensen, President of Relief Society Salina.
We had a very good time;  the officers were  sus-
tained.  We donated  40 dollars  in  quilts  and
other  articles  to Manti  Temple.  We have  a
Mutual  Improvement  Association, a  Primary
and a Sunday School, all  in good running order
and doing a good work.  We read the EXPONENT
and pray for its success.  Ever  praying for  the
welfare of all our Sisters  and for  the prosperity 
of all  righteous  undertakings, your  sister in the
Gospel,
		Agatha M. Baxter, Sec’y.

PRIMARY ASSOCIATION.

	Annual Report of the Primary Association of
Moroni, Sanpete Co. Organized by Mrs. E. B.
Wells and Mrs. E. Howard August 6th, 1879
with 138 members, increased to 203 during 10
months. Meetings held weekly on Saturday
afternoon. Average 32 meetings; (they being
suspended for a time on account of diphtheria).
Cards of merit distributed once.
	The exercises of the meeting are, after the
usual opening exercises, remarks made by Presi-
dent and Counselors; then recitations, songs,
essays, and select readings by the children: a
number of children bear their testimony; ques-
tions asked etc. They have taught them the
Ten Commandments and first principles of the
Gospel. We have received from the children on
their 5 and 10 cts. donations $10,00 and gleaned
8 bushels of wheat. Have had one party for
which the brethren and sisters donated money
enough to may all expenses. Have donated to
Manti Temple $4.60; paid out for presents for
the children $1.00; recieved of Moroni Co-op one
Minute Book. Officers of Association are, Mrs.
Deborah P. Irons, President; Mrs. Sarah Jolley
and Mrs. A. M. Nielson, Counselors; Jane Faux,
Secretary; Lizzie Bailey, Assistant Secretary;
Mary Yorgason, Treasurer. They are doing all in
their power to instruct the young. Our Associa-
tion is doing well,

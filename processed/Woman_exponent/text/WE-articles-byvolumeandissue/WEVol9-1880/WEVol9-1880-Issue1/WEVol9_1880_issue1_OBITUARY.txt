OBITUARY.

	Died at her residence in Oxford, Idaho, March
19th, 1880, Mary Hunt, wife of Samuel Hunt.
Born December 23d, 1835, at Corton, Norfolk,
England. Baptized 1853; Emigrated to Utah in
1868. Sister Hunt was a faithful Latter-day
Saint and an active member and teacher in the
Relief Society, and filled her position with
promptness and dignity, and was highly respec-
ted by all who knew her. The sisters of the Re-
lief Society deeply mourn her loss. Her testi-
mony was to the last mingled with faith and
hope of a glorious resurrection. Then we hope to
meet our beloved sister again and enjoy her so-
ciety.
						LOUSIA CROSHAW.
	P. S. According to her request, her remains
were taken to Salt Lake for interment.

ORGANIZATION OF SERICULTURE.

	Brother Daniel Graves, with Sisters E. R.
Snow Smith, Zina D. Young and M. I. Horne,
visited the Relief Society, of Mill Creek, for the
purpose of organizing the silk association.
	Under the direction of Bishop Reuben Miller,
Mrs. Margaret White was chosen president,
Mrs. Elizabeth Hill and Mrs. Ann Morgan,
counselors; Mrs. Elizabeth Carlisle, secretary,
and Mrs. Mary Samuels, treasurer. All were
unanimously sustained. After which Mrs.
Elizabeth Hill resigned and Miss E. S. Carlisle
was chosen second counselor. The majority of
the officers have been practically engaged in the
work.		ELIZABETH CARLISLE, Secretary.

OBITUARIES.
	Died, in Tooele, City,  of diphtheria,  May 12,
1880,  Mary  Ann  Spiers,  beloved  daughter of
George and Janet Spiers, and  granddaughter of
John Lyon, of Salt Lake  City, aged  18 years, 4
months and 28 days.
	In memory of our much respected and beloved
sister, who has been removed from our midst by
the hand of our Heavenly  Father, who giveth
and taketh away  at his  own  will and pleasure,
we desire  to say she was much beloved and re-
spected by all who knew her; she was a counsel-
or in the Y. L. M. I.  Association and a teacher
in the Sunday School.
	Be it Resolved,  That the members of the As-
sociation deeply mount her  loss and sympathize
with the bereaved parents, relatives, and friends
in the loss of so good and esteemed a friend and
beloved daughter.
	Resolved, That  these resolutions  be  given  to
the parents of the  deceased  and be recorded in
our General Record Book and  also furnished for
publication in the WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

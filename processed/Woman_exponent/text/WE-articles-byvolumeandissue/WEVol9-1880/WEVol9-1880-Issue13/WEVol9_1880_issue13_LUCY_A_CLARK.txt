								LUCY A. CLARK.

	These lines were written on the Twenty-fifth
Anniversary of the wedding of Bro. Edward
and Sister Elizabeth Stevenson, Oct. 28, 1880.
A silver wedding reception was given at their
residence in the 14th Ward; a large number
of guests assembled, congratulations were of-
fered and a sumptuous repast partaken of.
Afterwards remarks were made, suitable to the
occasion by Elder Stevenson, followed by
Apostle W. Woodruff, Coun. D. H. Wells,
Elder J. Horne and other brethren. A de-
licious wedding cake and other refreshments
were served; there were songs and music, and
the party enjoyed themselves heartily. Many
choice and elegant silver presents were made
to the worthy couple, and all went "merry as a
marriage bell."

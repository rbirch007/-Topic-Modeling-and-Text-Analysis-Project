A BIRTHDAY PARTY.

	Tuesday, Dec. 28, 1880, a few of the members
of the Relief Society of East Bountiful met at
the house of President E. Barlow, it being the
69th anniversary of the birth of our beloved
sister and President. The sisters brought with
them picnic and presents. The afternoon passed
off agreeably; a few remarks were made, bear-
ing testimony to her faithfulness in her official
capacity, and usefulness in the Relief Society,
always using her influence for good, and ever
faithful in discharging her duties. After wish-


ing her many happy returns of the day, and
every blessing that her heart might desire, the
company retired at an early hour.
East Bountiful.	M. J. C.

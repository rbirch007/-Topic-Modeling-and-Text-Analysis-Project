REPORTS.

	Relief Society of Mesa, Arizona, organized
March, 1880. Sarah Phelps, President; Eliza-
beth and Sarah M. Pomeroy, Counselors; Fran-
celle Robson, Secretary; Fannie Macdonald,
Treasurer. Have held meetings monthly; a good
spirit generally prevails. Have a city lot ar-
ranged for, and are going to build as soon as pos-
sible.
		Yours truly.

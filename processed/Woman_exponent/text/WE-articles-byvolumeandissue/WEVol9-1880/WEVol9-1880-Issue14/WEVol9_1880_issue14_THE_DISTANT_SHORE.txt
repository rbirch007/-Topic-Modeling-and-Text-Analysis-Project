THE DISTANT SHORE.

'Tis a struggle a gasp and a leap beyond,
	To the distant and unknown shore
Where we'll meet our friends who have passed the veil,
	Meet those who have gone before.

We'll have trod the path that the aged have trod.
	The youth and the infant form
We'll have traveled the road that all must tread.
	The ignorant great and small.

Do we think as we tread the path of life
	That our journey may soon be o'er?
That we'll meet with those that have passed the veil,
	With those who have gone before?

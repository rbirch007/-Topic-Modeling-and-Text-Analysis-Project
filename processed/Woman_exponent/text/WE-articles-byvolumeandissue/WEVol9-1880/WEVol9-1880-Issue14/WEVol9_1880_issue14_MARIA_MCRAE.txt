							MARIA MCRAE.

	Sister Phebe Wise, who died Oct. 29, 1880, of
consumption, in Farmington, Davis Co., had
been an invalid for a number of years, but her
sufferings did not prevent her from rejoicing in
her religion and bearing testimony of the same
with her latest breath. Deceased was the wife
of Thomas Wise, and the daughter of William
and Sarah Hatton, and was born at Brails, War-
wickshire, England, June 9, 1816, and was bap-
tized into the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-
day Saints in the year 1849, at Birmingham.
Emigrated from London to Utah in 1865, moved
to Farmington Oct. 29 of the same year. She
has been a faithful member of the Relief Socie-
ty, also a faithful wife and mother. We send
these few lines for publication, to show our re-
spect for her and her family, and to express, in
our feeble way, the sincere regret of the Presi-
dent, Mrs. O. Leonard, and all the officers and
members of the Relief Society with which she
was connected.

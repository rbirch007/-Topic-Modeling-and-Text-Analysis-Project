SURPRISE PARTY.
August 3rd a large number  of the Relief Soci-
ety of South Jordan  Ward  and  friends  of our
worthy  President  Ann  Holt assembled  at  her
home to offer her congratulations upon her birth-
day in the form of a surprise party.  The mem-
bers of the Relief Society brought her a few nice
presents,  she is a woman  that  we all  love  and
respect.  She  will  leave  her  bed  in  the  dark
hours of the night to go and comfort the sick, she
is a very useful woman, and I pray that she may
live as long  as she  desires, and  the Lord  bless
her in  her  labors  here  upon  the  earth,  is  the
prayer of her  numerous  friends.  Ever praying
for the welfare of Zion.

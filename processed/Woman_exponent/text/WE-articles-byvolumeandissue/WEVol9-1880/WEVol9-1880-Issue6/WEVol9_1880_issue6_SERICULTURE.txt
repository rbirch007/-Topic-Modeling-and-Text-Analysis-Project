SERICULTURE.

	Quite an interest has been manifested in the
Third Ward in Ogden City in the silk-raising,
especially in raising cocoons. We have raised
from eight to ten pounds of cocoons this summer
and have sold worms to different wards in the
country, and we have thousands of eggs on hand
for next Spring. We have the books treating on
silk culture. We have only commenced this
summer in a small way, but we have with the
blessing of God made it a success. I am deeply
interested in this enterprise and always feel it
my duty to respond to every call that is made
upon me in the Church and kingdom of God.
We would be glad to receive any information
from the sisters,which they may be in posession
of in regard to this useful and profitable home
industry.

OBITUARY.

	It is with deep regret that we record the death
of one of our beloved sisters. Janet Heaton
John was the daughter of William and Chris-
tina Sinclair. She was born June 11, 1843, at
Trenent, Haddingtonshire, Scotland; was bap-
tized Oct. 28, 1854; emigrated to America in 1856,
and to Utah in 1863. In 1857 she was married
to John Heaton, by whom she had nine children.
In 1873 her husband died, leaving her with the
care of a large family till the fall of 1879, when
she was married to William John. She was a
kind and affectionate mother, a true and faithful
Latter-day Saint, and an active member of the
Relief Society of this place, having held the of-
fice of Secretary for a number of years previous
to her death. She died March 3, 1881, of puer-
peral fever; her babe survived her but two days,
and was buried with her. She leaves a husband,
seven children and a host of friends to mourn


her loss. May she rest in peace and come forth,
in the morning of the first resurrection.

BEAVER.

	The first Ladies conference of the Beaver
Stake of Zion convened at Beaver in the meet-
ing house, May 14th, 1880. Present on the
stand were the several presidents and counselors
of the several branches of this Stake President
Lucinda Houd presided over the Conference.
President John Ashworth and several other
brethren met with the sisters and manifested a
liberal disposition to aid the ladies in their good
work.
	Reports of the different branches were handed
in. The societies of the Y. L. M. I. were well
represented, as also was the Primary Associa-
tion of the Beaver Branches. Two days were
spent by the sisters in this interesting capacity.
	Statistical and financial report of Beaver
Stake for the year ending May 14th, 1880. Mem-
bers, total, 207; real estate, $2,822.50; capital
stock in co-op. store, $100; wheat loaned out,
274 bushels; disbursements to the poor, $30.80.
			FLORA S. HILL, Stake Secretary.

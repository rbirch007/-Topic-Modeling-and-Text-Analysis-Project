ITEMS.

	THE Harvard Annex for Women has 40
students. 
	QUEEN Victoria, before her marriage, was
Victoria Guelph; since, if she were a private
lady, she would be known as Mrs. Wettin, that
being the family name of her late husband,
Prince Albert.
	FOURTEEN hundred students in Berlin alone
have signed the petition for depriving Jews of 
their civil rights. Their outburst would ap-
pear to be caused by jealousy; at a recent meet-
ing of students it was alleged that three-fourths
of the writers and poets in Germany are Jews.

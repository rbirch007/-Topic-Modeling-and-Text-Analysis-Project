WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


A TELEGRAM received by the Times, London
notifies the arrival at the Executive Mansion, 
Washington, of a writing table, made of tim-
ber from the ship Resolute, presented by Queen
Victoria to the President of the United States.

	SOME two years since the Russian Govern-
ment sent two musicians to Siberia to collect and
write down the national melodies. By travel-
ing from village to village, and attending the
various festivities of the peasants, they have
obtained a large number of tunes, including
about thirty which were previously unknown.
The collection is to be published during the
coming winter, and is looked for with much
curiosity in Russian musical circles.—Ex.

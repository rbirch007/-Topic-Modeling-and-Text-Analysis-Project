ITEMS FROM LEEDS.

	On New Year's day for the first time we had
a visit from our honored and beloved sisters E.
R. Snow and Zina D. Young. On that day they
met with the Relief Society, and they certainly
have the spirit of their office and calling upon
them as all must feel who come under the sound
of their voices; and I think their visit was truly
appreciated by many of the sisters. Our Relief
Society is in a prosperous condition under the
able managment of Sister Anna Wilkinson.
	On the following morning they met with and
organized a Primary Association which I believe
will be of great benefit to both parents and child-
ren, I believe the sisters all join in a hearty God
bless them, and may they live long to do good.

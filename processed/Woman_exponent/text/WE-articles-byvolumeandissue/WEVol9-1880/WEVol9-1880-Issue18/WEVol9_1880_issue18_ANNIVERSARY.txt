ANNIVERSARY.

	The Paris 2nd Ward Relief Society cele-
brated the anniversary of its organization on
Monday 3rd Jan. There were present: Pres.
Lindsay and Counselors, Maria Rich and
Elizabeth Collings of the stake presidency, also
Pres. Mary A. Shepherd and Bishop Rice be-
sides a great attendance of members and
friends. The first on the programme was
supper, after which, the report of the society
for the year was read and addresses made by
the sisters of the presidency followed by Bishop
Price who encouraged the sisters in their good
work. The remainder of the time was spent in
dancing and singing &c. All present seemed
to enjoy themselves thoroughly, and partake of
the good spirit that such gatherings promote.
								E. M. A.

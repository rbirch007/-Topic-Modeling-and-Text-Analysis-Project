BIRTH-DAY SURPRISE PARTY.
	Friday Aug. 11th 1880 being  the  84th  an-
niversary of the birth of our beloved sister and
friend "Aunt  Dicy  Perkins." a number of her
friends gave her a surprise party.
	President John  D.  T.  McAllister, Samuel
Adams, Bro.  Winsor  and  wife,  Mother
Bird, Aunt  Kate Church, Mother  Clark, and
Sisters Jeffreys, Worthen, Higgins, Alger, Em-
pey  and other; they brought  with  them  both
picnic  and presents, and had  very enjoyable
time.
	During the  evening  they  sang  songs  made
speeches and gave toasts,  and  wished dear  old
"aunt  Dicy"  all  the  hapiness  allotted  to  the
faithful.
The party was gotten  up by  Sisters  Winsor
and  Jeffreys,and was indeed what they intended
it should be a real surprise.
		A. M. W., and L. D. P.
St. George Aug. 17, 1880.

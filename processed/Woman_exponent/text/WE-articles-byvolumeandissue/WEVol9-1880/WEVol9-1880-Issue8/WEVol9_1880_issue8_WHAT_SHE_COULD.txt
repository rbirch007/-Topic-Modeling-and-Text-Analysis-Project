WHAT SHE COULD.

	"What She  Could," is  the  name of a   song
written  by Miss M. E. Servoss, and  inscribed
to Mrs. Hayes, begining:

	"More brave than they who at the stake
	Their lives for truth resign,
	Is she who from our Nation's feasts
	Hath dared to banish wine."

	It was written at Miss  Willard's request, and
is  published  by  the  author  with music by a
popular composer, and is sold for the benefit of
the Mrs Hayes  Memorial Fund by Miss M. E.
Servoss, 156 State St.,  Chicago,  Ill., who  will
give all profits  as author  and  publisher to the
Memorial Fund.—Ex.

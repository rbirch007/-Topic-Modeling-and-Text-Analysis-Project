SERICULTURE.
The Relief Society of  the Tenth Ward have
been  experimenting  in  Silk-culture, and onr
labors have been liberally rewarded. We have
nine pounds of cocoons,and sufficient eggs,racks
and  tables, to raise a large quantity next season.
Sister Fawcet has  also raised  seven  pounds  of
cocoons at home.  Charlotta Fawcet, Elizabeth
Paul, Elizabeth Morris.  Committee.


THE aerolite which fell in Iowa in May 1879
has been sold to the British Museum for $6,
500. It was the largest meteoric stone ever
seen in America. Its weight being 431,
pounds. Its composition was earth, triolite,
iron, nichel, cobalt phosphorus, copper, sul-
phur, lead, silver, gold, silicia, magnesia,
alumina,soda, lithia and potassium yet together
like particles of a granite block.

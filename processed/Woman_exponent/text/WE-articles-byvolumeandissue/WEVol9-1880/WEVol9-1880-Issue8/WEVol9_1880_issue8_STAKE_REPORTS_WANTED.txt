STAKE REPORTS WANTED.

	A full Statistical  and  Financial  Report  of
Relief  Societies  from April  1st, 1876 to 1880.
Also a Semi-Annual  Report from April 1st, 18
80 to October 1st, 1880, are specially requested
from each of the Stake Secretaries  throughout
all the Stakes of Zion.  Forward to the Secre-
tary of the First Presiding Board. SARAH M.
KIMBALL, Salt Lake City P. O. Box, 785.

Young Ladies Mutual Improvement  Associa-
tions from their first organization up to Oct. 1,
1880 is  specially requested  from  each  of  the
Stake Secretaries throughout all the Stakes of
Zion, by the President of the  central organiza-
tion of Y. L. M. I. A., Sister Elmina S. Taylor.
Direct  the  reports  to  the  Secretary,  Louie
Wells, care of WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

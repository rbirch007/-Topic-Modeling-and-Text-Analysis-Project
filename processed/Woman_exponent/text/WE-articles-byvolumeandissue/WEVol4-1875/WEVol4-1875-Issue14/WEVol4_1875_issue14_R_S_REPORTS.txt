R. S. REPORTS.

	The meeting of the Senior and Junior
Co-operative Retrenchment Association,
held in the Fourteenth Ward Assembly
Rooms, Saturday, Nov. 29th, was calculated
to awaken serious reflections in the minds
of all present in regard to re-baptism and
renewing of covenants. Many of the sis-
ters present expressed their wishes in re-
gard to reformation, with much fervency
and earnestness. The subject of the repre-
sentation of Utah at the Centennial was
dwelt upon with zeal and eloquence by Miss
E. R. Snow. No one who attends these
meetings of the sisters can be left in any
doubt as to the sincerity of the desires of
those who are connected with these socie-
ties, to improve themselves, and to use
their influence for the advancement of light
and truth upon the earth.

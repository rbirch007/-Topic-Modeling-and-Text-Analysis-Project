			FOREIGN.

	Mr. Holman Hunt has left England for
Jerusalem, intending to remain three years
to complete a large picture on an important
subject......Joe Jefferson has just recently
buried his youngest child, a boy four years
old, in London, in the midst of his profes-
sional triumphs......The Governor of Paris
has resolved to prevent disorderly meetings
......Spain will send an iron-clad fleet to
Cuba......A mineralogical society is to be
organized in London.....Japanese advisers
state that war between Japan and Corea is
inevitable.....Cholera is interfering with
the Prince of Wales movements in India
......The assassin of the German Consul-
General, in Uraguay, has been tried by
court-martial, and shot.

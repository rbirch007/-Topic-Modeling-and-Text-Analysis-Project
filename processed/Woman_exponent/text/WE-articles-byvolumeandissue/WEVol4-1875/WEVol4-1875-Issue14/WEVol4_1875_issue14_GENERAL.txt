			GENERAL.

	Messrs D. Appleton & Co. have presented
the public library at Haverhill, Mass., with
450 volumes, valued at $1,200......Naviga-
tion has been closed at Chicago, the ice is
so very heavy......Cathedral of the Holy
Cross at Boston, the largest Catholic Church
in the country, was dedicated Dec. 8th, with
great pomp and ceremony......The gambling
houses in Cincinnati have been closed by
the authorities......Revivals are very lively
in Philadelphia, under Moody and Sankey's
influence......Three men were accidentally
killed in the main building of the Centen-
nial building at Philadelphia recently......
The prize poem at the Cambridge Univer-
sity this year is to be written upon the Cen-
tennial......The number of insane people in
San Francisco is said to be 25 per cent larger
than last year......A company of negro min-
strels are meeting with great success in
their entertainments in Paris......The Chil-
dren's Aid Society in New York has dis-
tributed within the last 23 years $1,887,569.

			PERSONAL.

	William B. Astor, probably the most
wealthy citizen of America, died in New
York Nov. 24th, aged 85 years......M. Thiers
is engaged in writing his philosophical
work entitled "Man and Matter"......Rev.
Dr. Lord of Buffalo, proposes to will his
valuable library to the city of Buffalo......
Gen. V. S. Vanantwerp of the U. S. Army,
died at his daughter's residence, Prince
George's Co., Md., Dec. 8th, aged 86......
Captain Leonard Buell was fatally shot on
board his vessel on Dec. 7th......Gen. Bab-
cock has gone to Chicago to attend the court
of inquiry implicating him......Mr. B. E.
Woolf of Boston, has written a comic opera
in two acts, music and words, entitled "A
Cure for Love"......The Rev. Dr. Sprague of
Albany has presented to the New York
State library 8,000 historical and miscella-


neous pamphlets.........J.L. Drake, grand
Secretary of the Grand Lodge of Good Tem-
plars of Illinois, died recently......Senator
Dawes, of Massachusetts, was recently kicked
by a horse and seriously injured.

EXHORTATION.

Keep your lamps well trimmed and burning,
	For the time is drawing near,
We know not the day or hour,
	When the Savior will appear.

But we know that He is coming;
	For His promises are sure 
Though heaven and earth shall pass away,
	Yet His word will still endure.

Then keep your lamps well trimmed and burning,
	And be ready for the day,
When we'll meet our glorious Savior,
	With His saints in bright array.

And receive the crowns and blessings,
	He has promised to the just,
Let us all press boldly onward
	Faithful to our Father’s trust.

SODA SPRINGS, ONEIDA Co., IDAHO.
	Apr. 11, '76.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Thinking a line or two from our snowy
home would not be uninteresting to your
many readers, prompts me to write.
	Our winter has been unusually long and
severe, and very little signs of Spring yet.
We have been startled by twelve or four-
teen shocks of earthquakes, occurring on
the 5th, 6th, and 7th inst., doing no damage
however, more than to frighten a few people.

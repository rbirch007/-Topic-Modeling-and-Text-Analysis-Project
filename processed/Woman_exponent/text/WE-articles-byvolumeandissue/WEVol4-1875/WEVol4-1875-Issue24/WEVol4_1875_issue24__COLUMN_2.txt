PLAIN CITY, WEBER Co., Apr.
	29, 76.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	I write to let you know how we are get-
ting along with our Retrenchment Associa-
tion. We meet together once a week, open
our meetings with singing and prayer.
There are not many young ladies here in
Plain City, but there are a great many lit-
tle girls, who rise and bear testimony to the 
things of God.
	Our Retrenchment Association was organ-
ized the 16th of Nov., 1875, with the follow-
ing officers:
	Mrs. Emily Shirtliffe, President,
	" 	Mary Raymond,
	"	Burty Funk,
	"	Jane Stewart,
	"	Charlotta Green,		Counselors.
	"	Ameriam Stephens,
	Miss Annie Hansen,
	"	Margery Shomaker,	Sec'y's.
	"	Aggie Geddes, 
	Mrs. Charlotte Linderloff, Treasurer.
	We had a Leap-year Ball on the 16th of
March; the hall was beautifully decorated
for the occasion. Dancing commenced at 2
o'clock p. m., and closed at 10. We have
had no round-dancing this year.

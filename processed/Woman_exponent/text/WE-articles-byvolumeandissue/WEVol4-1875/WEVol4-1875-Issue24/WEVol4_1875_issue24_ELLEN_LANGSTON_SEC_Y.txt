	ELLEN LANGSTON, Sec'y.


FIFTY-ONE societies, associations and
conventions have appointed Philadelphia as
a place of meeting this summer. The Wo-
man's Congress will hold its fourth meeting
there in October. The President of this or-
ganization is Prof. Maria Mitche[-]l of Vas-
sar. The last meeting was at Syracuse, New
York, in 1875. The subjects discussed were
science for women, the immediate needs of
women in their social and business rela-
tions, and the most effective methods of
remedying those needs. Art for women,
and the relation of women to crime and
criminals.

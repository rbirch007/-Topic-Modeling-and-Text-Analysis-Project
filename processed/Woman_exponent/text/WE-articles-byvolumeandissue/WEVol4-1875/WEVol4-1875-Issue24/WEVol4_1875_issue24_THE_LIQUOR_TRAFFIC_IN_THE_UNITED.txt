	THE LIQUOR TRAFFIC IN THE UNITED
STATES.—There are 146,000 drinking sa-
loons; the manufacturers and sellers of
spirituous liquors number about 560,000.
It is estimated that in the saloons there are
5,000,000 daily customers; the 29,000 dis-
tilleries make annually 72,500,000 gallons of
ardent spirits.
	THE forests of Europe as estimated by
Dr. Brown, cover 500,000,000 acres, or
nearly 20 per cent of the surface of the
continent. In British North America 900,
000,000 acres. In the United States, 560,
000,000 acres. In South America 700,000,
000, acres. The total for Europe and Amer-
ica alone is equal to 3 600,000 geographical
miles.


THE ALPHA, is a monthly paper published
in Washington, D. C., by the Moral Educa-
tion Society. Its object is to aid and assist
the world in regeneration. It contains ar-
ticles excellently written on the subordina-
tion of women and "Pre-natal Influence."
These articles are strikingly appropriate to
the object in view of woman's elevation and
advancement, and the individual equality
of sex.

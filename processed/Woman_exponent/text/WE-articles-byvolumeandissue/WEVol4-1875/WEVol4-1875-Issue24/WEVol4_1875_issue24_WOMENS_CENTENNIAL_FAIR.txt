WOMENS' CENTENNIAL FAIR.

	All those who design furnishing specimens
for the Territorial Fair will do well to forward
soon as convenient--before or by the middle of
June. It is anticipated that the Fair will be
opened on, or before the first of July in the
"Social Hall, S. L. City. Specimens to be
sent to Sister E. R. Snow, Lion House, as
heretofore specified.


IN the San Francisco Mint there are man-
ufactured in the Coiner's Department dur-
ing the year at least $30,000,000, including
gold and silver of every description. Of
this amount the greater part is $20 pieces
or double eagles.

		DRAPERVILLE, Dec. 15th, 1875.
 EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The sisters at this place had the pleasure
of a very interesting visit from Sisters E. R.
Snow and M. I. Horne on Nov. 30th; Sister
Snow organized a society called the "Young
Ladies' Retrenchment Association of Dra-
perville;" we received from them very
good and valuable instructions, which we
hope to treasure up and profit by in future.
The officers were elected as follows.
	Mrs. Julia Stewart, President.
	"	Melissa Fitzgerald,
	"	Hannah Smith,
	"	Mary E. Smith,	Counselors.
	Miss Mary A. Stewart,
	"	Leah Day,
	Mrs. Minnie Garff,
	Miss Annie Burnham, Secretary,
	"	Elnora Day, Assistant Secretary.
			Yours respectfully,

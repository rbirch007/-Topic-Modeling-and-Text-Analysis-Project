1776	CENTENNIAL	1876

	By order of the president of the Woman's
Centennial Executive Committee for Utah,
(she having received imperative orders from the
President of the International Woman's De-
partment at Philadelphia, to send word imme-
diately what space will be required.) We earn-
estly request all the presidents of Relief Socie-
ties throughout this Territory to send in reports
forthwith of their movements upon this sub-
ject; and also to furnish statistics of all dona-
tions expended for the poor since the first or-
ganization of said societies. Prompt and en-
ergetic measures to comply with this request
should be immediately taken. In all the dif-
ferent Wards throughout this city similar re-
ports should be furnished forthwith, as these
reports will have to be put into proper form
after being received, and forwarded to Phila-
delphia as a general report.
ELIZA R. SNOW, President,
EMMELINE B. WELLS, Secretary.

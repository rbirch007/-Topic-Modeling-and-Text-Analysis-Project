NOTICE.

	The Society wishes to say that those persons
who have any cocoons that are fresh and good,
and wish to sell them or have them reeled, the
Association will purchase them or reel them
into any kind of sewing silk they desire;
also any person that understands the reeling of
silk in all its branches will please report im-
mediately to the president of the society Mrs.
Zina D. Young, Salt Lake City, or to the Sec-
retary, Reuben Sympson, P. O. Box, 584, S. L.
City, Utah.
REUBEN SYMPSON,
Sec. pro tem.

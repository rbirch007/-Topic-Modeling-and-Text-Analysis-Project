WOMAN'S VOICE.
DEAR EDITOR:
	I wish to mingle my voice with those of
my sisters through your precious columns.
Not having a very good education it is hard
to express my feelings on paper, yet my
faith and integrity are just as good as though
my learning was better. We are in the
kingdom of God, and it is rolling on, and
when we, dear sisters, take time to write a
few lines to the EXPONENT we are helping 
to advance it a little. We can help to ad-
vance this great work in many ways; one
is in talking to the young through the EX-
PONENT. Now, dear sisters, as the holi-
days are over, let us put our minds to study
better things, those that will elevate us in
the kingdom of God.
S. J. T.

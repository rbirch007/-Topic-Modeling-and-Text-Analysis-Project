		HARRISVILLE May 18th, 1875.
DEAR EDITOR:
	Your much valued paper is received and
read with much pleasure by my subscribers;
there are but  few of them; the sisters join
together and take the paper; there are many
more read the paper than there are that
subscribe yet they are all subscribers.
	I wish the little girls and even grown up
and aged women were as interested in it as
my little subscriber Lovisa G. Harris, she is 
eleven years old, she dries peaches and sells
them for the money; and this is the way
she pays for her paper. 
							S. J. T.


For the EXPONENT.

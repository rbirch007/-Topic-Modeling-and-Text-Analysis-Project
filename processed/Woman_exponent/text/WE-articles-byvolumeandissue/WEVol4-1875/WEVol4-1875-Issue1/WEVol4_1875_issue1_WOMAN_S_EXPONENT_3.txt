WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


BONDITCH on taxation published in pam-
phlet form is a book which should be care-
fully read and pondered upon by men and
women. He cites official report made by
the Mass. Legation in 1871. Showing that
women paid one-eleventh part of the entire
property tax in that year. He has written
boldly and and ably in defense of women;
and he deserves the highest praise for the
firm stand he has taken. He takes for the
basis of his arguments the Declaration of
Rights adopted by Mass. in 1770 and
proves conclusively that women were not
only included in the word "People" but in
the word "Men."

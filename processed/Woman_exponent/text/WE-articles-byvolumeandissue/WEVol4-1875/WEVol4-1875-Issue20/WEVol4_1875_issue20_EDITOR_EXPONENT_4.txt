EDITOR EXPONENT:
	I enclose you a brief report of a meeting
of the Relief society of this ward, held
March 1st 1876, at the residence of Mr. Hy-
rum P. Trim. President Mrs. Sarah P. Gib-
son presiding.
	After singing and prayer the financial re-
port for the year 1875 was read. Much
good counsel was given, the sisters all feel-
ing desirous to do right and use their influ-
ence to help build up the Kingdom of God
on the earth.
	Donations for the year 1875; for Society
$48,53. Donated for the erection of a
house for the society, $584,20.
	With kind regards for the success of the
EXPONENT. I am yours affectionately,

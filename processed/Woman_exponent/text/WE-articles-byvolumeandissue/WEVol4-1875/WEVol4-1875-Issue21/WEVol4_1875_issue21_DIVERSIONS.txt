DIVERSIONS.

	AN impossible quantity.—Too much
money.
	WATERING places that remain open all
winter—The mouths of milk-cans.
	IN what place are two heads better than
one?—In a barrel.
	RELATIONSHIPS are rather far-fetched
sometimes, both in Ireland and Scotland.
"Do you know Tom Duffy, Pat?" "Know
him is it," said Pat, "Shure he's a relation
of mine! He once wanted to marry my sis-
ter Kate."

For WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
To whom it may concern.
	It will be recollected that an article, written
by me, entitled "Mortal and Immortal Ele-
ments of the Human Body—A Philosophical
Objection to the Resurrection Removed." was
published in the WOMAN'S EXPONENT in Sep-
tember, 1875, and that subsequently an article
written and signed py Pres. Young appeared in
the WOMAN'S EXPONENT in which the former
was pronounced untrue.
	Permit me to say that I fully concur in the
views expressed by Pres. Young, and withdraw
everything contained in my article at variance
therewith, and trust that no Latter-Day Saint
may be led into erroneous doctrine through
anything written by me.
	Salt Lake City, March 19, 1876.

	PINE VALLEY, UTAH,
		Feb, 8, 1876.
DEAR EDITOR:
	On the 2nd, day of December, 1875, the
young ladies of Pine Valley were organized
into a Retrenchment Association with the
the following officers:
Miss Celestia Snow,		President,
	"	Ann J. Bryce,
	"	Mary M. MEEKS,
	"	Julia A Keele,		}Counselors,
	"	Sarah A. Bracken,
	"	Vilate Burgess,
Miss Mary L. Snow Secretary, and Miss
Cleopatra Burgess Assistant Secretary. We
meet once in two weeks, open our meeting
with singing and prayer. We have thirty-
four members enrolled. The society takes
the EXPONENT, and we occupy our time in
reading from its columns, and also from the
Bible, Book of Mormon and Doctrine and
Covenants, and in speaking to each other of
the things of the Kingdom. We have ta-
ken a vote in our meeting to refrain from
round dancing. We were very much pleas-
ed and interested with the instructions of
Sister E. S. Barney while on her visit south.
That we may be able to follow her advice is
our earnest desire.
	Yours respectfully,
	MARY L. SNOW, Secretary.

MILL CREEK WARD, SALT LAKE CO.,
	Jan. 29, 1876.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	It is at the request of our worthy Super-
intendent, Sister Spencer, that I write a
few lines giving you a brief report of our
association; although I have been silent, we
have been diligent in doing all the good we
could. We hold our meetings monthly in an
upper room of the Ward House, which has
been neatly furnished for the accommoda-
tion of the sisters. Our meetings are gen-
erally pretty well attended and a good spirit
prevails; we have excellent instructions,
from our beloved and honored Superintend-
ent and from the sisters of the Relief So-
ciety who visit us frequently, and also from
the young ladies of the Retrenchment As-
sociation.
	M. H. NORTH,
	Secretary.

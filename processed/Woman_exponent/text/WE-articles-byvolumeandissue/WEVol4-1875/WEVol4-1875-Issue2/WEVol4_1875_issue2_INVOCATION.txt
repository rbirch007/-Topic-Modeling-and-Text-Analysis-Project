INVOCATION.
[TUNE—GOD SAVE THE QUEEN.]
O Lord bless Brigham Young!
(From Saints of every tongue,
		Let this ascend;)
Thy spirit light his way,
Be thou his guide and stay,
And guard both night and day,
		Him to defend!
Grant he may live to see
Thy Saints in unity,	
	Go hand in hand;
When we are one in Thee,
We shall our triumph see,
Thy name shall honored be,
		In every land.
When thus our work is done,
Having the victory won,
	Saints meet again,
Keeping this still in view,
Whatever others do,
Let us stand firm and true
		Amen! Amen!

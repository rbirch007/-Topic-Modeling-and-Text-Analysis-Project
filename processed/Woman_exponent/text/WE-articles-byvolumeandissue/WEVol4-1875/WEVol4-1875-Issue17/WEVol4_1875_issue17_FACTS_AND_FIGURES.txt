FACTS AND FIGURES.
	The cotton crop in the South this year
will exceed the limits of 4,000,000 bales.
	THREE car loads of silk-worm eggs val-
ued at $86,000,000 have been taken from
San Francisco to New York recently.
	THE number of hogs killed at Cinncinati
this season was 239,874; for the same time
last year, 253,048.
	THE largest polished plate ever exhibited
was the work of the Thames Plate Glass
Company of England, measuring 19 feet, 5
inches long, and 10, feet 3 inches wide.
IN 1855 the Paris omnibuses carried 40,
000,000 passengers; in 1860, 72,000,000; in
1867, Exhibition year, 121,000,000; in 1869,
119,000,000; in 1971, 78,000,000; in 1872, 111
000,000; and in 1874, over 115,000,000.
	IT is doubtless safe to say that we have as
voters in the United States fully 1,000,000
of men who religiously hold that their first
and highest allegiance is due to the Pope of
Rome.—National Protestant.


AN easy observation of the most common,
sometimes the meanest things in nature,
will give the truest lights, where the great-
est sagacity and industry that slight such
observation must leave us in the dark, or
what is worse, amuse and mislead us by
false lights. In an inquiry, it is almost
everything to be once in a right road.

			ACROSTIC.

Prepare this sacred relic room.
Receive this holy trust O tomb!
Enshrined with love and tears,
Sad offering—dust to dust!
In memory oft will kindred hearts,
Deeming 'twas Father's will,
Embrace, though stay'd not sorrow's darts,
New light which that dear thought imparts,
Though dead, he liveth still!

Great and high honors, sweet and glorious rest,
Earned fairly here through faithfulness and zeal.
Obedience, strict integrity, the test
Required a Saints true life to seal.
God and His Councils have this record traced—
Even in their midst this friend, our friend is placed.

Admired of men for his intrinsic worth,
Loved of the Lord and all his bretheren gone.
Bereaved and sorrowing, his friends on earth,
Earth-bound remain, though strongly heavenward
	drawn:—


Reward their patience, bright Celestial birth,
	Thou glorious day, the Resurrection dawn!

	Saints, O! prepare ye! Stand no more aloof;
	Meek, wise submission like this good show;
	In anger sin not, but accept reproof,
	Taught by the Holy Spirit how to go.
	Him to rejoin on high, ye mourn below.

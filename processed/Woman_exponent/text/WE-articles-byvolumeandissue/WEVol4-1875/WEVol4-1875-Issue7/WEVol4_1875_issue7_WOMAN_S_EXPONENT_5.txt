WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


For the Exponent.
	BRIGHT AND EARLY.
Bright and early is the theme
	We should ever keep in view;
Always hold it as supreme
	This will bear us safely through.
If we're bright and early over,
	In the race through life to run,
When life's thread grini death shall sever,
	The glorious prize is surely won.
Those who are both bright and early
	'Mid the varied scenes of earth:
They can read their title clearly
	Unto a celestial birth.
Bright and early came the woman
	To the Sepulcher to weep;
On the Resurrection morning,
	It was not the time to sleep.
All who have been great or useful,
	In the battle of this life,
They have been both bright and early
	Foremost in the noble strife.
							SOLO.

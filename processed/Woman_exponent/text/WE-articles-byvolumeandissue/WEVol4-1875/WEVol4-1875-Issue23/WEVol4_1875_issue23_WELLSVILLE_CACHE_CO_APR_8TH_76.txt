	WELLSVILLE, CACHE CO. Apr. 8th, '76.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	We as a band of little girls in Wellsville
are organized into a Bible Society, and to let
you know that we are awake to our duties,
we thought, in our weak way, that we
would give you a synopsis of our doings.
	On the 21st of March we met for the pur-
pose of organizing our class at the residence
of Sister Ellen Whiton, President of our
Relief Society, and under her jurisdiction we
collectively nominated a President, four
Counselors, Secretary and Treasurer. We
hope through our united efforts to do much
good, and in place of spending our leisure
time in idleness, we wish to try to make
ourselves useful, and learn to fill our places
in life to the best of our abilities.
	I will add no more at present, but close
by saying we love to read the EXPONENT
and wish it every success.
	The officers send their kind regards.

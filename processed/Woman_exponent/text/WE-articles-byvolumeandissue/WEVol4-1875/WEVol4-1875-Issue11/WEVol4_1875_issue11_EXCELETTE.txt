						EXCELETTE.


TO-MORROW may never come to us. We
do not live in to morrow. We cannot find
it in any title deeds. The man who owns
whole blocks of real estate and great ships
on the sea does not own a single minute of
to morrow. To-morrow! It is a mysterious
possibility not yet born. It lies under the 
seal of midnight, behind the veil of glit-
tering constellations.

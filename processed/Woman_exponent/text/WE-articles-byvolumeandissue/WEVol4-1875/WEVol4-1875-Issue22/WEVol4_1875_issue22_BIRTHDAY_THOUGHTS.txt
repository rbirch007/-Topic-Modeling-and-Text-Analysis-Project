		BIRTHDAY THOUGHTS.

Year after year rolls down the stream of Time,
Propelling all unto the final goal,
Where eyes immortal view the emblazoned scroll
That error dims—or that with truth doth shine,
And which each prescient soul endorses "mine."
Oh! melting moment! when the quickened soul
Freed from mortality, and its control,
Can view the PAST and all its thrilling scenes,
And Life's biography at a glance can read!—
Its thrilling joys—its deathly woes—its themes
Of love and hope, its richness, and its need!
Its stern realities—and then its lulling dreams—
GOD, must prepare us for this glorious light,
For nothing mortal could endure the sight.

NORTH OGDEN, WEBER Co.
	March 20, 1876.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Our Retrenchment Association was or-
ganized the 24 of March 1875, since that 
time we have held 17 meetings, and we are
happy to say there is a gradual improve-
ment in the sisters in their search after
knowledge; and we have cause to rejoice
that we are so organized that we can meet
together and speak to each other on the 
principles pertaining to life and salvation.
	The young men also have been organized
into a Mutual Improvement Association,
they are making steady progress, and are
united with the younger sisters in abstaining
from round dances, which we voted against
in our association some six months ago, and
to which, we are happy to say, we have re-
mained firm up to the present.
	Eva Chadwick, President, Elizabeth
Chadwick, and Emily Knowles, Counsel-
ors.

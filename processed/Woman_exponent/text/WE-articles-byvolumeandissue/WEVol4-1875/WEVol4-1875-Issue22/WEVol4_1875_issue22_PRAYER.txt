PRAYER.

	My essay will be addressed to the girls, as
I am a girl myself. I have made it a prac-
tice for years to pray to my Heavenly Fa-
ther night and morning, and I advise all
my young friends to do the same; the time
is coming and it is near at hand, when if
we want to be saved and enter into the
presence and glory of the Lord, we will
have to do better than we have been doing.
All those who do not pray have no idea
what sweet and heavenly thoughts prayer
gives to the mind, if they pray in faith.
Girls I would advise you to give heed to
the word of the Lord which is given through
our President Brigham Young, who by the
revelation of God has been appointed to
lead and instruct us. We should be very
thankful that there is a chance for us to be-
come celestial beings, if we so wish, by be-
ing faithful diligent and prayerful. Pray
to the Lord in faith and He will give heed
and give us great wisdom and enlighten
our minds to a very great extent, we can
gain more wisdom in this way than any
other, I know by experience. May the
Lord bless us all and may we young sisters,
follow in the footsteps of the Lord Jesus
our beloved elder brother. May we all be
prayerful, and faithful subjects to God our
Father, and live so that we may come forth
in the morning of the first resurrection with
all the blessings and privileges of the faith-
ful, is my constant prayer.
	Your sister in the Gospel,

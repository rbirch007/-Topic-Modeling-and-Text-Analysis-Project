CEDAR CITY, IRON Co., March 27, 1876.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	I am happy to state that our Relief So-
ciety is progressing, we have started sever-
al branches of HOME INDUSTRY that are
doing real well, and have many more in
contemplation. The winter has been very
severe here, but we have had meetings
every two weeks (with I think one or two
exceptions) which have been well attended.
A great many renewed their covenants dur-
ing the past summer, and all seem to be try-
ing to live nearer to God, and carry out the 
instructions given by His servants.
	The Young Ladies' Association has a 
beneficial effect among our young people,
their meetings are also well attended and a 
good spirit prevails.
	Praying the Lord to bless and strength-
en you in your labors, I remain your sister
in the Gospel.

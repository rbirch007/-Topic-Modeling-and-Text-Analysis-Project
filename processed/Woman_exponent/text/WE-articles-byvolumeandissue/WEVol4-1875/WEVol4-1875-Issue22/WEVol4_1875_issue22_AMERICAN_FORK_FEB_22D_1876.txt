AMERICAN FORK, Feb 22d, 1876.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The annual meeting of the Young La-
dies' Mutual Improvement Association of 
this place was held Feb. 22d, 1876.
	A general invitation was extended to all,
and the meeting was largely attended. Es-
says were read and selections from good 
books. The discourses were spirited and 
full of principle, and were interspersed
with singing. All seemed to enjoy them-
selves very much. Bishop L. E. Harring-
ton being present gave us some very good 
advice.
	Great interest is taken in the meetings,
and we look forward to them with pleasure.
Our society has increased in numbers very
rapidly, and a good spirit prevails, and also
a spirit of improvement is apparent.
	Wishing success to all the faithful, we 
remain your sisters,
	Mrs. J. Durrant,
	"	E. Eastmond,	Superintendents.
	Miss M. Eastmond, President,

HOW much is there in one minute, when
we reflect that one minute extends over the
whole world?
	PAY little attention to those philosophers
who tell us that we should completely con-
trol all our passions—never to be elated at
success or depressed at sorrow. "They talk
like angels but act like men."
	A LITTLE boy, gazing upon an old picture
of his mother taken in a low dress, re-
marked: "Mamma, you was mos' ready for
bed when dat picter was tooken."

	GUNNISON, Feb. 1st, 1876.
DEAR EDITOR:
	I have been thinking about writing for
some time, but have neglected it, not being
accustomed to write for a paper. The 28th
of last June the sisters of this place had a
pleasant visit from Sisters Eliza R. Snow,
M. I. Horne, E. Howard, and E. S. Barney.
They organized a society called: The Young
Ladies's Retrenchment Association of Gun-
nison. We meet every two weeks; open
with prayer and singing, and have select
reading and essays; we try to improve in
every thing which is good and commenda-
ble.
	The officers were elected as follows:
	Mrs. Helena Madson, President,
	Nettie Sorenson,
	Trene Caldwell,

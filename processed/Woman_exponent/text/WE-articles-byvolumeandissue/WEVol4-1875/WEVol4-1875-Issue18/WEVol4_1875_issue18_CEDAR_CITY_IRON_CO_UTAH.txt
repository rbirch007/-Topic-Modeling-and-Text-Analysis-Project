	CEDAR CITY, IRON CO. UTAH.
		Jan. 26, 1875.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	A reorganization took place in our Re-
lief Society, June 3rd, 1875. Mrs. Sarah
Uric was chosen President, Mrs. Mary Ann
Lunt and Mrs. Sage Jones, Counselors; Miss
Sarah Jane Leigh, Treasurer; Mrs. Ellen
Lunt Secretary, and Miss Jane Parry Assist-
ant Secretary. The former officers having
resigned with the exception of the Secre-
tary.
	Our society is in a flourishing condition,
and we are trying to do all in our power
to aid in rolling on the great work of the
Latter-days.
	Bishop Henry Lunt, Elders C. J. Arthur,
Jens Neilson, and Francis Webster met with
the sisters of the Relief Society, Dec. 9th,
1875 to organize a Young Ladies, Retrench-
ment Association. After the Bishop had
spoken at some length on Retrenchment,
Home Manufacture, Word of Wisdom etc.
Miss Martha Henrietta Lunt was nomina-
ted and sustained as President,

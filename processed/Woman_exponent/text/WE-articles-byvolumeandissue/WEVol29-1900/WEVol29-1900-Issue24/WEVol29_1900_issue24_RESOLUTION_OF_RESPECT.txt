RESOLUTION OF RESPECT.

Resolved, That we cherish her memory and
that a copy of these resolutions be placed on our
record and one sent to the WOMAN'S EXPONENT
for publication, also one sent to the family.

“Methinks I see the book opened,
And an angel the pages turn o’er,
Till he comes to her name, when he pauses—
“Sainted woman thou’lt sorrow no more.
Thy life has been weighed in the balance,
Which tipped at the mark pure gold,
By thy name is recorded ‘most worthy,’
Enter into the joys of the Lord.”

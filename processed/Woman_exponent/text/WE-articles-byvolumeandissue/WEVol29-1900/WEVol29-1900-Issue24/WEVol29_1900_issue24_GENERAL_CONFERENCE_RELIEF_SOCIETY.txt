GENERAL CONFERENCE RELIEF SOCIETY.
	The General Conference of the Relief
Society was held on Monday, April 8, 1901,
in the Assembly Hall in this city, meeting
commencing at 2 p. m., Counselor Jane S.
Richards presiding. Singing, “Come, let
us anew,” etc. Prayer by Elder Theo-
dore Curtis. Singing, “Let us pray,
gladly pray.” Minutes of October confer-
ence read by the secretary and approved.
	Counselor Elizabeth Moody reported St.
Joseph Stake. The president and counsel-
ors had visited every society in the Stake
except one, and felt all were trying to
serve the Lord. Rejoiced in the work
being done by the women of Zion.

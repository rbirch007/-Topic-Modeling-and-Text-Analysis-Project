RESOLUTIONS.
	At the regular monthly meeting of the officers

[Column 2]
of the St. Joseph Stake Relief Society, the follow-
ing resolutions were adopted by unanimous vote:
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has called our
beloved sister, Maria McRae, from our midst, who
was a member of the Stake Board of our Relief
Society, a faithful worker, a kind and loving
mother and a friend to all. Therefore be it
	Resolved, That we the members of the St.
Joseph Stake Relief Society do hereby express our
sympathy to her bereaved husband and family,
and we also desire to emulate her noble examples.
	Resolved, that these resolutions be placed on
the record of the St. Joseph Relief Society, a copy
sent to the bereaved family, also one sent to the
WOMAN’S EXPONENT for publication.

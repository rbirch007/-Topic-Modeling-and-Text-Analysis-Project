		MARICOPA STAKE,

	The Relief Society conference of the
Maricopa Stake was held in the
Mesa Relief Society hall, Decem-
ber 20, 1900, Stake President Mabel Ann
Hakes presiding. Meeting opened with the
usual exercises, at 10 a. m. and 2 p. m.,
attendance of thirty-four officers besides the
members.
	The wards were all represented and re-
ported with one exception. The instruc-
tions were timely and such as would elevate
mankind. The subjects mostly dwelt upon
were the moral lives of ourselves and chil-
dren; reverence for the Gospel; the duties
of the Relief Society, both financially and
spiritually; restitution should not be put off
until tomorrow, also many strong testi-
monies were borne to the divinity of the
Gospel. We had a glorious conference. Ad-
journed for three months.

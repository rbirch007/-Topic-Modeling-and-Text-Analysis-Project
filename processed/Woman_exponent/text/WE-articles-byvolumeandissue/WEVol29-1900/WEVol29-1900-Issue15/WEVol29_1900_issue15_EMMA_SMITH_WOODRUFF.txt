		EMMA SMITH WOODRUFF.

	A few reflections on the passing and
coming centuries.
	Surely the one just closing has been a
wonderful and marvelous one. The many,
many great and grand incidents that have
come to pass, none of which any human
being understood,only the inspired Prophets
of God taught by His Holy Spirit long be-
fore our Era, looking down through the
vista of time predicted what would and has
come to pass in the last days, and has been
ushered in during the past century, even of
the revelation of the Gospel in its purity,
and a prophet being raised up, to which we
can testify.
	The establishment of the kingdom of
God, the coming forth of the Book of
Mormon, and how the Prophet Nahum pre-
dicted of the railroads being builded in the
day of the Lord’s preparation. And how
could those who had obeyed the Gospel in
distant parts or lands have been gathered to-

prophecies been fulfilled. Abundant are

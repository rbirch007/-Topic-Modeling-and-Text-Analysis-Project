IN MEMORIAM.
		MARY ANN CHRISTENSON
	It becomes our painful duty to record the death 
of our beloved sister, Mary Ann Christenson, who
departed this life February 2, 1901. She was a 
great sufferer for over two years with dropsy and 
other diseases. A few of the Relief Society sisters 
visited her two days before she died. She bore a 
faithful testimony of the goodness of God. She 
died as she had lived, a faithful Latter-day Saint.
She was born in Allbury Co., Denmark, February 
14, 1837, baptized into the Church of Jesus Christ 
of Latter-day Saints May 28, 1883, and emigrated 
to the valley in 1883. She was the mother of 
eleven children, nine of whom survive her. 
	The following resolutions were adopted by the 
Relief Society of the Lyman Ward in honor of our 
beloved sister and colaborer, Sister Mary Ann
Christenson.

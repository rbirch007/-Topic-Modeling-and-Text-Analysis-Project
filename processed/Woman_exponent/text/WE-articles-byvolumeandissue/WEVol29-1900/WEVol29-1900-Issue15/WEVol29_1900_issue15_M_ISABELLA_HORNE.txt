M. ISABELLA HORNE.

	To any Latter-day Saint the event
of the present century which eclipses
all others in importance is the restoration
of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. And I be-
lieve that the early years of the coming
century will show to the whole world that
the view now taken by only a handful of
people is the correct one. It is, perhaps,
not too much to think that all will soon see
that the Gospel will bring about the uni-
versal brotherhood and the reign of peace
on earth so long foreseen by the prophets.

SYMPOSIUM.
THE NINETEENTH AND TWENTIETH
CENTURIES.
	The nineteenth century has been fraught
with wonderful events. Our Father in
heaven and His Son Jesus Christ, appearing
before Joseph Smith and instructing him
about religion and sending Moroni to him
and delivering the ancient [-]ecord on golden
plates, from which he translated the Book
of Mormon. Also in sending Peter, James
and John to Joseph Smith and Oliver
Cowdery, and conferring the Holy Priest-

[Column 2]
hood upon them, and teaching them how to
organize the Church of Jesus Christ of
Latter-day Saints, and how to attain salva-
tion for the living and redemption for the
dead.
	How the Prophet Joseph, had to lay
down his precious life for the truths he had
taught.
	How about the next century? There
will be greater calamities, more sickness,
pestilence of every name and nature until
the earth will be cleaned of wickedness,
and the Gospel will be preached to every
nation, and the honest in heart gathered to
Zion, and the Center Stake built up, many
of the Lamanites converted and Jerusalem
will be rebuilt and the ten tribes will re-
turn.
	Jesus will come in the clouds of heaven
with all His holy angels, and the Saints be
caught up to meet Him.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Whereas, Our Father in heaven has called from
our midst Sister Rhoda Snell, who was always
true and faithful in the performance of her duties
in the Relief Society, and won the love and respect
of all who associated with her, we feel to express
our love for her.
	Resolved, That as a sister, associate and friend
we feel that words are inadequate to express the
high estimation in which we hold her, and while
we feel the deepest sorrow at the loss we sustain in

[Column 3]
being deprived of her society, we bow submissively
to the will of Him who doeth all things well, and
say, “Thy will, O Lord, not mine, be done.”
	That we cherish her memory and follow in the
example she set us, that of being a faithful Latter-
day Saint.
	That we extend our heartfelt sympathy to the
bereaved family and relatives.
	That one copy of these resolutions be presented
to them, a copy be entered on the record of our
society and a copy be sent to the WOMAN’S EXPON-
ENT for publication.
	In behalf of the First Ward Relief Society of
Spanish Fork.

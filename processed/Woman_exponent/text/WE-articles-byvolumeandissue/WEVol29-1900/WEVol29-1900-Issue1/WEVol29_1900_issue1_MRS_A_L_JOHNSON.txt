		MRS. A. L. JOHNSON.

    To understand the proper method of
breathing is essential to health, and when
the breathing organs are in order we are in
possession of that abundance of life which
Jesus brought to earth when He said, “I
come that those that have life may have it
more abundantly.” Abdominal breathing
is essential to life and happiness, and with
a knowledge of hygiene and rhythmic
movement of the body all disease can be ex-
pelled from the system, and we can gain,
to a great extent, the elasticity of youth.
    But before this much desired condition
can be brought about we must have con-
trol over the joints and muscular system,
giving the body its freedom. Knowledge
of self-culture, rightly applied, will enable
us to regain our lost strength and power,
giving strength and easy movement to the
body, developing that physical beauty
which nature has endowed her children
with. Most of our ailments, especially
throat and lung troubles, are brought on by
a lack of knowledge in breathing. When
we breathe only from the lungs we are soon
exhausted, the supply being limited.
    A knowledge of abdominal breathing
gives an increase of force and vitality to the
entire system undreamed of. With a little
practice every movement becomes natural,
giving easy grace. As health returns to
the body the eye becomes bright, the form
erect, the voice sweet and clear. Few
realize the actual benefits to be derived from
a knowledge of self-culture. Mrs. Johnson
has a book on the various subjects that gives
full instructions for hygienic treatment and
exercises. Those at a distance can write
for the book and she will be pleased to give
any instructions by letter or make arrange-
ments for classes in or out of the city. For
further particulars call or address Mrs. A.
L. Johnson, 503 and 504 Templeton 
Building.

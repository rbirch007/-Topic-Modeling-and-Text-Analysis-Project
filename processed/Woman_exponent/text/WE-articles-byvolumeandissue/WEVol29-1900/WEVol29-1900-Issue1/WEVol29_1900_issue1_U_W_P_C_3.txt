U. W. P. C.
	Minutes of meeting of the Utah Woman’s
Press Club held at the EXPONENT office’
August 31, 1900, President Aimee C.
Schiller in the chair. Prayer by Phebe C.
Young. Roll called. A letter was then
read pertaining to year books, from the
secretary of the Federation, corresponding
secretary instructed to answer it. Mem-
bers present paid ten cents tax for Feder-
ation reception. A paper on the Transvaal
war, by Luella M. Rhodes, proved to be an
excellent one. Current events were given
in an interesting manner by Ella W. Hyde.
Mrs. Rhodes’ paper was discussed by Mrs.
Phebe C. Young and others.
	Miss Helen Margaret Ring, the guest of
the evening, a newspaper woman of Denver,


being invited to address the club, also took
up the paper and said she had never heard a
paper read that covered so much ground in
so short a time. She related some very
nteresting incidents in connection with
Mrs. John Hayes Hammond, while traveling
in Mexico. She threw out some bright
ideas in regard to the subject.
	Meeting adjourned. Dismissed with
prayer by Ella W. Hyde. 
			RUTH M. FOX, Asst. Sec.

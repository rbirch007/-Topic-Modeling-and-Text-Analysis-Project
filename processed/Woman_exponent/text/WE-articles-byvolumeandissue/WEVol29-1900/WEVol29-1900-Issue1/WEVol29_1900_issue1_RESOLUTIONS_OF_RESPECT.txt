		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has seen fit in
His infinite love and mercy to call home our be-
loved president, Mary Almina Watkins, therefore.
	Resolved, That in her death the Relief Society
has lost a staunch and valiant worker, one who
was ever ready to give a helping hand and cheer-
ing word to those in need.
	That we, her associates, have lost a faithful,
loving sister, and that we will miss her wise
counsel and advice. but we feel that our loss will
be her gain.
	That we present a copy of these resolutions to
her bereaved husband and family to express our
love and appreciation of her good works, also that
a copy be forwarded to the WOMAN'S EXPONENT
for publication and a copy be placed on file in the
ward record book.
					SARAH J. WILDE.
					EMMA F. CRAWFORD,
								Counselors. 
					LIZZIE WRIGHT,
								Secretary.
					ADELINE PARKER,
								Asst. Sec.
					MARIA HANSEN,
								Treasurer.

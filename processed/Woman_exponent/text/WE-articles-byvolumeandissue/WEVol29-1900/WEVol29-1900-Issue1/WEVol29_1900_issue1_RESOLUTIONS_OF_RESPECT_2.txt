		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

   Whereas, Death has come into our midst and
taken from us our honored sister whom we all
loved, an earnest worker and teacher of our Relief
Society, be it
    Resolved, By the members of the society that we
tender to the bereaved family our sincere sympathy
and earnestly desire that God will comfort them in
their affliction.
    That a copy of these resolutions be sent to the
family, a copy be placed on record and one be
forwarded to the EXPONENT for publication.
					    E. R. UDALL, Pres.
					    MAGGIE McCUNE, Sec.
Second Ward Relief Society of Nephi.

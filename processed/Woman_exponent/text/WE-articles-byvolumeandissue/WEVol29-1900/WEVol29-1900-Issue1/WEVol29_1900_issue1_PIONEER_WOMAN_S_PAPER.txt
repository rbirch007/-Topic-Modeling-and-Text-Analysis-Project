PIONEER WOMAN’S PAPER.
	This number of the paper, June first,
nineteen hundred is the commencement of
the Twenty-ninth volume of the WOMAN’S
EXPONENT. It is the oldest woman’s
paper in the West, and the first established
except the New North West in Portland,
Oregon; that was discontinued several
years since. At the time this paper was
first issued, there were few women in any
of the professions, and even women teachers
received very small salaries and hardly
ventured to speak up in meeting; but
wonderful developments have been made
in woman’s work and woman’s sphere dur-
ing the last thirty years Those who have
watched the progress of affairs must be
aware how marvelously woman herself has
kept pace with the spirit of the age, not
only in the East, where every facility for
advancement is easy of access, but in the
far West, where all was very crude and
new but half a century ago.

WOMEN’S MEETINGS.
	The regular annual business meeting of
the National Woman’s Relief Society will
be held in the Salt Lake Assembly Hall on
Monday, October 1, at two o’clock p. m.
All officers and members of the society are
cordially invited to attend and participate in
the business of the meeting.
			E. B. WELLS, Secretary.
	The General Conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Salt Lake
Assembly Hall, in this city, on Thursday,
October 4, meetings commencing at 10 a.
m. and 2 p. m. It is desired that there
should be a large attendance of the mem-
bers, and that each Stake organization
should be represented by its president, or
some one aifthorized to act for her. A
cordial invitation is extended to the Bishops
and all brethren interested in the welfare of
the society, also the Y. L. M. I. A. and
Primary Association.
		ZINA D. H. YOUNG,
						President.
		JANE S. RICHARDS
		BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
		SARAH JENNE CANNON,
						Counselors.
	B. WELLS, Secretary.

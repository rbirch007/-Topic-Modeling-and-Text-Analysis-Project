O, BE JOYFUL!

	O, be joyful, O, be joyful,
		Sing ye Saints of Latter-days;
	Giving unto God the Father,
		Adoration, love and praise.

	For the blessings of the Gospel,
		In our day restored to man;
	With its powers, gifts and gladness,
		Revealing clear salvation’s plan. 

	On the mountains ring the tidings,
		In the East the glory breaks,
	Ush'ring in the day of promise,
		When the world from slumber wakes.

	See, the clouds of doubt and error,
		Rolling with the mists away,
	A pathway opened up to heaven,
		Christ descends in white array. 

O, trembling earth, thy King is come,
		Refulgent rays o’er thee shine;
	In thy bosom swells emotion,
		Fires of love, serene, divine.

See the living Saints appearing,
		In their robes of glistening white,
	Crowns are given to the victors,
		who triumphant win the fight.

PRIMARY OFFICERS REUNION.
    The Primary officers of the Jordan Stake
held their first annual reunion on Thursday,
October 25, at the ward house, Crescent, at
2 p. m. Brother Hyrum Goff, of the Stake
presidency, and many of the Stake officers
of the Relief Society and Y. L. M. I. A.
were present, and about a hundred of the
Stake and local Primary officers.
    The program was a very interesting one
and consisted of songs, recitations and
music, interspersed with brief addresses by
some of our visitors.
    President Hyrum Goff in the toast, “The
Ladies,” told of the many amiable qualities
of the fair sex and Primary officers, in par-
ticular. Much good counsel and en-
couragement was given by all the speakers.
    At the close of the program delicious re-
freshments were passed around by a com-
mittee of young ladies and a delightful time
was enjoyed by all present.
		    LUCY M. HILTON, Stake Sec.

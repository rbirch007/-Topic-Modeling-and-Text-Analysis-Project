U. W. P. C.
	The U. W. P. C. met in the EXPONENT
office, September 29, 1900. Opening
prayer was offered by Dr. Romania B.
Pratt. Roll call responded to by original
sentiments. Mrs. McVicker spoke on the
educational work. The president reported
from the Club Council. A new president is
to be elected for the council next month.
All the Kindergarten work was re-
ported. The Kindergarten has been opened
and all club members who can are asked to
contribute. Emma Jenson and Phebe C.
Young are appointed as members on that
committee.
	A paper was then read on the Phillipine
Situation, by Grace Anderson. Emma
Jensen read a paper on Women’s Congresses
in Paris. Aimee C. Schiller read a well
prepared paper on Current Events, and a
short discussion followed.
	It was decided to hold our business meet-
ing October 1, at 3:30 p. m., to prepare for
the annual meeting and the election of
officers.
	Meeting adjourned. Prayer by Phebe
C. Young.

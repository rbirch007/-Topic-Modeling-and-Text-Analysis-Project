		       MARIA LOVE.

    It becomes our painful duty to record the death
of our dear sister and colaborer of the Second
Ward Relief Society of Nephi, Utah, Sister Maria
Love, who died October 7, 1900.
    Sister Love was born in Toronto, Canada, April
16, 1833. was a faithful Latter-day Saint, was
chosen a teacher in the Relief Society of Nephi at
its first organization in June, 1868, which office she
has filled true and faithfully up to the time of
her death.
   At a meeting one week before her death she
bore a strong testimony to the truth of the work
she was engaged in.

	One whom we love,
	One who had nothing but blessings for all,
	One who responded to every call,
	One who was ever so faithful and true,
	One who gave blessings to me and to you,
	One whom the angels will watch in her rest,
	One who is safe in the realms of the blest.

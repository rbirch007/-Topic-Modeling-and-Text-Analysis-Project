OBITUARY.
	It becomes our painful duty to record the death
of our dear sister and counselor to the president of
the Relief Society, of Newton, Cache Co., Utah,
Sister Mary Christena Ingberg Christensen, who
died April 30, 1900. She was born in Denmark,
February 14, 1841.
	Sister Christensen embraced the Gospel in her
native land and emigrated to Utah in 1865. She
crossed the plains with ox teams and walked all
the way and forded the rivers from Omaha to Salt
Lake City. She was married to Elder James
Christensen and lived in Brigham City and later
moved to Newton, Cache Co., where she died. She
will meet her husband and three sons who pre-
ceded her. She leaves four sons and three grand-
children and a host of friends to mourn her loss.
	She was a kind and loving wife and mother, and 
died in full faith of the Gospel.
RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Whereas, Death has come into our midst and
taken from us our honored sister whom we all
l[-]ved, an earnest supporter and counselor of the
society, be it
	Resolved, By the members of this society, that
we tender to the bereaved family our sincere sym-
pathy, and earnestly desire that God will comfort
them in their affliction.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
sent to the family, a copy be placed on record and
one forwarded to the EXPONENT for publication.

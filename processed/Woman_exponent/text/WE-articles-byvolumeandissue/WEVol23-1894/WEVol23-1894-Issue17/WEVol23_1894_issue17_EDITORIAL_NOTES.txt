EDITORIAL NOTES.

	THE terrible accident at Almy, Wyom-
ing, which made so many widows and or-
phans momentarily, has been pathetically
portrayed in verse, and sent to the Expon-
ent but we were obliged to lay it over un-
til next issue.
	THE editor of this paper begs leave to
state that owing to uncontrollable circum-
stances, no paper was issued in March, and
trusting to the generosity of the sisters to
overlook this once, especially as it is the
question of finance, and we hope it may
never occur again, and certainly will not if
the sisters whose names are on the list of
subscribers will sustain the paper by their
subscriptions.

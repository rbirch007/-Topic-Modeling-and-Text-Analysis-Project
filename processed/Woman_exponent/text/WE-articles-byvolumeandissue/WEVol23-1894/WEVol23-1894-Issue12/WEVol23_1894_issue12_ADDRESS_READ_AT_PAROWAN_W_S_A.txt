ADDRESS READ AT PAROWAN W. S. A.

	I CONGRATULATE the ladies of Iron
County in organizing a branch of the
Woman’s Suffrage Association here. I con-
sider that it is a step in the right direction
and taken none too soon.
	Having always believed that the measure
of knowledge possessed by women marked
the standard of intelligence of coming pos-
terity, and believing as one has said “that
they who rock the cradle rule the world,” I
have looked forward with great hope to the
time when woman unfettered by the

OFFICIAL NOTICE.

	THE Stake Secretaries of the Relief
Society and (the secretary) of each mission
or branch not yet included in a Stake, are
hereby officially notified to carefully prepare
correct statistical and financial reports for
the current year from January 1st—1894 to
December 31st—1894. These reports must
be forwarded to the General Secretary at
the Office of the WOMAN'S EXPONENT,
Salt Lake City Utah, early in February
1895 or not later than the 10th, of that
month, so that a complete report of this
great organization may be ready at least ten
days before the General Conference in April
1895.
E. B. WELLS, Secretary.

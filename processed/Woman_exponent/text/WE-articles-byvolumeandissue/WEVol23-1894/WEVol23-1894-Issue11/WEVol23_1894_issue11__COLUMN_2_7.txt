	President Hess, “I have enjoyed this
meeting very much, it seems to me that it
has all been just as good as it could be, we 
have a treat today in having an Apostle
with us, it shows to us the interest that is
taken in these organizations. I with my
Counselors have made up our minds that
we can do more good in coming to these
meetings than in any other way, I have al-
ways felt amply paid in attending them,
there is a great labor to be done, among the 
people try and be a little more diligent in
talking up and encouraging the sisters to 
come out to these meetings, that they may
receive the same blessings that you do in
coming, it is to be regretted that no more 
attend these meetings, may God help us to
see our duties and do them at all times.”
President Grant “I think we have great
reason to rejoice in what we have heard,
we will now adjourn our Conference for 
three months, to be held at East Bountiful.”
Singing, “Do what is right, the day dawn 
is breaking” and benediction by Counselor 
Mary Clark.

GRAINS OF TRUTH.
	
	MANY yet are the secret truths of God,
which will be unfolded as they are needed.
—Beecher.

	DEFEAT is a school in which truth al-
ways grows strong.—Beecher.

	MINDS, like faces, are the real things.
Books, like photographs, are only impress-
ons.

	IF for any reason whatever a child can
be taught but one lesson, let that lesson
be Faith in God. For that one step towards
an education is of more permanent value to
a human being than all other learning put
together.

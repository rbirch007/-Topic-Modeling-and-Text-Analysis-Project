UINTAH CO. W. S. A.
	THE following are the minutes of or-
ganization of the “Woman’s Suffrage As-
sociation in this County.” The ladies of
Uintah Co. met at the Vernal Stake house 
to organize a Woman’s Suffrage Associa-
tion, Sept. 25th, 1894. Mrs. Mary A. Freeze
as chairman. The meeting commenced by


singing, “Oh, come come away.” Prayer
was offered by Mr. R. S. Collett. Singing,
“Freedom’s Daughters Rouse From
Slumber.”
After several nominations, the organiza-
tion was formed of the following ladies,
who were unanimously sustained. Mrs.
Caroline A. Strineam, President. Sarah
Colton, First Vice President. Tillie Young,
Second Vice President, Miss Rose Reynolds,
Secretary and Mrs. Addie Longhurst
Treasurer. For Executive Committee,
Mrs. Saddie Holdaway Chairman, Mrs.
Nancy Colton, J. E. Dillman, Agnes Ben-
nion, Vilate Bennion. After remarks by
Mr. R. S. Collett, Mrs. Stevenson and Mr.
Jas. Hacking the audience sang America.
Benediction by Mr. Jas. Hacking.

MRS. SUSAN B. ANTHONY and MRS.
CARRIE CHAPMAN CATT will hold a series
of meetings in the South, in advance of
the coming National Woman Suffrage Con-
vention at Atlanta. They will visit Lex-
ington and Louisville, Ky., Memphis,
Tenn., New Orleans, La,. Jackson. Miss.,
Birmingham, Ala., and other cities, in
each case by the invitation of the ladies in
that place. The tour of meetings will be-
gin with Lexington, Ky., on Jan. 9.—Ex.

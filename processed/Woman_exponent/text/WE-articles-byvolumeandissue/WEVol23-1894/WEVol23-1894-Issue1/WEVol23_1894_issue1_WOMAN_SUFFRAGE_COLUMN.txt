WOMAN SUFFRAGE COLUMN.
        UTAH COUNTY W. S. A.
	ON Tuesday morning, May first, a W. S.
A. convention was held at Provo, President
Electa Bullock in the chair.  Delegates
were present from most of the towns in the
County and the attendance was unusually
large.  A very interesting program was
carried out. a choir of beautiful young girls
from Spanish Fork, furnished sweet and
inspiring music for the occasion.  There
were some original papers read, one by
Fanny Stewart, of Pleasant Grove, and one
by Margaret Gaddy, of American Fork,
both were excellent; Mrs. Gaddy’s paper
was published in our issue of June first.

SALT LAKE W. S. A.

	MINUTES of W. S. Meeting, held Tues-
day Aug. 21st, in the 14th, Ward Hall.
Third Vice President Julia Druce in the
chair. Song, “Do what is right.” Prayer
by Mary A. Freeze. Song, “Know this
that every soul is free.” Roll called,
minutes for July read and approved. Read-
ing, by Lousie Badger, entitled, “A good
conscience.” Reading by Martha Greames
entitled, “Keeping up with the children.”
President Elizabeth MacFarlane read a piece
from the Salt Lake Herald on the Constitu-
tion. C. C. R. Wells made some very in-
teresting remarks on suffrage for women.
Ruth M. Fox made a short speech on the
same subject. Mary A. Freeze thought
when the time comes for us to vote, we
shall know who to vote for, referred to
what Hon. John E. Booth said at the re
union at Saltair, that women should de-
mand their rights. Adjourned till Sep-
tember.

EDITORIAL NOTES.

	THE poem in our Last issue entitled,
“In Loving Remembrance,” should have
been “In Loving Remembrance of Sister
Arabella Chandler Parkinson.”

	At the first election under the Equal
Suffrage held in Colorado it was demon-
strated that the lower the elements in
Society the less interest was taken in the
ballot, and vice versa.

	THE General Conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Assembly Hall
in this city on Thursday, October 4th,
meetings commencing at 10 a. m. at 2 p. m.
and and half-past seven in the evening.
	The Presidents of the Relief Society of
all the several Stakes of Zion are requested
to attend or send an official representative
to report, and all officers and members are
expected to be present at the Conference.
Presiding authorities in the several Stakes
and bishops and brethren interested in the
work and advancement of the society are
cordially invited to attend the meetings.
By order of the President Zina D. H.
Young.

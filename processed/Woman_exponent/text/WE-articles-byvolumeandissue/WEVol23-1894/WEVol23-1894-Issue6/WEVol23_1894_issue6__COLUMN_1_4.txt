	The call of the convention and other
rules and preliminaries were attentively
listened to by the visiting ladies.
	At the afternoon session many ladies
were in attendance and the Provo and
Salt Lake City Glee Clubs rendered some
very witty songs in an artistic style and the
music by the bands was very exhilarating
and like the Glee Clubs were cheered to
the encore, and when Mr. Arthur L.
Thomas read in the resolutions that Equal
Suffrage be granted to women, the ladies
enthusiastically waved their handkerchiefs
and gentlemen cheered and waved their
hats in an inspiring manner. May success
attend the cause and all others which tend
to the good of humanity.
M. A. TILL.
Provo City, Sep. 12th, 1894.

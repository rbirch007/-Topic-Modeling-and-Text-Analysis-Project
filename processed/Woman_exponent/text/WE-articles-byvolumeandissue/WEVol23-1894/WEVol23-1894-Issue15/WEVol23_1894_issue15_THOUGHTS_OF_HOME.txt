THOUGHTS OF HOME.

THE beauteous sky of gorgeous hue,
	Now seems to meet the waters blue,
And gallant ship and light canoe
	Are passing far beyond my view
But sweetest thought of all the rast,
	We’ll meet again out in the west.

Afar from ocean’s roar and brine
	There is a distant western clime,
Round which my heart-strings fondly twine,
	That is the home for me and mine,
Oh may we there all safely meet,
	And know the joys of home so sweet.

There is a fair and chosen land,
	Where dwells a true and faithful band,
Led by our Father’s allwise hand.
	And they the Truth so understand,
They would forsake all earthly ties,
	That they might gain eternal lives.

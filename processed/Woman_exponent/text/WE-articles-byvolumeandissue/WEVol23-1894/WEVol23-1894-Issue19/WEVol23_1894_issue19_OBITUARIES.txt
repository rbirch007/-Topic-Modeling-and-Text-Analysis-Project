OBITUARIES.

	DIED at Paragoonah Iron Co. November 11,
1894, of general debility, Margaret Owens, wife of
Thomas R. Owens, born at Cafan—Coedy, Cyr-
mer Brekonshire, South Wales, Nov. 4th, 1831,
baptized into the Church of Jesus Christ of Lat-
terday Saints in 1848; emigrated to America in
1859, emigrated to Utah in 1863. She bore her
afflictions without a murmur and died firm in the
faith. She leaves a husband, seven children, and
twenty-one grand-children to mourn her loss.
Sister Owen was also a teacher in the Relief
Society of Paragoonah ward, and she is greatly
missed in the Society. May she rest in peace un-
til the morning of the first resurrection.

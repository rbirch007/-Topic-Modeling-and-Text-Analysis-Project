IN MEMORIAM.
	We the sisters of the Emerson Relief Society of
the Granite Stake of Zion have been bereft of a
beloved and ever faithful sister and colaborer.
	Dear Faithful Teacher Mary Ann Coulam de-
parted this life Nov. 1906.
	In commeration of her noble example, good
works, pure and humble life undaunted energies
put forth to bless and comfort the poor, sick and
afflicted we pray that our Heavenly Father will
bless and comfort, with His Holy Spirit, Dear
Mary’s brothers, sisters and friends and help them
and us to emulate her worthy example.
	Through our heart-felt love for our dear sister
and respect for her relatives we resolve to pre[-]ent
a copy of this Memoriam to the family of the de-
ceased, to the Exponent and record on our books.
	Her exemplary life was a beacon light to all
who knew her.
	In behalf of her sisters of Emerson Ward.
					ARMEDA S. YOUNG,
								President.
					BARBARA M JONES,
					CECIY C. BARTON,
								Counselors.
					PRUDENCE BROWN,
								Secretary.

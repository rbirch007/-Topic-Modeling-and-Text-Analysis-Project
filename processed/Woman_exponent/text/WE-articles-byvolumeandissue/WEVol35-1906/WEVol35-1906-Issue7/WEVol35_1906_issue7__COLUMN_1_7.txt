		F. MARION CRAWFORD.
	F. Marion Crawford was born at the
Baths of Lucca, Italy, August 2, 1845,
son of Thomas Crawford, the American
sculptor, and nephew of Julia Ward Howe.
Was sent as a boy to St. Paul’s school,
at Concord, New Hampshire. Return-
ing to Europe he was at Cambridge Uni-
versity from 1870 to 1874, and for several
years studied philosophy and the lan-
guages at Heidleburg, the Polytechnicum
(Carlshruhe), and the university at Rome.

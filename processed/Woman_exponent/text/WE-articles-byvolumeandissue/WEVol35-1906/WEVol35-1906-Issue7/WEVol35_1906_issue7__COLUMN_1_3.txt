RELIEF SOCIETY REPORT.
BOX ELDER STAKE.
	The conference of the Relief Society
of Box Elder Stake was held on Tuesday,
November 13, 1906, at Brigham City
President Susannah P. Booth presiding.
Present of the stake presidency, Elder
Lucius A. Snow.
	After singing, prayer, singing again, the
roll was called, twelve branches were rep-
resented. Minutes were read and ac-
cepted.

Lord for the privilege of attending this
conference; was thankful to see a good

	Some time after the death of the
Prophet she married again, but still con-
tinued to live in what was known as the
New Mansion House until her death
which occurred April 30, 1879.
	On the slope of the hill near the old
home several members of  the family are
buried. The broad Mississippi River
flows near on its way to the sea. In a
somewhat neglected and uncared for spot
is a grave over which lies a flat stone
bearing the inscription:
		Emma Biddamon,
			Born
		July 10, 1804,
			Died
		April 30, 1879.

			RECEPTION.
	Thursday evening a reception was given
at the Bee Hive House to the Relief
Society Stake and Mission Presidents or
their representatives by the General
Officers’ Board, and Missionaries of the
Society. It was intended to be very in-
formal, but a spirit of good fellowship
and freedom pervaded the gathering, and
it is hoped a closer bond of unity might
thus be formed and all become better ac-
quainted with each other.
	The spacious and elegant rooms were
beautifully decorated, with lovely flow-
ers and autumn leaves and presented an
artistic appearance.

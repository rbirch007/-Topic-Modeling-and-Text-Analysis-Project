IN MEMORIAM.
	I send you a brief sketch of the life of Sister
Elizabeth Brook Fox, who passed away April 9,
1906:
	Sister Fox was born at Leeds, Yorkshire, Eng-
land, January 5, 1825; was baptized into the
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in
March, 1851. On April 23, 1861, she sailed for
America in the ship Underwriter; she arrived at
Florence June 2nd, and at Salt Lake City on Sep-
tember 12, 1861; she then moved to Lehi, where
she married Elder Charles W. Fox, February,
1862. In November she with her husband moved
to Franklin.
	June 2nd, 1874, Sister Fox was set apart as pres-
ident of the Franklin Relief Society and served
in that capacity for ten years.
	When the Oneida Stake was organizsd she was
set apart as Stake President of the Relief Society;
she labored faithfully in that office for 16 years,
when failing health compelled her resignation.

EDNA'S SONG.
MAUD ELLEN BAGGARLEY.
Night shadows have hidden the town,
And the lights of heaven shine;
While adown the river of dreams
Thou art drifting, child of mine;

That beautiful river of dreams
Floweth on through peaceful vales,
’Neath cloudless skies, where flowers bloom
And fairies tell wond'rous tales.

Dost find the country of thy dreams
More suited to baby feet?
Are they kind, the dream-folks there,
To little ones small and sweet?

Wilt thon return to mother soon,
O child, with thine eyes of blue?
Or is she a part of a dream,
And this world a dream-land, too?
Salt Lake City, June 12, 1906.

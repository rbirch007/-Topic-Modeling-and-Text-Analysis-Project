WEBER STAKE.

	Minutes of the Quarterly Conference of
the Relief Society of the Weber Stake of
Zion. held at Relief Society Hall in
Ogden City, Utah, on Thursday, June 14,
1906. Conference convened at ten
o’clock a. m., president Jane S. Richards
presiding. Singing “Arise, O glorious
Zion.” Prayer by Bishop Robert Mc-
quarrie. Singing, “Redeemer of Israel.”
Minutes of the March conference were
read and approved.
	President Richards made opening re-
marks; was pleased to meet again with
the sisters, and thankful that there were
so many present at such a busy time; felt
that those who were here were desirous
of being here, and that they were living
so as to deserye and secure the blessings
of the Lord.
	Counselor Emily M. Shurtliff was
thankful to be present; spoke of the
many times we have met throughout the
Church in this capacity, and of the many
brethren grown grey in this cause,and who
had been faithful through it all, it gave her
great joy to mingle with the Saints in
this capacity, and she felt that they were
all choice spirits, and considered it a great
blessing to have this privilege to live
and associate with them; her desire was
to have the loving confidence of her
brethren and sisters, and Sister Shurtliff
bore her testimony to the truth of the
Gospel.

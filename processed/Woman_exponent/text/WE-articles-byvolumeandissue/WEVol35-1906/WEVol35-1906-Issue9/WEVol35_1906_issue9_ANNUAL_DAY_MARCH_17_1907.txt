ANNUAL DAY, MARCH 17, 1907.
	The Relief Society held its meeting in
the Central School house in Fillmore,
Utah. After the opening exercises, the
following program was rendered:
	Opening address, Stake President Isa-
bella Robison; Song, Helen Greenwood;
Reading, Clara M. Swallow; Address, El-
der Christian Anderson; Reminiscences,
Helen Callister; Recitation, Francis Ras-
muson; Closing address, President of Fill-
more R. S., Melissa Olsen.
	We also have to record the death of
Sister Maggie Starley while driving with
her husband—the horses became fright-
ened and ran away. Sister Starley
jumped from the buggy, her dress became
entangled and she was dragged some dis-
tance, and when Brother Starley—who
was thrown from the vehicle—reached
her he found her quite dead. This comes
as a severe shock to Fillmore, where
Sister Starley has lived since childhood,
and where she was much beloved. The
appended resolution expresses our sorrow
and bereavement.
	CLARA M. SWALLOW, Secretary.

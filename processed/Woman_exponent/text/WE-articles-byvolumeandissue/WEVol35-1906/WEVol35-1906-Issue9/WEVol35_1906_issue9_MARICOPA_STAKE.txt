		MARICOPA STAKE.
	Relief Society Conference of the Mari-
copa Stake met in the Relief Society
Hall, Mesa, February 21, 1907. President
Melissa Hunsaker presiding. Opened by
singing “Think gently of the erring one.”
Prayer Brother Mahonri Stuart. Singing
“Let Zion in her bearty rise.” Roll Call.
Showing all the Wards represented. Mi-
nutes of previous conference read. Stake
officers and General Relief Society were

[Column 3]
sustained by vote. President Melissa
Hunsaker made opening remarks, said
she felt to rejoice in the blessings of the
Lord. Knew the Lord blessed the Relief
Society work and its faithful workers.
	Reports from four wards were given,
all were doing as well as possible. There
had been much sickness through the last
three months. Both, teachers and mem-
bers, were desirous of doing their duty
and seemed to have a testimony alive
with in themselves that the Gospel is true
and this Society being organized by the
Prophet Joseph Smith was one of our
Father’s purposes for the uplifting of
woman; and the Savior said where a few
met together in His name He would be
there to bless.
	Our brother speakers, felt to rejoice for
the inspiration that led to this organiza-
tion and that we were all the children of
our Father.
	One sister visiting relatives here from
Idaho felt to unite with the sisters in say-
ing that the Gospel as revealed by the
Prophet Joseph Smith was true, spoke on
prayer and teaching our children to pray.
	President Hunsaker made a few closing
remarks, thanked the brethren and sis-
ters, and hoped we felt to rejoice in the
words spoken. Hymn “Guide us, oh thou
great Jehovah.” Benediction Sarah M.
Pomeroy.

NOTES AND NEWS.
	Baroness Alexandra Gripenberg received
the largest vote of any candidate nominat-
ed for the Parliament of Finland by the
Old Finnish party.
	Senora Zelaya, wife of President Zela-
ya of Nicaragua, is said to have made 
herself the idol of the Nicaraguan army by
her active service at the head of the Red
Cross in the recent battles between the
Hondurans and Nicaraguans. She rode
more than 150 miles on muleback to reach
the scene of battle.

		ST. GEORGE, Dec. 24, 1876.

To my Family and Friends at Salt Lake and
		Elsewhere:
	The compliments of the season, wishing you all
a Merry Christmas and Happy New Year. May
you live to accomplish the work given you to do of
our Heavenly Father, and I pray you may do it
faithfully, that you may be prepared to dwell
eternally in the heavens. May God grant it.
Amen.

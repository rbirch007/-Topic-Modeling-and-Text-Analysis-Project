	Life.

	a. How to make oneself worthy of a good

Lecture III.—Marriage.

	a. Marriage ordained of God. b. The
necessity of Marriage. c. Knowledge gained
by obedience to the laws of God.

Lecture IV—Marriage (continued.)

	a. Teach the sacredness of the Temple
marriage compared with the temporal. b.
Dwell upon the rights of eternal inherit-
ance. c. Warn against disinheritance.

Lecture V.—The Home.

	a. Kingdom of woman, she should reign
as queen. b. The necessity of contentment
in the home.

Lecture VI.—Prenatal Culture.

	a. Parents should learn the lesson of self-
control. b. Preparation for motherhood.
c. Proper diet. d. Cleanliness.

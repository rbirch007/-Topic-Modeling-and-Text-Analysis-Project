EDITORIAL NOTES.
	The sisters will please excuse the minutes
of the General Conference of the Relief So-
ciety not appearing in this number as it
was impossible to prepare them in time.
They will appear in the May number of the
paper.
	There are now four new Stake organiza-
tions of the Relief Society in Salt Lake Co.
Liberty, Pioneer, Salt Lake and Ensign, as
yet no Conferences of these Relief Society
organizations have been held, but will be
probably in the near future.
	Sisters Mary Alice C. Lambert and Eliza-
beth J. Stevenson have been appointed by
the General President to attend Cache Stake
Relief Society Conference at Logan, April
30, and Hyrum Stake Conference May 2,
1904.
	On Friday, April 22, 1904, the first Re-
lief Society Conference of the Blackfoot
Stake of Zion was held in Shelley, Idaho,
the newly elected President Ann H. Walk-
er presiding. Sisters Emily S. Richards
and Priscilla Smith representatives from the
General Board attended the Conference and
gave instructions.
	By invitation of the President of Relief
Society Morgan Stake, Sisters Mary Alice
C. Lambert and Lydia D. Alder attended
the Relief Society Conference of that Stake
of Zion, on Sunday, April 17. Sister Eliza-
beth J. Stevenson attended the Relief So-
ciety Conference of Davis Stake held at
Centerville, April 28, by invitation.
	We have on hand minutes of many Re-
lief Society Conferences and Seventeenth of
March celebrations, we are glad the sisters
send them in and such condensed minutes
as we can find space for will be used, but
the material is at hand to help make history,
and therefore it is not sent in vain. The
Outlines of Mother’s Work have taken
much of the space in our little paper and
we trust our sisters understand and appre-
ciate this fact as it has been with a view of
helping the branches in their respective lo-
calities.

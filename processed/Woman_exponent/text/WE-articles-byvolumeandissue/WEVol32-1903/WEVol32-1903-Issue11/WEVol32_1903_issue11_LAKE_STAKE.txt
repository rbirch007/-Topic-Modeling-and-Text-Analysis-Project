	LAKE STAKE.

	After the first organization of the Primary
Associations by Sister Eliza R. Snow, the 
associations increased so rapidly throughout
the Salt Lake Stake of Zion that it was de-
cided to organize in a Stake capacity.
	The first stake organization was effected
on June 19, 1880, Ellen C. Clawson being
chosen as president, with Camilla C. Cobb
as first and Annie Davis (now Watson) as
second counselors, and Vilate Young as
secretary. Shortly after Lydia A. Wells
was appointed second counselor, Annie
Davis having resigned
	The sisters held these positions until the

BEAR LAKE STAKE

	Minutes of annual conference held in the
Bear Lake Stake Tabernacle, July 12, 1903.
	Officers meeting, 10 a. m., where all the
wards were represented, twenty-three in
number, it being the first time that we had
a full report since the Relief Society was
organized in a Stake capacity.
	At 2 p. m., President J. P. Lindsay pre-
siding. Conference began with singing and
prayer. Minutes of last conference read
and approved.
	Sister J. P. Lindsay said if we only put
ourselves in a position we can enjoy every
blessing that is possible to be obtained, we
should each one of us know for ourselves
that this is the true work of God. Was
pleased to meet the sisters from Salt Lake
City and to have their valuable instructions. 
“We have a great mission before us. I
know this to be the true church and
kingdom of God.” Prayed the Lord to
bless and inspire the speakers with His
Holy Spirit. God bless us. Amen. 
Sister Stevenson explained the work of
the Relief Society. Spoke of peace, and
how it should commence in our homes, also
in all our associations. Wished to deliver
Sister B. W. Smith’s message of love to all
the sisters of this Stake. Said we should 
exercise faith and not forget the Lord. The
sisters are "angels of mercy.” Teach our
children faith and instil in their hearts love

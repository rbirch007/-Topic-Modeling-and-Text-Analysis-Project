IN MEMORIAM.
'Blessed, are the dead who die in the Lord,’

	It becomes our sad duty to record the death of
a beloved sister who departed this life Sept. 30th
1903, in Weston, Idaho. Sister Ida Margarett
Jensen; the beloved wife of Elder Hyrum Jensen,
she was born at Weston. February 25th, 1874, and
has always been a faithful Latter-day Saint; when
taken ill with typhoid fever two weeks previous to
her death, her husband was filling a mission in the
North Western States, he was summond home,
and was at her bedside when she passed, to the
great beyond. All that loving hands could do was
done, but it has pleased our Heavenly Father to
remove our dear Sister to a higher sphere. She
leaves a devoted husband and six children to
mourn her loss, the loss of a faithful mother and a
wife. Let us do all we can to heal the wounds of
those who survive her, by deeds of love and kind-
ness, that her children and husband, may they
ever have her example before them that they may
grow in grace and be worthy to meet her in the
life beyond the grave, is the earnest desire of the
members of the Weston Relief Society.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Where as our Heavenly Father in his Wisdom,
has seen fit to remove our Sister Ida Margarett
Jensen from our midst, be it
	Resolved, That while we grieve to lose her and
we mourn with the bereaved husband and relatives,
we feel to bow in humble submission to the will of
our Father in Heaven and say thy will be done.
	Resolved, That we realize in her death, the loss
of a true friend and co-laborer in the Weston Ward
Relief Society.

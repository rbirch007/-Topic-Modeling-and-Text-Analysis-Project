Sixth Lecture:
	
Boyhood and maidenhood.
	(a) Special care now required in re-
	gard to dress and health.
	(b) Give special religious training in
	order that they may honor the Priest-
	hood
	(c) Assist them in selecting proper
	companions and books.
	(d) Courting and marriage in the
	Church so as not to be deprived of
	the blessings of the Gospel.

	The path of duty is the path of safety.

ITEMS FROM SPRING CITY. 
Dear Editor:
	Sister Mettie Maria Nielso[-] one of our 
dear sisters, died over a year ago, and as 
she was a subscriber for the EXPONENT 
and was a faithful member in the Church, 
and ever ready to help the needy, we miss 
her very much. 
	Our Relief Society is in good working 
order with good, energetic officers at the 
head. We have the mother’s work in its 
infancy and hope it will grow and be a suc-
cess. I was very much pleased in reading
the testimonials of the true character of Sis-
ter M. I. Horne and would that the same 
could be said of some of us. 
	The EXPONENT has been a great help to 
me, and would be to every mother if they 
were only converted to its merits. I pray 
the Lord to bless you in your noble efforts 
to do good. 
		Your sister in the Gospel,

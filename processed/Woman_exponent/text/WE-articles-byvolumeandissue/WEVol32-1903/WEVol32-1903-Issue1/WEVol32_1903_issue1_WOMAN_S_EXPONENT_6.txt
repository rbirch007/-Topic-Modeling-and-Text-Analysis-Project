			OBITUARIES.
		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	To the memory of Sister Lucinda Richey.

	Whereas our Heavenly Father has seen fit to call
from this life our beloved sister, Lucinda Richey,
in her seventy-seventh year, be it
	Resolved, that we humbly acknowledge the
wisdom of an Overruling Power, who doeth all
things well, in the loss of our aged sister, who was
a comfort in the hour of trouble, a devoted wife
and mother, a true Latter-day Saint, and a most
faithful worker in the Relief Society, being its
pioneer president for twelve successive years.
	Resolved, that we strive to carry out her wise
counsel, follow her noble example, especially in
never speaking ill of any one, and cherish in our
hearts that love for her which she so truly won.
	Resolved, that a copy of these resolutions be
sent to the bereaved family, one be placed on the
Relief Society record, and one sent to the EX-
PONENT for publication.
				LIZZIE B. PATTERSON,
				ANNA M. MALLORY.
							Secretaries.
	St. Johns, Arizona.

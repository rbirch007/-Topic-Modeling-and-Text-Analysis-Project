ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION.
	The sixty-first anniversary of the organ-
zation of the Relief Society was appropri-
ately observed by the six branches of the
Relief Society of Provo. A meeting was
held in the Tabernacle at 2 p. in., at which
President Mary John presided and the fol-
lowing program was rendered:
	Singing, “Praise to the man," etc.
Prayer by Sister Marilla Daniels. Singing,
"Redeemer of Israel."
	An address by President David John.
	Recitation, “How Leah and I Parted,"
by Miss Pearl Daniels.
	“Great Men have Great Mothers,” by
Sister Emma Jensen.
	Song by Miss Estella Gray.
	“Relief Society Work," by Sister Sarah
E. Cluff.
	An address by Prof. Edwin S. Hinckley.
	Song by Mr. John Johnson.
	An address by Sister Marilla Daniels.
	Singing, “Oh, my Father." Benedic-
tion by Sister Elizabeth Brown.

Lesson 1.—Woman:
1. Woman, who art thou?
	2. Where didst thou come from?
	3. What is thy mission?
	4. What will be thy reward if 
		thou hast fulfilled thy mission
		faithfully?
					Chloe B. Lee

Lesson II.—Home:
	1. Give ideas of a well-planned home 
		(Y. W. J. vol. xiii.)
	2. What are our duties in the home?
	3. Are our homes as we would like 
		them?
	4. Of what should home be typical?
	5. Influence of home and effects.
		(Ref. Young Woman’s Journal,
		vol. xiv, p. 516, vol. xi p. 577.
				Ellen H. Jolley.

home circle.

	1. How shall we awaken a love for

	4. How may we use Church publica-

	5. What stories shall we tell them?

					Ellen H. Jolley.

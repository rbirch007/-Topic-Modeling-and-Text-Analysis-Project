Mrs. Rebecca Douglass Lowe of Atlanta,
ex-president of the General Federation of
Women’s Clubs, was married on St. Valen-
tine’s day to Mr. George Gunton, editor of
Gunton’s Magazine. She will reside in
Washington, D. C.

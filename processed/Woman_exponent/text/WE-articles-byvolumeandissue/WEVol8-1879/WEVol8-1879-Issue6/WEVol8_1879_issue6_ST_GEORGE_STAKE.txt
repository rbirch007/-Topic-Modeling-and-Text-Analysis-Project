ST. GEORGE STAKE.

	Third Quarter Conference of Relief So-
ciety, St. George, Stake, held in St. George
Tabernacle, Friday, June 13, 1879, Presi-
dent Minerva W. Snow, with her Counsel-
ors and the Presidents and officers of the
St. George Relief Societies were present
and a number of brethren and sisters from
the settlements, Bishops and Elders. The
business of the Conference was attended to
and many excellent remarks made by the
sisters, who felt encouraged to see so many
sisters at meeting. Bishop Henry Eyring
of St. George, and Apostle Erastus Snow
made some kind and cheering remarks en-
treating us to persevere, telling us of the
good we had done and could do to each
other, said they were pleased to hear our
remarks and reports. They felt to bless us
in our labors, and were sure that we would
be blessed.
	Statistical and Financial Reports for the
quarter ending June 13, 1878. Teachers,
91; members, 532; meetings held, 93; aver-
age attendance, 189; Woman's Exponent,
91. On hand at date of last report, cash,
$96 20; property, $4.819 38; wheat, 70
bushels. Receipts during the quarter, cash,
$35 90; property, $199 71; wheat, 5 bushels.
Disbursements during the quarter—To the 
poor, cash and property, $290 20; emigra-
tion, $20 00; to Temple, $13 50; home in-
dustries, $18 05; missionaries, $6 50; build-
ings, $138 75. On hand last quarter,
$106 35; property, $4, 066 09; wheat, 73 1/2
bushels; flour, 323 1/2 lbs

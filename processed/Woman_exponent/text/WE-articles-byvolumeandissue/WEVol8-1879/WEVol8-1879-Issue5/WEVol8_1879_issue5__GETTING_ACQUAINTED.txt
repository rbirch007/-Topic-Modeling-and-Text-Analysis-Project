"GETTING ACQUAINTED"

I love the handmaids of the Lord,
The veterans of the cross,
Whose dainty fingers wield the pen
To clear the gold from dross.

I love those little warriors,
So fearless and so fleet,
Who snatch the weapons from the foe,
Then lay them at our feet

I love the Noble Matrons,
Whose daily honrly care.
Is to bring forth to Zion's help
Each jewel rich or rare.

I love to help the helpless,
I'd take them far away
From those who coil around the heart
Then fill it with dismay.

I love each soul that's lovable,
That's fill'd with heavenly light
Whose lives, a daily sacrifice
Are given for the right.

God bless them all, my sisters dear!
	A glorious happy day
Awaits the faithful, clothed in light
	The Saints of Latter Day.

7th, Ward S. L. City June 20th 79.
	A. Q.

MESSRS. CHAS. W. STAYNER AND JOS. J. DAYNES.

All honor to this memorable day!
Let gratitude inspire
Each sturdy heart with sweet, poetic joy,
And patriotic fire!
"Tis the day that noble band
In this wilderness did land,
To make a home for brethren long oppressed;
They left their worldly gains,
And traveled o’er the plains,
To raise the glorious Standard of the West.

CHOROUS (repeat):
Then give three cheers
For the Old Pioneers!
Though many have p[-]ssed away;
We'll thank each one
For the work he has done 
On this glorious pioneer Day."

All honor to this memorable day!
A time of wondrous power.
That made the desert barrenness of earth
To blossom from that hour.
Soon the meadows did expand,
And as if by magic hand,
The fruits and flowers too these valleys blessed;
The happy Pioneer
With naught but God to fear,
Had raised the glorious Standard of the West.

All honor to this memorable day!

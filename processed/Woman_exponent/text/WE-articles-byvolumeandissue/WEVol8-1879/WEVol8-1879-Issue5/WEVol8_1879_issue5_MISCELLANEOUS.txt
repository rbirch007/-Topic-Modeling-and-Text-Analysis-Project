MISCELLANEOUS

	A man's character is like a fence you can-
not strengthen it by whitewash.

	—"George Eliot” has given us one more
work. Its title is, “Impressions of The-
ophrastus" Such.

	The failure of the West of England Bank
is said to have brought financial ruin to two
hundred and eighty maiden ladies.

	The man who swears at his wife if dinner
is five minutes behind time, is a little too
regular in his habits to keep peace in the
family.

	If Adam had been created a boy instead
of a full-grown man, he would have clubbed
all the apples off from that tree before the
serpent had a chance to get through the
fence around the garden.

	Every housekeeper may not know that
if brooms are well dipped in a pail of suds,
after the washing is finished, they will
keep as good as new until they are actually
worn out.

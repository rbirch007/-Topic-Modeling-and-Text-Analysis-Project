ABOUT THE LADIES' [-]ETITION.

DOUBTLESS there are many of our readers who
will be surprised to see that the ladies have
presented a petition to the present Legislature
of Utah, praying the passage of a bill or some
enactment which will remove the obstacle now
in the way of women holding any office of
emolument or trust in the Territory. Suffice to
say, that eyer since the defeat of a similar bill
before the last session of the Legislative As-
sembly, it has been under contemplation. The
ladies of Beaver, without being aware of any
movement of the kind in Salt Lake City, got
up a similar petition, which we shall publish
in our next issue; their petition is numerously
signed.
	As we have not space to say much on this
subject now, we will give the sisters a little
time to think, and hope by the next issue
that we shall have an expression from some of
them in different parts of the Territory on this
subject. It is a matter that concerns all. And
the ladies will watch with intense interest the
action of members on this subject.

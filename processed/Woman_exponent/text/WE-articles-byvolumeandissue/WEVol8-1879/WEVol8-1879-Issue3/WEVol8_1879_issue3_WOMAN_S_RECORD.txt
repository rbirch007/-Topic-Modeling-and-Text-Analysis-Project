WOMAN'S RECORD.

	MRS. JULIA T. Landor, widow of the
poet Walter Savage Landor, died recently
at the Villa Landor in Florence in her
eighty-sixth year.—Ex.

	THE FIRST woman who has been admitted
as a member of the New Haven Medical
Society, is Amea Jackson Ferris, M. D.
The lady graduated at the Pennsylvania
Woman's Medical College.

	MRS. FANNY Holy, of St. Louis, has
written "a very fine article" for the Illinois
"Social Science Monthy," entitled "The
Mystic Element in George Eliot's writings."
Mrs. Holy is an earnest and successful
worker in the cause of suffrage for women.

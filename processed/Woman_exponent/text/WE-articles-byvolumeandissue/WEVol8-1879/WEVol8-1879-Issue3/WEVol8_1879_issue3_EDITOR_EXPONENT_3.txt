	EDITOR EXPONENT:

	We have a paper in our Society called
"The Ladies Garland." It has been edited
since May the 11th, 1879. It is issued once
in two weeks. The character of its arti-
cles, is moral. F. M. Neff is editor, and
M. M. Fisher assistant. The regular con-
tributors are mostly members of the Asso-
ciation with a few exceptions. Mrs. Mar-
garett P. Young, of Salt Lake City, has fav-
ored us with several very interesting
pieces.
			From Your Friend
		H.S. Stillman, Sec'y.

		GOSHEN Valley, June 21st, 1879.

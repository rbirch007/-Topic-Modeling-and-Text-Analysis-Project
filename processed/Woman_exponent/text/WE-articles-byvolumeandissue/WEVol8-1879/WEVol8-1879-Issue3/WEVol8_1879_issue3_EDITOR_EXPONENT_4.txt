	EDITOR EXPONENT:

	In answer to your notice to the Y. L. M.
I. A. in the EXPONENT of June the 1st, I
subjoin the following.
	We edit a paper, its title "The Gem." It
was first edited June the 30th 1876. It is
a semi-monthly paper. The chartcter of its

[Column 2]
articles is essays, an editorial, selected poe-
try, composed by one of the counselors who
edited it first for six months; since then the
most competent of our young ladies have
edited and the members contributed to it
as also the president and her counselors
and occasionally a senior sister.
		I remain your sister in the Gospel.

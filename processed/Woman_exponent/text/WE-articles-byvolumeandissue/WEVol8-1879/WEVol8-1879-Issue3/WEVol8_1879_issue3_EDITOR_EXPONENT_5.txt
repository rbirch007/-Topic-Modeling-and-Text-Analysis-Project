	EDITOR EXPONENT:

	The Young Ladies Mutual Improvement
Association meets every Friday. At every
other meeting we have the following pro-
gramme.
	Reading in the Book of Mormon, select
reading, prose and poetry, and essays. We
also edit a paper semi-monthly called the
"Young Ladies Companion." We take a
copy of the EXPONENT which is read in
our meetings with great interest. The
young ladies speak and bear testimony to
the Gospel.
	During the winter we met in joint ses-
sion with the young men, at which times
we all enjoyed the spirit of the Lord. We
meet twice a month and have sewing be-
sides meeting. We intend putting some
quilts in for the Manti-Temple.
	This spring we have sixty-seven names
enrolled, and have a good average attend-
ance considering the size of our settlement.
	Wishing success to your valuable paper,
we remain your sisters in the gospel,
				Mrs. Julia Rigby, Pres't
				Miss Ella Wilson, Sec'y.

						JUNE 20th 1879.

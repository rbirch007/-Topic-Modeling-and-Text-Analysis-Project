	EDITOR EXPONENT:

		We, the Young Ladies Improvement
Association of the 8th Ward edit a paper
called "The Amateur."
	The first paper was edited Nov. 29th '78
by Annie Lynch and Sarah Hawkins.
	At every fourth meeting the paper is
read and the editors chosen to edit the next
paper, among whom are Abbe R. Slater,
Sarah Barney and Libbie Sheets. The ar-
ticles are essays on different subjects, orig-
inal poetry and a few selections.
	A. R. Slater, Esther Fletcher, Rose Need-
ham, Eliza Snell, Emily Berry, A. Howard,
Annie Lynch, Sarah Hawkins, J. Connelly,
M. Palmer S. H Leaver, R. Chamberlain
are among the contributors.
	Since we have edited our paper more in-
terest is taken in literary pursuits.
					Sarah Hawkins, Sec'y.

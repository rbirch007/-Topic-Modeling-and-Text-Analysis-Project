			ST. GEORGE, Sept. 15, 1879.
	Fourth Quarterly Conference of the Re-
lief Society of St. George Stake, held in
St. George Tabernacle, Friday, Sept. 12,
1879. President Minerva W. Snow being
absent, Coun. Hannah H. Romney took
charge of the meeting.
	The statistical and financial reports were
read and accepted. The attendance was
not as large as usual, but a good spirit pre-
vailed, and we all felt that there was great
satisfaction in doing our duty and trying to
live the lives of Saints. Pres. McAllister
was present and encouraged the sisters to
continue in the good work.
	Statistical: Teachers, 74; members, 438;
total officers and members, 548; WOMAN'S
EXPONENT, 76. Financial: On hand at
date of last report, cash, $82.60, property,
$3,903.64; wheat, 35 5-6 bu.; flour, 23 1/2 lbs.
Receipts during the quarter; cash, $19.23;
property, $141.79; wheat 73 bu.; flour, 24 1/2
lbs. Disbursements: To the needy, in cash,
50 cts; property, $35.75; home industries,
cash, $5.25; property, $15; books, cash,
$2 50; missionary, cash, $11.40; building,
property, $10. On hand in cash, $85.33;
property, $3,976.18; wheat, 97 bu. 7 lbs.;
flour, 48lbs.

EDITOR EXPONENT:

	The Primary Association of St. John's,
Malad Valley, held its first Quarterly Con-
ference on the 4th of August, Sister Han-
nah James presiding. Coun. Reese, from
Malad, was present. Meeting opened at 2
o'clock p. m. After singing by the chil-
dren and prayer by Coun. Reese, there were
songs, recitations and reading by the mem-
bers. Mrs. Mary Kent, President of the
St. John's Relief Society, and many of the
members were present and gave some good
instructions to the little folks. Coun. Reese
spoke encouragingly to the children in re-
gard to reciting and general improvement.
Meeting closed by singing and benediction.
After the children were dismissed cakes
and candy were passed around and all
seemed pleased with the Conference. There
has been one quilt made by the members
of the Association and the means donated
to the Logan Temple. We have twenty-
eight members, and the Association is in a
good condition as regards improvement.
	Wishing success to your valuable paper,
we remain your sisters &c.,
				HANNAH JAMES, Pres't.
				F. M. KENT, Sec'y.

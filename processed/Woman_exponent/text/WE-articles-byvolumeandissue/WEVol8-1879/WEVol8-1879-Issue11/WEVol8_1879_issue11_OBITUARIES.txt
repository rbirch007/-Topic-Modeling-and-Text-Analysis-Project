OBITUARIES.

	DIED at Washington Washington  Co., Sep-
tember the 16 of Tiphoid Pneumonia, Harrison
Pearce, a twin son, of Elijah M. and  Henriet-
ta Steers, born June 17, 1879.
Why is it that we mortals feel
Such grief, when death draw's nigh?
It only brings us closer to
Our God, Who rules on high.

			LEWISTON, CACH CO.

DEAR SISTER: Our Relief Society here
held its annual meeting the 17th Jan. in
which some changes were made in the
officers, we thought proper to report. The
second counselor, assistant secretary and
treasurer resigned from office. Mrs. Mar-
tha Wiser was appointed second counselor
and Mrs, A. Kaun assistant secretary and
Mrs. Sophia Funk treasurer. The sisters
are trying to do the best they can.

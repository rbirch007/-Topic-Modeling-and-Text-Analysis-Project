LADIES.

	NEPHI, Sept, 19 1878.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The Young Ladies Mutual Improvement
Association meets every Friday. Our
exercises consist in lectures from the Bible
and Book of Mormon; also songs, select
reading and recitations. We have edited
a paper in our Association, three years, the
name of the paper is "The Young Ladies
Journal." It is issued weekly; contains
original articles, and selections upon relig-
ious subjects.	Respectfully

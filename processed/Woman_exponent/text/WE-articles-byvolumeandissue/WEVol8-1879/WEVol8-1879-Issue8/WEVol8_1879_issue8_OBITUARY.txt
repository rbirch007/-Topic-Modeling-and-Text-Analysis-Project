OBITUARY.

	DIED, at Manti City, Aug. 8, 1879, Anna Ma-
ria Sorenson Peterson.  Deceased was born
June 22, 1822, in Westburg, Scandinavia; bap-
tized into the Church of Jesus Christ of Lat-
ter-day Saints September 12, 1835, emigrated
in 1857.  She was a faithful teacher in the Re-
lief Society of Manti.  She has left her family
and nume[-]ous friends to mourn her loss, but
we feel assured that our los[-] is her gain, and
that she has gone to the home of the just, and
will come forth in the morn of the first resur-
rection, clothed with immortality and eternal
life.  Peace to her ashes.

MAY WE BE TRUE.

May we be true, and faithful too
To serve the Lord our God,
And him obey without delay
Nor need his chast'nig rod.

May we delight to honor those
Who do the priesthood hold,
That live for right, and not for might,
For truth, instead of gold.

We'll watch and pray, both night and day
And God his peace will give,
To those who seek to honor him.
And strive as saints to live.

May we as children of one God
Unite our hands and hearts,
And God will surely bless each one
That seeks to do his part.

We'll purge ourselves of ev'ry sin,
And purify our souls;
And fit ourselves to enter in
Our Heavenly Father's fold.

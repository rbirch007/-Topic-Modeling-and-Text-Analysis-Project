REBECCA WAREHAM.


Chicken Salad—Stew chickens tender and cut
the meat in small pieces; to every pint of meat
add same quantity of celery, cut fine; to every
quart of these, when mixed, add four hard-boiled
eggs chopped fine, one small potatoe mashed smooth,
a piece of butter, a teaspoonful each of ground
mustard, pepper and salt, and one-half cup of
vinegar.

OBITUARY.

	DIED, At St. George, Utah, on the 26th day
of June, 1879, at 10 o'clock a. m., in a quiet
and peaceful manner, Adella, daughter of
Charles E. and Ruth Young Johnson, aged 4
months and 5 days. "Not dead, but gone be-
fore."
	Respectfully yours,
		C. E. Johnson.
St. George, June 29, 1879.

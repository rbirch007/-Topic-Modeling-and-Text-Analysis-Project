	BRIGHAM CITY.
	We have edited a paper in the Young
Ladies Association of the first Ward, one 
year the first of last June. It is called,
"Young Ladies' Star." It is edited monthly
by the different members, and contributed
to by the same; it contains original essays
and selections, principally on religious sub-
jects.  Your Sisters.
		MISS. FANNY GRAEHL, Pres.

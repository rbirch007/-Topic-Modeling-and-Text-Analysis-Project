	King Alfonso of Spain and the Austrian
Archduchess Maria Christian were married
at Madrid on Saturday, the nuptials being
solemnized with great pomp and ceremony
at the Atocha Church. The city abounded
in decorations in honor of the occasion,
and the young sovereigns were everywhere
received with cordial manifestations of
favor.

		MISS. OCTAVIA PIERCE, Sec'y.

	The Y. L. M. [-]. Association of the second
Ward, Brigham City, edit a semi monthly,
manuscript paper entitled, The Young
Ladies' Gazette, and change Editors with
every number; size two sheets foolscap
paper, contributers the officers and members
of the Association who write upon various
subjects.
	The second Volume is now being written
which commenced last June.

	We edit a semi-monthly manuscript

of the Third Ward of Brigham City. We
change editor and contributors with each
number, the writings are mostly original

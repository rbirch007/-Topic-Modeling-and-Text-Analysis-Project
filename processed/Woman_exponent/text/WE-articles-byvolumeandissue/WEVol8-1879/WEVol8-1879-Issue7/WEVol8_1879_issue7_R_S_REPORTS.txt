R. S. REPORTS.

	Report of the Heber City Relief Society
for the quarter ending April 30, 1879.
	On hand Feb. 2, 1879, $24 30; received
during the quarter, $19 40; Total, $43 70.
Disbursed to the poor, $33 65. Balance on 
hand at date, $10 05.
	The Society at present numbers 79 active
members; average attendance, 48. Mrs.
Margaret Muir, President; Mrs. Ann Mur-
dock and Mrs. Elizabeth Rasband, Counsel-
lors; Mrs. Ellen Lee, Secretary; Mrs. Me-
lissa Reynolds, Assistant Secretary; Mrs.
Louisa Johnson, Treasurer.
	For the quarter ending July 31, 1879:
Donations received, $14 90. Disbursed to
the poor, $7 25. Balance in Treasury,
$7 65.

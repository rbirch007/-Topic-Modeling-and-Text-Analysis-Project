IN MEMORIAM.

	DIED, of diphtheria, at Harrisburg, Wash-
ington Co., Utah Territory. Damie Earl, be-
loved daughter of George A. and Zilpha D. Earl.
Damie was born July 8, 1875, and her death
occurred on the 7th of August, ult. She was
four years and one month old, and was a very
precocious child, seeming to give promise of
rare gifts, and knowledge far beyond her years.
	Also, at the same place, Ellen, infant
daughter of Harriet Ellen and Wm. Laney,
Jun., who died Aug. 21, aged eight months
and two days. She was taken with scarletina,
but suffered very much with pain in her head.
How many such are taken away, perhaps in
love, and although we mourn their loss to us,
yet we cannot but feel "Our Father" doeth all
things well. "Suffer little children to come
unto me, for of such is the kingdom of heav-
en."

		MILLICENT FERRIS,

	It becomes  our  painful  duty  to  record the
death of one of our oldest citizens.  Sister Ma-
ria  Colebrook peaceably passed away on Feb.
6, 1880.  She was born in Cheltenham, Gloces-
tershire, England, March 21, 1823; was mar-
ried to Charles Colebrook, March 10, 1847.
Arrived in Salt Lake City in 1851, where she
resided until her death.  She had been afflict-
ed for many years  with  that fell  disease, can-
cer, from which she suffered most intensely for
a long time.  She clung tenaciously to life, but
towards the last became quite resigned, and
even desired “to go  to  her rest.” She was re-
markable for her great  deeds  of  charity, often
administering  to the wants of the needy, and
relieving the distressed in a way that the re-
cipients knew not their benefactress. “Peace
to her ashes!” Her example might well be
imitated  by  many who make greater preten-
sions.                                                  E. B.

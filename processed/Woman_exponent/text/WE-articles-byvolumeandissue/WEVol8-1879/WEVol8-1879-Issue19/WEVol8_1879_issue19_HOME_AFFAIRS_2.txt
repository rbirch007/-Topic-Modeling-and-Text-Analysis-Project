	WE have recived by courtesy of Mrs. Spencer
of Washington, in pamphlet form, the Argu-
ments in behalf of the 16th, Amendment of
the ladies before the Judiciary Committee of
the House of Representitives Jan. 24, 1880.

	THE performance of the “Sorcerer” by Zion's
Musical Society in the Theatre recently is said
to have been very creditable indeed. As we
did not witness the performance on either oc-
casion we cannot criticise but think it wise and
proper to give due credit to home talent. The
young people of this Church should excel in
music which is one of the chief elements of a
higher life.


﻿The Sisters Quarterly Conference of this
Stake of Zion will convene in the 14th, Ward
Assembly Rooms on Friday and Saturday the
19 and 20th, insts. at 10 o’clock A. M. The
first day will be devoted to the Relief Society,
Saturday morning to Young Ladies Mutual
Improvement Associations and the afternoon
to the Primary Associations. The secretaries
of these various organizations will please send
reports as early as posible directed to this
office.

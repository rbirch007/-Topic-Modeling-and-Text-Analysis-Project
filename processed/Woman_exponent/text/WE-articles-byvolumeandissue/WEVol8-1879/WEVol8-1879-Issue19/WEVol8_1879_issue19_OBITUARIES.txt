OBITUARIES.

	DIED, at Tooele City, Feb. 14, 1880, in child-
birth, Sarah Emma, the beloved wife of Elder
George Crainer, born Oct., 5, 1842, in Nauvoo,
Hancock Co., Ill. Sister Crainer was a good,
faithful  La[-]ter-day S[-]int, and was greatly re-
spected by all who knew her. She leaves a
husband and seven children to mourn her loss.
She has gone to join her little ones and a host
of Saints behind the vail. May God, in His
infinite mercy and goodness, bless the bereaved
and comfort them as He alone can.

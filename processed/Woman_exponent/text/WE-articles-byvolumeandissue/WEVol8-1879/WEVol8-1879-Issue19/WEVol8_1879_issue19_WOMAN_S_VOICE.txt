WOMAN'S VOICE.
	Wonderful are the ways of God, and none
can find them out but the humble in spirit; and 
the pure in heart. The eye that is single to
the glory of God, can behold his work upon
the earth,that he has given us to do, we have
no time to throw away but like the busy bee
that gathers honey in the hive so we should
gather in all the love and power of the Gospel,
and help to establish Zion. Work for the 
kingdom of God that we may enjoy a fulness
of glory in the world to come. We have left
our comfortable homes and gathered togeth-
er as one great family of children to be taught
more perfectly the ways of life and salvation.
I feel thakful that I live in this day that the 
Gospel of Jesus has been restored to the earth,
and the sound has gone forth "Repent ye  for
the kingdom of heaven is at hand." I gave
heed to the sound thereof and sought to know
from whence it came, the truth of this great
work that has been commenced in this our day,
I still have that testimony to bear, and my
faith grows stronger as I grow in the knowledge
of God and his works. I am sure that the spirit 
of the Lord is working in the hearts of the 
children, and a great power is rising up in Zion;
and the time is near at hand when the wicked
in Zion shall fear and tremble and flee when
no man pursueth.
	Let us be patient and endure to the end and 
see the salvation of God.

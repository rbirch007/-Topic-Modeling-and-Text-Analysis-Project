THIS year Woman's  Suffrage  is making
considerable  progress  in  the  States.  In
Iowa a majority vote decided  to  submit  to
the de[-]ision  of  the people  an  amendment
to the Constitution, entitling men and wo-
men citizens to  equal  suffrage, irrespective
of sex.  In  New  York,  Massachusetts ,and
four other States  the  women  are  allowed
school suffrage, and to hold such offices
as pertain to educational matters.  In  Rhode
Island and Connecticut  the Governors have
recommended the same.

WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


ITEM.
EDITOR EXPONENT:	
	While reading to-day, I noticed a quotation
from a chapter on Character, which may be
found in Rev. William G. Elliot’s Lecture to
Young Women, which commences thus: “Lit-
tle as may be our reject for a man without
good principles, a  woman without good princi-
ples deserves and will receive less.”
	How can a woman  without good principles
be more despicable  than a man in the same
state?  Is it superiority  or  inferiority that  is
claimed for a woman? 
	Hoping that some of our talented sisters will
give us a true woman’s view of the subject, and
heartily wishing you success in your work for
the elevation of woman,
		 Yours respectfully,

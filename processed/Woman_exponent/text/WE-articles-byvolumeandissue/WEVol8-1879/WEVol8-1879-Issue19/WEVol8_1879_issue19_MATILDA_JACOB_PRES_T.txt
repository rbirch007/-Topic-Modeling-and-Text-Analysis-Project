			MATILDA JACOB, Pres't.


ON Saturday,  March 6,  an  interesting
concert was given in Farmington meeting
house by the  Primary  Association  of  that
place, under  the  supervision of  Mrs. Aure-
lia  S.  Rogers and her counselors, at which
Sisters E. R. Snow  and Z.D. Young were
present by invitation, The Farmington P.
A. was the first one organized, and it has
been effective  of  great  good  in  work and
influence. We have before us a programme
of the concert, which we shall publish in
full in our next issue, partly for the benefit
of other  associations  who  may like  some
hints on this subject.  Every day encourag-
ing reports reach us of these  organizations
among the young, and it is a great satisfac-
tion to  know  that the  children  are  being
carefully instructed in the proper channel.

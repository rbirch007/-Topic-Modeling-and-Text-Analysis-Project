PRIMARY CONCERT.

	On the 25th, of October it being the
birthday of our respected President Hannah
Brower, we held a Primary Concert which
consisted of many beautiful songs and little
essays got up for the occasion, and praise is
due to our young brothers and sisters for
the improvement that they have already
made. Coun M. Thompson presented the
President a beautiful bouquet of flowers
also the little girls a picture representing
childhood at play, which were received
with grateful acknowledgment by our


worth President. All went on in good
order and each returned to their several
homes rejoicing in the work of the Primary
Class. The Concert was adjourned till the
first day of June, 1880. The day on which
it was organized it being the birthday of
our beloved President and Prophet Brig-
ham Young.

	Richmond.		M. A. WEBB.

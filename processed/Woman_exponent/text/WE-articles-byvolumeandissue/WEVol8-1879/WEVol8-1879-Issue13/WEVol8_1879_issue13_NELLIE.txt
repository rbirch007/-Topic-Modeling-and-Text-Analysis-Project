	NELLIE.


A LITTLE girl addressing her sister asked,
"What was the chaos pa was reading about
to-day?" To which the sister replied,
"'Twas a great pile of nothing, and no
place to put it in."
	A LADY who had quarrelled with her
bald-headed lover said, in dismissing him,
"What is delightful about you, my friend,
is that I have not the trouble of sending
you back any locks of hair."

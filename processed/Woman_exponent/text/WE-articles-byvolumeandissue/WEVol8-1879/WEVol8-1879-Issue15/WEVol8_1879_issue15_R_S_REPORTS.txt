R. S. REPORTS.

	The Relief Society of Beaver has lately
been reorganized, by Pres. Murdock and
counselors as followes.
	Mrs. Delila F. Cox, President, Sarah M.
Dell and Harriet E. Sheperd, Counselors,
Mary E. Ashworth, Secretary, Lizzie
Farnsworth, Ass't. Sec'y, Mantie J. Shipp,
Treasurer.
	The members enrolled number 117.
Grain on hand 173 and a half bushels. Our
building is almost completed,and though we
are considerably in debt, we trust that through
union energyand perseverance, assisted by
the blessings of our heavenly Father, we
shall be enabled to go forward in the good
work.
	With many good wishes for the EX-
PONENT, I remain your Sister

	PRIMARY ENTERTAINMENTS.

	The children of Goshen Primary Associa-
tion on the 5th, of Nov. last gave their Pres.
Mrs. Sarah Roberts a Surprise Party and
presented her with a Book of Doc. and Cov.
for which they each contributed 5 cts. as a
token of their love and gratitude some ap-
propriate lines were inscribed therein com-
posed by Mrs. C. Higginson. Sister Roberts
tendered her appreciation of the respect
shown her, feeling quite overjoyed and
hoped God's blessings would rest upon her
labors and felt to bless each child. A
bountiful picnic, dancing, singing and re-
citations made a pleasant afternoon's enter-
tainment. The Bishop and Counselors the
Pres. of Relief Society and her Counselors
and many elderly persons were present; all
felt well repaid for coming together.
		M. A. Till Sec'y.
	The lines spoken of were very pretty but
as we cannot possibly publish poetry for all
the Primary Associations we must not pub-
lish for any.

	The Primary Association of East Bounti-
ful was organized April the 19, 1879. We
thought it would be interesting to send the
proceedings of a Concert held here on Tues-
day Nov. 1879, which consisted of songs
recitations prepared for the occasion and
great praise is due to our young brethren
and sisters for their good behavior. The
preformance of the May Queen was extreme-
ly good. We had two hours good entertain-
ment and the Congregation left the Taber-
nacle well satisfied.

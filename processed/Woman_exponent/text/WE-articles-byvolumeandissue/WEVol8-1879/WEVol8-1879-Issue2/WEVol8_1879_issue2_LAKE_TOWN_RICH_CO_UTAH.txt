	LAKE TOWN, RICH CO., UTAH.
		April 29, 1879.
EDITOR EXPONENT:
	The Relief Society of this place was re-
organized Feb. 16, 1879, by our worthy
Bishop Ira Nebeker and counselors, with
the following officers:
	Mary Nebeker, President.
	Delia Nebeker,
	Louisa Hodges,	Counselors.
	Emma Pike,
	Harriet Lloyd,		Secretaries.
	Mary E. Findlay, Treasurer.
	Our Society numbers fifty-one members.
Although living in this Northern region
and not being overburdened with this
world's goods, the sisters are liberal and feel
willing to share what they have with those
who are in need. We can truly say our
President is a good woman and very punc-
tual in every duty. We have a general
meeting the first Monday in every month,
a teachers meeting the last Monday, and a
sewing meeting every alternate Thursday.
We are doing our best in helping to build
the Logan Temple and storing grain. We
would be pleased and we hope to see some
of our sisters who visit this Northern coun-
try visit Lake Town before the season is
over.
	Ever your sister in the Gospel,

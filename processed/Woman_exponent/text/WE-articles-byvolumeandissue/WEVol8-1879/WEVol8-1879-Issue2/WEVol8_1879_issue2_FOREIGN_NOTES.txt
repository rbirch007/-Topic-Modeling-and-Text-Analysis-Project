FOREIGN NOTES.

	LIBERTON.—The election of the School
Board for Liberton took place in March.
Miss Redpath, a late member of the late
Board, was re-elected. Miss Burton and
other ladies worked assiduously in her be-
half.
	EDINBURGH.—The triennial election of
the members of the School Board took
place last month. The contest turned prin-
cipally on Church questions with regard to
the men candidates, but Miss Blyth and
Miss Stevenson, who have been members
of the two previous Boards (since 1873),
were re-elected.—Ex.

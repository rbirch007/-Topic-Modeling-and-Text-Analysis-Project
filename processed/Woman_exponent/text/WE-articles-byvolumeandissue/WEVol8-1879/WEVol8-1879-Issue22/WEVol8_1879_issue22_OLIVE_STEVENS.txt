					OLIVE STEVENS.

	The Y. L. M. I. A.,	of East  Mill  Creek  was
organized March 1879.  The  following are  the
officers now acting.  Miss S. A. Skidmore Pres
Miss E. V. Ranck and Miss B. M.Moses, coun-
selors, Miss H. S.  Stillman Sec'y.  Miss D. H.
Neff, Asst. Sec'y,  and  Miss L. Osguthorpe,
Treasuer; Mrs. A. E. Neff  Supt.  Mrs.  F.  M.
Neff Editor, and Mrs. M. M. Fisher Asst.
	We hold our meetings once in two weeks;
our exercises consist  of select reading, recita-
tions, songs, dialogues, and lectures.  We edit
a monthly paper  entitled "The Ladies' Gar-
land."  The members in all number 50. the
average attendance is 30 or 35.  Financial re-
port.  Received from parties $20.65, from Sister
Amy Smith 10 cts. Total $20.75; Disbursements
$19.25.

			R. S. REPORTS.

	The Relief Society of Holden was reorganized
Febuary 3d 1880 with Sister Maria Ashby Pres-
ident Emma J. Bennett and Mary A. Tanner,
Counselors; Olive  Stevens  Recording, and
Pauline Larson,  Corresponding  Secretaries
Hursillar Nixon, Treasurer.  Our society is in a
very thriving condition.  We have 51 members
enrolled  and  we  are  progressing favorable.
Our testimony meetings are held monthly and
working meetings  semi-monthly  they  are
generally well attended.
	On the 15th of March we celebrated the tenth
anniversary of the first organization of our soci-
ety, when the retiring officers were each pres-
ented with a handsome portrait of our beloved
President John Taylor.  We have a new meet-
ing house nearly  completed,  which will be a
great credit to our society when  finished.  A
general good feeling prevails  and a  desire  is
manifested to aid the cause of truth.
	Ever Praying For The Welfare Of Zion.
					PAULINE LARSON.

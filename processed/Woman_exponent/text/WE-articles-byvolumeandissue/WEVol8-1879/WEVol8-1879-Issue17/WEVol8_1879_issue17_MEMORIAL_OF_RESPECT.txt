MEMORIAL OF RESPECT,

	To the memory of Sister Torberg Kristine
Halvorsen.

	Whereas, our much esteemed sister and fel-
low-laborer has, by Divine Providence, been
removed by the hand of death; and, whereas,
by her faith and good works having sustained
the principle of Celestial Marriage; therefore,
	Be it resolved, that we, the sisters of Mar-
riot's Ward R. S., deeply mourn her loss, and
sympathize with her bereaved husband and
family in the loss of so good, honored, esteemed
wife and friend.
	Resolved, that we tender to her bereaved
husband our heartfelt sympathy for the loss
of so amiable a companion and affectionate
mother.
	Resolved, that a copy of these resolutions be
transmitted to the family of the deceased, that
they be recorded in our General Record, and
also furnished for publicatiou in the WOMAN'S

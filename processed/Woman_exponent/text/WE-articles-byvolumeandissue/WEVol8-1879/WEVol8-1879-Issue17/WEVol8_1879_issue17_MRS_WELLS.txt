MRS. WELLS:

	Dear Sister—	*	*	*	Since
my return to Sevier Stake, I enjoyed myself
very much in visiting, with Sisters Bean,
Thurber and Hunt; the settlements of Wil-
low Bend, where was organized a Relief
Society and a Primary Association; Salina,
where was re-organized a Relief Society, a
Primary and a Silk Association; and at
Pedmond, where was organized a Primary
Association. Newly returned from the
cold world, I was in a good condition to ap-
preciate meeting with the Saints and wit-
nessing the labors of our sisters in their
callings. I recognize in these organiza-
tions great good for Israel.
	Hoping that the Lord may bless you and
all who labor for Zion, is the desire of your
co-laborer,

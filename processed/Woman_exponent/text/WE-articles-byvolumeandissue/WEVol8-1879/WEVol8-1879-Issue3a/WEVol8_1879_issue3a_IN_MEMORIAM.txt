IN MEMORIAM.

	Died in the Seventeenth Ward of this
city on the morning of September 18, Elizabeth
Isabel infant daughter of Erastus F. and Ann
Maria Whitney Hall. Deceased was born March
19, 1878. and had been almost a constant
sufferer; She has entered upon a higher and
purer life; and gone to join the innumberable
host of little ones who sing praises to God in
that beautiful land of peace and rest, where
all suffering ceases and there is no more pain
nor sorrow.

	DIED, in the 17, Ward of this city Sept. 25,
1879, Eliza Rose daughter of Richard and
Mary Ann Matthews aged 10 years and three
days. Diseased was a loving and affectionate
child, and had great faith in the efficacy of
prayer, and the administrations of those hold-
ing the priesthood for the healing of the sick.
We sympathize deeply with the bereaved par-
ents, and trust their hearts may be comforted
by the Holy Spirit.

OBITUARIES.

	DIED, at Goodwin’s Ranch, Idaho, of inflama-
tion on the lungs, Mary Adelia, infant daughter
of John and Mary Adelia Homer.  Born  Nov.
30th, 1879; died Jan. 24th, 1880.
Another sweet, angelic child
Joins the bright company,
On which the Savior fondly smiled,
Saying. "Let them come to me!"
						LULA

DIED. In  Ogden  City  Nov. 20, 1879.  Lucy
daughter  of  Ralph  and  Jane  Douglas  born
November 1, 1867.  Her parents are very much
grieved at the loss of this darling child, but in 
aflictions like this there is no comfort save in
the Lord, and the principles of our most holy
religion.  We know that if we  are  faithful we
shall meet our darling Lucy again, where there
is no more pain or sorrow.

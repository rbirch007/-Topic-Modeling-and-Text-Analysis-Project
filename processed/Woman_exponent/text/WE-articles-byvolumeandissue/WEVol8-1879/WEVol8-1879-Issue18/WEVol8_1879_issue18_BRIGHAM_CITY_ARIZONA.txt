	BRIGHAM CITY, ARIZONA.
	The Relief Society of  this place  is doing
very well, considering  our  circumstances.
Mrs.  Mary  E.  Whiting, President,  Mrs.
Elizabeth  B. Smith  and  Mary E. Skinner,
Counselors.  Living as we do in the United
Order, we have no poor to look after, but
we take care of the sick and try to comfort
and bless them.  There are a great  many
ways of doing good in this Church--we can
help to build temples, gather the poor, etc.;
we are trying  to  gather a little  money to
send to the Manti Temple.  We have our
monthly meetings  the  first  Thursday  in
every month, and working meetings every
week;  our  meetings are generally well at-
tended.  We desire to be one with  our sis-
ters  in  the  cause  of  truth.  Your most
valuable pape[-] comes regularly, and is very
much appreciated by  all.  Wishing it suc-
cess, we are  very respectfully your sisters
in the covenant of Truth,
			MARY A. ADAMS Sec'y
Jan 28, 1880.

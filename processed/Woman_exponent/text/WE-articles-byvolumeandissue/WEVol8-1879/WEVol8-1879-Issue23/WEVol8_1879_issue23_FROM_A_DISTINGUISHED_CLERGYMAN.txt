FROM A DISTINGUISHED CLERGYMAN.—
I have known of several persons who re-
garded themselves as greatly benefited, and
some of them as permanently cured of dis-
eases of the kidneys and urinary organs by
your medicine, Warner's Safe Kidney and
Liver Cure. I have known, too, of its use
in similar cases by physicians of the highest
character and standing. I do not doubt
that it has great virtue. J. E. RANKIN.
Washington, D. C., June 19, 1879.

SOME PARAGRAPHS OF CIRCULAR.

	A Circular from the Twelve Apostles has
been published by the "Deseret Evening
News," which gives explicit and definite in-
structions to Presidents of Stakes and Bishops
how to arrange matters pertaining to remitting
P. E. Fund indebtedness, delinquent tithing,
and the donation of cows and sheep; also per-
taining to the wheat stored for Zion, and gen-
eral remarks.

CONFERENCE NOTICE.

	The Sixty-second Annual Conference of the
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints will
convene in the Tabernacle, Salt Lake City, at 10
o'clock on Sunday morning, April 3rd, 1892.
	At noon on Wednesday, the 6th of April, the
cap-stone of the Salt Lake Temple will be laid
with appropriate ceremonies.
	The officers and members of the Church gen-
erally are cordially invited to attend the meetings
of the Conference. Also to be present on the oc-
casion of the laying of the cap-stone of the
Temple.
			WILFORD WOODRUFF.
			GEORGE Q. CANNON, 
			JOSEPH F. SMITH,
				First Presidency.

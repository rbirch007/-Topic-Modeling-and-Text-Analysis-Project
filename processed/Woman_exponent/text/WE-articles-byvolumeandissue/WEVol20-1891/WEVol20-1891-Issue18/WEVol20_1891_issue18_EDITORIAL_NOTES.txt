EDITORIAL NOTES.

	On account of devoting almost our entire
space to the Relief Society Jubilee and its
history, together with the exercises in the
Tabernacle, we are unable to publish the next
chapter of the story "In Rural England," and
other matter contributed, but the story will 
appear in our next issue, and also important
notices of the celebration of the Jubilee in
other places.
	We hope our correspondents will continue
to favor us with an interesting variety of
articles on the various topics of the day, we
invite those who have something to communi-
cate, young or old to write for the WOMAN'S
EXPONENT and help in the advancement of
truth and purity and all those principles and
ideas which will uplift and strengthen woman
in the various walks of life, and aid in lead-
ing humanity to broader channels of enlighten-
ment and a higher conception of the work to
be accomplished in the redemption of the
human family and the establishment of the
reign of righteousness upon the earth.

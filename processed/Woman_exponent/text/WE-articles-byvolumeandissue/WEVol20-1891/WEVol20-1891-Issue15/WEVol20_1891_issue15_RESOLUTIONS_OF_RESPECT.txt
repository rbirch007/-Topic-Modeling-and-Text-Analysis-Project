RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT. 
	Whereas our Heavenly Father has called from our
midst our esteemed sister, therefore be it
	Resolved—that in her death, we the members of the
Relief Society recognize the loss of a faithful and zealous
co-worker.
	Resolved—that we extend our sympathy to the bereaved
husband and children, who have been deprived of a
devoted wife and tender mother.
	Resolved—that a copy of these resolutions be sent to
the sorrowing family, also published in the EXPONENT
and a copy be placed on the records of our Society. 

Dear Lois on earth your sufferings are o'er,
You've met the the lov'd ones on the "Beautiful Shore",
While bereaved ones repine, Oh sweet be thy rest
Father help us to bow to Thy righteous behest.
			JANE NOBLE.

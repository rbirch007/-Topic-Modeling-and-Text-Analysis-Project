			MARY ROCKWOOD, Sec. R. S.


Mrs Harriet Taylor Upton will be at the
suffrage parlors at the Wimodaughsis, 1328, I
Street, Washington, D. C., on two mornings of
each week, and on the other days some other
member of the Congressional Committee of the
National-American W. S. A. will endeavor to
be there. Any letters of inquiry relative to
Congressional work should be addressed to
Mrs. Upton, at 1328, I Street.

GONE TO REST.

	On the 18th of Nov. 1891, we were called to lay in the
silent tomb our much esteemed sister Amanda Dana,
whose death was caused through typhoid fever.
	She was the daughter of Morris and Sarah Phelps was
born in Utah Apr. 27th 1854.
	On the first of Nov. 1869 she was married to Bro George
C. Dana, in Salt Lake City, Utah. She was, for several
years, an energetic laborer in the Primary Association, and
a faithful teacher in the Relief Society at the time of her
demise.


She was a devoted mother, a loving wife and a true
Latter-Day Saint.
	She was the mother of eight children, five of whom are
living and, with their father and a large circle of friends
mourn her loss.
			MARY E. B. FARR
	St. Johns, Ariz.

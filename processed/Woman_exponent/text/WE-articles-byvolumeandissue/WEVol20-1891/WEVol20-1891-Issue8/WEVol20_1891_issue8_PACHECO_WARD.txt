PACHECO WARD.
	The Pacheco Relief Soci ty was organized
Jan. 1, 1891. Its officers are Mary Porter,
President, Olive Moffett, and Pauline Naigle,
Counselors; Eliza Farnsworth, Secretary; Mary
A. Smith, Ass't. Sec'y.; Sarah A. Lunt, Treas-
urer. Besides the officers there are 33 mem-
bers enrolled making a total of 39—during
the time they have held 15 meetings with an
average attendance of 19—their financial re-
port is a very good one.

EDITORIAL NOTES.

	President Zina D. H. Young attended the Relief
Society Conference of Emery Stake held at Price
on Saturday the 17th of October last. And in
company with Counselor Jane S. Richards at-
tended the Relief Society and Young Ladies'
Conference of Cache Stake at Logan on the 20th
of October. M. Isabella Horne President Relief
Society Salt Lake Stake attended the Relief
Society Conference of Davis Stake held at East
Bountiful on Friday Oct. 23rd. At each of these
conferences the sisters enjoyed themselves and
report a good work being accomplished in the
several localities.
	Report of Relief Society from Uintah, Cassia,
Salt Lake and other Stakes of Zion will be pub-
lished in due time—as we cannot devote too much
space to minutes of meetings and exclude other
interesting matter that helps to make variety. We
are very anxious to receive original articles and
poems from the sisters, or experiences which will 
be instructive and edifying and do good to the
writer as well as the reader. Sisters if you have
ideas that will benefit others put them upon paper
and send them to the press, they will reach a
larger number of people in that way, and by that
means you may be sowing some good seed that
will eventually bear fruit. Remember the saying
of Scripture "Cast your bread upon the waters it
will return after many days."

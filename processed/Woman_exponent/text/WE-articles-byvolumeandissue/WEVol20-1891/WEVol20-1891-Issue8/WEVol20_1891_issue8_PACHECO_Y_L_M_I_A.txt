PACHECO Y. L. M. I. A.
	This Association was organized Jan. 1, 1891,
at a meeting called for the purpose of organiz-
ing different associations in the Mexican
Mission, Bishop J. N. Smith presiding. Meet-
ings held 11, members enrolled 21, average
attendance 15, Testimonies 29, Bible exercises
6, Book of Mormon exercises 3, Select read-
ings 13; songs 8, recitations 3, essays 2, con-
joint sessions 3. Meetings discontinued April
10, 1891. Sabra Naigle, President, Sarah
Bancroft, Secretary.

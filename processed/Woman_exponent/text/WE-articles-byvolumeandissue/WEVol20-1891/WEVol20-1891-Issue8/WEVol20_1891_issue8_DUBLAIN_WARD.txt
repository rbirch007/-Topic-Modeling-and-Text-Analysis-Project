DUBLAIN WARD.
	The Dublain Relief Society was reorganized
July 20, 1891. Officers are Ida J. Mortenson,
President; Lydia K. Young, and Charlotte
Carroll, Counselors; Phebe I. Allred, Secre-
tary. The officers and members number 38—
meetings held 5. Average attendance 25.
Work meetings 2, average attendance 20.

OBSTETRICS AND NURSING.

	For the past fifteen years Dr. Ellis R. Shipp
has taught two classes yearly in Obstetrics
and Nursing, and her students have gone to
all parts of Utah on their missions of useful-
ness and good works. The members of her last
classes who have passed successful examina-
tions and received certificates of efficiency are
Mrs. Clara Kofford, Mt. Pleasant; Mrs. Emma
Green, and Miss Hattie Richards, Salt Lake
City; Miss Eliza Thackeray, Croyden; Miss
Susannah Hunsaker, Honeyville: Mrs. Abba
Stephens, Mrs. Emma Burnham, Mrs. Abba
S. Young, and Miss Mary A. Burnham, New
Mexico; Miss Emma Kofod, Levan; Mrs.
Camilla Danielson, Hyrum; Mrs. Penelope
Killpack; Mill Creek; Mrs. Artemesia Ander-
son, and Miss Jane Faux, Moroni; Mrs. Susan
A. Yates, Scipio; Mrs. Allie Young, Rabbit
Valley.
	Ladies desiring to join these classes will do
well to communicate with Dr. E. R. Shipp,
either personally or by letter at No. 34 South,
7th East St. Salt Lake City.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Passed by the Y. L. M. I. Association of Moroni, on
the death of Sister Olive Thomas, who departed this life
Sept. 15, 1891. She was born in Moroni July 17, 1873.
	Whereas, it has pleased an Allwise Providence to call
from our presence our beloved sister, Olive Thomas, a
faithful member and everwilling worker in our Association
whose life of promise has thus been cut short in the bloom 
of youth and usefulness.—
	Be it resolved, that while we mourn the loss of her un-
wavering example to us in her steadfast integrity to the
principles of our Holy Religion, we are comforted in the
assurance that it is well with our sister, who has gone to
receive the reward of a faithful member of the Church of
Jesus Christ.
	We hereby extend our heartfelt sympathy to her parents
and relatives who mourn her loss. May their hearts be
consoled in the assurance that all is well with our sister.


And may we all be as energetic in the performance of our
duties as our sister was.
						ANNA RASMUSSEN, Pres.
						CAROLINE JENSEN, 
						HANNAH LARSON,
									Counselors.

JUAREZ, Y. L. M. I. A.
	The Association was organized Jan. 1, 1891,
officers as follows, Anice R. Scowson, President;
Cynthia Bailey and Sarah E. Russell, Coun-
selors; Caroline Romney, Secretary; Mary A.
Hall, Ass't. Sec'y. and Treasurer. Names en-
rolled 57; average attendance 30; meetings
held 23.

DUBLAIN Y. L. M. I. A.
	This Association was organized August 15,
1891, with Catharine E. Porter, President,
Mary E. Call, and Nancy A. Williams Coun-
selors, Charlotte E. Carroll Secretary, Harriet
Call, Ass't. Sec'y., Persis A. Young, Treasurer,
Amanda E. Williams, Librarian; members en-
rolled 27, meetings held 6, average attendance
17, testimonies borne 44, miscellaneous readings
9, recitations 3, songs 5, essays 3, Bible
lectures 2, other lectures 3.

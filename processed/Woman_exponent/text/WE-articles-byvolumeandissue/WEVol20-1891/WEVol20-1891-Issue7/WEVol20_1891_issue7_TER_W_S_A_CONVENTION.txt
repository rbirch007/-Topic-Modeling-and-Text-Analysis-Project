TER. W. S. A. CONVENTION.

	The Annual Territorial Convention of the Utah
Woman Suffrage Association for the election of
officers and other purposes will convene in the
14th Ward Assembly Rooms in this City on
Saturday October 3. Meetings commencing a
10 a. m. and at 2 p. m. The morning meeting
will be an Executive session devoted to business
and it is earnestly desired that Presidents of the
Auxiliary Associations in the several counties at-
tend or send representatives. In the afternoon
session it is expected to present an interesting,
miscellaneous programme. A general invitation
is extended to all friends of the cause.
	SARAH M. KIMBALL, President.
	ADA CROXALL, Secretary.

CONFERENCE NORICE.

	The Sixty-second Semi-Annual Conference of
the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints,
will commence at ten o’clock on Sunday morning, 
October 4th, 1891, in the Tabernacle, Salt Lake
City.
	A cordial invitation is extended to all the
officers and members of the Church to be present
at the meetings. 
	WILFORD WOODRUFF,
	GEORGE Q. CANNON,
	JOSEPH F. SMITH.
	First Presidency.

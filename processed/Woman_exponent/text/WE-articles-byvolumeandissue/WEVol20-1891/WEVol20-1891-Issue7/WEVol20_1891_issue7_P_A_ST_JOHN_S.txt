		   P. A. ST. JOHN’S.
	Primary Conference of the St. John’s Stake
convened at St. Johns, Sept. 5, 1891. There
were in attendance of the Stake Presidency
Pres. E. B. Coleman, Coun. Anna Mohr, and
Sec. Nancy L. Noble. After the opening exer-
cises the Central Board, Stake, and Local
Officers were unanimously sustained by the 
Conference. After several recitations by the
children, Pres. Coleman encouraged the chil-
dren to tell the truth, be kind, and honor
their parents. Exhorted the parents to look
to the training of their boys, as a great deal
of our future hope depends upon them. Coun.
Anna Mohr, Sister Clara Neilson, David
Rencher, Wm. Gibbons, and Pres. David K.
Udall made interesting remarks. Reports of
the various Primaries were read. Singing,
benediction by Sister July Bingham.
		NANCY L. NOBLE, P. A. Stake Sec.

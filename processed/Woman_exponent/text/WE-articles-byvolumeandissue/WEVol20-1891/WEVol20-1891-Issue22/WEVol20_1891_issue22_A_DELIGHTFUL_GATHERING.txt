A DELIGHTFUL GATHERING.

	Tuesday evening, the 10th ult., the young ladies
of the Seventh Ward Y. L. M. I. A. held their
annual meeting in the ward meeting house, in
this City. The decorations were very artistic
and beautiful and the perfume of the apple
blossoms which had been used made the air
fragrant as a summer bower. There were many
lovely plants in blossom on the platform, and
elegant vases of a variety of flowers were ar-
ranged so ingeniously as to produce the most
charming effect.


The fair faces of the young women made the
picture the hall presented more interesting still,
and the programme was of special interest and
particularly well rendered.

presided, assisted by the other Counselor, Mrs.

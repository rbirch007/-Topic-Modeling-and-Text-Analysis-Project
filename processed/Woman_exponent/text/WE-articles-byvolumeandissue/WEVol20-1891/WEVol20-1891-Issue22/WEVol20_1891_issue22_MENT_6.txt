	Now, in the light of the evidence which we
have upon the subject, it appears to me very
plain that the Lord designed that all His chil-
dren should share alike in the blessings of the
earth, and that when He comes whose right it
is to reign,it will be so arranged; and as "com-
ing events cast their shadows before," many
thinking people will solve the problem, "in
their minds;" but a perfect system of National-


ism, or United Order, will never be established
until every man can recognize in his neighbor
a friend or brother, and all have come to a
unity of faith in the Lord Jesus Christ.

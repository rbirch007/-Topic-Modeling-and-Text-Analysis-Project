EDITORIAL NOTE.

	The statement in one of the Utah papers that
Miss Josephine Kellogg of Provo was the first
woman lawyer of this Territory admitted to
practice at the bar is not correct; years ago Miss
Georgianna Snow, daughter of Judge Z. Snow,
and now Mrs. Carleton, was admitted to the bar in
Salt Lake City, and there are we believe two other
young women who have graduated in the study of
law and been employed in the offices of their
fathers. One young lady, Miss Lee, it is expected
will soon apply for admission to the bar, if she
has not already done so, and if we are reliably
informed there are one or two in Eastern colleges
preparing for graduation in that science. The
well-known lawyer Miss Phebe Cousins was
also admitted to the bar here in Utah some years
ago.

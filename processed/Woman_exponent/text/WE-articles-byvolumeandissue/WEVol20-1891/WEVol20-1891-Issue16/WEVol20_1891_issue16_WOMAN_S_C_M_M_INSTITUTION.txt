WOMAN’S C. M. & M. INSTITUTION.
	Minutes of an annual meeting of  the stock-
holders of the W. C. M. and M. Institution held in
the 14th Ward Assembly Rooms, Feb. 13th 1893,
after the regular Ladies semi-monthly meeting.
	Prayer was offered by Pres. M. I. Horne. The
minutes of the previous, or semi annual, meeting
were read and accepted, after which Pres. Horne
spoke of the necessary absence of some of the
officers of the Institution which she very much re-
gretted, but we must proceed with the business of
our meeting.
	The secretary then read a financial report of the
busines for six months, or up to Feb. 1st, 1892.
Pres. M. I. Horne spoke of the discouragement
with which we have had to meet through the non-
patronage of the stock-holders, and the sisters in
general.  Referred to the faithful labors of the
officers and their desire to promote the welfare of
the enterprise.  Exhorted the sisters to patronize
the institution; she felt proud of the result con-
sidering the state of business generally.
	The resignations of the Treasure, E. H. Wood-
mansee, and the Secretary Gladys Woodmansee
were then read. Pres. M. I. Horne moved that the
resignations be accepted as the retiring officers
had other urgent duties pressing upon them,
though she regretted that they were not able to


still hold those positions, the motion was seconded
and put to vote. Vice Pres. M.Y. Dougall moved
that a vote of thanks be extended to the retiring 
officers, for their efficient service. The motion
was seconded put to vote and carried.
Mrs. E. H. Woodmansee spoke in praise of the
officers of the Institution and encouragingly of
the business, urged the sisters to come and take
more stock.
	The following Board of Directors were then
voted in.
	Mrs. M. I. Horne, M. Y. Dougall, M. W. Wil-
cox, C. C. R. Wells, Carrie S. Thomas, Matilda
P. Spencer, Z. D. H. Young, E. H. Woodmansee.
From which the following officers were chosen,
Pres., M. I. Horne, Vice Pres. M. Y. Dougall,
Treasurer, Mrs. Carrie Thomas.
	Mrs. Minnie James was voted in as Secretary.
Meeting adjourned until the 15th of Aug. or the
Saturday nearest to that date.

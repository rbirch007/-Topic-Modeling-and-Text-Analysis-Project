AMERICAN FORK W. S. A.

	The W. S. A. of American Fork convened
in the meeting house Nov. 29, 1891 to elect
officers for the ensuing term. The exercises
began with singing and prayer, after which
the secretary read financial report for the
year 1891. President Electa Bullock,
presented the business of the meeting. By
the unanimous vote of the meeting Hannah
S. Lapish, was re-elected for president, Mary
A. Evans, Sec., Alice Elsmore, Asst Sec.,
Mary Bonam, Treasurer. Emily Keppernick,
Chairman of the Executive Committee.
Esther Eastmond, Orpha Robinson, Phoebe
Crysteson and Elizabeth Shelley it was left
until another meeting to choose two more to
make the number complete. The Co. Pres.
expressed herself highly pleased with the
success attained the past year, and felt with
such energetic workers sure of success in the
coming year. A vote of thanks was given to
the several Committees for the efficiency in
which they have performed their different
labors. After singing and prayer—Adjourned.
			MARY A. EVANS, Sec.
	American Fork, Jan. 4th, 1892.

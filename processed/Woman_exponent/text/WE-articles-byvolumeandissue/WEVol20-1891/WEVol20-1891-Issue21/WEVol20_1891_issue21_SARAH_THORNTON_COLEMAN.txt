SARAH THORNTON COLEMAN.	
	Sister Sarah Thornton Coleman departed
this life March 1st, 1892, at 3,30 o'clock. She
was born at Little Paxton, Huntingtonshire,
England, June 11th, 1806; baptized into the
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints at
Honeydon, Bedfordshire, in June 1841.
	She was at Nauvoo at the time of the per-
secution of the Saints and emigrated to Utah
among the first settlers.
	Sister Coleman was president of the Relief
Society at Lehi for a number of years. She
lived a faithful saint to the end, and was
respected and loved by all who knew her. At
the time of her death she was aged 85 years, 8
months and 20 days. She had eight children,
seven of whom survive her, 68 grand-children
and about 105 great-grand-children to mourn
their loss. We hope our loss will be her gain.

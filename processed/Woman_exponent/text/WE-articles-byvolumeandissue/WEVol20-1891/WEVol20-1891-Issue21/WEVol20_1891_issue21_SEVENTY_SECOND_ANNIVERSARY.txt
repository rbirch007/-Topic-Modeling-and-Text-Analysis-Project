SEVENTY-SECOND ANNIVERSARY.

	Chihuahua, Mexico, May 1st, 1892.
EDITOR WOMAN'S EXPONENT:
	Dear Sister Wells.—Just two years ago this
very evening, yourself in connection with
about three hundred other precious and choice
souls, met in the 14th Ward Assembly Hall, to
celebrate the 70th anniversary of my mission here
upon this to me, a New World; and in concert
with our loved poetess, who has returned to God
our Heavenly Father from whence we came, this
I can say "inspiredly,"

	"For a wise and glorious purpose
	   Thou hast placed me here on earth,
	And withheld the recollection
	   Of my former friends and birth.

	This is my 72nd anniversary, and in Old
Mexico, far, far away, from loved ones; and now
I propose giving you a brief sketch of how my
72nd birthday was disposed of, in the City of
Chihuahua, Old Mexico, and also a discription of
this city and people, customs and mode of living,
or in other words my 72nd birthday in Mexico.

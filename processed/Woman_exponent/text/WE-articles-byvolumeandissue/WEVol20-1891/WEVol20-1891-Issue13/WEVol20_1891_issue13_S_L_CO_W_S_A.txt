S. L. CO., W. S. A.

	The Salt Lake Co. W. S. A. convened in
the 13th Ward Assembly Rooms on Tuesday,
Dec. 15, 1891. Pres. Elizabeth Howard in
the chair. The first exercise was a song read
by the President. Prayer was offered by
Mary E. Irvine. Comments on the Constitu-
tion by Elizabeth Price.
	Adella Eardley was elected Secy. pro tem.
Pres. E. Howard made some interesting re-
marks on the Constitution, thought we should
teach our sons to be honest, honor-
able men, and educate our girls in the same
manner. Made further remarks in regard to
honesty, indebtedness etc., regretted more
ladies did not attend.
	Minutes of the previous meeting were then
read, and with one amendment approved.
	Maggie Bassett then favored the assembly
with a song. Reading from Robert's Rules of
Order by Maggie Bassett. Political clippings
by Adella Eardley.
	Speech by Nellie C. Taylor, upon the order
and system which govern our meetings, and
 the great need of woman uplifting and sus-
taining woman.
	Phoebe C. Young gave a short address.
Pres. Howard made a few closing remarks.
Meeting adjourned untill the third Tuesday in
Jan. at the same hour and place.
			ADELLA EARDLEY, Sec. pro tem.

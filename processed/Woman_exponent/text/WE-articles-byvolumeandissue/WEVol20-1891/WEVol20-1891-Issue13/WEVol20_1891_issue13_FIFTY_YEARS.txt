FIFTY YEARS.

	To the officers and members of the Relief
Society in all its branches; the revolving wheels
of time remind us that on the 17th of March
1892, half a century will have passed since the
organization of women in this dispensation, after
the heavenly pattern. The first enrollment of the
Relief Society members was seventeen and seven
not present were received as members by recom-
mendation.
	Tens of thousands have since been enrolled
and branches are in healthy working conditions
(in various localities) in different nations, and on
the Islands of the sea.
	The good that has been accomplished through
the instrumentality of this Society is incalcul-
able.
	The General Presidency of the Relief Society
will in the near future issue a circular of instruc-
tion.
			SARAH M. KIMBALL, Secretary.

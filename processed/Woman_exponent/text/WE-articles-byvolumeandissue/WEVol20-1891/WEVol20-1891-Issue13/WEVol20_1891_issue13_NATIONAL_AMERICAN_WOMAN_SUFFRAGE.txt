NATIONAL AMERICAN WOMAN SUFFRAGE
CONVENTION.
	The twenty-fourth annual Washington Con-
vention will be held in The Church of Our
Father, corner L and Thirteenth St., January
17 to 21st inclusive.
	The address of the Treasurer, Jane H.
Spofford is 1412 G. St. N. W.
NOTICE TO THE MEMBERS OF N. A. W. S. A.
	The amendment proposed by me two years
ago to have the State vice-presidents elected by


the National American instead of as now hold-
ing that position by being president of an
auxiliary State society, will come before the
next convention for consideration. I shall
change my amendment so as to ask that the
auxiliary society elect the person who it may
choose to have represent it as the N. A. vice-
president. The point being to make the office
elective instead of ex-officio.

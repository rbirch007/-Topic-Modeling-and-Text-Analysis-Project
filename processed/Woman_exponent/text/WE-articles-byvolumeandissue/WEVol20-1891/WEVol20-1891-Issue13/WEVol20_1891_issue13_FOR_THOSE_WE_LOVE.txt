FOR THOSE WE LOVE.

Tis joy to work for those we love,
	To know our effort makes their care the less.
For our small sacrifices prove,
	So much of loving tenderness.

For those we love no task’s too great—
	For task it truly ceases then to be,
When little needs we can anticipate—
	And leave beloved ones from sorrow free.

For those we love we work and live,
	To us their lives are Heaven’s choicest gift


That make us e'en forget ourselves,
	And from our selfish aims our feelings lift.

For those we love we breathe a prayer
	To keep them safe from every ill,
Receive our Father’s tender care,
	And know and do His holy will.

For those we love, Alas! the dying year
	Has brought so much of pain and care,
We fain would dry the saddening tear—
	And all their cares and sorrows share.

For those we love we wish the glad New Year
	To dawn with rich effulgence bright!
And as its fleecy pinions flutter near
	To give a glimpse of Heaven’s radiant light.

And who are they? with this touch of art,
	To wake affections sweetest thought.
Go thou and ask each loving Mother-heart—
	For there the perfect love is wrought.
E R. S.

RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	[Resolutions of respect to the memory of Sister Aramita
Ashton who departed this life Wednesday June 10th
1891.]
	Whereas;—Our Heavenly Father has in His infinite
wisdom removed from our midst our beloved sister
Aramita Lawrence Ashton, and whereas: her faithful
labors as a teacher in the Relief Society and Sabath
School, also the interest she manifested in the Young
Peoples Association, both by her presence, and wise
counsel, make it fitting that we record our appreciation of
her labors.
	Therefore be it resolved;—that we the officers and
members of the Lehi Relief Sobiety, do mourn her loss,
realizing that we have lost,a noble and benevolent sister,
her heart being always willing, and her hand ready to
help the poor and comfort the sick and afflicted.
	Resolved;—That we tender our heartfelt sympathy to
her bereaved husband and children, her aged mother, and
her sisters in their great affliction.
	Resolved;—That a copy of these resolutions be placed
on the records of the Relief Society, and a copy be sent
to the bereaved family, the WOMAN'S EXPONENT, and
the Lehi Banner.
			M. A. STANDRING,
			KATE ALLDRED
			SARAH EVANS-
	[In behalf of the Lehi Relief Society.]

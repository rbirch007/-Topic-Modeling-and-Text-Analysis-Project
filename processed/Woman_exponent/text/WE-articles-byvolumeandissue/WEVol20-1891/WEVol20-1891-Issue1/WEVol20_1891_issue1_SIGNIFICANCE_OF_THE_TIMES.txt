SIGNIFICANCE OF THE TIMES.

	Wherever the ballot has been given to woman, 
whether for special purposes or equal suffrage, the
best results possible have been obtained, and 
opponents to woman suffrage are unable to point
out disastrous consequences, because of woman’s
vote. It is without doubt a time in the history of 
nations, when woman’s voice, her influence and 
power is absolutely necessary, to accomplish the 
grand achievements and reformations, morally and 
politically that are on the tapis. The time is fast 
approaching when men will find woman’s help
necessary and they will undoubtedly ask for it,
and then let woman be prepared to maintain the
position, and advantages that will be offered un-
derstandingly. So far all along the way of pro-
gress she has had to contend for her just rights,
and demand recognition, but it will not always be 
so; men will see that it will be for their own good
and the uplifting of humanity to acknowledge
woman’s equality, and even already they are 
slowly awaking to this supreme fact, and eventu-
ally they will reach out the “olive branch of
peace,” to woman and whenever this transpires, 
as it surely will, we trust woman’s intuition will
lead her to accept gracefully the proffered favor,

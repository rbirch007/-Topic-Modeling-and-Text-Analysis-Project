NOTICE TO AGENTS.

	The subscribers and agents of the EXPONENT
in the several localities will please notice that it
is time to renew subscriptions and to pay up
arrears, and we hope and trust that there will be
a special interest taken in the paper because of
its being the official organ of the Relief Society,
and also as it is the only publication in Utah that
keeps posted on the Woman Suffrage question,
and that takes pains to publish for the Primary
Associations. The WOMAN'S EXPONENT has an
established reputation outside among the wo-
men's papers, and has been the means of adver-
tising the status of the women of Utah more
than any other periodical. This is not an asser-
tion without proof. It is placed on the list of ex-
changes of prominent papers in England as well
as America, and in its columns the women of
Zion have an opportunity to express ther ideas to
the world. The value of a publication of this
kind can hardly be over-estimated, and the sisters
should be alive to the importance of this matter
and increase their efforts to enlarge the circulation
and make it more attractive in appearance which
cannot be done without money. If the exertion
were made generally this could be accomplished
successfully. Will each agent try what can be
done in her particular locality to secure more
subscribers; $1.00 a year is very little to give to-
wards supporting a literary enterprise. Send in
your lists as early as possible, with the money, do
not delay.

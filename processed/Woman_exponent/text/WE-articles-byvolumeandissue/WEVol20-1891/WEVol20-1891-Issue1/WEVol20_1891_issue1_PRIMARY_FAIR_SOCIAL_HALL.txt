PRIMARY FAIR, SOCIAL HALL.
	The Primary Fair of this Stake was held
in the Social Hall June 18, 19, and 20th,
under the direction of Ellen C. Clawson and
her Counselors. Several of the wards were
unrepresented, but of the Associations that
were represented there was a very good show-
ing of work. It was utterly impossible to
observe all the nice things even, but among
those that we did notice were of boy’s work,
Johnie H Boberg, Draper 9 years old,ladder,
rake and boot jack, Willie Turner South
Cotton Wood 13 years old, a puzzle rattle
cut in wood, also Geo. C. Brown, of the same
place, ladder and rake. Earl Cummings 13th
Ward this City, 3 brackets, Josiah Midgley 7th
Ward,13, tin bucket, Charlie Spencer 7 years
old, crochet covered ball, Stanley Partridge

HYRUM.
EDITOR EXPONENT: 
	Report of the 23rd anniversary of the Re-
lief Society of Hyrum, Cache Stake; meeting
convented at 10 a.m. on the 9th of May.
President E. Liljenquist presiding.
	Sister A. Barber, Pres. R. S. Cache Stake,
Sister Thatcher and M. E. Cook favored us
with their presence, also Pres. Molen, Bishop
Wright and counselors.  After the usual ex-
ercises,  Pres.  Liljenquist addressed the con-
gregation, “I have presided over this branch
of the Society for 21 years.  I love to work
with my sisters, it has been a great pleasure to
me, but my health is poor and the spirit tells 
me my work is accepted, and I wish to resign,
I hope you will kindly release me.”

	Sister M. E. Cook spoke very interestingly
on the laws of health,  Sister J. E. Molen
also spoke a short time, said Sister Liljenquist
has truly been a faithful worker in the Society.
Pres. S. M. Molen and Bishop Wright each
spoke to the sisters, exhorted them to sustain
the officers that should be chosen to preside
over them.
	Alice Unsworth was then nominated and
sustained as President by a unanimous vote,
with C. C. Hansen as first and H. A. Shaw as
second counselors.  Meeting adjourned,singing;
benediction by Coun. James Unsworth.

ST. JOHN'S P. A.
	Semi-annual Conference of the Primary As-
sociations of the St. John’s Stake, held March
5, 1892. Present of the Stake Presidency,
President E. B. Coleman, Coun. Anna Wohr,
and Sec. Nancy S. Noble.
	Pres. E. B. Coleman presiding.  After the
opening exercises the Central Board, Stake
and Local officers were unanimously sustained
by the conference. After which songs, recita-
tions etc., were recited by the children.  Re-
ports of the various associations were then
read.
	Sister Mary E. Farr encouraged the chil-
dren to honor father and mother and attend
their prayers.
	Pres. Coleman wished to have the Primary
teachers obtain charts as soon as possible, as
it would benefit the children greatly.
	Coun. Anna Mohr and Nancy S. Noble en-
couraged the children to do right.
	Pres. David K. Udall made some excellent
remarks.


Singing by the Primary children. Benedic- 
tion by E. A. Noble.

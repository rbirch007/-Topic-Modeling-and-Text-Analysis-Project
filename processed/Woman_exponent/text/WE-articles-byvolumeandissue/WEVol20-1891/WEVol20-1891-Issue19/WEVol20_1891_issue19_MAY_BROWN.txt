			MAY BROWN.


New York courts have recently rendered
the decision that while a woman is entitled
to money earned outside her home, yet the
husband has a right to her services not only in
household work, but in any other manner
which he may direct. "Such services as she
renders him, whether within, or without, the
strict line of her duty, belong to him, and if
he pays her for them it is a gift," becasue he
cannot make a valid contract to pay her for
"extraordinary services."—Ex.

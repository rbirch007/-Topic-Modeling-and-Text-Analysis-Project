UTAH SILK FOR THE WORLD’S FAIR.

Editor Exponent:
	Brother Graves, Supt. Utah Co. Silk Ass’n. at
the Relief Society Conference held in Provo on
the 15th inst. informed the meeting that Mrs. P.
P. Jennings of Salt Lake City, one of the Ladies’
Committee for the World’s Fair wished three silk
dress patterns made which they propose to wear
there,also a piece of Shot Silk for exhibition. The
following resolutions were sustained at the meet-
ing,
	1. Be it resolved that we, members of the Utah
Co. Stake Relief Society, will do all we can to
meet the wishes of the Ladies’ Committee in re-
gard the World’s Fair at Chicago.
	2. Be it resolved that the President of each
Ward Relief Society, appoint a committee of
three from their Ass’n. to assist in the same. 
	3.Be it resolved that an account of this meet-
ing be sent to the Deseret News and Exponent
asking all those who may have reeled silk or
cocoons that will reel,to send word to Mrs. M. A.
Till, Pres, of U. C. S. A. We hope to have the
active co-operation of these Societies, their
services having been enlisted in this industry
from the first organization in 1877. We have
sufficient spun silk to make one hundred and fifty
yds. fabric; but wish those having reeled silk or
good cocoons to confer with us as early as pos-
sible, we also wish to engage the services of one
or two good reelers. We desire to hear from
those interested in Silk Culture and will be
pleased to answer any question pertaining to the
same, and will post the societies in the near
future regarding their appointment of committees
and also what we wish them to do.
	M. A. TILL, Pres. U. Co. S. A.

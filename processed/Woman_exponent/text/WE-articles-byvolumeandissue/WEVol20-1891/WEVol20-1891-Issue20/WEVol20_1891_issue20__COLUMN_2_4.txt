	Pres. Young made a few closing remarks,
speaking in the kindliest manner to the sisters
encouraging them to persevere in their labors of
love and to exercise charity and cultivate the
spirit of humility and blest them with a mother’s
blessing, after which Conference was adjourned
until next October, singing Doxology and bene-
diction was pronounced by Sister R. B. Pratt.

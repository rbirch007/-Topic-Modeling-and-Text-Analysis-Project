W. ﻿S. A. REPORTS.

	The Salt Lake Co. W. S. A. convened in the
13th, Ward Assembly Rooms, April 19th, 1892,
Vice President Louise K. Spencer in the chair.
Opened by singing, "Help The Working Wo-
man", prayer by Elizabeth Mc'Farlane; sing-
ing, "Columbia's Daughters."
	Mrs Nellie C. Taylor made a stirring speech
in behalf of working women, and deprecated
the manner in which some women looked down
upon those of their sex who were obliged to
work for their own support.
	Miss M. Goss gave an organ Solo very nicely.
Elizabeth Mc'Farlane read some newspaper
clippings on the subject of woman's labor
and made some comments on the same.
	Mrs. Maggie E. Bassett and Mr. Thomas
Ashworth, sang a duet, accompanied on the
organ by Prof. E. Stephens, which was very
much appreciated. Speeches by Margaret A.
Caine and Vice Prest. Phebe. C. Young, follow-
ed by a song by Miss Maud Pratt, closed the
exercises. The roll was called and the meeting
adjourned until the third Tuesday in May at
the same time and place.

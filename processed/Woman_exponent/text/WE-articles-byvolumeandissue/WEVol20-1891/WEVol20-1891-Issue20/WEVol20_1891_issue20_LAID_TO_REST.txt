LAID TO REST.
	The beloved Cor. Secretary of the W. P. C.A.,
Mrs.Emilie Tracy Y. Parkhurst, has passed away.
Last Thursday eve. the 21st inst, news was re-


ceived of her death after a lingering illness of a
few months. A woman of rare powers, her in-
fluence has been widely felt.  An unfaltering
friend, her life-work has been devoted to the
bettering of her kind.  She had the devotion and
respect of hosts of admirers whose hearts are
well nigh broken in an irreparable loss. She was
the organizer and stay of the P. C.W. P. A. and
none can fill her place with the same tact and
balance as she exercised.
	Today (Sunday) the sad last rites of the Catho-
lic Church over her body were read. The Church
was filled with mourning friends who had come
to take a farewell glimpse of this noble woman
whose life work ended all too soon. 
	ALMA ALDEN.
San Francisco, April 24, 1892.

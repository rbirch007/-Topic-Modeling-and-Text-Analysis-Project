	TOOELE STAKE.

	Minutes of the conference of the Relief So-
ciety of Tooele Stake, held in Grantsville,
June 13, 1891. President Mary A. Hunter
presiding. There were present on the stand
Apostle F. M.  Lyman, Stake President H. S.
Gowans, his Counselors Chas. L. Anderson
and Geo. F. Richards and Bishop Atkin of
Tooele; of Salt Lake, Sisters Jane S. Richards
and Bathsheba W. Smith, Counselors to Prest.
Zina D. H. Young. The time was spent in
the morning by good instructions being given
by Sisters Jane S. Richards and Batheba
W. Smith. The attendance was not very
good because of the prevailing disease, diph-
theria. No Primary Association Conference
was held because of the above stated reason.
Sunday afternoon Conference was continued
and attended by the same few who attended
before. The Sisters again spoke in a very in-
teresting manner. Apostle F. M. Lyman in
his usual interesting way addressed the con
gregation. The officers of the Relief Society
were presented and sustained. Singing, and
then the benediction was pronounced by Prest.
H. S. Gowans. Conference was then ad-
journed for six months to be held in Tooele
City.
	ANN TATE, Cor. Sec.

	    W. S. A. CONVENTION.

	A Territorial Convention of the N. A. W.
S. A. of Utah will be held about the time of
the April Conference, at which it is desirable
there should be Delegates from each Auxiliary
W. S. A. in the Territory, prepared to give
a report of the Association represented.
	E. B. WELLS, Ch, Pub. Com.

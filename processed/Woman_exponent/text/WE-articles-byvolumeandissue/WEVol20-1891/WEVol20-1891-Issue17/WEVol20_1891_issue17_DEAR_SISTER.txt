DEAR SISTER
	While packing up my things preparatory to starting on
another mission to the Sandwich Islands I came across
these verses which my sister wrote while sitting not far
from you in meeting, desiring to meet you but feared you
had forgotten her and did not like to make herself known.
So I just thought I would copy them and send them as
they were products of early friendship. She is now living
at Swan Creek, Rich Co. Utah. I do not know your
whereabouts but will leave this where you can get it, as it
may be many years before I see you again. I have missed
you at Officer's Meeting and have concluded you must
be living in the country. With kind love and best wishes
for the future I remain your sister in the Gospel,

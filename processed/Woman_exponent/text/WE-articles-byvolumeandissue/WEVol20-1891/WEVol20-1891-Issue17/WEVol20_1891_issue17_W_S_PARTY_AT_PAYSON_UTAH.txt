W. S. PARTY AT PAYSON, UTAH.

	The Social Hand Shake Party given in Pay-
son on the 13th of Feb. was as near as pos-
sible a complete social and instructive enter-
tainment. Singing, music and dancing, inter-
spersed with recitations, speeches, and social
hand shaking was the order. One speech
given by the Right Rev. Todd, deserves to
be printed verbatim and placed in every wo-
man's hand throughout the Territory.

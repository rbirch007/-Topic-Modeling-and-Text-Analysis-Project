A NEW STORY BOOK.

	There is about to be published at the office of
the Juvenile Instructor in this City, a new home
book suitable for the holidays. It will be entirely
a home production of stories from real life; and
will contain vivid descriptions of scenery and
incidents of New England in the earlier days, and
also the New West.
	The title of the book will be “Aunt Em’s
Stories,” and will open with Hephzibah which
was published sometime ago as a serial.
	There is a field in Utah for books of this sort,
or this class of reading, the supply of home story
books is not equal to the demand. This will be
the first book of the kind published at home.
Many books of a more serious style have been
written and published here and quite a number of
books of poems, children’s books,etc., and this is
therefore a new venture in the field of home
literature.

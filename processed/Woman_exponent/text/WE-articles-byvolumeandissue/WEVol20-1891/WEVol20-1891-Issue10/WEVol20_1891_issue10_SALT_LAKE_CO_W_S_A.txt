SALT LAKE CO. W. S. A,

	The Salt Lake Co. W. S. A. convened in
the 13th ward Assembly Rooms, on Tuesday
the 17th of Oct., 1891. Pres. Elizabeth
Howard presiding. Prayer was offered by
Dr. E. S. Barney. Roll called, minutes of
last session read and accepted. The yearly
financial report was read, by Treasurer E. S.
Barney. Pres. Howard announced that the
time had arrived for the election of officers
for the ensuing year, which was then attended
to. Ter. Pres. Sarah M. Kimball occupied
the chair, while the following named officers
were elected by ballot. President, Elizabeth
Howard, Secretary, Caroline C. R. Wells,
Treasurer, Dr. E. S. Barney, vice presidents
First Louisa K. Spencer, Second, Nellie
Colebrook Taylor, Third, Phebe C Young.
	It was then moved and carried, that the
remainder of the officers be nominated, which
resulted as follows: Asst. Sec, Margaret A.
Caine, Cor. Sec. Emma Finch, Chairman
Ex. Com. Elizabeth McFarlane, Dr. M. C.
Shipp, Ardella W. Eardley, Lillie T Freeze,
Mary E. Irvine and Mary E Bassett.
	Pres. Howard, Vice Presidents Taylor
and Young, briefly thanked the ladies, for
their confidence in them which they had
manifested by electing them to office, which
each expressed a desire to fill, with honor to
themselves and the promotion of the cause, in
which they were engaged.
	Pres. Kimball made some suggestions, as to
the work to be performed in the coming year.
Recommended that meetings be held and branch
associations organized. Vice Presidents be
appointed in the Co. wards outside of the
City, and diligent efforts made to increase
the interest in the association, that it might
be an active one, and the result of our efforts
be felt.
	Several ladies came forward and renewed
their memberships and the meeting was
adjourned until the third Tuesday in Dec. at
the same hour and place.

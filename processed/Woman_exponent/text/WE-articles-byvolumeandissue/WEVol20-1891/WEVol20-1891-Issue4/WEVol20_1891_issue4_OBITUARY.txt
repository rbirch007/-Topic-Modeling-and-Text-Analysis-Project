OBITUARY.
DEAR SISTER WELLS:
	It is with feelings of sadness that I here record the death
of our beloved Sister Stena Ericksen, which took place in
Mt. Pleasant on the 11th of July, at 17 minutes past 10
p.m., of pneumonia, she had been sick two weeks and
everything had been done that human skill could devise,
but all to no purpose.
	She peacefully passed from this sphere, surrounded by
her husband, children, and many dear friends, who deeply
mourn her sudden departure.
	Sister Erickson was a good woman, a true and faithful
Latter-day Saint; she embraced the Gospel in her native
land, Denmark, when a girl, and availed herself of the first
opportunity of emigrating with the Saints, to this the land
of her adoption.
	She became the wife of Brother Ericksen, by whom she
had 8 children, 9 of whom still survive her, two sons, and
four daughters.
	She was a faithful member of our Relief Society and a
most energetic teacher for the last fifteen years; of a kind
disposition, always on hand to help the sick, or those who
were in trouble; an affectionate wife, and a most indulgent
mother, and the counsel and admonition she gave her
children while she was able to talk will long be remem-
bered. May they emulate her good example and profit by
her wise and judicious instructions, and show that her
labor in their behalf has not been in vain.
	We deeply sympathize with the bereaved children on
this trying occasion seeing their mother has been taken
in the midst of a life of usefulness, being only 51 years
old. May she rest in peace, and may we who mourn her
departure, prepare to meet her again, that our joy may
be full.

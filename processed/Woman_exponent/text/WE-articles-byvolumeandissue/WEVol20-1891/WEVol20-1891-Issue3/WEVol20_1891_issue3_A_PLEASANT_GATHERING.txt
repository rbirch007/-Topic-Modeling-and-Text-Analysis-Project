A PLEASANT GATHERING.

	The teachers and a few of the members of
the Relief Society of Union Ward met on the
12th of June at the residence of her daughter,
Mrs. Lucy A. Brady by way of a surprise to
our aged and much beloved President, Mrs.
Elizabeth Richards, on the anniversary of her
82nd birthday. The time was most pleasantly
spent in social conversation, songs with organ
accompaniment and recitations. She received
a very nice present from her sisters with bless-
ings and good wishes.
	And, though feeble in body, her desires are
to see the work of the Lord triumph. All
partook of a bounteous supper which had been
prepared by the ladies of the Association, and
in the twilight of the evening all bade her an
affectionate adieu, realizing it is good to bless
and to be blessed.
	Sister Richards has been associated with the
Church from the days of Nauvoo; her house
always being open to the brethren and Saints her
hospitality is well known; she is one of those
loveable persons, whom to know her longest, is
to love her best.

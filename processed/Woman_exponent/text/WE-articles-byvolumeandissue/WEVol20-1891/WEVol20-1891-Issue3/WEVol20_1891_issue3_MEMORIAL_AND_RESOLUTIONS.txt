	MEMORIAL AND RESOLUTIONS.

	Memorial of respect to the family of Mary Blackburn.
	WHEREAS, Our beloved Sister has been called away 
by the hand of death, and therfore be it
	RESOLVED, That we the members of the Relief So-
ciety do deeply deplore the loss of an earnest worker
and a true friend, and that we tender to the bereaved 
husband and son, our heartfelt sympathy, and pray our
Heavenly Father to let the comforting influence of his
Holy Spirit rest upon them in their hour of bitter
trial.
	HANNAH HOYT,
	SUSAN HEATON,
	AMELIA CARROLL,
	SARAH ROBERTSON,
	Committee,

	We miss our Sister sadly,
	And those orphan children dear


Miss grandma's admonitions,
And her kind and loving care;
But she's resting from her labors,
Where no pain to her can come;
And we bow to Father's wishes,
And say His will be done.

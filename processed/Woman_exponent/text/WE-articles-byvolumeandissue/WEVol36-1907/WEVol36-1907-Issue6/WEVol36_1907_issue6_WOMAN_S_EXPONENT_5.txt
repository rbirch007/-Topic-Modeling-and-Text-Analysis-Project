WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


The homestead of John Alden and Pris-
cilla, at Duxbury, Mass., which was sold at
auction under foreclosure a few days ago,
was bought by the Alden Kindred Associa-
tion of America, and will be preserved as a
memorial. The house was built in 1653, and
has been inhabited by descendants of John
Alden and Priscilla ever since.

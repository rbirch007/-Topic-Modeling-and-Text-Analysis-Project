NOTICE TO STAKE SECRETARIES.
	The stake secretaries of the Relief Soci-
ety are reminded that the annual report for
the year 1907, from January 1 to December
31, should now be prepared. Please note
all changes in officers and new branches
organized, condition of grain in storage as
well as the amount on hand, the number of
buildings or parts of buildings, and rooms
in meeting houses, if owned by the Society.
Address General Secretary, Mrs. E. B.
Wells, Templeton Building, Salt Lake City,
Utah.
	P. S,—This notice applies to all the
countries where branches of the Society are
organized in missions at home or abroad,
in whatever part of the world.
	Millennial Star, Scandinavian, Swiss and
German and Dutch Latter-day Saint papers
please use this notice in the respective lan-
guages, so that it may be understood abroad.

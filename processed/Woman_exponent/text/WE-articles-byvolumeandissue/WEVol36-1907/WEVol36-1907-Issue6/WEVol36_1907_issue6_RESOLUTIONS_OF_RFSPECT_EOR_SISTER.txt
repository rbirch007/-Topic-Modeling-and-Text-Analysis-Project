RESOLUTIONS OF RFSPECT EOR SISTER
		CAROLINE D. S. BROWN.
		Kind Words Cost Nothing.
	Whereas our Father in heaven has in His infi-
nite mercy removed from among us our worthy
and dearly loved Sister, Caroline D. S. Brown,
and,
	Whereas the long and intimate relations with
her as a faithful member of the Wellsville Relief
Society.—We deem it our duty as officers of said
Society that we record our appreciation.
	Therefore be it resolved, That her wisdom and
ability as a faithful member and her wise counsel
will be held in grateful remembrance.
	Resolved, That the removal of such a life from
among our number leaves a vacancy and a shadow
that will be deeply realized by all the members of
this Society and a serious loss to all who knew
her for the exemplary life she lived.
	Besolved, That we deeply sympathize with the
bereaved family and pray our Heavenly Father to
sustain and comfort them in the hour of their bit-
ter trials.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
recorded in the record book of the Relief Society
and a copy published in the WOMAN’S EXPONENT
and a copy handed to the bereaved family.
				SARA A. PARKER, Prest.

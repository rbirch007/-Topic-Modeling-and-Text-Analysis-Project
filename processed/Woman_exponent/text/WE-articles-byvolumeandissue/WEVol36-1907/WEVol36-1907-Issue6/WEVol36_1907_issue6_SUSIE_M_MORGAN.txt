				SUSIE M. MORGAN.

	P. S. May I ask you to please give my
greeting to Sister Susa Young Gates and tell
her how much I enjoyed reading her address
to “Women of the World," I think it fine.
I received with joy Sister Smith’s letter and
I will answer it shortly.


The famous old Liberty Bell now belongs
to four women, Mrs. James B. McCloskey,
Mrs. C. D. Emerson and Mrs. S. B. Coward
of Philadelphia, and Mrs. S. W. B. Diehl of
Washington, D. C. These four women are
sisters, and the heiresses of John Willbank.

LEAH PYNE.
	Whereas: God our Eternal Father, in His Di-
vine wisdom, has taken from our midst our belov-
ed sister and fellow-laborer. Sister Leah Pyne, an
earnest worker in the Relief Society of the Fourth
ward of Provo, of the Utah Stake of Zion;
	Whereas: In her separation from us, we have
lost from our number a wise counselor whose pre-
cept and example have, both in public and private
life been of a nature to elevate mankind and draw
them nearer to the fountain of all truth,
	Therefore, be it Resolved: that we the officers
and members of said Relief Society, extend our
condolence and sympathy to the bereaved family
of the deceased, and recognizing in her removal
our great loss, but realizing that the Hosts of
Heaven will rejoice on her arrival among them,
we humbly bow in submission to the Divine will
of our Father in Heaven.
	And be it Further Resolved. That a copy of
these resolutions be placed upon the records of
the Relief Society and a copy presented to the
family.
		Signed:	MRS. MARGARET WATSON,
				MRS. EMMA ASHWORTH,
				MRS. ELIZA A. SPERRY,
								Committee.

		BANNOCK STAKE.
	The Relief Society Conference of the
Bannock Stake was held at Grace, Idaho,
June 18th, 1907. at 10 a. m., Stake Presi-
dent Eliza Hale presiding. After singing
and prayer by Patriarch Sterritt, roll call.
	Sister Margaret Rose reported Soda
Springs Relief Society. Sister Minnie
Hansen reported the Central Relief So-
ciety. Sister Esther Banks reported
Sterrit Relief Society. Sister Tolman re-
ported Chesterfield Relief Society. Sis-
ter Almira Meechum reported Trout Creek
Relief Society, all these in good working
order.
	Prest. Eliza Hale felt that her prayers
had been answered and the Relief Socie-
ty was a blessing to the sisters.
	Sister Emma Woodruff was pleased to
see so many of the brethren and sisters;

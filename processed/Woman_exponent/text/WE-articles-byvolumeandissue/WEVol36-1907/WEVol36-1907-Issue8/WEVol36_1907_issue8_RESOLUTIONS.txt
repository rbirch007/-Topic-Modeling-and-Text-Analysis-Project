RESOLUTIONS.
	Whereas our Allwise Heavenly Father has seen
fit to take back into His presence our much es-
teemed Sister Mary Ann Atkin, who departed this
life on February 6, 1908, being 69 years old, and
	Whereas she was the President of the Tooele
Relief Society for several years, having resigned
a year or so ago on account of failing health. In
this capacity she was a kind, dutiful leader, which
endeared her to all having the pleasure of know-
ing her.
	She set an example worthy of imitation in her
every walk in life.
	Therefore;—Be it resolved that we, the members
of the Tooele Ward Relief Society, who do so
keenly feel her loss, endeavor to follow the ex-
ample she set before us and keep in mind the
kindly advice she gave.
	Resolved that this resolution be recorded and a
copy of same be handed to the bereaved family
and one sent to be published in the Woman’s
Exponent.
				R. ALICE MCBRIDE,
						President.
				ADDIE VOWLES, Secretary.

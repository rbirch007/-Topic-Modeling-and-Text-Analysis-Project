BLACKFOOT STAKE OUTLINE.
		Lecture 1.—Life.
	a. Life is to develop body, mind and
soul. God made man and breathed into
his nostrils and man became a living soul.
(Gen. 2:7.) b. Life is the arena of many
forms of courage; as many, in fact, as there
are lines of human action (Our Home p.
401.)
	“Our daily paths with thorns or flowers
		We can at will bestrew them,  
	What bliss will gild the passing hours,
		If we but rightly knew them.
	The way of life is rough at best.
		But briars yield the roses,
	So that which leads to joy and rest,
		The hardest path discloses.”
	Lecture 2.—Responsibilities.
	a. In the nurse of power it is obvious that
man has to do with two different kinds of
responsibility, viz., individually and collect-
vely. (Our Home, p. 405.) b. Home-
making, order, cleanliness, love, patience,
endurance, and good environments.

OUTLINE MOTHERS’ WORK.
		PAROWAN STAKE.
	Lesson 1.—Life and its responsibilities.
	Why are we here? We must sometime give
an account for the way in which our lives
have been spent.
	Lessson 2.—Have an aim in life.
	That of making happy homes—Good
cooking an important factor. Good health
another. Politeness and courtesy at home
are also important. Count your blessings
but not your trials and you will find much
cause for happiness.
	Lesson 3.—Women in professions.
	(a) What professions are most suitable
for women to follow? (b) Motherhood and
a professional woman. (c) Motherhood is
the instinct of all women and greater breadth
and clearness of understanding should not
militate against any natural human instinct
but only serve to develop it.
	Lesson 4.—Mothers to be companions.
	(a) Mothers should share the joys, sor-
rows and amusements of their children, 
(b) Strive to do justice to the nature of the
child.
	Lesson 5.—Faith.
	(a) What is faith? (b) On whom does
our faith rest? (c) Show that it is a prin-
ciple of action. (d) Show that it is a prin-
ciple of power. (e) Example of faith.

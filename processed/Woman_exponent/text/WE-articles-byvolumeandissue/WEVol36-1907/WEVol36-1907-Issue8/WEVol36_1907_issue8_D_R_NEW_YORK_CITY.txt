D. R. NEW YORK CITY.
	The annual meeting and biennial election
of officers of the General Society of the
Daughters of the Revolution will be held in
New York during the week of April 27th,
with headquarters at the Waldorf Astoria.
Ten new members will be elected to the
Board of Managers.
	The program for the week will include a
reception at the new plaza, given by the
New York Society D. R., a theatre party, a
banquet at the Waldorf and excursions to
points of local and historic interest.
	An especialiy pleasant and interesting
convention is expected and a full attendance
is desired.

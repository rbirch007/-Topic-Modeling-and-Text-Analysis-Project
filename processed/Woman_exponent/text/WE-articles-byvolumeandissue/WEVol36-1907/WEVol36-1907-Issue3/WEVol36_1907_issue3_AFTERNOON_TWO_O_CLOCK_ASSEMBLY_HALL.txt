AFTERNOON, TWO O’CLOCK ASSEMBLY HALL.

	Third quinquennial of the N. W. R. S.
	Hymn, How Firm a Foundation.”
	Prayer.
	Hymn, “O Ye Mountains High.”
	Roll Call: Reading of minutes by the
secretary, Mrs. Emmeline B. Wells.
	Duet, “Bright be Our Pathway Till We
Meet Again,” Mrs. Lizzie Thomas Ed-
ward and Miss Edna Dwyer.
	Remarks, President Bathsheba W.
Smith.
	Transaction of important business.
	Hymn, “We Thank Thee O God For a
Prophet.” 
	Benediction.

	The third Quinquennial of the National
Woman’s Relief society will convene in
the Salt Lake Assembly hall Monday,
Oct. 7, 1907, at 2 p.m. for the transaction
of important business.
	By order of the president.
			BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
	EMMELINE B. WELLS,
			Secretary.

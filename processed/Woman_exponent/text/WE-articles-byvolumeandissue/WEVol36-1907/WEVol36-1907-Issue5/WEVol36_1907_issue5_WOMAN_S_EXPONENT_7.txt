WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


Miss Anna Whitlock, president of the
National Woman Suffrage Association of
Sweden, has organized in Stockholm a
course of lectures for women on citizen-
ship. The lectures are given twice a
week, and are attended by two or three
hundred women of all classes. Tickets
for working women are issued at a re-
duced price. Eminent speakers, both
men and women, give their services.

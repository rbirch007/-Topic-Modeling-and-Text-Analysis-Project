HONORS.
	On Wednesday evening, the 6th inst., a
reception was given in honor of Sisters
Martha A Critchlow, Nancy L. Burch and
Eveline C. Brown, the retiring presidency of
the Relief Society of the Ogden first ward.
	Sisters Critchlow and Burch have been
faithful workers in the Relief Society cause
for the past thirty years, and for twenty-
eight years held the positions of President
and First Counselor. Sister Brown acting
with them as Second Counselor for the last
eighteen years.
	By their earnest and constant labors of
love and sacrifice these sisters have en-
deared themselves to the hearts of the peo-
ple of this ward, and the present officers and
members of the organization made this a
fitting occasion for expressing the gratitude
and love felt for them.

MOTHER’S WORK.
	OUTLINE MILLARD STAKE.
	First. Temperance and the Word of
Wisdom. Effects of example and how ex-
tensive. What physical benefit? What
spiritual benefit to be derived therefrom?
	Second. How to care for the body to
preserve health.
	Third. Counsel. What is the highest
counsel that can be sought? In what spirit
should it be given?
	Fourth. Motherhood and its responsi-
bility.
	Fifth. How to secure the obedience and
love of our children.
	Sixth. How to train our boys.
	Seventh. Work as a means of establish-
inggood habits.
	Eighth. A father’s influence.
	Ninth. Effects of cigaret smoking.
	Tenth. The ideal mother.
	Eleventh. Regularity of meals and kinds
of food to eat, and how to cook the food.
	Twelfth. How the father should counsel
his sons.
	Thirteenth. Companionship of mothers
and daughters.
	Fourteenth. Work as an educator.
	Fifteenth. Purity in the home.
	Sixteenth. Last meeting in Decemcer:
celebrate Joseph’s Smith’s birthday.
					Stake Committee.

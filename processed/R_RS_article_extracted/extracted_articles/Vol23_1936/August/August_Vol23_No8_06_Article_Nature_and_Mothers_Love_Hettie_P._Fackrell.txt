How perfectly did the little thing warble the clear sweet notes? No dearer was the sweet song than were the gentle words of my mother. A few steps farther, in one corner of the perennial row, was a creamy narcissus, with its waxen petals just opening to the welcoming rays of the warm morning sun. No purer was the blooming beauty than were the sweet thoughts of my mother. On down the row, just a few steps farther were the soft, sweet petals of a dainty pink rose-bud, just blooming and as I touched them to my lips, they were like the sweet kiss of my mother. The fragrance of the delicate lavender lilac, near my window, lingers as my darling mother's teachings linger in my memory.

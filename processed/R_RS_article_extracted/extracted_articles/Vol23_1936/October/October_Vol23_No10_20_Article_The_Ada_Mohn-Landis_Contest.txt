The Ada Mohn-Landis Contest
"THROUGH the generosity of Mrs. Ada Mohn-Landis, the Woman's Christian Temperance Union offers annual prizes for original manuscripts suitable to include in collections of recitations and declamations prepared for use in the Medal Contests sponsored by the organization. In the 1937 contest Declamations are desired. The general theme is Total Abstinence from Alcoholic Drinks an Asset to:
Business Efficiency. Health. Citizenship?Civic Welfare. Success in Sports and Athletics. Safety, in Traffic and Elsewhere. Social Life. Spiritual Life.
The announcements giving rules for the 1937 contest are ready. Copies may be secured by sending stamp with request to the National Woman's Temperance Union, 1730 Chicago Avenue, Evanston, Illinois.

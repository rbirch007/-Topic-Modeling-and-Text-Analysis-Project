EARTH'S SYMPHONY By Linnie Fisher Robinson
Earth is replete with sunshine now, And blossoms riot on the bough; Out of its frozen sleep the stream Leaps singing where its banks are green: And on the shoulders of the hill Flashes the gold of daffodil; The valleys wake to bursts of song As the soaring lark his notes prolong. Over the dew of morning grass The harmony of colors pass Mirrored in each crystal prism Reflecting life and light and rhythm. But oh, the glory over all And oh, the glory in it all Is, "Christ the Lord is Risen!"

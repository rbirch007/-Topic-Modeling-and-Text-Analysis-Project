LADDIE By Emma Rigby Coleman A baby's such a common thing, There's one born every minute. And yet, I think my laddie's worth The world and all that's in it! I cuddle him so close and tight, Observe the way he grows; I sing to him of fairyland, Play, "piggie" with his toes. Though babies are a common thing, With one born every minute, I wouldn't give my laddie for The world and all that's in it!

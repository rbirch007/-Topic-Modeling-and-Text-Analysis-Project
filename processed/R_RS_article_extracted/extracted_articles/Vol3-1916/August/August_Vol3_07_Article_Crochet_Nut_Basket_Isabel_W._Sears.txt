CROCHET NUT BASKET. 457 Chain 22, turn, thread over, put hook in fourth st. from hook with t.c. Make 1 t. c. in each ch., 3 t. c. in last ch. Keep on round with t. c. in each ch. and 3 in first and last ch. Fasten to first t. c. with sl. st., ch. 3. 2 t. c. in first t. c, t. c. in next t. c. giving round widening round ends to keep flat. Make 9 rows of t. c. 10th round — Ch. 3, t. c. in next 3 t. c, ch. 4, skip 4, 4 t. c. in next 4 t. c. Repeat to place of beginning and fasten with sl. st. in 3rd ch. at beginning. 11th round — Ch. 7, 3 t. c. in next 4 ch., ch. 4, 4 t. c. in next 4 ch. to end of row. Fasten in third ch. from beginning. 12th round — Same as 11th round. 13th round — Ch. 3, 1 t. c. in each t. c. and each ch. to end of row. Fasten with sl. st. 14th round — 4 s. c. in first 4 t. c, ch. 4, skip 4, 4 s. c. in next 4 t. c, ch. 4, 4 s. c. in next 4 t. c. Continue to end of row and fasten with sl. st. in first s. c. You may have to skip more or less to make them come out even. 15th round — Ch. 7, fasten in center of the 4 s. c. with sl. st., ch. 4, 4 t. c. in 4 ch., ch. 4, fasten in center of next 4 s. c, ch. 4, 4 t. c. in next 4 ch. to end of row, making 3 t. c. in last 4 ch. Fasten with sl. st. in 3rd ch. of the first 7 ch. 16th round — Ch. 4, 4 s. c. in next 4 t. c, ch. 4, 4 s. c. in next 4 t. c. to end of row. 
 17th round — Same as fifteenth round. 18th round — Same as 16th round. 19th round — Same as 15th round. 20th round — Same as 16th round, except ch. 5 instead of 4. 21st round — Ch. 3, 1 t. c. in each s. c. and each ch. to end and fasten with sl. st. 22nd round — Ch. 3, 3 to t. c. in next t. c, ch. 3, skip 2, 5 s. c. in next 5 t. c, ch. 3, skip 2, 4 t. c. in next t. c. Continue to end of row, may have to skip one or more to make scallops come out even. There must be an even number of scallops. 23rd round — Draw thread to center of the 4 t. c, ch. 3, 1 t. c. in center of 4 t. c, ch. 2, 2 t. c. in same place, ch. 4, 3 s. c. in the 5 s. c, ch. 4, 2 t. c. in center of 4 t. c, ch. 2, 2 t. c. in same place, ch. 4, 3 s. c. in 5 s. c, continue round, fasten last 4 ch. in third ch. you started with. 24th round — Ch. 3, 2 t. c. in the 2 ch., ch. 3, 3 t. c. in same place, ch. 4, 1 s. c. in center of 3 s. c, ch. 4, 3 t. c. in 2 ch., ch. 3, 3 t. c. in same place, ch. 4, 1 s. c. in center of 3 s. c, ch. 4, 3 t. c. in 2 ch., ch. 3, 3 t. c. in same 2 ch. Continue to end of row and fasten last 4 ch. in third ch. from where you started. 25th round — Ch. 3, 3 t. c. in 3 t. c, ch. 5, 4 t. c. in next 3 t. c, 4 t. c. in next 3 t. c, ch. 5, 4 t. c. in next 3 t. c. Repeat to end of row and fasten with sl. st. CROCHET NUT BASKET. 459 26th round — 4 s. c. in 4 t. c, ch. 2, 1 t. c. in the 5th ch., ch. 2, 1 t. c. in same place, ch. 2, 1 t. c. in same place, ch. 2, 1 t. c. in same place, ch. 2, 8 s. c. in 8 t. c, ch. 2, 1 t. c. in the 5th ch., ch. 2, 1 t. c. in same place, ch. 2, 1 t. c. in same place, ch. 2, 1 t. c. in same place, ch. 2, 8 s. c. in 8 t. c. Repeat to end and fasten with needle and cotton. HANDLE Start between two scallops on side. Make 4 s. c, ch. 1, turn. 4 s. c. in 4 s. c, ch. 4, make p., turn, 4 s. c. in 4 s. c, ch. 1, turn, 4 s. c. in 4 s. c, ch. 1, 4 s. c. in 4 s. c, ch. 4, make p, turn 4 s. c. in 4 s. c. Continue until you have 2½ inches. Fasten thread on under side, skip 3 scallops and make 2½ inches same as last then catch up the first piece and continue across end making the 8 s. c. wide, ch. 1, turn and continue on until you have 3¼ inches, then make 4 s. c. which will bring you to the center, ch. 1, turn and make 4 s. c, ch. 4, p. turn, 4 s. c. in last 4 s. c, ch. 1, turn and continue until you have 2½ inches. Fasten between 2 scallops same as other side, then go back and make another 2½ inches on the other side and fasten, leaving 3 scallops between. Fasten on wrong side with needle and thread. Wash and starch in thick boiled starch or sugar syrup, and stretch over a mold or dish until dry.

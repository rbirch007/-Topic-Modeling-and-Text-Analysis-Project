A PRAYER. By Edith McClendon. Help me, dear Lord, to ever pray For light to guide my way, For wisdom pure that I might see The path that leads to thee. Help me, each day, to stronger grow. Thy children's needs to see, That I may help some weaker soul In safety back to thee. For with thy help all tasks seem light, Our path with flowers are strewn, Without it, all is very dark. And we are left in gloom. Ofttimes the world seems sad and dark, And trials hard to bear. Oh help us, then, to seek for light! In true and earnest prayer. MESA, ARIZONA.

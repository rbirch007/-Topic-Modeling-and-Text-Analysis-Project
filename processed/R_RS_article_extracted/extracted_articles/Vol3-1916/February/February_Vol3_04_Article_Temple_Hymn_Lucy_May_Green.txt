TEMPLE HYMN. By Lucy May Green. In thy service. Heavenly Father, we thy children meet today. Bless us with Thy Holy Spirit, aid us in our work, we pray. We are thankful for thy gospel, for thy blessed truth and love. May we ever be found faithful, never from thy pathway rove. Purify our hearts, our Father, as we promise unto thee All our life, our time, and talents, until death shall set us free May our lives be pure and holy may we never go astray; Ever strive to do our duty, walking in the narrow way, Until life on earth is over. Faithful to the end may we Dwell with thee in heavenly mansions, throughout all eternity. ;

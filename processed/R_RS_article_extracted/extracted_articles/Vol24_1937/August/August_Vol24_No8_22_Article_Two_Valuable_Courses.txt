Two Valuable Courses
WE call our readers' attention to two very valuable courses of study that are appearing in our Relief Society Magazine. The one "Child Guidance" is a continuation of a two-years' course of study written by a professional who gives most helpful suggestions to mothers in the rearing of children. The other, a new course, "Typical Women of the Church," is a treatise in biographical study, that will be most beneficial to all who will read it. Whether these lessons are taken up in the organizations in any way or not we feel that no subscriber can afford to miss reading these valuable contributions.

SEGO LILY By Vesta P. Crawford
Oh lily of the desert, Flower of the arid loom, The year now turned to summer Has brought thy velvet bloom;
Oh lily of the desert, Flower of the lonely place, Long have thy fragile blossoms Adorned this barren space;
Oh lily of the desert, A chalice brimmed with light, The shadowed rocks are lumined By petals shining, white;
Blossom of the promised land, Bright on history's page, Forever may thy splendor glow Among the western sage!

Do Women Want the Truth?
February, 1939
Dear Subscriber:
You want the truth about products and services.
It is perfectly natural for you to purchase that product or that service about which you are best and most reliably informed.
We pledge ourselves to do all in our power to make our advertisers aware of their tremendous responsibility to you as a consumer.
We will encourage them to make themselves sure of the truth about the product or service they are offering and to present their claims with simple understandable statements that will stand the test of use.
May we have your cooperation, expressed in a careful reading of the ads and as much actual buying support as you can consistently give.
Let us hear from you, please.

Pageant Notice
FREQUENTLY requests come to the office from Societies throughout the Church for plays and pageants. The committee of the General Board having this matter in charge meet the demands wherever possible, but they feel that encouragement should be given to local people to prepare these things for themselves. In your own wards and stakes there are conditions, incidents and historical data which could well be dramatized most effectively. The committee therefore suggests that an effort be made in each community to encourage people of talent to prepare such things of this type as might prove useful for presentation and valuable in the development of embryonic talents.?R. C. I.

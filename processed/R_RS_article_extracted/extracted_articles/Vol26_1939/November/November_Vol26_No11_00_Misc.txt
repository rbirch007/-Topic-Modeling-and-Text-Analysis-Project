The RELIEF SOCIETY MAGAZINE had a circulation average for the first six months of 1939 of 43,364 each month. This is a fine record to be made by any Church publication. The present subscription results indicate that a substantial increase is in order for the coming season. This all speaks well for the Magazine and its contents as it does for the Organization which makes such a record possible. Many local firms whose customers are mostly women are overlooking a splendid opportunity to advertise in the pages of the Magazine.

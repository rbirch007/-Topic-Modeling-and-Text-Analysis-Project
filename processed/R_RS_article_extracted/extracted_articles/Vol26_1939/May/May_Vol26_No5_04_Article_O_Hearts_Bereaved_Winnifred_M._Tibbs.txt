OH HEARTS BEREAVED By Winnifred M. Tibbs Be comforted, and know that God is just. He knows the pain the parting brings From those we love; but they are His And when He calls them home It is His will, and we must yield And thankful be for knowledge He has given, that death is not the end But the beginning of a better, Happier life, where time's not gauged By days nor years, But by eternal law. Be brave, oh hearts bereaved, And keep the faith; For then, when we are called, We, too, shall live again And mingle with the ones we loved so well, Throughout Eternity. Be reconciled and wait!

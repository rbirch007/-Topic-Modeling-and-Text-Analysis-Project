LITTLE BONNET OF BLUE By Jessie J. Dalton
Little bonnet of blue, with your frill of white lace— A dainty sweet frame for a dear little face; Blue eyes filled with laughter, Mouth a rose's pink fold, And hair just a promise, A whispering of gold.
Little bonnet of blue, with your frill of white lace— You have lain for so long in this dim-scented place, Beside these wee dresses— Worn and wasted they seem; But you, little bonnet, Still halo a dream.

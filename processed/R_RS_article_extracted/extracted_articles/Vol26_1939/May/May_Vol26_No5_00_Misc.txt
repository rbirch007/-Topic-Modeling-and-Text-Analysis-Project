The Magazine needs advertisers If our Magazine is to become not only more useful but as beautiful in every respect as the best women's magazines of the day, it will cost more money than we are now spending.
We have no intention of increasing the subscription rate, but if we can increase our advertising income we shall then have the means to make whatever improvements are necessary to establish and maintain leadership.
All our advertisers hope for is that as you readers go through the Magazine you will read their advertisements.
As people tend to buy the products and services about which they are best informed, the advertisers are sure that if their messages are read they will get their money's worth in trade.

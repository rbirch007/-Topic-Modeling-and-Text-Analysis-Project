The fundamental philosophy of family finance should be discussed and a plan for handling the family pocketbook should be decided upon before the marriage ceremony. Whatever the plan may be, it should not be considered static, but rather as a method to be experimented with until a more suitable plan for the particular family has been evolved. It is wise, however, to make sure that one plan has been given a thorough trial before it is discarded in favor of some untried plan.

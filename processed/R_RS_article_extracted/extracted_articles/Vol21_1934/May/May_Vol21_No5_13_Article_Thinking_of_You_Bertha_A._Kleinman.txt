Thinking of You By Bertha A. Kleinman To the mother whose arms have never pressed, A baby's head to her lonely breast, To the mother whose lips have never sung. A lullaby in a baby's tongue, To the mother whose prayers, through time and space, Have seemed to fail at the Throne of Grace— No plea, no prayers, no tears of thine. Are ever wasted in God's design, For He who counteth the sparrow's fall, Eternal increase shall yield to all, Shall yet be given and glorified!

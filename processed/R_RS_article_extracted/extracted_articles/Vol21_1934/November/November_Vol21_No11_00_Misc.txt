D. N. STRAUP CHIEF JUSTICE Supreme Court of Utah Candidate for Re-Election

One of the most outstanding assets of a judge is experience. Judge Straup has served the people of Utah on the Supreme Court for twenty-two years?a longer service than any other judge in our state. For more than two decades this wise and able judge has held the confidence and esteem of our people. He has been a pillar of strength to secure us our inalienable rights guaranteed under our constitution. The great public opinion of this country is that politics should not affect the judiciary. We must not lose the experience of this great judge. It is always sound for any office, to vote for the best man regardless of his political affiliations.

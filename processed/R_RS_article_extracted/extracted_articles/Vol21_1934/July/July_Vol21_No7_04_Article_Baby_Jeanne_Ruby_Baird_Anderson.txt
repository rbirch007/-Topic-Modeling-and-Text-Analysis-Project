Baby Jeanne By Ruby Baird Anderson Eyes of blue— Sky's 'own hue. And plump and dainty arms. Flashing smile To beguile With all your baby charms, Darling Jeanne! Lovesome Jeanne! Your cunning baby ways Will ever be A dream for me In olden, golden days. Sweet to hold! Joy manifold Within my happy heart. I love you so! I'd have you know You're my career, and art.

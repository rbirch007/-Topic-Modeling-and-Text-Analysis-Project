Relief Society Conference April 4 and 5, 1933. By Julia A, F. Lund—General Secretary HE Relief' Society of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held its Annual Conference, April 4 and 5, 1933, in Salt Lake City, Utah, President Louise Y. Robison was in charge. The meeting's were seven in number: Officers' Meeting, and the following department meetings—Social Service Case Work, Secretaries, Work and Business, Choristers and Organists. There were also two General Sessions. At the conclusion of the Officers' Meeting, a luncheon was served at the historic Lion House, the General Board acting as hostess to the Stake Executive Officers.

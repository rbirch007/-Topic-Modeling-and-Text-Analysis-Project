A MEANDERING STREAM Lifting the Veil By Ann Jarvis With stealthy steps on heels of night Fog creeps in from the bay. Low-lying sinister shadows fight The early light of day. The workers to the factory bound, The messengers on the run Bewildered pause, all turned around Until behold the sun! Gray mist hangs o'er this perplexed land And we shall go astray. Lift, lift the veil with Thine great Hand, Oh, Lord, and lead the way.

Mistakes By Ida Hansen Steed If you were the one you talked about And she were you, I pray, Would you be so ready to speak aloud The error of her ways? You have made mistakes in your life time, No matter how good you are, They may have been of a different sort, But they're errors just the same. For if you were the one you talked about And she were you, I say, Perhaps you'd make the same mistakes In the same mistaken way. Susan B.

MRS. LUACINE SAVAGE CLARK Wife of President J. Reuben Clark, Jr.
My Wealth By H. H. Ramsay
My wealth consists not of silver and gold, But in a truer and finer expression of soul. Day by day I store the precious gems, Filling life's great coffers o'er their brims.
With the passing of the fleeting hours, My wealth brings to me unbounded powers, For I am sure to invest the increasing toll, With the better elements of a living soul.
My wealth I would not selfishly hide away, But give it back freely in work or play. It's the saving of the spirit of humankind, Beckoning men the better things of life to find.
My priceless wealth none can defile, For it is the sacred smile of a child, Given so freely and with ardor so fine, For one that a moment before was mine.

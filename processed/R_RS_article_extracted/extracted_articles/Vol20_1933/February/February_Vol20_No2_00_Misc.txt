HALF A LOAF A colored congregation in Louisiana, hearing that a college in Kansas was conferring the degree of D. D. When Mrs. Hartley inquired as to the cause, he answered, dolefully: "I'm mighty misfortunate, ma'am; that's what it is. You see, it was like this: I went to church last night, and when they came round for the collection I give a quarter. Yes'm, that's what I thought.

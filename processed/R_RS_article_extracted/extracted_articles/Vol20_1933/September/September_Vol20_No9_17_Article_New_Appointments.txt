New Appointments
DR. JOHN A. WIDTSOE has been released after six years of service as president of the European Mission, to return home and Dr. Joseph F. Merrill has been appointed to this important position. Elder James H. Moyle has been released from presiding over the Eastern States Mission and Elder Don B. Colton appointed to take his place. It is wonderful how many efficient people there are in the Church able, ready and willing to take positions of responsibility. Wives of Mission presidents play an important part in their success. Sister Widtsoe has done a marvelous work throughout the European Mission and Sister Moyle has done very fine Relief Society work in the Eastern States. We will welcome the returning missionaries and say God speed to those who take their places.

LINCOLN
By Christie Lund Coles
Upon the altar of the Common Good Lincoln stood, A martyr in the cause of truth, Giving his youth, His strength, his very life, Suffering strife For the far hope the dreamers see? Equality.
So each of those who dares to say: "This is the way To Universal brotherhood," Is misunderstood. But oh, the endless chain of right That day and night Is being welded by these few Who make dreams true.

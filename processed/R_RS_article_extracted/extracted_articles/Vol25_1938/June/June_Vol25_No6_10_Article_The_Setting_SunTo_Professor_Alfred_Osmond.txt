THE SETTING SUN
To Professor Alfred Osmond
By Lorine Hutchinson Lee

The sun has set,
Reflecting its glory across the sky
In eloquent ecstasy,
Giving birth to a new day,
Beyond the horizon;
Another world of work and hope,
Where people strive with life to cope.

Life's sun has set,
Reflecting its glory across the mind
Of services well done,
Announcing a new day
Beyond the horizon;
Another sphere of faith and hope
Where spirits strive with truth to cope.

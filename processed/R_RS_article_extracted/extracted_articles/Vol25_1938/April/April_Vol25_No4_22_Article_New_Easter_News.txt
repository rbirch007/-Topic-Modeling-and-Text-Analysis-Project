New Easter News By Nephi Jensen
Speak not to me of endless death; Beyond the Grave's dreadful portal Ever stirs spirits' deathless breath Waking souls to life immortal.
The One who heroically won The Tomb's conquest and victory— The Maker of earth, stars and sun, Brought our unending life to be.
In Palmyra's temple of trees Shone anew the Light resplendent, A youth's troubled soul to appease With truth and glory transcendent.
From out celestial burning, Spoke again the voice Eternal Answering a Seer's heart-yearning With new Easter news, supernal.

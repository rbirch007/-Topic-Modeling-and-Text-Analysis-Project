Book NoticeA DELIGHTFUL and attractive book of verse entitled "Main Street'' has been published by Rachel Barney Taft. It should be especially enjoyed by those interested in pioneer life in Salt Lake Valley. In addition to the poems of pioneer life the book contains. Numerous short poems dealing with-a wide variety of subjects. Her poetry expresses a high seriousness and provides inspirational reading. 'Main Street" may be purchased at the Deseret Book Co. Or Auerbach Book Co. For $1.00.

Second Edition Of Relief Society Song Book
THE second edition of the Relief Society Song Book is now off the press. This edition is exactly like the first one, and, despite increasing costs of paper and other materials, the price of the Song Book will remain at 85c per copy, postpaid. Address orders to Relief Society  , Utah.

AUTUMN MOOD Leone G. Layton Now sadness comes? The long autumnal days That pierce our hearts. The birds go on their way To southland sunshine. I am left alone? Here left to listen To the swishing moan Of winds come seeking, Seeking dying leaves Playing the dance of death. My spirit grieves, Loving the poignant sadness of the song Autumn winds sing, Yet wishing that from dying beauty's bier T too could wing. 

 ?OCTOBER, 1941

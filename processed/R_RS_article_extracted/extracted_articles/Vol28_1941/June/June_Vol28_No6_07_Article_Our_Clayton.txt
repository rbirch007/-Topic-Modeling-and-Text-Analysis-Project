OUR YOUTH Phyllis Clayton
Youth of our nation, stand firm and true, Hold fast the ideals of the Red, White, and Blue, Throw back your shoulders, hold up your head, Let nothing dismay you or hinder your tread.
Inspired by courage of those gone before, Loyal to patriots and trusts as of yore, Meet the world squarely, with eyes clear and bright, Glad you are learning to do what is right.
Live that each day makes you better for living, Learn and gain knowledge from those who are giving, Love and be loyal to ideals so true— Youth of our nation, we're depending on you!

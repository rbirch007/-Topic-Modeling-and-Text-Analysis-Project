"The Merry-Go-Round"
ALICE MORREY BAILEY
Have you ever felt that you were on a Merry-Go-Round whirling dizzily through days and weeks, wondering where it would all end and how you could ever achieve your purposes? Then you can understand and enjoy Alyn Fordyce as she struggled with life to find happiness and contentment. Meet her this month, but don't judge her yet, follow her through the succeeding installments of her "Merry-Go-Round.

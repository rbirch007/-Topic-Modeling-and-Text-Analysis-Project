NOTES FROM THE FIELD Relief Society General Conference HE semi-annual general conference of Relief Society will convene in Salt Lake City, Wednesday and Thursday, April 2 and 3, 1941. The conference will open at 10 a. M. Wednesday in the Assembly Hall with a meeting for stake and mission officers and board members. The departments to be featured during the two-day conference will be as follows: Social Welfare Magazine Work-and-Business and Mormon Handicraft Centennial and Membership Music Secretary-Treasurers 'Two sessions, open to the public, will be held in the Tabernacle on Thursday, at 10 a. M and 2 p. M. Respectively. Inasmuch as the Relief Society centennial will be observed next year, special emphasis will be given at the conference to plans for the centennial; the Thursday morning general session will be largely devoted to centennial and membership activities. Detailed information relating to the conference will be mailed to Relief Society stake and mission presidents early in March.

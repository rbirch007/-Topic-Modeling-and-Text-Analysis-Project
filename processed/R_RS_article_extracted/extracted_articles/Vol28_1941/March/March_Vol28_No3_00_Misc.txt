THE FRONTISPIECE
Of great interest to all who visit the Relief Society General Board room in the Bishop's Building are the oil paintings of the Relief Society general presidents. The frontispiece picture of this issue of the Magazine is a copy of the painting of President Amy Brown Lyman which was added to the collection February 7, 1941, honoring President Lyman on the date of her birthday. The painting is the work of Lee Greene Richards.

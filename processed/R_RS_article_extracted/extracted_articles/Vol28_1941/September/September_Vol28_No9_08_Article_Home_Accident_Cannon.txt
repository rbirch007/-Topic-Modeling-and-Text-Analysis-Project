Sarah A. Park Brockbank, who was feted on her 98th birthday, and who received special honors this year on Utah Day, July 24, as the sole remaining pioneer of Utah of 1847, passed away late in August. REMARKABLE group of Utah pioneer grandmothers, recently feted, follows: Mary F. Garner of Roy, age 105; Sarah A. Gibbons, 94, of Myton; Ellen Sandall, 91, of Layton; Melissa K. Lee, go, of Caliente; Annie C. Shaw, 89, of Salt Lake City; Mary K. Johnson, 88, of Brigham City, still an ardent angler; and Eugenia Snow, 85, daughter of President Lorenzo Snow.

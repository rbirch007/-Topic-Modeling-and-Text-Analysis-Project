Reports on Magazine Drive In making their reports, most of the Stake Magazine agents are delighted with the efficient way in which the Magazine Campaign has been carried on. The use of the penny boxes has been very extensive and most Stakes feel that it has helped them very materially in getting large subscription lists. A house to house campaign has been very general, socials have been given, and the Magazine play used extensively. Blackfoot Stake reports that one of their wards bought magazines and sold them to the Sisters for ten cents each. Two Wards held socials and with the profits paid half of the subscription price of each Magazine taken. Pacheco, in Juarez Stake, heads their list with 105% subscriptions.  Fanny Gilbert is the Magazine agent. In Nebo Stake President Harding presented the Genoa Ward with a jar containing two hundred pennies in appreciation for their success in the Magazine Drive. Birdseye Branch organized in November with only thirteen members reports fourteen subscriptions. President Hazel B. Tingey of the Australian Mission sent in thirty-nine subscriptions for various parts of her Mission, a larger list than they have ever sent in before. Malta Ward of Raft River Stake has 42 members and 43 subscriptions.

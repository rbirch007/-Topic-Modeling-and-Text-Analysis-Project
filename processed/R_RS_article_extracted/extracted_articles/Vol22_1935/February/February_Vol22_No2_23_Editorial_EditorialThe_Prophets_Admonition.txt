Children who are not trained to pray, who are not fed spiritually, EDITORIAL rarely develop spirituality in later life. So, now that many responsibilities that the Relief Society formerly carried are taken care of by government agencies, we urge our officers and members to devote their energies to stimulating luke-warm parents to see to it that their children attend regularly 115 and participate actively in the auxiliary organizations. Our officers should see to it that the spark of spirituality that has been allowed to almost be smothered in many breasts shall be fanned into a flame. This is the great need of today. This is the great call of the present time. Our women 'will not fail.

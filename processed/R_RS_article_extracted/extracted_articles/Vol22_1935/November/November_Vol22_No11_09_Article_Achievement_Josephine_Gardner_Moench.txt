Achievement By Josephine Gardner Moench Are you waiting for ships that never come in? Why wait any longer? Try bringing them in. The skies may be cloudy, the seas may be rough, The cold weather stinging, wild waves a rebuff: But the thrill of adventure will aid in the strife And keep up your strength in the battle for life. Smooth seas for a landing may come to amend. And the power of achievement be yours in the end.

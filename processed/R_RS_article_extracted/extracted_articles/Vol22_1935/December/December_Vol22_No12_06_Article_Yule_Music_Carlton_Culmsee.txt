The Yule eMusic By Carlton Culmsee Far over the white hill In the frosty dusk, The voice of an old bell sang to temper the chill And mellow the wind that had been quick and brusque. Dark fell. Came deep and slow. Again the song We knew the folk in the valley, the quiet and strong, Were gathering there in the little church in the snow, To hear old Christmas sung, And quaintly told In cadences of a half-forgotten tongue Heard only as a music sweet and old.

"Modern Miracles" By Jeremiah Stokes 'THIS little volume gives a record of many modern miracles and is very faith-promoting. Brother Stokes dedicates the volume "'to the youth of the Church, who are beset and sorely perplexed by theories and philosophies that strike at the heart of religion, the divinity of Christ and the existence of God." He hopes it will "prove an anchor to their faith and a rock of safety, comfort and joy to their souls." The incidents related are well authenticated and the appendix contains a list of miracles wrought in the New Testament and Book of Mormon times. Price $1.00. Deseret Book Company, distributors.

Old Testament Reading to be Continued WE are delighted with the great interest shown by our officers and members in the Project. So many requests have come in asking that the Old Testament reading be continued that the General Board Mrs. Amy Whipple Evans Mrs. Marcia K. Howells EDITORIAL has decided that the organizations shall be asked to continue their Old 315 Testament studies for another year.

Visitors From Afar N attendance at our April Conference was Mrs. Verna F. Murphy, President of the Relief Societies in Hawaii, accompanied by a party of Hawaiians including Mrs. Thelma Lindsey, Mrs. Diana Pokini, Mrs. Lillie Cummings Deering, Rachel Kinney Johnson and Elders David Mokuilima, David Kailiponi, William Deering, Paul Ilia and little Marvel Murphy. This is the first time representatives have been sent from the Islands to a Conference. Many social gatherings were given in honor of these far-away visitors. They greatly enjoyed their visit and will take back to their organization many helpful suggestions. They intended to stay for the June Conference but will return home in May to be present when a Stake is to be organized in Hawaii. We wish them a safe return home and hope their work will have added impetus through the visit to headquarters.

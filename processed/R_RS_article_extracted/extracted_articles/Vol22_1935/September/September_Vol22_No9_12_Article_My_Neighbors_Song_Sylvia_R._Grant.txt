My Neighbor's Song By Sylvia R. Grant
I put a snowy white wash on the line; But my neighbor can sing a song.
I scrub my pans till they sparkle and shine, No other floor is more spotless than mine, Here no dust ever lingers long.
My window panes reflect glowing fire From the rays of the evening sun. Folks passing by do not stop to admire Mine—for my neighbor's are open near And from out of them comes a song.

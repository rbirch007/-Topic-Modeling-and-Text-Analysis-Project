President Robison Honored
OUR readers will be glad to know that President Louise Y. Robison has been appointed by Governor Henry H. Blood as a member of the new state board of public welfare. The other members are: Frederick P. Champ, Logan; J. W. Gillman, Orem; Hugo Anderson, T. L. Holman and the Rev. Jacob Trapp, all of Salt Lake. We hope this board will have vision to see the needs of the state along welfare lines and will be resourceful in meeting these needs.

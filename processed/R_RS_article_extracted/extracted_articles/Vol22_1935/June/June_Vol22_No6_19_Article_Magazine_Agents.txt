Magazine Agents
WE wish to call our Magazine Agents' attention to the fact that the lessons for next season are being published in the summer months. This makes it very desirable that all subscriptions that have expired now or will expire in the next few months should be renewed as soon as possible in order that no one may be unfitted for her work by not being able to study her lessons. One Stake has very wisely featured the renewing of expired subscriptions as gifts for Mother's Day. We are delighted to hear from time to time how carefully each phase of our work is checked on and how our officers plan so that no stone is left unturned for the greatest success.

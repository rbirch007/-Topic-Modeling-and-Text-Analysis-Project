L. D. S. CHURCH HISTORY Lesson 9 Events In Missouri (To be used in place of Literary Lesson)
IN the last lesson we learned that the Saints lived in two places mainly. One of these places was in Ohio and the other was in Missouri. We must bear in mind that both of these states were but sparsely populated then as compared with what they are now. However, all the Saints in Ohio, and everywhere else for that matter, were looking forward eagerly to the time when they, too, might go up to the "Land of Zion". That is, they did so in the latter part of the year 1831, the whole of the year 1832, and the first half of the year 1833. Then something happened to blast their hopes of gathering in Jackson county, Missouri. That something occurred in the fall of 1833.

"We Write Upon Walls" By Lucy Goodrich Lind
Wee Pippa passed by with her paint and brush, Just a song and an innocent face, But her spirit sped on to hungry hearts Where time does not erase.
She chose not an arrow but music Though its power was to her unknown, For its magic made new a faltering faith, Shed light where light was flown.
In that urge, innate, expression, 'Midst throngs or in silence apart, Let us write for good as Pippa wrote On the walls of the human heart.

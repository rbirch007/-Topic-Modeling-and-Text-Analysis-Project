Procrastination By Ella J. Coulam
I found in my cupboard one morning A neat little package of seeds. I blushed with shame when I saw them, For I had a garden of weeds!
I thought of the lovely flowers To cheer the lonely and sad, Those seeds could bring forth in my garden To make some aching heart feel glad.
I thought of kind words left unspoken, Sealed up in the depths of my heart; Of the joy and cheer and gladness Those words to a friend could impart.

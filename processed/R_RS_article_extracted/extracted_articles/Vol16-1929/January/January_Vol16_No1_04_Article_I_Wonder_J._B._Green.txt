I Wonder By J. B. Green
I wonder when my soul is weighed Against the measure given I wonder will the verdict be? "Well done, rest thou in heaven."
Or will He read my record o'er With sorrow in His face And say, "Thou art a stranger here For you there is no place."
God grant my lessons I may learn Though trials my teacher be, So well, thou'lt be pleased to say "Rest thou thy soul with Me."

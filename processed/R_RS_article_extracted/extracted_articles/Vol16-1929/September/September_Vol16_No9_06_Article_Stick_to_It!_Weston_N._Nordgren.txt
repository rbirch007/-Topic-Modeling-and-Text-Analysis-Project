Stick To It! By Weston N. Nordgran No matter what trials you have in this life— No matter what burdens you're called on to bear— No matter what suff'ring and toil and care— No matter what problems you meet in the strife— Stick to it! No matter what others may think of you, friend— No matter if money is gone; The thing that will count as you go on and on— Is your courage and grit, and your definite end! Stick to it! Your loved ones may leave you alone to the world— Your friends may desert you afield. But the one who goes on, never thinking to yield— Is the one for whom flags are unfurled! Stick to it! Your God is above you, to lend you a hand; Your sweetheart or mate may be true; But the test of your character is up to you! Can you weather the storm—and stand? Stick to it!

Raked-Up Leaves By Vesta Pierce Crawford
Here are the last of all the leaves. Painter, I've seen your new-budded tree And your summer-swayed bough, I've seen your Autumn grandeur, But these leaves, you cannot paint them now.
Once they wore the colors of the dawn; Now they are raked-up leaves With all their splendor gone.

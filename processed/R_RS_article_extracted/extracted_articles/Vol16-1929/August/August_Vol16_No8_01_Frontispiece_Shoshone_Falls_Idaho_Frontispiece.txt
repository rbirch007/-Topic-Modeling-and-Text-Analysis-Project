SHOSHONE Falls, IDAHO "Niagara of the West” THE NIAGARA OF THE WEST By Glen Perrins Mighty Shoshone Falls, Idaho is a sight one will never forget. To gaze upon this creation of Mother Nature, 212 feet high, the water booming over the precipice in continuous roar, holds one fast in awe The water, answering the call of the sea, is rushing, tumbling,: falling down over the high cliffs in a hurry to get to level territory. Truly worth the visit is the sight of this powerful waterfall, the Niagara of the West, Shoshone Falls.

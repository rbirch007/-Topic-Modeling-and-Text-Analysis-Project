(THE Enp) Love's Recompense By Mrs. Grace Jacobson It was in the early June time When I first met you, dear; And the happy birds were singing In the tree tops far and near. Our hearts were young and tender, Tuned to love's enduring lay, As we dreamed of life and beauty In that trusting lovers' way. Yet our love has not grown cold; Its golden tie is just as strong And true, as we grow old. In the years that come and go, dear, Love once more will sing anew, In our love's approaching sunset, Deeper far than that we knew.,

The child walked into them! He was no longer hungry and thirsty and cold. He was warmed and fed by the Mother-love in her face! Neither said a word. A warm embrace and a long look?a parting. Happy, he started for the outdoors without a kiss for his father. He knew now! His father started to call him back?but the other mother motioned, No. "Strange child!" said he. "Wonder child!" said she. The mother in her knew!
Out into the garden went the child, straight to the clump of Madonna lilies. It was early season, but one tall green stalk was crowned with a dazzling white blossom. The child broke the stem.

Those who heard her marveled at her brilliant exposition and wondered how long it would be before any American woman could go into the Orient and deliver an address of equal excellence, speaking a foreign language with anything like the perfection with which this gifted Japanese woman spoke English. One of the things that the American woman does not seem to achieve very frequently, is the ability to speak fluently any other language than her own; she is thus deprived of the ability to put her message over to those who speak other languages.

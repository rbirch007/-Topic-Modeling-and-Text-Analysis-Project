CouNSELOR JENNIE BRIMHALL KNIGHT Jennie Brimhall Knight By Inez Knight Allen On a visit last year to San Juan, I was greeted with expressions of appreciation and messages of love for Jennie B. Knight. All this, because many years ago the lives of these persons had been enriched by her teaching in their community. Her influence had stood the test of time. Her ideals had been upheld by her example. As surely as the flowers continue their growth by the warmth of the sun, even throughout the darkenss of the night, the imprint of her character has been with that people for good through all the years since then. The companions of her childhood, from those days of sorrow and of struggle, are pleased now to be counted among her friends. Because she is a friend, she has never had too Jittle or too much to share with others. Her unusual human understanding and sympathy are of rare value. In a truly motherly way, she has charity for the weakness and mistakes of others, seeking always for the motive of action rather than to chastise.

Appreciation On behalf of President Louise Y. Robison and the members of the present Executive Committee we wish to extend our heartfelt thanks to all who have written to the new president and the members of the Executive Committee since the reorganization. President Robison wishes us to say that it would be a very great pleasure to her to answer each letter personally, but as that is impossible we are making use of the pages of the Magazine to assure all well wishers and those who have extended their congratulations and good wishes that they ar accepted in the spirit in which they have been tendered with grateful appreciation.:

Leadership Week, Brigham Young University January 21 to 26 Adult education will be stressed again during the annual Leadership Week Convention which will be held at Brigham Young University, January 21 to 26. Departments will be organized to meet not only the need of the workers in the auxiliaries of the Church but of all adults as well. "Education for the enrichment of rural life" is the theme selected for the convention, partly because the Utah State Farm Bureau will hold its convention in connection with Leadership Week and partly because it is a theme which may well be stressed in the inter-mountain region where rural life is the only kind of life there is. Committees are working out a program which is intended to meet the needs of all who attend. The entire week of instruction as well as the entertainment of the week will be absolutely free as they have been in times past.

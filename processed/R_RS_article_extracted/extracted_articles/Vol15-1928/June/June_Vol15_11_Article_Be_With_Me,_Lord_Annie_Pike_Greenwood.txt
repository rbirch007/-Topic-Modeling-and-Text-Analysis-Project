BE WITH ME, LORD Annie Pike Greenwood Be with me, Lord; Be with me yet awhile; Such weary thoughts the vacant hours beguile? My heart is sad while all around me smile; Be with me, Lord. Be with me, Lord; Thou wilt not leave me now? I am Thy child?before Thy feet I bow; I live, O Lord, but Thou wilt teach me how; Be with me, Lord. Be with me, Lord; Thou wilt my Shepherd be, For One who stilled the waves hath power with me; O lest I lose the path which leads to Thee, Be with me, Lord!

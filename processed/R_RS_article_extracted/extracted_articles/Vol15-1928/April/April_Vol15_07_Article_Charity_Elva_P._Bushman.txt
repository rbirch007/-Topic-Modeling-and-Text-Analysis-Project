Charity By Elva P. Bushman
That bit of something Which clings like frost upon the board, That bit of love and friendship Which we hoard.
That love which binds one to his fellow-man, That beckons and leads on Through life's brief span;
Yea, in disgrace, and direst hell That hand of friendship beckons on To break the spell.
That love which binds through time and all eternity— That is the thing which man may call True charity.
THE RELIEF SOCIETY OF THE CHURCH OF JESUS CHRIST OF LATTER-DAY SAINTS

With the idea of offering courses desired by the people of the State, in all grades of collegiate work, the UNIVERSITY OF UTAH will again present an outstanding Summer School of Service.

MISS GRACE L. BEBB, Visiting Teacher, Bureau of Child Welfare, Board of Education, Lincoln, Nebraska.

DR. JOHN SUNDWALL, Head of Department of Hygiene and Public Health, and Director of Students' Physical Welfare, University of Michigan (two weeks.)

Utah's Summer School of Service

With the idea of offering courses desired by the people of the State, in all grades of collegiate work, the UNIVERSITY OF UTAH will again present an outstanding Summer School of Service.

Courses of general interest, as well as many courses of special value to the teachers of the State, will be offered. The well-trained University staff (mostly department heads), will be supplemented by the following carefully chosen distinguished educational specialists:

MISS GRACE L. BEBB, Visiting Teacher, Bureau of Child Welfare, Board of Education, Lincoln, Nebraska.

MRS. RHEA WAHLE CORNELIUS, Assistant Professor in Physical Education, University of Iowa and Iowa State Teachers College.

DR. HOWARD R. DRIGGS, Head of Department of English Education, New York University (fifth week.)

DR. JOHN SUNDWALL, Head of Department of Hygiene and Public Health, and Director of Students' Physical Welfare, University of Michigan (two weeks.)

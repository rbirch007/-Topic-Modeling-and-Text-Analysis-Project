FAITH. By Grace Ingles Frost. There sounds from the dusk of the even, No longer the katydid's cry, The rain drips from eaves and from branches, A veil hath been drawn o'er the sky, That hides all its blue from my vision, And the gold of the sun is hid, too, But well do I know still is gleaming, 'Round the mist and the gray of that pall.

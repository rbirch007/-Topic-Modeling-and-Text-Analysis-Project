A Woman's Prayer Mrs. Parley Nelson
======================================================================

WOMAN'S PRAYER. Mrs. Parley Nelson. Dear Lord tonight I ask of Thee this prayer, For help and guidance in the little things The countless, trivial, vexing daily cares Which make my cross seem more than I can bear. The precious boon of patience Lord, I crave, Then when I'm ill or tired — sick at heart, The unkind stabbing word may not dart out; O help me then, and all times to be brave, That I may face the petty cares of life, With understanding and calm self control, Knowing, full well, that in this world of strife, Each has her burden — each her trial of soul.

WHO CAN SAY THERE IS NO GOD? Mabel Jones Gabbott Who can say there is no God? All earth proclaims his power; The seed beneath the moistened clod Now blossoms into flower; The star, high in the firmament, God's perfect pattern follows, And God's own guiding love is lent To northward winging swallows. Who can say that God is not? The prayer that soars to heaven, If born of faith and child-like thought, Will find its answer given. And who can say no prayer is heard?

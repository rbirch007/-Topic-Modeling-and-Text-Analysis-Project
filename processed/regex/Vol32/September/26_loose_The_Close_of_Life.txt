THE CLOSE OF LIFE Evelyn Fjelsted Shall not the close of life be sweet-Like coming home some tranquil day When one has wished so long to come, But home seemed ever far away? Like knowing suddenly there was No cause at all for grief or fear When one has been so much afraid And nothing in the world seemed clear;' Like realizing that, at last, A burden one has carried long Is laid aside and one is free To live again with hope and song; Like seeing life?yes, glorious life In all its grand simplicity. When life has seemed complex and strange. When one has seen small victory?

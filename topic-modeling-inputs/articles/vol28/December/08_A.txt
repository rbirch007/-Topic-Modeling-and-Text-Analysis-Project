MORNING PRAYER Rachel Barney Tatt With gratitude my heart is filled lor all the bounties Thou hast willed, For beauties of the earth and sky And all the joy that in them lie. Help me to smile in sun or rain, My soul to sing a sweet refrain | From when I see the morning light Until my eyes are closed at night. Renew my faith in truth each day, Inspire my work and guide my play, My thoughts, my hopes, and all my talk, My mind to seek all good—to walk In paths of peace, of Thee a part. Oh, give me most of all a heart To know forgiveness known by Him Who dying asked "grace for their sin."

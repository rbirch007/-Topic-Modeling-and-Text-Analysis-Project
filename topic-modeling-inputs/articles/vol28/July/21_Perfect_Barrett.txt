(To be continued) PERFECT TRUST Elsie E. Barrett So long as there are homes Where women beckon tired men, Where children play, and then Where thanks are given, duties done? So long as there are homes Where prayers are said to daily guide, Where love and faith abide, Where each dawn sees new tasks begun? So long as there are homes Remembering the Christmas star? Though many nations war, In darkness grope, The loyal souls within those homes Will still have hope.

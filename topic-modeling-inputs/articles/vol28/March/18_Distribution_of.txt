Distribution of Alcohol Pamphlet HE pamphlet, "Injurious Effects Resulting From the Moderate Use of Alcohol," by L. Mervin Maus, is now being mailed to the stakes and missions in the United States for distribution. Inasmuch as there is not a sufficient number for every Latter-day Saint home, it is suggested that the distribution be carefully considered; that pamphlets be placed in homes where they will be most appreciated.

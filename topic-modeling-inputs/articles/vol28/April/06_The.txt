"THE RAINS ARE OVER" Mrs. Marion Clegg
The rain is over! The blue of the sky Is tinted with rainbow hues on high; While out in my bush, the song birds raise A tumultuous, glorious song of praise.
Flowers that bowed their heads to the storm Are smiling again—upright and warm; Lifting glad colors up to the sun Rejoicing that the rain is done.
The doubts that tore at my heart are still, Shamed by the faith in the songsters' trill; Humbled by the flowers' undaunted trust In a Power greater than rain or dust.

Red Cross Membership Drive
FOR years the red cross has been the emblem of mercy at scenes of disaster and suffering. Dependent solely on the willingness of the public to alleviate suffering, the American Red Cross is again appealing to the nation for a million more members to expand its various services and to stand ready for the unpredictable days ahead. The Organization will embark November 11 on the greatest membership campaign since the World War. Only through strengthened membership can it meet more effectively the eventualities of suffering in the year to come. It is only good citizenship to respond to the call of this great humanitarian organization, to pledge our confidence in it by enrolling as members. The cost of individual membership is $1 per year, or as much more as a person wishes to give. Though many calls are made upon our members in furthering our own welfare program, we feel that we should cooperate and participate in the service of the Red Cross.
Attention: Ward Presidents
ONCE again we call the attention of Relief Society presidents to the fact that the General Board is not endorsing any commodity of any manufacturer irrespective of what it may be or the merit of same. Presidents will be notified by the General Office or through the columns of the Magazine regarding any endorsements of the Society. We trust presidents will bear this in mind when approached by salesmen.

Our Cover
WE are happy this month to publish on our cover excerpts from the 89th Section of the Doctrine and Covenants, "The Word of Wisdom." We have done this in the hope that those who have never read, or those who have not read for some time, the word of the Lord in this matter, might be brought to a consciousness of His Wisdom which He gave to us through the Prophet Joseph Smith, February 27, 1833, at Kirtland, Ohio. We are sorry that the space on our cover would not lend itself to use the complete revelation. We have left out some parts of the revelation which may in your opinion be more important than some we have included. In any case, we suggest that you read the 89th Section completely and read again "The Prophets Speak," found on pages 215 and 216 of this magazine.

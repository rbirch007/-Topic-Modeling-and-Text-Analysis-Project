os EVIDENCE By Della Adams Leitner We say we love the Sermon On The Mount, It is the true ideal for all good things. In contemplation of its noble truths Our hearts are thrilled, our inspiration wings, And we are lifted up, our spirits soar; And this is well. But if we fail to take Back to the valley of our daily lives These things to live by, then we only make A pretense that the doubters scoff to see. Interpreting is our responsibility.

HOW WISE
By Olive C. Wehr
How wise is man! His wisdom has added years To his life span. He achieves to conquer Adam's curse And to alleviate Eve's distress; He has verily learned to create Human comfort and happiness.
How wise is man? He has wantonly created, As only man can, For himself and for his generations, The ways and means that destroy Mankind, body and soul, and rob Life itself of meaning and joy.
How wise is man? How good is he? O God, in thy goodness Judge mercifully!

FIRE A SONG Across Judean hills arose A song, the angels singing; It touched the stillness of the night Softly as church bells ringing. A song of peace—peace and good will From Holy realms above For all the tired hungry hearts Who felt need of His Love. A song—immortal, heaven born— We'd hear it if we would; Look to Him now, for angels sing, Still sing of brotherhood. —Sylvia Probst.

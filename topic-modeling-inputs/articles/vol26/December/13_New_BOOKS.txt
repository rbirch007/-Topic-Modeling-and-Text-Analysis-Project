New Books BOOK two of the Jimmy Microbe series, under the title of HitchHiking With Jimmy Microbe, written by Virginia and Drew Jacobsen and Lyman L. Daines, M. D., all of Salt Lake City, made its appearance in the book stores of our country during the summer. This book has succeeded in presenting an adventuresome story for children providing at the same time in a very clever and original way scientific counsel on good health habits. A SECOND edition of Heart Throbs of the West, compiled by Mrs. Kate B. Carter and published by the Daughters of Utah Pioneers, is now off the press. The book deals with 24 definite phases of Pioneer life. It contains many new human-interest stories as well as valuable historical facts. Pioneer life will always be of interest, and this new volume will add much to our appreciation of the history of the West.

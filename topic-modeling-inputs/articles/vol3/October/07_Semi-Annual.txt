We are pleased to announce that the Relief Society School of Obstetrics and Nursing will begin its present term on Monday, September 18, 1916, at 9 o'clock a. m. Fourth Floor, Bishop's Building. From many letters of inquiry, we predict an increased attendance over last year. Any information regarding the school will be promptly given upon inquiry, at Room No. 29, Bishop's Building. Phone, Wasatch 3123. Amy Brown Lyman, General Secretary.

We desire to have a canvas made, this winter, for all the women in this Society to become annual or life members of the Genealogical Society of Utah. Once a life member, and there are no more dues to pay. No adult in the Church can afford to neglect this plain duty. The labors of this Society lie at the root of all our Temple work. Let us secure ten thousand life members for the Genealogical Society, during the year 1916. What the Relief Society sisters attempt will be a success. God bless the Relief Society

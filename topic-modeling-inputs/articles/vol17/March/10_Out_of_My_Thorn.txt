Out Of My Thorn By Helen Kimball Orgill I wove my broken blossoms Into a chaplet fair; I treasured every tear drop And strung a necklet rare; And all the heart-felt longing I made into a song? O boon of sweet evolving, From out my thorn and thong! How fully have I tested Deep joy and biting pains; I've found that without losses One never can have gains.

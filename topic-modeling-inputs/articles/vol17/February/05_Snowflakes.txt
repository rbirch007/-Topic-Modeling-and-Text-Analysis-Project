SNOWFLAKES By Nina Eckhart Kerrick. See snowflakes in the air, Falling, falling, everywhere; In their crystal purity? Thoughts, by words sent forth on earth, Oft in sadnes, oft in mirth. Oft in just the quiet way That the snowflakes fall today, Beautiful and pure and Oh! It is God who made them so. He who send the snow from heaven To the world has also given Blessed thoughts, so pure, devine, Just to drift to earth and shine Like the snowflake as it lay Glistening on the ground today.

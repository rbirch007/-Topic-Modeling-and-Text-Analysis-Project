Let the Holly Wreath Be Hung Margaret B. Shomaker When snow falls silent on the hill, Or flutters to my window sill, When all the paths we used to know Are hidden under drifts of snow, When Christmas songs are sweetly sung, Then let the holly wreath be hung. When 'earth is frozen silver white, And winter brings the longest night, When Santa with his reindeer stops Above the snow-capped chimney tops, When Christmas songs are sweetly sung, Then let the holly wreath be hung.

Fragrant Memories Grace Barker Wilson Remembered things are poignant as today: The scent of violet, The young, green odor of a fresh-cut lawn, Essence of mignonette; Elusive sweetness from the orchard trees When apples are in flower, The clean smell of a forest glade After a summer shower. Night From Capitol Fill Salt Lake City, Utah Margaret B. Shomaker The city lights its rich array Of window stars.... Yellow, orange, and red On the deepening blue. Temple spires pierce through the dark, And silence fills my heart.

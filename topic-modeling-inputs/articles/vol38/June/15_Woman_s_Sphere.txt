SWENSON, of Provo, Utah's Mother of the Year, has spent much of her time for the last seven years in assisting her eighty-year-old blind neighbor, Mss. Fannie Patton, in the work of converting into Braille type choice selections of literature and religious | writings. Mrs. Swenson, mother of nine children, is the wife of Professor Emeritus John C. Swenson of Brigham Young University, and the couple still live in the home which they entered as bride and groom fifty-two years ago. A successful teacher for many years, Mrs. Swenson still acts as a special tutor for children and young people in music and reading. RS. MARY T.

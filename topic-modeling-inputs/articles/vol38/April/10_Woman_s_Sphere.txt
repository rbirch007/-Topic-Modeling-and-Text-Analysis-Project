ELLEN ASH PETERSON, ninety-six, of Logan, Utah, cares for herself, crochets babies' bootees by the hundreds, and writes her own checks and birthday cards. She emigrated from England, and was a member of the first handcart company to cross the plains in 1856. At eighteen, Elizabeth Bryan of Salt Lake City won the grand prize in the "Make-It-YourselfWith-Wool" national sewing contest.

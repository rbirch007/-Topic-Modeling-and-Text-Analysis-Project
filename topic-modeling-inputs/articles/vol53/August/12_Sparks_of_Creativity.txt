SPARKS OF CREATIVITY Pearle M. Olsen Fine sparks of creativity, expressed in various ways, Exist in all — to some degree When we stop to appraise. Not only on a paper, canvas, screen or on a stage— Or through an instrument or voice Do artists best engage. More often with a salad, or fashioning a dress— In managing a loving home Real artists find success. The neighbor who rejoices or grieves along with you Releases sparks and sometimes flames of love— Creating, too!

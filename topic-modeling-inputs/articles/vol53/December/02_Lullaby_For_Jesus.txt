All bright stars that shine tonight Lend your brilliance to his light That guides the shepherds, say to them: "The Babe is born in Bethlehem." Cradled in a manger, Gentled by the hay, He is softly sleeping. It is Christmas day. Wrapped in swaddling clothes In the stable there, Look, the mother Mary Has sunshine in her hair. Warmed by oxen's breathing On this most blessed morn, Unto all men everywhere The holy Child is born. Waken, buds on olive boughs, And springtime blossoms open now.

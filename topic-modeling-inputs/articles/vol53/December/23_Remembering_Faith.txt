REMEMBERING FAITH Enola Chamberlin | remember seeing faith In my mother's eyes As, following my father's dreams, She looked at alien skies. | remember seeing faith In smiles with which she met The hardships of a dusty road, The children's tired fret. | remember seeing faith In her busy hands, As she set dear plants to grow In strange and unknown lands. | remember hearing faith, No matter drought or blight, in her voice when she knelt down To say her prayers at night.

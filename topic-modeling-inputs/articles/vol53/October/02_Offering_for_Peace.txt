OFFERING FOR PEACE Mabel Jones Gabbott Then Moses came when it was dark and still, Before the sun had tipped the jagged peak Of Sinai, and day was but a streak Of pink, and built an altar near the hill; And made an offering for peace, to fill The measure of the law. He came to seek Forgiveness for his people's wrong and speak In their behalf, that they would do God's will. And now before the lucent heavens trace The dawn, | humbly kneel. O Lord, impart Thy peace to me; and seeking to erase My sin from memory's sand, I, too, will chart Thy law upon my mountain, learn thy grace, And, penitent, will write it on my heart. DOROTHY J. ROBERTS

FROM A MOTHER'S HEART Kathryn Kay | did not think these Hands of mine, so simply styled, Unused to miracles and very plain, Could ever be a source of balsam to my child And by their gentle touch help ease the pain. | know in life it must be each must have his share Of pain and grief, so in my mother-heart | pray, Let my child have the strength to bear what he must bear When hurts are deeper than my hands can brush away.

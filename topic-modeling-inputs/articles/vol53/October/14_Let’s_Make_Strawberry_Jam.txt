LET'S MAKE STRAWBERRY JAM! Ida A. Isaacson 1 qt. Strawberries, hulled, washed, and drained Pour scalding water over strawberries, let stand 3 minutes. Drain. Add 1 cup sugar to berries, bring to a boil, and boil ten minutes by the clock. Add 2 cups more sugar. Cook 20 minutes (by clock). Stir constantly to avoid scorching. Remove from the stove and pour into an earthen dish, or crock (no metal bowl). Let stand for 24 hours. Stir every time you pass the bowl. After the jam has set for 24 hours, put it in clean, sterile bottles, which have been prepared beforehand, and placed on a thick towel so that they will be completely dry. Then seal with wax or self-sealing lids which have been dipped in boiling water.

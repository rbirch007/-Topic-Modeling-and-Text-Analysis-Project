MORNING GLORY MORNING Linda J. Houston The morning-glory morning Of celestial blue Came joyfully with Butterflies and warmth. Birds praised her; Roses opened their arms. She caressed two children at play, Filling their sand pails with gold dust Almost as bright as their innocent eyes. The slender cornstalks Straightened their shoulders, The brook sighed blissfully; And the hearts of those who felt Her splendor Were airy and light that day.

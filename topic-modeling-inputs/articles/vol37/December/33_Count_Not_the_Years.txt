Count Not She Years C, Frank Steele Count not the years of life Though here they may be brief— She would be pained by tears, Our unrestrained grief. Think rather of the joys That came to her each day; The memory of her smile— That does not pass away. Forget her fleeting years, Erase the reckoning; Did we not have her love Making our own hearts sing? Count not the years of life, With eternity to share; Open the gates some dawn And you will find her there.

Ethel C. Smith Released as General Board Member T is with keen regret that the general board on Wednesday, October 4, 1950, acted upon and voted the release of Ethel G. Smith as a member of the general board. During the three years which Sister Smith has served on the board, she has given devoted and careful attention to those duties which have been assigned to her. Her labors as chairman of the theology committee have always been characterized by a thoroughness and exactness to detail which have been praiseworthy. She has filled all other assignments given her with equal fidelity and ability. Sister Ethel Smith leaves the board with the warm affection of all the board members and with the hope that her health may improve when the arduous duties performed by general board members are no longer required of her. With her great love for Relief Society, she will continue to serve Relief Society as her health and circumstances permit. + Where Shepherds KRnelt Margery S.

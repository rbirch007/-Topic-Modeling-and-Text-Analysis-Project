Pastel of Spring Dorothy J]. Roberts But a few days now till spring be over And summer whiten the lanes with clover. Swift were the metamorphoses From the branches' brown cocoons to these Tiny, lifted wings—like feather— Sprouting from every twig. Oh, tether This gauze of bud-lace a moment longer Until the image be made stronger Of this first live pastel of spring, After a year's remembering.

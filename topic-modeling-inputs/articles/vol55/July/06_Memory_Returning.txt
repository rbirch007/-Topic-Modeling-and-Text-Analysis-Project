Coles Wheel and Woman Ruth C.

When I had an opportunity to tell the cake baker how much joy she had brought to me and to so many others by this lovely cake to the little girl, she smiled. And said: "Then I am well repaid for the time spent in shaping and decorating the butterfly." She grew silent in reflection and then added, "And I have helped fulfill a long-standing promise made when I myself was a little girl." The cake was a memorial to a very special spiritual experience.

Casserole of Mixed Vegetables

1 lb. Kidney beans
2 leeks
2 onions, medium sized
2 oz. Butter or margarine
2 carrots 1/2 pint stock (chicken or beef)
1/2 lb. Artichokes, tips and hearts only
1 tbsp. Tomato ketchup (or any other root vegetable) salt and pepper

Soak beans overnight. Peel, wash, and slice onions, carrots, artichokes, and leeks. Melt the fat in a saucepan. Fry onions, leeks, and carrots until golden brown. Place in casserole and add the stock, artichokes, beans (drained), and ketchup. Season well, cover with lid, and bake at 325 degrees until brown.

Nut Cutlets

1 oz. Butter
juice of 2 lemon
1 oz. Flour: salt and pepper
1/2 pint milk
1 egg yolk, beaten
1 oz. Nuts

Brown the butter in a saucepan. Add the lemon juice, flour, and seasonings. Stir until smooth. Slowly add the milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the nuts. Serve with a salad or vegetables.
 
Mixed minced nuts 
1 c. Brown bread crumbs 
2 oz. White bread crumbs 
fat for deep frying 
1 tsp. Finely minced onion 
tomato sauce, if desired 
4 tsp. Chopped parsley 

Make a mixture with the butter, flour, and milk, and add nuts, white crumbs, onion, parsley, lemon juice, and seasoning and mix well. Allow to cool. Form into cutlets, dip into yolk of egg, coat with brown bread crumbs, and fry in hot fat. Tomato sauce may be served with the dish.

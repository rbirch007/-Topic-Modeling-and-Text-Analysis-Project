Open the Door Through The ." This was the theme chosen for the 1967 Relief Society Annual General Conference Magazine display. Five stakes were selected to prepare displays depicting the values to be found in the various sections of The . These exhibits revealed the resourcefulness of the Stake Relief Society leadership and provided tangible evidence of the professionalism which may be achieved by sisters who work lovingly together. Most of the displays were also used in the local stake leadership meetings and proved a great stimulation to the sisters in encouraging increased participation.

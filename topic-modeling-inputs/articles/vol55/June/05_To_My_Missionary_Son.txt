Flitting from one idea to the next will only bring frustration to you and your family. When what you are doing dulls a little, then search for that idea you have stored away. Keep your mind open, always weighing carefully all you hear, all you read. Ponder on new ideas, and then pray to your Father in heaven asking, "Is this best for my family?" Consistency, with love, the heavy hand with the whipcream touch, will bring you success.

A Toy Tub for the Children a Peggy Ann Horton, Murray South Stake (Displayed at the Work Meeting Exhibit of the Relief Society Annual General Conference, 1964) 924 Hat Rume! The toy tub in the picture is 151% inches tall and 201% inches in diameter. It is glue. Bias tape is glued along the top to cover the rough edge, and red fringe is attached to make a gay-looking trim for the tub. The felt train, made from 2 pattern in a coloring book, is glued on the denim. Toy tubs and toy boxes can be mounted on rollers or on wheels for easy moving from place to place. Handles can be attached to the tub or box for ease in lifting.

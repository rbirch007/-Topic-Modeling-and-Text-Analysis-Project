AT CHRISTMAS TIME The tree on the table is made of raffia. Some of the raffia is left in its original dark nink color and some of it is sprayed a pale pink. Gold and silver glitter crystals are sprinkled on the raffia and small lights glow in the branches. The raffia is given body for the branches by being intertwined with wire.

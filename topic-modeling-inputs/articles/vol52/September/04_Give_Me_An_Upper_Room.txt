Give Me an Upper Room Christie Lund Coles Dear Lord, give me an upper room Where | may come—even as you Came into a place of quietude — Where | may rest and pray, renew Myself. Where | may face my soul Quite unafraid; and, too, face thee, Freeing my spirit of its doubt, 'Its fear, its brief uncertainty. Let it be filled with utter love, Make it a sanctuary of grace, Of peace and beauty and of hope,. Setting my world's most tranquil pace. Here, let me recollect the words, Admonishing, ''Peace, peace, be still." Here, let me aspire to all that | may be,' Learning to know and love and live thy will.

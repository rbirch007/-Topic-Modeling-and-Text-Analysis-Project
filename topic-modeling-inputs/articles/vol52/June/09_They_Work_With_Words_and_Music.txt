She still seems such a child. And yet she's the same age that I was when I married. She's my serious one. Her face is so intent. I think these Home Evenings are exceptionally good for her. She's usually withdrawn. It's strange. As many friends as she has, she often seems alone. Time after time I've wondered what she's thinking. She's always been a good child, 432 and we've been fairly close without really saying anything about it. But these Family Home Evenings. Why, I've heard her give, as her own opinions, suggestion after suggestion that I've made to her and thought she hadn't even heard! Thought she had resented. Look at Tracy.

To be Continued Hill Fever By Elzada C. Brinkerhoff (With Apologies to John Masefield) I must go back to the hills again, Must answer the call of the quail; Go follow the breeze till I come To the end of the sunset trail. And all I ask is the time and chance To wander far and wide, With the crests of the hills and the light Of the stars to be my guide. I must go tramp in the hills again, For the call is loud and clear; I must go search for the bedded fern And the buds of the early year. And when I'm tired I'll lay me down To rest a bit on the sod. I'll dream of love and the days gone lye While I sleep at peace with my God.

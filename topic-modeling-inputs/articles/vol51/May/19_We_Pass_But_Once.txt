We Pass But Once Catherine B. Bowles The evening shades had fallen And my long day's work was done. Was | a little better Than when my labors had begun? Had kindly words been spoken, Had a heart been filled with cheer, Had | lent a helping hand To my neighbors who live near — Helped them to seek for blessings Richly given from above? Then my life would be uplifted And | would share with them my love. For life holds such lovely things— My heart is grateful for the joy it brings.

Sowing Dragon's Teeth
When, for the gratification of their appetites, or the promotion of their interests, lawyers, bankers, great merchants and manufacturers, and social leaders, both men and women, disobey and scoff at this law, (the Prohibition Law) or any other law, they are aiding the cause of anarchy and promoting mob violence, robbery and homicide; they are sowing dragon's teeth; and they need not be surprised when they find that no judicial or police authority can save our country or humanity from reaping the harvest."—The Judicial Section of the American Bar Association, August 31, 1921,

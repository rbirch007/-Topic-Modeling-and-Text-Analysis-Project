A Case of Devotion By Superintendent Ralph B. Keeler ERE is a case of exceptional devotion to church work that I believe is worthy of a little recognition. Mrs. Maude Farnsworth, President of the Colonia Garcia Ward Relief Society, mother of thirteen children, rode twelve miles on horseback and forty-five on the fender of a Ford roadster, over unimproved mountain roads in northern Mexico to attend the Quarterly Stake Conference of the Juarez Stake which was held the later part of last November. The thirteenth child of this mother was held by another mother in the crowded car during the journey. * The above incident is only typical of this good sister's interest in gathering information, and otherwise better equipping herself to direct Relief Society work in her little ward.

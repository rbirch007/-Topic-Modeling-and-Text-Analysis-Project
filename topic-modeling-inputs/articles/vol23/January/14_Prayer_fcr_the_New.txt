CuRTAIN PRAYER FOR THE NEW YEAR By Linnie Parker 'Tis midnight, and the whistles blow, The New Year comes—the Old must go— He moves with measured step and slow Into the past; I watch him go and breathe a pray'r That this New Year might be as fair— As filled with joy—as free from care As was the last; That my mistakes in last year's tries Might make me just a bit more wise— Be stepping-stones to help me rise In this New Year; That all mistakes that others made Might leave within my heart no shade Of malice—may I let them fade— With the Old Year!

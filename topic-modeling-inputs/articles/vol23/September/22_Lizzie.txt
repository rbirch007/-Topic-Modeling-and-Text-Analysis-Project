Lizzie Thomas Edward N the death of Lizzie Thomas Edward July 23, 1936, there passed away one of our loveliest singers. She gave a lifetime of service to the musical uplift of the people among whom she lived. From the Atlantic to the Pacific and from Canada to Mexico people were charmed by her marvelous voice. Not only as a soloist of the Tabernacle Choir did she serve but as director of the choir of her own ward, in concerts and opera work, and for years she furnished the music for our Relief Society Conferences. She leaves us ripe in experience and rich because of the generous use she made of her talent.

Ss co CX GOLDEN WEDDING By Sarah Ahlstrom Nelson Fifty years you've climbed together O'er the rugged hill of life, Fifty years of joys and sorrows, Fifty years as man and wife. Stalwart youth and graceful maiden Knelt beside the altar low, Pledged the vows that naught couid sever, On that day so long ago. Hopes were strong and young hearts eager To begin the tasks of life, Toil and hardships left undaunted, Brave hearts, ready for the strife. Just a simple little cottage, But love made a home complete, Baby faces came to bless you,— Baby voices, wondrous sweet. Tender memories cluster round those Happy days of by-gone years, Boys and girls around your fireside, Telling you their hopes and fears. - Yours, the guiding hands that led them In the paths of truth and right, Yours, the words of hope and courage, Yours, the prayers for them at night. Life has brought both cloud and sunshine, Joys and grief have been your own, But you trod the path of duty, And your names for good areknown. Sons and daughters gather round you With their children's children too, Love in every heart abounding For your lives so fine and true. Friends and kindred fondly greet. You Wish you well along the way, May God add His choicest blessings, On your golden wedding day.

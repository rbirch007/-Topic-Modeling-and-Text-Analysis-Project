UTAH LAKE Night Grace Barker Wilson The day has turned the sunset off, And night, with slippered tread, Comes in on carpets of the dark, As stars bloom overhead. The mountains still wear caps of light, And robes of purple mist; The mirror lake has gone to rest With a goodnight moon-kissed.

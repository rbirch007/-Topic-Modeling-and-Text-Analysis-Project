Mss JENNIE CAMPBELL has been appointed associate professor of elementary education at the Brigham Young University, Provo. She was formerly international president of Childhood Education. RUTH PAINTER RANDALL has written a biography of Mary Lincoln, which has been acclaimed by historians and literary critics as an excellent piece of research, beautifully written.

One Shining Day Josephine ]. Harvey Today I walk in happiness, heart-deep, Again the ancient earth has stirred From wintered sleep. Again the spring is on the land With newborn sounds, soft-heard. I hold one crimson blossom In my hand. The gentle winds of May Touch new and tender leaves This lilac-scented day. You will love the time of blossoming, The magic pattern springtime weaves; One shining day, calmly you will come. Joy will be a living thing, And where we walk, meadow larks will sing. Page 305

She Gift She Liked the Best Frances C. Yost Grandma's birthday rolled around, Rolled to eighty-six. Friends and family brought her gifts — Clothes and candy-mix. Folks gave paper, ink, and pen— Things to keep her writing, Warm wool hose, and paisley shawls. Oh, it was exciting! One gift made her face illume— Glamorized "sweetheart" perfume. Page 841

A PRAYER OF GRATITUDE. Matilda K. Galloway. I thank thee, heavenly Father, For thy blessings unto me. I thank thee for my loved ones, And for the gospel free. I thank thee for salvation; That I learned to love the truth; And I thank thee for the parents Who were guardians of my youth. I thank thee for redemption Obtained thru' his great love, And help me, heavenly Father, To always worthy prove.

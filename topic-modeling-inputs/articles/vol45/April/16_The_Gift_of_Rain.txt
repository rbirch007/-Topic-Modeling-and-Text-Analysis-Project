She Gift of Rain Dora Toone Brough The gift of rain—a blessing and delight; It cools the mountain's crest, the burning sand, Revives the drooping blossoms, bathes the trees, And heals for man the dry, drouth-stricken land. Like human tears, the rain falls and relieves; Dissolves and washes ills from God's domain, Bestows sweet comfort, lessens want and fear— Yields gratitude, helps conquer and sustain.

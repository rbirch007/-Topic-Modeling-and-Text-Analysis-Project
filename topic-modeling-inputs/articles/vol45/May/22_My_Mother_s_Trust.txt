My Mothers Srust Hannah C. Ashby My mother's trust was like a star, To guide my path that led afar. She had such confidence in me, I always felt that I was free To make a choice without a bar. And yet for worlds, I would not mar That sacred tie, or leave a scat To blemish in a slight degree My mother's trust. How helpful loving mothers are, They gently leave the door ajar. The teachings at my mother's knee Have shaped my earthly destiny. The greatest debt I know, is for My mother's trust. —o<—i-o—

Of Power and Love Genevieve Wyatt For God hath not given us the spirit of fear; but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind (II Timothy 1:7). Peace of mind I asked for, "Oh, peace of mind," I prayed, "God grant me this one blessing For I am sore afraid." And through the dark T struggled And through the dark cried out, For fear still lingered with me In troubled thoughts and doubt. Then, seeing peace in loved one's eyes, I saw, too, perfect trust, For peace of mind is perfect faith— God's love is true and just. And as my faith grows stronger God's greatest gift is sure. I know it will be completely mine When faith is full and pure.

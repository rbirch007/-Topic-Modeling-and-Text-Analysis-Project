Change In Price of Relief Society Pins EFFECTIVE April 1, 1944, the federal tax on jewelry was increased from ten per cent to twenty per cent. This tax applies to Relief Society pins, prices of which, including the increased tax, are now: Pin No. 1? $1.20 postpaid (24 carat gold-plated). Pin No. 2? $2.00 postpaid (10 carat solid-gold front with 24 carat gold-plated back).

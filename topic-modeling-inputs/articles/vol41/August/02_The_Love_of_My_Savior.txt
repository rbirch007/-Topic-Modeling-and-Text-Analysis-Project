She Love of My Savior Sarah Carlisle I sat alone by the fireside Thinking of days past and gone. I thought of friends and surroundings; I thought of loved ones and home. Then over me came stealing an infinite gladness, So deep, so serene, so divine— It was the thought of the love of my Savior Who had given his life for mankind. My heart filed with joy and rejoicing To think he did this for me. Now I know I too must be giving For all he has given to me.

After First Grief Maude Rubin How sudden is the hour of ice, Of frosted fern. Can the heart renew hope's sweet device, Can the mind re-learn? We walked together through a land Of butterflies... Shimmer of June. Now, wintered and cold, mv heart is bound In a tight cocoon. Oh, out of this chrysalis of night Let bright wings lift To ride another summert's light, Time's golden gift!

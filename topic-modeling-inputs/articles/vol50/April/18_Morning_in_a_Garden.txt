Morning in a Garden Sylvia Probst Young Day opens in my garden to a rose, Pink beauty glowing with the dew's caress, To queenly, blue delphinium and phlox. Her golden fingers touch with gentleness Young pansy faces, gladiola cup, The leaves of silver birch.... My eyes look up — The sky, wide arched above — serenity. Now in my garden in this waking hour My heart is lifted to a quiet peace, With singing bird, with every blooming flower; Softly I walk in morning's solitude, My garden speaks an answer — God is good.

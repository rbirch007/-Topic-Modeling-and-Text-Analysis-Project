For Tomorrow Dorothy J. Roberts Burden are the moments — Save those love-tempered ones — Called back by blossoming plum, Revived by rivers,. Wakened by a winding road. Burden, are the days, Walking a lonely way, Burden, the refusals. Adamant, the contour Of the prideful deed Sculptured on the years.... Impending and allotted time, The better self be yours. Cupped here is essence of desire, Bouquet to pour Whole, upon the task, The tender eyes, the hour. Aoe MAD

BUTTER FROSTING MADE WITH A MIXER Judith Leigh-Kendall Using a small mixer bowl, pour in one pound of unsifted powdered sugar. Add 17, cup soft butter and 3 tablespoons liquid (water, milk, or cream). Turn the mixer on to the lowest speed and mix well. Add one teaspoon flavoring, and turn the mixer up high for whipping. If the frosting seems too thick, add another tablespoon of liquid. Whip at high speed a few minutes. This frosting is lump free and saves a few minutes of a busy homemaker's time. It will frost the tops and sides of most layer cakes or a sheet cake.

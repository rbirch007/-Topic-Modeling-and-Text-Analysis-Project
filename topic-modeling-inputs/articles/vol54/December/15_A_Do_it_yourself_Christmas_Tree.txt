A Do-it-Yourself Christmas Tree! M. Heslop A rubber tree plant cleverly converted into a Christmas tree adorns the family room of the Lorenzo S. Youngs. A few cedar boughs fill out the tree, which is adorned with red balls, colored lights, and silver bows. The table is decorated with holly and poinsettias. The poinsettia pattern is carried out in the napkins and the center runner of red satin adds much to a colorful and decorative room.

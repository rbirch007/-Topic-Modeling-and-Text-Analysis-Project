NO SACRIFICE Linnie Fisher Robinson | made a promise in my early youth To sacrifice for thee, dear Lord, above; To learn thy sovereign will, thy every word, And serve my fellows with unstinting love. And | have served in myriad ways And shared with those | found in need; But, oh, the sweetness that came back to me, Repaid so many times each single deed. So never once in all these years, Has there been aught of sacrifice from me; Accept my good desires, through rueful tears, | have but gained the while and better see.

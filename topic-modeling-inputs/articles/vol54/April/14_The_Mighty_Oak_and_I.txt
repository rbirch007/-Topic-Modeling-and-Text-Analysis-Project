THE MIGHTY OAK AND | Laura M. Gowing Lowestoft, Suffolk, England You are tall and great and strong; You have been here ages, Long before this town began. | shelter beneath your branches, Lean against your gnarled old frame: And though | am nearly three score years and ten, | feel but a child again As | measure Your years, against mine. You, too, must feel the same, When measured against his eternity: For he was, before the world began.

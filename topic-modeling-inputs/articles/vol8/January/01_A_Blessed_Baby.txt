A Blessed Baby. We are using for our New Year's frontispiece a picture which is the most remarkable group we have ever seen, for the baby on the lap of the mother is surrounded by her two living grandmothers and her four great grandmothers, and below her is one of her great-great grandmothers. The names are as follows: Baby Peterson—born May 1, 1920, in Levan, Juab Co., Utah. Mother, Thelma Bosh Peterson, born Dec: 9, 1902, Levan, Juab co., Utah. Wo Grandmothers Millie M. Wankeer, born Jan. 28, 1867, age 53, in Denmark. Leah S. Bosh, born August 23, 1882, at Sandy.

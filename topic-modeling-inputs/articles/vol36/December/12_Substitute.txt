Blanched almonds — Wa WW W Cream the shortening and sugar; add the beaten yolks of the eggs, lemon juice, and rind. Sift the flour and baking powder two or three times, and add, mixing well. Separate the dough in three parts, and form into rolls. Place these in the refrigerator for a few hours. When ready to bake, slice thin, arrange on cookie sheet, brush each cookie with slightly beaten egg whites, sprinkle with sugar and slivered almonds. Bake in a moderate oven. =<

Always be an example to your child. 2. Establish confidence among your boys and girls. 3. Establish traditions for your home so strong that nothing can come to break it. Have various days stand out as events in the home. 4. Use the agencies that are available for building character. Morality can be taught as well as arithmetic. The parents who can plant devotion and faith in God in their children have already laid a good foundation for a fine and happy life. 'The value of the blessing on the food and family prayers gives spiritual joy and gratitude in the home. People who pray together do not get very far apart. In our modern Latter-day Saint homes we must teach by example the principles of honesty, dependability, and righteousness. It is the responsibility of the home to teach the three R's: righteous living; reverence; and responsibility. How do we teach righteousness? By righteous living. Reverence? By devotion to prayer and to Church principles and teachings. Responsibility? Each member should be given responsibility. Home work

Tempered PART I Gladys I. Hamilton ARNET BLENDALL tried to appear natural as she stood on tiptoe to kiss her husband, Lee, goodbye. Then, quite casually, she plucked a minute speck of lint from her father-in-law's rough whipcord coat.

FRIEND Grace B. Davis For all the beauty of this lovely tree,
For stately grace and verdant mystery
Of restless leaves whose movements lightly trace
Their graceful patterns as they interlace
In shadowed arabesques upon the velvet sod, My heart sends up its grateful thanks to God.
Her brooding branches gently sway and bend
To moods of wandering winds that lightly tend
The tiny nestlings as their cradles high
Swing gently to a twilight lullaby.
For shadowed coolness, strength, and majesty,
My heart rejoices in this friendly tree. -

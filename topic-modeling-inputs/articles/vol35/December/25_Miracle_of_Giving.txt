TAKE A TRIP TO OLD RUINS Mexico and Central America Sponsored by Florence Pierce, author of "The Story of the Book of Mormon'' and "The Golden Plates." Conducted by Dewey Farnsworth, author of "The Americas Before Columbus."

Leaving Salt Lake City January 5, 1949

An all-expense-paid 30-day trip by bus and plane to the fantastic ruins of Mexico and Central America, where magnificent civilizations flourished and vanished centuries before Columbus. Travel through Mexico, Guatemala, San Salvador, Honduras, and Yucatan.

See Mexico City with its National Palace, Palace of Fine Arts, and Cathedral; the great Cement City of Teotihuacan, Pyramid of the Sun and Moon; Toluca with its native markets; Xochicalco and the "Silver City"; Floating Gardens, Teneyuca, Cuicuilco, Cholula, Monte Alban and museums; Mitla, Chichen-Itza, Uxmal, and Kabah.

See Carlsbad Caverns and other points en route. Travel in luxurious chartered buses and safe Pan American Airline planes; live in the best hotels; eat the finest meals.

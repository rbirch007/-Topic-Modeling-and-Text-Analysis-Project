MY WEALTH Della Adams Leitner

I thank Thee, Lord, for everything That makes life fair for me. My humble home, my clean white bed, The beauty that I see In my small garden's vivid blooms, Old-fashioned pinks and phlox, Sweet heliotrope and columbine And sleepy four-o'clocks; The comfort of a garden swing, Birds nesting in my trees, The singing wind and pattering rain, The hum of busy bees. The laughter of my child at play, My husband's glad "Hello"; Oh, I am rich beyond all words, Dear Lord, I thank thee so.

To one and all, we wish the happiest New Year ever. And to all, we thank you for your appreciated patronage.

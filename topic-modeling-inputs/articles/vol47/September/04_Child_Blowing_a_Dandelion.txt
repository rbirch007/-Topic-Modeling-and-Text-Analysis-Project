Child Blowing a Dandelion Ida Elaine James Blow, little one, blow hard on the fluff! If you close your eyes, and wish hard enough There's a secret that nobody else may know, But a dandelion, and a child to blow. There's magic from furry, soft ball to stem; A field full of wishes when children find them. They wait to be held to the lips of a child, Your little half-brothers, free and wild!

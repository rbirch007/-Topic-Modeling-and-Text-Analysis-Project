CHRISTENING THE NEW CARRIAGE we children chattered excitedly about those whizzing automobiles we had seen. Would we ever, ever ride in one, or wear one of those glamorous veils, we wondered? Papa might have been wondering a little, too. He held the reins 263 loosely, looking straight ahead with no comments on the corn we passed. Only now and then he'd speak, and when he did, it was to say something about automobiles. As yet, our new car was only a gleam in his eye, but the gleam was there.

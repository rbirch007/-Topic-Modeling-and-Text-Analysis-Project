GRANAHAN is chairman of the Postal Operations Subcommittee of the House Post Office Committee, which is making an examination of obscenity in motion pictures and motion picture advertising accepted by newspapers and carried through the mail. A number of influential newspapers of wide circulation have adopted codes of standards for acceptance of movie advertising. 'They ban illustrations such as those showing people in suggestive dress, using alcohol or narcotics, or "showing a high state of violence." ORENE H. PEARSON, a novelist of Santa Fe, New Mexico, is one of three water commissioners for the ancient aqueduct "Acequia Muralla." Mrs. Pearson is the only woman to hold this position in the 150 years since this historic irrigation ditch was first in use. Mrs. Pearson's other accomplishments and hobbies include: designing houses and assisting in their construction; carding, dyeing, and spinning wool; baking various kinds of bread in an outdoor adobe oven; designing and sewing a_ beautiful line of patio dresses; raising fruits and vegetables and drying and canning them; and tailoring coats and suits for men and women.

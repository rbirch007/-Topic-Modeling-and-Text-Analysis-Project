I could not cry the day you left me standing Unprepared for this, our last farewell. 'The world around was bursting forth with promise That life was sweet and spring about to swell. My only premonition of disaster Was weeping sky that filled the tulip's brim. I thought at once of sweet, thirst-quenching moisture That hastens growing buds along each limb. My mind was on this earthly resurrection Of flowing sap and flowering gardens gay. I could not sense the fact that you were leaving; Yet weeping skies are blue beyond the gray.

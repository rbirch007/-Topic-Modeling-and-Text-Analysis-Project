Presiding Bishopric
The General Presidency and Board of Relief Society are privileged to have as their advisory council the Presiding Bishopric. During the administration of Bishop Sylvester Q. Cannon, David A. Smith and John Wells, the relationship has been most pleasant and of inestimable value. The clear vision of these leaders with regard to Relief Society objectives and problems, their sympathetic understanding, has enabled them to counsel with wisdom. The Relief Society is indebted to these men for the service they have rendered in furthering its work.
The new Presiding Bishopric, Elders LeGrande Richards, Marvin O. Ashton and Joseph L. Wirthlin, bring to the position the wisdom and understanding of years of service in the Church. As Church workers they have had opportunity to understand and appreciate the work of Relief Society. That the same spirit of understanding and cooperation will continue to exist there can be no doubt. We extend to these brethren the best wishes of the Relief Society Organization.

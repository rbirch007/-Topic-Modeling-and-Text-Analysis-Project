—_-< THE WATERFALL Terrence Sylvester Glennamaddy Swift, mystic torrent! How unceasingly You pour your mighty waters o'er yon brink Downward in laughing ecstacy— A cataclysm. In always active change of rhythmic tone Your cataracts boom forth a song Deener in melody of joy Than music's strain. What: power supreme your mist exemplifies, Oh! Mighty, rushing waterfall, As if in answer to divine command, Forever on. Nope you stop to wonder where you cap, But plunge into the chasm far below, *Break in eddies 'pon the rocks Beneath the sun. There is no pageantry can be compared With nature's noble handiwork. Through countless ages your spray has dashed Into the gorge. Dash on then. Leap and foam into the vale below. The rainbow in thy mist shall live, And trout shall dance in glee, When I am gone. Oh! Mighty God of nature and of man, Thy likeness in the waterfall I see; Thy image in the rising mist behold, And worship Thee.

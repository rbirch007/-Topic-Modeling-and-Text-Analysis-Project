Ki ootsteps Catherine B. Bowles "Hold up my goings in thy paths, that my footsteps slip not" (Psalms 17:5). Footsteps, footsteps, here and there Searching, hunting everywhere — In the wood, through the heather, Facing sun or stormy weather. Some lead over the rocky ridge, In dark places where the light Dims the way, obscures the sight; Missing flowers along the way, Singing birds, the sun's bright ray, Gentle clasp of friendship's hand And many beauties of the land. Footsteps, footsteps, lead aright, Walk in paths that bring delight. Let our footsteps ever be A pathway to eternity.

LAVER KNUDSEN MILLARD, a Latter-day Saint, of Reseda, California, is California State Music Chairman for the Parent-Teachers Association, and during the past six years of her direction, the Mothersingers, having more than one thousand singers in the central organization, have presented the music at the California State Parent-Teachers Association Conventions. Mrs. Millard has served on Relief Society and M. I. A. Stake boards and is active in other Church and community organizations. She is the mother of two children, and since her husband died three years ago she has been doing choral work in adult education in Los Angeles County. ME: ARTHUR LAHTINEN, of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, handicapped by polio at the age of three years, was nominated by the Broward County Chapter of the National Foundation as Mother of the Year. The requirement for this honor is being of service (despite a handicap) to self, family, and community. Mrs. Lahtinen is an excellent wife, mother, musician, and laboratory technician.

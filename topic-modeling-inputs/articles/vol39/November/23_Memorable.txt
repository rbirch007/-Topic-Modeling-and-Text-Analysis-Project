Memorable Iris W. Schow He who seeks for beauty but to please his eye Finds it as elusive as a butterfly, Passing like a rainbow ephemerally away, Unimportant as the dream forgotten in a day. He who, while at labor, has observed the glow Of a perfect sunrise, comes at length to know Beauty held in duty's mesh keeps its rightful place, Permanent as emerald, remembered like love's face.

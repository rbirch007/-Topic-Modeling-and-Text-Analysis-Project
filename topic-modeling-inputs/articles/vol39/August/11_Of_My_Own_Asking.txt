Of My Own Asking Iris W. Schow Why does a dash of spray or a salt breeze ' Become an invitation, luring me? What is this longing for a heaving deck I feel each time I watch a ship at sea? Is it a reckless remnant in my blood From some old salt who laughed at Neptune's frown? Or is it just a wistful heritage Ancestral stay-at-homes have handed down?

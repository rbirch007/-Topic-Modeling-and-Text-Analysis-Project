I would sing of the stars that welcome The traveler to balsam bed, And a cover that scents of the forest As the night drifts overhead, 'Aunt Emma Webb" Makes Six Quilts in One Year "Aunt Emma Webb" of Parowan, Utah, is eighty-nine years old, but her age does not prevent her from being busy and happy. During one year she made the six beautiful quilts shown in the photograph. The dress in the left-hand corner was made entirely by hand and won first prize at the Iron County Fair. Mrs. Webb also crochets, makes rugs, blouses, aprons, and many other beautifully designed and carefully constructed articles. She does all her own housework, laundry, and shopping, and is a gracious hostess who always has homemade cookies, cake, or pie on hand to serve to her many friends when they call on her. She is still an active member of the Parowan Third Ward Relief Society, and takes great interest in Church and community affairs. Two of Mrs. Webb's six children are still living. She has been a widow for many years, but she has adjusted so well to sorrow and disappointment, and has kept herself so busy and so cheerful, that she is known among the people of her community as an outstanding example of "growing old gracefully." a

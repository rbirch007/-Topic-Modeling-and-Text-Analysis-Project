A CHRISTMAS DECORATION FOR RELIEF SOCIETY SOCIAL OR PROGRAM Inez R. Allen What could be more typical of the Christmas thought than to have the shepherds watching the new star of Bethlehem, and the Wise Men with their camels following its guiding rays? The mantel in our Relief Society room was the setting for this holiday theme. The weeds and leaves which were used to frame the picture were gilded to harmonize with the color scheme of the room.

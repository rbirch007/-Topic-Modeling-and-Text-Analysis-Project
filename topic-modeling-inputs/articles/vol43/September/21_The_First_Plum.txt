She First Plum Kathryn Tanner So red, so round and beautiful— And watched the tree develop; We handled it with care, Now the trunk was firm and thick. And felt that we had never seen The luscious fruit we tasted then Its equal anywhere. Was proof enough to me When my two youngsters brought it in, Our efforts in the orchard Their faces all alight—. Had turned out successfully. "Look, Mom, a plum; it's off our tree! And as I munched with my young sons, You get the biggest bite!"

She Almighty Jessie Dagsland Johannessen The wind is shaking the trees. The leaves tremble. They know the time soon is here for them to leave this Wonderful world of birds, and sweet smell of flowers. They went too fast—spring with its birth—and summer— This beautiful summer with its laughter and happiness. They are turning yellow now, these leaves, and they try Desperately to let people remember them. They put on their most colorful garments, and to the Music of the wind they tread the last dance— But the people—they hasten—don't stop to see This magnificent work of nature— Except a handful out of the world who look up in wonder, In wonder of him, the Almighty, who made all this possible. Who are we—not to stop and think of him who gave everything, Even his Only Begotten, so that all who believe should not perish— But have everlasting life? N DEPARTMENT

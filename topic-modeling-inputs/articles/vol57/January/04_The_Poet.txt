THE POET Ruth G. Rothe The Blessings and Power of the Priesthood But even in these families the powers of the priesthood may be enjoyed. Faithful wives and mothers may teach their children to pray and to believe and live the gospel. All of our sisters may call for the ministration of their bishops who are fathers of the entire ward. These sisters should also have the ministration of their home teachers, whose influence may be felt continually, not only in their frequent priestly visits, but in their constant nearness and availability to all the homes of the ward or the branch. These brethren may be called upon to bless in time of need. They may counsel and give advice. No family in the Church need go without the inspired guidance of the priesthood of God.

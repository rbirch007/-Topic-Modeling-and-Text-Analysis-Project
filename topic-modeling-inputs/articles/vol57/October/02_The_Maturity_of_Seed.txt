Contest General Announcement The Relief Society General Board is pleased to announce the third contest for Latter-day Saint women in song writing (lyrics and music) to be conducted annually. The purpose of the contest is to encourage creativity in this form of art and excellence in the general field of music among the women of the Church. The General Board will be happy to receive entries following this announcement until the closing date of the contest (see below).

Name of Contest: The contest is known as THE RELIEF SOCIETY SONG CONTEST.

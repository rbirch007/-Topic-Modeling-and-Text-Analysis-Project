The sea the sky, in mist
The city floats.
Never static
Like the mountains are static.
Frozen waves
Are the mountains.
The sea and the city move.

Grayce E. Cutler

Ges

Catherine O'Donnell Kemp Hiatt, Riverton Stake, Utah, is always eager to learn new methods or kinds of handiwork. Her home and the homes of her family and many friends are beautified by the various types of work she has completed. Her quilts are beautiful creations of many types, and she is always willing to help others to learn the arts she has enjoyed for so many years.

Sister Hiatt is the mother of ten children. There are forty-eight grandchildren and twenty-two great-grandchildren. All of them have received lovely gifts made especially for them.

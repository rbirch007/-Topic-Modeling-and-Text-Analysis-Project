"Celestia Rees Jessie Evans Smith, wife of President Joseph Fielding Smith of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, was Signally honored with the presentation of a bronze medal at a meeting of six hundred music directors at the annual conference of the Mutual Improvement Association of the Church in June. The award was given to Mrs. Smith for her 'Service to Youth' and for her outstanding musical gifts and especially for her 'singing joyfully.'"

At the meeting, the President and Mrs. Smith sang a duet.

Roll Recipe
1 yeast cake
Yc. melted butter
1 tbsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1 c. scalded milk
3 well-beaten eggs
1/2 c. sugar
3-1/2 to 4 c. sifted flour

Crumble yeast cake, add 1 tbsp. sugar and stir until liquid.
(I use dry yeast and put it and the sugar in about 1/2 c. water.)
Add milk which has been cooled to lukewarm.
Add sugar, butter, salt, and eggs to yeast mixture and mix thoroughly.
Add flour enough to make a soft dough, but a dough stiff enough to knead. Beware of getting the dough too stiff, however.

ONLY A SMILE By Josephine Gardner Moench
A kindly smile and a pleasant word May break through barriers wide, And lead us back to common ground, When differences abide.
We might exchange some recipes, Or favorite flower seed, Or give a timely word of praise, Of which we're all in need.
And lo! The barrier may vanish quite, Like mist before the sun, And bitterness and quarrels end Before they're scarce begun.

To Stake Presidents ABOUT the time that this Magazine reaches you, you should receive a package containing enough "Instructions to Magazine Agents," receipt books and order blanks for each Ward in your Stake. Will you kindly send these to the Ward Magazine Agents at once, urging them to secure as many subscriptions as possible now? Call their attention to the fact that the lessons for next season will begin in the July issue, and that all those whose subscriptions expire before that date should renew at once in order that they may have their lessons ready for next fall.

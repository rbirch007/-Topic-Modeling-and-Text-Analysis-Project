BAUMGARTNER, New York City Commissioner of Health, points out from statistics that the mothers who get the best quality of medical care during and after pregnancy, are the ones who produce the healthiest infants. Perinatal deaths (those occurring within thirty days after birth) take the lives of 165,000 infants a year. This number of deaths is exceeded only by the mortality from heart disease, cancer, and cerebral hemorrhage, or stroke. Page 507 CQ

NOTES TO THE FIELD 405 Relief Society Beautification Assignment HE Church Beautification committee some time ago recommended that every ward have a Beautification committee to be composed of a representative of each of the auxiliary groups and Priesthood quotums. 'These committees were requested to make a careful study to determine the needs for improving and beautifying Church buildings and grounds and to definitely plan how the desired results might be accomplished. The Church committee now suggests a division of responsibility among auxiliary groups and Priesthood quorums in seeing that every phase of cleanliness and beautification receive careful attention.

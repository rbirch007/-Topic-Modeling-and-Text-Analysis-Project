Brave leader hearts! The soul of A land made sweet Through bitter tears and blood, From thy dear eyes and feet.
Heart of a people once forlorn, Fleeting years but bring thee near, And hearts grow brave, remembering Thy task; nor weep nor fear.
O'er burning sands of limitless Expanse, the way you led; And now I walk the paths Made easier by your tread.
Bravely you worked and fought With fruitless, barren, soil; And now in joy, I reap The harvest of your toil.
Dear pioneer, thy life Has hallowed this fair land; Where blooms the velvet rose, All once was desert sand.
All once was bleak and desolate, Forsaken was the land Transformed into an Eden, By the magic of thy hand.

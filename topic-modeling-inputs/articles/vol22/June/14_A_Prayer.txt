A Prayer My Father God, I come to Thee; I need no priest to pray Nor add his blessing to thine own; Lead thou me in the way. No intercessor can present My case the way I can; Nor can he save me from myself; For he is but a man. Just hold my hand and hold my tongue, And guide my feet aright; Then even through the darkest storms, I shall behold the Light. —Terrance Sylvester Glennamaddy.

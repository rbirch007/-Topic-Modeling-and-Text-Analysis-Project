Have you opened the door on Christmas morning to a Madonna and child whose Christmas gift was WE, TOO, FOLLOW THE STAR the holy sight of the three, and in the arms of the might-be Joseph a yule log to brighten your fire? Such glimmerings of faith are life's divine manifestations of man's heritage of light. Still some wise men must be compelled to look deep into the wells of need or sorrow or suffering in order to catch the reflection of the star of Faith. Yet that star is present in the daytime when life is joyous and at night time when life is deep. And the Journey goes on until we humbly approach the stable and kneel in reverence to the Christ, knowing that shepherd and wise man symbolize body and mind and both must bow before the spirit

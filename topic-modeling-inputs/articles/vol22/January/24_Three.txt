Three New Stakes E are glad to learn of the organization of two more Stakes in California, Gridley and Sacramento. Ruth B. Sampson is President of the Gridley Stake Relief Society and Olive Lindblad Stake Relief Society President of Sacramento. We rejoice also that the New York Stake, making the 110th Stake in the Church, was organized Sunday, December 11th. We have not yet received the name of their Relief Society President. We wish these Stakes every success and much joy in their work.

A PRAYER Celia Van Cott Lord, we give thanks? The sword is sheathed; the bullets cease, Across the earth we hope for peace; White doves are loosed to fly Unmolested through the sky. Bring comfort, Lord, to those who yearn For loved ones who will not return, To crippled lads and those in pain? Teach them to live and laugh again. Cleanse hearts of hate, O Lord, with your giving, Touch eyes gone blind; teach us Christ's way of living.

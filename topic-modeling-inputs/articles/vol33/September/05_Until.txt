UNTIL Clarence Edwin Flynn None knows how lovely is the door That in the evening swings. Until it welcomes him no more From daily journeyings. None knows how lovely is a gate That opens easily, Till unresponsive hinges wait In silence, drearily. None knows how lovely are the hands That touch him in the gloam. The voice of love that understands, Till he must stand alone. Give thanks, and make each hour that goes, A golden memory; So steadily the river flows On to the silent sea.

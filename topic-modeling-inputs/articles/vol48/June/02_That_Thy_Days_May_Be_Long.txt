On_ these special occasions we recall the constant concern of our parents for our welfare and acknowledge, with gratitude, their loving care and devotion. How we appraise these special days of tribute depends upon our position in the family cycle. If we ourselves are parents, our minds not only return appreciatively to our individual parents, but are led to evaluate our own worthiness.

I remember Grandma rocking — rocking in rhythm with the ticking of the oldfashioned wall clock. If one listened carefully, a faint melody which sounded much like a lullaby could be heard. Grandmother loved babies and flowers and was usually nursing both. Her gardens were well-kept, as were her little ones. I remember Granny best when we visited her. After a bountiful supper and home entertainment around the organ, we were scnt upstairs to feather beds, where we immediately sank into dreamland. Grandma loved beauty and goodness and kindness. She loved life! Grandma had so little, and she had so much. She is remembered by many, and among them, by me!

Conversion Evelyn Darlington They said that I might know as he, When reading in the Holy Book, He found the scripture in St. James — If you lack wisdom, ask of God In faith and prayer. They said that I might know as he, When on that still spring morn, He sought the quiet grove to pray. If there is aught that you would know, Ask then of God in prayer. They said that I might know as he, When he beheld the light descend, And spoke to God and to his Son — If you lack wisdom, ask of God In faith and prayer. They said that I might know as he, So in the quiet of my room I knelt beside my bed to pray. I felt another Presence there. I felt his Spirit touch my soul.

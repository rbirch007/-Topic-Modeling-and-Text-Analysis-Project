Something Different For Your Home Arrangements
by Florence G. Williams (O)

The covers for these delightful pillows were crocheted by Kate Schleich. Sister Williams thought up the idea of applying the brightly colorful figures of yarn. These made delightful Christmas gifts for her grandchildren.

A lovely arrangement of petunias in a Sevres (a rare French) bowl graced the piano of the Williams home at Christmastime. The gold angel and scented candle symbolized the season. Such arrangements can be made easily by purchasing a certain product from a floral shop, putting it in the bottom of the vase or bowl, soaking it with water, and arranging the flowers to suit one's taste.

A delightful arrangement of wheat and nuts graced the door of the lovely Gilbert Williams home in Salt Lake City, Utah, at Thanksgiving time. The squirrels are handmade of wood and were a gift to Sister Florence Williams. She gathered the wheat and purchased the nuts.

Their apprehension increased when the clock moved from three and a half hours to four from the time Mrs. Miles had gone into the operating room. A grim-faced doctor approached, and gently touched a middle-aged man on the shoulder. "I'm sorry, Mr. Warren, we did all we could." The silent room was filled with the man's sobs. Jennifer's heart went out to him. How hard it is to lose a loved one, but what a comfort the gospel could be at such a time.

She had fidgeted and paced the floor for more than five hours when the door down the hall opened again. She recognized Dr. Mayhew's tall frame coming toward them. She tried vainly to read his expression.

46. Broken noodles (1 lb). 
8 oz grated American cheese. 
1 can (10 oz.) cream of mushroom soup. 
1 tsp. dehydrated onion. 
2 oz. evaporated milk or 2 tbsp. chopped onion. 
1 can (10 oz.) corned beef. 
2 cups crushed potato chips. 

Cook noodles in 7 quarts unsalted boiling water until tender (about 10 to 15 minutes). Drain. Mix cooked noodles, cream of mushroom soup, cheese pieces (bite size), dehydrated onion, and milk. Pour into greased 1-quart casserole. Top with broken potato chips. Bake in 425° oven for 19 minutes or until bubbly hot. Serves 6.

Variation: Use 1 can (7 oz.) tuna fish (well drained), salt to taste (if bouillon cube is used, do not add salt).

Sweet and Sour Chicken 
1 large cooked and boned chicken. 
2 cups pineapple juice. 
4 tbsp. cornstarch. 
2 tbsp. soy sauce. 
1 tsp. salt. 
1/4 tsp. green pepper strips. 
1/2 cup brown sugar. 
2 cans (20 oz.) pineapple chunks. 
1 tsp. vinegar. 
1 tsp. minced and chopped onion. 

Combine cornstarch, salt, brown sugar, vinegar, and pineapple juice. Cook until thickened. Add chicken, green pepper, and pineapple chunks. Serve hot over rice.

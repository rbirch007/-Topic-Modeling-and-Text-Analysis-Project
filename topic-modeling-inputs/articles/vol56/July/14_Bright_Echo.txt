watched a gull fly low
Across the sky,
Just where the sunset,
Mirrored in the lake,
Caused all its beauty
To intensify.
Alone, where rosy ripples
Raced to break,
Its bright wings echoed
In the lake's reply,
Flew its reflected self,
In splendor's wake,
And seemed the mystic
Flight to glorify.

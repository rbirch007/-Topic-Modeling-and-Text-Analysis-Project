LATE BORN

Lael W. Hill

To Abraham,
    Isaac;
to Zacharias,
    John—
each having many years
before he held a son;
strange are the answers
given some to know
after long desiring
and the dream let go.

(Young men, bold men,
easy in your minds,
can you equal this joy
a later father finds?)

—After unbelieving,
the blessed promise done:
to Abraham,
    Isaac—
to Zacharias,
    John.

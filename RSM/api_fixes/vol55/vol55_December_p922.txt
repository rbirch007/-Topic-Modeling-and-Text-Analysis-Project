• Plan a casserole as the main dish when you entertain your friends during the holiday season. Begin the dinner with a fruit cup—perhaps a mixture of orange, grapefruit, and pineapple segments, interspersed with maraschino cherries for sparkling color; prepare a tossed green salad (with radishes); and, for dessert, thin slices of fruit cake, or frosted homemade eclairs.

Recipes for three favorite casseroles are given here. Perhaps you will want to try them all during the holidays. Pieces of turkey could be substituted for chicken in two of the casseroles.

Chicken-Broccoli Casserole

1 to 2 lbs. broccoli (parboil)           Make white sauce by mixing:
Brown 4 to 6 chicken breasts in butter.   ¼ cup butter (2 ounces)
                                          2 tbsp. flour

Add milk to make a thick paste or use one can of cream of chicken soup (10 ounces) in place of the milk. Pour over chicken. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake slowly about 1 hour in 300° oven, cover dish with aluminum foil.

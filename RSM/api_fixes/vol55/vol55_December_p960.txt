HAPPINESS

Happiness is a four-year-old
Dancing in the wind—
Flying hair and hands outstretched,
Skirts billowing behind.

Happiness is a four-year-old
Exhibiting her charms,
Seized by shyness suddenly,
Held fast in Mother's arms.

                —Zara Sabin

Autumn Wind

Rowena Jensen Bills

Wind has left the garden bare
    piling leaves high upon the lawn,
Flushed the drainways free of dust,
    bade chirping birds "Be gone!"
Closed and shuttered windows,
    left the doorways stark,
Rushing children into schoolrooms
    from the gold-toned park.

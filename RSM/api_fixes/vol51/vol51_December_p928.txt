A candy house for Christmas

A "snow-covered" house, decorated with candy and placed in a setting of trees and hills and snowdrifts, will delight the children at Christmastime. It would be appropriate for placing on a wide mantel or a low table, or might be used as a centerpiece for the dining table. A child would feel a spirit of joyful appreciation if he could have a Christmas house for his own room.

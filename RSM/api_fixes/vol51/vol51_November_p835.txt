You may attach your coin purse to the upper part of your bag lining, or to the Tydee, with a bit of ribbon, so it will be easier to find, and less easy to lose. Your handkerchief and the few other objects left out of the Tydee will not be a problem. If you have more than one purse the size of the Tydee or larger, you can change the Tydee from purse to purse with ease.

If you have a purse too shallow

for your pen and pencil to stand upright, you can sew strips near the top of your Tydee in a horizontal position, and leave them open at the end. The pen will slide easily into such a pocket, and will not get lost in the bottom of your purse.

When you get yourself supplied, remember your friends and relatives who would thank you for such a thoughtful, if inexpensive, gift.

PINK PARFAIT ROSE

"Pink Parfait," one of the 1961 winners, is particularly showy for mass planting. It bears abundantly; has pink petals, frosted with white at tips and base, and is beautiful in bud and as a full-bloom rose, with its gold heart and its curled, white-frosted petals.

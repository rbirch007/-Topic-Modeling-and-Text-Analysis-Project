Main Lounge, Relief Society Building                                J. M. HESLOP

The names of the great leaders of Relief Society are indelibly imprinted upon the progress of women throughout the world. Woman's advancement has come since the day in 1842 when the Prophet Joseph Smith turned the key for women in this last dispensation. The restoration of the gospel of Jesus Christ has restored to woman on earth her true place in the eternal plan. Relief Society is an ensign to women in the world. If there is anything "virtuous, lovely, or of good report or praiseworthy," the members of Relief Society seek after those things.

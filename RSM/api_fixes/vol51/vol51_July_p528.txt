RELIEF SOCIETY ACTIVITIES

[PHOTOGRAPH]

Minidoka Stake (Idaho) Relief Society Presidents Honored at
Visiting Teachers Convention, November 1, 1963

Left to right: May Jones, first president, Minidoka Stake Relief Society (1924-1942); Katherine Barnes (1944-1954); Doris May (1942-1944); Bertha Burch (1954- ).

Sister Burch reports: "We feel that it is quite unusual that in the nearly forty years our stake has been organized, there have been only four presidents. The three former presidents are all living in our stake, and each holds office in her respective ward. At our visiting teachers convention, we paid special honor to our former presidents."

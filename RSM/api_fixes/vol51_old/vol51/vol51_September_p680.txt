Distribution of 262,000 Relief Society Members

The Relief Society accomplishes the work given to the women of the Church. New Relief Society units are organized throughout the world as the Church enlarges its borders. Once a year a Relief Society general conference is held in Salt Lake City, Utah, with 10,000 Relief Society leaders in attendance from over the world. Once a year Relief Society General Board members journey to all of the stakes which are now located in Australia, New Zealand, Mexico, Canada, England, Scotland, Samoa, West Germany, Berlin, Switzerland, and in the states of the United States to hold day-long meetings with the Relief Society leaders. Guidance is given in the two-fold program of Relief Society — welfare and education.

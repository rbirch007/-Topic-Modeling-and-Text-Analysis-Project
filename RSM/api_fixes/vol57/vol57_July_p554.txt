THE ANTIQUITY OF THE GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST

The idea that the gospel of Jesus Christ was known from the days of Adam to the days of Christ is not a part of the common "Christian" creed. New Testament writers, however, indicate that baptism and salvation through Christ were known to Old Testament prophets. (Gal. 3:8, 16, 17, 19, 24-26; (1 Cor. 10:2.) These truths were known to the patriarchs and to Moses. (Moses 5:9-12, 58-59; 6:58-66.)

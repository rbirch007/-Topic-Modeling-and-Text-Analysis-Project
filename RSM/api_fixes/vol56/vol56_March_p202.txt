Designs and Articles Made
by Winifred D. Anderson

■ Winifred D. Anderson, decoupage expert, says decoupage, now in vogue, is the revival of an art-craft which originated in Venice during the seventeenth century: de (decorate), cou (cut), page (page). In the eighteenth century the ladies of the French court were required to decorate the ornate palace furniture in this way. The poor, unable to afford the paintings of the masters in their homes, simulated these paintings with small pieces of pictures

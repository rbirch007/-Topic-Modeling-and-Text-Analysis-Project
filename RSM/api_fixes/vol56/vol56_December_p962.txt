Swedish Lucia Queen and Attendants

□ Each year in the Swedish Branch of the Church in Salt Lake City, Utah, the Lucia festival is held. In Sweden this is known as Little Christmas, and is held on the 13th of December, the longest night of the year in Scandinavia. Lucia means Festival of Light, and is symbolic of the triumph over darkness. The Lucia Queen is the Queen of Light, and spreads cheer and light throughout the villages, with the help of her attendants.

The candles are of course symbolic, and this crown is imported from Sweden. Part of the festival includes a parade through the building, and lovely music. Following the celebration, there are refreshments and a visit from Saint Nicholas.

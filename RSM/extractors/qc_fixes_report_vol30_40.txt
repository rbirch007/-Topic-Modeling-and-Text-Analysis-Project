ADDITIONAL OBSERVATIONS
======================================================================

1. The Art 1 mid-sentence issue is systemic across Vols 34-40, affecting
   nearly every month. The extraction process appears to have consistently
   split the first article's content between the Misc file and the Art 1 file.
   A batch fix would require careful boundary detection in each Misc file.

2. Many article files throughout Vols 30-40 contain trailing advertisement
   text from the original magazine pages (Deseret News Press, LDS Business
   College, Beneficial Life Insurance, ZCMI, etc.). These were not reclassified
   because the PRIMARY content is a real article/poem. The trailing ads are
   an artifact of the OCR/extraction picking up the full magazine page.

3. Several "Article not found" extraction errors exist throughout the volumes.
   23 were caught among the last 3 files per month; more may exist in
   earlier sequence positions.

4. Three poem-renamed files (Nov Vol36 #31, Oct Vol36 #27, Apr Vol37 #28)
   are essentially ad/garbage files that were given incorrect "Category: Poem"
   headers by the API extraction. These could be further reclassified as Misc
   if desired.

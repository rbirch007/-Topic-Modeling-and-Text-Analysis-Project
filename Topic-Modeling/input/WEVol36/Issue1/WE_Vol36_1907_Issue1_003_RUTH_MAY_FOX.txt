[WomansExponent | Vol36 | 1907 | June | Issue: 1 | Title: RUTH MAY FOX. | Author: Unknown]

						RUTH MAY FOX.


The Norwegian women have petitioned
Parliament for the full ballot. The peti-
tion refers to “the men of Finland, who
showed magnanimity and understanding
of modern demands” in enfranchising their
women, and urged the Parliament of
Norway to take like action.

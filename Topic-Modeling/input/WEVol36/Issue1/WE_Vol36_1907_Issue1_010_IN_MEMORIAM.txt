IN MEMORIAM.
	On the 22nd day of March, 1907, our dear
sister and friend, Emma, the beloved wife of John
B. Pratt of Hinckley, departed this life. She left
a family of a husband and six children, the eldest
a girl of about thirteen years.
	Sister Emma’s death cast a gloom over the
whole community, as everyone that knew her
loved her sweet and sunny disposition, She was
on hand at every call, and true and faithful to
every trust. 
	She was born at Lehi, August 31st 1876, Died
at Provo hospital, March 22nd, 1907. Although
we miss her at our gatherings, yet we do not
mourn as those without hope, for we feel that when
the Lord makes up his jewels we shall meet our
dear sister again. May God grant it.
				CATHARINE A. ALLRED,
					Sec. Relief Society.

LECTURE 11—HONESTY.
	(a) If thou shalt find that which thy
neighbor has lost, thou shalt make diligent
search till thou shalt deliver it to him
again. Doc. and Cov. When children
pick up articles of value do we encourage
them to try to find the lawful owner?

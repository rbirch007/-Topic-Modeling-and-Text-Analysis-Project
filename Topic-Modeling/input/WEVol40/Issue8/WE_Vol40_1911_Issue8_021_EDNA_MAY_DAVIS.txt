	EDNA MAY DAVIS.


WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


A friend whom you have been gaining
during your whole life you ought not to be
displeased with in a moment. A stone is
many years becoming a ruby; take care that
you do not destroy it in an instant against
another stone.—Saadi.


WOMAN’S EXPONENT.

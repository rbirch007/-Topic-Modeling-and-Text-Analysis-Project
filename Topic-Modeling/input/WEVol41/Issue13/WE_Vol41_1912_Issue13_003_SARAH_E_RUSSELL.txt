[WomansExponent | Vol41 | 1912 | January | Issue: 13 | Title: Sarah E. Russell | Author: Unknown]

			Sarah E. Russell (Hope).


Take joy home, and make a place in thy
great heart for her, and give her time to 
grow, and cherish her. Then will she come, 
and oft will sing to thee when thou art work-
ing in the furrows; aye, or weeding in the
sacred hour of dawn. 
	It is a comely fashion to be glad; joy is
the grace we say to God.—Jean Ingelow.

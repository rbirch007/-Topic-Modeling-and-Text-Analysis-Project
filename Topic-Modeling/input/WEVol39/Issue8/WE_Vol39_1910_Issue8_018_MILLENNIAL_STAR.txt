				MILLENNIAL STAR.


WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


Women from all parts of the State of Il-
linois attended the hearing on Woman Suf-
frage before the Senate, on February 9, held
in the Senate Chamber. At the close the
committee unanimously recommended the
bill for passage.

[Vol8 | February 1921 | Sequence: 08 | Category: Article | Title: Baby Brother Abbie R. Madsen]

BABY BROTHER: Fleet as a deer Was my baby brother, All over the place Worrying mother. Laughing or crying, Mischievous still, Tumbling ever, Like Jack and Jill. Oh! But we love you, Noisy elf, We'll follow and guard you, In spite of yourself.

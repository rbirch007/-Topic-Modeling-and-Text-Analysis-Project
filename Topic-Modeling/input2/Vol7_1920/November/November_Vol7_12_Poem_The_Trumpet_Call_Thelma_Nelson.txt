[Vol7 | November 1920 | Sequence: 12 | Category: Article | Title: The Trumpet Call Thelma Nelson]

THE TRUMPET CALL. Thelma Nelson. Awaken! awaken! ye Saints that slumber, The Lord of host is drawing nigh;  Accompanied by angels that are numbered, Descending from the heavens high. Let not your souls be found a-sleeping. Your eyes be dimmed by the clouds of sin, But let it be found your light you were keeping, That you may go forth and welcome him.

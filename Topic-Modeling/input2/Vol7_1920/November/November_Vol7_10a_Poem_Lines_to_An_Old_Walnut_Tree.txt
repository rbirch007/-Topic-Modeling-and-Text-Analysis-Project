[Vol7 | November 1920 | Sequence: 10 | Category: Poem | Title: Lines to An Old Walnut Tree]

LINES  TO  AN  OLD  WALNUT  TREE.
Mrs.  Donna  M.  Meacham.
Majestic  spreading  walnut  tree,
Whose  grateful  shade   I  have   enjoyed.
Delicious  fruit  you've  given  me
I  trust  you  may  not  be  destroyed,
That  strangers  will  be  kind  to  thee,
For  I  am  bidding  thee  farewell,
Thou  dear,  old  stately  walnut  tree,
Henceforth  apart  from  you  to  dwell.

A FRAGMENT. By Sarah E. Hawley Pearson. In tears and yet in trust we stumble on; One step ahead to know is all we ask; Light for the one day's duties to be done, Strength for the one day's superhuman task. Rest for the weary at its stormy close; Hope for the morrow; peace beyond the strife; With eyes to see beneath the snow the rose; God and each other at the end of life. MRS. ELIZABETH ANN WHITNEY MISS ELIZA R. SNOW MRS. EMMELINE B. WELLS, STANDING

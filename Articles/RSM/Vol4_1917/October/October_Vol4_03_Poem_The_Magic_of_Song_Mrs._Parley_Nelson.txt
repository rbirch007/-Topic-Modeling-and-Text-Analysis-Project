[Vol4 | October 1917 | Sequence: 03 | Category: Article | Title: The Magic of Song Mrs. Parley Nelson]

THE MAGIC OF SONG. Mrs. Parley Nelson. When the heart is sad and lonely, And the day seems long. Did you ever try the magic Of a lilting song?1 Eyes begin to sparkle And lips smile e'er long For they can't resist the magic Of a lilting song. When life seems to have no bright side, And you fret and sigh. And you only see the shadows As the clouds sail by, Sing a joyful stanza. Sing it clear and strong, For the heart forgets its burden In a lilting song. Monti, Utah. DELWYN THOMAS.

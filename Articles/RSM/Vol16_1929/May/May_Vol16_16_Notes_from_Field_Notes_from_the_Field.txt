NOTES FROM THE FIELD

The Relief Society of the Ogden Stake held a very successful conference on March 16th. The theme was "The Ideal Relief Society Sister." The speakers were Mrs. Amy Brown Lyman, general president; Mrs. Louise Y. Robison, general secretary; Mrs. Julia A. F. Child, general treasurer; and Mrs. Kate Montgomery Barker, member of the general board. The attendance was excellent and the spirit of the meeting was inspiring.

The Relief Society of the Weber Stake held their conference on March 23rd. The theme was "Relief Society Ideals." The speakers were Mrs. Amy Brown Lyman, general president; Mrs. Louise Y. Robison, general secretary; Mrs. Julia A. F. Child, general treasurer; and Mrs. Clarissa S. Williams, former general president. The attendance was large and the interest keen.

The Relief Society of the Davis Stake held their conference on March 30th. The theme was "The Relief Society Sister and Her Responsibilities." The speakers were Mrs. Amy Brown Lyman, general president; Mrs. Louise Y. Robison, general secretary; Mrs. Julia A. F. Child, general treasurer; and Mrs. Kate Montgomery Barker, member of the general board. The meeting was well attended and very successful.

The Relief Society of the Salt Lake Stake held their conference on April 6th. The theme was "Relief Society Objectives." The speakers were Mrs. Amy Brown Lyman, general president; Mrs. Louise Y. Robison, general secretary; Mrs. Julia A. F. Child, general treasurer; and Mrs. Clarissa S. Williams, former general president. The attendance was excellent and the spirit inspiring.

The Relief Society of the Granite Stake held their conference on April 13th. The theme was "The Relief Society Sister as a Citizen." The speakers were Mrs. Amy Brown Lyman, general president; Mrs. Louise Y. Robison, general secretary; Mrs. Julia A. F. Child, general treasurer; and Mrs. Kate Montgomery Barker, member of the general board. The meeting was well attended and very successful.

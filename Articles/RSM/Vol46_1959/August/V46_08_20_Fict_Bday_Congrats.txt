5. Socialized
behavior.
The ma-
ture
person
is
familiar

the

dition

He

In general, he chooses his course

maximum
long-term

for himself and for the entire group
affected.
His
social behavior
is de-
termined,
in
part, on

principles, rather than on
selfish
in-
terests

He

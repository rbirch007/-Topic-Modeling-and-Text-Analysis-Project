[Vol46 | January 1959 | Sequence: 13 | Category: Article | Title: Four | Author: Magazine]
In December, a reproduction of the lovely painting "The Rest on the Flight Into Egypt," by Gerard David, was used as the cover for the Magazine. Beginning with this issue, January 1959, the missions of Continental United States will be represented by cover pictures in four colors. Following the plan commenced in January 1956, featuring the foreign missions, the Spanish-speaking missions, and the Southwest Indian Mission, each of the other missions of Continental United States will be represented by cover pictures in four colors. A brief history of each mission, with illustrations, will be presented, and recipes from each mission will be featured.

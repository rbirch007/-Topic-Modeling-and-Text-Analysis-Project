 | Author: [No author listed]]Notes to the Field: Relief Society Assigned Evening Meeting of Fast Sunday in March

The Relief Society General Board has assigned the evening meeting of fast Sunday in March to the Relief Society. This meeting should be held in each ward and branch of the Church where Relief Society is organized.

The purpose of this meeting is to give the Relief Society sisters an opportunity to bear their testimonies and to strengthen one another in the gospel. It is suggested that the meeting be conducted by the Relief Society presidency, with the bishop's approval.

The meeting should follow the general pattern of a testimony meeting, with appropriate opening and closing exercises. Sisters should be encouraged to bear brief testimonies of their faith in the gospel and to express gratitude for blessings received.

This assignment provides an excellent opportunity for Relief Society sisters to participate more fully in the spiritual life of the ward and to contribute to the testimony meeting in a meaningful way.

Bishops and Relief Society presidencies should work together in planning for this meeting to ensure its success and to encourage good attendance.

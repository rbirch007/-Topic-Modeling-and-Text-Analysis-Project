MRS. ADDISON PRATT, A WOMAN MISSIONARY ON HER WAY TO
THE SOCIETY ISLANDS:
We have music
in the camp
[on

plains],
two
violins and
a
flute.
Besides
there
are many
singing
birds
in
this
country which

The evening was spent in

How

What wandering pilgrims we have been!

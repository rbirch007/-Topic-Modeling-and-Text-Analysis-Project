What Do You Know?
======================================================================

WHAT DO YOU KNOW? How are you preparing for the coming season? What plans are you making about fixing up the premises outside and the house inside for hot weather? How are you going to safeguard the health of your children? Have you given any thought to the school and playground? Are the women of the neighborhood doing anything to get it in good shape? Are you letting the children share in the fruits of their labors in the home and garden? What are you going to do toward conserving food? Have you given one thought to the summer recreation? The

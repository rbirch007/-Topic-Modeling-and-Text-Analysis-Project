To the Departed Year 1918
=========================

To the Departed Year 1918. By Mrs. Parley Nelson. Your birth was heralded 'mid clouds of gloom, So dark, we scarce dared think of happiness; Famine and Death stalked broadcast o'er the earth, While millions mourned in sorrow and distress. Oppression's cruel hand was at our throat, The tyrant's power threatened land and sea, But Right prevailed against the power of Might! O, glorious year, you brought the Victory.

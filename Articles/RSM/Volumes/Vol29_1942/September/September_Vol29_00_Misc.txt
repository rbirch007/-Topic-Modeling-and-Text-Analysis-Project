What the Pioneers Can Teach Us About War
With glorious courage and strength the Pioneers fought daily against drought, grasshoppers, famine?whatever enemy appeared. But with glowing hope they kept the spiritual eye fixed upon a far, high goal. And this vision made them valiant for each day's battle.
Young people who accept the guidance of the inspired Pioneers will help America win the war. But they will look ahead to the building of a noble and higher world.
The strongest war weapon today is Intelligence edged with technical knowledge. Courage, vision. America asks the colleges for more young people thus equipped. And fortunately, this same weapon will best serve its possessors in the struggles of the peace to come.
Brigham Young University provides technical training in aviation, mechanics, home economics; in bacteriology, chemistry, physics, zoology, pre-medics; in many other subjects essential to a successful war effort.

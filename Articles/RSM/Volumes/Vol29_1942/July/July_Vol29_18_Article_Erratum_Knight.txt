[Vol29 | July 1942 | Sequence: 18 | Category: Article | Title: Erratum Knight]

ERRATUM Martha McBride Knight HE article, "The Eighteen Charter Members of Relief Society," by Marjorie M. Woolf, published in the May issue of the Magazine, page 306, states: "Her (Martha Knight) descendants, the Knights of Provo, are glad to remember Martha as their kin.'" Mary Belnap Robbins, of Smithfield, Utah, in a letter written May 30, 1942, states that, '"'Martha McBride Knight is not related to the Knights of Provo."

[Vol29 | March 1942 | Sequence: 07 | Category: Poem | Title: Mary-Martha Am I]

Mary-Martha Am I

Mary-Martha am I—
Sometimes I sit at the Master's feet
And drink of the living water sweet,
While all around me the world goes by—
Mary-Martha am I.

Mary-Martha am I—
Sometimes I serve with willing hands,
Fulfilling the Master's dear commands,
While precious moments go swiftly by—
Mary-Martha am I.

Mary-Martha am I—
Blessed am I in this dual role,
For service sweetens the listening soul,
And communion strengthens the hands that try—
Mary-Martha am I.

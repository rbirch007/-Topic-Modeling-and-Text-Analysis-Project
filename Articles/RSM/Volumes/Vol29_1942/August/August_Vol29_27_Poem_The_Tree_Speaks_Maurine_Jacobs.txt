cJhe  oJree  Speaks 
Ma  urine  Jacobs 
Skyhioom,  you  call  me, 
Watching  my  tall  hianches 
Rake  the  blue  and  summon  clouds 
To  bring  you  sustenance  and  shade. 
My  wots  grope  deep  to  bind  the  soil; 
My  veins  distend  to  gather  rain, 
Elixir  oi  Hie  ior  you  and  yours; 
My  branches  spread  to  give  surcease 
And  bring  you  hope  through  beauty. 
In  my  wise  passing. 
Houses  take  their  shape; 
Books,  their  form; 
Stoves,  their  fuel. 
In  my  needless  death. 
Floods  sweep  the  hnd; 
Desolation  reigns; 
And  man  is  cursed. 
I  am  both  beauty  and  necessity. 
I  am  earth-healer,  water-saver. 
I  am  earth's  skybroom. 
I  am  content.

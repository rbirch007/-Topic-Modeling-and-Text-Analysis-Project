Literature (Third Week in May)
JOSEPH AND HIS BRETHREN By Louis N. Parker
"Joseph and His Brethren," by Louis N. Parker, was produced both in New York and in London in 1913. This play is not a problem play. It is a pageant play, and as presented in His Majesty's Theatre in London under the able management of Sir Herbert Tree gave to us a pageant of Bible history that has seldom been equalled.

Easter Thoughts Elsie E. Barrett Glad Easter Morn'. Break forth in song! Sing praises to your King! The clouds of doubt have rolled away, And death has lost its sting. How soul-inspiring just to think That Jesus died for me; So wonderful to have the thought That He'll provide for me; That He has risen from the tomb For such as I—robbed death of gloom. O Jesus, help my faith to grow, Help me to feel my debt; Though I can never, never pay, Help me, lest I forget.

Beloved By Nona H. Brown I thank my Maker for you, sweetheart mine. Your love has made a Paradise, earth's way. I thank Him that you found me here, and claimed Me for your own, one blessed April day. You've stood by me through joys and fear alike, Bringing the sunshine to my darkest days; You've changed despair into rich happiness! Your love has lessened keenest pain always. You walked with me and tightly held my hand, Into the torturing shadow-land of birth, And smiled first at the wee one we brought back? The wond'rous gift God sent us here on earth! Oh, may God grant us years to realize The happiness on earth together here. May life's experience but teach us two To hold each other ever yet more dear.

An Interesting Fact
"None would go so far, perhaps, as to rate the standing of a family by the brand of Cocoa it uses, but it is an interesting fact that the modern well-managed home is likely to be particular about its Cocoa. If the pantry reveals a jar of Scowcroft's Blue Pine Cocoa, you can be sure. That family has adopted a Cocoa that is full of flavor and a standard for comparison."

[Vol32 | August 1945 | Sequence: 03 | Category: Article | Title: Worship Meservy]
WORSHIP
Oiiver K. Meservy
I love to stroll through nature's scenes When no one else is nigh.
Where I in peace may worship God And on his arm rely.
I love the rivers and the hills
And all wild scenery. In every place that I may roam

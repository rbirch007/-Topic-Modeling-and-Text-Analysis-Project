Suggestive Program For Sunday Evening, March 4, 1945
IN accordance with the plan announced by the First Presidency in 1942, designating the evening meeting of Fast Sundays for use by the auxiliaries, the Relief Society was assigned the Fast Sunday evening meetings of March and November. The November meeting constitutes the annual ward Relief Society conference, but the March meeting is open for any type of program dating to Relief Society, coming as it does on the first Sunday of the month in which the Relief Society was organized.

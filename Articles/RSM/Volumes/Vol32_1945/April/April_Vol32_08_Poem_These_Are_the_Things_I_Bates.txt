[Vol32 | April 1945 | Sequence: 08 | Category: Article | Title: These Are the Things I Bates]

THESE ARE THE THINGS I HOLD OF DEAREST WORTH Roberta Bates These are the things I hold of dearest worth; An evening sky of gold, And rose, and blue, The peace of wooded hills, Bird song, the murmuring of rills, Green lace upon each April twig, And, after showers, The scent of good black earth Mingling with flowers, And, best of all, as each day ends? Laughter and friends.

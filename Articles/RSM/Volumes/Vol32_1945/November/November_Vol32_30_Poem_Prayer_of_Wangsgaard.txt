[Vol32 | November 1945 | Sequence: 30 | Category: Article | Title: Prayer of Wangsgaard]

PRAYER OF THANKS Eva Willes Wangsgaard I?thanked thee other years, dear Lord, For flowers and fruit, For beauty that the years afford From seed and root.
This year I thank thee for the peace That faith can hold, For love that grew a sheltering fleece Against all cold, And for the wound my heart received. J I murmured when It came, but learned that while I grieved I grew. Amen.

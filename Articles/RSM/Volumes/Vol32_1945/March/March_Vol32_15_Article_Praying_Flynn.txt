Bluﬀ City. Editor Exponent: Thinking that a few lines from Bluﬀ City

is trying to keep along evenly with the rest of the branches of the Relief
Society as near as possible under the circumstances, with a good spirit and
feeling at heart. We hold our testimony and teacher's meetings once a
month, and our working meetings once in two weeks. Wc are few in
number, but our meetings are well attended and our desire is to do all the
good we can in helping to build up the Kingdom of God in this far oﬀ land.
.'. . Wc have had a very pleasant winter and the health of the people is very

Zion, we remain, Your sisters in the Gospel, Jane M. Walton, President;
Irene Haskell, Secretary.

[Vol32 | July 1945 | Sequence: 28 | Category: Article | Title: Alleviation Dix]

ALLEVIATION Fae Decker Dix Under a pale sky, After the sun has set, I come to this valley Appealing for solace. Broad are its wheat fields, Crystal bright the stream's flow, Wild geese cry plaintively. And fly to the still hills. Something lifts within The shackles of my heart, I, too, will seek the hills At even-tide!

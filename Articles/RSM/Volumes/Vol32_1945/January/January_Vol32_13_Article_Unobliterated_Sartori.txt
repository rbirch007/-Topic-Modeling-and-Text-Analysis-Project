UNOBLITERATED STILL
Dott J. Sartori
When the wintertime that ﬁlls
Every footprint on these hills
Shall make the mountain slope as white
As the summer moon tonight,
I shall still see columbine
And blue clematis that twine
In the thickets, and the doe
With her swollen udder go
Bounding out a lace design.
The summer canyon shall be mine
And memory retrace the trail
Unobliterated still.
Palestine
Dr. Lehnd Hargrave Creer Head of the Department of History and Political
Science, University of Utah

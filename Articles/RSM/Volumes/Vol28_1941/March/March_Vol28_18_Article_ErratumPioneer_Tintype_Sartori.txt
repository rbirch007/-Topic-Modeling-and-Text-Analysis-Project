West South Temple Street, extends a welcome to those who wish to view its fine and artistic handiwork. The General Board looks forward with pleasure to meeting Relief Society officers and members from the stakes and missions throughout the Church. Cae tum AN error was made in publishing the name of Edith E. Anderson as the author of the poem, "Pioneer Tintype," which appeared in the February issue of the Magazine. This poem is the work of Dott Sartori. "Pioneer Tintype" was one of the poems receiving favorable comment in the Eliza R. Snow Poem Contest. We greatly appreciate this excellent contribution to the Magazine and sincerely regret the error which occurred.

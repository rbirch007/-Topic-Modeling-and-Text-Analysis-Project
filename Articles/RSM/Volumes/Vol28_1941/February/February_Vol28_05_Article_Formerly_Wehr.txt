When  the  bus  stopped  at  their 
corner,  Daddy  was  there  waiting  for 
her. 
''Hello  there,  little  funnyface. 
You  have  a  baby  sister,"  he  greeted 
her  joyously. 
She  put  her  hand  in  his  and  walk- 
ed silently  beside  him  down  the  path 
toward  home. 
"Daddy,"  she  inquired  at  length. 
"is  your  new  little  girl  pretty,  and

Daddy  stopped  walking  and  look- 
ed straight  at  Hester  in  a  way  that 
made  her  cheeks  burn.  Did  she 
seem  homelier  than  ever  to  him

went  misty,  and  he  swooped  her  up

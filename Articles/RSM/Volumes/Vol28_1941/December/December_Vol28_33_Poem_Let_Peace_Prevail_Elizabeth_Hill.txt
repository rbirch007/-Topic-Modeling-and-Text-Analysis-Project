[Vol28 | December 1941 | Sequence: 33 | Category: Poem | Title: Let Peace Prevail]

O God, this Christmas Eve let peace prevail,
Let love abound for all beneath Thy sky,
Let hearts lift up hosannas to Thy name
And weary souls have strength to live, not die.

Oh, give us hope and faith to seek Thee out.
Teach us how to find Thee.
Bring us near.
Let us know the wonder of Thy love,
And may our hearts know naught of hate and fear.

O God, this night we pause to seek afresh
The path Thy Son trod.
Guide us then aright,
And may our faith, like lanterns in the dark,
Lead us on to everlasting light.

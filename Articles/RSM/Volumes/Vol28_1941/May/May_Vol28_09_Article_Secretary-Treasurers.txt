[Vol28 | May 1941 | Sequence: 09 | Category: Article | Title: Secretary-Treasurers]

It is regretted that there are no copies of the proofs available for those secretary-treasurers who were not in attendance at the conference, but the opinions of secretary-treasurers who were present from three-fourths of the stakes are considered to be adequately representative. Further suggestions and comments, both from secretary-treasurers in attendance and those who were not present, will be welcome, and should be sent to reach the General Secretary-Treasurer not later than July 1, 1941.

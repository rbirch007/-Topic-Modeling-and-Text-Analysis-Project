A  LITTLE  BOY  I  KNOW 
Saiah  Willes  Adamson 
Eyes  as  bright  as  diamonds  in  their  setting, 
Cheeks  that  nature  paints  a  cherry  red, 
Ears  attuned  to  words  bereft  of  fretting. 
Lips  like  rosebuds  in  their  sunny  beds- 
Yet  the  sweetest  words  that  e'er  were  uttered 
And  the  same  dear  story  old  but  true 
Comes  again  in  childish  pitch  and  tender, 
"Granny  dear,"  he  whispers,  "I  Love  You."

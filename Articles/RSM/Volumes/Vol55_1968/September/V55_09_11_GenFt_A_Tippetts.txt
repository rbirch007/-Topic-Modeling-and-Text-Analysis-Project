A Nursery for Loving Care Virginia Tippetts, President, Eleventh Ward Relief Society Annie Merrill Ballantyne, President, University West Stake Relief Society # Four things inspired the Eleventh Ward Relief Society of University West Stake, Salt Lake City, Utah, to redecorate the ward nursery room: 1. 2. 3. 4. A desire to encourage young mothers to come and become better acquainted with Relief Society. A wish to create a place where children would love to come, and have a happy time while there. A way in which to use wisely some of the money a dear, loving sister left to the Reltef Society in her eighty-ninth year. A way to help little children learn to love the gospel. With permission to do this, many hours were spent in planning, measuring, shopping, sanding, drawing, and painting roads on tables, covering chairs, decorating a "Doll House Cupboard," designing and painting giraffes to outline the blackboard. We used our nursery for the first time at the March 17 Relief Society Birthday Party, building our program around children, and asking the sisters to enter into the spirit of children and birthdays by bringing gifts to fill the new cupboard. Their response was wonderful.

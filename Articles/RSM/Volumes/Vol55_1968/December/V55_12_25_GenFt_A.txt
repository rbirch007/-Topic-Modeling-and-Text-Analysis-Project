Kohler, President Laramie Ward Relief Society @ A toy booth made to represent a generous clown, graced the first bazaar held in the Laramie Ward of Cheyenne Stake. The booth was designed by Jeanette F. Mikesel (pictured) and the art work was completed by Dorothy F. Cole. Many toys, easy to make but delightful to the children, were featured in the booth.

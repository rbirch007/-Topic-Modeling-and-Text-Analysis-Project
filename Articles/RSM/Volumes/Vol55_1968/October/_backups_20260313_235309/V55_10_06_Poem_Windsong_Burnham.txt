[Windsong Speaks Gladys Hesser Burnham]

[Address given at the annual picnic of the Nauvoo Historical Society at the Nauvoo State Park, July 16, 1968] # When I chose the topic that I am to use this evening, I hoped that it would make you wonder, or think a bit. You have been aware, each summer, of the work going on, the dirt being moved, the trowels and whisk brooms being meticulously wielded, but do you really understand why all this time and money and effort are being expended?

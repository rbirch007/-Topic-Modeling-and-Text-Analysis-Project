Also, when he is home, the phone rings constantly. So, I decided to try having special breakfasts occasionally, at a time of morning when the phone wouldn't ring too often, and when everyone could enjoy one another and really start the day off right. After the children were asleep, preparations were begun for the morning. The table was set with our prettiest, most colorful place mats and dishes. A centerpiece of flowers adorned the table. Each fork had a pink napkin, folded diagonally, threaded through the tines, with a menu, typed on bright pink paper, at each place. The menus were something as follows: I made a lovely breakfast cake which can be mixed the night before, poured into the pan and covered with plastic wrap and refrigerated.

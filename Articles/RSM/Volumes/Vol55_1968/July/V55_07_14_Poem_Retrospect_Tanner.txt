[Retrospect Annie Atkin Tanner]
RETROSPECT

Memory returning
was like fragrance:
pine needles in the dawn;
apple blossoms
after sudden rain;

lilacs, evening sweet.

It was like color
seen for the first time
in rainbow spectrums;
in sunset's orange,
its pink, mauve, and yellow;

like green against day's sky.

And it was like music,
melodious and kind;
filled with new peace,
with wild, young rapture;
lifted anticipation,

filled with all of knowing.

It was joy and pain,
ecstasy and sorrowing,
this silken moment
of memory returning.

—Annie Atkin Tanner

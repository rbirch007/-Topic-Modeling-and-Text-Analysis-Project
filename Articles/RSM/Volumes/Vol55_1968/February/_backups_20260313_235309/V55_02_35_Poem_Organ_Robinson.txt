ORGAN SONG Linnie Fisher Robinson Organ, sing your varied songs, Contrapuntal, note on note; Shake the air with counterpoint, Soothe me with your voice afloat; End the day with folklore lyrics Or with hymnals' solemn praise. Let the air be filled with color, Sound and beauty for my days. Oh, make harmony a stair Of melodic atmosphere; A medley of tones and rhythm Singing of man's idealism; Out beyond all memory— Arias of lands to be. T

ALWAYS BUSY, ALWAYS HAPPY Martha Annice Hepworth Hayward, American Fork, Utah, has a special philosophy—always busy, always happy. If this is true, Sister Hayward is a very happy person indeed. Her handiwork includes beautiful quilts, crocheted bedspreads, tablecloths, pillow edgings, doilies, luncheon sets, hot pads, handkerchiefs, and afghans. She is adept at making knitted, crocheted, and braided rugs, knitted clothing, needlepoint items, dolls, clothing, and handbags. She is equally capable of finishing leather goods, decorating wastebaskets, copper tooling, glass and metal etchings, and painting figurines. She has devoted many years of service to Relief Society. She was a visiting teacher message leader for nine years, and has been a visiting teacher for fiftyseven.

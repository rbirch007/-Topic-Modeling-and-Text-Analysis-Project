PRAYER Christie Lund Coles Spare me from self-pity, Lord, Let my sympathy Be for those warped of mind, Those who cannot see The world of wonder and of truth, The mountains, rising tall; Aware of God's great goodness Around, about us all. Spare me from self-pity, Lord, Let each morning dawn As a new promise and a hope Knowing darkness gone, Knowing that the hardships Made me grow and stand Taller in the bright new day, Aware of his strong hand.

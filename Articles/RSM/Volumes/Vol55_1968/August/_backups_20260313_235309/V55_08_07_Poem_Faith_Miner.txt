[Faith Caroline Eyring Miner]

—Alice Brady Myers 570 woke ¢ at 43 < Ee ai s Valley, Utah Light and shadow in Kama FAITH Faith, like a match glow In the misty morning, Flickers briefly And is gone. In mid-day its fire Glows with the sun In splendid brilliance Like a field of Stars. But in the evening, When it is the only light, Like a lone star On a black blanket It is as the sun at night The whole illumination Of my life.

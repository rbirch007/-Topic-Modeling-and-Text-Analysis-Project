[Vol55 | August 1968 | Page 570 | Poem | Faith Caroline | Eyring Miner]
[Faith Caroline Eyring Miner]
FAITH Faith, like a match glow In the misty morning, Flickers briefly And is gone. In mid-day its fire Glows with the sun In splendid brilliance Like a field of Stars. But in the evening, When it is the only light, Like a lone star On a black blanket It is as the sun at night The whole illumination Of my life.

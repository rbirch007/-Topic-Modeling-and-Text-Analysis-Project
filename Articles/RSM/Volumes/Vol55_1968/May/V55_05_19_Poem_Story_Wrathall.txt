STORY TIME Night hangs her lantern on the evening bars, Opens the book of heaven, and the stars Come twinkling in across the galaxy To. Learn the lesson of eternity: How each, in his own orbit swinging clear, And then 'impel a beam into the dark To. Find some far place on the shadowed arc So one, still waiting, might look up and say, "Oh, Star! Send me your everlasting ray!" Norma A.
Wrathall Must light his small dim corner of the sphere;

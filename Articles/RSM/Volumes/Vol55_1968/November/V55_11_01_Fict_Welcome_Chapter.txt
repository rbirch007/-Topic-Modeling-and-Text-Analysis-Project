ws Jennifer Miles leaned against the big walnut tree gracing the back yard of the large family home in Springville, Utah. It was early spring, and the mountains to the south were still a beautiful blue and white from the winter's snow. The lake to the west and the sky above were an identical blue. The eastern mountains were beginning to turn green. The air was pleasantly warm and flowers were beginning to bloom all across the valley. Jennifer loved the springtime, for it signified new birth and was a change from the final dreary months of winter; but she loved each new season as it came. She sighed audibly.

[Vol55 | April 1968 | Page 252 | Poem | My Song Della | Adams Leitner]
[My Song Della Adams Leitner]
Every Magazine placed in the homes of the Church is helping to combat, perhaps in a small way, the tremendous load of low standard publications dumped on to the public every day. May the Lord bless you in your efforts. I testify to the fact that God lives, that Jesus is the Christ, that he directs the affairs of his kingdom through his_ prophet President McKay, and we are all recipients of the blessings that result from service and dedication in his work. In the name of Jesus Christ. Amen.

[Vol7 | April 1920 | Sequence: 12 | Category: Poem | Title: Song]

song. ;  The world seems strange and dark to me. I gaze upon the woodland vales. Xo more they seem so wondrous fair;  I look up to the mountain heights. But storm clouds cast a shadow there. I try to see my duty clear. To smile, though sorrow pierce my heart;  I try to conquer doubt and fear. To hide my grief, and do my part. I try to walk the narrow way, To keep my face turned toward the light. And in my soul, I feel and know There's one who doeth all things right. Rexburg, Idaho.

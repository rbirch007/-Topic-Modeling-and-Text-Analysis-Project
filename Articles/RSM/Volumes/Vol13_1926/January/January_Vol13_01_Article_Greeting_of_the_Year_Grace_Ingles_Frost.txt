GREETING OF THE YEAR
By Grace Ingles Frost
I bring you happiness.
Will you not receive it?
Will you not tune your heart and mind to harmonize
With God's great, universal law of service,
That you the greatness of my gift may realize?
The soul unkind, the idle hand will grasp it;
To have and hold
it, man must ever be
One with the will Divine, in thought transcending
The grovelings of his own mortality.

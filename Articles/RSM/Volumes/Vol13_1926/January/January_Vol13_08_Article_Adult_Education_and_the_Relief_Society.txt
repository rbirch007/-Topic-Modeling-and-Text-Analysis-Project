Adult Education and the Relief Society
During the late sessions of the Utah Educational Association the organization was addressed by Dr. L. J. Richardson, director of the Extension Division of the University of California, whose theme was "Adult Education." After describing the very efficient manner in which Denmark educates the adults of her nation, he discussed the agencies in the United States that are contributing to the education of Adults.

He mentioned, in particular, among women's organizations, the Federated Clubs and the League of Women Voters.

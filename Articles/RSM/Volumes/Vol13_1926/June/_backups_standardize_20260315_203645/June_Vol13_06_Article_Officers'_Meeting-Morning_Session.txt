OFFICERS' MEETING, MORNING SESSION
President Clarissa S. Williams
Six months have gone since we last met. It has not seemed long and yet it has been one-half year. The members of the General Board have had the privilege of visiting you in your homes during the last year, and the reports that have come from the work which you are doing in your stakes are very gratifying. We greet you this morning with love in our hearts for what you are, what you are doing, and what you expect to do, and our prayers go out constantly to our heavenly Father that he will bless you in your labors and broaden you that you may be towers of strength in the communities where you live.

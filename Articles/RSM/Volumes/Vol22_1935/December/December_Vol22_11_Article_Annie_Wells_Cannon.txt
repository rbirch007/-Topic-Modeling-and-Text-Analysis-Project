GALILEE By Annie Wells Cannon If I could stand upon the shore of Galilee Where Jesus walked in majesty My soul would trace in imagery The glorious light upon his face; His sweet humility and grace; And I would hear his words sublime Pierce through the vaporous mists of time Then would I kneel upon the shore of Galilee And kiss the sands in ecstasy Where Jesus walked in majesty.

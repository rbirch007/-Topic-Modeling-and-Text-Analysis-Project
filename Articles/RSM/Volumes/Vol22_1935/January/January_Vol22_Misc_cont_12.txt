In the world where the wise use of leisure is becoming a serious problem, mothers need to aid their youngsters in learning how to play fairly with any class of children who are honorable. If mothers will make the most of this opportunity to encourage their children in the wise selection and in the careful preservation of friendships, they will find that the next generation will be much nearer the ideal of world peace toward which all mothers are looking forward eagerly.

When April Comes
Ij^ACH month brings
its
delights
and
its special days.
April
is a
month of gladness and of new life
Each clod seems to quicken under the
sun's bright
rays, and seeds spring
up
in verdure.
Trees put on
their
green dresses and their glorious blos-
soms.
The churches give forth their
Easter
messages
and
from
choirs
everywhere the glorious tidings "He
is risen'' ring out.
To the people
of
the Church
of
Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints the
month brings its annual Conference.
From
all the Stakes of the Church
and from many Missions come men
and women hungering for the bread
of
life.
They have a few days of
rejoicing.
They
hear
the
Church
Leaders
give
inspired
admonition,
council and direction, then they re-
turn to their homes
filled and ready
to
take up
life's
labors
with new
courage, because they are better fitted
to meet its problems.
April
also brings the
Relief
So-
ciety Conference.
Practically every
Stake
is represented and
this great
work given impetus and new life.
So we rejoice when April comes
with its joyous inspiration and bene-
fits.

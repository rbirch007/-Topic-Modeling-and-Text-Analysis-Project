[Vol22 | March 1935 | Sequence: 07 | Category: Article | Title: Relief Society Baxter]
Over a period of years I have eagerly listened to instructions given and tributes paid to Relief Society teachers and have thrilled to the beauty of all that was said; but I still believe that the possibilities and opportunities in this field have scarcely been opened. We have been good teachers, we can be better; we have comforted many, we can comfort more.
Social Activity in the Relief Society By Achsa E. Paxman

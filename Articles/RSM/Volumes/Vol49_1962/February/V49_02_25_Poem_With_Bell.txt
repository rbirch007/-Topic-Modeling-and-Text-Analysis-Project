[Vol49 | February 1962 | Sequence: 25 | Category: Poem | Title: With | Author: Bell]
-~>- With Nobleness of Fleart Pauline M. Bell Oh, youth, wait not until the years shall prove the way. Seek now, with all your strength the path to joy, With nobleness of heart and love of honest toil. Why wait to mar your strength with little gain? Work cheerfully this day and find your worth. You shall not seek in vain, Fulfillment's at your feet — If you reach out and seek.

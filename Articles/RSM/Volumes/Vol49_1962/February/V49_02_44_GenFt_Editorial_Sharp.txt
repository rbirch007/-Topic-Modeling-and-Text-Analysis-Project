EDITORIAL kingdom of heaven.... For after much tribulation come the blessings' (D & C 58:2, 4). Experiences in mortality, accepted in humility, add greater faith, deeper repentance, a stronger power to overcome the temptation, less desire to judge others, a clearer understanding, refinement, and heightened joy. And in the midst of every testing in the world one may 97 possess, through righteousness, that parting gift of the Savior — "Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid" (John 14:27). The acceptance of daily trials and anxieties and sortows, cheerfully and uncomplainingly borne, refine and shape the individual to endure to the end and gain eternal life. —M. C. S.

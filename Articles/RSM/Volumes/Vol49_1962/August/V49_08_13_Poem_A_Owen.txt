(To be continued ) A Summarization of the Brief Life of Edgar Allan Poe Hazel Owen If you were to walk in the shoes of Poe, Tormented by drink, aloneness, and woe, What would you do, if your life were so? What would you turn to, and where would you go? If you had a desperate desire to write, And the world was against you in your fight, Would you see the blue of sunlit skies? Or a darker hue, with your darkened eves? Life was confusion, and noise, and din, Overwhelmingly crowding and shutting him in; No one to hear him and no one to care, No relief sought through the power of prayer.

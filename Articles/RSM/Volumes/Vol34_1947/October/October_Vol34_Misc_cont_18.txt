The oblong hassock serves as an ottoman by my slip-covered chair, and holds shoes and fancy work which may be tucked away and taken out again in odd moments. In the illustration the hinges in the rectangular hassock are visible on the outside. It would be better to insert them in the seam, or still better to use pin hinges of metal. She smiled apologetically, and the children still listened with rapt attention. "I tell them that thees boy ees of no great wealth." We looked at Danny's white overalls, bought in the mercado to resist the cement wash tray, and at his brown "'meeky" shoes. "T want them to know," her voice was sad now, "that such theengs as the health and speereets of thees child are possible some day to all in Mexico." Then she bowed quickly and said, "Con permiso." Before I could say a word in appreciation, she was lost in the fast moving traffic. The children, too, 'broke away and let Danny ride past them. He pedaled slowly so that I could walk by his side. "In Mexico," he told me, the slightly fatuous grin still on his face, "everyone seems to like me."

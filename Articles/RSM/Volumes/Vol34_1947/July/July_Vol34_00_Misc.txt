THE RELIEF SOCIETY MAGAZINE SEGO LILY Eva Willes Wangsgaard Have you seen the sego lily Lift her cup upon the hills?
Multiplying willy-nilly, Catching light the desert spills? _ Have you plucked a dainty cluster?
Touched the petals, white and thin?
Thrilled to note translucent luster Fine as Dresden's porcelain?
Have you heard the simple story How the segos played a part In our people's pain and glory?. See!
Each wears a purple heart.
Little hillside desert flower, Take the thanks which you deserve; In our fathers' saddest hour Nothing was too small to serve. The Cover: "Sego Lilies," Photograph by Dr. Walter P. Cottam VOL 34, NO. 7 JULY 194

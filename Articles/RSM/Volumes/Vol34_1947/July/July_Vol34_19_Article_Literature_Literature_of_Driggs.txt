Literature-Literature of the Doctrine and Covenants Elder H. Wayne Driggs Preview of Lessons for 1947-48 THE three-year series of Church history lessons brought to a close last May dealt with the theological study of the Doctrine and Covenants. It has been felt that some time could, with profit, be spent in enjoying this modern scripture from a literary point of view.

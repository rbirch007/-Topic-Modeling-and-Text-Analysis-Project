[Vol34 | August 1947 | Sequence: 16 | Category: Article | Title: How the Magazine Hart]
There is no tangible source in existence which can fortify the visiting teacher more than the Magazine. It represents Relief Society in its completeness the Magazine gives her official status. It helps the visiting teacher to feel the objectives of Relief Society; to strengthen the testimonies of the women; to strengthen Latter-day Saint homes; and to enrich the lives of women by providing opportunities for personal development.

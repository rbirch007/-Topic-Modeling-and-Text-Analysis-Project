There  were  other  women  poets  in
pioneer  days,  but  these  are  names
that  appeared  most  often,  and  these
selections  present  but  few  of  the
words  they  wrote.  Only  when  a
book  is  compiled  on  the  subject,  can
early  songs  and  poems  be  adequately

[Vol5 | February 1918 | Sequence: 01 | Category: Article | Title: My Baby Sophy Ricks Saurey]

MY BABY. I have a little baby. To me of priceless worth; One of the fairest "flowers Of all the flowers of earth. Such wondrous love she brought me. I never knew before. O joy supreme, to have a child So lovely to adore! She recompenses all I lack, She to my heart is more Than jewels rare, or silver crowns. Or all earth's golden store. God grant her health and noble aims, My precious little Ruth, And g"ui.de her footsteps ever in The path that leads to Truth. Keep her true and virtuous, This is my daily prayer; Give her strength to overcome. And avoid the tempter's snare. May her coming years be fruitful. And bring her happiness; And may the name of Mother Some day be hers to bless. Sophy Ricks Saurey. REXBURG. ID.VIIO.

LESSON II Work and Business: (Second Week in December) TEACHERS' TOPIC FOR DECEMBER—CIVIC PRIDE Waste Disposal. I. Sewage: How is your sewage disposed of? If you do not have sewer connections, are your cesspools sanitary? If privies are used are they strictly sanitary? (A slight covering of ordinary earth or ashes daily will do much to keep privies sanitary. ) II. Garbage: How is your garbage disposed of? Until disposed of, is it properly covered? IIJ. Junk: How do you dispose of tin cans, bottles and discarded cooking utensils, etc.?

The Silent Message By Grace Jacobsen
See, in the distance yonder Signalling to all the world The flag of a nation's birthright Is now from its heights unfurled.
Bars of white and crimson Bear in their glorious fold, Courage of patriots' life blood Pure as the dream we hold.
Blue is the field of honor True as the heaven above; Hope of Columbia's future, Flag that our nation loves.
I see the little one dark-eyed and shy, sigh.

NEW DAY

By MERLING CLYDE

The morning sun shines bright and clear,
The birds are singing in the trees,
The flowers bloom without a fear,
And dance upon the morning breeze.

The world seems fresh and clean and new,
As if it had been born today,
The sky above is crystal blue,
And all the earth seems glad and gay.

O heart of mine, be glad and sing,
Forget the sorrows of the night,
For this is dawn, and dawn will bring
New hope, new joy, and morning light.

The past is gone, the future waits,
But this glad moment is your own,
So open wide your heart's bright gates
And let the morning sunshine in.

For every dawn brings something new,
Some gift of beauty, joy, or grace,
Some blessing that will see you through
The trials that you have to face.

So greet each morning with a song,
And face each day with courage true,
For God is good, and right is strong,
And He will see you safely through.

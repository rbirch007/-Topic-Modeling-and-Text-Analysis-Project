NOTES TO THE FIELD

The Relief Society General Board wishes to call attention to the following:

The Relief Society Magazine should be in every Latter-day Saint home. It is published for the women of the Church and contains much valuable information for mothers and daughters. The subscription price is only $1.00 per year.

Every Relief Society should have a good supply of the Magazine on hand for distribution. Orders should be sent to the Relief Society Magazine, 20 Bishop's Building, Salt Lake City, Utah.

The General Board is pleased to announce that Sister Amy Brown Lyman has been appointed to represent the Relief Society on the National Council of Women. This is a great honor and we feel that Sister Lyman will represent us worthily.

We wish to remind all Relief Societies that the annual reports are due at the General Office not later than May 1st. These reports are very important and should be filled out carefully and completely.

The General Board wishes to thank all the sisters who have contributed to the success of the Relief Society work during the past year. Your cooperation and support have been greatly appreciated.

We hope that all Relief Societies will continue to grow and prosper in the coming year. Let us work together to make our organization even more effective in serving the needs of our people.

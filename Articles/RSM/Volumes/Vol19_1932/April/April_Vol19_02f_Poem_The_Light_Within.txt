The Light Within
Blanche Kendall McKey

There is a light within each soul
That glows with heavenly fire,
A spark divine that lifts the heart
With holy, pure desire.

It may be dimmed by worldly care,
Or darkened by despair,
But never can it be destroyed—
God placed that glory there.

When sorrow bows the weary head,
And tears of anguish fall,
That light within will guide us home
If we but heed its call.

It whispers of eternal things,
Of love that knows no end,
Of Him who died that we might live,
Our Savior and our Friend.

O soul of mine, look up and see
That light so pure and bright!
Let not the shadows of the world
Obscure thy inner light.

For he who keeps that flame alive
Within his heart each day,
Shall find the peace that passeth thought
Along life's rugged way.

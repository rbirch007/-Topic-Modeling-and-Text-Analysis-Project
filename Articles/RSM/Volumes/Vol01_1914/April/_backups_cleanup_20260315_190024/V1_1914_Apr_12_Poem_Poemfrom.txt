HOSPITAL TRAINING D.
S.
SCHOOL FOR NURSES has an average enrollment of about
100 Nurses In training.
The hospital has capacity of 250 beds.
The training school is under the direction and supervision of highly trained graduate nurses from schools of national reputation.
It gives a three years course in training in nursing in all its branches.
For further particulars address the Superintendent of Nurses Dr.
W.H.

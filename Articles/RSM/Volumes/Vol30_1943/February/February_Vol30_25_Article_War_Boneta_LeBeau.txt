13 et passim The Relief Society Magazine, 1942, "In Retrospect" WAR Boneta Le Beau War, Gaunt shadow of iniquity, What stagnant pool of conscience Nourishes you, Carries your feverish breath From land to land!
What weakness In our body politic Admits your germ, Accepts your death, Deplores you, Brings you back eternally!
Tonight your heavy gloom Engulfs the world. An hundred thousand men Clad in olive, green, and blue, Toss in narrow bunks And wait tomorrow's doom. In homes, From vacant beds and chairs Your huge, unseeing eyes Gape ceaselessly.

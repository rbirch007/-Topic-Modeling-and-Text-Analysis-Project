[Vol30 | December 1943 | Sequence: 18 | Category: Poem | Title: Across the Plains of Bethany]

Across the plains of Bethany,
Across the waves of Galilee,
Hark, list, the angels sing!

Beyond the proud Jerusalem,
From Bethel to Capernaum,
List to the echoes ring!

Angelic hosts from out the sky,
Bring adoration from on high,
To hail the new-born King.

Peace, peace on earth, good will to men,
Peace, peace on earth, good will to men,
Hail, gloria, and sing!

ERRATUM
ATTENTION is called to an error in the social science lesson, no. 9, in the August Relief Society Magazine, page 515, which erroneously names John Marshall as the first Chief Justice of the United States. While John Marshall was the most notable of American jurists and a Chief Justice, John Jay was the first Chief Justice.

[Vol35 | February 1948 | Sequence: 22 | Category: Article | Title: Aleine Margetts Young]

Aleine Margetts Young (Continued from page 82) Brother Young is a capable architect, a kind husband and father, who will give full support and co-operation to Sister Young in her new calling. Together, they have built a beautiful home where love and harmony dwell. They have four splendid children, Richard, who is a missionary in the Central States, Renee, Jane, and Bobby. Sister Young accepts this new position with humility, but her devotion to Relief Society, her many gifts of personality, her experience, and education are qualities that will make her a beloved and useful member of the General Board.

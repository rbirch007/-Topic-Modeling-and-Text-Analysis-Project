PAYMENT
Meiling  D.  Clyde
I  like  to  do  kind  things  for  you.
My  fee  is  far  above
The  recompense  of  others.
You  pay  me,  dear,  in  love.
And  so,  for  service  given,
Your  warming  praises  stem;
If  they  bring  you  happiness
I  richly  share  in  them.

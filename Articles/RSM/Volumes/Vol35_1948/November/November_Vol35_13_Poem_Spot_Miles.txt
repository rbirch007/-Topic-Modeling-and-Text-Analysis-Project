[Vol35 | November 1948 | Sequence: 13 | Category: Article | Title: Spot Miles]

THE DEAREST SPOT Eunice J. Miles A place of peace, A rendezvous from harm, A roof against the gale To keep us warm; A cozy kitchen Where a kettle sings, And children prattle Over the artless things, Where love and friendliness Hold greatest worth This is our home, The dearest spot on earth,

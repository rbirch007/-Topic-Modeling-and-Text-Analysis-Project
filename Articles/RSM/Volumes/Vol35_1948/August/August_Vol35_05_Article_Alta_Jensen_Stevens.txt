[Vol35 | August 1948 | Sequence: 05 | Category: Article | Title: Alta Jensen Stevens]

ALTA JENSEN VANCE NEW BOARD MEMBERS
She was married to Kimball Vance in the Salt Lake Temple, January 17, 1928, after which they spent some time in Chicago, returning in 1931 to organize the Vance Electric Company. In 1939, Brother Vance left on a two-year mission to Canada and during his absence the responsibility of business and family fell to Sister Vance. 515
She was very successful in both these undertakings. She is the mother of two fine sons, Roland and Byron, and a very gracious daughter, Loretta. Sister Vance has remarkable organizational ability, wisdom, caution, and a deep feeling of compassion and tolerance. In her humility and steadfastness, hers will be a service Of love and joy in connection with her general board work.

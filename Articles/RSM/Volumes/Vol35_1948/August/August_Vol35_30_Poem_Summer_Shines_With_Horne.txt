SUMMER SHINES WITH SILKEN SHOWERS
Helen Martin Horne
Summer shines with silken showers On mountains to the south. Blends odors from the freshening flowers When evening drops the sun.
From pine-ribbed hills and canyon nooks A pungent smell she takes To scent the breeze that bends the trees, And sports the mountain lakes.
The summer lenders to the fall Her colors brightly blended; But artful fall retints them all When summertime is ended.

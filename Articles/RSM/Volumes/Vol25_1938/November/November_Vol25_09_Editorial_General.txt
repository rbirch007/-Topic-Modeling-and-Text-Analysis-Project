VOL. XXV NOVEMBER, 1938 No. 11

Editorial

THE Relief Society Conference held in the Tabernacle October 1st and 2nd was one of the most inspiring and helpful in the history of the organization. The addresses were of unusual merit and the spirit of the meetings was uplifting and encouraging.

The reports given by the General Board members showed remarkable progress in all departments of Relief Society work. The membership has increased during the past year and the activities have been expanded and improved.

The visiting teachers' reports indicated that this important work is being carried on more effectively than ever before. The percentage of families visited has increased and the quality of the service rendered has been improved.

The work and business committee reports showed that the Relief Society is keeping pace with modern developments in the fields of health, education, and social service. The programs recommended for the coming year are practical and timely.

The Relief Society Magazine continues to grow in circulation and influence. It is now being read in many countries and is recognized as one of the outstanding women's magazines published anywhere.

We are grateful for the splendid cooperation of all Relief Society officers and members throughout the Church. Their loyalty and devotion make possible the continued growth and development of this great organization.

Utah State Conference of Social Work
THE Annual Sessions of the Utah State Conference of Social Work were held on the Campus of the University of Utah, November 12 and 13, 1937. Authorities in specialized fields, and speakers of national prominence discussed subjects of paramount importance. Mr. D. A. Skeen, President of the Conference, gave the opening address.

TO MARY KIMBALL
By Claire Stewart Boyer

"Blessed are the meek," I read,
"For they shall inherit the earth,"
"Blessed have you been," I said,
"For gentleness gave you birth;
And through the years humility
Has graced your unfaltering way—
Moonlight to your ability,
And art to the words you say;

"Blessed are they that nobly bear,
From out the celestial sphere,
Essence that all of us can share—
A boon to the now and here!
Your meekness is an aureole,
Divine credential of your soul."

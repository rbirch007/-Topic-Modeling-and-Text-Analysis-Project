ACKNOWLEDGMENT

The General Board of the Relief Society acknowledges with grateful appreciation the splendid cooperation of the stake Relief Society presidencies in making the Relief Society Conference a success. We are grateful for the excellent reports given by the stake presidents, and for the fine spirit manifested by all who attended the sessions.

We wish to express our thanks to the Tabernacle Choir for the beautiful music rendered during the conference sessions, and to all others who contributed to the success of the meetings.

We are grateful to the Church News and to the Deseret News for the excellent coverage given the conference proceedings.

To all who in any way contributed to the success of the Relief Society Conference, we extend our sincere thanks and appreciation.

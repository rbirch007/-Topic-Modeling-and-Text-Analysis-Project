HAPPENINGS

By Annie Wells Cannon

The Relief Society General Board held its regular monthly meeting June 2nd, with President Amy Brown Lyman presiding. All members of the Board were present except Sister Clarissa A. Beesley who is in California.

Sister Lyman reported on her recent trip to the Eastern States Mission where she visited Relief Society organizations in New York, Philadelphia, Washington, D. C., and other cities. She spoke at a number of meetings and was much pleased with the interest and enthusiasm shown by the sisters.

Sister Louise Y. Robison, first counselor, reported on her visit to the Northwestern States Mission where she attended conferences and spoke at Relief Society meetings in Seattle, Portland, and other cities.

Sister Julia A. F. Lund, second counselor, reported on her recent trip to California where she visited Relief Society organizations in Los Angeles, San Francisco, and other cities.

The Board discussed plans for the Relief Society conference to be held in October in connection with the general conference of the Church.

Sister Lyman announced that the Relief Society has been asked to furnish quilts for the new Deseret Hospital which is being built in Salt Lake City.

The Board voted to contribute $500 to the Church Building Fund.

Sister Belle S. Spafford, secretary of the General Board, reported that the Relief Society Magazine circulation has increased during the past year.

The meeting was closed with prayer by Sister Robison.

LIFE'S MYSTERY
By Olive C. Wehr
You never would suppose
The thorny old desert cactus
Could bloom like the rose;
Nor that the slimy, creeping snail
Could build a pearl house.
So beautifully
frail;
Nor that pain and grief
Could bring understanding
To
give another
relief.
How strange Hfe's mystery
That out of pain or ugliness
Should come most beauty

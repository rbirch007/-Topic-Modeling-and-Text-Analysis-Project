I shall go out beneath the stars,
Shall walk with darkness, feel the dew,
I shall come back from night's sweet bars
More humble than I ever knew.

I shall go out to work each day
Among my kind, to give, to do,
I shall come back from work and play
More loving than I ever grew.

For out of sky and stars and sod,
Out of my work, my joy, my pain,
I shall come back more near to God,
More conscious of His love again.

Johnson SPOKANE STAKE, MOSCOW (IDAHO) WARD RELIEF SOCIETY ANNIVERSARY PARTY, March 14, 1949
President Gwen Green stands at the center front, immediately behind the birthday cake; at Sister Green's left is her First Counselor Leona Canfield and at Sister Green's right, her Second Counselor Aleen McCowan. President Edna H. Bennion, Spokane Stake Relief Society, reports: 'Located at Moscow, home of the University of Idaho, this group is unusual in Relief Society history. Of its forty-five paid members, twenty-eight are student-wives, who exemplify compassionate service, for they help each other in sickness and share each other's burdens, sorrows, and joys, with the same crusading spirit as their pioneer ancestors. Their youth and enthusiasm, despite financial hardships and crowded housing facilities, have written a new and heroic chapter in Relief Society history."

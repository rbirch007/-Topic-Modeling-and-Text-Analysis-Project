1  do  not  need  a  portrait  of  your  face  .... 
Each  loved,  remembered  feature  I  can  trace 
With  tender  care  ....  an  art  of  memory. 
I  do  not  need  a  record  of  your  voice; 
To  me,  each  cadence  is  as  clear  as  some  sweet  melody 
Which,  half -forgotten,  springs  to  life  again. 
I  have  no  need  for  pictures  to  reveal  the  way 
You  smile;  your  charm,  the  things  you  do  and  say  .  .

I  feel  your  presence;  you  are  always  near.

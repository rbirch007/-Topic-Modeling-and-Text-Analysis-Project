Charmaine Kohler
DEBRA awoke suddenly, as she
did each morning,
plans

the day running through her
head.
Today

cupboard

After
that,
there
might
be
time
to

that
stack

came home from work.
Debra prided herself on her neat-
as-a-pin home.
She knew her neigh-
bors remarked on how she kept

that

with
two
small
atom-
powered
boys
and
a
husband
to

Her formula was "a

Her

Ronnie, age four,

ing to the bedside, both

"Good morning, Mommie!" Ron-
nie
flashed

be-
neath the covers.
' 'Morning, Mommie/'
Greggie

nie

Greggie

Debra smiled quickly at her wig-
gling

The

When Dan
left

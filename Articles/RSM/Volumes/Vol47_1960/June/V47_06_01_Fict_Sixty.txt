OUR SCANDINAVIAN WOMEN
Thank heaven for
all these sturdy ones,
These men of
toil, these women true,
Who, working on through storms and
suns,
Found naught too hard
for their hands
to do,
And whether on earth or whether above,
We open the records of
life and love,
By the side of the names of our Danish
sires,
Whose hearts grew warm in the Gospel light
Will our mothers be,
for those living
fires
Have kept them as truly bright.
—Alofa

CPECIAL
dinners
for
Sundays,
holidays,
anniversaries,
family
gatherings, and other occasions may
be made
appetizing and
attractive
by planning the menu and
prepar-
ing most of the food the day before
the dinner
is to be served.
The
casseroles
in
the
following
menus may
all be prepared the day
before the special dinner and stored
in the refrigerator over night. Then,
thirty
or
forty minutes
before
the
time
for
serving
the
dinner,
the
casseroles
may
be
placed
in
the
oven,
with
the
thermostat
set
at
moderate heat
(300
to 350
). By
the
time
the
quick-cooking
green
vegetables are done, and the
salads
and
fruit
cups
have
been
served,
the main dish will be ready.

Some wards have sent in their reports and are included even though the stakes were MY SHIPS By Annie Pike Greenwood Stay home, my ships?here in the harbor stay: But one last trip,?till then, stay here with me. You shall not come back wrecked another day; Never again forth-speeded shall you be. Remiss. I waste no tears to find you stripped so bare; Nor weep I that you brought no cargo back. Glad am I that I built you all so fair? My ships were good; in me alone the lack. Dear ships of Youth and Hope and High Desire, Here in the harbor let us rest awhile? A little while, and you shall be my pyre: Flaming, we'll sail, high-heartedly, and smile.

Have tended our long baby years
;
With unceasing vigilance watched, day and night,
To shield, lest some danger come near.
No one else humbly, earnestly would have prayed
For patience to teach us aright
The laws of rectitude, virtue, and truth
So pleasing in God's holy sight.
So, too, as we came to maturer estate
Her sympathy, guidance, and love,
As a sweet benediction, inspiring fell
'Round our lives, as a light from above.
And though oft we were thoughtless, and grieved her,
'tis true,
Perhaps caused her much sorrow and pain,
Her faith never faltered, her love,
still as true,
Brought us back to our senses again.
Our Father, we thank Thee, for this Holy day,
So different from any other
;
And sincerely our honor, and homage we pay,
Each one, to his own dearest Mother.
We ask Thee to bless her, and keep her from harm,
That she may continue to be
Our loving companion, inspiring us on
To Heaven, to Glory, To Thee..

L. D. S.

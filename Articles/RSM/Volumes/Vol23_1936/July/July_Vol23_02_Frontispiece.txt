Is a Statehood welded with Brotherhood
In the wake of the old frontier,
There the fruited trails of the plain attest
Where the feet of the conquerors have pressed,
O Utah, land of the Golden West,
Is the Promised Land to me
!

MOUNT OWEN FROM THE GRAND NATIONAL PARK AT
JENNY LAKE

belief Society" Magazine

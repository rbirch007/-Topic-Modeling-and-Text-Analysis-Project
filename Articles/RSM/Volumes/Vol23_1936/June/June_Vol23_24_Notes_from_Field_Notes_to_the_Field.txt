Notes to the Field Special Activities ]X hearty accord with the Churchwide program of relief and recovery, the Relief Society has suggested two Projects for Summer activity within the organization.
Details of these have been sent to every Stake.
There is an opportunity in these great projects for the members of the Church, forgetful of self, to grow spiritually, to attain unity and happiness, if they enter whole-heartedly into this cooperative enterprise.
Because a brother or sister has had misfortune, he should not be deprived of the blessings of service.
There is a wonderful opportunity of knitting together the lives of those whom the forces of the world have tended to separate. Let us build to a high degree the "we" feeling, and make every effort to give all the joy of cooperation.

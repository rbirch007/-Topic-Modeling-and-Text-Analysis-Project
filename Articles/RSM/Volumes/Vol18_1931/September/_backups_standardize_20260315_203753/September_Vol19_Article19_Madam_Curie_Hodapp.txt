Madame Curie — By Minnie Iverson Hodapp Subtle enchantress, Sorceress bright, Delicate delver Poised in pure light. O for the fairy Wand that you wield, Questioning Nature Making her yield Deep, hidden secrets Luminous, rare, Wresting from Nature Making her share Spirit unswerving Patience supreme, Vigil ecstatic: "Follow the gleam." Essence of genius Flower of trust! Wondrous, priceless, Diamond dust! Dreamer and doer, Peerless, serene— Truth's stern pursuer Scientist—Queen!

Thanks for Today By Linnie Fisher Robinson Oh, wasn't it lovely the way The robin built her nest? Wasn't it fine to see the sun sink in the golden west? Oh wouldn't we miss the flowers, If we couldn't walk where they grow? And wouldn't we miss the grasses If we could no more see them blow?
So, just to give us happiness, And make the day seem glad, Let's thank God for the day we have Whether its good or bad.
For, if the day should be cloudy And we can't see the sun, Let's thank God we can look for it Before that day is done.

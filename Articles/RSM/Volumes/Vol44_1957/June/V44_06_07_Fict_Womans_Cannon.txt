stale  doughnuts.  We  can  eat  those." 
"No  thanks,"  Katherine  laughed. 
"I'm  just  in  need  of— of  someone  to 
talk  to,  I  guess.  Someone  not  in 
the  family— if  you  know  what  I 
mean."

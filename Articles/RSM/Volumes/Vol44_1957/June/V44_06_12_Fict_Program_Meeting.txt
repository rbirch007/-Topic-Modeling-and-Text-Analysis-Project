Program for the Llovember Sast Sunday &vening Meeting THE special program for Sunday, November 3, 1957, ""I'o Build a Spiritual House," was mailed to Relief Society stake presidents in May 1957. We urge that these programs be distributed to the wards without delay. The program includes two numbers from the Singing Mothers chorus. For these numbers it is recommended that a sacred anthem already in their repertoire be chosen, and an appropriate hymn from the Latter-day Saint hymn book. It is suggested that these numbers be practiced during the summer months.

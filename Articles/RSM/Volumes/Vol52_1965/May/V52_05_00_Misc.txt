FAMILY HOME EVENING SPECIAL CASSEROLE Ida Isaacson Cook 1 package frozen green lima beans, as directed on the package. (Fresh green lima beans may be substituted.) Drain off the juice and drink it. (Don't throw it away.) Then add 1% c. Of corn (kernel or creamed) to the hot, cooked limas, return to the stove and cook 1 minute. Open a can of creamed mushroom soup (do not dilute soup)— heat 14 can of soup in separate pan. Put hot beans and corn in a casserole dish. Pour hot, steaming mushroom soup (undiluted) over hot beans and corn and sprinkle a dash or two of cumin over the whole. Brown in 370° oven.

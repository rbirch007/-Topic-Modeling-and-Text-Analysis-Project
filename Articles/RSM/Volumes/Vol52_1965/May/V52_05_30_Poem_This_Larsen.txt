[Vol52 | May 1965 | Page 384 | Poem | This House | Leora Larsen]
This House Leora Larsen This house is peopled. Friends have rested in this room. If loneliness sits here, it is well-fed, well-prayed for. Here grandchildren bring their laughter. Each shadow is blessed by love's sweet language of living, and each joy is beloved and strengthened by our hope for safe return. And when I, too, must one day leave, Lord, let me make a home as peopled.

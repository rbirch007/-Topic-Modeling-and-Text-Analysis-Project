Measure flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt; sift over fruit, blend well. Beat eggs, add vanilla, blend into nut-fruit mixture, spoon into loaf pan, spread evenly. Bake at 300° 134 hr. To 2 hrs., until firm on top. This makes one 9x5x3 loaf cake. Keeps well in refrigerator. It makes a very special gift. LIGHT FRUIT CAKE 14 c. Flour 1 c. Shortening 1 pkg. Fruit mix (about 1 Ib.) 14, c. Sugar 14, Ib. Candied cherries 14, c. Honey 14 Ib. Dates 5 beaten eggs 3 ozs. Candied pineapple 114 c. Flour 4 ozs. Raisins 1 tsp. Salt 14 c. Walnuts 1 tsp. Baking powder 14 c. Pecans 14 tsp. Nutmeg 14 c. Orange juice 14, tsp. Cloves Mix nuts and fruit in large pan with 14 cup flour. Cream shortening and sugar, add honey and eggs. Mix dry ingredients, add alternately with orange juice. Pour batter over nut and fruit mix and blend well. Bake at 250° three hours and 15 minutes.

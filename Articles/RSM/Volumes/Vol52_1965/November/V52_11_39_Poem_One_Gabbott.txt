"One More Story" Mabel Jones Gabbott How many times have I heard these words: "One more story... Just one, please...?" The hour is late; the lights are low; Their eyes are heavy, and still they tease, "One more story...." And how many times Have I let the dishes and ironing wait, While I led their thoughts adventuring Down thunderous plains, or through fancy's gate? How many times in years to come Will we remember poignantly, The intimate half-dark, the urgent words, And the heroes riding brave and free?

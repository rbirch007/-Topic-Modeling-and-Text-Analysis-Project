[Vol52 | October 1965 | Page 797 | Poem | A Gift | Elva T. Cowley]
Gift Elva T. Cowley Should you come to the end of a dreary day And check on the things you've done And find them good, or poor, as you say, Or unfinished, the things you've begun. Just know that tomorrow the sun will shine, The rain clouds will float away, And he whose power is all supreme Will give you another day.

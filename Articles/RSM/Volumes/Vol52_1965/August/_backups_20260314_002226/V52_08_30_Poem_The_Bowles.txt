Catherine B. Bowles What have I sent to the garden of God While I plant flowers in the earthly sod? Beautiful lilies, pure and white, Thoughtful pansies with faces bright; The modest violet, quiet, serene, When wisdom and faithfulness are seen. Roses of beauty in a mirrored place, Reflecting loveliness and grace.

Strength of the Hills Grace Barker Wilson The memory of wooded mountain tops Has brought me strength. I find a comfort in the thought Of sweet spruce boughs All dripping from a shower. I have no mountains here, nor evergreens, But in my heart they will remain For comfort and assurance When I stumble in the sand.

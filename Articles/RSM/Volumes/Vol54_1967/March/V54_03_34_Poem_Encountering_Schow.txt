[Vol54 | March 1967 | Page 211 | Poem | Encountering Soon Iris | W. Schow]
ENCOUNTERING SOON Iris W. Schow We are waiting at the crossroads Where spring and winter meet; Each hopes to rule the elements With dominance complete. But soon the sky will sparkle, The brooks will wake and sing, When winter, worsted, abdicates To promise-laden spring.

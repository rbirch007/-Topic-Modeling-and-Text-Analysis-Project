[Vol54 | August 1967 | Page 586 | Poem | Bright Begonias | Bernice Ames]
R Color translates BRIGHT BEGONIAS Bernice Ames Here at the edge of a garden As velvet as Eden The sun has taken root To hold down moments of light. Begonias rise on brittle stems From Netherlands fields Of infinite care and choosing— Magenta, lemon, fuchsia, tangerine. The love that made them flourish. They give back a beauty Learned from partial shade. > y MT.

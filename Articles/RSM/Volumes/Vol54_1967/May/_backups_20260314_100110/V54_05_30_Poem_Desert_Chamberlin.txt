Stock Oh, weary mothers, when you sew Don't you wish that clothes would grow On a pinafore bush or a school-dress tree? Then how happy little girls would be! Team-sewing isn't exactly like picking clothes off a tree, but it is the next best thing. When two women work together in the spirit of an old-fashioned quilting-bee, little clothes almost seem to roll off the sewing machine and _ ironing board. Two or more dresses can be completed in the same time it takes a lone woman to make one dress. W

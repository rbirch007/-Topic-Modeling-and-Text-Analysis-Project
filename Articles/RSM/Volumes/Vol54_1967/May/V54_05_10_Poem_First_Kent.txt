[Vol54 | May 1967 | Page 339 | Poem | First Lullaby | Armora Kent]
@ Mabel Jones Gabbott 339 FIRST LULLABY Lull of the sea and sway of the pine trees, Silence of stars and peace of the sky, Silver of dreams and surge of my heart's love These shall go into my first lullaby. Lift of the spray and lilt of the songbird, Hush of a prayer and wish of a sigh, Light of my faith in days that are darkest; These shall go into my first lullaby.

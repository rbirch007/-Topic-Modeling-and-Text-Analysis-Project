SPACE THE DAYS Beulah Huish Sadlier Space the days—lest swift as running deer, October ends. Lf in heaven's sight, she spread a flame of sorrow On the hills, then only in pretense shall I confiscate How to extoll the opulence of apple And the clinging peach, the clustered grape, And burnished essence of aquatic dust Floating on the leaf-banked stream. It was October when we met— And days went swift as running deer a

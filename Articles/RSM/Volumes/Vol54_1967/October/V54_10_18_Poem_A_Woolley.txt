[A Prayer Enid F. Woolley]
A PRAYER Enid F. Woolley Australia Father, forgive the times of doubting, Of never being sure If what I do is right or wrong. And give me wisdom pure, That when two paths are there before me And one I needs must tread, Let me know without a doubt, A worry or a dread, That the path I take is right, And meant for me to use. Father forgive my indecision; And teach me how to choose.

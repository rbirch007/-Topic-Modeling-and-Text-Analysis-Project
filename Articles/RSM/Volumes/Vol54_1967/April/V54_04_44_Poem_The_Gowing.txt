[Vol54 | April 1967 | Page 280 | Poem | The Mighty Oak and I Laura | M. Gowing]
THE MIGHTY OAK AND I Laura M. Gowing Lowestoft, Suffolk, England You are tall and great and strong; You have been here ages, Long before this town began.! Shelter beneath your branches, Lean against your gnarled old frame: And though 1 am nearly three score years and ten, I feel but a child again As I measure Your years, against mine. You, too, must feel the same, When measured against his eternity: For he was, before the world began. 2

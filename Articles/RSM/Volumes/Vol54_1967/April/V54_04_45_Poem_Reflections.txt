REFLECTION Alverna Manning Allender When I! Met her, I pictured her dressed in cotton, In a humble cotage, baking cookies for fat, rosy babies. Her smile was sunlight reflected on a running brook; The shine and shimmer spilling over into her eyes, The ripple invading her laughter. 3

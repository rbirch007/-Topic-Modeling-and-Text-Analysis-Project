[Vol54 | September 1967 | Page 712 | Poem | Child in a Doorway | Ethel Jacobsen]
[— CHILD IN A DOORWAY Ethel Jacobson A darker shadow in the shadowed hall You stand, withdrawn but peering From darkness wrapped about you like a shawl, Hoping... Or fearing.... What? That a splendid stranger might come by? A stray cat nuzzle your hand? A leaf dance down? Careless playmates cry To have you join their band?! Smile. Dark eyes in deeper shade immersed, You waver, then duck from view Into familiar shadows. But first, oh, first You smiled back.... Didn't you?

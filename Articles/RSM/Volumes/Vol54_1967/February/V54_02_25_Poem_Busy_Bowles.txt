[Vol54 | February 1967 | Page 160 | Poem | Busy Fingers Catherine | B. Bowles]
Anna Sophia Nelson Gee Larsen Bell, California Marsing, Idaho BUSY FINGERS Catherine B. Bowles Recording fingers mark our way: A wonderful record of our day, Tracing the path where sorrow leads; Intricate patterns of our deeds. Then many bright ones good and true Shine out, in splendor in all we do. He leadeth us where he has trod, Showing the pathway back to God.

KATE'S COOKIES Kate Swainston 14 cup white sugar Y cup brown sugar 1 egg 1 cube butter (14 Ib.) *2 tablespoons of cream *1 tablespoon of lemon juice 1144 cup flour 14% tsp. Soda 1 tsp. Baking powder 1 tsp. Almond flavoring 14 cup rolled oats Mix ingredients together in order listed. Roll and store in refrigerator for two to four hours before baking. Slice and place on baking sheet. Bake at 400° for 12 minutes. *Three tbsp. Sour cream can be substituted.

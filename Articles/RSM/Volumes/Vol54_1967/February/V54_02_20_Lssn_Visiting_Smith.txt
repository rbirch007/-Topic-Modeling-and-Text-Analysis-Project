 said, "Verily I say, men should be anxiously engaged in a good cause, and do many things of their own free will, and bring to pass much righteousness"? (D&C 58:27). In these scriptures and many others, the Lord calls us to constant and persevering effort. No great achievement was ever accomplished without an enormous outpouring of spirit, energy, faith, and effort. No earthly or heavenly victory, in any area, is granted with less. By faithfulness, we recognize that we are' to grow in keeping the commandments of the Lord, to. Study, to seek wisdom, to achieve victory over the limitations of self, to learn to love God and our fellow men, and to serve all. "Remember that without faith you can do nothing; therefore ask in faith" (D&C 8:10). The prayer of faith opens the door through which we receive divine assistance.

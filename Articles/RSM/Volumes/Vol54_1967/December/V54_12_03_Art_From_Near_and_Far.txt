From Near and Far Thanks for the wonderful Magazine. It truly brings all the sisters in the world together in a common bond. Our branch here in Ames sends the Magazine to our local library each month, and when the library is finished with them, we pick them up every six months. This year we are putting them to extra use and circulation by leaving them at our hospital. Since I am the only registered Latter-day Saint nurse here, I am looking for all sorts of ways to introduce the gospel to my co-workers. More and more, as a result of the Magazine, I am asked questions about the gospel. June E. Booth Ames, Iowa

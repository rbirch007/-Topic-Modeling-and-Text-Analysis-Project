CANDY LOLLIPOPS 3 cups gronulated U and I Sugar 2/3 cup water 1 cup light corn syrup 1/4 teaspoon salt 4 teaspoons raspberry extract (or other fruit flavor) 4 to 5 drops food coloring lollipop sticks Combine sugar, water, syrup, salt in top of double boiler. Place over moderate heat, stirring constantly until sugar dissolves. Boil to 290° or soft crack stage. Remove from heat. Cool to 220°. Add extract and food coloring. Place over very hot water. Arrange lollipop sticks on buttered cookie sheets. Drop enough candy from tip of teaspoon onto each stick toform 1-14" lollipops. Cool. Wrap in squares of waxed paper. Store in airtight container.

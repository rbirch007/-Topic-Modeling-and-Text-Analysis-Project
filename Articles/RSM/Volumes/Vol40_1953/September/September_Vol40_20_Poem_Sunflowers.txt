[Vol40 | September 1953 | Sequence: 20 | Category: Article | Title: Sunflowers]

<> Sunflowers Evelyn Fjeldsted Along the farm roads in the fall, Sunflowers, standing straight and tall, With bonnets made of petaled gold Are nodding in the first wind's cold.
With autumn coming as a guide, Their bonnets soon are laid aside; With heads bowed in the warming sun, They whisper that their work is done.

[Vol40 | November 1953 | Sequence: 26 | Category: Poem | Title: Winter Wheat]

Winter Wheat
Eleanor W. Schow

Harvest to sow;
To keep life green and growing:
But few have known
The full worth of sowing.

Plant the late crop
That the world is needing;
Someone will reap
From your autumn's seeding.

Only by faith
In the seed God has given
Can your heart love earth
And your soul know heaven.

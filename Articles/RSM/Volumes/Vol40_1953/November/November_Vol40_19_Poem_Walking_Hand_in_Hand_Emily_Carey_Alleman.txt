Walking Hand in Hand
Emily Carey Alleman

Hand in hand, together,
Traveling life's highway;
We find the journey pleasant,
Sharing, day by day.

Sharing the joys and sorrows,
Sharing the pleasures and pain;
We take the sun with the shadow,
Laughing at the rain.

Laughing at stormy weather,
Seeking to understand;
We find the fruits of life sweeter,
Walking hand in hand.

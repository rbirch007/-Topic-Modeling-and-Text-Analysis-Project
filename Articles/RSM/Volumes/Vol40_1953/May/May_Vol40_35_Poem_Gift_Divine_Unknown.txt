[Vol40 | May 1953 | Sequence: 35 | Category: Poem | Title: Gift Divine]

Bring  me  to  my  knees.
It  is  a  gift  of  beauty,
A  joyous  thing  to  know,
A  guide,  a  light,  a  peace  serene,
That  holds  me  in  its  glow.
As  silently  as  starlight,
Falhng  on  a  flower.
It  can  leave  me,  as  it  came.
And  mar  this  golden  hour.
The  way  that  I  can  keep  it?
This  treasure  rare,  sublime
Is  just  to  love  my  neighbor.
And  serve  my  Lord  divine.

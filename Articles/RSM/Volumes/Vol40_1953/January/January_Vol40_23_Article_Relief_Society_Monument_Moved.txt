[Vol40 | January 1953 | Sequence: 23 | Category: Article | Title: Relief Society Monument Moved]

Relief Society members everywhere tender their heartfelt sympathy. | Relief Society Mlonument at Tauvoo Mloved HE monument erected in 1933 by Relief Society to mark the site of the Joseph Smith Store in Nauvoo, where Relief Society was organized. By the Prophet Joseph Smith, March 17, 1842, was moved on August 7, 1952, at the request of the Reorganized Church, owners of the property. Under the direction of the First Presidency, the monument has now been placed in a beautiful location on the Nauvoo Temple Block. It is a short distance south of the Bureau of Information at the front of the Temple Block, looking toward the Mississippi River. Votes

Bluffs  and  parts  of  the  Devil's  Gar- 
den haven't  been  thoroughly  ex- 
plored. So,  with  all  that  is  strange 
and  different  today  in  the  Arches 
National  Monument,  there  will  be 
even  more  tomorrow,  as  men  and 
roads  reach  out  farther  and  farther 
into  the  unknown. 
The  scenery  of  the  Arches  is  not 
only  different,  it  is  beautiful,  color- 
ful, and  somehow  breath-taking  and 
unbelievable.  It  is  a  place  to  see 
and  see  again.

[Vol31 | September 1944 | Sequence: 06 | Category: Article | Title: Fall Singley]

er  Singley
Gray  days,  gay  days,
Falling  leaves  shroud  all,
Paisley  cloak,  spiral  smoke,
Colorful  days  of  Fall.
Barren  trees,  drowsy  bees.
Lazy  days  to  loll,
Honking  geese,  cat-tail  fleece.
Glorious  days  of  Fall.
Nature's  worth,  resting  earth,
Wind  with  white  wings  call;
Cellars  filled,  flour  milled,
Bounteous  days  of  Fall.

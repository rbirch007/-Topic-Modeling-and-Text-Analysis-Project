NEW BOARD MEMBERS

Sister Amy Brown Lyman was sustained as second counselor in the General Board of the Relief Society at the general conference in April. Sister Lyman has been a member of the General Board for many years and has had charge of the social service work. She is well known throughout the Church for her splendid work in this field.

Sister Clarissa A. Beesley was sustained as a member of the General Board to fill the vacancy caused by the advancement of Sister Lyman. Sister Beesley has been president of the Liberty Stake Relief Society for several years and has done excellent work in that capacity. She is the wife of Ebenezer Beesley and the mother of six children.

Sister Donna D. Sorensen was also sustained as a member of the General Board. She has been president of the Granite Stake Relief Society and has shown marked ability in Relief Society work. She is the wife of Andrew Sorensen and the mother of four children.

We welcome these sisters to the General Board and feel confident that they will render valuable service in the great work of the Relief Society.

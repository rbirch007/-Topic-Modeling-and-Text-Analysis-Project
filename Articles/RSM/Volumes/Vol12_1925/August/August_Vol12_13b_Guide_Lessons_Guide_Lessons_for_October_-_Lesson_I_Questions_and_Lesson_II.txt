TEACHERS' TOPIC FOR OCTOBER A Better and Saner Hallowe'en
Importance of directing instead of repressing the children's activities.
Need of: (a) Understanding a child's idea of right and wrong. (b) Good, wholesome fun.
Parents should know where their children are and what they are doing. Are they destroying their neighbors' property? Do they know the origin and purpose of Hallowe'en? When little ones use jack-o-lanterns make sure they are safe. Serious accidents have happened from the use of paper and pasteboard ones.

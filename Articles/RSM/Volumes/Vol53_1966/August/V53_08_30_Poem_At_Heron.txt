[At Conference Sarah Heron]

AT CONFERENCE Sarah Heron Paisley, Scotland The Cannon girls look wonderful, White blouse and black, black hair.
Their faces are so radiant!
No beauty can compare.
Their eyes so dark, but full of light, A picture to behold, As their lips, they part in song, Make sounds like tinkling gold.
All our girls are beautiful— Each one without compare; Heather Macrae with auburn hair, Smiling face with radiant glow; Two rows of snow white blouses!
Adorned our girls so pure!
It makes one think of heaven With light of a bright azure.

[Vol53 | August 1966 | Page 575 | Poem | For Those Who See | Rose Thomas Graham]
FOR THOSE WHO SEE Rose Thomas Graham I cannot scale the mountain tops, Nor touch a single star. My foot would falter in the climb, My hand not reach so far. But, oh! I have a grateful heart For these far-seeing eyes, That rocket to the highest peak, Encompass all the skies.

[Vol53 | April 1966 | Page 300 | Poem | Thank You, Visiting Teachers | Carolle Denton.]
THANK YOU, VISITING TEACHERS Carolle Denton Time was long and seeming dark... A knock came to my door, Two sisters brought a light within I had not seen before. Warmth of sun on shadowed clay Softened and formed anew The latent powers of hope and love, And let the daylight through. The Lord who saw this hardened clay Knew well the urgent cry To bring my life out of the shade, And now, how bright the sky!

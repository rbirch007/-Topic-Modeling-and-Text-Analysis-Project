[Sea Etching, Vesta Nickerson Fairbairn, 86\l Resolution Estella S. Johnson]
SEA ETCHING Vesta Nickerson Fairbairn The sound of waves becomes translated Into ripples, scrolled and fanned, That show the pattern of the tide Clear-etched upon the wet sea sand. RESOLUTION Estella S. Johnson Age eighty-three My hands got too tired to work on a rug, My eyes with cataracts sore, I said to a heavy quilt I was making ll never look at you more. So after my flowers got hit with the frost, I picked up my tablet and pen; They can call, they can shout, They can let the calves out, I'll never be bothered again.

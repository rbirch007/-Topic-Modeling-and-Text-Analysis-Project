FLOWERS FOR WINTER Fay Ottesen "Mom," called my son one wintry day, "The flowers outside have all gone away. "You don't have any to set on your sink, So Ill make you some; I can, I think." He took his tinker toys, and worked with care, And made me some flowers; those wooden ones there. No flower in the world matches the beauty of those; No tulip, no orchid, no hyacinth nor rose.

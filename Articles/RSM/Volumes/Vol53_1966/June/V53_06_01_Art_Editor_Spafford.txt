[Editor Marianne C. Sharp Associate Editor Vesta P. Crawford General Manager Belle S. Spafford]
Words are such hasty things,
Yet they may mean
All that can ever be,
All that has been.

Words are such little things,
Yet they may make
Destinies totter,
Centuries shake.

Words are so uniform,
Yet they may be
Anything, everything,
To you and me.

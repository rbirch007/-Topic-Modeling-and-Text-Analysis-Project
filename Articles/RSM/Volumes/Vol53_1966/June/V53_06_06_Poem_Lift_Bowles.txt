[Lift the Heart to Gladness Catherine B. Bowles]

Kind words are like the flowers That brighten the shadowed day And lift the heart to gladness Above the troubled way. When unkind words are spoken That wound the tender heart, God in his infinite mercy Will love and strength impart. The fragrance of life, is measured -By deeds of lasting worth— Each act a flower of goodness To brighten the path of earth.

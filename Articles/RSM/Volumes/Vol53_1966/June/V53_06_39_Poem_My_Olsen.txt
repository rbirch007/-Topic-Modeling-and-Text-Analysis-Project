[My Hour Pearle M. Olsen]

This hour is mine to lose or use— To mar, to brighten—as I choose. This hour is all I have secure; Now I am breathing, calm and sure! This hour is brief, yet will be gold Invested in myself—when old. This hour of mine has power to be Important in eternity!

[Vol53 | December 1966 | Page 950 | Poem | Only One Song Alda | L. Brown]
ONLY ONE SONG Alda L. Brown For this brief moment He was her Child... Newborn and soft and warm Against 'her breast. Outside the world could wait To claim this infant King.... But for this brief time He was Mary's Child! Let the star falter in the sky! Let the Wise Men pause in their journey! Let the heavenly angels hush their singing, "All Hail, the newborn King!" For in Mary's heart There was only one song. A silent lullaby That only mothers sing....

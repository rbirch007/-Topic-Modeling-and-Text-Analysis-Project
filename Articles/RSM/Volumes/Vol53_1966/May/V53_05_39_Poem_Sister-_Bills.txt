[Vol53 | May 1966 | Page 397 | Poem | Sister- Talk | Rowena Jensen Bills]
SISTER-TALK Rowena Jensen Bills The little things we talk about— Bits of this and that, How charming was the poem she read And color of her hat, The dinner menu, the baby's teeth, And did the headache dast? Superficial gaieties P Of future and of past.. Yet deep affection, trusting love, In every carefree phrase. Sister-talk, friendship-talk, Fulfillment of joyous days.

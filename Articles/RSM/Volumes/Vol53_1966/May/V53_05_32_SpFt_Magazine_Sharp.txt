The General Board also thanks stake and ward presidencies for the support they give to their Magazine representatives. Sometimes the difference between one stake having over 100% subscriptions and another under 75% is largely dependent upon the attitude of the respective presidencies. A stake which takes for granted that each ward will be over 100% in subscriptions establishes a warm momentum, and carries along any newcomers in the confident assurance of success. There were 353 stakes on the Honor Roll and 164 stakes had over 100%. There were 19 missions on the Honor Roll and 81 mission districts. For the nineteenth year, South Los Angeles Stake led the Church with 217%, one per cent higher than last year. The North Central States Mission led the missions with 119%. The number of subscriptions has increased by 9,344 over 1964, from 231,933 to 241,277. The General Board extends heartfelt gratitude to everyone who had a part in this excellent increase. 375 Honors for Highest Ratings.

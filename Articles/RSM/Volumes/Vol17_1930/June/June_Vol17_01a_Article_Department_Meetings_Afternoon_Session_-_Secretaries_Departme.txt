Department Meetings (Afternoon Session) Secretaries' Department ES meeting convened in the Auditorium of the Bishop's Building after the close of the Stake Officers' Meeting.

General Secretary Julia A. F. Lund presided.

It was the first Department Meeting of the Stake secretaries, and was very largely attended.

Mrs. Lund paid a tribute to the past and present secretaries, and stressed the importance of their work, since an organization is judged by the records it keeps.

Relief Society secretaries are also the historians of the organization.

A practical demonstration of "How to Audit the Annual Report," was conducted by Mrs. Ellen F. Shepherd.

The copy of a report form was used, each step explained, and the most common errors made by secretaries pointed out.

Time was the only element that curtailed the discussion, which was most practical and interesting.

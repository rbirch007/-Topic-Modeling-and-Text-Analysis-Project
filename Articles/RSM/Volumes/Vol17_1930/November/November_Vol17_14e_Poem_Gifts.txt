Gifts; By Claire Stewart Boyer In omniscient wisdom God sits on His throne Extending His gifts from the sky, And we wondering mortals in constant parade Looking upward, pass silently by.

And some hearts unseeing go hopelessly on, Bound fast to the earth and its ways; And some see the joys and the sorrows of life Like banners in vibrant arrays.

One plucks at a treasure with great greedy hands And then toying it, throws it aside, Another holds sacred a heaven-sent grief That God to his brother denied.. So each in his turn with the light of his faith Determines his share of God's grace And up past His treasures of sorrow and joy Is the all knowing smile of His face.

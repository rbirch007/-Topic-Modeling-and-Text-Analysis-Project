Between Anniversaries

Ethel Jacobson

versartes Ethel Jacobson I need no special day to store Sweet memories, to love you more.
This marks no milestone set apart By troth first plighted, heart to heart, No blessed date we ringed in red As heaven smiled, and we were wed. No anniversary is this, To bring a rose, an extra kiss. But every day the tale's the same: Clearer, brighter burns the flame, Burns and glows. So it goes.
Years pass; love grows.

[Vol45 | November 1958 | Sequence: 21 | Category: Poem | Title: On the Rim | Author: Christie Lund Coles]
On the Rim Christie Lund Coles Here below me is a world of colors— Vermilion, strawberry, pomegranate red; Saffron, umber, and chrysanthemum yellow; Heliotrope, lavender, wisteria, and the blue Of robin's eggs, and blue delphiniums; And colors for which there are no names As there are no words for this scene on scene Carved by the hand of water and of time Before years or water had a name; Before man stood, trembling here, as I Looking down into an eagle's nest. From the far floor down the bird ascends, Up up higher and ever higher Toward the rim... This is the ultimate This is the gossamer sky to him, But above the sky Stand I.

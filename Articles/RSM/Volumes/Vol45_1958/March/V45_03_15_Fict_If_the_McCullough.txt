6.  When  materials  such  as  corduroy, 
velvet,  or  velveteen,  which  have  a  definite 
up  and  down,  are  used,  it  is  necessary 
that  all  pattern  pieces  are  turned  in  the 
same  direction.  That  is,  the  top  of  each 
section  must  be  toward  the  same  end  of 
the  fabric.  The  same  care  must  be  taken 
when  cutting  printed  fabrics  whose  de- 
sign has  an  up  and  down.

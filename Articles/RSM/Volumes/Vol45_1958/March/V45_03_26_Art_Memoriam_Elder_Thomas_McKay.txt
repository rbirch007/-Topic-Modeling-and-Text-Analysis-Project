of  the  truth,  and  the  State  a  valued,

Brother  McKay  was  the  son  of 
David  and  Jeannette  Evans  McKay 
and  was  born  in  Huntsville,  Utah, 
October  29,  1875.  His  parents  were 
converts  to  the  Church,  his  father 
having  come  from  Scotland,  his 
mother  from  Wales.  He  was  a 
brother  of  our  beloved  President 
David  O.  McKay  and  between  these 
two  there  was  an  affectionate  bond 
comparable  to  that  of  Jonathan  and 
David  of  old.  Brother  McKav  often

Ogden  Valley  and  stalwarts  in  the 
Church.  The  son,  Thomas,  having 
come  from  such  excellent  parents 
reflected  throughout  his  life  their 
integrity  and  strength  of  character. 
He  served  as  a  missionary,  stake 
president,  twice  President  of  the 
Swiss-German  Mission,  and  acting 
president  of  the  European  Mission 
during  World  War  II.  At  the  April 
Conference  in  1941,  he  was  called 
to  be  one  of  the  Assistants  to  the 
Quorum  of  the  Twelve. 
In  his  ministry  throughout  the

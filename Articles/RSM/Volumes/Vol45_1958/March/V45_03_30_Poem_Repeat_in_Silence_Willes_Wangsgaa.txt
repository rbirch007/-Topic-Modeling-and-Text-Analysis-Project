Repeat in Silence
Eva Willes W angsgaard

A pitcher fashioned like an ear of corn
Holds life within Japonica s bright gift
Of crimson beauty where loosed petals drift
On wisdom from St. Paul, the Roman-born.
Red springtime from the Orient, the gold
Of Indian maize, ceramic artistry,
All blended in symbolic unity
Repeat in silence here the brief we hold:
If hands that love these blossoms in the East
Clasped red man's and white artist's, too, to find
That charity, which suffers long, is kind,
They would discover human strife had ceased.

tle problems  for  them  all.  Girls,  as 
well  as  boys,  felt  his  strength  and 
influence  for  good,  here  in  the  house 
on  Juniper  Street. 
Kristine  liked  going  to  the  mar- 
ket. She  liked  to  cook  and  serve 
the  delicious  meals  without  formal- 
ity or  pretentiousness.  It  was  fun, 
sharing  time  and  hospitality  with 
old  neighbors.

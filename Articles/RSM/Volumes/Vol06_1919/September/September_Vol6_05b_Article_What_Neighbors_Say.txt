What Neighbors Say
======================================================================

WHAT NEIGHBORS SAY. If you are regular in attending church, you are too pious. If you don't attend church, you are on the road to perdition. If you send your family away for the summer, it is more than you can afford. If you don't, you are stingy. If you happen to be successful in business, you achieved success by swindling.

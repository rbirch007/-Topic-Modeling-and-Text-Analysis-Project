Many quilts pieced and quilted by Sister Lewis have been donated to the Relief Society, and she has worked on hundreds of others in company with her sisters in Relief Society meetings. 'Busy fingers make happy hearts," is a philosophy of life that has meant much to Sister Lewis, for even when she is bedfast, her fingers can still ply the needle and her stitches are small and even — measured in beauty. Sister Lewis is an expert at hand and machine sewing, and at one time she owned a loom and wove many beautiful rugs. She has served for many years in various official capacities in Relief Society, contributing much time and effort towards the success of the work meeting program.

Howell, President, San Jose Stake Relief Society, reports that former presidents of San Jose First Ward Relief Society and also the eldest sisters in the ward were honored guests at the banquet. 'The theme for the evening was ""The Very Young and Young at Heart." One hundred seventy-six attended. A most unique program was presented by both the young and the old. Yesteryear fashions of five eras were modeled by mothers and daughters. "Since the San Jose Ward was organized from the California Mission in 1935, five wards and a dependent Spanish branch have grown out of the San Jose First Ward.

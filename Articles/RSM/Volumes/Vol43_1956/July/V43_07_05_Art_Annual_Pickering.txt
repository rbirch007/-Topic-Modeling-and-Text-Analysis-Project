Since  Tucson  is  a  square  dancing

Since  1940,  the  movie  companies 
have  moved  back  to  Old  Tucson  on 
several  occasions.  In  1954  they 
came  back  for  the  Cinemascope 
production  of  Greer  Garson's 
"Strange  Lady  in  Town."  They 
completely  remodeled  Old  Tucson. 
They  covered  some  of  the  adobe

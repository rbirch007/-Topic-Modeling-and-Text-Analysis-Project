our  Relief  Society  home  shall  en- 
rich the  lives  and  lead  toward  the 
eternal  well-being  of  the  daughters 
of  our  Heavenly  Father.  We  pray 
that  the  new  building  may  facilitate 
the  work  of  our  divinely  inspired, 
Priesthood-directed  society,  and  en- 
able the  sisters  to  serve  effectively 
as  the  handmaids  of  the  Priesthood 
in  the  great  work  of  the  Church. 
That  the  choice  blessings  of  the 
Lord  may  ever  rest  upon  Relief  So- 
ciety, upon  this  home  which  we 
dedicate  today,  and  upon  each  of  us 
I  sincerely  pray.

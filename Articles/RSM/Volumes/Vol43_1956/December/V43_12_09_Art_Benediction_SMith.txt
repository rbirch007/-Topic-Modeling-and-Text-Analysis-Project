[Vol43 | December 1956 | Sequence: 09 | Category: Article | Title: Benediction SMith]
'The benediction will be offered by President Joseph Fielding Smith, President of the Council of the Twelve and an advisor to the general board of Relief Society. The Singing Mothers chorus and congregation sang "The Spirit of God." (Benediction—WDedicatory Service PRESIDENT JOSEPH FIELDING SmiITH Of the Council of the Twelve €O) on Father in heaven, we have now come to the conclusion of these inspired services—the dedication of the building for the Relief Societies. We pray thee, Father, that thy spirit may rest upon these sisters who will occupy this building. They have done a wonderful work from the days of the Prophet until now. Be with those who preside, the members of their general board, that they may go forth in the power of thy spirit, building up and strengthening the sisters of the Church. Guide them, we pray thee, by the counsels of the Holy Priesthood, and in their labors by the guidance of thy Holy Spirit. Help them and direct them in all things in their wonderful labors in mercy and in wisdom. We bless them, our Father, and pray that thy spirit may be with them constantly, that they may appreciate in the fullness the privileges which are theirs, in the name of the Lord, Jesus Christ. Amen.

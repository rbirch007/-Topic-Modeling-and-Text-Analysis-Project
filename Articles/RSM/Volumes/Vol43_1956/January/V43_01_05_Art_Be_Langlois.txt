Be still, my heart, and listen! *Note: For biographical sketches of the award winners in the Eliza R. Snow Poem Contest, see page 53. O-<pP-2 Threshold Catherine E. Berry In a few brief moments the bells will ring Proclaiming the old year has gone, A requiem for the memories that cling, A salute to the coming dawn. Here in this small space of time we review The blessing the new year imparts, The hopes and the promises life will renew, The vision of dreams for our hearts. On the threshold of time we say a prayer For all of the days that will be, May we follow the star that is gleaming out there, To the goal of our destiny.

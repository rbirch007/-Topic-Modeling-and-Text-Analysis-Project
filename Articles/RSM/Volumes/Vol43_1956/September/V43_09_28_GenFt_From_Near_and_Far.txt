I  want  to  tell  you  that  the  Magazine 
has  been  a  blessed  spiritual  messenger  in 
our  home  while  we  have  been  in  this  far- 
off  land.  We  have  used  its  messages  in 
our  lesson  work,  in  our  talks,  and  in  our 
family  hours.  We  all  read  the  stories  and 
the  poems  and  love  every  one.  We  have 
been  holding  our  own  little  Church  sen- 
ices  here,  as  we  arc  the  only  L.  D.  S. 
family  in  Tabriz. 
— Lillic  Argylc 
Tabriz,  Iran

We  visited  one  family,  and  one  of  the 
first  things  the  woman  asked  was:  "How 
can  I  get  The  Relief  Society  Magazine?" 
She  said  repeatedly  how  much  she  has 
enjoyed  the  material  contained  in  this 
little  Magazine.  We  tried  to  interest  them 
in  some  other  Church  literature,  but  all 
they  want  now  is  your  little  Magazine. 
— Elder  Harold  S.  Barnes,  Jr. 
Cattaragus  Indian  Reservation 
New  York

live.

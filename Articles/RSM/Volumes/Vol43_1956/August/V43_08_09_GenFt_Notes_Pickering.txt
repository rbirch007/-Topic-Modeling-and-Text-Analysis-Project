RELIEF  SOCIETY  ACTIVITIES 
Photograph  submitted  by  Veda  F.  Moss 
RENO  STAKE    (NEVADA),   SPARKS   EAST  WARD  ANNIVERSARY   PARTY 
March  17,  1956 
Front  row,  left  to  right:  Thelda  Martin,  Second  Counselor;  Mildred  Ballingham, 
President;  Lois  Hauder,  First  Counselor;  Ethel  Marr  Ball,  Secretary;  Agnes  Kenfield, 
Magazine  representative;  Christine  Richins,  work  meeting  leader. 
Back  row,  left  to  right:  Rose  Burgess,  visiting  teacher  message  leader;  Jean  Edgar, 
work  meeting  leader;  Gladys  Grieve,  theology  class  leader;  June  Hancock,  chorister; 
Emma  Lou  Belnap,  literature  class  leader;  Delores  Brown,  organist. 
Veda  F.  Moss,  President,  Reno  Stake  Relief  Society,  reports:  "The  beautiful  table 
decorations  were  made  by  Christine  Richins.  The  dolls,  in  costumes  of  pastel  shades, 
were  used  to  depict  different  phases  of  Relief  Society  work.  On  this  delightful  occasion 
a  delicious  dinner  was  served  for  all  Relief  Society  members  and  their  partners.  A  pro- 
gram and  games  were  then  enjoyed."

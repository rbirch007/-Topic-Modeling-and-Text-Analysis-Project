HAPPY NEW YEAR
Lucy May Green
Happy, glad New Year, my friends, Full of joyous cheer! Blessings may it bring to you, Happy, glad New Year!
Greetings, brothers, sisters, true, Friends both far and near, Happiness, my wish for you, Happy, glad New Year!
Peace and love attend your way, Hope be ever near, Light up the darkest day Through the glad New Year.

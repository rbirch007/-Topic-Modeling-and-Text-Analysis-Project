NOW IS THE TIME Annie G. Lauritzen, Oakland, Calif. Now is the time to write and to rhyme Serious thoughts in a serious way; Now is the time to work and to think, To think, to work, and to faithfully pray. Now is the time to say a good ward, Put kindness and hope in the words we say, Help lighten the burden of all oppressed Whom we chance to meet on life's highway. Now is the time to do a good deed, If you'd be liked and loved some day; Now is the time to scatter the seed, For flowers of love that grow in love's way.

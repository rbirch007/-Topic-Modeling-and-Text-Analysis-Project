[Vol10 | September 1923 | Sequence: 01 | Category: Poem | Title: Tapestries]

Tapestries Grace Ingles Frost As to and fro my needle flies, A canvas close to weave With sheen of threads that harmonize, My mind does oft achieve The art more radiant in hue, For it weaves tapestries of you. No flower that ever blooms to fade, Can vie with colors that are laid Across the fibres of my heart, With rhythmic touch and blend, And when complete in every part, I view my work, O Friend! Fain would I find a thread more true, To weave my tapestries of you.

Keep faces and hands in the picture clean of the antique glaze by keeping them wiped off with a cloth moistened with thinner, so they will not be darkened. Let dry. 7. Begin varnishing again, and varnish over entire surface. When imperfections show on surface, sand with 400 sandpaper, with three to five coats between sandings. Varnish will need straining through a nylon hose for the last third of the can and will need a little thinning toward the end. 8. Sand evenly and_ gently as directed, until there is no shine at all.

 Lora Ann Gifford Christensen, Springdale, Utah, Zion Park Stake, has been sewing and making lovely articles of handwork since she was twelve years old. Much of the clothing she has sewed has been created through her own ingenuity. She makes patterns herself. Her most recent undertakings have been making miniature and regular sized hats for tourists. She has averaged making twenty quilts a year for more than twenty-five years. Her family of eleven children, thirty-six grandchildren, and twenty-eight great-grandchildren have received many items of clothing and quilts from her.

I was on the verge of tears when, suddenly, I saw the parchment paper, its contents splattered every which way in a mass of yellow ruin. The tears that had gathered close to my eyelids flowed over the edge and down my face. "Good old expensive stuff," I cried, as I saw that only a small amount of butter remained on the paper.

[81 Listen to Silence I Think of Beaches Pearle M. Olsen]

LISTEN TO SILENCE Listen to silence: Let its silver spill Into the tired spirit, And the uncertain will. Find the deep, cool well Of darkness where it sleeps, Renew yourself from the pound Of noise the city keeps. Listen to silence: Let it mother you so You will Know who you are, And where you are to go. Listen to silence: lt can heal your pain; It can lift you upward, And make you whole again. Christie Lund Coles The Cover: 5 Jupiter Columns at Baalbek, Lebanon

Have an annual check-up and watch for the seven warning signals: Unusual bleeding or discharge A lump or thickening in the breast or elsewhere A sore that does not heal. Change in bowel or bladder habits Hoarseness or cough Indigestion or difficulty in swallowing Change in size or color of a wart or mole _ HELP THE AMERICAN CANCER SOCIETY'S PROGRAMS OF RESEARCH! THE LIFE YOU SAVE MAY BE YOUR OWN.

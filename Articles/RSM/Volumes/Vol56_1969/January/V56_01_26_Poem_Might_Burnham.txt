[Vol56 | January 1969 | Page 50 | Poem | Might | Gladys Hesser Burnham]
MIGHT = God's might encompasses our world — Enfolding each of us in love. If only we unveil our eyes = To know the truth. A home above Awaits the trusting soul who sees = That God protects the least of these. = —Gladys Hesser Burnham = QLICY SYCYUCYVCVCIYCVE!

Spafford, General President of Relief Society, in June was given special honor and a gold pendant in recognition of her leadership in supporting the March of Dimes program of the National Foundation, which sponsors clinical research, a nationwide network of Birth Defects Centers, professional and public education, and other well organized and important activities. In presenting the pendant to President Spafford, Mrs. Elaine Whitelaw, National Director of Volunteer Service, emphasized the influence and leadership of President Spafford in writing letters, promoting programs, and supporting a wide publicity for the Foundation. Mrs. Lucretia M. Evans, President, Bonneville Stake Relief Society, and State Director of Volunteer Services, sponsored the special honor for President Spafford. Mrs. LaVern W. Parmley, President of the Primary Association of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, who, last year was named a member of the National Scouting Committee, has been named a member at large of the National Council of Boy Scouts.

Chapter  4 
SYNOPSIS:  Mary  Ann  and  Kim  Freeman 
have  taken  Kim's  aged  Father  from  his 
home  in  Mayville,  Utah,  to  their  home 
in  Los  Angeles  to  care  for  him  after  an 
accident.  Mary  Ann  has  submitted  her 
resignation  as  a  teacher  in  the  grade 
school.  This  will  cut  their  income  in  half 
at  the  time  of  their  children  Rosemary's 
wedding,  and  Tommy's  graduation.

who  responded  with  a  squeeze  of

the  scholarship  my  dean  has 
been  trying  to  thrust  upon  me 
for  the  last  two  quarters,  on  the 
basis  of  need." 
"Ha!"  said  Rosemary.  "What

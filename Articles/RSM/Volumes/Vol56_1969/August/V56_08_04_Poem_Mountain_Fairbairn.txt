oS) MOUNTAIN NAMES For mountain flowers, trees, and birds We need the world's most lovely words. Brodiaea, lilies, gentians, clover Are like a song to linger over. Tamarack, fir, and cedar... These Are sounds that label snow-line trees. Flickers, warblers, chickadees, And jays have winged names that please.

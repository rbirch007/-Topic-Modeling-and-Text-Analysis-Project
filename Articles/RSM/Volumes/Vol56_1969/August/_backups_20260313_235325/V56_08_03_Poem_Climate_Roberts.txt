Roberts Dick Scopes Mary Scopes 561 I have read the serial ''Welcome the Task'' by Michele Bartmess (concluded in the June Magazine) and enjoyed it very much. I have also enjoyed seeing the lovely poems by Pearle M. Olsen (see the Magazine for April and June 1969, and other issues). Sister Olsen's poetry gives me inspiration to keep writing myself. Wilma Morley Despain, Centerfield, Utah I would like to tell you how much I have enjoyed my year's subscription to the wonderful Relief Society Magazine. I have been a member of the Church abouf two years. I am happy that my husband has renewed my subscription to the Magazine, and I hope I don't miss any of the wonderful stories and articles in the future. I first became acquainted with the Magazine by receiving a copy from a Latter-day Saint woman, and now I am always waiting for the next issue to come. I have never enjoyed any Magazine as much as I have this one. Jean Smith, Le Roy, Illinois Welcome Relief Society Magazine! How I have missed you the past three months. Not your fault, I know, but what a joy it was to see you back in my mail box again and to know that once more my friend had arrivedthis time three together. How wonderful our membership in ReliefSociety is.

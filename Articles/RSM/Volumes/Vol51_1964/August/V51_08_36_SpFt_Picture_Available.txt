1964-65 Theology Lessons Available

A picture kit for use as a visual aid in presenting the theology lessons for 1964-65 is available at the Deseret Book Company, 44 East South Temple, Salt Lake City, Utah 84111, price $1.00, plus 10¢ for postage. The kit consists of black and white pictures of the Prophet Joseph Smith, Newel K. Whitney, Lorenzo Snow, Wilford Woodruff, Orson Hyde, and Joseph F. Smith; and colored pictures of Oliver Cowdery, Sidney Rigdon, Frederick G. Williams, and Edward Partridge.

The Shadowfall

Shirley M. Howard

Once earth
Called to me,
"Be a tree,
Be a tree."
Acorn—I answered
With a leaf,
A stem,
And a brief
Root shooting down;
Sending
A young trunk
Bending in the wind,
And a
New limb strong
Enough for just a bird
And a song.

Now I
Have heard
A thousand songs,
Stirred
In a thousand winds
And a
Thousand leaves
Wave
Through my
Branches.
My roots
Lie deep
And I keep
Thinking
That when
One is tall
The shadowfall
Is long
Upon
An afternoon.

The Christmas Tree Wears ROSES

Green pine tree, flocked with artificial snow. The roses are made of tissue paper in light blue and pale blue and dark and light green. The roses are arranged in various groupings, using different combinations of color tints in each group. They provide an inexpensive trimming. Christmas tree lights are blue and green.

Cannon children in the picture: Ann, seated on the floor; Carolyn; Allison.

All end up with approximately the same results. Relief Society presidents probably appreciate their families more than any other women on earth—because their moments are so precious. Each day is a challenge, to accomplish the impossible and still be home in time to meet that 2:45 school bus. All Relief Society presidents are most comfortable on their knees.

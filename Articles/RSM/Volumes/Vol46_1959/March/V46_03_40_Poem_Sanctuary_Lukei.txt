Vesta N. Lukei
We know
Two wheel
tracks worn
Like furrows
faint and brown and
rutted
Amid the
hillside weeds
And new
spring
green.
We know
This
short,
steep road
That ends by lichen-covered rocks
And weathered pasture fence.
We overlook a gentle valley
In
the
curve
of
hills.
We know,
Always and over
all.
The benediction
of
sky.
We have been here before.
We
shall come again.

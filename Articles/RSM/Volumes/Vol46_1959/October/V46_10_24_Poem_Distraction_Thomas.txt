Distraction

Winona F. Thomas

ctraction Winona F. Thomas I went to paint an autumn scene With canvas, paints and brush.
Only the music of the stream Disturbed the canyon's hush. I meant to do a rustic hut Beneath an old oak tree, But a squirrel in search of nuts Came and posed for me.

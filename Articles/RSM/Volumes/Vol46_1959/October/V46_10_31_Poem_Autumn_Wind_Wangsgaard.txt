Autumn Wind

Eva Willes Wangsgaard

from it. Autumn Wind Eva Willes Wangsgaard Now bountifully autumn brings A wealth of hours newly shined, While days rush by on painted wings And leave a lonesome sound behind. Birthday Congratulations Ninety-three Mrs. Exiza

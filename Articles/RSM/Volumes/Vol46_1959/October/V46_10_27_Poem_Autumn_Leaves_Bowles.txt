Autumn Leaves

Katherine B. Bowles

ldren. ~~ Autumn Leases Katherine B. Bowles Painted leaves upon the hillside, Brilliant shades of every hue, Reflect the beauty of the autumn— Transient colors pass in review.
Painted by: the artist paint brush Guided by the master hand, All their many tinted colors Made to beautify the land.

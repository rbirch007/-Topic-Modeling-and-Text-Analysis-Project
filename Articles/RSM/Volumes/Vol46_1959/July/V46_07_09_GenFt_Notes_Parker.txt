Sister Erekson
reports:
"This branch,
Parramatta,
used
to be Harris
Park.
They
now have a
lovely chapel, and these
sisters
are running
a
store,
taking
turns each
day,
to pay
for
it.
They are a very energetic group, and many
of
these women
spent days
of hard work on
the
grounds
while
the
building was
being
built.
They
all
have
a
strong
testimony
of the Church and
of Rehef
Society work.
This
small branch was
organized August
18,
1957, and
has grown
steadily
ever
since,
until now
they
have
161 members."
Edith
Josephine Moore
is
the new
president
of
the
Australian
Mission
Relief
Society.

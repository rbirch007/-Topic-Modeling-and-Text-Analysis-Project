Sun in Bloom

Eva Willes Wangsgaard
 Oh, do you know the Doronicum— The early daisy that holds the sun Of all the goldenness born of petal, Of summer sun, and precious metal, Its gleaming radii purest yellow? Uncertain air is bound to mellow, And every corner the daffodils Have left unsunned this daisy fills With radiant rising suns of gold 'Till spring is set in a sunny mold.

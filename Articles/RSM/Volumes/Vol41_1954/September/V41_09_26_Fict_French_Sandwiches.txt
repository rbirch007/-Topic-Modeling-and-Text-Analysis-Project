Mother Clara Horne Park Courtesy National Cotton Council French, Yoasted Ham Sandwiches 12 slices day-old bread. 14 @ mk shortening for spreading 3. 'tbsp. Flour baked or boiled ham Ys tbsp. Salt mustard shortening for frying 3 €8gs Make sandwiches by spreading slices of bread lightly with shortening. Spread every other slice with ham and mustard to taste. Cover with plain slices. Cut sandwiches in halves diagonally, making triangles. Make batter for dipping as follows: in shallow pan, such as cake pan, beat eggs with fork. Mix flour into thin paste with a little of the milk. Add to eggs, with rest of milk and salt. Mix well. Dip sandwiches, first one side then the other, in batter. Be careful not to let them soak. Fry in hot skillet or on hot griddle, turning sandwiches once to brown both sides. Serve hot. Makes six servings, > Cale.

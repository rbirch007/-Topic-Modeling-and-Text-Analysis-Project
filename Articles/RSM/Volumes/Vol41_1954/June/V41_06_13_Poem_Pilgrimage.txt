-—- Pilgrimage Ida Isaacson I have been on a pilgrimage, And would you ask 'me where? It has been to the home of a daughter To help her and to share The joy of being at a crib-side And to watch the hours unfold— Be a part of the sweetest story That has ever yet been told, Page 394 vr \\ FROM THE FIELD I~Z Margaret C.

<>< After Long Years Beatrice Knowlton Ekman After long years of questing to and fro, This homely kitchen is a restful place; The smell of buming pine, the rudy glow Of firelight upon each loved one's face. Let me find anchor here and end my quest In this old house that memory has blessed.

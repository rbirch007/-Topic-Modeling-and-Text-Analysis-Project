"I Will Lift Up Mine Eyes" (Psalms 121:1)
Christie Lund Coles

The crags are hewn against the autumn sky,
Their color heightened by each baring tree;
The quivering aspens which have reached the high
Place of the pine, despite their frailty,
Accent the lonely skyline, and the hawk
Swoops and cries, sensing the wind and snows,

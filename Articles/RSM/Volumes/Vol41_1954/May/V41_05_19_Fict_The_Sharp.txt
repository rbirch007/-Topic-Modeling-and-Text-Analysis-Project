Regarding this inspirational occasion, Sister Ferrin writes: "At our January union meeting we presented the very impressive special program which was given at our October Relief Society conference. We divided the program into speaking, choral groups, and Singing Mothers. We assigned different wards to do certain parts of these. We invited all officers and class leaders and all visiting teachers and anyone else who wished to attend, including the members of the Priesthood. The program was very well attended and well received. We feel that all who saw it had an increased appreciation of our heritage." Mata'aho, Princess and wife of the Crown Prince, Tungi, opened the bazaar, and then it was opened to the general public, who... Bought every article in the house in less than an hour.

RELIEF SOCIETY ACTIVITIES mdi Photograph submitted by Ione Loveland MINIDOKA STAKE (IDAHO), PAUL WARD RELIEF SOCIETY VISITING TEACHERS ACHIEVE ONE HUNDRED PER CENT RECORD FOR TEN YEARS Ione Loveland, President, Paul Ward Relief Society, reports that in 1944 this ward achieved one hundred per cent visiting teaching. "Every month since then this has been maintained. So, in January 1954, we had a ten-year record of unbroken one hundred per cent visiting teaching. We are very proud and grateful to our teachers. Sister Clarinda Green was president when the achievement was first made. In 1948 Sister Ione Loveland became president, with Zola Hunsaker as first counselor and Rhoda Barclay as second counselor, and Grace Merrill as secretary.

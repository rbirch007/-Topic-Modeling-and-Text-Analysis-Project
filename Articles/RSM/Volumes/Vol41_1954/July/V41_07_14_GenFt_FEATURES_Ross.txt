Cleona  W.  Hedenstrom  reports  the  success  of  this  unique  apron  project:  "Sister 
Stratford  had  a  large  coverall  apron  which  many  of  the  sisters  liked.  The  pattern 
was  one  which  needed  to  be  sized  to  the  individual,  so  we  asked  all  those  sisters  who 
wished  to  make  this  apron  to  bring  their  material  to  the  February  work  meeting.  There 
a  pattern  was  cut  to  fit  each  sister,  and  each  was  assisted  in  cutting  out  her  apron.

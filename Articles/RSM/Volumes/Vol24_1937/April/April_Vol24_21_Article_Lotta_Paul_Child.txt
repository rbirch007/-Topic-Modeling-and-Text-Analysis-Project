[Vol24 | April 1937 | Sequence: 21 | Category: Article | Title: Lotta Paul Child]

Lotta Paul Baxter makes no claim of noteworthy attainment, but to the writer of this article her life is beautiful. Goethe, who was educated to judge the good, the true and the beautiful said, "My teacher taught me that the ideal of beauty is simplicity and repose, hence it follows that no youth can be a master." She is a master. "Strait is the gate, and narrow is the way which leadeth unto life, and few there be that find it."

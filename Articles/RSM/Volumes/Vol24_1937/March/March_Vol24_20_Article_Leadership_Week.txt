Leadership Week
YX/'E
congratulate
the
Brigham
Young University on the suc-
cess of the 1937 Leadership Week.
President
Franklin
S.
Harris and
Harrison R. Merrill were outstand-
ing
in extending hospitality, seeing
that
everything
moved
smoothly,
that all were comfortable and happy,
and that every
facility of the
insti-
tution was available to their guests.
The program was as always, very
fine.
One wished she could be
in
several sections every period.
The
hundreds who attended returned to
their
homes
enlightened,
inspired,
enthused.
We are
all indebted to the Brig-
ham Young University for this won-
derful period when our vision is en-
larged, our
intellect quickened and
our
spirits

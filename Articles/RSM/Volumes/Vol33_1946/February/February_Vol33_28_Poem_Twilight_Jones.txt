TWILIGHT
Ada  N.  /ones
Twilight — faint  blush  of  pink  on  blue  mountain.
The  sun's  golden  fingers  withdrawn  from  the  sky;
A  deep  settled  hush  on  the  murmuring  fountain-
A  silence,  a  promise,  a  prayer,  and  a  sigh.

THE WEIGHT OF YOUR CLOUD Edna S. Dustin The weight of your cloud is far away Until it shadows you.
Then it's as near as it seemed far, Its weight can crush you where you are If you let it; its strong folds Can strangle you with talon holds. ? weight of your cloud is far away Unless it shadows you. It may shrink your vision to somber light; Your soul will expand as far as your sight; Though it's as near as it seemed far, You still can't touch it where you are. The weight of your cloud is far away Unless it shadows you.

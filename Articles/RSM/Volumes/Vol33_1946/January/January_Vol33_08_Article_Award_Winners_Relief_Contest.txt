There  were  sixty-seven  poems  sub- 
mitted in  this  year's  contest,  entries 
coming  from  many  of  the  states,  as 
well  as  from  Alaska,  Canada,  Au- 
stralia, and  England.  The  General 
Board  congratulates  the  prize  win- 
ners and  expresses  appreciation  to  all 
entrants  for  their  interest  in  the  con- 
test and  the  general  excellence  of 
the  work  submitted. 
The  Board  wishes,  also,  to  thank 
the  three  judges  for  their  care  and 
diligence  in  selecting  the  prize-win- 
ning poems.  The  assistance  of  the 
poetry  committee  of  the  General 
Board  is  very  much  appreciated.

The Settlement of Sanpete Valley (Continued from page 377) Sanpitch River. Two years later about fifteen Welsh families settled on the beautiful spot, and named the colony Wales in honor of their native country. Settlements began to dot the Valley from north to south, homes were built, the problems of supplying the necessities of life became less hazardous, and the settlers found time to turn their attention to new projects and cultural pursuits which augured well for the future of Sanpete Valley.

OFFERINGS
Etta S. Robbins

Thanks from our hearts we give
For blessings small and great,
For homes and ties that bind
In this estate.

For liberty, we still maintain—
That heritage sublime;
For faith in him of Calvary,
Our Lord divine.

O Thou, who freely ministers,
Supplying all our needs,
Accept the offerings we bring
In words and deeds.

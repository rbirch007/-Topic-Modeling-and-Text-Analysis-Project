[Vol26 | September 1939 | Sequence: 12 | Category: Article | Title: Grand Allen]
Like a flash, Martha turned her head. This was no time for scars.
The bandages descended. "It's going to be a side view, Martha, when I paint your portrait," said Johnny. "The profile of a grand lady!"
Martha scrambled toward the door in confusion. Dr. Tolman followed, laughing. By now, Nona's hands were back of Johnny's head, drawing him close. And as Martha took one backward look, she saw his arms go tightly about his girl, as he felt the radiance within her.
Out in the hall, Martha stopped for one dazed moment. "Glory be," she whispered in awed tones. "A profile portrait of me! And the way he said it, it's going to be good!"

Work and Business Department Lalene H. Hart, Chairman
ONE feature of the Work and Business Department was an exhibit showing what has been and can be accomplished in the field of nutrition. A demonstration of food preparation and preservation proved very attractive as well as educational. The stakes participating under the direction of Mrs. Ivy L. Hall were Highland, Cottonwood, Wells, Grant, Oquirrh, West Jordan and East Jordan.

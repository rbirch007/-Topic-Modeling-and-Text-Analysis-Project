Spiritualized Education
In keeping with the ideals of its founder, Brigham Young, the Church University is dedicated to the work of providing the youth of Zion an education which ennobles the spirit and the heart and trains the hand and the mind. This kind of education prepares young people for the responsibilities of citizenship in both the Church and the nation.
Thirty-six departments of the University offer courses leading to success in more than a hundred vocations. Congenial social environment of the University campus adds further to the value of educational experience there.

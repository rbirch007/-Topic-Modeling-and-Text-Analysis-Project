[Josephine Ortiz Makes Cloth Dolls for Fun and for Profit]

JOSEPHINE ORTIZ, FRESNO, CALIFORNIA, MAKES CLOTH DOLLS FOR FUN—AND FOR PROFIT JQPHINE Ortiz has a natural gift for making and dressing cloth dolls. She gives them beauty and personality, as well as color and style. The doll which Mts.
Ortiz is holding is named "Juanita," and was sent to. A little gitl in Provo, Utah.
There the doll created so much interest among the Primary children that an inquiry was made regarding the pattern. A large department store in Albuquerque, New Mexico, gave an order for two dozen dolls last fall and has given other orders for more dolls to grace the 1949 holiday season. In the "Talents" project of the Spanish-American Mission, Sister Ortiz earned $66 for her Relief Society and received much joy and satisfaction in the use of her unusual gift. >

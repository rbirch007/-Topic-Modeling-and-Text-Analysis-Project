Sunset Art Pearle M. Ohen Street lights Vesta N. Fairbairn Crimson velvet mountains Brushed with blues in Shadowed indentations; Nature's varied markings — Valley, vale, and crevice. Time for evening meditations. Bergundy the velvet — Indigo the blues, as Color-throwing sunset rays Hurl deepest hues On plushy eastern mountains. Page the artist and the muse! Minutes quietly beget Slumberous peaks in silhouette; Velvet of the darkest jet.

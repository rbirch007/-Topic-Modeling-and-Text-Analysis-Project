[Vol50 | January 1963 | Page 17 | Special Feature | Celestia J. Taylor Appointed to the General Board Alice | L. Wilkinson]
She has held stake positions in all of the CELESTIA JOHNSON TAYLOR Church auxiliaries and has served as a ward Relief Society president. She was stake literature leader in the Fast Sharon Stake at the time of her appointment. She has also held important positions in community organizations and in university circles. She maintains a keen interest in reading, music, and drama, and is an accomplished knitter. Sister Taylor has a warm and gracious personality. Her lovely home radiates refinement and love for her children and grandchildren and a wide circle of friends. The work of Relief Society will go forward assisted by her many capabilities.

Two by Two Arline Cummings Two by two on a mission of love To reach out their arms to you, And we never can count the good they do As they travel two by two. Two by two, to the humble cot Or up to the mansion door — Does the sorrowing widow need them most Or the wealthy matron more? Two by two as friends they come, And their message is truth and light To show the way to celestial joy And teach us the Savior's might. Two by two on a mission of love To reach out their arms to you, And we never will know the good they do As they travel two by two.

Ist South I / Salt Lake City 11, Utah] The Old Gate Catherine B. Bowles Do memories linger, when at night You see an old gate now shut tight? It used to stand open, swing to and fro; Children ran through it with faces aglow. It stands as a symbol — life passes by; Enjoy each day where sweet memories lie. There is a straight path that leads to a gate That will be open, be it early or late — A welcoming smile where loved ones meet, Memories linger, precious and sweet.

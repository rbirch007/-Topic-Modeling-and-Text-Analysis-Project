the Home Churning Day Maude Rubin Slow-splashing rhythm, steady beat Of dasher... Metronome Of slow-paced music, honey-sweet, - Time riding clover foam A pantry window toward the creek — Biue mountain steep beyond; Green lace of leaves where robins speak; Mint-freshness; fern-fringed pond. Cool pantry shelves where shallow pans Hold yellow Guernsey cream, Blue-checkered gingham apron spans Grandmother's waist; each seam Starch-crisp and straight, its strings bow-tied Her crinkled smiles unfold — Then fold, as she takes care to hide Her pride in butter gold!

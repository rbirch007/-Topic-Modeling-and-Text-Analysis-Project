Grandson Grows n My Garden Summer Fancy Stitching for your daughters £ addition to providing a delightful sense of companionship between a mother and her young daughter, the learning of embroidery stitches provides an acquaintanceship with the handling of a needle and thread, the wearing of a thimble, the use of embroidery hoops, and the feel and texture of material. Also, a discriminating love of color and design may be fostered, and perhaps a handicraft skill that may be a lifetime joy can be initiated by a mother for her daughter through summer afternoons of love and stitching. Some accomplishments valuable to seamstresses can be first achieved by practice of embroidery stitches, such as buttonhole work, and the delightful basket-weave stitch which may become an asset in later mending and darning. It would bea good beginning if the first item chosen was a dish towel. The pattern can be simple, and mistakes are not so disastrous.

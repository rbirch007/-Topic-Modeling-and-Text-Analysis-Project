Recompense
By Mina Blake Oblad
Oh, thank you God
For this brief glimpse of Heav'n
This hour of recompense.
The long, hard months,
The hours of pain, are past
And in my arms, at last,
I hold my newborn babe.
I see the tiny head,
The perfect hands and feet
And feel that life is rich, complete.

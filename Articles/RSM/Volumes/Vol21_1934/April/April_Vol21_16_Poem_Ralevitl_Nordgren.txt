[Vol21 | April 1934 | Sequence: 16 | Category: Article | Title: Ralevitl Nordgren]

Would he choose your town? Beloved By Weston N. Nordgren Your dark eyes draw me, dearest girl— Your lips invite me near!
Your even teeth of whitest; pearl Are dazzling and clear!
Your flashing smile enraptures me! You're full of joy and fun! I thrill whene'er I think ot the Proud beauty I have won! Oh glorious bud of womanhood, Just bursting into flower— I love to feel your every mood— To see you every hour! To see your dark eyelashes close— To watch your dimpling chin— No slimmer hands are there than those You cup your young face in!

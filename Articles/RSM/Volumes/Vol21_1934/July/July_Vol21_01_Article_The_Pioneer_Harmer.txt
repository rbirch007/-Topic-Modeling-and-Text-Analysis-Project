c[[\e Pioneer eMother
By Mabel S. Harmer
he gathered her babes and her frugal store
For a homeland yet to be won.
With resolve in her heart and unfaltering step
She walked toward the setting sun.
he left the green valleys and limpid streams
For a desert bare and forlorn.
But through
peril, hardship and
toil she kept
Her eyes toward the
setting
sun.
he paused but to cheer a breaking heart,
To comfort an ailing one,
To offer a prayer to God for peace
In the land of the setting sun.

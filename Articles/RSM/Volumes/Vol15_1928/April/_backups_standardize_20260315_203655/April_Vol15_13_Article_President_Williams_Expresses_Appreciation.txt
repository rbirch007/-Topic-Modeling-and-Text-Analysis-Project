President Williams Expresses Appreciation
Many letters of sympathy and condolence have reached the home of President Clarissa Smith Williams in the interval since the passing of her husband, Senator William N. Williams. For all of these expressions of sympathy and love she is deeply grateful. Letters have come to her from far and near. It would take many months to answer these communications even if it were possible under any circumstances. Consequently, Sister Williams is taking this opportunity of expressing her heart-felt thanks to all who have extended the hand of comfort and blessing to her in her hour of trial.

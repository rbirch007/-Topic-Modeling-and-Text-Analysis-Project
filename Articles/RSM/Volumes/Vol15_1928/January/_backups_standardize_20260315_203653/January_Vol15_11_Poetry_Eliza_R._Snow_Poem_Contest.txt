Eliza Roxey Snow Poem Contest
It is our pleasure once again to announce the winners in the Eliza Roxey Snow Poem Contest. This is the fifth year of the contest and in some respects at least, it is the outstanding year. In 1923 there were 47 entries, in 1924, 46, in 1925, 50, in 1926, 54 and in 1927 there are 65 entries. This shows an increase of 18 entries over the first year and 11 over last year's contest. Yet what is even more gratifying than the interest indicated by an


increased number of contestants is the fact that no fewer than 15 of our recognized writers entered the 1927 contest.

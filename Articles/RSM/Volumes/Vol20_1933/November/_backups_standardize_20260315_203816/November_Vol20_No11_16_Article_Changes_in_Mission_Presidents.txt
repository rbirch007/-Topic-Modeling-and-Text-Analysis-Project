Changes in Mission Presidents FLLDER SAMUEL O. BENNION has been relieved from his duties as President of the Central States Mission to return home, and Elder Elias S. Woodruff has been appointed to succeed him. Elder Joseph J. Daynes has been appointed to preside over the Western States Mission and Elder LeGrand Richards to preside over the Southern States Mission. These brethren have had long experience in Church work and will be valued wherever they are are placed. May they be made equal to their task and may the work grow under their ministry.

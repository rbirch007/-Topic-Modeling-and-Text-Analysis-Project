Angels and Amazons

The Relief Society has been asked to cooperate with the General Authorities in the matter of dress. We are asked to use our influence to encourage the sisters to dress modestly and appropriately.

We are living in a day when fashion seems to have gone to extremes. Some of our young women are following the fashions of the world too closely. They are wearing clothes that are immodest and inappropriate for Latter-day Saint women.

We should remember that our bodies are temples of God, and we should dress them accordingly. We should dress modestly and appropriately at all times, but especially when we attend our meetings and go to the temple.

The Relief Society presidency asks that all Relief Society officers use their influence to encourage modest dress among the sisters. We should set a good example ourselves, and we should kindly counsel those who need guidance in this matter.

Let us remember that we are daughters of God, and we should dress as becomes saints. We should be angels, not amazons.

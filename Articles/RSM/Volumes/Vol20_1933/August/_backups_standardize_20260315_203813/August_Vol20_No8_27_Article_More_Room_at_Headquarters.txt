More Room at Headquarters
We are glad to tell our readers that we now have more room at our General Headquarters. The Social Welfare Department has been moved from the Bishop's Building to the Y Building where fine offices have been provided. The Burial Clothes Department has more room than heretofore so that better attention and finer service can be given its patrons. A much needed Committee Room is now available. A room has been prepared for Stake and Mission Relief Society Presidents where they may meet their friends, have their mail sent, and rest while they are in Salt Lake City. We deeply appreciate the courtesy the Church has extended in giving us adequate room for our needs.

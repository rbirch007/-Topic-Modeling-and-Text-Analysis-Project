as

American Prophet"

can
be
no doubt that his figure as times go

bulk
larger
and
larger
against the background of history,"
this treatise will therefore be widely

book
is
divided
into
three

;

:

traordinary Career ;" Second

;"

A Promise of Spring
By Tressa Love Hanson
The Winter has been long and dreary
The ground
is
still covered with snow
And yet as I gaze from my doorway
There's a promise of spring, soft and low.
There's a breeze coming up from the southland
That whispers through trees
tall and bare
And listen
! A meadowlark's singing
There's a promise of spring
in the
air.

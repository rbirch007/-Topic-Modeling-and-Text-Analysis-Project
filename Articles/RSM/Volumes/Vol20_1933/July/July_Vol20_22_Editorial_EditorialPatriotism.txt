Pioneer Ladies
By
Claire S. Boyer
Ladies linked with a pictured past,
In your silver hair we see disappointment, suffering,
grief and long anxiety.
In your eyes we read again, hope and courage
Sparkly beams of the faith your heart created
When you planned your
fairest dreams.
You are hallowed with the glory
And the strength of vibrant years,
And we joyously salute you,
Valiant, noble pioneers.

Alice Fielding Butterworth

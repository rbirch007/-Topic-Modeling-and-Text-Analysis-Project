President Robison Calls on Relief Society to Support N. R. A.
WE all remember those tragic periods of long and arduous labor so freely given by our women during the World War; and how we skimped and saved and worked to buy Liberty Bonds! Today our beloved country is facing a far more serious crisis than it met during the War. The best informed public servants, the greatest authorities in economics in the world, do not hesitate to say that the banking system has failed, that industry has failed, even education has failed to save us in the crisis. The only resource left to which we may look for solution is our Government. This can succeed only with the cooperation of the people. The Government can plan and supply the means the people must make these plans a reality by concerted action and whole-hearted support. The past called for one form of expression for love of country?the present demands another?but in spirit it is the same. In 1917-1918 it was patriotic to buy Liberty Bonds?it is just as patriotic today to support the N. R. A. In the name of our Relief Society organization we are calling you to rally to the support of our beloved country. To cooperate with the local committee in the study and enforcement of the provisions of the N. R. A. You have been tried in the past, and never been found wanting. We know we can depend upon your loyal support in the present.

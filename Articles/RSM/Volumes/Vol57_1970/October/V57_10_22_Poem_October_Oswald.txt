[October 21st, Marilyn M. Oswald]

Oswald LATE WORD 2 When the garnet and the gold of October yp em Leave that final gray word of all dead fire, wif Sinking to ember in a red leaf caught in the stubble , When all twigs blurr in a mist of mauve, 4 I have seen birds leave earth for the glory of the sky 4 Where clouds gather the memory of all seasons "I And swirl it into a rainbow turbulence over death, A vast hosanna of hues, a concert of color in the sky. Dorthy J.

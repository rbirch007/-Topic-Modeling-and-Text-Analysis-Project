[Vol57 | July 1970 | Page 522 | Poem | Wondering, Louise | S. Davis]
[Wondering, Louise S. Davis]
WONDERING Mother, when you were a girl, When you were just my age, Was 'lectricity invented? Were bustles all the rage? Mother, when you were a girl You had no T. V. Set! Tell me, then, did you and Daddy Dance the minuet? Louise S. Davis

[Vol57 | May 1970 | Page 398 | Poem | I Would be Flexible, Beulah | H. Sadleir]
Defying cold, immune To stinging rain, flowered Branches dripping fragility, Move, as winds sweep through Them from a frothy sea I would be flexible As is this tree, Sharing fruitful blessings, Quite unknowingly. —Beulah H. Sadleir

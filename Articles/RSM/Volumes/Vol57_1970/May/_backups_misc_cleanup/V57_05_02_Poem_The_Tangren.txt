The Magazine is not responsible for unsolicited manuscripts. 323 A BRAUTIFUL s a result of an emergency telephone call, my _ sister and I, after several hours of flying time, found ourselves walking down the corridor of the San Gabriel Community Hospital to our mother's room. Our brother greeted us. Mother did not know we were there. All night she was restless and occasionally muttered in tones barely audible. Towards morning she started to speak more clearly. As we listened, we could hear the words of the Twentythird Psalm. "The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want." We leaned over to hear "Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow..."

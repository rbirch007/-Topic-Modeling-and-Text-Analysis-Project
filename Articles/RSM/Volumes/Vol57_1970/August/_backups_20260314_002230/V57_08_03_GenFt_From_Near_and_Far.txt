I was just looking over the contents of the May Magazine when i I came across the

story "Whatever Will Become of Rory!" It just made me sit down and read it right

away. We have a son Rory who is nine years old, and the story, word for word, and

our son Rory are alike. The day the Magazine came the principal of the school had

just phoned to tell us Rory had been in some trouble. When we talked to him about

it he just said, "Mom, I really don't mean to get into trouble. It just happens." The

story made my husband and myself just laugh because we really felt the story had been

written about our Rory. Jolayne Hudson, Calgary, Alberta, Canada

ENGLISH TOFFEE COOKIES

1 cup butter
1 cup U AND I SUGAR
1 egg yolk
1 egg white, beaten
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups sifted flour
1 cup ground pecans

Cream butter and sugar thoroughly; add egg yolk (unbeaten) and beat well. Add vanilla, flour and one-half of the ground nuts. Spread dough thin on greased shallow pan, 10½ x 15 inches. Spread beaten egg white over top. Sprinkle with remaining cup of ground nuts. Bake in slow oven 300° for 45 min. Cut at once in small squares.

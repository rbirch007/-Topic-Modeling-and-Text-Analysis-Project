["Sunflowers" by Claude Monet]

If a lower centerpiece is desired, an arrangement on a table mirror adds reflected beauty. In a low bowl-shaped compote, or on a large plate, the fruit is ideal for living room tables. A thousand starry lights shimmer when the fruit is placed under a lamp. One of the appealing aspects of this fruit is its fragile and delicate appearance, yet in reality it is firm and durable.

(to be continued) 117 118 Counselor Marianne C. Sharp ® Relief Society Magazine representatives continued during 1969 to maintain the high subscription rate. The total number of subscriptions has reached 281,790, with the Spanish number increasing to 6,839, making a grand total of the number of subscriptions of The Relief Society Magazine of 288,629 as of August 1, 1969. At the last Relief Society Annual General Conference in a meeting with sisters from overseas speaking different languages, the most frequently expressed request was that The Relief Society Magazine could be translated into their own languages as it was in the Spanish. It is regretted that the limited number of members in the different language groups makes it financially impossible at this time. There are 238 stakes on the Honor Roll which made 100% and over, and sixty-eight stakes in which all wards exceeded 100%. The stake with the highest percentage in 1969 is Sydney Stake, Australia. Congratulations go to these sisters who have made the high record of 196%. There are 429 stakes on the Honor Roll in 1969, which is an increase of eleven over last year. The missions are to be congratulated on having twenty-one missions on the Honor Roll. The General Board is always endeavoring to improve The Relief Society Magazine. A welcome improvement this past year is the better grade paper being used in the color sections. The colors reproduce brighter and clearer and make for more attractive reproductions. The Magazine is ably fulfilling the plans of the founders as it provides a means whereby the writing talents of Latter-day Saint women can be printed and preserved. The Magazine literary compositions in the last contests showed marked improvement in the writing fields. Through the pages of the Magazine there comes a closeness of the spirit among sisters throughout the Church. Many members today living in different lands are learning to read English. They also receive enlightenment from the pictures which carry a universal language, so that English copies which go to foreign-speaking Relief Society sisters are being utilized. May the blessings of the Lord attend the devoted Magazine representatives who carry out their callings so faithfully and the presidencies under whose direction the work progresses. 119 STAKE WARD *MISSION MISSION DISTRICT MISSION BRANCH Sydney (Australia) 196% Magazine RepresentativeElsie F.

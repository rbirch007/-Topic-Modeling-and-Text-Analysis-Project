[Vol57 | January 1970 | Page 64 | Poem | Tomorrow's Embryo, | Margaret B. Shomaker]
GEO TOMORROW'S EMBRYO Come, children hold my hand, As a family let us kneel and Pray, in earthly life, to see A glimpse of our eternity. With time to sift the chaff and weed Then carefully plant the fruitful seed. The harvest yield is what we sow Tomorrows, now in embryo. Margaret B.

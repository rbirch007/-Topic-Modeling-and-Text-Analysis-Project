[Pointing Spires, Pearle M. Olsen]
POINTING SPIRES Symbols of a humble faith; tapered spires Point above the lacy trees in a way To hold my eyes, and warm my heart with fires Of hope that make me want to kneel and pray. | have seen them shine above a loose cloud Rolling in like fog rising from the sod Where people live and toil, speaking aloud Their gratitude while worshipping their God. Firm pointing fills an elemental need Impelling me to lift my voice in praise. Without these symbols others may mislead Me as | grope in darkness for the ways To find a special place with doors ajar Where newer light awaits. There spires are. Pearle M. Olsen

["New Years Prayer," by Vesta N. Lukei]

New Year s Prayer Vesta N. Lukei Upon the New Year's shining scroll. Beloved, now, let me enroll Our names, our need for special care, And, with humility, my prayer To God that he protect and bless Our pure bright love with happiness; And guide us with his wisdom, clear And understanding, through this year.

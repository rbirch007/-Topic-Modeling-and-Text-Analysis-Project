MORE THAN LOVE
Grace Sayre Music is song on a silver night. Sunlight over a brook's mossed stones, Beads on the air in a slant of rain, A meadow lark's liquid tones. Music is petaled bloom adrift, Fragrance of summer's mystic white. Whispers of gladness, shadow of pain. Clouds of the darkness, glories of light. Music is more than love can spill In overflowing of joy and song. Music is more than the heart can hold. And more than love, the whole day long.

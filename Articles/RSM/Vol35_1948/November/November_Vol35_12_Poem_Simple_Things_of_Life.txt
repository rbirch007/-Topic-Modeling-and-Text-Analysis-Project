[Vol35 | November 1948 | Sequence: 12 | Category: Article | Title: Simple Things of Life]

THE SIMPLE THINGS OF LIFE Geneva I. Oldroyd I love the simple things of life That come to us each day, A baby's smile, a collie's bark, A group of boys at play; A garden growing in the sun And giving of its store, A rosebush in its second bloom Of beauty as before; My neighbor's nod across the fence, The postman's friendly smile, The sociability of friends All these make life worth while.

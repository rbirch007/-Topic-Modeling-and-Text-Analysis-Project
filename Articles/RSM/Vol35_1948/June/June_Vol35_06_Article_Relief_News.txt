Relief Society Building News
WITH the passing of June, there remains only the last quarter of the year which was set aside for the collection of the money for the erection of the Relief Society Building. In order for the books to be balanced in time for the general Relief Society conference, September 29 and 30, it is necessary for the final report accompanied by remittances to reach the general board by September 10. The year October 1947 to October 1948 will be marked as the time that the women of the Church, supported by the brethren, gathered the funds for an undertaking of which the sisters of the Church had dreamed since the days of Nauvoo.

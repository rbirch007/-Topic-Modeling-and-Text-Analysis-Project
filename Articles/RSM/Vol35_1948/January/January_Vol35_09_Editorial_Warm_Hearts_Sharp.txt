35 JANUARY 1948 NO. 1 "Warm Hearts and Friendly Hands Loyalty to the teachings of the gospel shown by one's daily conduct, loyalty to one's family, loyalty to friends and those placed in Church positions of authority, bring to a person the blessings of heaven. In the midst of trials and afflictions, how sweet, how solacing is the loyalty of friends.

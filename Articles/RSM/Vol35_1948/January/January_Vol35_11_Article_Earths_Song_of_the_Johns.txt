Across the earth, and marked incisively My charted ways.
What wondrous traffickings Through canyoned hills and over quilted field Have been my lot!
What strange and awesome things The rich gold tapestry of earth will yield. Men traverse age on age and mile on mile, And to each era countless changes bring; Today a beggar walks where just a while ago Upon my breast, there walked a King. Page 29

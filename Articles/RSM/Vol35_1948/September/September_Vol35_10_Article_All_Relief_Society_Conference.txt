[Vol35 | September 1948 | Sequence: 10 | Category: Article | Title: All Relief Society Conference]
All Relief Society Members Invited to Thursday September 30th Sessions of the Annual General Relief Society Conference VERY Relief Society member is invited to attend the two sessions of the annual general Relief Society conference to be held in the Tabernacle on September 30, Thursday morning from 10 to 12 and Thursday afternoon from 2 to 4. The entire proceedings of the morning meeting will be devoted to the recognition of the completion of the Relief Society Building Fund.

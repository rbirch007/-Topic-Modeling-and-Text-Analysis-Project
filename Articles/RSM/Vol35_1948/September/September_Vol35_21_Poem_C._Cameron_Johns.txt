[Vol35 | September 1948 | Sequence: 21 | Category: Article | Title: C. Cameron Johns]

WINDS OF FEAR C. Cameron Johns  Winds of fear are blowing through the night That the windbreaks of the heart cannot abate. With subtle infiltrations, they would kill The tendrils of our hope, regenerate. Only barricades within the soul Of ancient kindnesses withhold their blight. Only love's assurance can resist The winds of fear that blow across the night.

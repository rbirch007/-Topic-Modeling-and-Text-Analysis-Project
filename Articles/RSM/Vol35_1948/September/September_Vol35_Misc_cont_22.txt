Is the Sermon on the Mount obsolete because it was delivered almost 2,000 years ago? Have 300 years made obsolete the drama of Shakespeare, or has it been made obsolete by the modern discovery of the silver screen? Have 170 years made obsolete the Declaration of Independence? Have the years made obsolete man's love for liberty? Have the years made obsolete man's burning desire to worship God according to the dictates of his own conscience? The Constitution of the United States is the greatest charter of religious liberty ever written. It will never become obsolete so long as men love democracy more than despotism, liberty more than bondage, God more than the paganism of unbelief.

[Vol30 | December 1943 | Sequence: 20 | Category: Poem | Title: One Star]

The heavens were filled with myriad stars,
But one star led the way
Unerringly through unknown lands
To where the Christ Child lay.

And in this day of flickering lights
One steadfast beam from far
Leads Christward every soul who seeks,
God's love, His guiding star.

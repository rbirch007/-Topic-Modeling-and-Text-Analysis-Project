Keep Close to the Church Dr. Edgar B. Brossard Member United States Tariff Commission close to the Church and its teachings, is God's command.
Obedience to this admonition will establish proper values and objectives for life's work, and will contribute to man's greatest joy and satisfaction.
Keeping this counsel and demonstrating its fruits will aid others to obtain for themselves the same consequent blessings.
Abiding by it will thus strengthen us and the Church and make for better living standards the world over.
Compliance with it will provide salvation and exaltation in the life hereafter. The authorities, ancient and present, have stressed the necessity of our active affiliation with the Church and of the practical application of its doctrine in our lives. We are taught to "remember the Sabbath day to keep it holy," to "go to the house of prayer and offer up thy sacraments upon my holy day"; that it is good to meet together often to renew our covenants and to study the Gospel; that we should bear our testimonies to each other often because that will strengthen our faith and make it easier to keep it, and to live as we should.

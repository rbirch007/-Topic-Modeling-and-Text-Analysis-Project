Kleiuman To the mother whose arms have never pressed A baby's head to her lonely breast, To the mother whose lips have never sung A lullaby in a baby's tongue, To the mother whose prayers through time and space Have seemed to fail at the Throne of Grace No plea, no prayer, no tears of thine Are ever wasted in God's design, For He who counteth the sparrow's fall Eternal increase shall yield to all, And the motherhood that is here denied Shall yet be given and glorified!

PURPLE SAGE
By Lydia Hall
Roses for the lovers, Star-bright skies o'erhead, Lilies, white and lovely, For someone who is dead.
Daisies for the children, Happy in their play, And the sweet carnations For the mother gray.
But I love the sage brush More than all the rest: Rain-wet plumes of fragrance Purpling the west.

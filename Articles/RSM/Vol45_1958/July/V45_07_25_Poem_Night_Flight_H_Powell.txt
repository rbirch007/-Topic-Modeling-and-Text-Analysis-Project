[Vol45 | July 1958 | Sequence: 25 | Category: Poem | Title: Night Flight | Author: Pansye H. Powell]
Carried on silver wings through milk-mist night,
I ride this giant bird in peaceful flight,
Trusting my pilot's knowledge of the way.
And thus my migrant soul on wings of death,
Floating beyond our bourne of time and breath,
Will trust a greater Pilot's skill some day.

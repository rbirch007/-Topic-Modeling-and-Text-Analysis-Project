[Vol45 | July 1958 | Sequence: 26 | Category: Poem | Title: Where Jesus Walked | Author: Remelda Nielsen Gibson]
Where Jesus walked the deaf were made to hear,
The blind were shown the beauties of the earth;
The crippled and the sick were healed. Each place
Where Jesus softly walked, the wondrous worth
Of faith and trust and truth was prevalent—
The principles of love were kindly taught.
Where Jesus walked his followers revered
Each humble action and each holy thought!

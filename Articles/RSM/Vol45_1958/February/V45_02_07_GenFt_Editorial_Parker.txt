[Vol45 | February 1958 | Sequence: 07 | Category: General Feature | Title: EDITORIAL | Author: M. C. S.]
The true spirit of Valentine's Day is one of warmth and loving kindness which may leave behind a tangible expression, be it ever so small, so that the remembrance of the occasion will linger on.

This valentine spirit of solicitude and affirmation should not be confined to February the Fourteenth, but should be lavishly given throughout the year, so that "I Love You Truly" and "Be My Valentine" will be constantly echoing and re-echoing in hearts made warm and joyful.
—M. C. S.

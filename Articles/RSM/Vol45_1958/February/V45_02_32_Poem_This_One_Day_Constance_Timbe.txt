Shis One Day
Constance Timberlake

Let us arise to greet the Sabbath day
With grateful hearts. 'This one day of the week
Is ours to have, to worship, and to pray;
Remembering Christ, as we his presence seck.

What love! He had to suffer and to die!
The highest price was paid for you and me
That we might live, our bodies not to lie
Forever dead, but raised eternally.

As we this day the sacred emblems take,
With meekness thus renew again our vows
To strive to live, and day by day to make
Our light to shine; no clouds darken our brows;

Until the dawn of perfect joy and peace,
To do his will our labors shall not cease.

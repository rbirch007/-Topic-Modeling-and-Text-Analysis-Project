Lesson  11— The  Worth  of  Souls 
Elder  Roy  W.  Doxey 
(Text:  The  Doctrine  and  Covenants,  Sections  15,  16,  and  18) 
For  Tuesday,  December  2,  1958 
Objective:      "Remember    the    worth    of    souls    is    great    in    the    sight    of    God" 
(D  &  C  18:10). 
Stctions  15  and  16 
npHE  first  two  revelations  for  study 
in  this  lesson  are  identical,  but 
they  were  given  to  different  persons. 
The  message  thus  given  was  of  great 
importance  to  those  two  sons  of 
Peter  Whitmer,  into  whose  home 
the  Prophet  Joseph  Smith,  Emma, 
his  wife,  and  Oliver  Cowdery  were 
received  in  June  1829.  (See  D.  H.  C. 
1:48,  49.)  John  and  Peter  Whit- 
nier,  Jr.,  to  whom  Sections  15  and 
16  were  given,  and  especially  the 
former,  became  zealous  friends  of 
Joseph  Smith  and  assistants  in  the 
work.  It  was  their  desire  to  learn 
from  the  Lord  what  was  of  most 
worth  to  them  in  life.  (See  D  &  C 
16:4.)  The  answer  of  the  Lord  was 
definite  and  clear:

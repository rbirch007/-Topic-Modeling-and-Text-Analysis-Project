[Vol45 | September 1958 | Sequence: 21 | Category: Poem | Title: Mystic Word | Author: Eva Willes Wangsgaard]
Mystic Word Eva Willes Wangsgaard Golden is a mystic word Calling up a topaz bird, The molten song a meadowlark Sings to dawn with skies still dark; A daffodil, a marigold, The midday sun the cowslips hold, An apple bite, banana husk, A melon's amber smell of musk; The golden dust a bee let fall From hoilyhocks' pink-ruffled wall, Swept away by the wind's thin broom To find the future's infinite bloom; The living gold forever descending To life beginning from life ending.

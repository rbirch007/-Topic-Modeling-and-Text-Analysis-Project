Only Hearts Can Hear

Maude O. Cook

ah - ue - Only Hearts Can Hear Maude O. Cook In tones that only hearts can hear, When once attuned to loftier strains, Faith speaks in accents firm and clear. No sound is heard by mortal ear, For faith a subtler speech attains; It whispers hope and dries the tear, And gives the courage which sustains; In tones that only hearts can hear, Faith speaks in accents firm and clear.

SARAH  A.  Sanders  of  LaVerkin,  Utah,  has  found  that  her  hobby  of  quiltmaking 
brings  contentment  to  her  and  comfort  and  joy  to  her  friends  and  relatives.  She 
has  made,  without  assistance,  more  than  one  hundred  large  quilts  and  more  than  a 
hundred  quilts  for  children's  beds.  She  has  lost  count  of  the  number  of  Relief  Society 
quilts  which  she  has  helped  to  make  in  her  long  years  of  active  membership  in  the 
society.  She  was  ward  Relief  Society  president  for  seven  and  a  half  years,  and  has 
been  a  visiting  teacher  for  over  fifty  years,  besides  holding  teaching  and  executive 
positions  in  the  Primary  and  Sunday  School. 
Now,  at  the  age  of  eighty-one,  Mrs.  Sanders  counts  her  lo\ely  family  as  her 
choicest  blessings.  She  has  nine  children,  forty-two  grandchildren,  and  fifty-eight 
great-grandchildren.

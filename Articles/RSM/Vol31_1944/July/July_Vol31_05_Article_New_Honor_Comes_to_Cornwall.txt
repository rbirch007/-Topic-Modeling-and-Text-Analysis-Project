[Vol31 | July 1944 | Sequence: 05 | Category: Article | Title: New Honor Comes to Cornwall]

~ New Honor Comes to Tabernacle Choir Elder J. Spencer Cornwall Conductor Tabernacle Choir Elder J. Spencer Cornwall Conductor Tabernacle Choir Adjudged worthy of the George Foster Peabody award for ?outstanding entertainment in music,? the weekly broadcast of ?Music and the Spoken Word? from the Salt Lake Tabernacle over KSL has brought new honors to the Church and to Utah. The George Foster Peabody award is rated as one of the outstanding awards made each year to programs and stations in recognition of meritorious public service. The adjudicating committee was made up of representations of the University of Georgia, and the Peabody advisory Board in co-operation with the National Association of Broadcasters.

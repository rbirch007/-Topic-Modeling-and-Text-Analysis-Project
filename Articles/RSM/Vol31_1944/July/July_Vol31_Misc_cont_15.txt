CLEAN PLATE CAMPAIGN
There's one way every family can contribute to the success of the War?and that is by saving food?Food is a weapon of War?Don't waste it. Now is the time for every American to help conserve the food resources of America by Cleaning up his Plate. Avoidable waste is our largest and most economical extra food supply available. Twenty per cent of all food produced in America is never eaten. It is wasted?fills the garbage cans?Improper handling of foods in markets, faulty preparation of foods in restaurants and in homes and poor eating habits on the part of the American populace accounts for this gross and random waste. Most food waste occurs not in pounds, bushels or acre lots, but in ounces, bits, and driblets. The food one civilian wastes in a week will provide food for one military field ration, or feed one Army man for one day. Think what one hundred and thirty million times that quantity could do. Conservation of this food is not a problem for the economist.

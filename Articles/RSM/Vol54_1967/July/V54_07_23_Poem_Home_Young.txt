HOME Sylvia Probst Young I shall return when twilight comes this way; Here in this sheltered place I close the door Against the hurried pattern of the day. This is an isle of peace a quiet shore, Away from storm and strife inviting rest, Where dreams are born and tender thoughts may flower; Here memory is an ever-welcome guest, Companion for a solitary hour.

[Vol54 | April 1967 | Page 288 | Poetry | My Hands Sadie | J. Stevens]
[My Hands Sadie J. Stevens]

Sing of the tulips, Some bowing their heads, Blossoms air-branching And dainty flowered beds. Sing to me, April, While I keep a. Tryst With lavender lilacs In gentle rain mist. Sing of the nesting birds, Make love your theme, Now all is awakened From winter's long dream.

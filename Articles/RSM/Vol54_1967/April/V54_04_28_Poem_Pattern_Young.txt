[Vol54 | April 1967 | Page 293 | Poem | Pattern of Blossoms Aleine | M. Young]
{ PATTERN OF BLOSSOMS \ I Aleine M. Young Yesterday the apple blossoms Made my lawn all white. Today, it's falling snowflakes, And it's really quite a sight To see the crimson tulips And the blossoms on the trees Snuggled down in snowy crystals— Spring is really quite a tease.

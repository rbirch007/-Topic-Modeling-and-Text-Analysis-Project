[Vol54 | April 1967 | Page 287 | Poem | Wind Lullabye Beulah | Huish Sadleir]
WIND LULLABY Beulah Huish Sadleir Sing to me, spring, With your sun in the sky, A smooth April sonnet Or wind lullaby. Sing of the tulips, Some bowing their heads, Blossoms air-branching And dainty flowered beds. Sing to me, April, While I keep a. Tryst With lavender lilacs In gentle rain mist. Sing of the nesting birds, Make love your theme, Now all is awakened From winter's long dream.

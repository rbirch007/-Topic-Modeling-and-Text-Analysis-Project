A DREAM COME TRUE Della Adams Leitner Sometimes among demanding tasks That fill our crowded days This dream floats in, a wistful A fleeting hour it stays— A dream of glad companionship With kindred souls that share Our deep-felt aims, our heart's desires— A longing that is prayer. Hope, And when—as if in answer—comes The dream fulfilled, we know A satisfaction so complete Our spirits are aglow; We are inspired, uplifted, thrilled To be with those who find Vast riches of the soul in thought The treasurers of the mind. 837 « Christmas was almost here! Joyfully I hopped behind the old yellow road grader as it slowly nudged soft, deep drifts. Great flakes had frosted our little Canadian town in marshmallow icing that glittered now in the sunlight like a tinseled fairyland. Just about as radiant as the wonderful tree at school today, I thought. Christmas trees were very dear to prairie people.

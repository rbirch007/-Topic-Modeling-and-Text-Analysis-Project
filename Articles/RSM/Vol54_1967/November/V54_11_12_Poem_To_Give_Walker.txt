[To "Give For," Elizabeth Cottan) Walker]

I must realize it is another's right to be forgiven. How great the grief the unforgiven carry; denying relief binds the unforgiving one forever. This I know, and this must I do —! Must "give for'' to "'forgive''; give love for hatred, for to live with love is the Master's way of life. 822

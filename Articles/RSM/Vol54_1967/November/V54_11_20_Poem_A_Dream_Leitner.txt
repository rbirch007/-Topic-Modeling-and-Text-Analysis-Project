A DREAM COME TRUE Della Adams Leitner Sometimes among demanding tasks That fill our crowded days This dream floats in, a wistful A fleeting hour it stays— A dream of glad companionship With kindred souls that share Our deep-felt aims, our heart's desires— A longing that is prayer. Hope, And when—as if in answer—comes The dream fulfilled, we know A satisfaction so complete Our spirits are aglow; We are inspired, uplifted, thrilled To be with those who find Vast riches of the soul in thought The treasurers of the mind. 837 « Christmas was almost here!

The pine cones on the tree were gathered by the family, sprayed gold, and tied together with pink satin ribbon. Pink balls and pink and gold lights enhance the beauty of the tree. Heslop A Gingerbread Tree in the James C. Davis Jr. Home. This tree will delight any youngster and bring back fond memories to parents and grandparents.

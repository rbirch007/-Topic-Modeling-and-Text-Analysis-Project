EDITORIAL The 137th Semi-Annual General Church Conference ■ The 137th Semi-Annual General Conference of the Church, held September 30th, 31st, and October 1st, 1967, was especially significant in many ways. The historic Tabernacle, wherein the sessions were held, was being remembered as a centennial structure, having housed the saints for one hundred years.

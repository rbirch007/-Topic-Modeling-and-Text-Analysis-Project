[Vol54 | March 1967 | Page 180 | Poem | My Beautiful Grace | Barker Wilson]
[My Beautiful Grace Barker Wilson]
My Beautiful Grace Barker Wilson She walks abroad, my beautiful, The sunlight in her glance Makes all the world a brighter place, And all the sunbeams dance. She walks abroad, my beautiful, My grandchild, small and sweet, With star dust shining in her eyes, And flowers at her feet.

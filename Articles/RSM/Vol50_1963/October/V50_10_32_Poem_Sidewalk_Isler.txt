Rose ApAMs LiInDSEY CuRTIS El] Paso, Texas Salt Lake City, Utah Sidewalk Vignette Betty Isler Between parked cars I caught the scene — Three young women, bright and clean, Greeting their mother (she must have been, Dressed in deep rose, with soft gray hair). With unabashed affection there They kissed;. And through the traffic's blare I missed their words, but saw the gay Gathering in, as they walked away, Arm in arm, on the sunny day. I watched them wistfully out of range, Waiting for the light to change.

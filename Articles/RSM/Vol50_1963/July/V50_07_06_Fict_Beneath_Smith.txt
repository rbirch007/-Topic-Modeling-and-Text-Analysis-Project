Monday proved to be a very busy day, with washing, cooking a big meal for the men who had come to help Carl, and, later, baking bread. Teresa tried to help, but she gave out before the day was even half over. She was appalled at how hard her daughter worked. When Rosemary took a moment to sit down, Teresa couldn't keep quiet. "I think that it is terrible the way that Carl lets you work. I have never seen anyone work so hard." "Now, Mother, I don't work any harder than the other people around here.

[Renewal, by Grace Sayre,]

Renewal Grace Sayre Beauty renews itself each spring. See where the patched hillside, Mottled with the brown stain of loam, Shows through the melting snow. And see the streams assert themselves!
Willow catkins fluff, Trailing arbutus opens its fragile pink trumpet, Fragrance lies in the throat of the lily; Dogwood branches hold out their pure flame - Of green and white beauty, Lighting the roads and wooded paths; Beauty renews itself in the land.

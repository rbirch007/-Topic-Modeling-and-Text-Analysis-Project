[Vol57 | July 1970 | Page 515 | Poem | You Never Knew I Kept All These, Alda | Larson Brown]
This pine cone that you salvaged from the leaves You never knew that I would keep all these. _ But such small treasures I have not kept in vain,..\. For I have all these memories. To relive again. Alda Larson Brown 515 Mama Was a Hero-ine!

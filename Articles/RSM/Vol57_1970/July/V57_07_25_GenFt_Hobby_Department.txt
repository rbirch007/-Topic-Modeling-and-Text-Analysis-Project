[Hobby Features— Catherine K. Hiatt and Lucinda H. Cardon Lesson Department]

All who know her are inspired by her love of life. Lucinda Hurst Cardon, Mesa Stake, Arizona, has been quilting for eighty-one years, or since she was eight years old, and she is still making quilts for her family and for Relief Society. In her patchwork quilts, the materials bring back fond memories of clothing she has sewed. She has made at least one quilt for each of her ten children, forty-seven grandchildren, 103 great-grandchildren, and one great-great-grandchild. In addition to her quilting, she attends eight or ten temple sessions each week. She has been an active worker in the Church organizations, and is still a visiting teacher in Relief Society. Everyone who knows her is impressed by her devotion to the gospel and her love for others.

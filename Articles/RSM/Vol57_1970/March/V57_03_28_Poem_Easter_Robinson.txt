Linnie F. Robinson 221 Hobby Features Agnes J. Buttars, Cornish Ward, Benson Stake, Utah, has spent much of her life making others happy with her gifts of lovely quilts, crocheted and knitted articles, and other creations of handwork. She has spent her lifetime serving Relief Society. She served ten years as ward president, and twelve as secretary-treasurer, during which time she took great pride in her accurate reports. She has also served many years as a visiting teacher, and has given valued service to the other auxiliaries of the Church. Sister Buttars Ys the mother of five children. There are twenty-four grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. Besides her lovely family, she is enjoyed by a large circle of friends. Noreen K. Rodgers, Covina Ward, Covina Stake, California, has made a hobby of Relief Society. S

Why Poetry?
Margaret B. Jorgensen

The flame of Phlox
And the molten color
Of the Virginia creeper
Brighten my cottage door
Enough to speak
Our thought
Or read another's as our own?
To soar with music,
Leap in dance,
Weep with prose,
Exult in drama?
Why, then the poet?
His to magnify a sunbeam
Till its focus burns into a soul,
Setting it afire
With truththe Poet's goal

[Vol57 | May 1970 | Page 348 | Poem | Thy Spirit Everywhere, W/7ma | Morley Despain]
THY SPIRIT EVERYWHERE jam blessed to see thy hand— In fragile leaf and bud and tree. New miracles, with reverence, Each greening spring I see— Wild bird's flight across the river, Sunsets fired on high when day is done. Thrice thanks for blessings manifold, Our children and their children dear— On Sinai, to feel, to know my purpose here.

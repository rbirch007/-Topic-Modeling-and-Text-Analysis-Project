Salt Lake City, Utah 84111 home of radio REFLECTION OF LIGHT God gave his Son To speed the day When I in light, Could find the way. Can I do less? My sons must go To other sheep To let them know! He gave the gift to all Eternal life can we Do less for him who said '"'Here am I, send me!"' We have sent three sons Our blessings manifold And count it not as sacrifice This great rejoicing in the fold.

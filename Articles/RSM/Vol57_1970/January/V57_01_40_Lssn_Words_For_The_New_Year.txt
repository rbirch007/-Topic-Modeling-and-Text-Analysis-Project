Relief Society.

At the beginning of a New Year, as is the custom, we examine the

prospects for the success of Relief Society activities. We pause to appraise

the needs of the sisters in this exciting, wonderful dispensation with its

sublime and breathtaking accomplishments. We are fully cognizant ot the

wickedness and evil rampant in the world and endeavor to make Relief

Society a bulwark for the sisters and for the guiding of their families in

withstanding the unrighteous temptations of the world, just the same, we

know the privilege of having been born into the world at this time. The

daughters of God are vital to his work in these latter days.

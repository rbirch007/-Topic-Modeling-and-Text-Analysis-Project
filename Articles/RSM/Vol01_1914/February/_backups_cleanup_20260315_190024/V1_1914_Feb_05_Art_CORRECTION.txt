CORRECTION.
In the opening sentence of the lesson statement, for first lesson, an erroneous application of verse 17, 1st chap, of Luke, was inadvertently made by the writer, who keenly regrets its publication.
A reading of the text clearly shows that the matter relates to an important feature of the mission of John the Baptist. i THIKD MEETING Ten Minutes' Talk on Current Topics.

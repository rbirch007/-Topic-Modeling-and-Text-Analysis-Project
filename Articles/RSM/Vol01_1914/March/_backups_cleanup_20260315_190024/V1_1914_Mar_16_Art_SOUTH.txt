SOUTH MAIN ST.
WHILE LABORING FOR THE POOR AND AIDING THE WIDOW IN WANT, DON'T NEGLECT YOURSELF AND FAMILY Have they the proper protection in ease of sickness or death of your bread winner or will you be compelled to look for charity?
Has he sufficient life insurance to assure you this protection?

Divine Relief
By Wenonah M, Shirley
God spoke the word, His servant heard And turned the mighty key And opened wide the door which gave To women liberty.
To homes of grief, we often go To carry hope and cheer And smooth the weary fevered brow When death's dark form draws near.
Oh, mighty seer, oh favored one, The spokesman of our Lord Thy name and calling we revere, And gladly heed thy word,
That set us free from tyranny, Our thanks Thee we accord That guides men's destiny.
We'll prove our faith and gratitude By always serving Thee.
Ah, now we conquer all!

In conclusion let us unite in an appeal to our Heavenly Father for peace and goodwill throughout the earth, for national and international understanding, for security for all, for willingness on the part of His children to abide by the Golden Rule; and for faith to seek first the Kingdom of God and all other things that are righteous.

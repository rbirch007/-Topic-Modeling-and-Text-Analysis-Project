Ersjoy these beautiful poems. We congratulate the authors, and thank all who entered the contest. Annie Wells Cannon, Chairman, Eliza Roxey Snow Memorial Poem Contest. My Friend By Edith E, Anderson I like you, Friend, Because you understand That when I'm grave Or wrapt in solemn thought, My love wanes not; You know the love you gave Shall not be banned By mood of mine, dear friend.

Some only find the gold of the setting sun! ERASTUS AND ANNITS POTTER CHRISTENSEN AND THEIR 7 SONS AND 6 DAUGHTERS. ~ Shadows Awarded Second Honorable Mention in the Eliza Roxey Snow Poetry Contest By Vesta P. Crawford I love the time when shadows slant Across the valley's waiting breast And drape the world in loveliness When night comes down to rest.
Though eyes of mine can never see The places where the darkness goes, The heart of earth is rifted wide And the dusk-dimmed valley knows, I hope to wear my shadows well, With dignity and quiet power For so the valley robes herself In the evening's sentient hour.

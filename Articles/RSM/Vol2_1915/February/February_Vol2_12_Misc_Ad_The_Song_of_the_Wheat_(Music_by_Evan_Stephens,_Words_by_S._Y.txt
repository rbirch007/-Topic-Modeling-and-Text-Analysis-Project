[Vol2 | February 1915 | Sequence: 12 | Category: Article | Title: The Song of the Wheat (Music by Evan Stephens, Words by S. Y]

THE SONG OF THE WHEAT Lovingly dedicated to Mrs. Emmeline B. Wells Words by S. Y. G. Music by Evan Stephens. [Musical notation included with lyrics about wheat, harvest, and gospel themes] HE women of the Relief Society have responded nobly to the appeal for subscriptions to this magazine. Even the missions have taken up the work, the North Western States Mission sending in nearly two hundred paid up subscriptions. This magazine is designed for every officer and member of the society, and hopes to give every subscriber value received in live, up-to-date articles on our work and activities, as well as fiction, poetry and travel articles. Are you a subscriber? If not, why not?

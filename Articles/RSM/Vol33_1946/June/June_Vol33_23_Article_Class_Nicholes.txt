(To be continued ) CLASS REUNION Olive M. Nicholes Time's measured tread along the campus ways Brings memories back ? one old friend with me still ? As burgeoning of spring, and summer days Point to the crimson sumac on the hill, Bleak winter's sculptures, and the barricade Of crystal bridges on the silent stream. The locust shrills a haunting serenade Where lovers' Jane is but a happy dream, And phantom classmates climb the hill with me, For you, alone, remained to give it grace.

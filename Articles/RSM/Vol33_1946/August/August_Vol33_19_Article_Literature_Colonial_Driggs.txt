MATERNAL REVERIE
Della Adams Leitner
Facing the days, I wait with awe and wonder: The life in me is ancient: it has come Through generations I can never number. To this new soul all have donated some Of their capacities, their weaknesses, desires Brooks, flowing through strange ways to meet the sea, Bring to the river elements their sires, The mountains, gave by their serenity.

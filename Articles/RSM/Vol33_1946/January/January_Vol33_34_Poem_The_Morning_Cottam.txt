THE MORNING STAR
Courtney E. Cottam Black is the night of my despair, Cold, cold before the dawn; Hope's congealed within my heart, My idols are fallen and gone; I am alone. In the anguish of my grief, One ray of hope shines through, The Morning Star, the Light of Christ, His promise, ever new; And night is gone.

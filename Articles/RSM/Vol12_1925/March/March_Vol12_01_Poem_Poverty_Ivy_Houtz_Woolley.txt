[Vol12 | March 1925 | Sequence: 01 | Category: Article | Title: Poverty Ivy Houtz Woolley]
Poverty
Poor soul that has no pain, Poor heart that never caught The rhythm of a kindred heart? Oh, poor indeed thy lot.
Poor throat that ne'er burst forth In song of hope or tears; Poor tongue that never spoke Of love to loving ears.
Poor eyes that cannot see The world in beauty drowned; Poor ears that ne'er have heard Life's welling, joyous sound.

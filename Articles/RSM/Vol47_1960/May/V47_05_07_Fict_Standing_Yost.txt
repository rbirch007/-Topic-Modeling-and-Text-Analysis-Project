A look of complete understanding passed _between them. After that there was a long, blissful interval, with Helga still fussing over the now peacefully sleeping baby, and Jimmie and Laura comfortably talking it out together in each other's arms. Helga looked at them and sighed. Her mind went back to those first years after her husband, Ned, had died. She recalled the many times she might have been completely lost had it not been for the strength of her Church teachings, the things she learned in Relief Society, and an occasional talk with her kindly bishop. At that very moment she appointed herself official Grandmother to the Sturm family. She would see that Laura had plenty of time to attend her meetings. We'll grow wise together, she thought with a smile. 'There's always something new and interesting to learn. She picked up the baby carefully. Pil just have to fetch my own blanket, she thought happily.

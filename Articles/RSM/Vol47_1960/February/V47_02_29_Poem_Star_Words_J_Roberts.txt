[Vol47 | February 1960 | Sequence: 29 | Category: Poem | Title: Star Words | Author: Dorothy J. Roberts]
> Star Wor S Dorothy J]. Roberts If the dart of bitterness Has pierced the layered bark of silence To the living center, pour The Savior's words over the wound. Here in the midnight silence, let His syllables mend the tissue's bruise, Till the wound becomes scar, the scar Becomes healed and at length forgotten.

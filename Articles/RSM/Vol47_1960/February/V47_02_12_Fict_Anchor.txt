i. What

"Better Human

a. Where do you

tasks and

the
feelings
so

warm,
happy

b. How
many
are
little,
bitter,
re-
sentful

5. What do you contribute
to
relation-
ships?
Do you "love things and use peo-
ple"

it
should
be
the
other
way
around?

**

Several years ago Mrs. Jensen was so seriously

This was part

She supported

Mrs. Jensen, once a counselor in the Relief Society
in Copenhagen, Denmark,

life practices the charitable ways so dear

Three of her eight children are living; she
is grandmother to
fifteen and
great-grandmother to twenty-two.

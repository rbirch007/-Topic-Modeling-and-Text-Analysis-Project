DEBRA awoke suddenly, as she
did each morning,
plans
for
the day running through her
head.
Today

to wash
the kitchen windows, straighten the

After

Debra prided herself on her neat-

She knew her neigh-

to

Her formula was "a

Her

around the door.
Ronnie, age four,

ing to the bedside, both

"Good morning, Mommie!" Ron-
nie

neath the covers.
' 'Morning, Mommie/'

Greggie

Debra smiled quickly at her wig-

for her

The

little blonde head.
Debra

When

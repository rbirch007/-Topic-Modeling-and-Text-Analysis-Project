Eliza Roxey Snow Poem Contest

The Relief Society Magazine announces the winners of the Eliza Roxey Snow Poetry Contest. First prize was awarded to Mrs. Maud Chegwidden for her poem "Riches," and second prize to Mrs. Linda S. Fletcher for "To the Sego-Lily." Both poems appear in this issue of the magazine.

The contest was established to encourage poetic expression among the sisters of the Relief Society and to honor the memory of Eliza Roxey Snow, beloved poet and leader. The quality of entries received was exceptionally high, making the selection of winners a difficult task for the judges.

We congratulate the winners and thank all who participated in this literary endeavor. Plans are being made for future poetry contests to continue fostering creative expression within our organization.

[Vol14 | October 1927 | Sequence: 01 | Category: Frontispiece | Title: Hollyhocks Frontispiece]

Hollyhocks! I hear you call to me With faintly fluted voices as I pass The old deserted garden tall with grass And weeds. I pause, with heartfelt sympathy, Beneath the full blown, unpruned apple tree That still holds nectar for the busy bee. Dear

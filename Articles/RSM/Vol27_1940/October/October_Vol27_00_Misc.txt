The Cover
The cover picture this month is a copy of The Gleaners, by Jean Francois Millett (1814-75). Noted for his peasant scenes, the one reproduced on the cover is regarded as one of his best. Himself a peasant in origin, familiar with the toil and privation of farm life, he interpreted peasant life with a truthfulness, simplicity and pathos unequaled in all art. The pose of his figures is superb and there is an extraordinary unity between them and the landscape backgrounds which he handled with the greatest skill.

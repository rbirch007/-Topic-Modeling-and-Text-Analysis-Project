[Vol28 | October 1941 | Sequence: 15 | Category: Article | Title: Two New Centennial Songs Book]

NOTES TO THE FIELD Two New Centennial Songs For Relief Society Song Book THE General Board announces the publication of two songs in commemoration of the Relief Society centennial.
These songs, punched to fit the Relief Society Song Book, are the first additions to the loose-leaf Song Book since its publication early in 1940. The new songs are listed below, with their prices! A Song of Triumph, words and music by the late Beatrice IF'. Stevens, 2 pages, 5c per copy, postpaid; In His Form, words by Dr. Carlton Culmsee, music by Dr. Florence Jepperson Madsen, member of the General Board, 7 pages, 10c per copy, postpaid.
These songs will be off the press during the forepart of October, and will be for sale only at the offices of the General Board, 28
Bishop's Building, Salt Lake City, Utah. Inasmuch as the combined price of the two songs is 15c, the Song Book, which now sells at 85c per copy, postpaid, will hereafter be priced at $1 per copy, postpaid, with the two new songs included.

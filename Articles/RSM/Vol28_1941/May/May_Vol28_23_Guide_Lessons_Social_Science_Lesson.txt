Social Science Lessons For a number of years, the lessons scheduled for the fourth Tuesday of the month have been titled, "Social Service." In recent years, as the courses have referred to educational work rather than to principles of  social work, it is felt that a broader title should be used which would cover both phases of the work. We shall, therefore, in the future use the title, "Social Science," for this course. Blue Bulletins The blue bulletins, issued by the General Board to stake and mission Relief Society presidents, should be carefully read, not only by stake presidencies, but by the entire stake board.

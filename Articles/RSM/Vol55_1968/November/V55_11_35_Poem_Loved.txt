[Vol55 | November 1968 | Page 878 | Poem | Loved | Carolle Denton]
[Loved Carolle Denton]

LOVED A child breathes And with this life Held for a moment In loved curved arms, Come light and shadows. A child grows, Is molded to His environment, And reflects the lives Of those that break the shadows. A child matures, And, if cradled well, The world is to him As a rainbow prismed On an edge of glass. —Carolle Denton

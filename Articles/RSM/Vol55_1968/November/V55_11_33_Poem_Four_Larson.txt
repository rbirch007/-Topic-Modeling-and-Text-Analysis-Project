[Four Words Defined Leora G. Larson]
FOUR WORDS DEFINED PEACE is being loved in the deepest dimension. LIVING is loving someone beyond explanation. DEVOTION is serving others with sincere dedication. FAITH is saying ''I will" when the time comes for action. —Leora G. Larson

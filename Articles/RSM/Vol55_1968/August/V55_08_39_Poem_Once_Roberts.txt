[Once Upon a Moment Dorothy J. Roberts]

ONCE UPON A MOMENT

Now time stands still,
No moment of extension lies beyond
Her curve of smile,
The dark vistas
Of her eyes,
Liquid in the light.

In the bond
Almost tangible between us,
Time,
All the wide world of it,
Converged in a round
Moment of fulfillment.

—Dorothy J. Roberts

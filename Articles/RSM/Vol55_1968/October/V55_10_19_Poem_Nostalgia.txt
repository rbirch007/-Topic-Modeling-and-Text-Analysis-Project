[Vol55 | October 1968 | Page 757 | Poem | Nostalgia | Margaret Read]
[Nostalgia Margaret Read]

- Sn NOSTALGIA What rapture fills my heart When strains of half-forgotten melody Take me back across the years— To where its start Awakened, in my untried youth, The luring hold of beauty, And captured, spellbound my mind to soothe In dreams of ecstasy and charm. Now youth has left the shore Of carefree, reckless phase. The tune is sweeter than before, For memory wraps the notes In softer tones, that reach to penetrate With stronger thrust, and thus remain Immortal, beloved, never ceasing to inflate My soul with quiet peace.

They are subject to the powers and authority of the Church, and they are not independent of them; nor can they exercise any rights in their organizations independently of the Priesthood and of the Church. Furthermore, the officers of these organizations are duly presented at the general or local conferences, as the case may be, and are sustained by the vote of the people of the Church, and, as such, the officers should be respected in their callings and given' recognition and support in the performance of their duties in all that relates to the organizations which they represent. I would like to say to Relief Society, that it is the one great organization in the Church, whose duty it is to look after the interests of the women in Zion.

[Vol55 | September 1968 | Page 708 | Poem | Faith Bonnie | S. Gudmundson]
[Faith Bonnie S. Gudmundson]

FAITH You say my path has ended—it has just begun, Winding through many woods, seeing many changes, The spring of my growing has bubbled into memories; Summer of my understanding leafed into knowledge, Autumn of evaluation turned leaf into wisdom. When the winter of truth descended like a cold dusk, You say it vanquished my woodland; it chased the leaf and froze the spring. Would a mustard seed cease to believe and grow? Would a song sparrow forget to nest and sing? My path has no endings, only branching beginnings. —Bonnie S.

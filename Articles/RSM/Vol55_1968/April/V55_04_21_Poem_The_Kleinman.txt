Butter, melted grated rind of 42 lemon Mix together 3 oz. White bread crumbs, sugar, and lemon rind. Peel the apples and grate onto a plate. In a pie dish, place alternate layers of mixed crumbs and apples, the last layer being crumbs, over which pour the melted butter. Sprinkle the remaining 1 oz. Of crumbs on top and bake at 350 degrees for about 25 minutes. Fruit variation: cherries, rhubarb, plums, etc.

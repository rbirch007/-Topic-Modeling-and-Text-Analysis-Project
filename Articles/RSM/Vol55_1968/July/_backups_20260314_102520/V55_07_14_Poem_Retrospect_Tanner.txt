[Retrospect Annie Atkin Tanner]

Now the little girl was happily calling friends and relatives to admire the cake before her mother should cut and serve it. Her father was taking a colored picture of this special cake, so the family could always remember how beautiful had been this unexpected gift. When I had opportunity to tell the cake baker how much joy she had brought to me and to so many others by this lovely cake to the little girl, she smiled. And said: "Then I am well repaid for the time spent in shaping and decorating the butterfly." She grew silent in reflection and then added, "And I have helped fulfill a long-standing promise made when I myself was a little girl." The cake was a memorial to a very special spiritual experience.

—Gladys Hesser Burnham 505 — f ~ £ ~ L T We Leone W. Doxey Let your children be happy. Laugh with them, never at them. Good humor is kind, never bitter or sarcastic. Laughter ls infectious. Let your children do some giving. Try not to do all the giving yourself. It is more blessed to give than to receive. Blessings will spread. Let your children know that you love them and that you accept them as they are. You may not accept some of their actions, but you will accept and love them always. Love is contagious. Let your children do something. They may not do that something as well as you can do it, but some day, by persistent doing, they may do it better than you. Success is communicable. Let your children respect you. They will when you are the kind of person they can respect. Respect is catching.

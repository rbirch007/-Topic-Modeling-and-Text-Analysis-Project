FARM SCENES NEAR LAKE GENEVA
Elizabeth Williamson Illustration by the Author ON the outskirts of the village of Thonon, on the French side of Lake Geneva, small patchwork farms border the highway. As you drive along, you are attracted by the old stone Provincial farmhouse with the hayloft built high above the ground and adjoined to the living quarters. You feel you would like to know these country people who love and know every inch of their ground. You feel certain that they must possess a rare sense of humor, for near the road is a charming scarecrow astride an old bicycle. He looks ready for anything, with his black felt hat and big umbrella.

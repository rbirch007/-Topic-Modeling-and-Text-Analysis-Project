Notes v0 THE FIELD Annual General Relief Society Conference THE annual general Relief Society conference will be held Wednesday and Thursday, September 30 and October 1, 1953. On Thursday afternoon in the Tabernacle, a meeting will be held to which the general membership of Relief Society and the public are invited. The attendance at the meetings on Wednesday and on Thursday morning is limited to stake and mission officers.

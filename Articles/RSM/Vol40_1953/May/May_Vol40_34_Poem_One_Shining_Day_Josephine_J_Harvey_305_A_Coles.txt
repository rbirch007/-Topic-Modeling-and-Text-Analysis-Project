[Vol40 | May 1953 | Sequence: 34 | Category: Poem | Title: One Shining Day Josephine J. Harvey 305 A Mother's Debt by]

Don  Knight 
ALMOND  ORCHARD  NEAR  OAKLEY,  CALIFORNIA 
Gyfie  Shining   ^Jjay 
Josephine  J.  Harvey 
Today  I  walk  in  happiness,  heart-deep, 
Again  the  ancient  earth  has  stirred 
From  wintered  sleep. 
Again  the  spring  is  on  the  land 
With  newborn  sounds,  soft-heard. 
I  hold  one  crimson  blossom 
In  my  hand. 
The  gentle  winds  of  May 
Touch  new  and  tender  leaves 
This  lilac-scented  day. 
You  will  love  the  time  of  blossoming. 
The  magic  pattern  springtime  weaves; 
One  shining  day,  calmly  you  will  come. 
Joy  will  be  a  living  thing. 
And  where  we  walk,  meadow  larks  will  sing.

QUEEN  ELIZABETH  II 
Elizabeth  is  wearing  the  diamond  and  pearl  circlet  of  Queen  Victoria.  The  design 
incorporates  the  Tudor  rose,  Scotch  thistle,  and  Irish  shamrock.  The  Queen  is  also 
wearing  the  diamond  necklace  which  was  a  wedding  gift  from  the  Nizam  of  Hyderabad, 
and  a  bracelet  given  her  by  her  husband,  the  Duke  of  Edinburgh.

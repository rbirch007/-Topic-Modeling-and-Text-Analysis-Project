Nephi 11:16-17). HE Easter season causes us to meditate the great sacrifice of the Son of God, his resurrection to glory, which accomplishes the resurrection of all mankind. The appearance of the resurrected Savior to the Nephites on this continent, following his ascension into heaven with his body of flesh and bone, brought great joy and happiness to the Nephites, also hope and assurance of eternal life. His glorious manifestation, his divine teachings,. The great miracles performed, and the visitation of heavenly hosts led to the conversion and complete faithfulness of both Nephites and Lamanites.

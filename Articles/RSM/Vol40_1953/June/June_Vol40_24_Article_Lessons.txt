Suggestions on Teaching Aids for 1953-54
Lessons Leone G. Layton Member, Relief Society General Board HE general board encourages the use of such teaching aids as are appropriate for Relief Society. They should add lesson interest, clarify subject matter, aid in remembering, and forward lesson objectives. In planning for their use we suggest you refer to the article, "The Value and Use of Audio-Visual Aids in Relief Society," published in the January 1953 issue of T'he Relief Society Magazine, page 27.

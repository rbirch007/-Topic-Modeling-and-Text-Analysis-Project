A Visit to Relief Society Headquarters Leone O. Jacobs Member, General Board of Relief Society T is a real pleasure to meet you, Mrs. D - - -, and tell you something about the Relief Society. I enjoy discussing our organization with visitors, and, particularly, with someone like yourself, who has come to our country especially to learn about women's organizations, for we maintain that the Relief Society is unique among all women's organizations. Here we are at the headquarters of Relief Society on the second floor of this building at 4o North Main Street.

[Vol44 | January 1957 | Sequence: 36 | Category: Poem | Title: Today | Author: Carroll]
> Joday Elsie Chamberlain Carroll Today is a sunlit pathway Between two shadowy nights, Where oblivion and darkness Shut out the sun's bright lights. May I let no shadows of tomorrow, No griefs of yesterday Cast their clouds upon the hours That are mine, all mine today. If tomorrow holds but sadness, Or never comes, for me, I have today in which to weave From life a lovely tapestry. iW 9} Wa? Inte? Trewt coe enacne oe REX A. @KEOMERE ° ——-* 1.

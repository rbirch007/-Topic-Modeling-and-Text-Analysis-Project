[Vol44 | February 1957 | Sequence: 20 | Category: Lesson | Title: Jt | Author: Romolo]
> Lt Ys Love Gene Romolo It is love that knows the word to speak, To increase hope and courage in the meek. Love's inspiration creates smiles and song, And helps us cleave to right and conquer wrong. It is love that glorifies each day, And sees some good in all along life's way— Even those whose fect have never trod The faith-paved way that Icads mankind to God. It is love that tunes the soul to be In harmony with tones of Deity— For it is only love that can impart A Christ-like fortitude unto the heart.

[Vol44 | March 1957 | Sequence: 28 | Category: Poem | Title: Apricot | Author: Leitner]
—_> Apricot Sree Della Adams Leitner From my north window I can see A tree lace-clad in spring array Against the background of the hills, 'It heralds all the joys of May. Each time I look out I rejoice In loveliness that I can share; My heart is stirred to gratitude, I softly breathe a thankful prayer. It sways so gently in the breeze, The birds are flitting in and out; They voice the praise that I would sing, Dispelling thoughts of fear and doubt. At harvest time the luscious fruit On laden boughs will fill the tree, But all the beauty it now gives Supplies a feast of joy to me.

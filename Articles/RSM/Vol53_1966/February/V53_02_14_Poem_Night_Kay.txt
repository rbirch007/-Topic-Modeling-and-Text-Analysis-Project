[Vol53 | February 1966 | Page 109 | Poem | Night Life | Kathryn Kay]
NIGHT LIFE Kathryn Kay "You are courageous,"' People tell me, You are brave and valiant, too." Which, though vastly complimentary Is not absolutely true, For I think perhaps my pillow Knows me better than most folk It thinks me more a weeping willow Than a sturdy oak!

[Rain River Clara Laster]
Hate sends its hurtful message Straight to the anguished heart that must give heed; And lust and greed are noisy and derisive— Into the mangled air they shriek their creed. But love speaks softly, and is often silent, Trusting frail threads of thought too swiftly broken.

[Vol53 | February 1966 | Page 158 | Poem | New Friend | Ethel Jacobson]
New Friend Ethel Jacobson There is something more aware In the air. The cedars wear a sheen Of deeper green, And lark song strikes the ear More blithe and clear. Horizons circle wide, With love inside, And life has a rich bouquet, For today Brought me this dividend: A new friend.

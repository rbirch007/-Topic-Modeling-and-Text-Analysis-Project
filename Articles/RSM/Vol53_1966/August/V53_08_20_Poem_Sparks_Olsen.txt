Olsen Fine sparks of creativity, expressed in various ways, Exist in all — to some degree When we stop to appraise. Not only on a paper, canvas, screen or on a stage— Or through an instrument or voice Do artists best engage. More often with a salad, or fashioning a dress— In managing a loving home Real artists find success. The neighbor who rejoices or grieves along with you Releases sparks and sometimes flames of love— Creating, too! In all human relationships, artistic skill of some Will kindle charity of heart For many years to come!

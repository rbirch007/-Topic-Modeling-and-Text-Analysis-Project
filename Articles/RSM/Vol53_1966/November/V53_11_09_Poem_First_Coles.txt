FIRST THINGS FIRST Christie Lund Coles The party indeed has been lovely: The music, the program, the friends; Your garden is truly enchanting Just see how that willow tree bends. I do dislike having to leave you, But must rush to another affair: A small girl's birthday party, Incomplete without Grandma there.

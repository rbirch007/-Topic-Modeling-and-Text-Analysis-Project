If so, please notify The Relief Society Magazine office, 76 North Main Street, Salt Lake City, Utah 84111, four weeks in advance, what your new address will be. Be sure to include your old address, or send the address label from one of your magazines. The Post Office does not forward copies of the Magazine without charging additional postage, but the Magazine oftice will send the Magazine to you without interruption, if you promptly notify them of your move.

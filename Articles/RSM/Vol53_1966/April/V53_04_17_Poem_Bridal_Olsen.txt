[Vol53 | April 1966 | Page 277 | Poem | Bridal Spring Pearle | M. Olsen.]
BRIDAL SPRING Pearle M. Olsen Expected beauties warm our ready hearts With things familiar in spring's lavish days Of blossoming in bridal finery— The drifting petals, choirs of lilting birds— And bees in winged ballet Give us delight—in bountiful degree!

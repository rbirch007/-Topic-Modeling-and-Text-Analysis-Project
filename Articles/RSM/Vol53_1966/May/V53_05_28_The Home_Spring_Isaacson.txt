[Spring Dream Salad Dressing Ida A. Isaacson]
14 c. Vegetable oil 14 ¢. Light vinegar 14 c. Sugar dash paprika sprinkle of pepper 1 green pepper (medium sized, finely 1 can tomato soup cut) 1 tsp. Salt (or less for individual taste) 1 onion (size of small egg), minced 1 tsp. Mustard (prepared) 1% c. Finely cut celery Blend in your blender or mixmaster, or shake well in a bottle, all ingredients except the green pepper, onion, and celery. When the other ingredients are blended, toss in the cut green pepper, the minced onion, and the cut celery. Keep in a covered jar in the refrigerator. This wonderful dressing is delicious with lettuce and sliced tomatoes, or lettuce alone. Any combination of salad vegetables is delectable with this dressing. 364

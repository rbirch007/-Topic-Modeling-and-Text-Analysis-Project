JOURNAL OF MARRIAGE Vesta Nickerson Fairbairn Bound by the sterling clasp of time, This journal keeps remembrances These living lines Of joys and sorrows, Are memories of marriage years The proof that loves grows strong to meet In shared designs. Unknown tomorrows. The lengthening inheritance Unwritten are the years ahead. Fills leaf on leaf, These pages hold Recorded wisdom, strength, and faith The final truths that only time In treasured sheaf. And God unfold.

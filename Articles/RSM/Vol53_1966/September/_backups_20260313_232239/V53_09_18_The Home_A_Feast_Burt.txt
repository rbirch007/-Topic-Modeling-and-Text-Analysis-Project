682 Along the dock are stacks of fish. Baked kingfish with broccoli. The famous "Key Lime Pie" ENTREE: _ Baked Kingfish Steak one 1-inch steak per person lime juice corn meal Parmesan cheese, grated butter or margarine Sprinkle each steak with lime juice. Rub well with melted butter. Place in a shallow baking dish and cover with corn meal and grated cheese mixed together. Dot with butter and bake at 350°F for 25 minutes. (If kingfish is unavailable, halibut, salmon, or other fish steaks do very well.) Sea Food Sauce 2 c. Chili sauce 1 c. Catsup 14 tbsp. Worcester sauce 2 tbsp. Horseradish 2 tbsp.

ERRATA—ERRORS IN PERCENTAGES—MAGAZINE HONOR ROLL FOR 1966 Through an oversight, subscriptions for Middleton Ward, Nampa Stake, were omitted in total for the stake. Subscriptions should have been 1,000, with 980 members, resulting in 102% instead of 96%. This would place them with the ten stakes with the highest number of subscriptions. Also, Melbourne Stake (Australia) was erroneously listed with 72%, and it should have been 87%. Also Juarez Stake should have been listed as 100% instead of 74%. 662

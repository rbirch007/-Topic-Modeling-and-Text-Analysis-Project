The Breath of Life The National Tuberculosis Association (United States of America) aut d = ) — ' AS as 2 pt 7 ~ > GY? MN ah b GQ. "GREETINGS SP Holidays have a way of catching you off guard. It sometimes seems that before you even finish your Fourth of July celebration, you're busy making your Christmas shopping list. That's just the way time seems to fly for those who work and play hard 365 days of the year. But the year goes much slower for hundreds of thousands of our fellow citizens. They can't play or work hard because they suffer from tuberculosis or another one of the serious respiratory diseases that threaten the life and breath of us all. Emphysema and chronic bronchitis are lung diseases that are increasing in alarming, epidemic proportions in the United States. Deaths from these diseases have doubled in the last five years. Emphysema has grown to be the second leading cause of disability in the nation. It has medical scientists worried because the cause and cure of emphysema are not known. There is some progress, however, in rehabilitating those who have the disease, particularly in the early stages. Your annual Christmas Seal Campaign is an important source of funds that support tuberculosis case finding and treatment, and the health education and research needed to combat these breath-robbing illnesses. When you get your Christmas Seal letter, please answer it immediately, and then be generous also in using Christmas Seals on all the greeting cards, letters, and gift packages that you mail out. It is all right if the holidays sneak up on you, but don't let crippling respiratory diseases catch you off guard. Remember Christmas Seals and protect your breath of life.

Orders received at the office of The General Board of Relief Society 76 North Main Salt Lake City, Utah 84111 957 REMEMBERING FAITH Enola Chamberlin I remember seeing faith In my mother's eyes As, following my father's dreams, She looked at alien skies. I remember seeing faith In smiles with which she met The hardships of a dusty road, The children's tired fret. I remember seeing faith In her busy hands, As she set dear plants to grow In strange and unknown lands. I remember hearing faith, No matter drought or blight, in her voice when she knelt down To say her prayers at night.

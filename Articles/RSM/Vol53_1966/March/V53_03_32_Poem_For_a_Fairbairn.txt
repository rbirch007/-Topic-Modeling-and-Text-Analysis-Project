[Vol53 | March 1966 | Page 236 | Poem | For a New Stepdaughter | Vesta N. Fairbairn]
STEPDAUGHTER Vesta Nickerson Fairbairn How lovely and rare And cherished the more Because of the years We did not share, And now on the threshold Of womanhood, A daughter new Is mine to enfold.

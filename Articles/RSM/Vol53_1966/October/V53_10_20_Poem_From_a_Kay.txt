[Vol53 | October 1966 | Page 759 | Poem | From a Mother's Heart | Kathryn Kay]
FROM A MOTHER'S HEART Kathryn Kay I did not think these Hands of mine, so simply styled, Unused to miracles and very plain, Could ever be a source of balsam to my child And by their gentle touch help ease the pain. I know in life it must be each must have his share Of pain and grief, so in my mother-heart I pray, Let my child have the strength to bear what he must bear When hurts are deeper than my hands can brush away.

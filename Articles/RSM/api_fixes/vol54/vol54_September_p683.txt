Materials
Basically, there are two types of paper mâché, the smooth and the rough. The smooth type is made by gluing layers of newspaper together with a white glue, and the rough type is made by mixing tiny bits of shredded paper (mâché), water, and glue to form a paste. Paper mâché already shredded may be purchased under a variety of trade names.

CHICKEN "LABSCOVS" LUNCHEON    Linnie F. Robinson

I have heard this word and tasted this dish all my life. Since talking to my cousins all over the country, I find they are fond of this dish, but each has some slightly different version of it. The recipe I give, using chicken, is one mother made most often, but any meat could be substituted — beefsteak, beef cubes, lamb, mutton, veal, or lean pork.

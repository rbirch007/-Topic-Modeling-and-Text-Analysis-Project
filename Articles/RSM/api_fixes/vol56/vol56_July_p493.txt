Lesson Previews

Music: "Blanik" from Ma Vlast (My Fatherland) by Bedrich Smetana (1824-1884) Czech

Lesson 2—The Role of Education
"The object of true education is to make people not merely do the right things but enjoy them."—John Ruskin
    Northern Hemisphere: Fourth Meeting, November 1969
    Southern Hemisphere: April 1970
Objective: To show the development of individual capacity is a primary source of social or community strength.
Art: "The Girl in White" by Julian Alden Weir (1852-1919) American

Lesson 3—The Problem of Communication
"There is no more sure tie between friends than when they are united in their objects and wishes."—Cicero
    Northern Hemisphere: Fourth Meeting, January 1970
    Southern Hemisphere: May 1970
Objective: To illustrate that those who can communicate can work together, and those who work together make civilization possible.
Art: "Advice to a Young Artist" by Honore Daumier (1808-1879) French

Lesson 4—The Place of Entertainment
"Any pleasure which keeps the heart from God will be fatal to the soul."—Richard Fuller
    Northern Hemisphere: Fourth Meeting, February 1970
    Southern Hemisphere: June 1970
Objective: To show that there is a valid place for entertainment and pleasure in life but that these should always be in good taste and in accordance with Church standards.
Music: "The Telephone, a "mini-opera" by Gian Carlo Menotti (1911-    ) American (Italian-born)

Lesson 5—The Necessity of Service
"Humblest service done by willing and discerning souls is glory."—George Eliot
    Northern Hemisphere: Fourth Meeting, March 1970
    Southern Hemisphere: July 1970
Objective: To emphasize that service makes a citizen out of an inhabitant and a community out of a town.
Art: "The Blind Girl" by John Everett Millais (1829-1896) English

Lesson 6—The Power of Work and the Use of Leisure
"All true work is sacred; in all true work, were it but true hand-labor, there is something of divineness."—Thomas Carlyle
"Without leisure we must live above the surface of things; leisure brings penetration."—Manly Hall
    Northern Hemisphere: Fourth Meeting, April 1970
    Southern Hemisphere: August 1970
Objective: To stress that both work and leisure can contribute to improvement when balanced wisely.
Art: "La Grande Jatte" by Georges Seurat (1859-1891) French

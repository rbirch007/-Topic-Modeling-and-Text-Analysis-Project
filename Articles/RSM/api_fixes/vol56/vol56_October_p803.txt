SAN DIEGO STAKE (CALIFORNIA) RELIEF SOCIETY SPONSORS A
CULTURAL ARTS FESTIVAL, April 25, 1969

Presidents of San Diego Stake Relief Society who were honored, left to right:
Carolyn Westover, Counselor to the first stake Relief Society President, Mary Christen-
sen who is deceased, Rasmina Price, Counselor to Josie Bay, former member of the
General Board; Thora Halgren; Enid Miller, Ida Andersen; Thair Milne, current Presi-
dent.

Sister Milne reports: "We held a cultural arts festival featuring music, art, litera-
ture, and drama, to honor our city as it celebrated its 200th anniversary, and to
edify and enrich the lives of our sisters. An original pageant was presented which
portrayed the part Latter-day Saint women have played in the development of San
Diego, ending with a scene honoring each of our past and present Relief Society
presidents.

"Music added its grace to the evening in the form of a women's string quartet.
There were two original musical scores in the pageant. Over 100 pieces of art work
were on display, including many lovely creations of needlework. The creative writing
talents of our sisters were displayed through a printed booklet.

"Over 350 sisters participated in the festival and more than 700 attended. We
feel it was a very edifying event."

Preparing the Picture Background

The next step is to obtain a sturdy picture frame. Brother Ralph Huber was recruited to miter corners and to make some odd-dimensional frames, but the sisters also searched many fun hours in second-hand shops and old barns, and watched for sales to find the coveted frames at the best prices. The frames must be sufficiently strong to hold the weight of the glass and the plywood backing to which the glass is glued.

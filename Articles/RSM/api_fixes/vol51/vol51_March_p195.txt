TROPICANA ROSE

"Tropicana," 1963 winner, has won the most prizes—thirteen international awards. Seldom a new color bursts on the world of roses, and in such a perfect plant — a brilliant, almost fluorescent, pure orange-red. Medium large, very full, fragrant, very vigorous, tall; tough, glossy foliage. Non-fading. May attain the popularity of "Peace."

The
World's Favourite
Roses Dorothy J. Roberts

OF all the flowers on the earth, the rose has probably had the longest and closest association with man. It is believed to be the first flower brought under cultivation and has the most extensive written record, dating back thousands of years.

Rose—the word is the mother of romance and legend. It is the symbol of perfection and love. Roses graced the gardens of kings and climbed the cottages of the poor. Roses are a part of our life and our heritage. People, places, and things wear the word rose in their names. Roses abound in literature and music, as well as in the gardens of the world. They are grown extensively, not only for their beauty but for perfume and flavorings, for medicines and vitamin C content of the rose hips.

From that time when the key was turned for women, has come the great advancement of womankind over the face of the earth. From the eighteen members in 1842, the Society today has grown to over a quarter of a million members residing in every state in the United States and in fifty-one other countries.

Nauvoo, Illinois, in 1842, was situated on the broad Mississippi, near the frontier of America. On the brow of the hill a temple was rising through the sacrifice of the men and women of the Church.

Notes  to  the  Field 
Change  in  Order  of  Relief  Society  Lessons 
Attention  is  called  to  the  change  in  the  order  of  teaching  the 
social  science  and  literature  lessons.  Beginning  with  October 
1965,  the  social  science  "Teaching  the  Gospel  in  the  Home" 
lessons  will  be  taught  on  the  third  meeting  day  of  the  month 
instead  of  the  fourth  in  order  that  eight  lessons  may  be  presented 
in  these  correlation  lessons.  The  literature  lessons,  formerly 
taught  on  the  third  meeting  day  of  the  month,  will  move  to  the 
fourth  meeting  day. 
Two  social  science  lessons  "Teaching  the  Gospel  in  the  Home" 
(the  lessons  for  October  and  November  1965)  will  be  printed  in 
the  August  issue. 
The  work  meeting  lessons  for  October,  November,  and  De- 
cember will  be  a  continuation  of  the  course  "Molding  a  Happy 
Life,"  by  Winnifred  C.  Jardine. 
Erratum 
In  the  Honor  Roll  for  Nampa  Stake  (published  in  the  May  1965  issue 
of  the  Magazine,  page  381),  the  correct  figures  should  be  1,000  sub- 
scriptions, and  980  members,  resulting  in  102  per  cent,  which  places 
Nampa  Stake  among  the  ten  stakes  with  the  highest  number  of  sub- 
scriptions.

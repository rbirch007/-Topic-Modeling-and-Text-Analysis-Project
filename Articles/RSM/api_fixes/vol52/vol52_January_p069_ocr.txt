of  your  unrealized  objectives?

you  need  to  make  were  you  Sister  A?

Dr.  Bruce  B.  Clark 
Lesson  7  —  Exploring  Right  and  Wrong  Attitudes  Through  Literature  —  Part   III 
For  Third   Meeting,  April   1965 
Objective:  To  show  how  literature  exposes  the  waste  of 
self-righteousness  and  hypocrisy,  and  affirms 
the  worth  of  genuine  righteousness  and  sincerity. 
The  lesson  for  this  month  is 
closely  related  in  purpose  to  the 
one  for  last  month.  Again  we  ex- 
plore several  significant  selections 
to  see  how  literature  exposes  evil 
and  defends  goodness.  However, 
whereas  last  month  we  studied 
four  short  stories,  this  month  we 
examine  five  poems;  and  whereas 
last  month  the  focus  was  on  self- 
ishness in  relation  to  unselfish- 
ness, this  month  the  focus  is  on 
self-righteousness  and  hypocrisy 
in  relation  to  genuine  righteous- 
ness and  sincerity.

A HERITAGE OF FAITH
by Joseph L. Wirthlin

An inspiring collection of speeches and sermons of the former Presiding Bishop of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. A valuable addition to every LDS library.

OUR WORD OF WISDOM
by David Geddes

Many new aspects of the "Word of Wisdom" are analyzed in view of scientific evidence. The true wisdom of the LDS law of abstinence from liquor, tea, coffee, and tobacco is shown. Other phases of the "Word of Wisdom" also included.

$2.95

by Elmer Peterson

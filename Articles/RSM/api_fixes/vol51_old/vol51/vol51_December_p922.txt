Christmas Tablecloth

Handmade Christmas tablecloth of white felt is bordered with gold braid and fruit motifs. Each fruit cluster surrounds a tiny Christmas light to give it an unusual glow. The centerpiece consists of fruit which also lights up, repeating the theme of Christmas boughs, candles, and glowing fruits. The felt cloth has been traditionally used on the Jacobsen's table for many years and was made by Sister Jacobsen.

Singing Mothers Chorus in the Salt Lake Tabernacle                LORRY RYTTING

Singing has ever been in the hearts of Relief Society members, whether they were crooning to their babies in a wagon box, in a log cabin, or in a split-level home today. Singing Mother choruses are located in every stake. In 1963 there were 41,865 Singing Mothers of Relief Society. Concerts are held periodically in some localities, music is furnished for funerals, and choruses render music at small and large Church gatherings.

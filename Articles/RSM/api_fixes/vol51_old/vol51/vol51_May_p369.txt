Alma A. Fernelius "Lady of Lovely Crocheting"

Alma A. Fernelius, Ogden, Utah, is very busy with housework, garden work, and Relief Society activities and yet she finds time for crocheting, her principal and special hobby. She has crocheted eight bedspreads and nine tablecloths, as well as edgings for many pairs of pillowslips, doilies, corners for napkins, and numerous other designs and motifs. She is a meticulous housekeeper and takes care of a beautiful garden. Her busy hands and her happy heart are an inspiration to her family and her many friends. She has been a teacher for almost fifty years, and has been a counselor to three Relief Society presidents.

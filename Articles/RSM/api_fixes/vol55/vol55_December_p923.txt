Chicken Parisienne

Place 4 large or 6 medium-sized chicken breasts, skinned side up, in an 11 x 7 x 1½ inch baking dish. Combine 1 can (13 ounces) condensed milk and one can (10 ounces) mushroom soup, and 1 3-ounce can or ¾ cup mushrooms and liquid with 1 c. dairy sour cream. Pour over uncooked chicken and sprinkle generously with paprika. Bake at 300° for 1 to 1¼ hours or until tender and a golden brown. Serve over hot, fluffy rice.

Ground Beef Casserole

1 large onion (chopped and                  1 c. chopped celery (uncooked,
    sautéed in oil)                             finely chopped)
1 can cream of mushroom                     soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste
    soup (10 ounces)

Brown the beef in a large frying pan. Add the chopped onion, cream soup, rice, and seasoning, and cook slowly until done, approximately one hour. Just before removing from heat add the celery. It should be crisp when eaten.

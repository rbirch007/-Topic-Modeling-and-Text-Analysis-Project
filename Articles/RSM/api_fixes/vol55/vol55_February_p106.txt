To You With Love

■ Each time the visiting teachers call at a home, they bring a valentine of love. "To You With Love," their visit says.
They are following a path worn smooth by the feet of faithful sisters who have walked the path of loving service for nearly one hundred twenty six years.
They walk over sun-baked sandy paths, between lush green growth, through waving palms, over icy trails, through crowded streets; they go by automobile, horseback, scooters, bus, and even by plane, on occasion.
All over the world where the gospel light is piercing the darkness of unbelief in the Savior of the world, two messengers, in a spirit of sisterly solicitude, bring their valentine of love once each month throughout the year.

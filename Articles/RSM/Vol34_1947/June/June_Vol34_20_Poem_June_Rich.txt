JUNE Alice R. Rich Do you question it is here Bluest skies of all the year, Sweetest perfumes from the flowers, Densest shade in leafy bowers, Gayest songs from warblers' throats, Lilting lays and gladsome notes, Reddest cherries, busiest bees, Gentlest breeze in orchard trees, Mellowest moonlit skies above, Dearest time for youthful love? You have guessed it, oh so soon, Month of love and beautyJune! 414 started in used cologne or perfume bottles so RELIEF SOCIETY MAGAZINEJUNE 1947 you have chosen the "right" thing.

[Vol34 | July 1947 | Sequence: 25 | Category: Frontispiece | Title: Sego Lily Frontispiece Eva Willes Wangsgaard]
Willes  Wangsgaard 
Have  you  seen  the  sego  lily 
Lift  her  cup  upon  the  hills? 
Multiplying  willy-nilly, 
Catching  light  the  desert  spills? 
Have  you  plucked  a  dainty  cluster? 
Tojuched  the  petals,  white  and  thin? 
Thrilled  to  note  translucent  luster 
Fine  as  Dresden's  porcelain? 
Have  you  heard  the  simple  story 
How  the  segos  played  a  part 
In  our  people's  pain  and  glory? 
See!  Each  wears  a  purple  heart. 
Little  hillside  desert  flower, 
Take  the  thanks  which  you  deserve; 
In  our  fathers'  saddest  hour 
Nothing  was  too  small  to  serve. 
The  Cover:  "Sego  Lilies,"  Photograph  by  Dr.  Walter  P.  Cottam

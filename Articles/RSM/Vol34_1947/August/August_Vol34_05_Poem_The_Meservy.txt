[Vol34 | August 1947 | Sequence: 05 | Category: Article | Title: The Meservy]

EVENING Oliver K. Meservy When, from the crowding work of day, At twilight I am free, I love to walk among the trees And with my Maker be. And then my soul, in gratitude, Offers up a prayer; In the glory of the woodland, I feel God's presence there.

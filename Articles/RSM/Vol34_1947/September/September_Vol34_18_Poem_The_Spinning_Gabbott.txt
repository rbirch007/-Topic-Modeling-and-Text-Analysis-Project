THE SPINNING WHEEL
Mabel Jones Gabbott The spinning wheel Went round and round, And spun fine flax For Grandma's gown. And sometimes it spun Woolen thread, To make a cap For Grandpa's head. But now it doesn't Spin at all, It stands so quiet In the hall. Perhaps it dreams Of other years Of handcart trails, And pioneers.

[Vol34 | November 1947 | Sequence: 03 | Category: Article | Title: Anchored Andersen]

J ANCHORED Andrew M. Andersen My father paused as he plowed his land When a sudden wind turned the emerald fields Into billowing seas. The fields in motion, the undulating hills, The Sawtooth Mountains, and the transfixed crests Of the royal Tetons Became northern seas on the Danish coast. And his strong hands On the handles of a plow Felt a helmsman's wheel. But his feet stood firm on this land that he loved And his heart was anchored to a mighty faith.

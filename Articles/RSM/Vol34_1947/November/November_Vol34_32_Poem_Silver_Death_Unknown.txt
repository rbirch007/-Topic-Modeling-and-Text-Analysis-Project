SILVER  DEATH
Melba  S.  Payne
The  night  had  cast  a  silent  spell
Upon  the  scene  below  my  window
Where  the  flowers  sleep.
There  was  no  way  for  me  to  tell
That  night  would  bring  a  withering  breath
Of  hoary  frost  upon  the  vines.
How  could  I  know  that  with  the  dawn
I'd  find  this  silver  death?

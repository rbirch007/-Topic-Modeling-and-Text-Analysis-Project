Stewart This is not mine, to walk rough wagonways, To shield the dying from the rain, or blaze Of prairie sun; to portion meal, To take a doctor's role, and heal The sick, or hold a babe, newborn; _ To push a handcart so a wife might mourn The loss of husband.

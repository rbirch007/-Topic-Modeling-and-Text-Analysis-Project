Thoughts of Father By Amy Kemp
Of a pal I've known for years now, sent to Earth from God above. And this pal is always ready to realize my wants and cares; And I know he truly loves me; for I feel his silent prayers.
Father is the pal I speak of and the one I love so well, Always ready to assist me with more love than tongue can tell. Let me say he's pure and holy, humble in both thoughts and deeds, And he tries to make me happy, and provides my daily needs.

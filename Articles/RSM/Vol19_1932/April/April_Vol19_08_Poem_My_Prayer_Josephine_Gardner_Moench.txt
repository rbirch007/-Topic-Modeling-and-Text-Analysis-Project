MY PRAYER

By Josephine Gardner Moench

Grant me, O Lord, the strength to bear
The burdens that are mine to share;
To meet each trial with courage true,
And trust completely, Lord, in You.

Help me to see beyond today
The purpose of each trying way;
To know that sorrow, pain, and tears
Are but the harvest of the years.

Give me the faith to understand
That all things work by Thy dear hand;
That naught can come to me but good,
If I but live as Christians should.

Teach me to love my fellow man,
To help him in whate'er I can;
To speak kind words, to gentle be,
And thus reflect my love for Thee.

And when at last my work is done,
And I have fought and I have won,
May I hear Thy sweet voice say,
"Well done, my child, well done today."

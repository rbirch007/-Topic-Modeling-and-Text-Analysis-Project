First Home of George Washington By Glen Perrins Fredericksburg, Virginia, and especially this home near the city, will be the scene of special celebrations this year on Independence Day, July 4th, for it was here that George Washington was born. The home is in, excellent shape today and this year, the 200th anniversary of Washington's birth, it has been the Mecca of thousands. George was one of six children on this Virginia estate and spent his boyhood life in the fields about this home. But for his birth, Independence Day today might mean nothing to America, and the Revolutionary war might have ended in defeat!

Could We But See Ourselves as Others See Us
IN reading in Collier's this incident regarding Rupert Hughes and Peter B. Kyne, we are reminded of the Scotch bard's quaint saying?
"Oh wad some Power the giftie gie us To see oursels as ithers see us! It wad frae mony a blunder free us, And foolish notion."

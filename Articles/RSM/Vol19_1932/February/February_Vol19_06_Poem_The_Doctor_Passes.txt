The Doctor Passes
By Maxa Million

The doctor passes down the street,
His bag in hand, his step complete
With purpose. In his quiet way
He brings relief from pain today.

No fanfare marks his daily round,
No trumpets with their martial sound;
Just steady service, tried and true,
And skill that sees the sick ones through.

He knows the heartache and the fear
That come when loved ones suffer here;
He knows the joy when health returns
And hope within the bosom burns.

The doctor passes—blessed man!
Who serves according to God's plan,
Who heals the body, soothes the mind,
And leaves a trail of peace behind.

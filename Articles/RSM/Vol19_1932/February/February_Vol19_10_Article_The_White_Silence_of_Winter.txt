Acrostic—February
February; thou art like unto my soul.
Ever I am waiting for spring to unfold.
Be steadfast and true, my soul, like the months,
Remain in thy strength like the sod of the
field,
Unmoved by the passing events of the world,
Aided by faith in thy infinite goal,
Rest thee in peace and Jove and
faith.
Your time of joy is now—Be not afraid.

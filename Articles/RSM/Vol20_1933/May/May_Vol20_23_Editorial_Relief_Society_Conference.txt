RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE

THE Relief Society Conference held in the Tabernacle April 6th and 7th was one of the most inspiring and helpful conferences in the history of the organization. The attendance was large and the spirit of devotion and unity was manifest throughout all the sessions.

The officers' meeting held Thursday evening, April 6th, was attended by stake and ward officers from all parts of the Church. President Amy Brown Lyman presided and conducted the meeting. The reports given by the general officers showed the splendid progress being made in all departments of Relief Society work.

The general sessions held Friday, April 7th, morning and afternoon, were well attended. President Heber J. Grant spoke at the morning session, giving counsel and encouragement to the sisters. Other speakers included President J. Reuben Clark, Jr., Elder Melvin J. Ballard, and the general officers of the Relief Society.

The reports showed that the Relief Society is growing in membership and influence. The work being done in charity, in education, and in all phases of women's activities in the Church is most commendable. The spirit of service and devotion manifested by the sisters throughout the Church is an inspiration to all.

The conference emphasized the importance of the Relief Society in the great work of the Church and the vital part it plays in building up the kingdom of God on earth.

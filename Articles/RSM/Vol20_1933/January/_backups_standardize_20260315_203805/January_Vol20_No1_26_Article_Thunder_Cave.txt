"Thunder Cave"
"THUNDER CAVE," a series of twenty-one delightful adventures written by Jeremiah Stokes, beautifully and lavishly illustrated by Jack Sears will find a warm place in the hearts of the children. Giant Wigwah and Jasper and Zebbie will be eagerly followed for the stories sparkle with humor and the mystery element is clean and wholesome. The book has the endorsement of The Parent's Magazine, Office of the Publication is 142 E. First South.

Amanda McEwan Knight Called Home
AMANDA KNIGHT widow of Jesse Knight and mother of our Board Member Inez Knight Allen and mother in law of Jennie Brimhall Knight answered the summons of the angel of death December 15, 1932. She was a beautiful character kindly, generous, hospitable. She had an outstanding love for humanity and a gift for looking out after those in distress. She was eager to assist aspiring young people to gain an education. The entire Knight family have for years given unstintedly of their means to assist the Brigham Young University and have been a source of inspiration and encouragement in times of need to this worthy institution. Sister Knight was a fine home maker; friends and relatives were eager to go to her home and were loathe to leave its hospitable shelter. Her deep interest in the affairs of the day and her keen sense of humor were a delight to those who tarried with her. She has raised a worthy family of sons and daughters who bless her memory and will carry on the work of helpfulness and encouragement which so delighted their mother.

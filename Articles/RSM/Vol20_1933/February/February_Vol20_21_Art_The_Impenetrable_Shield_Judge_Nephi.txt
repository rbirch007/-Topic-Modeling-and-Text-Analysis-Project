Irving

His conclusion

But

I cannot say, "I will," unless

That is quite evident.
I cannot say "I can" unless I can say
"I believe
I can."
That

Moreover,

I Will not say "I

And,
I cannot say "I ought," unless
I can say "I believe
I ought."
So
back of will is the belief that we can
and ought to do the thing we will to
do.
That

faith.
It

faith that
moves the will to do, to dare, and to

It is faith that controls the

!
And guards the soul against

The Cloud
I saw a cloud the other day
Gloomy, dark and cold;
But
from
the
outer
edge
was thrown
A rim of purest gold.
By Miranda Walton
It made me think
the sun would
shine
Throughout
eternity;
I
pondered what
a
lovely
place
The other side must be.

WHO has the thread ?"

prised

"I

Annie Condor's hands

It was

Annie's

It's

There was a storm of protest. To

the
neatest

Annie looked calmly at the group

"I just want to say there's no use

Maybe—well,

"I guess

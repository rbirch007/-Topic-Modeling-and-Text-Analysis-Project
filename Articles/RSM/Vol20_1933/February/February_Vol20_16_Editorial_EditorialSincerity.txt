2. Some children seem unable to
raise the tongue to produce sounds
as
"1,
t, d, n."
If the tongue is tied
it
can
be
corrected
by
a
surgeon.
If the condition
is due to sluggish-
ness in the tongue muscles it can be
prevented by clear speaking models
in the home and social demands for
well pronounced words.
It can be
corrected by exercise.

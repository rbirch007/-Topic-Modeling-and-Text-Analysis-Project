Your Honor By Arthur James Bowers This is your day; To hope, to dream, to build. Stride forth with lances bare, Your heart with courage filled. This is your time; To fashion of the clay The dream, that tomorrow, May fade and pass away. This is your hour; The hour that you can change From just so many minutes Into a work of fame; Your hour—and it is yours— To make your work so true That name and gilded fame May live long after you.

Work of Agents Appreciated WE deeply appreciate the very fine work done by our Magazine Agents and those who assisted them. We are also grateful for the loyal response to their canvass. We are delighted with our present large subscription list. We remind our agents, however, that unless those whose subscriptions expire with the December number renew, that our lists will fall back again very materially. In soliciting for new subscriptions, agents might ask, "Where can you get a better magazine for one dollar? What could you give for a present that would be more appreciated than the Relief Society Magazine? ' While our intensive campaign is over, there is need for constant attention to see that expirations are renewed. Errata N our November issue two poems were printed with the wrong names. "Rebirth" was written by Claire Stewart Boyer and "Life Perfect" by Miriam Jeanne W. Wright.

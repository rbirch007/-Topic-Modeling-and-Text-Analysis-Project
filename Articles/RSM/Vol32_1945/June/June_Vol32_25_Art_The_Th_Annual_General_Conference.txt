Civilization is not wealth or numbers. It is the appreciation of human brotherhood and the.
Ceaseless pursuit of real justice. War is the outward sign of inner spiritual decay.
Give tireless watch-care to our boys.
When they are around, they may be a "pain in the neck," but when they are gone, they are a pain in the heart.
Nothing has strengthened my faith more than seeing the gospel in action in the lives of our men in the service.
Chastity strengthens man. It is the source of peace and happiness in the home. I like to think of life as beginning before mortality and enduring beyond death. Don't substitute money for welfare assignments.
Hungry saints in Europe can't eat money earmarked sugar or beef.
They need real sugar, beef, cheese, flour, and butter. I could not believe in a God who would be so unjust as to punish those who die without knowledge of his teachings. We must gain light and truth to go on toward Godhood.

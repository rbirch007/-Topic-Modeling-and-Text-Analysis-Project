[Vol32 | September 1945 | Sequence: 22 | Category: Article | Title: Hollyhocks Leitner]

HOLLYHOCKS Delia Adams Leitnei Hollyhocks so prim and tall, All along my garden wall; Bright array of pink and white, Shades of red and yellow bright, Single, double, frilled and plain, Stately but without disdain; Such a regal air they bear. And their dignity they wear Kingly, but so graciously. They are the garden's royalty.

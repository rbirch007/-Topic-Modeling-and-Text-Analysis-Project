[Vol32 | September 1945 | Sequence: 26 | Category: Article | Title: My Kitchen Hendrickson]

MY KITCHEN WINDOWS Violet Harris Hendrickson Today I planned my kitchen With crisp new window curtains. I fashioned them from bits of Swiss With dots like falling snow, And lest they seem too wintry, I appliqued bright flowers In a saucy row. I shined and polished every pane. To feel the sunlight's golden kiss, And prayed, "Oh, Father, Bless all the world With peace and joy like this!"

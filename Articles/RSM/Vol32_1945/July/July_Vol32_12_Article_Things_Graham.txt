[Vol32 | July 1945 | Sequence: 12 | Category: Article | Title: Things Graham]

Quoting Sister Jacobs' words, "Activity in Relief Society has brought me much joy, a greater testimony of the gospel, and many cherished friendships." THINGS BEAUTIFUL Rose T. Graham These are beautiful things: The peacock's rounded, wide-spread tail, The rainbow trout with shining scale. The armadillo's coat of mail; These are beautiful things. These are beautiful things: A baby in your arms to fold, A big boy quarrelsome and bold, A mother who can wisely mold; These are beautiful things.

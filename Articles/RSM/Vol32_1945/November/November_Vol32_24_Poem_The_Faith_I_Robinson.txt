[Vol32 | November 1945 | Sequence: 24 | Category: Article | Title: The Faith I Robinson]

THE FAITH I KNOW Linnie Fisher Robinson I face defeat, yet, in surprise, I find That even now I look to future skies; Before the ruins of my castle walls I sense that hope within me never dies. For I recall the beauty of my dream, The sweetness of each day with work to do, And though my labor netted naught, at last, There is a glory that my cause was true. And there is triumph in pursuit of such.
Each memory can soothe my present woe; Moments more favored, that I?ve known, crowd close-This is not doubt?this is the faith I know.

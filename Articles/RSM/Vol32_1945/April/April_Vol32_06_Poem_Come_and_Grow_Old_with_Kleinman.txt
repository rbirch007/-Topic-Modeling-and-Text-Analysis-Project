[Vol32 | April 1945 | Sequence: 06 | Category: Article | Title: Come and Grow Old with Kleinman]

COME AND GROW OLD WITH ME Bertha A. Kleinman Come and grow old with me. As trees grow old, Each season adding newer, finer wood? A timbered arch defying storm and cold, And nobler for adversity withstood. God made a tree? He likewise fashioned me To brave the rain, the thunder?shock and blast, A weathered oak that sheds security, From fruited boughs uplifted to the last.

[Vol32 | February 1945 | Sequence: 20 | Category: Poem | Title: Last Night They Played His Songs]

He left—
Without saying goodbye—
Just, "Hey, Mom!" as he pedaled away—
His little-boy voice, consumed by space,
Eludes my heart,
No sound comes back to me.

Aeons ago he left—
Without saying goodbye—
But that melody, all his songs—
And he sat close to me.

Hold your standards high.
Happiness will be the result; the higher the standards, the greater the happiness. God grant when he gathers his jewels that our mothers, wives and daughters, those who have worthily represented womankind in the world, may find themselves rewarded by a glorious welcome home to enjoy the sweet companionship in eternal happiness of those that they have lived with right here upon this earth. And in conclusion I pray that the spirit, the peace and the love of our Heavenly Father may continue to abide with you, my dear sisters, forever, in the name of Jesus Christ, our Lord. Amen.

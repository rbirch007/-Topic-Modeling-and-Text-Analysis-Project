[Vol32 | March 1945 | Sequence: 01 | Category: Article | Title: Notice of Presidency]

If the parents or wives of wounded men and women, if any, will send to the office of the First Presidency the names and addresses of their sons, husbands, or daughters who have been wounded and who are in this country for hospital treatment, we will see that their names reach the proper officer stake, ward, or mission so that such officer may be able to get in touch with the wounded and render such service as may be possible. Heber J. Grant J. Reuben Clark, Jr. David O. McKay The First Presidency The Cover: The cover is from a photograph by Willard Luce, "Spring Silhouette

[Vol49 | November 1962 | Sequence: 25 | Category: Poem | Title: Snow | Author: Douglas]
Snow Flowering Gilean Douglas How can we speak of May who have seen this blooming Of argent bough, this virginal blossoming: When, from the dark indifference of November, The evergreens have brought triumphant spring?

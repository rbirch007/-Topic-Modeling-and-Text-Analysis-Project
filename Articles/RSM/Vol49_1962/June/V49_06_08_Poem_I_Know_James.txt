[Vol49 | June 1962 | Sequence: 08 | Category: Poem | Title: I Know | Author: James]
Elaine James I know a thing, I know a thing! The birds must know it for they sing! And why should all the flowers turn bright Unless they, too, heard it aright? A ( The leaves know it, the winds blow it Softly where they nod and kiss —: Now why should they do this? If flowers below and trees above E Nod and mingle in their love, = It must be because it's spring; The secret's out — that's why they sing!

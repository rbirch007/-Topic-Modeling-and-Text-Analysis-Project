If you would rather, you can cut out the points and bind them with bias tape of matching color, so the tape only shows on the wrong side. If you want them to hang especially well, press them into pleats by folding the material from each point to the bottom of the curtain (the way you used to make a paper fan when a child). This enhances the appearance and the hang of the curtain. Attach a ring to each point and hang the rings over a rod.

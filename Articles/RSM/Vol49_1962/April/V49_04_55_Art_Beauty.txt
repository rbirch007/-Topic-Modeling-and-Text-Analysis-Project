Beauty Ida Isaacson Life is filled with beauty! See it! Feel it everywhere! There may be a thousand sorrows Almost too great to bear. But God has knowledge of this And took precautions there To fill our souls with beauty So that we could bear To lift the broken willow, Find another precious stone— For he would always be with us, Never leave us all alone.

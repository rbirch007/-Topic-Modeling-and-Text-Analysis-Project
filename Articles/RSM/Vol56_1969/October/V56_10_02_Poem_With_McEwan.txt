Miltenberger Appointed General reasurer The General Presidency of Relief Society is pleased to announce the appointment of Mayola Rogers Miltenberger as General Secretary-Treasurer of Relief Society. Sister Miltenberger has been serving as acting SecretaryTreasurer since June of 1969. Sister Miltenberger brings to this office devotion to the gospel and Relief Society in addition to her fine training and experience. She possesses grace and dignity and endears herself to those with whom she works. She is capable in meeting the requirements of any assignment given her, and finds joy in her work. Sister Miltenberger was named to the General Board in October of 1968. Prior to her appointment as acting Secretary-Treasurer she had rendered valuable service in the field of social service. She was no stranger to Relief Society at the time of her appointment to the Board, for she had served as director of the Relief Society Social Service Program for Arizona. Sister Miltenberger is the daughter of Marion and Leanora Smith Rogers. She was married in 1937 to the late Edwin John Miltenberger. Sister Miltenberger attended Brigham Young University and was graduated from the University of Arizona. She obtained her Master's degree in social work from Tulane University, where she also served as a field instructor. She is a member of the Academy of Social Workers, and a charter member of the National Association of Social Workers. Sister Miltenberger has served as a ward and branch Relief Society president, as MIA president, and as a Sunday School teacher.

[Nauvoo, Sadie 0. Clark]

Hunt has endeared herself to sisters throughout the world since her appointment to the General Board in 1957. In her twelve years of service on the board she has traveled extensively, and has always been equipped to meet the responsibilities of conference assignments with intelligence and dignity. Eisa T. Peterson as served on the General Board since 1958. In her eleven years as a board member she has demonstrated countless qualities of leadership at regional conferences. Her social experiences and good taste have been invaluable in helping with conference receptions and other social activities. I 748 General Board Members Released Zola J. McGhie became a member of the General Board in 1962. Her service in the homemaking area of Relief Society and her contributions to the social activities of the Board have made her a valuable asset during her seven years of service. She has willingly fulfilled every assignment that has been given to her. Anne R. Gledhill received her appointment to the General Board in 1964. As a board member she has rendered outstanding service in regional conferences, and has achieved a valuable rapport with those whom she has served. The united love of the Board goes with these sisters for their loyal support of the organization. May the service they have rendered continue to inspire and elevate others to loftier goals, and may Relief Society always be a joy and blessing to them. NAUVOO -Nauvoo was a young man's town, When they braced the fearful fury of the foe.

He dearly loves them and would like them to remain with him always, but for their development and progress, they must be parted for awhile. And so each one, as the time is right, is sent to earth to gain experiences here. Perhaps you were feeling and know to some degree, how very difficult it was for us to leave you walking alone up that beautiful tree-lined path toward the door of your future. As you turned to wave goodbye, you suddenly seemed so very young. This in direct contrast to the thoughts I had been thinking for many monthsthat you had grown tall and were becoming an adult. And now you seemed almost too young to leave on your own. The years have slipped by rapidlyso very rapidly! As you turned and walked up the pathway, I felt an assurance that many doors would open for you following this day, because of the choice you made when you chose to open the door of education for your future. 7 <=> _ >=.

If one does not have the advantages of being surrounded with artists in his own family, there is a renaissance of art the world over and good art is available in city galleries and even in small communities. Traveling exhibits, art schools, and art books are plentiful where one can learn and discern a good work of art and truly determine what he likes when the time comes to add a painting or a piece of sculpture to his valued possessions. If I could be given a wish for beauty and contentment, I would choose a good work of art to live by.

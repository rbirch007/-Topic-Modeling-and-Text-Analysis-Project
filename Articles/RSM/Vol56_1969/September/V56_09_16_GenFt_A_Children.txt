A Bazaar Designed Especially for Children Helen M. Dall, President, Kearns North Stake Relief Society Karen Sorensen, President, Seventeenth Ward Relief Society @ Our whole Bazaar was especially focused on items of interest for children and this plan turned out to be a great success. A special booth was prepared to illustrate helps for Family Home Evening. Assignment charts, flannel boards, plaques, lap boards, and activity sheets were prepared. Items for children were attractively displayed. The items for sale in the varous displays were all donated. Sister Sharon Seamons was chairman. She had made up a sample item and then assigned several sisters to make the desired number of each item. Over thirty sisters were involved in one single booth, and all seemed willing to help. Several sisters who do not sew or do handwork were especially willing to help with decorating the booths and ar- 682 ranging the displays. Many sisters who are a little timid seemed glad when they were shown what to do.

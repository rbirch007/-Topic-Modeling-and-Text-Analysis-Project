SCAVENGERS? Ruth G. Rothe

We are the scavengers
Of time and space,
Picking up the pieces
Of a broken place.

We gather fragments
Of a shattered dream,
Sorting through the rubble
Of what used to seem

So whole, so perfect,
So complete and right—
Now scattered, broken,
Lost from sight.

But in our searching,
In our patient care,
We find the beauty
That is always there

In smallest pieces,
In the broken parts—
The love that mends
All broken hearts.

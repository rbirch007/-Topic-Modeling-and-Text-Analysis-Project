WAYFARER
Ethel Jacobson

I have walked many a weary mile
Along life's dusty way,
And I have learned that just a smile
Can brighten up the day.

I've found that when the road seems long
And burdens hard to bear,
A cheerful word, a little song
Can lighten every care.

So as I journey on my way,
Whatever storms may come,
I'll try to make each passing day
A bit of heaven's sum.

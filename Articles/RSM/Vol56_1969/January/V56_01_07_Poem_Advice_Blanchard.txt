[Vol56 | January 1969 | Page 20 | Poem | Advice to the Vulnerable Gay | N. Blanchard]
19 Glacier Point; Yosemite Photo by Don Knight ADVICE TO THE VULNERABLE Never say, ''For the last time' About a lovely thing. Savor with joy the moment And let the heart sing. Too final a sound, ''For the last time," Too poignant the sudden ache. If ecstasy's gift be thus stolen The heart may silently break. Drink to the full each rapture Take life through the open gate. And if it should be ''For the last time " Oh, let that knowledge wait. ~Gay N.

[Vol56 | January 1969 | Page 78 | Poem | Veil the Future | Norma Bowkett]
Happiness is otal electric living UTAH POWER & LIGHT CO. VEIL THE FUTURE If I knew then What I know now— That death would take you In your youth— Would I With my protective Cry Inhibit your ascent to truth? Beneath the Lord's protective bough Time was your friend. Free as the sun You smiled your warmth On everyone. Compassionate, wise, The One above Veils the future With his love. —Norma Bowkett

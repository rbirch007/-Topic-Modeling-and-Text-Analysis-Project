SS NIGHT FALL So quietly creeps the evening gray, As the birds' last song floats gently Through the brown beach, copper red by day From the sun's kindly gleam. Softly blend these darkened shadows Rocked in the bosom of the wind, As night falls their brightness fades Into the sleeping dusk. Margaret Read Norwich, Norfolk, England.

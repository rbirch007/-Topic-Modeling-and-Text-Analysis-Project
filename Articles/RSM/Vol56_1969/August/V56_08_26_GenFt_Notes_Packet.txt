Finlinson, member, General Board of Relief Society; Gale Sanders, President, Idaho Stake Relief Society; Ordis Williams, Education Counselor; Beverly Michaelson, Homemaking Counselor; Asa Hatch, High Council Priesthood Representative. Remaining sisters are officers and honored visiting teachers of the Soda Springs Second Ward Relief Society. Sister Sanders reports: ''The theme for the visiting teacher convention in the Soda Springs Second Ward was 'It's a feather in Your Hat to Be a Visiting Teacher.' This theme was carried out in the clever invitations delivered to each sister by the visiting teachers, and in the table decorations. This function was held in celebration of the birthday of Relief Society. 'Luncheon was served to eighty-five visitors and guests. Sister Zina Balls was honored for having served forty years as a visiting teacher. Sister Luella Finlinson of the General Board addressed the sisters and praised and encouraged respect for the calling of visiting teacher. "This was a lovely occasion and was enjoyed by al!

Miltenberger Appointed Acting SecretaryTreasurer The General Presidency of Relief Society is pleased to announc the appointment of Mayola Rogers Miltenberger, member of the General Board, as acting Secretary-Treasurer, to fill the vacancy occasioned by the death of former Secretary-Treasurer Evon W. Peterson. Sister Miltenberger was appointed to the General Board in October 1968. (See The Relief Society Magazine, January 1969, p. 16.) She will continue as the administrative supervisor of the Relief Society Social Service programs in Salt Lake City, Arizona, Nevada, and Idaho, while devoting a major part of her time as SecretaryTreasurer. Sister Miltenberger has great executive abilities and an abiding love for Relief Society. She desires to serve in any capacity to which she is called and will render valuable service for Relief Society at this time in her new assignment. She has a deep interest in the work of the sisterhood throughout the Church. They will enjoy their association with her.

The Cover: Frontispiece: Art Layout: Illustrations: Matterhorn and Riffels Transparency by Camet Lithographed in Full Colé Calla Lilies Photograph by Don Knight Dick Scopes ' Mary Scopes LET ME BE FREE Let me be free! For there are stars to lean upon And newer worlds with lovelier songs to sing. Long have I companied the world and hungered, Thinking to find them in earth's fostering. Let me be free! For there are ways untrammeled, As there are lilies with their petals still unblown; And always thoughts like heartbeats calling. Write, write, for we are yet unknown. Let me be free!

QF GRANDMA'S COOKIES No one could make the cookies just like she could, Some were soft and spicy, raisin filled, Others were real crunchy, topped with sugar, At making brownies she was highly skilled. For holidays she used her special cutters, Hearts, and stars, and chubby boys and girls, Ginger, butterscotch, and plain vanilla, Baked and trimmed with little frosting swirls. Her cookie jar was very seldom empty, She always seemed delighted when we'd come, And what was best of all about her cookies, She invited one and all to, ''Please try some." Ruth G.

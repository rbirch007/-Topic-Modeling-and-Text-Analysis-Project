[In Memoriam Elder John Longden]

The darkness was a lack of assurance of life beyond the time of earth and a knowledge of a previous habitationa lack of direction and purpose for the pathway between birth and the next estate. The birth of the Savior signified the divine gift of a restoration and a promise of life everlasting through the mission of the Savior, who ''ascended up on high... And gave gifts unto men."' (Ephesians 4:8.) Volume 56 November 1969 Number 11

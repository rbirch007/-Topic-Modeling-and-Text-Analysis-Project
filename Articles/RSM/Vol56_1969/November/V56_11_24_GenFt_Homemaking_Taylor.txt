**WINGED RETURN**

I sent her out to shake a rug
When she was six and three:
She watched a bird with wings of wind
And never quite came back to me.

One day, full-grown, she went away
With love strong for her nest.
Still, when the days and duties weigh,
She flies to me for rest.

—Leora Larsen

Dorothy J. Roberts

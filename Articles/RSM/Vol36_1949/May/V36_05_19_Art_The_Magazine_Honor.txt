Honor Roll Percentages Continuing the policy established in 1948, the membership figures on which the Honor Roll percentages for 1949 will be based, will be counted as of January 1949. This allows each Magazine representative to obtain from the secretary, at once, the membership figures of January 1949 on which her percentage for this year will be figured. In this way a Magazine representative may know all through the year the number of subscriptions she must obtain in order to be on the 1949

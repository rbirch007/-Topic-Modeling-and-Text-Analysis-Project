Laurgaard ITlustration by Elizabeth Williamson Wey not start Why not start a tradition in your family this year of keeping Christmas candles burning throughout the holidays? 

Begin on Christmas Eve by lighting your first candle—a single white taper will do. Let it burn throughout the evening, and relight it each night until Twelfth Night. Each evening as you light your candle, read a Christmas story or sing a Christmas carol.

If you have several children, let each one have his own candle to tend. The warm glow of candlelight will add much to the Christmas spirit in your home, and the daily lighting ceremony will help keep the true meaning of Christmas alive in young hearts throughout the season.

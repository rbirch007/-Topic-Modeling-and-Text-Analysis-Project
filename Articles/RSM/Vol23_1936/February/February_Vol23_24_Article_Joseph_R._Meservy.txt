[Vol23 | February 1936 | Sequence: 24 | Category: Article | Title: Joseph R. Meservy]

Discuss the safest, most practical and most economical method of caring for such fabrics as silk, wool. Cotton, linen and rayon. How and why do they differ? VICTORS By Joseph R. Meservy We must be tried and taste the bitterness of life. If we endure the sorrow and the strife That chance to meet us as we go our way, And if we're ever faithful day by day, We shall be all the stronger for each ill We've struggled past and overcome, until We'll find ourselves with strength enough to win And always be the victor over sin.

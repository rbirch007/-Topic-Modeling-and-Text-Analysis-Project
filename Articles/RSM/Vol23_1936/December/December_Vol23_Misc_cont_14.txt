(Continued from page 773) A thought is such a simple gift, Its price is just an eager thrift Of mind, a yearning of the heart To share, its beauty to impart. A thought is such a precious thing No gift of gold could ever bring The joy eternal and the light, O pause and send a thought tonight!
Christ had no wealth to give away, And yet the world is rich today Because His wisdom had divined The grace of thought to save mankind.

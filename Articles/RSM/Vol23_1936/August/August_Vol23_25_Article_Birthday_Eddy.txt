They always used to be so gay and jolly But now it's just another lonely day, Futile and dull with haunting melancholy. Until you speak,—"It means a lot to me, It, gave me you." So tender is your smile That suddenly it is a happy day. Undoubtedly these birthdays are worth while! "UNIVERSITY IN THE WILDERNESS" Only three years after their arrival in Deseret, the Pioneers founded the University of Urtah—the first school west of the Missouri River bearing the title of university. Steadily has it pursued the ideal of leadership and progress, J NOW "Utah" has enviable rank among higher institutions of learning in America. A beautiful campus, with imposing buildings; a scholarly faculty and varied training—are yours to command.

With deep respect my solemn thoughts salute! And then assuaged and speechless they are still. Hark!
Millions of slow footsteps in the cove: Brave lives spent for the good of those they love? My solemn thoughts salute and then are still.
Where are Youth's unachieved hopes long since born, Though steps are slowed and hair is silver grey?
Where disappointments in grave silence borne And hid with smile's deception, day by day?

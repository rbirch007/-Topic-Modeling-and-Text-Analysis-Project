[Vol24 | August 1937 | Sequence: 23 | Category: Article | Title: Anthem Serve and Praise Richards]
Kingdoms?nations?cease from warring, To all lands their rights restoring, To each tribe its own accord. Watch the course of "Jesse's Stem?" "Jews rebuild Jerusalem?" Ephraim here, "Earth's" polished gem, All shall serve the Lord.
By His gracious hand protected, With the just be resurrected? To secure such high award, Selfish pride and envy shun, See each duty justly done, And with Christ the perfect One Serve and praise the Lord.

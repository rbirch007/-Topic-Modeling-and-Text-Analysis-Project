[Vol24 | November 1937 | Sequence: 22 | Category: Guide Lessons | Title: Work and Business
I gave from a sense of duty
And looked for reward of praise,
My efforts and work were fruitless,
Ingratitude burdened my days.

I gave for the joy of giving,
Nor thought of returns to be,
And lo! from unnumbered sources
My recompense came to me.

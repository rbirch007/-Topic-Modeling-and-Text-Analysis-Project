One Year Old

Christie Lund Coles

Today I taught her
How to blow the fluff
From a seeded dandelion,
Watching the dainty stuff

Rise with the slightest breeze.
I watched her unbelief
At a bubble, rounded,
Iridescent, brief.

I saw her shape her lips,
Blowing hard. After,
I heard a small whistle,
And her proud laughter.

Butter Mints

Dorothy Scott

3 c. sugar
1 sq. butter (¼ lb.)
   coloring
1 c. hot water
   dash salt
   peppermint flavor

Stir sugar, butter, hot water, and salt together in a pan and bring to a boil. Cover with a lid for 3 minutes. Take off lid and wash down sides of pan with brush dipped in water. Cook without stirring to 248°, on a clear day. Pour on buttered slab and cool enough to handle. Add few drops flavoring and coloring and pull until it loses gloss. Stretch into a rope and cut into pieces. Store in an air-tight can.

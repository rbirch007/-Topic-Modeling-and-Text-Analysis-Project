The woman giving the lesson evidently was unprepared, and our new member was very disinterested to the point that she thought she would not go again. Shortly, the Magazine began arriving in her home. She read it from cover to cover. When she came to the lesson material that the unprepared sister had poorly given, she read it and thought, why this is beautiful. I guess I didn't understand it. She decided she must be a part of the discussion of the lessons which she was enjoying reading. The Magazine had aroused her interest, rejuvenated her spirit, and rekindled her desire to be a part of the kingdom. Yes, The Relief Society Magazine is a great power, an influence, and a factor of worth in our fight with Satan. I have been reading it for thirty-five years. When on my first mission to Switzerland, as a young boy, I was given the assignment of working with the Relief Society.

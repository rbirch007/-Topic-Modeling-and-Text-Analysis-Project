And makes this present to the past how poor Here I do come unto this sacred room, And shut the world out as I lock the door. Yet still thine eyes look on me from the gloom, As through the hoijrs I ceaseless pace the floor. O when I take thcpen, as now, and write, Yet think of thee and finish not the line.

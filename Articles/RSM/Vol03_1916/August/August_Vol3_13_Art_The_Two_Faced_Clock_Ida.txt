Although the

Surprises are

The family threw themselves into the preparations gallantly.
Fanny,
Bob's

all

Geraldine, the second sister-in-law,

The affair was to be one of father's favorite
chicken

Margie,

She was to

At eight o'clock on the eventful day, the whole family assem-
bled.
Bob, stout and jocular, as became the eldest member, tip-
toed through the kitchen door with Bob,
Jr., and Fanny trailing
breathlessly after him.

Isobel

So the family settled down in the parlor to wait.

I

I believe this has the distinc-

sion

We only have
one mail boat a month to our main
island, which
is Tahiti.
On account

in America and

from April 24 until July 19.
There

Travel between

trading

es each with a Relief Society organi-
zation

It

the

There are French
schools now

Tahiti

some
few
of
the
other
islands,
and
the
people
are

of
the
chance
to

There

little literature printed in the native
language, as what there

the
different
churches.
Our

is outlined under the direction

to

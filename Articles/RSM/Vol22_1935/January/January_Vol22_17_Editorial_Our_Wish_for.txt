Editor Manager Assistant Manager Mary Connelly Kimball Louise Y. Robison Amy Brown Lyman Vou. XXII JANUARY, 1935 No. 1 EDITORIAL Our Wish For You
MAY 1935 brings to all of our readers blessings unnumbered. May peace abound in their hearts and homes. May their: spirituality increase and may they seize and profit by the many opportunities offered for intellectual growth. May they merit the continued blessings of our Father.

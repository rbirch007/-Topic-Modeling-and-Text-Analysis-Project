Magazine Drive
IT is a great advantage for an organization to have a magazine that voices its ideals, upholds its standards and looks after its interests. Apostle John A. Widtsoe says, "No organization can get very far without an organ, without a voice out of the printing press."
The Relief Society Magazine belongs to the Relief Society. It is indispensable to its members, since it prints the lessons. It keeps its officers and members in touch with what the Society is doing in different parts of the world. It furnishes good reading matter and gives food for thought and conversation.
Our subscription list has mounted by leaps and bounds within the last year. We expect it to reach an even higher peak after our drive, which extends from September 15th to October 15th.

[Vol22 | October 1935 | Sequence: 28 | Category: Article | Title: Article by Elsie E. Barrett]

By Elsie E. Barrett Autumn days are in the making With a forecast of the Fall; Brushes ready full of color, Motives from a paisley shawl.
Soon we'll see the seed pods changing Into dresses aureate; She will fill our hearts with rapture With her strokes elaborate. May we cherish Autumn's beauty To inspire when drab days vex It will help the thoughts soar upward When earth's schooling seem complex. Batching

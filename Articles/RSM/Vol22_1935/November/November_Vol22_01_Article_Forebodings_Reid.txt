Forebodings
By Agnes
Just Reid
'Twas November,
The clouds were heavy,
And their faces threatening;
Winter was coming
Cruel and relentless
;
Dread
filled my heart
For
I was
idle.
'Twas November,
The clouds were soft and gray.
They wrapped about me
Like the folds of a soft shawl
;
Winter was coming
But
I was gay and joyous
For I was busy.

CHRISTMAS GIFTS By Clarence Edwin Flynn Children we are. We want bright gifts from Life At Christmas time—a treasure or a toy —Forgetful how the world, so worn with strife, Needs gifts that time and change cannot destroy —The gift of love deep-planted in our hearts, Of kindliness to motivate all hands, Of skill in friendly and fraternal arts, Of warm good will abroad in all the lands. The gift of peace, of knowledge, and of grace, The gift of willingness to serve and bless, The gift of friendliness in every place, In every place the gift of happiness —We ask Thee, Ruler of our destiny, Hang all of these upon our Christmas tree.

This is the Day
By Mrs. Ellen L. Jakeman

This is the day of the Lord's own choosing,
This is the day He has made for me;
Why should I spend precious moments in losing
Time that is given so graciously?

This is the day—let me use it wisely,
Filling each hour with some worthy deed;
Let me speak gently and kindly, precisely
Meeting some lonely heart's desperate need.

This is the day—I will not waste it
In idle repining o'er yesterday's woes;
I will not let gloomy foreboding displace it—
This day of promise the good Father knows.

This is the day—I will meet it bravely,
Trusting in Him who has planned it for me;
I will go forward—not timidly, gravely—
But with a heart that is happy and free.

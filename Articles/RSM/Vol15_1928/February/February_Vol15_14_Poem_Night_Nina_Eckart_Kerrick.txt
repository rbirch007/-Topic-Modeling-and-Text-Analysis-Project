[Vol15 | February 1928 | Sequence: 14 | Category: Article | Title: Night Nina Eckart Kerrick]

Nina E chart Kerrick
When the day
is done,
When the night has come,
Oh!
Father,
I am weary,
As
I sink to rest
In my little nest,
With a heart that's far from cheery.
Oh!
I dread each day,
For the troubled way
Is rough, but thou wilt guide me,
Thou wilt help me through,
While
I
strive to do
The work thou hast laid beside me.
Morning
Now
I wake at morn,
And the snow adorns
The earth with its wondrous, beauty
;
And
its magic art
Gives me
strength
of heart
To go on and do my duty.
I am thankful then
For the snow, and when
It bathes the earth
in brightness,
May
it joy renew
When the world
is "blue,"
And impart to the heart its lightness.

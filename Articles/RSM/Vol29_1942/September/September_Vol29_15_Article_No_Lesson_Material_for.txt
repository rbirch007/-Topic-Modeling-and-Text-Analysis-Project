sharp,  and  the  center  section  of 
twelve  feet  is  flat.  The  distance 
from  the  lowest  point  of  the  main 
floor  to  the  highest  point  of  the 
ceiling  is  64  feet  3  inches  rather  than 
about  84  feet,  which  it  would  be  if 
the  semicircular  design  (which  was 
originally  contemplated)  had  been 
used.

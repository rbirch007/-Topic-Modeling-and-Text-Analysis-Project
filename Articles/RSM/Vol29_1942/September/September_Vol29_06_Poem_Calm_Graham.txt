[Vol29 | September 1942 | Sequence: 06 | Category: Poem | Title: Calm Graham]

CALM
Rose Thomas Graham
The raging wind against my windowpane
Gave way, at last, to cooling drops of rain
That washed the clouds away.
The dull hard lump within my aching breast
Melted in tears that brought me peace and rest,
Again my heart could pray.

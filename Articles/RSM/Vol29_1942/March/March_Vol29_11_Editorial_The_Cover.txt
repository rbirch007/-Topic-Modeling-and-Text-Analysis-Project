[Vol29 | March 1942 | Sequence: 11 | Category: Editorial | Title: The Cover]

Relief Society souvenir centennial plate is reproduced on the cover of this issue of the Magazine. The design of the plate is a copy of an old etching of the organization meeting of the Society, showing Joseph Smith, John Taylor, and Willard Richards, and the eighteen charter members. Cover arrangements are by Evan Jensen. Pictures of the First Presidency and Council of Seventy are published through courtesy of Deseret News; the Council of the Twelve through courtesy of Salt Lake Tribune.

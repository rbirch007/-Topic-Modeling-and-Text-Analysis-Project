[Vol29 | February 1942 | Sequence: 23 | Category: Poem | Title: Art of Living]

Art of Living

To live each day as though it were
The only one we'd ever share
With those we love; to speak each word
As though it were the last they'd heard
From us; to act as though each deed
Would be our final chance to feed
The hungry heart, or dry the tear,
Or calm some troubled soul's dark fear;
To love as though we loved in vain
Unless we loved through joy and pain;
To give as though our gift might be
The key to someone's destiny;
To live as though each passing hour
Might be our last to use the power
God gives to make this world more bright—
This is to live... this is life's height!

Civilians will not go unclothed, but we will have to clean out our closets, make use of our scrap bags, and doa bit of creative work. ~~ TODAY IS A SHIELD Alice L. Eddy "I never knew," I used to say When back in memory turning, "How happy was that long-gone day." But with the years I'm learning  Learning at last to recognize The gay and gentle face Of joy which comes in humble guise, Gift of the commonplace. The blessings that I hold most dear Are with me here and now: While sun is bright and skies are clear I make this secret vow: When troubles come, as troubles may With bitter woe and sorrow,: I will hold high this bright today Against a dark tomorrow.

Clothing Bulletin of Church Welfare Committee No. 1
THE General Church Welfare Committee has issued a pamphlet on the care and storage of clothing. The Relief Society has always worked in close cooperation with the Church welfare program and has offered to distribute this pamphlet to each Latter-day Saint home through the Relief Society visiting teachers.
Each ward president will receive these pamphlets during the month of June, and the General Board requests that these be supplied to the visiting teachers immediately so that they may distribute them in their monthly visits to the homes in July.

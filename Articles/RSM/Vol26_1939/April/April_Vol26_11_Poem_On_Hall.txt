[Vol26 | April 1939 | Sequence: 11 | Category: Article | Title: On Hall]

APRIL
By Mildred B. Hall
Warm sun slanting down the sky, Fleecy clouds a-drifting; Scent of blossoms floating by, Song of bird a-lifting.
Tulips, yellow, pink and red, Lettuce seed a-bursting; New hat on a saucy head, Youth for love a-thirsting.

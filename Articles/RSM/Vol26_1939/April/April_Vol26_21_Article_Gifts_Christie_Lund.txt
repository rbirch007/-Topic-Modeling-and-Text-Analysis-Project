THE CRUCIFIXION
By Christie Lund
They nailed his stainless hands upon a cross Against His sky, His heaven that soon must weep At sight of slow and costly drops of blood From Him, the Christ, whose sorrow was so deep That man cannot conceive of it.
They quarreled And bartered for His robe that many times before Had healed those who had stooped to touch it, those Who wept about Him now.... The maimed, the poor.

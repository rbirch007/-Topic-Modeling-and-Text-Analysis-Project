Wheat Interest
Ward Relief Society presidents are advised that wheat interest checks for this year will be mailed to the bishops. Checks will be made payable to Relief Society presidents, as always, but are mailed to the bishops in order to facilitate the handling of the immense amount of mail in the Presiding Bishop's office. Presidents will kindly contact the bishops. Checks are to be used by the Relief Society as formerly in the interest of maternity and child welfare.

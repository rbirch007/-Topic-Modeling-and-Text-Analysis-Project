 | Author: [No author listed]]In Memoriam — Mary Grant Judd

Mary Grant Judd, beloved wife of Elder Joseph F. Judd, passed away at her home in Salt Lake City, Utah, on December 15, 1955, at the age of 78 years.

Sister Judd was born in Salt Lake City on March 15, 1877, the daughter of Heber J. Grant and Lucy Stringham Grant. She was married to Joseph F. Judd on June 10, 1896, in the Salt Lake Temple.

She was a faithful and devoted member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints throughout her life. She served as a counselor in the Relief Society presidency of the Ensign Ward for many years and was active in all phases of Church work.

Sister Judd was the mother of eleven children, nine of whom survive her. She was blessed with 44 grandchildren and 23 great-grandchildren.

Her life was one of service and devotion to her family and to the Church. She will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved her.

Funeral services were held December 19, 1955, in the Ensign Ward Chapel, with Bishop George Q. Morris conducting. Interment was in the Salt Lake City Cemetery.

At  this  time  the  voice  of  the

Vengeance  is  mine,  and  I  will  repay;

be  cut  off  from  the  face  of  the  earth 
(Mormon   3:15).

(Mormon  3:16).

Nephites  Fight  Last  Battle 
Around  Hill  Cumorah 
In  380  a.d.  the  Lamanites  came 
in  such  great  numbers  that  they 
defeated  the  Nephites.  When  384 
years  had  passed  away  since  Christ's 
birth,  Mormon,  by  means  of  an 
exchange  of  letters  with  the  king 
of  the  Lamanites,  had  gathered  in 
all  his  people  around  about  the  Hill 
Cumorah.  The  Nephites,  230,000 
strong,  were  soon  engaged  in  deadly 
conflict  with  the  Lamanites.

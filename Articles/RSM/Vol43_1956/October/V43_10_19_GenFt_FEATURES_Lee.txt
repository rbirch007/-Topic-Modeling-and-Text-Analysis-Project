NAMPA  STAKE  (IDAHO)  WORK  MEETING,  April  19,  1956 
Kneeling  in  front,  left  to  right:  Clara  Adamson;  Ella  Fair  Foster;  Helen  Young. 
Standing,  left  to  right:  Vella  Moon;  Mary  Ferrell;  Winnie  Andrews;  Mavis  Humph- 
reys; Margaret  Hook;  Carrie  Pierce;  Larue  McKnight;  Melba  Reynolds,  Work  Director 
Counselor;  Hyla  Clopicr,  with  baby;  Oneta  Burman;  Gladys  Egbert,  project  supervisor; 
Anna  Peay,  work  meeting  leader;  Alta  Fuhriman,  President,  Nampa  Stake  Relief  Society. 
Sister  Fuhriman  reports  that  this  all-day  work  meeting  was  spent  in  reflnishing 
furniture.  Meetings  of  this  type  are  held  in  Nampa  Stake  four  or  five  times  a  year 
during  the  lesson  months  of  Relief  Society.  Each  work  director  counselor  and  work 
meeting  leader  is  invited  to  attend.  Each  meeting  features  a  different  art  or  craft, 
which  is  later  presented  in  the  wards  by  the  ward  work  meeting  leaders.

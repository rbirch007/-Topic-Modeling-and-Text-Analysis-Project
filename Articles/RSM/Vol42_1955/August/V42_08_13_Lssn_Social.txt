Rose B. Brimley, Secretary, Maricopa Stake Relief Society, reports: "(On March 17th, we honored the seventieth year of the stake organization. We called our program "Through the Years' and honored each president. Those still alive represented themselves, and the others were represented by members of their immediate families. Sister Mildred B. Jarvis wrote the history of the stake from its organization in 1885. 'The program was very successful and brought together many old friends of past years. Following our lead, quite a few of the wards wrote the history of their organizations and honored their past presidents."

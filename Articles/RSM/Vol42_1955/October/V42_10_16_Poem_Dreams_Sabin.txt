[Vol42 | October 1955 | Sequence: 16 | Category: Poem | Title: Dreams | Author: Sabin]
<i Dre ams Zara Sabin Little boy, with wide blue eyes And sunny summer smile, Of what do you think as you look so wise, And hold my hand the while? Do you know that I love you and wish you were minc? That we would never part; Is that why your eyes so brightly shine As you snuggle against my heart? Dear little boy with wide blue eyes, If all of my dreams had come true, I would have had children of every size, With, maybe, a boy like you!

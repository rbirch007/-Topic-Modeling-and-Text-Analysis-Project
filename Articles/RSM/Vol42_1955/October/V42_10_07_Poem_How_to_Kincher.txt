to Make a Dream Come Right Ethelyn M. Kincher How to make a dream come right? Look it in the face and see Where it leads eventually; Where it goes and what it means, And all other little dreams That may follow in its light. How to make a dream come right? Work at it from sun to sun, Keep it close when day is done; Give it scope of vision true, See what it will do for you When it really comes in sight. How to make a dream come right? Know it for the thing it is, Keep it clean; remember this: Dreams gone wild are tyrant kings, You must master all it brings, If you make a dream come night! Wells AUTUMN Thou burden of all songs the earth has sung, Thou retrospect in Time's reverted eyes, Thou metaphor of everything that dies, That dies ill-starred, or dies beloved and young, And therefore blest and wise— O be less beautiful, or be less brief, Thou tragic splendor, strange and full of fear! In vain her pageant shall the Summer rear: At thy mute signal, leaf by golden leaf, Crumbles the gorgeous year.

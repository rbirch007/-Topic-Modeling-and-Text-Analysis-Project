A Priceless Collection

Francelia Goddard

Some women cherish old glassware,
Others prize pieces of lace.
But mine's a collection of memories,
Filling no physical space.

I don't have to launder or dust them,
These treasures of laughter and tears.
They sparkle from loving remembrance,
As I recross the chasm of years.

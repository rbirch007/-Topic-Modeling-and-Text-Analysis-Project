[Vol42 | January 1955 | Sequence: 42 | Category: General Feature | Title: Bound Volumes of 1954 Relief Society Magazines]
(Bound Volumes of 1054 Reliof Society Magazines ELIEF Society officers and members who wish to have their 1954 issues of The Relief Society Magazine bound may do so through The Deseret News Press, 31 Richards Street, Salt Lake City, 1, Utah. The cost for binding the twelve issues in a permanent cloth binding is $2.50, including the index. If a leather binding is preferred, the cost is $3.50. See schedules of postage rates in this issue of the Magazine, page 71.

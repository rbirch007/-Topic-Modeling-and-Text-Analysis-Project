Make Quilt Blocks Quickly Thalia Black KEE a large box handy, containing scissors, pencil, pins, and pattern for quilt blocks. When sewing, instead of rolling the scraps together and storing them in the bottom of a trunk, cut out the quilt blocks. Pin all the blocks of like color together, and store flat in the box.

[Fruitcake—Through the Holidays by Marie Gifford]

Fruitcake adds to the friendly, informal air when served to the family gathered about the fireplace. You'll find this recipe calls for a minimum of ingredients as far as fruitcakes go. Then, too, it is less expensive than the usual holiday fruitcakes. At the same time, this light cake is ever so rich, for it's made with butter and eggs—two basic ingredients which add flavor to all baked products.
This fruitcake carries a wish for a "Happy and Prosperous New Year," when served during the holiday week.

[Roses Bloom Upon a Winter Tree]

Curtis, Bonneville Stake, and Jane N. Sheffield, Monument Park Stake. A graceful evergreen tree (pine, fir, spruce, juniper, cedar) sprayed a glistening white, and adorned from top to bottom (in the center), with a streamer of pink roses makes an unusually lovely Christmas tree. The tree in the picture, in addition to the trimming on the tree itself, has a garland of roses trimmed with pearls, arranged on a crescent of greenery, to form a ''frontage,'' and to make a place for stacking the Christmas presents.

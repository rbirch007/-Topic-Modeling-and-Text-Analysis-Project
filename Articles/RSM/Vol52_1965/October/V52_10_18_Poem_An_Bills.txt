the Home
inside and out

An Apple

Rowena Jensen Bills

An apple is fulfillment!
It is matured blossoms from shading trees,
Spring's ball of green—sour, and quickly tasted,
Red crabapples—jellied in jars,
Crunchy lunch around a seeded core,
A yellow transparent, honey-sweet in dew,
The nectar of cool October and November,
Fresh apple pie and apple sauce,
Symbol of a bushel basket,
Saucy, juicy grin of youth,
Smell of winter basements and small markets,
An apple is a green, red, or yellow skin
Enclosing nature's bounty!

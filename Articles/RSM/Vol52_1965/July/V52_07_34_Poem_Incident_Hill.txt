Incident of the Red Rose Lael W. Hill (Less transient than the red rose, Oh, let your love be!) The red, red rose, dew-heavy, Stood sweet with scent; How carefully you stripped each thorn To offer me Only the perfect blossom! Yet I am not content. The red, red rose has faded Before today was born, Stem bent down and broken, Twisted apart. I would accept together, now, The rose, the thorn.

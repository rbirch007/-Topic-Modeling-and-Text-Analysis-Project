Sleepwear and Sunsuits Janet W. Breeze Here is an interesting idea for making the most of those larger material scraps: sleepwear or sunsuits for children can be constructed by using a different print of cotton for each pattern piece. If second-time-around buttons are used, and you have patterns on hand, the cost of such articles of clothing can be limited to thread, and elastic for pajamas. Best of all—children call them '"'fun clothes!"

Fabric of Our Years Pearle M. Olsen Dear old friends! Burnished golden threads Weaving love through mellow I. Patterned years!,. Dear new-found friends! Silver filaments ~ Now giving luster To our forming days! Designs of varied measurements Enrich the hours as each arrives— Unfolding fabric of our lives! 718 BEAUTIFUL HANDY DURABLE A sure Wy of keeping alive the valuable instruction of each month's Relief Society Magazine is in a handsomely bound cover.

She will long be remembered as a distinguished daughter of pioneer families, and as a loving and devoted wife and mother. From a Schoolroom Window Bernice Ames The teacher asked Jane to spell ''foretold,'' But meadows below her are brimming with gold, And the voice in the room is drifting again As cicadas shrill like a scratchy pen. Tom should explain the Civil War, But a groundhog hole, unnoticed before In the field outside, makes his fingers itch, And two crows hang over a muddy ditch.

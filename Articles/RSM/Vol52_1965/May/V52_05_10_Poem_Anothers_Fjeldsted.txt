(To be continued) Another's Need Evelyn Fjeldsted It seemed to her she could not live; No one gave to her, she had naught to give. She could not live; there was no hope, With sordid life she could not cope. And then she saw another's need She had failed in word and deed. Herself effaced, she somehow knew, If there was time what she could do.

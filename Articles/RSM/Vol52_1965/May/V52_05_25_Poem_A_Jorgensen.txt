[A Mother's Solitude Margaret B. Jorgensen]

Celia sat down at the breakfast table, and dug, impatiently, into the grapefruit before her. "Oh, Mum! It is_ positively dated. Brian wouldn't think much of me in that." "I don't think a lot of hin, if that is the impression he gives his girl friends." Jeff slipped into the chair beside his sister, comparing his watch with the kitchen clock.

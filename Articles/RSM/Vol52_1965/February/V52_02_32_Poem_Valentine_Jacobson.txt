Valentine for Stephanie Ethel Jacobson It's Valentine's Day, Which is always fun, But this is an extraSpecial one— The very first For a girl I know Who's six months old And starting to crow And gurgle ''Ma-ma,"' And chew her toes And flirt with every Man she knows— What else indeed For a rosy elf Who's a real-life valentine Herself? HAWAIIAN TOURS January.

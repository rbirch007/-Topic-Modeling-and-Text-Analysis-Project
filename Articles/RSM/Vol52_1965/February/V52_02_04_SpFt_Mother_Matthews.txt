Mother-Do You Know? Robert J. Matthews, Editor Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, Department of Education That the Church has a week-day religious, cultural, and social offering for your sons and daughters while they are in high school and also when they go to college? That this program now functions in practically all of the fifty States and in five foreign countries, with a total enrollment of over 111,000 students? Next year it will be even more widespread. That wonderful opportunities will come to your sons and daughters when they enroll in the program?

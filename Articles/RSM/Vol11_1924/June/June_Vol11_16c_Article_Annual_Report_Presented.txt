ANNUAL REPORT PRESENTED The annual report was presented by the general secretary, Mrs. Amy Brown Lyman, and the comparisons with reports of other years showed growth and development in all departments of the Relief Society. This report is published in full in the May issue of the Magazine. Mrs. Lyman expressed appreciation for the splendid and efficient work done by the stake secretaries in compiling and forwarding the stake reports, many of which were perfectly compiled. The major part of the time of the afternoon session was devoted to a consideration of "Educational Work in the Relief Society."" Topics under this subject were treated by members of the General Board in the order in which they follow.

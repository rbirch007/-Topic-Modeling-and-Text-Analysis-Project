BIG BUSINESS
Everything in these days is on the side of the woman who systematizes her household duties as persistently and inexorably as "Big Business" pursues its pet, efficiency, hunting down waste of motion as relentlessly as waste of money. For with all the outside demands upon her feminine talent and judgment and fruits of experience which modern woman has inherited, graduated into, as it were, it does not matter how unambitious she may be toward occupation outside her home, how resistant to outside recognition, she is bound to be swept out of her chosen backwater into the current of community affairs. ?New York Sun.

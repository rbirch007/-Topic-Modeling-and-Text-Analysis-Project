TEACHERS'  TOPIC  FOR  JUNE 
Recreation 
"If  thou  art  merry,  praise  the  Lord  with  singing,  with  music, 
with  dancing  and  with  a  prayer  of  praise  and  thanksgiving/' 
Doc.  and  Cov.  Sec.  136:25. 
A.  Discuss  careful  preparation  for  recreation  and  amuse- 
ment during  the  summer  holidays. 
B.  Avoid  extremes  and  excesses  in  patronage  of  resorts,  at 
the  same  time  enjoy  in  moderation  and  seek  the  out- 
of-doors.

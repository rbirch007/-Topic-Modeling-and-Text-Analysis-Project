"MAY" By Ivy Houtz Woolley
Dreams, sweet iridescent dreams With colors woven through, And snowy pearls stitched here and there Upon a sky of blue; The sunset paints a picture rare, In every nook and space, The clouds are rosy tissues sheer Piled high with golden lace.
The quivering heat waves swimming by Reveal the blue scarf of the sky. A young thrush sounds his golden notes Then lightly into ether floats.

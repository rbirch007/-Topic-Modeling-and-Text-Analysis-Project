[Vol25 | March 1938 | Sequence: 24 | Category: Notes from Field | Title: Notes to the Field]
Notes to the Field Time of Lesson Presentation NUMBER of letters have come to the office requesting that the month the lesson is to be presented be published in connection with each lesson. All of the organizations do not begin lesson work at the same time, thus making it impossible for this procedure to be followed. It is optional with the stakes whether they begin the lesson courses in September or October. To meet the convenience of those who wish their lesson courses earlier, the first lessons for the year are published in the July issue of the Magazine.
This gives the stakes starting in September the opportunity of having the lessons two months in advance of their presentation. If the class leaders will watch the publication of the lessons and keep this fact in mind, they will be able to determine which lesson should be presented each month.

Leadership Week

The Relief Society is cooperating with the other auxiliaries in observing Leadership Week, February 13-20. The purpose is to give recognition to leadership and to inspire better leadership.

During this week each ward Relief Society should plan some special recognition of its leaders—past and present. This might take the form of a special program, a social hour, or some other appropriate observance.

The Relief Society has always been blessed with devoted, capable leadership. From the Prophet's wife, Emma Smith, who was the first president, down to the present time, the women who have served as officers have given generously of their time and talents.

We should be grateful for good leadership and should support our leaders loyally. We should also prepare ourselves to serve capably when called upon to do so.

Leadership is not a matter of position alone, but of influence. Every Relief Society sister has opportunities to lead—in her home, in her neighborhood, in the organizations with which she is associated. Let us all strive to be better leaders.

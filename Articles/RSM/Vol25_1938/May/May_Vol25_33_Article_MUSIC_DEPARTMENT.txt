MUSIC DEPARTMENT
Janet M. Thompson, Chairman
Great enthusiasm was manifested in the music program for the coming year 1938-1939. The response to the roll call indicated that there were very few stakes not represented. This was very gratifying and an index to the interest in improvement of music in the wards and stakes.
The chairman complimented the choristers on their devotion to the work and also gave suggestions which will prove helpful in future accomplishments.
A rallying song, "One Hundred Thousand Strong," composed by Mrs. Beatrice F. Stevens, a member of the Music Committee of the General Board, was sung by a double trio from Ensign Stake directed by Mrs. Ruth Jensen Clawson. It was enthusiastically received. This song was composed as a "rallying song" to assist in our membership drive.

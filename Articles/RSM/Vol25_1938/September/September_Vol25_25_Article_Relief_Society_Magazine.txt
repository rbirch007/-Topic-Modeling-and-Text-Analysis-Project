[Vol25 | September 1938 | Sequence: 25 | Category: Article | Title: Relief Society Magazine]

By Edna J. Gardiner
SINCE its inception the Relief Society Magazine has been an organ of spiritual uplift for all Latter-day Saint women. Its advent into the homes each month elevates and promotes a spirit of cooperation and peace. It links members of the various stakes and missions into one united sisterhood. Containing the subject matter for each week's lessons in Relief Society, it is invaluable as the official guide. Stories, articles, poems, biography and notes of interest constitute the subject matter found here.
Editorials are timely and well chosen. May we, as members, appreciate this publication and be loyal to the slogan: "The Relief Society Magazine in the home of every member."

Collect and Classify Ytems of Interest Thalia Black KE a large box handy, containing a loose-leaf notcbook, art paper, type paper, and lined paper, scissors, paste, and a pencil or pen, and carbon paper. In different sections of the loose-leaf notebook collect pictures, poems, stories, jokes, games. Use only one side of each page, so that when a picture or article is used something else of interest will not be destroyed.

Driggs LIBERTY STAKE (UTAH), HARVARD WARD VISITING TEACHERS MAKE ONE HUNDRED PER CENT RECORD The Harvard Ward Relief Society has made a record of which they can well be proud. Since March 1952, they have made a one hundred per cent record of visiting teaching. Every district has been covered every month. 'The teachers are very conscientious and willing, and have many times expressed their love for this work. The Relief Society presidency: Marelda Gottfredson, President, and her Counselors Verna Hunter and Adele Ernstsen, feel that a great deal of missionary work and the spreading of the gospel have been done by this valiant group of workers. LaNola C. Driggs is former president of Liberty Stake Relief Society. The new president is Verna A. Hunter. NOTES FROM THE FIELD 3] Photograph submitted by Mavil A.

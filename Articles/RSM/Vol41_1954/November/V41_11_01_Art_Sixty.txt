the  hearts  of  those  expressing  grati-

preciative of  the  earth  itself,  more

their  dependence  upon  the  Heaven- 
ly Father. 
There  are  many  among  us  whose 
lives  reveal  a  constant  gratitude  and 
a  continual  faith  and  hope.  Their 
expressions  of  thankfulness  and

ings have  become  an  integral  part 
of  their  way  of  life,  and  they  need 
no  special  day  of  thanksgiving,  al- 
though their  thoughts  may  be 
turned  towards  the  special  signifi- 
cance of  a  particular  day  and  a  par- 
ticular season.

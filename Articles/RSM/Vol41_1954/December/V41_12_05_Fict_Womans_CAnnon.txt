ANNE  HUTCHINSON  is  the 
subject  of  Boston's  only  statue 
of  a  woman.  She  was  a  very  bril- 
liant person  who  disagreed  with  the 
Calvinistic  Puritans  and  insisted  on 
speaking  her  mind.  She  was  ban- 
ished with  her  family,  in  1638,  and 
settled  in  Rhode  Island.  The  sculp- 
tor was  a  native  Utahn— Cyrus  E. 
Dallin.

	ALLOW US TO COMMEND
The gentlemanly officials of the Utah South-
ern Rail Road. They gratuitously landed
"old Santa" in the 15th Ward of this city,
laden with four tons of coal to be distribu-
ted among the poor of the Ward. May the
frost king never block the wheels on their
track.
	THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
For Utah Territory met in the City Hall of
this city, on Monday the 12th inst., at 2 P M.
		YOUR REPORTER
Was kindly received, and soon found her-
self pleasantly located in the House of Rep-
resentatives, surrounded by a goodly por-
tion of what is understood to be the choic-
est intellect of the Territory.

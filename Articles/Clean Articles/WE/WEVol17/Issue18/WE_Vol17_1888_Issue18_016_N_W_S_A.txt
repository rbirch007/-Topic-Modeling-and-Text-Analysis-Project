N. W. S. A.

	At the third Executive session of the National
Woman Suffrage Association, which was held in
the parlors of the Riggs House, Washington, D.
C., Thursday afternoon, Jan. 24, Mrs. Sewall for
the Committee on Constitution and By-laws, re-
ported. We have not room in this issue for the
whole, but will publish the By-laws in the next
number of the paper.  Following is the Constitu-
tion adopted.—ED.

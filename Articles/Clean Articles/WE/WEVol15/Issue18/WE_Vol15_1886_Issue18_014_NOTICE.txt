﻿NOTICE.

	Reports from the Relief Society branches of
this Stake are requested to be sent in by the 1st
of March. Address, Elizabeth Howard, 60 East,
Fourth South St., Salt Sake City.

	Relief Society Secretaries desiring blanks, will
please send directly to my address, 107, Main St.,
Salt Lake City, and save time. Stake reports are
respectfully requested to be sent in by March 20th
at the latest.
	ROMANIA B. PRATT, M. D., Ass't Sec.

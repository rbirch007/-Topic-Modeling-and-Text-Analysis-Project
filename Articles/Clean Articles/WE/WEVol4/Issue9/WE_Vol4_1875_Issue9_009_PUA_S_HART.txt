	PUA[--] S. HART


Holding the hand of the very sick and
the dying is a comfort unspeakable to them,
and if you are holding it and the spirit is
passing away, do not release the grasp un-
til the poor heart is at rest, for it gives the
feeling of company, of sympathy, of help.
--Dr. W. W. Hall.

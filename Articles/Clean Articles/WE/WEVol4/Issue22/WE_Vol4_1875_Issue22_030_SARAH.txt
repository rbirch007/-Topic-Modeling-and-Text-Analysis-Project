								SARAH.


SCENE at a butcher's stand: Butcher—
"Come, John, be lively now; break the
bones in Mrs. William's chops, and put Mr.
Smith's ribs in the basket for him." John,
(briskly)—"All right, sir, just as soon as
I've sawed off Mrs. Murphy's leg."

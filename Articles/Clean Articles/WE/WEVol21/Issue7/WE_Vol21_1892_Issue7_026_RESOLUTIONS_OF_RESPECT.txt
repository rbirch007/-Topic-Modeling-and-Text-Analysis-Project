RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.
	Resolutions of respect to the memory of Sister
Mary Goatman, who departed this life at Mendon
Utah, on Wednesday, July 6th, 1892, aged sixty-
seven years.
	Whereas, Our Heavenly Father has in his in-
finite wisdom removed from our midst our be-
loved sister, and

[Column 2]

	Whereas, her faithful labors as a counselor in
the Relief Society, and a generous, and useful
member thereof, a beloved neighbor and com-
panion. Therefore be it
	Resolved, That we the officers and members of
the Mendon Ward Relief Society do mourn her
loss realizing that we have lost a noble sister
whose hand was ever ready to help the sick and
afflicted.
	Resolved, That we tender our heartfelt sym-
pathy to her bereaved family.
	Resolved, That a copy of these resolutions be
placed on the records of the Relief Society, and 
a copy be sent to the WOMAN'S EXPONENT.
			MARIA BAKER, President.
			MARY SONNE, Counselor.
			EMMA HUGHES, Ass't. Sec.

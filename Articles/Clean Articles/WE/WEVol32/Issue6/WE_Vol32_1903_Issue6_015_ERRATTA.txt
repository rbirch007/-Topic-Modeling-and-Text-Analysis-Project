ERRATTA.
	In the September issue of this paper, in
a copy of the letter which was sent with
the fac simile of the Declaration of Inde-
pendence, to the schools and universities,
the word “heritage” was changed to “his-
tory,” which entirely destroyed the sense
of the idea the writer wished to convey.

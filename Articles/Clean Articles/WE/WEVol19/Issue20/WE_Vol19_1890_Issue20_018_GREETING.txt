GREETING.

	“The journalistic temperament is almost the
finest in the world—keen, kind, progressive and
humanitarian.  Take away the hallucination of
nicotine and the craze of alcoholic dreams, and
you would have remaining an incomparable set of
brother-hearted men.”
					FRANCES E. WILLARD.

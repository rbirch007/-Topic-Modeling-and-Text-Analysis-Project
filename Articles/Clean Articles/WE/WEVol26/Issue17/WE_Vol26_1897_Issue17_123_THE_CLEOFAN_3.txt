THE CLEOFAN.

	ON Tuesday, March 26, the Cleofan en-
joyed an Epitome on King Lear by Mrs.
Blanche P. Lee. The character of Cordelia
in comparison with Goneril and Regan by
Mrs. Anna S. Young and Mrs. Lee. Char-
acter of Edmund and Edgar by Mrs. May
P. Moyle. The club adjourned for one
week.
	CLARA T. FELT. Cor. Sec. pro tem.

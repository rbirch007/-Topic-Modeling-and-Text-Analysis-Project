A ROSE TO THE LIVING.

A ROSE to the living is more 
	Than sumptuous wreaths to the dead:
In filling love’s infinite store,
A rose to the living is more—
If graciously given before
	The hungering spirit is fled— 
A rose to the living is more 
	Than sumptuous wreaths to the dead.
				SELECTED.

				CHOW CHOW.

	2 qts. small onions, 2 qt. cucumbers, 2
qts. cauliflower, 1 qt. green beans 8 qts.
green peppers. Soak in brine 24 hours,
then scald enough vinegar to cover, pour
over and let it stand 24 hours. For sauce
take 2 qts. vinegar, 12 tablespoonfuls of
mustard, 4 tablespoenfuls of curry powder,
4 of flour 2 oz. celery seed, 1 cup of sugar,
cook until clear then pour over the pickles.


WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


The Ballot in the Hands of the Women of Utah should be a Power to better the Home, the State and the Nation.

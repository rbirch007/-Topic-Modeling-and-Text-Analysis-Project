				MARTHA WILENSON.


IT is expected that Dr. Ellen B. Ferguson will
lecture in Ogden, Logan, Kaysville, Farming-
ton and Salt Lake City, during the coming
month. Sister Ferguson's ability as a lectnrer
is highly spoken of both here and in the States
where she resided before coming to Utah.

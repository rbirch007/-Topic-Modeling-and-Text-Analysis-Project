			MOTHER’ WORK.
PLAN OF MOTHERS’ WORK FOR THE RELIEF
	SOCIETY BRANCHES OF THE UTAH STAKE
	OF ZION, FOR THE YEAR 1905.
		Self Culture.
1—Physical culture for mothers:
	(a) Care of general health.
	(b) Preparation for maternity.
	(c) Personal appearance.
2—Intellectual culture of mothers.
	(a) What literature shall she read?
	(b) Art in the home.
	(c) Practical science in the home.
3—Moral culture for mothers:
	(a) Gospel ideals of Morality.
	(b) Self control.
	(c) Social duties.
4—Religious culture for mothers:
	(a) Study of the principles of the Gos-
		pel.
	(b) Application of the principles of the
		Gospel.
	(c) Result of study and Application of
		the Principles of the Gospel, as
		manifest in the Relief Society and
		in Church work.

[WomansExponent | Vol23 | 1894 | February | Issue: 15 | Title: CHORUS. | Author: Unknown]

CHORUS.
Thus clouds are ever shadows casting,
		O’er Times rough sea;
But pleasures will be pure and lasting,
		There, where we hope to be.
Sometimes, in hallow’d dream or vision,
		Earth seems to flee;
One glimpse of Heaven brings decision— 
		There's where we, too, must be.

	We are sure our readers will feel glad to see the faces of these young girl students beaming upon them when they open the paper.
These young ladies show in the expression of their countenances the faith and hope that will help them forward to success if they
continue in the course marked out for their progress by their parents and teachers. What promise the Latter-day Saints have of the
future of Zion in the growth of wisdom and intelligence, perceptible in the Universities and schools established by the Church of Jesus
Christ of Latter-day Saints. The underlying principles of life and salvation that the world at large is reaching out after, lies in the
seeds of truth taught in these very schools. What grand opportunities are afforded our children, and how little they realize the
splendid attainments they might reach, if they would but take advantage of the glorious privileges thus accorded. Let us hope and
believe that the saying of the Psalmist may be fulfilled in the children of the present day, that “our sons may be as plants grown up in
their youth; that our daughters may be as corner-stones, polished after the similitude of a palace.”

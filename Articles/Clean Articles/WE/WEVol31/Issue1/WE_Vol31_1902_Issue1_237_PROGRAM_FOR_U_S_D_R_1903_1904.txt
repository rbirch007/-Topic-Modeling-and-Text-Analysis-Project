PROGRAM FOR U. S. D. R. 1903, 1904.
	March 16, 1903. Reading of Declaration
of Independence, with a brief biography of
the signers, Clarissa Y. Spencer. Story
told or read relating to the topic of the day,
Isabel M. W. Sears. Reading of patriotic
poem or hymn, briefly name origin and
writer, Maria Y. Dougall.
	April 16. Origin and history of first
flag, Phebe Y. Beatie. Story told or read
relating to the topic of the day, Ella W.
Hyde. Reading of patriotic poem or hymn,
Lucy W. Smith.
	May 16. Life of Abraham Lincoln, Eliza-
beth R Wright. Story told or read relat- 
ing to the topic of the day, Clarissa S. Wil-
liams. Reading of patriotic poem or hymn,
briefly name origin and writer, Catharine
Wells.
	Saptember 18. Life of Abagail Adams,
Emily C Willey. Story told or read relat-
ing to the topic of the day, Alice S. M.
Horne. Reading of patriotic poem or hymn,
briefly name origin and writer, C. Y. Can-
non.
	October 16. Life of Benjamin Franklin,
Diana F. Rich. Story told or read relating
to the topic of the day, J. P. M. Farns-
worth. Reading of patriotic poem or hymn,
briefly name origin and writer, Mary A. H.
Cannon.
	November 16. Life of Paul Revere,
Julia F. Lund. Original story, subject
Thanksgiving day, Susa Y. Gates. Read-
ing of patriotic poem or hymn, briefly name
origin and writer, Myra Y. Rossiter.
	Dececember 16. Life of Dolly Madison,
Almira H. M. DeWolfe. Story told or
read relating to the topic of the day, Mary
E. R. Webber. Reading of patriotic
poem or hymn, briefly name origin and
writer, Claire C. Benedict.
	January 16. Social life of Thomas Jeffer-
son, Martha R. Groo. Story told or read
relating to the topic of the day, Florence
Scholes. Reading of patriotic poem or
hymn, briefly name origin and writer,
Clarissa Y. Spencer.
	February 16. Life of Mrs. John Jay,
Carol Cannon. Story told or read relating
to the topic of the day, Sarah F. Smith.
Reading of patriotic poem or hymn, briefly
name origin and writer, Emmeline B.
Wells.

NOTES AND NEWS.
	On the 3rd of November, another stake
of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day
Saints was added to the chain. The Boise
stake, comprising that large tract of terri-
tory reaching from the Snake river on the
east, to Ontario, Oregon, on the west, and
embracing within its limits twelve counties.
Heber O. Hale is the stake president, and
has most able counselors and other officers
associated with him. The Relief Society
president for the new stake is Sister Mary
A. Rawson. The new stake is distinctive in
that it is the only single stake which has
its headquarters in the state capital.

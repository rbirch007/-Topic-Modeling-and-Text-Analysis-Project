[WomansExponent | Vol36 | 1907 | February | Issue: 7 | Title: SARAH JENNE CANNON. | Author: SARAH JENNE CANNON]

				SARAH JENNE CANNON.

	P. S. I am ashamed of this letter, but
the neighbors on each side of me are play-
ing on different instruments and if I wait it
is not likely to be better.
							JENNE.

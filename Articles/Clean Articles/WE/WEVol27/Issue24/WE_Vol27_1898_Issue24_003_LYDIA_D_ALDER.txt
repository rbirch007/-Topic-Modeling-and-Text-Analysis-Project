LYDIA D. ALDER.


Mrs. Charlotte Perkins Stetson spoke be-
fore the Political Equality Club of Cam-
bridge on Wednesday, April 26, and for
the Walt Whitman Fellowship of Boston
on April 27. She sailed for Europe May
4 by the First Bismarck of the Hamburg
line.

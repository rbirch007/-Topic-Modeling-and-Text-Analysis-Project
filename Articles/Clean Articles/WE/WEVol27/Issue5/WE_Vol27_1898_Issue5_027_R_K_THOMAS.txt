R. K. THOMAS

	Mlle. Marguerite Bottard has just been 
added to the small group of women whom
France has honored with the Cross of the 
Legion of Honor. She is the eldest of the 
nurses in the asylum known in Paris as the
Salpetriere, where she has labored for fifty-
seven years.

[WomansExponent | Vol9 | 1880 | March | Issue: 19 | Title: EDITOR EXPONENT: | Author: ELMIRA PINKHAM TUFTS]

EDITOR EXPONENT:
	Dear Sister—I have to thank you for giving
space in your excellent paper for the circular
of the  Tristrim  Coffin  re-union,  and  to  offer
you  an  apology  for  the  trouble I am  giving
you;  but when the same  is inserted I shall  re-
quire many Nos. of the EXPONENT to send  to
my numerous  relatives  and friends in different
parts of the United States, that they may join,
as  myself  and family have done.  I expect to
attend the “Memorial Re-union,” if my  health
permits, and expect some of my family will ac-
company me;  each one of them will contribute
to the  Memorial Fund.  I desire  to  show re-
spect to the first  of  our  ancesters, who settled
on the island  of Nantucket;  and to  clasp the
hand  of  my  living  relatives in the bond of
unity and friendship, which 1 trust I shall be
spared to do.
	ELMIRA PINKHAM TUFTS.
Salt Lake City, Feb.14, 1881.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT. 151

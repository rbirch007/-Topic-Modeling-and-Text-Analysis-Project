[WomansExponent | Vol9 | 1880 | May | Issue: 24 | Title: KATE M. CHASE. | Author: M. L. W]

						KATE M. CHASE.

	DIED, at Hyde Park, Cache Co., April 1, 1881,
Mary Elizabeth, daughter of William and Susie
Hawks, aged 1 year and 27 days.

Oh, how sad to part with our dear one,
	Little Mary, so gentle and sweet,
In all her sufferings so patient,
	So loving, angelic and meek.

								M. L. W.

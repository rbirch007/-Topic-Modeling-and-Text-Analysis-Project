	CRITIC.


Don't, above all things, walk smoking
by the side of women. No matter if she
does give you permission when you ask it. 
We don't wish you to do it you may be sure.
It is a disrespectful thing. It partakes of
the nature of an insult. No matter how
grand or learned or distingushed you may
be don't do it.
					—Gail Hamilton.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

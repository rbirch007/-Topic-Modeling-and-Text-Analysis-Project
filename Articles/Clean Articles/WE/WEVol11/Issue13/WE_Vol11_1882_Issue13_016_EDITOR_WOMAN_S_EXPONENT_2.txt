EDITOR WOMAN'S EXPONENT:
	In my article entitled "Personal Experience
which appeared In your issue of Nov. 15, the
following typographical errors occur, which
please correct: Instead of "political polygamists,"
it should read "practical polygamists;" and In-
stead of "how the blessings of the Lord rested
upon the world," "how the blessings of the
Lord rested upon the work."
	Respectfully yours,

[WomansExponent | Vol8 | 1879 | September | Issue: 8 | Title: PHYSICIAN AND SURGEON, | Author: Unknown]

		  	   PHYSICIAN AND SURGEON,

	Has opened her Medical Class at her residence in the
13th Ward, two blocks and a half east of the Post Office
south side of the street, nearly opposite the Presbyterian
Church.
	Special attention given to Obstetrics, and diseases of
women.

				FRANCELLE E. RONSON.


WE HAVE Vol. 1, No. 2, of "The Union,'
published in St. George by J. W. Carpen-
ter. The motto is "United we stand, di-
vided we fall." Price, $1.00 a year. We
are glad to see a paper started in that vi-
cinity, and trust it may prove a successful
enterprise.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

[WomansExponent | Vol22 | 1893 | June | Issue: 18 | Title: A. M. BLANCHARD. | Author: Unknown]

						A. M. BLANCHARD.


A GOOD book is one that tells, in good
English, its story: sees no necessity for
using foreign words, and does not quote
from the Arabic or the Sanscrit, as if the
author had written it with the “Ency-
clopedia Britannica” beside him.

NOTES AND NEWS.

	CHICAGO has been finally decided upon
by the Board of Lady Managers as the site
for a permanent Woman’s Building.

	MRS. ANNIE BESANT is coming to this
conntry on her way to India, to represent
Theosophy at the Parliament of Religions
in Chicago.

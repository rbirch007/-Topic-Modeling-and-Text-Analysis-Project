SUSA YOUNG GATES


Mrs. Pearl Young and Mrs. Estelia
Truenell, of New York, have proclaimed
the incorporation of a billion dollar mining
company with themselves as the probable
heads and the certain establishers and
promoters. The name of the new concern
is the Queen Mining Company, and it has
been incorporated in Pierre, S. D., with a
putative capital of $1,000,000,000.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

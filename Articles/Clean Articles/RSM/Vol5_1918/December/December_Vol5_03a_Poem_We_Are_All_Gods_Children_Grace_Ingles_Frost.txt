[Vol5 | December 1918 | Sequence: 03 | Category: Poem | Title: We Are All Gods Children | Author: Grace Ingles]

WE ARE ALL GOD'S CHILDREN
We are all God's children. Some are weak and some are strong. But the scales are well adjusted, Strength must bear the burden long.
We are all God's children. Why question His decree? A wise omniscient Father knows What's best for you and me.
Grace Ingles

[Vol5 | March 1918 | Sequence: 08 | Category: Article | Title: Our Music Book]

OUR MUSIC BOOK. The General Board of the Relief Society have authorized Prof. Brigham Cecil Gates and Elder Edwin F. Parry, Jr., to prepare and publish suitable hymns and songs for our local meetings and for use in conferences. The collection will include many new hymns and songs by our best home composers while not neglecting entirely the classics and popular music of today. We are happy to extend this help to our faithful and deserving choirs and musicians. The selections will be classified in opening and closing choral numbers, solos, duets, trios, quartettes, choruses for women's voices, all within easy vocal range and chosen with especial fitness for Relief Society choir work.

[Vol13 | April 1926 | Sequence: 03 | Category: Article | Title: The Mushroom | Author: Ivy Houtz Woolley]

Six mushrooms came out
To feel the cool rain,
They never will come out
To do that again,
For Madge came along,
The six mushrooms she took,
On a very fast trip
Right home to the cook
—
They made such good sauce.
That on each rainy day
Madge brings in their cousins
To cook the same way.

[Vol8 | June 1921 | Sequence: 09 | Category: Article | Title: To Mrs. Emmeline B. Wells | Author: Louise Coulson]

TO MRS. EMMELINE B. WELLS Oh! Wondrous woman! Adown the ages yet to come, Your name will be spoken In sweetness and with love. The farthest, most remote of lands, And on islands, wind swept coves, The blessings of the Master reach, That came from thy dear hands. —Lovingly your Friend, Mrs. Louise Coulson.

A NEW YEAR'S PRAYER FOR 1921

L. Lula Greene Richards

Father who art in Heaven,
    Hallowed be Thy name!
As Jesus taught the humble
    Who to His presence came,
Joseph Thy modern Prophet
    Hath taught Thy Saints to pray,
And thus we would approach Thee
    On this great New Year's day.

We thank Thee that Thy Kingdom
    Hath come on earth again,
With Prophets and Apostles,
    Devout and holy men.
Come Jesus, Heaven's Kingdom
    In light and power display,
Bring Thou with all its glory
    A New Eternal Day!

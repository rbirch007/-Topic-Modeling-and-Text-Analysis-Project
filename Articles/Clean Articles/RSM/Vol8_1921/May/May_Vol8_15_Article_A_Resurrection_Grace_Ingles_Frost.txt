[Vol8 | May 1921 | Sequence: 15 | Category: Article | Title: A Resurrection Grace Ingles Frost]

A RESURRECTION—To My Friend Grace Ingles Frost When time was fraught with honors of the tomb, You came to me an angel in disguise; With patient care you soothed my pain racked brow, And by your touch brought to my tear-dimmed eyes A clearer vision, gifts divine to see. Forth from a crysalis of somber gloom, And bade it live anew, Gave it the buoyant wings to soar Above the petty grovelings of earth, And face to face with God, it evermore Has glorified your name for its rebirth.

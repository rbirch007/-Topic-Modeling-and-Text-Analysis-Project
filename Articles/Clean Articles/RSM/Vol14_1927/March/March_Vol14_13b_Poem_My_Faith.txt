[Vol14 | March 1927 | Sequence: 13b | Category: Poetry | Title: My Faith | Author: Helen McQuarrie]

My Faith Helen McQuarrie
"Long time ago I prayed And Heavenly Father heard, And lo: I stand on Zion's soil Rejoicing, glad and unafraid
Oh Father dear, may I 'Mid all these wondrous scenes Within Thy house be found Purified, for Thou art nigh.

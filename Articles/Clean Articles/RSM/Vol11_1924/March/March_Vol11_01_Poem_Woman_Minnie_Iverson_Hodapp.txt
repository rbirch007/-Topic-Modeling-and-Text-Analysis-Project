Woman By Minnie Iverson Hodapp
Glorious woman! Realms of light Circle round thee pure and bright; Thou art-swayed, withdrawn, with high resolve. Not from thy absolute; and freedom equal, good Crown thy lovely sisterhood!
Fairest woman, rise to see Thy diviner destiny; Regnant queen, adorn thy place, Glorious Mother-of-our-race!

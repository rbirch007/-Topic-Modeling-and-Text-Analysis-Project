Mrs. Woodrow Wilson
Twice within the year it has been the sad duty of the Magazine to extend heartfelt sympathy to one who has been the companion of a president of the United States.

To Mrs. Harding, who suffered the severe shock of having President Harding stricken down, our deepest and heartfelt sympathy.

To Mrs. Woodrow Wilson, who has stood for months by the bedside of the brilliant man, whose physical vigor, our hearts go out in profound sympathy in this the hour of her deep bereavement and separation.

May she draw comfort, in reliance on the blessings of the Lord, and in the ever-sure words of consolation to be found within the Holy Bible.

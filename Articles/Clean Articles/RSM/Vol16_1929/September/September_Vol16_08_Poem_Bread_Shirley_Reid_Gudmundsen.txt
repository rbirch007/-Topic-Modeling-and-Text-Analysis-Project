[Vol16 | September 1929 | Sequence: 08 | Category: Poem | Title: Bread | Author: Shirley Reid Gudmundsen]

Bread By Shirley Rei Gudmundsen And some there are That stand and serve The bread of each day's necessity To the hungry world, And smile?

Knowing its hunger And its need.

Ah?you wonder, then, At the fine cruelty of excellence P? That he who hungers most Should serve the best!

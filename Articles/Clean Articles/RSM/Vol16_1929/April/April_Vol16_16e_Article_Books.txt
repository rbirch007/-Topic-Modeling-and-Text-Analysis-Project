Books

THE STORY OF THE BOOK OF MORMON, by George Reynolds and Janne M. Sjodahl. Published by the Deseret News Press, Salt Lake City, Utah. Price $3.50.

This is a book that should be in every Latter-day Saint home. It is a commentary on the Book of Mormon, written by two men who have made a life study of that sacred record. The authors have brought to their task a wealth of scholarship and a deep spiritual understanding of the truths contained in the Nephite record.

The book is divided into two parts. Part I deals with the historical background of the Book of Mormon, tracing the migrations of the various peoples who came to the American continent, and showing how their records were preserved and finally brought forth in our day. Part II is a verse-by-verse commentary on the text of the Book of Mormon, explaining difficult passages and bringing out the spiritual significance of the teachings contained therein.

The authors have drawn upon the best available sources in preparing this work. They have consulted the writings of early Church leaders, the findings of archaeologists and ethnologists, and the results of their own careful study of the text. The result is a book that will be invaluable to students of the Book of Mormon, whether they be teachers in our Church schools or individual members seeking a better understanding of this sacred record.

The book is well printed and bound, and contains numerous maps and illustrations that add to its value. We recommend it heartily to all who desire to increase their knowledge and appreciation of the Book of Mormon.

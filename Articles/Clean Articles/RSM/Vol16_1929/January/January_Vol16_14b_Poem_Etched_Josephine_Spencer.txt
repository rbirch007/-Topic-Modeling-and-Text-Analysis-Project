[Vol16 | January 1929 | Sequence: 14b | Category: Poem | Title: Etched | Author: Josephine Spencer]

Etched Josephine Spencer Poem awarded second prize in the Eliza Roxey Snow Poetry Contest
The horizon a hazy line; Foreground, a patch of crusted brine; Buff beach, brown shore, a waveless sea? With noon sun burning all the three.
South edge of serrate, Vandyke peaks; Pale island headlands laid in streaks Against the west; a northern shore Set dark against the lake's blue floor.

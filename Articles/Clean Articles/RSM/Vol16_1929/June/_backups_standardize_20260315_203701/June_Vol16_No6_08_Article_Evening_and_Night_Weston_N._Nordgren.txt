Evening and Night By Weston N. Nordgran The burning sun sank in the west And hid beneath the mountain crest. A sharp-trilled night-bird's cry was heard? And gentle winds the pine boughs stirred. The darkened sky, with stars filmed o'er, Displaying night?and nature's lore. A snowy owl on noiseless wing? A hov'ring bat beside the spring. Two trusting souls, confidingly, Made one for all eternity. A calm; a peace; a happiness? And dying embers?perfectness!

[Vol4 | August 1917 | Sequence: 01 | Category: Article | Title: Laudamus Mrs. Carrie S. Thomas]

Mrs. Carrie S. Thomas. Great King of Heaven, our hearts we raise To Thee in prayer, to Thee in praise. The vales exult, the hills acclaim, And all Thy works revere Thy Name. O Israel's God! Thine arm is strong. To Thee all earth and skies belong, And with one voice in one glad chord. With myriad echoes, praise their Lord.

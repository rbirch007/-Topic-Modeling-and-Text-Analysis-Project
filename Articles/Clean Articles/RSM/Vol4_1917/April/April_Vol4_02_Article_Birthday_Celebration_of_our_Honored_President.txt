[Vol4 | April 1917 | Sequence: 02 | Category: Article | Title: Birthday Celebration of our Honored President]

BIRTHDAY CELEBRATION. 201 with Nauvoo and the martyrdom. She remembered when the Temple was built in Nauvoo, how honored she felt to assist in preparing dinner every day, in the upper floor of Parley P. Pratt's partially finished house, for nine of the twelve apostles. She thought and still thinks that no greater earthly honor could be given socially to any person, and it seemed almost as remarkable as dining with the Savior. Now-a-days it seemed to her that our young people dine with the Presidency and Twelve Apostles without any sense of reverence or real appreciation of the honor they enjoy. She closed with her blessing upon all and a strong testimony to the truth. The meeting was dismissed by Elder Ridger Clawson, and a beautiful souvenir card was given to all present.

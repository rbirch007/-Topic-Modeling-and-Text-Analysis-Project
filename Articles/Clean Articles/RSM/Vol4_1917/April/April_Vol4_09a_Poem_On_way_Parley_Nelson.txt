[Vol4 | April 1917 | Sequence: 09a | Category: Poem | Title: On the Way | Author: Mrs. Parley Nelson ]

ON THE WAY. Spring is comin'. Think I hear the bees a hummin' ; Caught a glimpse of bluebird's wing, Heard a speckled med'lark sing, Felt a touch of balmy breeze, Heard it whisperin' to the trees, "Spring is comin'." Spring is comin'. Hear the wood-pecker drummin' ; See the green blades peepin' thru, And a blue-eyed violet too! Hark, I hear a robin's song! Makes me happy all day long, MANTI, UTAH. Spring is comin'. Mrs. Parley Nelson.

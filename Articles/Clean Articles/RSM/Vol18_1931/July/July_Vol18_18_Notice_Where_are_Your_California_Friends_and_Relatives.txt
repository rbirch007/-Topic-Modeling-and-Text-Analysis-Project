WHERE ARE YOUR CALIFORNIA FRIENDS AND RELATIVES?

Many of our friends and relatives have moved to California. We would like to get in touch with them and invite them to attend our Relief Society meetings.

If you know of any L. D. S. women living in California, will you please send their names and addresses to the General Board of Relief Society, 20 Bishop's Building, Salt Lake City, Utah?

We are particularly anxious to reach those who are not now attending Relief Society meetings regularly.

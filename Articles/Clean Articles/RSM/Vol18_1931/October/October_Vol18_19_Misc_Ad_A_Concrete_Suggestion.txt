A CONCRETE SUGGESTION

The Relief Society Magazine suggests that each Relief Society in the Church make a special effort to secure at least one new subscriber during the month of October. Many sisters who are not now taking the Magazine would subscribe if the matter were brought to their attention in the right way. The Magazine is published for the women of the Church. It should be in every Latter-day Saint home.

The subscription price is only $1.00 a year. The Magazine contains each month helpful articles on home-making, child training, literature, current events, Relief Society work, and other subjects of interest to women. It also contains the Relief Society lessons.

We suggest that the Relief Society presidency in each ward appoint a committee to make a house-to-house canvass during the month of October for the purpose of securing new subscribers. The committee should be provided with sample copies of the Magazine to show to prospective subscribers.

Let us make October a banner month for new subscriptions to the Relief Society Magazine.

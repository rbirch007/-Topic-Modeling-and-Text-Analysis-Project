Death
By Carlo, Wever
Death is a path
We travel only once.
'Tis dark, but 'tis not long,
And fields at either end
Are filled with beauty
If we make them so.
Beyond this path
We may look back,
But not return.
That
life
is lived
;
Another bids us come.

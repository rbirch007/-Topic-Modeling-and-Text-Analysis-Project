Who?

Hers a Martha's hand to serve Hers a Mary's heart to love Hers to gild the clouds of gloom Hers to build beyond the tomb. —G. H. Brimhall.* *These lines were suggested while attending the funeral of the late W. A. Morton and listening to the splendid eulogy given the wonderful wife.

Since this verse has general application to the women whose name is "Legion" in the world, we are pleased to present it to our readers.

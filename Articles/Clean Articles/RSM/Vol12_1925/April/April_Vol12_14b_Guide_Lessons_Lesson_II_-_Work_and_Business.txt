LESSON II (Second Week in June) Work and Business TEACHERS TOPIC FOR JUNE Cooperation of parents and teachers in simplicity of graduation exercises. 1. Cooperation is essential to simplicity in graduation exercises. 2. Finishing the eighth grade is simply passing from the grades into the junior high and not a graduation. 3. Elaborate exercises in the grades sometimes detracts from the desire to go on to high school and college. 4. Gifts of jewelry and money at this time are inappropriate. 5. Mothers and children have been made ill through the preparations for such elaborate and wholly uncalled for exercises.

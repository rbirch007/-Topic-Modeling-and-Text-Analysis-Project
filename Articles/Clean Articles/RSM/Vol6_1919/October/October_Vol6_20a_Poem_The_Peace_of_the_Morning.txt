The Peace of the Morning
======================================================================

PEACE OF THE MORNING Maud Baggarley. Oh, if only the peace of the morning Would go with us all the day, We might walk with souls undaunted All the long and solemn way; If its mystery and glory Would but linger in the heart, We might follow its high vision And with heroes there depart!

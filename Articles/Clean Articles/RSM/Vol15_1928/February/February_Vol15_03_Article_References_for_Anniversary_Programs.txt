A Samaritan High Priest and the Pentateuch

"Moreover, thou son of man, take thee one
stick, and write upon
it, For Judah, and for
the children
of
Israel
his companions:
then
take another
stick, and
write upon
it, For
Joseph, the stick of Ephraim, and for all the
house
of
Israel
his companions/'—Ezekiel
37:16.
The fontispiece shows a picture of a Samar-
itan high
priest and an
original
roll of
the
pentateuch supposed to be written by Abish-
na, a descendant of Aaron. We are publishing
it
primarily
that our
readers may
discover
why the ancient scripture was referred
to
as
a stick.

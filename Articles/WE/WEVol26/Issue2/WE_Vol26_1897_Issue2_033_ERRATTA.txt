ERRATTA.

Editor Woman's Exponent:

	In the issue of June 1, a sketch of the
life and labors of Sister Isabella Pratt
Price is given. Please publish the follow-
ing corrections and omissons: Bishop J.
C. Hamilton, instead of Bishop J. C. Han-
son, and the word “Mill Creek” Salt
Lake Co. after the words “Feb. 25, 1897,”
in the second line.		And oblige,
			FRANCES H. HANSON.

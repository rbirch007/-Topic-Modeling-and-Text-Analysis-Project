STATE SUFFRAGE ASSOCIATION.

	A MEETING of the State Suffrage As-
sociation will be held during the Conference
rates, the time and the place will be given
hereafter. Mrs. Richards and Boyer will
give reports of their attendance at the Na-
tional Convention, and addresses will be
given by some of the leading Suffragists of
the State.

WOMAN SUFFRAGE COLUMN.

Auckland, N. Z., Nov. 30.—The first election
in New Zealand under the female suffrage law
resulted favorably to the government. The wo-
men voted in large numbers, supporting mainly
the candidates who professed Christianity and
favored temperance.

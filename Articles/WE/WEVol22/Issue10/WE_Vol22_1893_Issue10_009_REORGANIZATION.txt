REORGANIZATION.
	A SPECIAL meeting of the 8th, Ward,
Relief Society was held in the Ward as-
sembly rooms, Salt Lake City Oct. 24, 1893;
for the purpose of reorganizing and select-
ing a new president our former one having
passed away.
	The meeting was opened by singing “We
thank Thee O, God for a Prophet.” Prayer
by J. D. H. Mc.Allister. Singing “Oh
my Father.”
	The following sisters were then selected
and set apart; Catherine K. Palmer, Presi-
dent, Jane Cowan first counselor, Sarah
J. Chamberlain second counselor, Jennie
Mortensen secretary, Jennette McMurin
Ass’t. Sec’y, Eliza Frost treasurer.
	The Primaary Association was also re-
organized as follows, Sister Ann Cartwright
president, Jennie Mortensen first counselor,
Nettie Frost second counselor, Annie Cart-
wright secretary.
	Remarks were then made by Sisters Elmina
S. Taylor and E. B. Wells and Bishop
Sheets, after which the meeting was ad-
journed. Singing “Guide us O, Thou
Great Jehovah.” Benediction by Charles
Tuckfield.

[WomansExponent | Vol8 | 1879 | December | Issue: 14 | Title: AMELIA D. MADSEN, Pres. | Author: Unknown]

			AMELIA D. MADSEN, Pres.

	The Paper edited by the Young Ladies
of the Fourth Ward, Brigham City, is en-
titled "The Young Ladies' Journal" a man-
uscript, published Semi-Monthly, each issue
changes editor, chosen from the Association:
	It contains select readings, essays upon
various religious subjects as also the prin-
ciples of our religion and other matter.
Original Poetry, Wit, Humor, etc., etc.
				MARTHA WILDE, Pres.
				LETTIE L. DUNN, Sec'y

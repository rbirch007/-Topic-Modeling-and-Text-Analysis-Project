PICTURES.
	BY GRACE INGLES FROST.
TWILIGHT, UTAH.
Shadows are slowly creeping,
O'er valley and mountain height;
And flowers that bloom when the day is done-
Night lilies, yellow and white, 
Nod their fair heads in the gathering gloom, 
And perfume the air with their fragrant breath, 
To welcome a silvery moon.

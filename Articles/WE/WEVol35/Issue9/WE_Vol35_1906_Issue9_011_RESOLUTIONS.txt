			RESOLUTIONS:
	Whereas, our heavenly Father, in His infinite
wisdom, has seen fit to call from our midst ou
much esteemed and beloved Sister Maggie Wade
Starley, who departed this life April 14th, 1907.


And Whereas, our intimate association with her
in the performance of her duties as a teacher in
the Relief Society of the Fillmore ward has so en-
deared her to us that we feel it necessary and be-
fitting to express our appreciat[-]on of her labors in
our Society, and of her earnest endeavor to do
right, of her strong integrity and faithfulness in the
Gospel, in which she was an earnest worker; al-
ways ready to perform any duty assigned her, no
matter what the call might be, Sister Maggie
could always be relied upon.
	She was a devoted daughter to her mother; a
faithful and loving helpmate to her husband, and
a kind, tender and affectionate mother to her chil-
dren.

WOMEN DOCTORS IN OLDEN TIMES.—It is
said there is in the British Museum an illumi-
nated manuscript, bearing the date of 1470,
which illustrates the history of Tobit. One of
the scenes represents a chamber wherein the
old Israelite is lying blind and sick upon his
couch, while his wife Anna sits by the fire pre-
paring medicine for him, with an open book by
her side.

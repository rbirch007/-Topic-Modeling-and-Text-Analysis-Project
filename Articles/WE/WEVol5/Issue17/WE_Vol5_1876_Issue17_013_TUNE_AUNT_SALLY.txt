			[TUNE, "AUNT SALLY."]

I'll not give heed to vanity, to fashion or to pride
Nor any of those silly ways God's servants have decried,
Appearing ever neat and clean, avoiding all excess,
Empolying my own handiwork in whatsoe'er I dress.

[WomansExponent | Vol19 | 1890 | December | Issue: 12 | Title: GIVE ME MUSIC. | Author: L. M. H]

GIVE ME MUSIC.

Oh give me music! tho’ my brow is calm,
	My heart seems bursting in its cell below,
And gloomy thoughts come crowding thickly up,
	From that deep fount of bitterness and woe

O, give me music would I might forget,
	What life was when I first its pathway trod,
Too much its pleasures have my heart beguiled,
	From Him to whom alone 'tis due,—my God.

O, give me music, soft and sad and low,
	'Twill cheer my weary fainting heart, and raise.
My drooping spirits from this dull cold world;
	And turn my mournful thoughts to songs of praise.
										L. M. H.

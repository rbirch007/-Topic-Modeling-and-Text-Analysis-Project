NOTICE.
	The Relief Society Conference of the Salt
Lake Stake of Zion will commence in the
Fourteenth Ward Hall on Sept. 12th, at 10
o’clock a. m., and at 2 p. m. All are invited
to be present.
			M. Isabella Horne, Prest.

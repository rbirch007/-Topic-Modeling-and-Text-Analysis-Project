PRIMARY OFFICERS MEETING.

	There will be a meeting of the officers of the
Primary, Association held at the residence of C.
B. Felt, 155 North Main St. Thursday Oct. 6, at
7 p. m. Representatives from each of the Stakes
of Zion are requested to be present.

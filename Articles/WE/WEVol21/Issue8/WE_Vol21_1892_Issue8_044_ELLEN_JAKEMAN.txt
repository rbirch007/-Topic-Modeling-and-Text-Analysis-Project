ELLEN JAKEMAN.


THE Woman Suffrage Association of Salt
Lake County intend holding an Annual
Convention on Tuesday November 15, 1892,
at the Thirteenth Ward Assembly Rooms
at 2 o’clock p. m. All who are interested
in the political education of women are in-
vited to attend.

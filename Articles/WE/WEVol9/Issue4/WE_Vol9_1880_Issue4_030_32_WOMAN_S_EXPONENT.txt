32		WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


PRINCESS Louise has sent to England a box of
valuable and curious articles of Indian manufac-
ture, as a contribution to the Cheadle Bazaar
opened at Cheadle Rdctory.

	AT an annual meeting of Friends in Philadel-
phia, the renowned veteran lady Lucretia Mott
now in her 88th year lifted her sweet and elo-
qunt voice strongly against light litrature, which
is now doing such extensive injury to the minds
of the young. Would that brave strong words
like these could be sounded like a trumpet in the
ears of those who devote their best days to such
like occupation.

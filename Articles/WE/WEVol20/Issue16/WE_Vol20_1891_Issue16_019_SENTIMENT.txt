[WomansExponent | Vol20 | 1891 | March | Issue: 16 | Title: SENTIMENT. | Author: S. M. K]

SENTIMENT.
Dear Susan, we appreciate her past labors for
the uplifting of womankind, we love her as a
sister and friend. May she live until the political
shackles are stricken from her sister woman.
This Sentiment I wished to express, or to have
expressed but no opportunity presented. 
S. M. K.

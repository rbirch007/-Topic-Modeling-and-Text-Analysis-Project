	RELIEF SOCIETY REPORTS.
		PANGUITCH STAKE.
	Panguitch Stake Relief Society Confer-
ference convened at Panguitch, September
15, 1904, at 10 a. m. Commenced in the
usual manner. Three wards were reported. 
Sister Priscilla Smith of Salt Lake City
gave some very good instructions to the
sisters. Brother Giles gave an instru-
mental selection. Sister Clawson of Salt
Lake City spoke very good, giving good
instructions. 
	Stake President David Cameron spoke a
short time and President Elizabeth De Long,
and Counselor Hannah A. Crosby conference
was then adjourned until 2 p. m. Bene-
diction by Patriarch Joseph L. Heywood.
	Conference reconvened at 2 p. m., com-
menced in the usual manner. Speakers in
the afternoon being Sister Margaret H. 
Clark, Sister Mary Houston, Bishop James
B. Heywood and Sister Smith. Congrega-
tion sang a song, followed by the following
speakers: Sister Clara L. Clawson, Sister
Hannah I. Henrie, Sister Priscilla Smith,
Brother James Houston and Prest. Eliza-
beth De Long. Conference was then ad-
journed. Benediction by Elder John S.
Crosby.
	ELIZABETH DE LONG, President.

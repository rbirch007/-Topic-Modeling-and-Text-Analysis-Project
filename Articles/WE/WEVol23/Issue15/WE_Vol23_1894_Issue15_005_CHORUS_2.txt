[WomansExponent | Vol23 | 1894 | February | Issue: 15 | Title: CHORUS. | Author: Unknown]

CHORUS.
Soon will the mystic veil be riven—
	Sing, Sing with glee!
While faithful to each trust that’s given, 
	In what we all must be.
Blest Faith, which gives the soul expansion,
	God’s grand decree;
That each may earn a glor’ous mansion,
	There, where we all shall be!
CHORUS.
In that life most bright and cheery,
	Filled with peace and love,
Our hearts will never more grow weary,
	Safe in that home above.

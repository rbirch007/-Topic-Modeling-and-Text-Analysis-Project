[WomansExponent | Vol4 | 1875 | April | Issue: 22 | Title: E. J. DURANT. | Author: Unknown]

						E. J. DURANT.


POETRY and romance are often spoken of
as things of mere fancy wanting substance.
Yet there is nothing more real than the poe-
try of nature itself, and nothing sweeter.
sadder, or more sublime than the romance
which may be traced through every-day
practical life.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

THE WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

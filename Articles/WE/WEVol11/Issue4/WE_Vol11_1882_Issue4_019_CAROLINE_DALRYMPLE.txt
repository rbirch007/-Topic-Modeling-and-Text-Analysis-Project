		CAROLINE DALRYMPLE.


Even the women of India are becoming in-
spired with the spirit of liberty, and are mak-
ing an effort, to emancipate themselves from
the old oppressive customs. a number of
Hindoo widows are preparing a memorial to
Queen Victoria, praying for relief against
caste, excommunication or re-marriage. -Ex.

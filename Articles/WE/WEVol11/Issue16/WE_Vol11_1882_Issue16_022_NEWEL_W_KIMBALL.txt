[WomansExponent | Vol11 | 1882 | January | Issue: 16 | Title: NEWEL W. KIMBALL. | Author: N. W. K]

					NEWEL W. KIMBALL.

	P. S.—I have seen a notice in the EXPONENT
sent me by Aunt Em, of a pamphlet written by
you, which I would like to have if you would
send it to me. Give me your faith and prayers.
God bless you all, is the prayer of your brother,
								N. W. K.

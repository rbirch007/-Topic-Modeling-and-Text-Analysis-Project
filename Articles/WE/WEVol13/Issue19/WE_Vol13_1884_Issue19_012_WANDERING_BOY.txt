	WANDERING BOY.


On Wednesday, January 28th, the direct ca-
ble was broken about 400 miles from the coast
of Ireland, and it is believed that it cannot be
repaired until spring. This makes the fourth
cable lost by the allied companies--the two
Gould cables, the French and the direct. The
Anglo and the Commercial are the only two 
lines in working order.

	Patents have been issued for the week end-
ing Feb. 3, 1884:
	Martha E. Boyd, Carthage, Ill., foot-warm-
er.
	Sarah A. Moulton, Grand Rapids, Michigan.
meat-tender.
	Margaret E. Knight, Ashland, Mass., spit
for roasting meat.
	Anna M. Rittenhouse, Philadelphia, Pa., ink-
well guard.
	Mary R. Walpole, Mayfield, Ky., wash-
boiler.

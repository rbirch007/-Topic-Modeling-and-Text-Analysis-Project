	MARGUERITE.


Ristori, the celebrated Italian actress, visited
our city last week and played Elizabeth of
England and Mary Queen of Scotts. She still
possesses a wonderful power of fascination
over her audiences, holding them as though
spell bound. To see her once is to remember
her always.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

THE WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

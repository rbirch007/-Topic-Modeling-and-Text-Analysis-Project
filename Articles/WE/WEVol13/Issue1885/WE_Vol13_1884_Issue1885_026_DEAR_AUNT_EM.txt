DEAR AUNT EM:
	Remembering the great interest and sympa-
thy you expressed for my little afflicted niece,
Hattie Young, after you saw her two or three
years ago, I feel sure you will be pleased and
gratified to see the foregoing letter and poem
from her pen. If you think proper you are at
liberty to publish them.	LULA.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT.


﻿Miss Helen Gladstone's engagement to Prof-
essor James Stuart has been announced
through the papers. Prof. Stuart has been
lately elected to Parliament in place of Post-
master General Fawcett deceased. Miss Glad-
stone is the daughter of the Premier of Great
Britain.

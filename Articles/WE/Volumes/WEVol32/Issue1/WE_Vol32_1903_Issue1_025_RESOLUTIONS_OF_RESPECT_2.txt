[WomansExponent | Vol32 | 1903 | June | Issue: 1 | Title: RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT. | Author: EMMA FEATHERSTONE,]

		RESOLUTIONS OF RESPECT.

	Resolved, that we her colaborers in this great work of
love express our appreciation of her example.
	That we emulate her noble traits of character, of faith,
and charity, and her devotion to her religion.
					EMMA FEATHERSTONE,
					ALICE T. STEELE,
									Committee.

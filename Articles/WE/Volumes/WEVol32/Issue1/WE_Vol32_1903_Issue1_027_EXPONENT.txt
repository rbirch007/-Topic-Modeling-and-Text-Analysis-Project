EXPONENT.

	“Death softly came and bore away
		A sister true and tried,
	In the calm twilight of her days
		In faith and hope she died.
	She suffered long, then calmly bent,
		To meet death’s fatal blow
	An arrow sure in mercy sent,
		To sufferers here below.”
						AMY HANCOCK,
						M. A. ROSS,
						J. A. HANCOCK,
								Committee.

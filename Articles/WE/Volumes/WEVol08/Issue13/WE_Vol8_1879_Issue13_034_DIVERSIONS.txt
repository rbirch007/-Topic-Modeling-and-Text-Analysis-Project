DIVERSIONS.

	ONE fact in natural history is pig-headed
men are always bores.

	HEE-HAW!—"That's a relation of yours,"
said a devoted husband to his most affec-
tionate wife, pointing to a donkey. "Yes,
by marriage," was the scientific reply.

	"Do you have any hops in Kent?' asked
a London belle, who was dressing for a ball,
of a country cousin from the "Garden of
England." "Oh, yes, lots! Pa has forty
acres of 'em in one field."

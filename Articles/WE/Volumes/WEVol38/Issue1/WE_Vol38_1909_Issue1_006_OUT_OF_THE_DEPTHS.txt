[WomansExponent | Vol38 | 1909 | June | Issue: 1 | Title: OUT OF THE DEPTHS. | Author: Unknown]

OUT OF THE DEPTHS.
Lord God of hosts, Thou art
	A God of peace,
From all the woes of war,
	O send release.
We caunot trace Thy hand
	In this dark war,
For sin and cruelty
	Thou dost abhor.
Lord, Thou canst sin defeat
	In every form;
Canst still the wind and wave,
	Control the storm.
Thou canst reach forth Thine hand
	And quell all strife,
Out of the depths of death
	Bring hope and life.
As so we come, O Lord,
	In this dark hour,
And pray Thee manifest
	Thy mighty power,
And o’er all the earth
	Bid warfare cease.
Out of the depths we cry
	To Thee—for peace.

[WomansExponent | Vol6 | 1877 | March | Issue: 19 | Title: CITIZENS. | Author: J. H. THOMPSON,]

		CITIZENS.

	I wish that this bill might be passed, and
the sooner the better.
	HENRY H. WELLS, District Atty.
	There is most pressing necessity for this
Institution. Common humanity demands
it.
				J. H. THOMPSON,
	Surgeon in Chief Columbia Lying-in
Hospital.

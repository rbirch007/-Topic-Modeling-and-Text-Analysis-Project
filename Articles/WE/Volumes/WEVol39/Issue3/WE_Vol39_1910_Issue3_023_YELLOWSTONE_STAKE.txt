[WomansExponent | Vol39 | 1910 | September | Issue: 3 | Title: YELLOWSTONE STAKE. | Author: MARY E. BROWER,]

		YELLOWSTONE STAKE.
	Report of Yellowstone Stake Relief So-
ciety conference held at St. Anthony, Aug.
15, 1910. There were present Stake Presi-
dent Daniel G. Miller, First Counselor Mar-
ion J. Kerr, Bishop A. D. Miller, Sr., one
High Councilman and Sisters Barnes, Ballif
and Briggs of the Fremont Stake Relief
Society. President Julia E. Miller presided.
	Singing, “We Thank Thee O God for a
Prophet;” prayer by President Daniel G.
Miller; singing, “We are sowing daily sow-
ing.” President Julia E. Miller welcomed
the sisters to conference. Parker, Wilford,
Warm River, Twin Groves and Ashton
wards were Reported as being in good con-
dition. Musical selection was given by Sis-
ter Grace Williams. Counselor Mary J.
Miller advised the officers along the lines
of their duties.
	A reading, “The Lord looketh upon the
Heart,” by Sister Grace Z. Robertson; solo,
“There’s a mother needs me now,” Sister
Amelia Barnes. Benediction by Sister Em-
ily Lamborn.
	Afternoon session: Singing, “High on
the Mountain Top;” prayer, President M.
J. Kerr singing; “The Happy Day.”
	Ora, St. Anthony, Marysville and Kil-
gore branches were reported as doing well.
President Julia E. Miller reported the stake.
Song, “Oh, Heart of Mine” by Brother and
Sister Thos. Elliot. Sister Barnes of Fre-
mont Stake spoke on Relief Society work.
Sister Briggs spoke on the duties of the
teacher. An instrumental selection by
Brother Elliot. The general and stake offi-
cers were then sustained. Solo, “Some
Happy Day,” by Sister Emily Ballif. Presi-
dent Daniel G. Miller spoke for a short
time. Duet, “Light at Eventime,” by Wil-
ford Society. After a few remarks by Bish-
op A. D. Miller conference was adjourned.
Singing, “Doxology.” Benediction, Sister
Francis Hunter.
				MARY E. BROWER,
							Secretary.

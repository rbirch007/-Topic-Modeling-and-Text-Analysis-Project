HOUSEHOLD HINTS.

	Marble Cake.—Light part—White sugar, 1½
cups; butter, ½ cup; sweet milk, ½ cup; soda, ½
teaspoonful; cream of tartar, 1 teaspoonful; whites
four eggs; flour, 2 ½ cups, beat the eggs and sugar
of together; mix the cream of tartar with the flour,
and dissolve the soda in the milk.
	To dress a Loin of Mutton like Venison.—Skin
and bone a leg of mutton and lay it into a stew-
pan, with a pint of water, a large onion, stuck
with a dozen cloves, and a spoonful of vinegar;
add, when it boils, a few sprigs of thyme and
parsley, and some salt and pepper; let it stew
gently three hours, and turn often. Make some
gravy of the bones, and add it at intervals to the
mutton when a r[-]quired.

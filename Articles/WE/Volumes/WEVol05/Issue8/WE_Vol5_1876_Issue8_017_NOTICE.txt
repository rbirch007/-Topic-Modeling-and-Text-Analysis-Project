NOTICE.

	We would say to our agents and subscrib-
ers that our terms to clubs are the same as
heretofore, no change having been made;
but we withdrew the article (Club Rates) as
our paper is small and we thought it suffi-
ciently understood; had any change been
made our agents and the public would have
been apprised of it.

"HOW TO FEED THE BABY."

	I have very carefully read specimen copie
of both, and find nothing in them but what
might have been written by Latter-day
Saints, and I heartily recommend them to
every household—not only as books to be read,
but to be studied and kept on hand for refer-
ence.	E. R. S. S.

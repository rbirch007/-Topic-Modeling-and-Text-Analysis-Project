PONENT. 					'M.D.D.

	DIED, at Mt. Pleasant, Sanpete Co., Feb. 6th, 1884,
Mrs. Ellen Lake, beloved wife of Wm. Lake, after being
sick only a few days. Sister Lake was born April 16th,
1840, in Findon, Sussexshire, England. Immigrated to
Utah with her parents about twenty-seven years ago.
She was a firm, faithful Latter-day Saint, a true and
loving wife and a most affectionate mother.
	She was the mother of ten children, nine of whom are
left to mourn their sad loss.  May she rest in peace.
								M.F. C. MORRISON.

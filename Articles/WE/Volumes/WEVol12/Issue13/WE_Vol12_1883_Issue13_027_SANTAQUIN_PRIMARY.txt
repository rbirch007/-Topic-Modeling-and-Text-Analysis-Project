SANTAQUIN PRIMARY.

	The President of the Primary Association of
Santaquin having also resignd, a reorganization
of the same was attended to by Bishop George
Hallady and First Counselor, Eli Openshaw,
which took place Nov. 17th, 1883, with the
following sisters as officers: Elizabeth Wall,
President; Agnes Clark and Annie Broad-
bent, Counselors; Josephine Ahleen, Secretary;
Maud Kirkman, Assistant Secretary; Augusta
Tietjen, Treasurer. E J. Strickney, Supt.

SARAH M. KIMBALL.


To produce good conversation, earnest
thought and general knowledge is requisite,
and a thoroughly excellent conversational-
ist may be considered a wise man or woman.
Cultivate the gift, then, if you possess but
its germ, since nothing can contribute more
highly to the happiness of friends at home
or abroad.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT.

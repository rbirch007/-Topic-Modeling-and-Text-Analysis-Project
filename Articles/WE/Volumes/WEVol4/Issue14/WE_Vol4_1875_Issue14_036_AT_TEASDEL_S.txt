[WomansExponent | Vol4 | 1875 | December | Issue: 14 | Title: AT TEASDEL'S | Author: Unknown]

			AT TEASDEL'S
	On and after December 1st I willsell all Raisins and
Currant and Christmas Fruits, cleaned and prepared
[---] giving a benefit to those wishing to prepare their
Mince Meat for Christmas. A full Supply of Presents for
Christmas and New Year in useful and substantial items.
	Dried Apples, to the many inquiries: I will take more
good, sound Fruit,
		S.P. TEASDEL, Eagle House.

ANNIE WELLS CANNON.


WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


Miss Josephine Baker, as chief of the
Division of Child Hygiene of the New York
City Health Department, is concerned with
the well-being of over 825,000 children.
There are 135,000 babies born in New York
each year and during the four years of Dr.
Baker’s administration infant mortality has
been reduced by thousands annually.

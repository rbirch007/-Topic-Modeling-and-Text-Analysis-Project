EDITORIAL NOTE.
	Interesting programs were rendered
March 17 by nearly all the Relief Societies
in the Church, so many indeed it would be
impossible to publish them all; but it is with
much pleasure that we read of the renewed
interest in Relief Society work these occa-
sions arouse and we trust the Latter-day
Saint women will always appreciate their
membership in this splendid organization.
There are reports from Payette, Cannon,
Liberty, Roy wards and many others.

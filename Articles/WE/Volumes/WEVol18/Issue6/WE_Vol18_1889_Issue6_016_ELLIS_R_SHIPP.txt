[WomansExponent | Vol18 | 1889 | August | Issue: 6 | Title: ELLIS R. SHIPP. | Author: Unknown]

				ELLIS R. SHIPP.

     The birthday party was a perfect success,
not only in speeches and gifts and songs but in
the most delicious refreshments.  The Exponent
offers most fervent congratulations wishing the
genial and intellectual Dr. long life, and prosperity
peace and happiness.

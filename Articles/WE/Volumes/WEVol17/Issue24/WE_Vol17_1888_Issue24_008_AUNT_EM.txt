							AUNT EM.


The Woman’s Congress will meet in Denver,
Col., next October. The following speakers
will take part:
	Mrs. Ednah D. Cheney; “Civil Marriage,”
Laura Linton: “Women in Science,” Helen S.
Shedd: “Women in Affairs,” Mrs. Hazard:
"Crime and Its Punishment,” Speakers of last
year: “Immigration,” Mrs. Stevenson: “Child
Life Among Zunis,” Symposium; “Employ-
ment of Prisoners,” Maria P. Bray: “House-
keeping as a Profession,” Miss Ripley: “The
Sacrifice of Education to Examinations,” Dr.
Smith: “Common-Sense in the Nursery,” Mrs.
Myers: “What Authors Influence the Mind of
Youth To-day,” and Mrs. H. L. T. Wolcott
“Horticulture.”

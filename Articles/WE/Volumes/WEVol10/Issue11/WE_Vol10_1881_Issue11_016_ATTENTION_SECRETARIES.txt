ATTENTION, SECRETARIES!

	The Secretaries of Relief Societies in Salt
Lake Stake are urgently requested to send in
their semi-annual reports by the 5th of March,
as it is impossible for the Stake Secretary to
makes a correct report unless she receives the
report from each Society in due time.
				M. ISABELLA HORNE, Prest.

	ELLIS R. SHIPP, Rec. Sec.


MISS MINNIE NEBERGALL is enrolling
clerk of the Iowa House of Representa-
tives, and Mrs. Carrie G. Sherman is en-
grossing clerk of the Senate.

	A RECENT decision of the Supreme 
Court of Washington holds that in cities of
10,000 or more inhabitants, women are en-
titled to vote at elections held to legalize
indebtedness for school purposes.

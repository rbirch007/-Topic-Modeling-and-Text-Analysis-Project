CARDSTON, ALBERTA, CANADA.
SPECIAL EXCURSION.
	May 18th, the Union Pacific will run a
special excursion Salt Lake to Lethbridge,
Canada, the nearest railroad point to
Cardston, Canada. Fare for the round
trip only $40.50.
	Tickets good returning 30 days.

“WHY A WOMAN SHOULD DESIRE TO
			BE A MORMON.”
			(Concluded.)
	(Written by special request and read at
Womens’ Clubs in New York.)

	Parley P. Pratt brought to Kirtland the
Gospel and also the Book of Mormon and
presented these truths to Sidney Rigdon,
who was also a Campbellite, and con-
verted him to the Gospel, as also many
others, notably Eliza R. Snow and her
brother Lorenzo, who were also members
of the Campbellite faith. Sidney Rigdon
was afterwards Counselor to Joseph
Smith, the Prophet, and Parley P. Pratt
and Lorenzo Snow became members of
the Quorum of Twelve Apostles. Loren-
zo Snow was at the time of his death,
October, 1901, President of the Church of
Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Eliza
R. Snow, his sister, was a gifted poet and
is alluded to by “Mormons” and Gentiles
as “Zion’s Poetess.”
	One of her hymns has become famous

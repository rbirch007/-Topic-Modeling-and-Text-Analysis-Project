EDITORIAL NOTES.

	THERE ARE many criminals who are both
educated and intelligent; yet it is estimated
that ten to one of the crimes committed
throughout the country, are done by the
illiterate and ignorant.
	THE KANSAS Legislature has passed the
joint resolution to have the question of
woman suffrage decided by a vote of the
people. Will the men alone conclude the
decision of the question after all, or will the
women be considered as a portion of the
people?

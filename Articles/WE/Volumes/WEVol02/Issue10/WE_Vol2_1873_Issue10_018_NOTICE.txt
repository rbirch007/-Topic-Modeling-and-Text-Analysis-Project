NOTICE.

	The EXPONENT Committee of Consultation
is requested to meet at the residence of Presi-
dent George A. Smith, on Tuesday, the 21st
inst., at 2 o'clock, p. m. A full attendance is
respectfully solicited.
				   ELIZA R. SNOW, Pres.
	MINNIE HORNE, Sec.

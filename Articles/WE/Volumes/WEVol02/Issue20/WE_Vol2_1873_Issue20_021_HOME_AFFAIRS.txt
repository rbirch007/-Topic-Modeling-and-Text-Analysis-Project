HOME AFFAIRS.

	IRREGULARITIES IN the Cache Co. mail have
caused considerable discussion, in the papers,
lately. We hope better mail facilities in that
section will be the result.
	THE PROPOSITION to number the buildings
in this city is being agitated. The matter is
one of public importance, and, if properly car-
ried out, will doubtless be of great benefit.

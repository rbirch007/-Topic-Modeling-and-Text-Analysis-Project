RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	The Third Semi-annual Conference was held
in the S. L. Assembly Hall in this City, Tuesday
Oct. 7, 1891, commencing at 10 a. m., Pres.
Zina D. H. Young presiding. Conference opened
with singing,

"Prayer is the soul's sincere desire, &c."

RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

       The fourth April Conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the Salt Lake Assembly
Hall in this City on Tuesday evening April 5,
1892, commencing at seven o’clock. It is very
desirable that there should be representatives
from each Stake of Zion, as some business of
importance is to be presented to the sisters.
On account of the greatness of the occasion of
this General Conference of the Church and the
multiplicity of business the Relief Society will
have but one meeting, and therefore a
prompt attendance is essential. All members
of the Relief Society and all interested in this
labor of love are cordially invited.
	ZINA D. H. YOUNG,
	President.
	JANE S. RICHARDS,
	BATHSHEBA W. SMITH,
	Counselors.


Christianty commands us to pass by injuries;
policy, to let them pass by us. 
          Passionate reproofs are like medicines given
scalding hot; the patient cannot take them.
If we wish to do good to those we rebuke we
should labor for meekness an[-] wisdomand use
soft words for hard arguments.—Dodd.

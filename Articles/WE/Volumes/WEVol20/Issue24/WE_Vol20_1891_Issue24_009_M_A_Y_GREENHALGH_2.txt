		M. A. Y. GREENHALGH.


Miss. Louise Francis, of Castroville, Cal., has
a valuable piece of property in her daily news-
paper, the Enterprise.  Miss Francis is tall,
rather slim and has beautiful hair and eyes.
Her work is marked by common sense, ac-
curacy and a complete knowledge of her sub-
jects.

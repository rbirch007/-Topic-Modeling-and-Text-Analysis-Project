OBITUARY.

	PRIMROSE CUNNINGHAM Shields, wife of
John Shields, was born at Renfrew, Renfrew-
shire, Scotland, Aug. 17th, 1805; was married
to Bro. Shields Aug. 16, 1827; was baptized
May 10th, 1842, died July 4th, 1878. She was
the mother of nine children, four of whom
are now living, and fifty-six grand-children,
and three great grand children.
	Her name will ever be held in honorable re-
membrance for her firm faith in the gospel,
for her integrity and uprightness, and her dili-
gent, earnest efforts for the good of all within
her reach. She bore her last sickness with the
greatest patience, and calmly fell asleep,
awaiting the morning of the resurrection.

		You've dune your duty here mither,
			As mair than me can tell;
		You've taught your bairns the road to gang

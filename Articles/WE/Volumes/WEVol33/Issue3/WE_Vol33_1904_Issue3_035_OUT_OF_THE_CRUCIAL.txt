OUT OF THE CRUCIAL.
	BY MABELLE BIGGART.
I said:
	I will walk in the fields,
	Where the flowers are fresh
	And the skies are blue-
God said:
	"Nay, walk in the town,
	There hearts are burdened-
	And homes despoiled!"
I said:
	But the clouds are black- 
	The way is dark- 
	And my courage is faint!
God said:
	"But hearts are sick-
	In the noise and the din-
	There is work for thee-
	In the sorrow and sin!"

[WomansExponent | Vol33 | 1904 | February | Issue: 8 | Title: AMERICA—WASHINGTON. | Author: heav’n inspired, true, loyal hearts led on]

AMERICA—WASHINGTON.
America! fair land of Freedom’s sires!
Alas! how low doth burn the brilliant fires
Enkindled long ago! when Washington
By heav’n inspired, true, loyal hearts led on
To fight and nobly die in freedom’s cause,
And for this new-born land make righteous laws.
Oh Washington! nor patriot so true—
Our countrys’ tender father! unto you
We give all honor due; this natal day
Commemorate thine honored name and pray
That God for us perpetuate thy birth,
And send another just like thee to earth.
						ELLIS R. SHIPP.
	Feb. 22, 1905.

NEW ORGANIZATION. 
	Jay, Leavenworth Co., Kansas.
Sister E. B. Wells:
	According to appointment a meeting was
held for the purpose of organizing a
Relief Society at Jay, Leavenworth Co.,
Kansas, on June 17, 1901, President James
G. Duffin, of the Southwestern States
mission, presiding.
	After a few timely remarks the following
organization was effected and the officers
unanimously sustained: For president,
Mollie Stevens; counselors, Mary E.
Clinkenheard and Mary E. Hill; secretary,
Luella Hasty treasurer, Mary Adams.
	We would further say that we are en-
tirely inexperienced as far as Relief Society
work is concerned, and would be glad for
any advice you might have to offer and al-
so to know what is required of this organ-
zation, any information at any time will be
gladly received by us as we are desirous
of doing our duty. Hoping to hear from
you soon, we'are your sisters in the great
cause of truth.
			LUELLA HASTY, Sec. 
	June 26, 1901.

					EMILY HILL WOODSMANSEE.


THE ladies engaged in home industries and
enterprises of various kinds, should make a good
representation of their several departments of
work on the 24th inst, especially as they enjoy
the privilege of the franchice, of which nearly
all the women are deprived.

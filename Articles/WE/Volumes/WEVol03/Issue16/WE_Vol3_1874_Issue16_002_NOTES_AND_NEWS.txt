NOTES AND NEWS.

	THE REAL winter in this valley did not
set in until January; and now the question
arises, will it stretch out till the latter end
of May?
	THE DIABOLICAL practice of lynching, or
hanging men upon suspicion of crime, was
last fall perpetrated upon a son of ex-Gover-
nor Ford of Illinois. A friend of the EXPO-
NENT sends us an account of the affair, taken
from the Iowa “State Journal,” the particu-
lars of which, were recently published in
the daily papers of Utah. We are not now
prepared to comment on the subject, and
have not space for details.

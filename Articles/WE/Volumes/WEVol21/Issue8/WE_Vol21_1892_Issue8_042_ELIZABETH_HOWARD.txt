	ELIZABETH HOWARD.


Mrs. President Dwight, of Yale College,
originated the idea of a “Yale Home” for
students who may need medical care or
nursing. Mrs. Dwight, with the assistance
of many ladies of New York, among them
Mrs. William C. Whitney, obtained funds
for the erection of the building last spring,
and it has been completed during the sum-
mer. There every student who is ill can
be under the care of a matron and physician
and receive all the attention which could be
given him at his home.

COURTING SUNSHINE.

		TO N.
Dear and true and patient one,
Well thy task is being done.
Yet continue, be unmoved,
Be thy course af heav'n approved,
As thy life thus far hath been,
Chaste and meek and free from sin.

In thy day of test and trial,
Sacrifice and self-denial,
Trust not human strength or skill,
Look to God for guidence still;
He shall grant thee grace and pow'r
To endure this mortal hour

I have counted secret prayer,
Courting Sunshine,bright and fair,
Better than the shining gold,
Which the eyes alone behold,
Prayer doth graciously impart,
Golden Sunshine to the heart.

Firm in faith,with steadfast mind,
To thy portion still resigned,
What though fortune smile or frown,
So you gain the victor's crown,
Bright and glorious like the sun,
Tried and true and patient one.
				L. L. G. RICHARDS.

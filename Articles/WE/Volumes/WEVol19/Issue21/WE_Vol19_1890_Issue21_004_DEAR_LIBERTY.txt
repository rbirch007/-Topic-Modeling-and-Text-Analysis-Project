DEAR LIBERTY

		TUNE—"Bethel."
Nearer we come to thee,
		Freedom, to thee;
Pleading on bended knee,
		Freedom, for thee.
Strike from our fettered hands,
Tradition's galling bands,
In near and distant lands,
		Dear Liberty.

Come we o'er frozen seas,
		Freedom, to thee,
From blooming southern leas,
		Seeking for thee.


From every land and clime,
From every age and time,
Calling thy name sublime,
		Dear Liberty.

Proudly we'll face the world,
		When we are free,
Thy starry flag unfurled,
		Over us free.
Spread, spread thy wings of light,
Dispel our long, long night,
Kindle thy glory bright,
		Dear Liberty.

Altars we'll raise to thee,
		Freedom, to thee,
Altars to God and thee,
		To God and thee.
Our voices high we'll raise
In hymns of grateful praise,
For thine unshaded rays,
		Dear Liberty.

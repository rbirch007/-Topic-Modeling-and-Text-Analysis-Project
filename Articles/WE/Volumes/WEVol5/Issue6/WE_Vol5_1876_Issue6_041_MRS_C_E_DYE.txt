MRS. C. E. DYE,
	Is now prepared for the Spring Trade, with all the
latest styles of Hats and Bonnets, and newest shades of
Silks and Ribbons. Flowers in endless variety.

fine assortment, which cannot be excelled in quality and
price.				17, East Temple Street.

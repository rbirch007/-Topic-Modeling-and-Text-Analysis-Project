RELIEF SOCIETY CONFERENCE.

	THE General Conference of the Relief
Society will be held in the large Taber-
nacle in this city, Thursday, October 7,
1897, meetings commencing at 10 a. m. and
at 2. p. m. as usual. It is desirable that
each Stake organization of the Society
should be represented by the President or
an authorized representative. All mem-
bers of the Society and of the Church are
cordially invited, the meetings of the Con-
ference are open to all interested in charita-
ble or philanthropic work.
			ZINA D. H. YOUNG,
					     President.
			JANE S. RICAARDS, 
			BATHSHEBA W. SMITH
					    Counselors.

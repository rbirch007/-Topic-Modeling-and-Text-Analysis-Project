[WomansExponent | Vol26 | 1897 | August | Issue: 6 | Title: CHORUS | Author: EMILY H. WOODMANSEE]

CHORUS
Praise the Lord, Praise the Lord! and the Pion-
	eers too!
The Founders of Utah, undaunted and true!
Hail! Hail! to the Heroes whose debtors are
	we—
For this great Jubilee! Utah’s grand Jubilee!
			EMILY H. WOODMANSEE.
*Joseph Smith.

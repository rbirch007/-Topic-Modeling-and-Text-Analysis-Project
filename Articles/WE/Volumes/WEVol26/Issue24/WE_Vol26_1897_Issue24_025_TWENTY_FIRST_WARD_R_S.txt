TWENTY-FIRST WARD R. S.
	The directors met and the following sis-
ters were unanimously elected and set apart
by Bishop M. S. Woolley and Counselors
H. J. Foulger and Jos. Maxwell, in the
positions named below: Eliza Barton as
president by Bishop M. S. Woolley; Martha
B. Taylor as first vice-president by Counsel-
or H. J. Foulger; Frances L. Castleton as
second vice-president by Counselor Jos
Maxwell; Mary A. Tuddenham as secre
tary by Bishop M. S. Woolley; Helen B

[Column 2]
Lyon as treasurer by Counselor H. J.
Foulger. On motion of Sister Castleton
Sister Eliza Barton was sustained as execu-
tive committee, to transact all necessary
business between stated meetings.
H. J. FOULGER, Sec. pro tem.

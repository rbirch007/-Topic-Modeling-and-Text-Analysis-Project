			A QUESTION.
Having, in common  with other women of this
community, enjoyed  the  right  of franchise for
ten  years  past, and  having  broken  no  law by
which I would forfeit  that privilege, I ask, as I
feel I have a right to ask, why an attempt is be-
ing made to deprive me of my lawful rights?
			 	MERCY R. THOMPSON.

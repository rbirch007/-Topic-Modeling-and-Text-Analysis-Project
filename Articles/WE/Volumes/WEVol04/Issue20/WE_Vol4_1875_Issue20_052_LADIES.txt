[WomansExponent | Vol4 | 1875 | March | Issue: 20 | Title: LADIES | Author: UNITED ORDER OF TAILORS]

					LADIES
 If you want your husbands to have a good, substantial
           Suit, either Home-made or Foreign, send
                                  them to the
                  UNITED ORDER OF TAILORS
    They will take in payment, CASH, FLOUR,
                          BUTTER and EGGS,
							F. GOSS, Supt.

MISCELLANEOUS

	A GENTLEMEN, who was recently mar-
ried, said to the officiating clergyman (a
confirmed wit) in bad grammer: "I think I
have done wise."—"Yes," replied the oth-
er, undoubtedly you have done wise, or
otherwise!"

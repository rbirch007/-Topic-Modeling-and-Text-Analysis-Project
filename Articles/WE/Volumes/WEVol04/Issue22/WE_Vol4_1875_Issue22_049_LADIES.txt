LADIES
If you want your husbands to have a good, substautia
Suit, either Home-made or Foreign, send
them to the 
UNITED ORDER OF TAILORS
	They will take in payment, CASH, FLOUR,
			BUTTER and EGGS,
					F. GOSS, Supt.

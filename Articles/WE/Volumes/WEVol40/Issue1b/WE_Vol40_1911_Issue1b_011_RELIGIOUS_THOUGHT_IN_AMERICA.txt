RELIGIOUS THOUGHT IN AMERICA.
	[The following interesting paper was prepared
by Mrs. Myra Young Rossiter and presented be-
fore the Utah Society of the Daughters of the
Revolution at a recent meeting. Containing a vast
amount of information upon an important subject
in condensed form, it will be found of interest,
we are sure, to every reader.—EDITOR.]

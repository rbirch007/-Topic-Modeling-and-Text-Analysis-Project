Gospel,			MATILDA P. KILLIAN.


﻿A Congress of German Women was re-
cently opened at Frankfort, Germany. The
subjects discussed were prejudices, reform
in the education of women, the literature
of youth, woman in the family, the obsta-
cles to the extension of the employment of
women, influence in social life etc.—Ex.

		CORNIELIA S. ROBINSON.


WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


“Are you the defendant in this case?”
asked the judge sharply. “No, sah," an-
swered the mild-eyed prisoner. “I has a
lawyer hired to do de defendin’. I’s de
man dat done stole de ahticles.”

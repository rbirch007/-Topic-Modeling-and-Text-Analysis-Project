RELIEF SOCIETY SECRETARIES.

	THE secretaries of the several branches
of the Relief Societies of the Salt Lake
Stake of Zion are urgently requested to
send in their reports before the 20th of the
present month. Address—WOMAN'S EX-
PONENT office.

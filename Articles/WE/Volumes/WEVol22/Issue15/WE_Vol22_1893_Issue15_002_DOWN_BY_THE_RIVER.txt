[WomansExponent | Vol22 | 1893 | April | Issue: 15 | Title: DOWN BY THE RIVER. | Author: Unknown]

DOWN BY THE RIVER.

Down by the River:
The dark flowing river,
	That flows to Eternity’s Sea:
Kneel to the Giver
To God, the Good Giver 
	And pray for my vessel and me.
Cold is it? Dark it is
Hushed as my heart it is.
	Dark flowing river sublime!
Many ships sail on it
Many fleets fail on it;
	Strange, crooked river-of time!
Down by the Riverside
Wreck-covered Riverside!
	Pray for my vessel and me
Pray for us: Pray for us
Solemnly say for us: 
	“God guide ye safe to the sea—”
Swift is the Rivertide
Rough is the Riverside
	Pray for my vessel and me—
S. E. CARMICHAEL.

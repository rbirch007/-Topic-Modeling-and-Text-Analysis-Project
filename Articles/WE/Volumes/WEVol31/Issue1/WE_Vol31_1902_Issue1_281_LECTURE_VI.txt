				LECTURE VI.
THE SABBATH DAY—ITS SACREDNESS AND
				HOLINESS.
	Prepare your home duties as far as pos-
sible, that the Sabbath may be truly given
to rest and worship.
	Ref. 3rd Commandment.
	Doctrine and Covenants 59th section,
12th and 13th verses.
			ANNIE WELLS CANNON,
			CARRIE S. THOMAS,
			JULIA S. WOOLLEY,
						Committee.
			M. ISABELLA HORNE,
						President.
			SARAH JENNE CANNON,
			CLARA C. CANNON
						Counselors.

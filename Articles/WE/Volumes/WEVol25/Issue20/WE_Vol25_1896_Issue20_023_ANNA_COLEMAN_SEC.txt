			ANNA COLEMAN, Sec.


WOMAN'S EXPONENT


MRS. AMALIE B. POST, of Cheyenne,
Wyoming, has recently died. Mrs. Post
was vice-president of the National Wo-
man Suffrage Association for Wyoming for
many years, and as such had the honor to
sit beside the Governor of Wyoming and
participate in the ceremonies which cele-
brated its admission as a State.

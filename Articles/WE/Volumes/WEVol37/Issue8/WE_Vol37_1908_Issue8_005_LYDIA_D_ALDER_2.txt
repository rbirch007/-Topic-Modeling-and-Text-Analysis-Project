						LYDIA D. ALDER.


Suffrage for women is looking up in New
Mexico. Mrs. Ellen M. Bolles of Lumber-
ton, N. M., has undertaken to organize a
Territorial campaign committee, and has
succeeded in securing the co-operation of
fourteen newspapers, whose editors have
agreed to publish articles in advocacy of
the movement.

ROVER.


Miss Susan Fenimore Cooper is educating or-
phan children in industrial occupations in
Cooperstown, N. Y.

	Lady Dufferin raised £10,000 for charity in
a single year, and managed its distribution.
She devotes much of her time to work for the
poor.

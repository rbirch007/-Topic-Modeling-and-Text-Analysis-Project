LECTURE 8—ERRORS TO BE AVOIDED.
	(a) Using your influence against the
authority of the Church, Stake or Ward,
whom you have by uplifted hand agreed
to sustain. (b) Unkind criticism. Im-
puting evil to actions not understood.

—Ex. 	RUTH RANDOM.


WOMAN’S EXPONENT.


In the New York Senate Chamber at
Albany hangs the oldest portrait of Columbus.
It was presented by Mrs.  Maria Farmer, a
granddaughter of Jacob Leslie, governor of
New York in 1689, and bears date 1592.

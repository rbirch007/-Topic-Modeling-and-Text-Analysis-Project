	[PUBLISHED BY REQUEST.]
Sister give thy baby up—
	It is thy Father's will;
The little time he stayed with you
	A mission 'twas to fill.

He was a little angel sent,
	From fairer worlds on high,
To take a body here on earth
	The laws of life to try.

But now it is the Savior calls
	The little sufferer home;
Why should we mourn to lay him down
	When he his work hath done?

Our Father we now dedicate
	This baby unto Thee:
May we be worthy still to claim
	Him in eternity.
					MARGRET A. WHITE.

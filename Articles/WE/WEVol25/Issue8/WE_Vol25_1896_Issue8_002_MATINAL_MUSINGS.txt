MATINAL MUSINGS.

BEFORE the early rays of morn
Dispel the gloom and bring us dawn,
My heart ascends to Father’s throne,
And all His love and mercies own.
I thank Him for this night of rest  
And that through all my life He’s blest
And watched o’er me, with tender care,
And heeded every earnest prayer.
In peaceful joy my heart responds
To nature’s fair alluring charms;
Each leaf and tree and floweret sweet,
The shady glen and lone retreat,
The babbling brook with mossy side,
The forests grand and deep and wide,
The clear blue sky, the fleecy cloud,
The rising sun that speaks aloud
Of Father’s wondrous love and power
To His frail children every hour.
With solemn thoughts and reverent mood,
My soul pours forth its gratitude.
						E. R. SHIPP.

NOTES.
	Idaho women sent their suffrage banner
to New York for the parade.  On one [--]
of it is the design of the State s[--] which
was executed in 1890 by M[--] J. G. Green.
This is said to be the only State seal ever
designed by a woman. The pin of the State
Federation is also formed from the seal.

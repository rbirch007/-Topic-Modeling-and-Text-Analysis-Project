[WomansExponent | Vol15 | 1886 | February | Issue: 17 | Title: EMMELINE B. WELLS. | Author: Unknown]

			EMMELINE B. WELLS.

	Miss Susan B. Anthony, the President of the
Association, to-day received a telegram from
Kansas, stating that the Municipal woman suffrage
bill had passed the Senate of that State by a veto
of 25 to 13.
